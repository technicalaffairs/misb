Introduction

This documents details the directives for GIS Internal Component is prepared using  IEC 617-7 “May, 1996” the first revision was at 06 Feb 2002

Information

 

Policy

  • In order to make the identification of internal parts of GIS equipment simple and error free, it is recommended to paint, or through the application of tape, the symbols of the station single line diagram on the outside of the pipe work .

  • Only standard symbols must be used for circuit breakers, switching resisters, Capacitors, disconnect switches, earthing switches, potential transformers, current transformers, incoming lines and outgoing feeders .

  • The different SF6 pressures for bus compartments and interrupter compartments must also be identified on both sides of internal closed pipe cones. Gas envelopes containing all 3-bus phases should also be clearly marked.

Directives 

  1. Wear standard safety equipment.

  2. Use fiberglass ladders to climb GIS switchgear.

  3. Do not step on control cables, piping or pressure gauges.

  4. Carefully clean the pipe work before applying paint or tape

  5. Use pencil or marker pen to outline the single line diagram with it’s individual components.

  6. Line thickness should be a minimum of 20 mm and a maximum of 30 mm.

  7. If using paint, use masking tape or cardboard templates to prevent paint runs
