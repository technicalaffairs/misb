Introduction

This document outlines the list of oil tests, which must performed on current transformers and voltage transformers and the test method reference. The first draft initiated at High Dam HPS, by eng. Hossain Mahrous, at 7 July 02

Information

The following tests should be performed as the manufacturer instructions:

    1- Break down voltage test.
    2- Dissipation factor test:
    3- Flash point.
    4- Total acid No.

    5- Water content.
    6- Impurities.
