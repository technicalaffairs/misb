Silicon Filter in Oil Cooling Cycle		 Document No:   TB-SS-007-r0

Bulletin:
 When Acidity of Oil increased more than 0.4		 Approved Date:     08 May 2002
Date to be Reviewed:   May 2007

Introduction

A Silicon filter full of silica gel is mounted in cooling cycle for 500kv transformer and reactors for every phase also there are two silicon filters in the 132kv transformer.

Information

The function of silicon filters is to absorb the humidity from the oil, and improved the acidity. The diameter of silica gel granular inside the silicon filter about 2.7-7 mm according to the manufacturer certificate.

Note: As mentioned, when the acidity of oil increased for transformers or reactors silica gel inside silicon filter must be replaced

Recommendations

The following procedure should be carried out every five years Y5 or when the Acidity of oil increase than 0.4

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The low, medium, high voltage CB's of the transformer must be isolated and proper earthing at the 3 sides required.

  • Wearing safety helmet and safety belt.

  • Use only fiberglass ladder.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounded the work area is required.

  • Personal carrying the work must be trained and qualified by some one who is

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool kit/set, including a torque wrench,

  • Air blower, Lubricating grease (light grade),

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes,

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Close the two valves (1,2 fig 1)

  2. The ground work area under the silicon filter has to be covered with a clean sheet of plastic to receive the silica gel grains when it is fallen.

  3. Open the oil drain valve (5 fig1) to drain the oil from the filter.

  4. Disconnect the lower flange (4) and the upper flange (3) allowing the silica gel grains fall down on the plastic sheet.

  5. Prepare enough quantity of new silica gel and get rid do small grains using a sieve.

  6. Reconnect the valve (5) with its flange (4) to its previous position.

  7. Put the new silica gel in silicon filter bottle till the level of the upper flange (3) and open the air outlet valve (6).

  8. Open the valve (1) to let the oil flew inside the silicon filter till the level of air outlet valve (6) gradually allowing the oil to replace the air and Repeat this statement two time to be sure there is no air in filter.

  9. Open the valve no 2 and wait for few minutes to be sure that all the bubbles of the air drain out from the valve no (6).

  10. Be sure that the pressure inside and outside the silicon filter is the same and the oil surface level reaches to the highest point in the filter.

  11. Close the valve (6) properly and operate the oil pumps and observe any air bubbles inside the gas protection relay (bochlis) and open the valve equipped to get rid of these air.

  12. Get the transformer ready to be put into operation.
