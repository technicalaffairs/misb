Introduction

This document lists the tests, which must be performed on all Power transformers as the manufacturer’s recommendations for the frequency of tests.

Information

This Bulletin outlines the testing Record Data Sheet for the testing of all transformers to be fulfilled by the testing group.

Recommendation

It is recommended for all power transformers to be tested and satisfy the following test sheet certificates.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes and safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tan d equipment, 5000Volt Megger, Turns Ratio test equipment.
