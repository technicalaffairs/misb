Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure I1, which must be performed every six years as indicated by Y6in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes safety harness

    •

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing on both sides of the breaker is required

  • The position indicator of the closing spring shows discharged

  • Disconnect supply and motor voltage

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes

  • Gas detector

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visual check for damages especially porcelains

  2. Check for corrosion on metallic parts and pipings

  3. Check the ventilation aperatures for operating mechanisms, control cubicle, linkage cover sheet

  4. Check of the anti - condensation heater

  5. Check of the SF6 pressure the SF6 density monitor in the green range

  6. Check of cable connection fixed inside the operating mechamisms and control cubicle

  7. Check of the securing elements of the drive rod loching nuts

  8. Functional check of the electrical control circuits by one close and one open operation

  9. Measurenent of the operating times take the operating times on one close and one open operation at rated supply voltage.   the reference values:  close time 91 ms,  opening time 32 ms

  10. Check of the SF6 gas quality with respect to the water contact, SF6 content, acidity, and dew point ≤ 10 Cº
    at nominal prassure                SF6 content > 98 %                    acidity ≤ 10 PPM

  11. Measurenent of the resistance of the main contact circuit breaker mcasure at the HV terminals directly. The reference values 45 micro-ohm

  12. Check of the bolt connections, for the correct tightening torque on all bolts
