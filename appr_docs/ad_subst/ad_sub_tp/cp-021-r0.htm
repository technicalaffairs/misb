Introduction

    This document outlines the inspection activities as indicated by I3 in the above title block to be performed on an annual basis Y1 or after 400 operation hours .

Note: This procedure initiated by Cairo South 220kv substation

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses,... etc

  • Ensure that the compressor isolated properly,

  • Don't wear loose clothes near the compressor while it is running.

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower to clean air filter, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Wear your mask if you have to use solvent such as gasoline and others for cleaning the oil filter because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Cleaning materials

  • Rags, air blower

  • Solvents and gasoline

  • Air suction and delivery valves, diaphragm

Work to be Carried out

  1. Repeats the monthly inspection as indicated by MPIS document CP-020-r0

  2. Check the air valves suction and delivery valves for cracks and replace it by a new one if you found cracks.

  3. Check the diaphragm for cracks for cracks and replace it by a new one if you found cracks.

  4. Check the valves seats for air leaks.
