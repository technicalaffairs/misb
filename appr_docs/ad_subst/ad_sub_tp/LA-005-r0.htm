Introduction

This document lists the inspection check I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Nashwa Hussein Mohamed, tested by eng. Atef Mohamed Abd El Rahman, accepted by eng. Samir Mohamed El Azab: general director, and approved by EETC Cairo Zone coordinator eng. Ahmed Shelaby Faisal Head Sector of EHV networks.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses,

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the arrestor, so you must wear your safety belt when cleaning insulators or checking connections

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required.

Tools and Equipment 

  • Safety helmet, safety belt,

  • SF6 gas leak detector

  • Air blower

 Work to be Carried out

  1. Read and record the discharge counter reading if fitted.

  2. Check for loose caps and tighten if necessary.

  3. Check the earthing wire connected and retighten each screw.

  4. Replace correct any suspected defective units revealed by test 2

  5. If discharge counter is fitted, check for proper insulation of arrestor's base to earth

    6.

  6. Check the SF6 pressure ref. value 4.6 bar.

    7.

  7. Using leak gas detector check the entire LA for SF6 gas leak

  8. Using moisture tester measure the dew point of the insulating gas it should be greater than 15ppm as indicated at MPIS doc TD-SS-004-r0

  9. Measure and record the operating of the SF6 pressure switch P1 should operate at 4.1bar and P2 at 3.9bar at 20oC.
