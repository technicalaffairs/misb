Introduction

This procedure details the patrol visual inspection I1 of energized circuit breaker should be carried out on a monthly base as indicated by M1 in the above title block.

 

This inspection is done during the patrol of the switchyard, there is no need to open the doors or covers of the operating mechanism housing and control cabinet for this inspection, if the SF6 gas low pressure alarm is given, SF6 gas should be supplied until the gas pressure reaches the rated gas pressure. The SF6 gas can be supplied even with the breaker energized.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the DS.

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Isolate the DC voltage.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Before using a blower to clean the control cabinet or when cleaning the insulators, wear breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particles.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump. 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks.

  • Tool set of  drivers, screw drivers, cloth

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the abnormal noise at the circuit breaker exterior.

  2. Check the rust or damage of components.

  3. Check the gas pressure and temperature measurement at the operating mechanism housing of  the gas system within rated values.

  4. Check the setting of air pressure switches at the air system in the operating mechanism housing within rated values. (air pressure is refer to approval drawing)

  5. Check air stop valves in position at the air system in the operating mechanism housing

  6. Drain the water from air reservoir at the operating mechanism housing.

  7. Check position of position indicator at control system / operating mechanism housing.

  8. Check registered number of operation counters at control system / operating mechanism housing.

Reference values

63 GA (ON) P1=0.45 + 0.03 MPa      (4.6 + 0.3 kgf/cm2 )

63 GL (ON) P2=0.40 + 0.03 MPa      (4.1 + 0.3 kgf/cm2 )

P1-P2 = 0.05 + 0.025 MPa                 (0.5 + 0.25 kgf/cm2 )

 

63 AR (ON) 1.40 + 0.03 MPa             (14.3 + 0.3 kgf/cm2 )
