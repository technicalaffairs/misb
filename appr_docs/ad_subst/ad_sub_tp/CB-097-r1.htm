Introduction 

This procedure outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed on every 5 years Y5 as indicated in the above title block. The first draft initiated by Demo substation, Eng. Abd Eltawab Ramadan. The first review at Nov 2007.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with cautions marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • CB must be isolated and proper earthing at the two sides of CB is required.

  • Interrupt remote control and motor circuits (this achieved by receiving the 
    multi-pin plug at the left hand side at the operating mechanism)

  • Switch breaker ON and OFF manually in order to discharge the closing and trip springs. 

Tools and Equipments

  • Gas moisture Tester.

  • Safety Personal Equipment ( helmet, Shoes,....)

  • Complete tool set.

Work to be carried out 


 The following inspections are to be carried-out in addition to the visual inspection as in MPIS doc. no CB-096-r0

Densimeter

  1. Reduce the pressure and determine the operating point, compare with the value obtained from the gas pressure curve. if the specified tolerances are exceeded reset the densimeter or replace.

  2. Measure the SF6 gas moisture content, the maximum permissible moisture content of  SF6 gas must not exceed 350 ppmv (parts per million by volume) if too high a value of gas moisture is recorded open the circuit breaker and replace the adsorbed.

Densimeter Adjustment


Pressure Adjustment

The densimeter has 2 or 3 independently adjustable microswitches (item 3) with adjusting screws (item 2). Pressure adjustment is reduced when adjusting screws are turned clockwise see figure 1. During this operation, bimetal strips (item 4) may not be touched.

Densimeter Replacement

Disassembly

  1. Prepare new densimeter and seal gasket (10),

  2. Unscrew cable connection,

  3. Unscrew the 4 M8 mounting with an Allen wrech,

  4. Remove densimeter,

  5. Remove seal gasket (10).

Assembly

  1. Insert new seal gasket (10),

  2. For assembly, proceed in reverse order of disassembly,

  3. Check gas pressure and correct adjustment if necessary

Note:    The densimeter of the switchgear supplied after January 1978 can be replaced under pressure. (copnnectio to gas compartment through nozzle position 13)
