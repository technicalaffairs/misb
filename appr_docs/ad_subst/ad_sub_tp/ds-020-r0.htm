Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks for (Z-type isolator) which must performed on a monthly bases as indicated in the above titled block  The first draft initiated by Tibbeen South 220kv substation, tested 14/10/2001, and approved 12 Jan 2002, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required

  • Change remote switch to local position

  • Interrupt the control circuit DC volt

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Cleaning materials (rags, cloth), soft cloth, mask,

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check supply voltage of the disconnector motor

  2. Clean the parts exposed to the atmosphere (insulators - pantograph - Scissor and earthing contacts)

  3. Clean the fixed contacts depend upon the climatic conditions and degree of pollution (where the isolator is installed)
