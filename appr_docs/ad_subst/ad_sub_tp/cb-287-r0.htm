Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P2 which must be carried out every six years as indicated by Y6 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mervat Mahmoud Al-Sayed Hasouna, tested by eng. George Pheliep, accepted & certified by eng. Abdel-Maqsoud Ahmed Mahmoud: general director of North Stations & Cairo Zone MPIS coordinator, and approved by eng. Ahmed Shelabi Faisal: Head Sector of EHV Network Cairo Zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc.

  • Circuit breaker must be fully isolated and grounded at both sides.

  • Disconnect the control voltage.

  • Change the selector switch to service position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of maintenance hand tools, air blower, and gas leak detector

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the mechanism, auxiliary switch by blower.

  2. Check the auxiliary switch function by its correct position through position indication.

  3. Clean the local cabinet by blower.

  4. Retighten all connections terminals and check the earth connections.

  5. Look around for uncommon noises and odor.

  6. Check all gas densities refer to monthly inspection.

  7. Check of anti condensation heating.

  8. Discharge the closing spring in the operation mechanism by performing one open - close - open operating sequence with motor circuit interrupted. It must be checked for proper operation

  9. Record the switching numbers from the counter of the switching operations of the circuit breaker

  10. Record the number of the short circuit operations when the cumulative current has been reached under normal operating conditions as shown in the curve it is necessary to replace the contact system
