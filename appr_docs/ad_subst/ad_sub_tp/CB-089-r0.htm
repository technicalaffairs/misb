Introduction

This document outlines the Minor Maintenance Procedure P1, to be performed on an annually bases year Y1 as indicated at the above title block. The first draft initiated by eng. Mostafa Abd_Alla Beni Suef East 220kv Substation and approved at 15 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded on both sides

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety equipment.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc".

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Read the number of operations.    

  2. Check the operation of anticondensate and heating resistors.

  3. Check absence of oxidation in the cabinet (otherwise, perform the necessary retouches):

    - Check the heating is working properly
    - Check the ventilation inlets are properly adjusted: they must not be blocked nor be directly splashed by water.
    - If Oxidation persist after inspection and repair, you should contact Merlin Gerin

  4. Clean the porcelain insulators.
