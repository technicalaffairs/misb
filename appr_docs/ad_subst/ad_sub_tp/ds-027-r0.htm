Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I2, to be performed on an annually bases Y1 as indicated in the above title block. The first draft initiated by Estad substation Cairo zone, tested at 1 Jan 2001 and approved at 5 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required.

  • Safety fence with cautions marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet & shoes .... etc.

  • Change remote / local control switch to local position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Air blower, gas detector, buzzer, screw driver, and set of common keys

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Through local, check the operation off / on remote and manual for the correct position

  2. Grease the outside moving parts

  3. Check the motion transmission shaft and retighten coupling joints bolts

  4. Check motor, ele. circuits (tightness, cleaning)

  5. Check earthing connections of compartment (tightness)

  6. Check door gasket.
