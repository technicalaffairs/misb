Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection, which must be performed every Six months as indicated by M6 in the title block 
for 220kv disconnect switch (DSB)

The first draft initiated by Eng. Saleh El-Sayed Saleh and revised by Operation and maintenance's General Director: eng Mohamed Reda Abd El-Azez
 

Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued.

Tools and Equipment

  • A complete set of tools.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. The switch should be cleaned to remove contaminant particles

  2. Check for loose bolts and nuts, tighten if needed.

  3. Examine the contact surfaces for wear pitting. 

  4. If a bright surface is desired use fine steel wool to polish, wipe, clean, and apply
    " NO - OX -ID " A special grease to the surface, remove grease with clean rag.

  5. If severe damage has occurred, replace the damaged part with Allis charmer's replacement ports.

  6. Examine the switch blade- contact alignment and realign if necessary.

  7. Check all galvanized surfaces for chips. If chipping has occurred use Galvanox R2 or equivalent as a touchup.
