Introduction

This document lists the steps for testing the turns ratio for the power transformers. 

Safety Precautions

  • Isolate the transformer out of service earthen

  • A word order permit must be issued.

  • The qualified test staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Disassembly all clamps from bushings (High Voltage side, Low Voltage side & tertiary if exists)

  • Connect the earth of tester to the common earth of substation.

  • Don't misapply the High leads and Low leads of the test equipment to the transformer bushings. 
    This will cause damage to the test equipment.

  • Keep test leads apart from the buahings.

Tools and Equipment

  • Test equipment type DRX 2000, The company address: Electrical Testing Instruments Scarbough, Ontario Canada M1V1E7  telephone 416-292-8181, fax: 416-292-8188

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Earth the test equipment with common earth of the substation.

  2. Connect the HV three-phase test leads (H1, H2, and H3) to the three phases HV bushings (R, S, T) of the transformer. Notice that Ho is neutral point with the neutral bushing.

  3. Connect the LV three phase test leads (X1,X2,X3) to the three phase LV bushing of the transformer notice that Xo is neutral point with the neutral bushing.

  4. Run the test between each as the following steps:
    - Switch on the tester and press H1-H0
    - Press X1-X0
    - Press the test button Record the result.

  5. Repeat the previous test procedure with rest phases H2, H0.....   Press X2-X0

  6. Repeat the previous test procedure with rest phases H3, H1  ..... Press X3-X0

  7. Follow the test procedures as the next drawing

  8. Record the result In the Attached Test sheet
