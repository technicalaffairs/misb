Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every three years or every 1000 operation as indicated  by Y3 in the above title block.

 

The first draft initiated by eng. Fawzy Hanafy Mahmoud, tested at Solb El Makhsous 220kv substation at 19 May 2003, reviewed by eng. El-Sayed Mohamed Ebaid , accepted by eng. Mohamed Abdel Latief:: General Director of Sadat & Tahrer Badr Substation and approved at 21 May 2003, West Delta Zone. 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • Circuit breaker must be fully isolated.

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off and ground the CB an both sides.

  • Switch off control current for CB (d.c)

Tools and Equipment

  • Tester (digital Avometer).

  • Set off spanners and screw drivers.

  • Oscillograph.

  • Silicon grease, Vacuum cleaner.

  • Operating oil (MIL - H - 5606)

  • 500/1000 V Megger.

  • Contact resistance meter.

Work to be Carried Out

Visual checking

  1. Check the tightening of bolts and nuts .

  2. Check rusting or peeling of paints.

  3. Check discoloration or heat hase due to over heating of main terminal.

  4. Check cracking.

Switching operation

  1. Check position indicator and operating counter.

  2. Check auxiliary switches by tester.

  3. Check pole discrepancy.

  4. Check operating time by osillograph,
        - Opening time below 0.03 sec.
        - Closing time below 0.1 sec.
    Note : Operating times are to be within 10% from the data obtained last time .

Greasing 

  1. Coat the joints with silicone grease.

Tests

  1. Insulation resistance of control circuit 2MΩ or more , measuring by 500 v megger 

  2. Insulation resistance of main circuit 1000 MΩ or more, measuring by 1000 v megger  

  3. Moisture content measuring (max. 300 ppm v)

  4. Check gas leakage.

  5. Check contact resistance of main circuit.

Final steps:

  1. Record counter reading of breaker and pump for each pole .

  2. Check no oil leakage

  3. Check the rated gas pressure (6 bar at 20   )

  4. Check oil level in the tank
    Note : this document must be carried out to each pole of the circuit breaker.
