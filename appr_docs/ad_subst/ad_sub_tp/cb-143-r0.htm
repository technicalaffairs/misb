Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1 (visual check + maintenance), to be performed every ten years or > 1000 operations as indicated by Y10 in the title block The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel-Mohsen Hanfy at Cairo 500 substation ME zone.and tested at 18/8/96 (CB of BB2 for Samalout circuit)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Procedures should be done by trained maintenance staff or by AEG specialist

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your appreciated safety tools: helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.... etc

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Gas detector, cleaning material, and tools set.

  • Appreciated safety tools: helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Electronic Moisture measuring device Dilo  3-031-R001

  • Measuring device for SF6 gas decomposition products Dilo  3-032-R001

  • Measuring device for SF6 gas percentage 3-027-R002

Work to be Carried Out

General Works

  1. Identify the general data like: substation serial-no, E-no, type, year of manufacturing of circuit-breaker

  2. Identify the operating conditions:
       - Number of Co-operations (see operation counter).
       - Number of short-circuit interruptions.
       - Sum of short-circuit interruptions.

  3. Check SF6 tightness (look at scale of densimeter)

  4. Check the tightness of compressed air system of circuit-breaker, ICU (if applicable) and compressed air reduction of central compressor unit (if applicable).

  5. Starts of compressor

  6. Check the running time of compressor (if applicable).

  7. Check the number of refillings at central compressor unit (if applicable).

  8. Check of drain valve

  9. Check of paint finish and touch up where necessary.

  10. Visual check of porcelains (general condition, damages, pollution).

  11. Check of exhausts of the valves.

  12. Check of supply voltages, check heater voltage, check the current input of the anticodensation heaters, heating effect.

  13. Check of control voltages.

Test measurements

  1. Measure the transition resistance of main contacts from HV terminal to HV terminal at
    - S1- pole columns over one break
    - S2- pole columns over two breaks (also valid for circuit-breakers type S4)

  2. Measure SF6 gas quality, Moisture content (ppmv)

  3. Function check of circuit-breaker according to routine test.

  4. Measure the operating times of main break and auxiliary switch

  5. Measure the Time - travel - curves of interrupter unit:
    - Time - travel - curves of release coils at
    - the operations C,0,C0 and 0-0,3s-C0 at
    - rated operating voltage (UN) and voltage (0,85,UN) and
    - Rated operating pressure.

  6. Measurement at differential times between main break and auxiliary break.

  7. Measurement at resistance of auxiliary break.

  8. Function check of breaker - control

Check of drives

  1. Check of drives (cylinder and piston) with regard to the fastening of
       - Cylinder (4 thread bolts)
       - Drive piston (safety nut)
       - Drive rod of auxiliary switch
       - Greasing of cylinder tread

  2. Check of closing valves with regard to
       - Interlocking
       - Exhausting

  3. Check of opening valve stage 1 + 2 with regard to
       - Exhausting
       - Replacement of piston 0-ring if necessary (dynamic 0-ring)

  4. Check of opening valves, stage 3
       - Greasing of cylinder tread (without disassembly)

  5. Check of exhaust valves with regard to
       - Movableness of valve screw
       - Fastening of piston
       - Greasing of cylinder tread (without disassembly)

  6. Check of emergency tripping (if applicable) with regard to
       - Non return valve
       - Exhausting
       - Greasing of treads

  7. Check of auxiliary switch with regard to
       - Fastening
       - Movableness of controller cylinder
       - Correct coupling with drive rod
       - Fastening of electrical connections
       - Condition of heating resistor
       - Check of contact timing

  8. Check of guide flanges with regard to
       - Tightness
       - Greasing of guide flange

  9. Check of fastenings of pole columns with regard to the rated torques
       - Steel structure - foundation
       - Channel - steel structure
       - Pole column - channel

  10. Check of compressor (if applicable)

Check of control cubicle

  1. Check of control cubicle housing with regard to
       - Surface protection
       - Tightness
       - Ventilation
       - Fastening
       - Grounding
       - Cable entry

  2. Check of control panel and pneumatic apparatus equipment with regard to
       - Operation of control elements
       - Check of electrical connections
       - General condition

General check

  1. Check of high voltage terminal pads
       - If necessary, clean and grease terminal pads
       - Tighten fastening bolts with correct torque

  2. Visual check of insulators, if necessary, clean insulators and renew silicone cover of cementing

  3. Check tightness and surface protection of all covers

Function check before recommissioning

  1. Topping up of air receiver respectively air receivers to rated operating pressure.

  2. Test operations on each pole column measurement of:
       - Operating times of main break and auxiliary switch
       - Time - travel - curves of interrupter unit
       - Time - current - curves of release coils at
       - The operations C,0,C0 and 0-0,3s-C0 at
       - Rated operating voltage (UN) and rated operating
       - Determination of air consumption.

  3. Measurement of differential times between main break and auxiliary break.

  4. Measurement of resistance of auxiliary break.

  5. Check function of auxiliary switch and position indicator
       - Pay attention to correct coupling.

  6. Check safety valve:
       - Pick up value
       - Reset value

Recommissioning

  1. Check set up values of time relais and thermostates
       - Check set up values of pressure scale.

  2. Check function of position alarms, lockouts and heater control

  3. Check anti pumping, pole discrepancy, emergency tripping compressor control, air dry control.

  4. Perform C and 0 test operations from local and remote site and check position indicator.

  5. Touch up surface protection.

  6. Note technical data as:
       - Number of C0-operalinos
       - Number of operating hours of compressor
       - Number of starts of compressor.

  7. Release of breaker for recomissioning
       - Note the standards

  8. Note technical data for report
      - Ambient conditions
      - Names of customer`s staff
      - Data and time when breaker is recommissioned.
