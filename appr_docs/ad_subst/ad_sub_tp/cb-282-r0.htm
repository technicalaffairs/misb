Introduction

This document outlines minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every six years as indicated by Y6 in the title block.

The first draft document initiated by eng. Ibrahim Ibrahim Shehata, tested by eng. Aasem Mohamed at El-Manaief substation 220/66/11KV, revised by eng. Mohamed Emran: quality control manager, accepted approved by eng. Ahmed El-Ebrashy: networks manager, and certified by eng. Ali Atiia Eldeeb: head sector of Canal Western Networks & MPIS coordinator

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • CB must be isolated completely and the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the CB

  • Isolate the DC voltage. (switch off the control circuit and insert closing and tripping preventive pins)

  • Ensure that the CB grounded on both sides

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the appropriate personal protective tools, safety, helmet, shoes, safety glasses, breathing mask ... etc

  • Discharge closing spring

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable common tool set (keys, screw drivers ... etc)

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower

  • SF6 gas detector

  • SF6 gas tester (dew point, moisture content, air content, decomposion, acidity)

  • Pieces of clothes and rags

  • Grease and cleaning liquid (like gasoline)

  • SF6 gas and refilling machine (if needed)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Read and annotate the CB general data: type, serial number, year of manufacturing, number of operations, number of short circuit interruption

  2. Do the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months according to document no. : CB-167-r0

  3. Check the SF6 quality (according to technical bulletin in document no. :
    TB-SS-008-r0)
    Dew point                                             ≤ 41   °C
    Humidity                                               ≤ 16   ppmw
    SF 6 %                                                 ≥  98  %
    Decomposion products (SO2 + SOF2) ≤ 15 ppmv

    Note : New SF6 gas should have a moisture content less than 125 ppmv equivalent 15 ppmg

    Used gas should have a moisture content less than 500 ppmv (62 ppmg), and if it is more, the the gas should be exchanged with new gas or in the case GIS equipment ( a very large quantity of gas) it is more economical to use the gas truck to re-condition the gas

  4. Close the work permit and inform shift engineer that the circuit breaker is ready to be in service
