Introduction

This procedure details the inspection  steps I1  for the disconnect switch, which should be carried out on an annual basis as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

    Note: The first draft document initiated by Cairo North 220kv, and Tebbin 500 substation, Cairo zone

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the DS.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Isolate the DC voltage.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

Tools and Equipment

  •   Multi meter, tool set,

  • Air blower.

  • 220 Vac supply.

  • SF6 gas leak detector.

  • End scope equipment, moisture tester.

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Complete a visual inspection of all components of the disconnect switch.

  2. Using the multi meter, check for correct control voltage 220Vac and operating voltage 220 Vdc.

  3. Using the air blower, clean the mechanism cabinet and check the auxiliary contacts look for signs of overheating.

  4. Checking of control circuits (thorough examination of all circuit wiring and terminals).

  5. Check the enclosure earth cable to ensure it is properly fastened and making good connection.

  6. Check the condition of the operation mechanism ( auxiliary switches, interlocks, etc …) and the operating rod ( motion  transmission shaft ) look for signs of distortion.

  7. Retighten all coupling bolts and joints on the operating rods.

  8. Complete a second function operation of the switch via LOCAL and REMOTE control, and check the indicator position is operating correctly, also observe any unusual noises.

  9. Check the Sf6 pressure this value should be 4 bar.

  10. Using the leak detector check the entire disconnect switch for Sf6 gas leak.

  11. Using moisture tester measure the dew point of the insulating gas it should be not greater than 15ppm as indicated in MPIS doc no. TD-SS-004-r0

  12. Measure  and record the operating pressure of the SF6 pressure switch. the 2 setting, P1 operate at 3.2bar and P2 at 3bar.

  13. Using endscope equipment, check the position of the main contacts both open and close operations.
