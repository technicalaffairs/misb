Introduction

This document outlines the equipment checklist I1, which must be performed five years as indicated by Y5 in the above titled block. The first draft initiated by eng Mahmoud Awad Aly Salama at 26/3/02, Sandob 220kv substation, Delta zone

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry suitable safety category ID

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding work area,

    •

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .. etc

  • Isolate the breaker

  • Discharge the capacitors by reclosing the breaker.

  • Earth and short-circuit the breaker in accordance with VDE 105, part 1.

  • Disconnect the auxiliary supply by tripping the line-side MCB

  • Reduce the oil pressure of the electro hydraulic operating system to zero by operating pressure release valve

  • Comply with all the local safety regulations

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes .... etc, SF6 gas detector.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. General checks for the circuit breaker. Figure 1

  2. Read and record the number of make-and-break operations and any particular occurrences            

  3. Check SF6 filling.   SF6 gas pressure = 6.5bar

  4. Carryout the test operations:  for electro-hydraulic operating mechanism.
    Hydraulic oil alarm at 273bar, and lockout at 253bar.

  5. Make a visual check for oil leaks of the hydraulic system.

  6. Check the oil level and its condition. (on the mark)

  7. Check the provisional Nitrogen pressure.   Alarm of Loss of N2 at 345bar

  8. Check the heating effect.

  9. Check that the external connecting leads are seated firmly.

  10. Check the paint finish and touch up where necessary

 

11. Breaker base 14. Electro hydraulic operating mechanism 22. Interrupter unit
12. Control unit  16. Post insulator 23. Grading capacitor
13. Hydraulic-storage 21. Ball-crank mechanism 24. Conductor
