Introduction 

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every three months intervals bases as indicated by M3 in the title block.

The aim of these instructions is to recommend a minimum requirements for checks and inspections in order to maintain the reliability of the fire fighting systems in the transformer yards.

The first draft initiated by Eng. Emad Farouq Moustafa at Katamia 220kv substation Cairo Zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective safety equipment, i.e. safety shoes, overall, and helmet

  • Transformer outage is required

  • After the functioning test of the system, make sure

  • That the valve between compressor and main tank is closed before refilling the tank again with water

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet, Safety Shoes, and breathing mask .

Work to be carried out

  1. Carry out the weekly inspection steps as described at MPIS document number FS-009-r0

  2. Check water level in main tank (up to red mark) for this, open shout - off devices of water level indicator

  3. Check excess pressure in tank, refill with water, if necessary pressure must be between (9.5 : 10 bar)
               * Start filling - pump manually
               * Open shut - off devices of water filling - line 

Every three months (M3)

  1. Test the single - chamber remote control valve (C):
    Close the main gate valve (B1) and the air filling valve (M) / relieve the incitation pipe system by operating the solenoid valve (J). The valve disk (C1) will unlock

  2. Open hand - hole cover

  3. Clean the sealing surface of the valve disk and seat by means of a saft rag

  4. Place valve disk again on its seat

  5. Loosen the adjusting screw (C2) about 1 turn and move the lever (C3) to the right up to the stop (Its nose must then rest on the bridge of the valve disk)

  6. Open air filling valve (M) and fill the incitation pipe system with compressed air

  7. Slowly open the main gate valve (B1) fully and secure it in open position by a padlock strap

  8. Close hand hole cover

  9. Open vent valve (E2) until water is discharging, then close it again   

Yearly  (Y1)

  1. Carry out the above steps

  2. Close main gate valve (B2), open and clean the strainer if a pressure tank or elevated tank is existing, clean the in side and repair the coat of paint, if necessary

Two Years  (Y2)

  1-  Carry out the three months inspection and the annually steps

  2-  Drain pressure tank, repair anti-corrosion protection inside and out side if necessary
