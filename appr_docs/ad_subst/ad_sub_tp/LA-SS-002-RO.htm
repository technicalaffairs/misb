Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The first draft initiated by Mr. Helmut Canadian SISP, S/S specialist, and first approval at 2 Sep 1996, first review date 03 March 2002, and second review date 15 Jan. 2006. The document was updated by Ataka PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, i.e. safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the arrester when

  • Cleaning insulators or checking connections wear your safety belt

Tools and Equipment 

  • Safety Helmut, safety belt, 

Work to be carried out

  1. In addition to the 2 Monthly inspection check list, perform the following tasks below

  2. Disconnect bus or conductor and megger arrestor.

  3. Replace defective units revealed by test 2

  4. Correct any suspected primary connections (hot spots) that might have been revealed during thermovision test.

  5. Reconnect bus or conductor and check for tightness of line and earth bus connection

  6. If discharge counter is fitted, check for proper insulation of arrester's base to earth.
