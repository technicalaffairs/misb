Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves

    • ... etc.

  • No smoking is permitted in battery room.

  • The door of the battery room must be provided with NO Smoking signs .

  • The battery instructions must be displayed visibly on the wall..

  • Water source must be available in the battery room.

  • When working in the battery room bewares of parts under voltage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydrometer

  • Digital voltmeter.

  • Thermometer

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Keep the batteries clean and dry protect them against solid, liquid and gaseous contamination.

  2. Inspect terminals of battery and clean any salt deposits with distilled water.

  3. Clean battery room floor with water.

  4. Check for correct operation of battery room ventilation fan.

  5. Check for the correct position of all vent covers.

  6. Check acid level 10 mm above separators or between max / min levels.

  7. Write down the voltage, temperature, and acid density of pilot cells.
