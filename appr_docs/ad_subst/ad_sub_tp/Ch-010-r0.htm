Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1, which must be carried out on a monthly bases as indicated in the above title block by M1. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 21 April 2002 for charger no.1 at Sheraton El Matar 66kv Substation, Cairo zone.

 

The Charger technical data:

Input voltage 3*380 v AC,    Output voltage 220 v DC,     Output current 50A DC

Input voltage tolerance + 15%,    Input  frequency tolerance + 5%

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issuedand the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Wearing goggles, safety shoes, helmet, and rubber gloves is very important

  • Switch off the power supply

  • No smoking permitted

Tools and Equipments

  • Digital voltmeter

  • Suitable tool set, Brush

  • Vacuum Cleaner or blower

Work to be carried out 

  1. By selector transfer the battery to the stand-by charger

  2. Disconnect the power supply

  3. Check ventilation system

  4. Clean the charger from dust and dirties (be careful when using the brush to avoid dismantling any connections of wires)

  5. Check fuses, contacts and terminals

  6. Check the overheating compartments of control board

  7. Switch on input and output supply

  8. Check output voltages, the standard values is:

               - FLOATING VOLTAGE = 175* 1.4 = 245 V ± 1 %
               - HI-RATE VOLTAGE = 175* 1.45 = 253.75 V ± 1 %
