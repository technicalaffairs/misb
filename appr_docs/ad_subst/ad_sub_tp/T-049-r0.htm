Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I2, which must be performed every six months bases as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by: eng. Abdel Aziz El Bagoury, tested by eng. Doaa Anis, revised by quality control eng. Adel Abdel Qader El Nagar at EDEPC, accepted by general director eng. Mahmoud Saker and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talkha PS.

Safety Precaution 

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding on all its sides is required.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and tool set

  • Blower, voltmeter, Mugger device.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes,
