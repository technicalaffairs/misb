Introduction

The following equipment inspection as a first level of inspection I1, will be carried out by shift operation staff in substation on a daily bases as indicated by D1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • Please don't cloth too much to the energized parts.

Tools and Equipment

  • No need for tools.

Work to be carried out

  1. Visually check the oil level in the circuit breaker oil tank at the advisable level between the maximum and minimum marks on the oil level gauge.

  2. By binocular check the oil level of the bushings, the oil glass indicator must be full of oil.

  3. Check the oil level in the pump tank, oil level must be between the two marks of the gauge.

  4. Check all the oil tubes and all its connections to detect any leakage.
