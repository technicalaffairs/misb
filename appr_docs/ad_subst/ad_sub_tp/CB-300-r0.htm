Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 on GIS circuit breaker 220 kv, which must be performed on monthly bases as indicted by M1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Kamal Oshba, tested by eng. Reda Ragab Karam, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station, and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia Diab WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required..

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, and safety glasses.

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical tool kit.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check gas pressure for all equipments and record.

  2. Check the anticondensation resistance.

  3. Check the compressor oil.

  4. Make sure the condensate drain valve is in good operation.

  5. Take the number of operations for CB and record.
