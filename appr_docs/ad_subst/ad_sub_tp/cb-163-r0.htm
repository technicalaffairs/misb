Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P2, which must be performed every six years as indicated by Y6 in the above title block.

This document initiated by eng Gamal Soliman+ Asuit 500kv substation, Middle Egypt Zone..

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes safety harness

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Gas detector

  • Air blower

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the indicator of the breaker position indicates open.

  2. Check the discharged indicator of the breaker indicates discharged.

  3. Exhaust SF6 gas to open air.

  4. The room must be ventilated fully.

  5. Confirmation that PR.SW. operates between 3.0 - 3.2 kg/cm2

  6. Remove the cover of the bushing contact.

  7. Check the bushing contact.

  8. Remove the top cover of the stationary contact.

  9. Check the stationary contact.

  10. Repair the stationary contact surface with sandpaper applying thin coat of grease.

  11. Exchange if stationary contact length less than 18 mm.

  12. check and clean the puffer cyl .cap, puffer nozzle, moving contact ,cover cap and insulated contact cover.

  13. Measure nozzle inner dia.if it  is more than 32 mm replace it.

  14. Measure insulated cover's inner dia..if it  is more than 28 mm replace it.

  15. Check the moving contact tulip finger replace if wearing more than 1 mm.

  16. Apply thin coat of grease.

  17. Clean the puffer cylinder apply thin coat of grease to its interior.

  18. Replace the absorbent with new one don't expose the absorbent in the atmosphere for long time.

  19. Replace the gas sealed packing with new ones.

  20.  Rreassembly evacuate completely then fill SF6 gas, adjust the pressure.

  21. Adjust the touch length of the contact stroke.
