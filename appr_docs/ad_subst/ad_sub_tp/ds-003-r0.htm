Introduction

The purpose of this procedure is to provide detailed safe method for substations maintenance staff to minimize the possibility of error causing personal injury or damage to equipment.

This document outlines the inspection and maintenance procedures P2 to be performed on frequent intervals every 10 years Y10.

EEC substations engineers in head office and Great Cairo EHV Network Sector using their background field experience and EEC/OHI/manufacturer documentation to create this procedure.          

Safety Precautions 

  • Combined disconnect switch and earthling switch must be isolated and earthling at the two side.

  • The second combined disconnect switch and earthling (Q2) figure 1 must be isolated and earthling.

  • Isolate the DC voltage. 

Tools and Equipment 

  • Set of common keys, torque spring key, screw driver set, timing equipment, climb ampere, buzzer, vacuum pump, cleaning material (rags, cloth), PVC disposable gloves,

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Ethanol A15 M, Ethanol F25 M.

  • Electrical grease.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check drive and gearbox.

  2. Check for proper function of the motor

  3. Check The Transmission And Auxiliary Switch (Figure 2)

  4. With the disconnector/ earthing switch in the middle (open) position

  5. Remove both control plugs 3223.

  6. Disconnect the wiring to the drive motor 3002 the secondary terminal block 3007 of the plug.

  7. Mark and remove the solenoid 3999 wiring.

  8. Unscrew the solenoid base plate 3995 together with the solenoid 3999.

  9. Loosen the transmission unit 3010 at the screw 3994

  10. Loosen the fixing screws 3992 and remove the cam disc 3993.

  11. Lift off the transmission plate 3991 after loosening the nuts 3990 and the retaining 3989

  12. Clean and grease the transmission according to plan.

  13. Reassemble in the reverse order noting the markings.

Check The Main Contact (Figure 3)

  1. With the switch in the middle (open) position.

  2. Dismantle the complete drive unit from the enclosure flange.

  3. When lifting off the drive be careful of the spring 3501 mounted in the insulator shaft 3500.

  4. The spring must be refitted when reassembling.

  5. Check The Fixed Contacts of the Disconnector 3207 and Earthing Switch 3206

  6. The contacts silver plating must not be worn through, swing the contact blade 3502 figure 3 out of the corona shield.

  7. The silver plating must be folly intact within the contact mating area.

  8. If the contacts are is good condition clean and grease

  9. Fitting a new 0-ring 3503 figure 3 remount the drive unit M8      14  Nm (1.4 kpm)  tightening the screw  according to:
               M4       1.5    Nm (0.15 kpm)
               M5         3     Nm (0.4 kpm)
               M6       5.5    Nm  (0.55kpm)     +       20% 
               M10      26    Nm ( 2.6 kpm)      
               M12      45    Nm (4.5 kpm)
               M16     100   Nm (10 kpm)

  10. Cleaning the opened gas compartment, as the MPIS document no. TD-SS-005- -r0, TD-SS-001-r0
