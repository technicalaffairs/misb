Introduction

This document lists Equipment Inspection Checklist I1, which must be performed annualy as indicated by Y1 in the title block for the air blast Circuit Breaker, 11kv type ZWAR-JEC, rated current =1000A & short circuit current 40KA /3 second. Air pressure 15 Bar. This equipment in Heliopolis substation in Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Surround the work area using rope and caution marks.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, safety Helmet)

  • Switch off the CB and withdraw it from the condut.

  • Ground the cable head, and Switch off the DC.

  • Use fiberglasses ladders

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet and safety shoes

  • Portable air blower.

  • Set of common screw drivers, spanners, wrenches, cleaning material (rag, cloth).

  • Graphet grease, grease for lubricating, emery, fiberglasses ladder.

  • Metal brush, Set of dies.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check air pressure in operation (13.5 15 kg/cm2)

  2. Check the arc chamber

  3. Mechanism of connected - Check opening switch

  4. Check the mechanism of operation of the circuit breaker

  5. Check tighten, nut, bolts, etc

  6. Lubricate the bled of disconnected switch

  7. Lubricate the axis

  8. All porcelain surfaces of all bushings insulator must clean

  9. Disconnect the arc chamber and clean and it from dust and oxidation

  10. Checking the moving and fixing contacts

  11. Checking all spring of circuit breaker

  12. Checking the contacts if needed

  13. Checking all gasket of the pistons and rings

  14. Check cleaning and lubricating of the operation mechanism

  15. Checking the piston of the disconnecting switch

  16. Measuring the resistance of the arc absorbing (must be zero ohm)

  17. Measuring the resistance of contacts (must not exceed 100M ohm

  18. Measuring the open-close time:
    (a) for one phase the time between the disconnected switch and the chamber must not exceed 5ms (b) The difference in time between the 3:10 must not exceed10ms

  19. Checking the consumption for opening-closing operation
    (a) Checking the consumption for opening operation (must be between 4.1:4.7kg/cm2) 
    (b) Air consumption at closing operation (must be between 0.35:0.4 kg/cm2)

  20. The distance between moving and fixed contact not exceed 2.5mm.
