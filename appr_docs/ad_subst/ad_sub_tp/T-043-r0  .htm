Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I3, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated in the above title block by Y1. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 10/8/02 for T1 at Sheraton El-Matar 66kv Substation, Cairo zone

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety rode and precaution marks must be used

  • Wearing safety Helmet, safety show, safety belt

  • Switch off from two sides

  • Putting portable earthing is necessary

  • Be sure that transformer ladder is well fixed

  • Switch off DC source

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower for cleaning purposes, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Wear your breathing mask if you have to use any chemical solvent because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't through oil or grease on ground used, collect it and send to the central chemical labs to recycle it.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

  • Check the sound level of fans /////////////////review environmental document

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool sets

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower

  • Brush, Pieces of soft cloths

  • Suitable grease

Work to be carried out

  1. Repeat steps of three Months inspection w r t MPIS document # T-042-r0

  2. Inspect bushings terminal connections

  3. Inspect arcing horns and check the save distance between the tow parts of the horn according to environmental conditions (humidity, pressure, temperature, pollution, etc)

  4. Inspect the porcelain bodies and be sure that:
                 - It is in good condition
                 - It has no traces of creeping and arcing

  5. Clean porcelain bodies with spirit and soft cloth

  6. Inspect oil level in bushings

  7. Inspect oil level in conservator

  8. Inspect the operation of oil - level indicator

  9. Inspect the membrane of the emergency pipe

  10. Inspect the sediments from sediment - boxes of the conservator (should be drained)

  11. Inspect the contacts of the thermometer for oil temperature

  12. Manually actuate oil temperature thermometer contacts to check if it gives signals, trips or not

  13. Manually actuate the contacts of thermometer of ventilation system temperature control

  14. Check the grease in the bearings of the fan motors

  15. Check - visually - the main tank and its accessories, specially the parts that could not be seen from the ground level

  16. Inspect thermometer pockets, the presence of required quantity of oil in pockets

  17. Check correct installation of active elements of thermometers

  18. Inspect the earthing switch and grease its contacts

  19. Inspect the earthing system

  20. Inspect silica gel in the breather, change it if necessary
          Change silica gel              YES :                                NO:

  21. Inspect oil in breather, quantity and condition, it is preferable to change it
        Change silica gel              YES :                                NO:

  22. Check dielectric strength of the insulation oil

  23. Check gas analysis in insulation oil

  24. Check chemical analysis in insulation oil

  25. Check the dielectric loss tangent (tg  Õ) for winding insulation

  26. Check DC resistance at all tap-changer steps

  27. Check transformation - ratio at all steps for the three phases 

  28. Check R60 and R15 for windings

  29. Check the  paint coat

  30. Check corrosion in unpainted metal parts:
     -No corrosion   -There corrosion and we treat it   -There corrosion and has not been treated  

  31. Inspect the auxiliary current transformer for winding temperature indicator (it is at he S phase bushing at 11kv side)

  32. Check  buchhulz relay float and contacts action and check the SIGNAL and TRIP function of the relay by pressing on the push - button (which occurred on the cover of buchholz relay and used for mechanical test of the circuit), firstly push gently on the push - button until signal happen, then continue pushing until tripping of transformer circuit breaker at tow side

  33. Inspect the condition of grease in the power transmission box for tap - changer add new grease if necessary

  34. For tap - changer, inspect the condition of blocking at manual driving, when you put the hand - crank in its bed, the electric motor should be cut off

  35. Inspect the condition of the electric limit blocking of the operating circuit, mechanical limit blocking at the tow end positions

  36. Inspect the whole cabinet to discover eventual damages
