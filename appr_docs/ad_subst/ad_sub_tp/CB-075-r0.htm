Introduction

This document lists major maintenance procedures P2, which must be performed from twelve to sixteen years as indicated by Y12:16 in the title block.
The first draft document initiated by eng Hamza Ibrahem Aly, El-Hadaba substation (220kv) 

Safety Precautions

  • A word permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • CB must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides.

  • Ensure that the earthling switch must be connected on both sides of the CB.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask,

  • Control power disconnected

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydraulic test gauge, SF6 test gauge meter

  • Micro-ohmmeter, Blower

  • Acidity equipment, air content equipment,

  • Hydraulic oil and SF6 gas if needed

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Empty the SF6 gas of the CB to atmosphere pressure The line is out of service.

  2. Dismount the breaking unit and place it on appropriate transport base.

  3. Transport the breaking unit to the workshop for the overhaul

Dismounting of breaking units

  1. Place the breaking unit on the mounting device (special tool) and connect their mechanism together.

  2. Dismount the (capacitors - top covers - desiccants - upper current collector with upper terminal flange)

  3. Dismount breaking units isolator carefully

  4. Dismount the lower current collector and take out the puffer

  5. Check the oil level in the dash - pots

  6. Check the functional values for the CB are met

  7. The main components of the breaker pole will now be accessible for cleaning, inspection and replace the burnt or damaged parts

  8. Grease the are and main contacts with (Grease K) and remount in reveres order.

  9. The following parts for puffer should be replaced (nozzle - sleeve - contacts - springs - breaking contact) these parts and contact sets are delivered in composed parts mounted together.

  10. Contact surface, puffer contact and puffer cylinder shall be treated and lubricated with thin layer of (grease K)

  11. All o- rings replaced and lubricated

  12. Connect and fix all parts of CB.

  13. Check alignment of the fixed and moving contact by operate the puffer slowly towards the closing position

  14. After place the breaking unit in closing position by mounting device making a vacuum and refill with SF6 gas until 7.8bar

  15. Check for any leakage for SF6

  16. Making for all of test "timing DC contact gas test"

  17. Check the function values for the circuit breaker against the appropriate values in the document "function values" and annotate the measured values in the table below:
