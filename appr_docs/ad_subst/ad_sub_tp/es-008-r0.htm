Introduction

This document lists the Minor Maintenance Procedures P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

Safety Precautions
  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required
  • A work permit must be issued.
  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes…etc.
  • Change remote/ local control switch to local position.

Tools and Equipment
  • Multimate, blower gas detector, buzzer, screw driver and set of common keys

Work to be Carried Out

1.

Check supply voltage.

2.

Check screw joints of the electric connections by pulling retighten, if necessary.

3.

Check pull-reliefs at cables.

4.

Check mechanical connections at drive or auxiliary switch by visual control for tightness and wear.

5

Check mechanical position indicator and adjustment of linkages (shown setting values at the individual poles must agree with each other ).

6

Check density of shaft seal in housing cover

7

Check functioning of auxiliary switch contacts ( current passage test, switching in the end positions

8

Check contact penetration depth rated value max. 101 mm/min. 92 mm until touching

9

Check wear of annular contacts and switched     1) 2)

10

Check electrical position signals (position indicator).

11

Check running time of drive. Compare with reference values from commissioning.

Note :

1) Requires opening of the gas compartment.

2) Random test at equipment haring undergone highest load.
