Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves

    • ... etc.
  • No smoking is permitted in battery room.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • Digital voltmeter.

  • Hydrometer.

  • Gloves.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect AC supply of battery charger.

  2. Draw out battery set on a flat clean wood plate.

  3. Inspect out side battery containers for cracks and leakage of electrolyte.

  4. Inspect contact area of inter connection, lugs, terminal connections, tightening and clean by plastic bristle brush, then coated with a thin film of No - ox - ID grease.

  5. Clean a top of battery containers with clear water and neutralized acid spilt only with a solution of sodium bicarbonate baking soda.

  6. Inspect and record electrolyte level, density for each cell ( 1.25 - 1.28 ).

  7. Lubricate terminals for cells with a thin film of Vaseline.

  8. Record voltage / cell it should be ( 2.05 ± 0.04 ) at 1. 25/1.28 , 77 ºf .

  9. If cell voltage more than 0.04 below min. float voltage put battery under equalize charge.

  10. Record overall voltage of battery it should be ( 2.05 x NO. of cell / battery if it is below put under equalizing.

  11. Measure internal resistance per cell.

  12. Replace defective battery.

  13. Check  battery house and dry it.
