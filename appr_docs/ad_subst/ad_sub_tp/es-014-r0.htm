Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, to be performed every three years Y3 as indicated in the above title block 

The first draft initiated by eng. Gamal Foad Soltan at Abu Zaabal500 at 15 July 02 Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with cautions marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Turn off the power source.

  • Lock the mechanism by mechanical lock pin.

  • Wear safety (helmet, shoes & glasses).

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check presence of rust outside the mechanism box

  2. Check presence of dew condensation and rust withen mechanism box

  3. Check movement of mechanism

  4. Check condition of position indicator

  5. Check lever stopper gap:
                      open position        0.5 ---- 8 mm
                      closed position      0.5 ---- 8 mm
               


                                  Figure 3 Lever stopper gap
     

  6. Check the wiring terminal condition

  7. Check the tightening parts condition

  8. Check the insulation resistance between control circuit and ground (more than 2 M ohm with 500v megger)
