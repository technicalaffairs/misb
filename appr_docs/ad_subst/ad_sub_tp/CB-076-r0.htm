Introduction

    This document outlines the equipment inspection steps I1, which must be carried out on an monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

    The first draft initiated by eng. Ezat AbdelMeniem and tested at  06 April 2001 at El Bassateen 220kv substation, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses …

  • CB must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the CB.

Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter, Air blower, Buzzer,

  • Complete substation maintenance tool set (srew drivers, common set of keys)

Work to be carried out

  1. Read and record the on/off operation number.

  2. Check the supply voltage 220V dc and 220/380 V ac.

  3. Check  oil level indicator , the oil level between min and max sign.

  4. Check for oil leaks.

  5. Clean the mechanism by dry air using air blower.

  6. Check the auxuilary switch functioning by it's correct position through position indication and retight electrical terminals.

  7. Check mech. position indicators.

  8. Clean the local cabinet by using dry air using blower.

  9. Retighten all connection (terminals) of local cabinet.

  10. Clean Insulators.

  11. Grease contacts (B.B & cable).

  12. Check the number of SC times (If the 3 times occurs change the oil).
