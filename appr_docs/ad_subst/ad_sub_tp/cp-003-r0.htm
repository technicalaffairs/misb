Introduction

    This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety and healthy equipment as safety boots, safety goggles, rubber gloves, rubber apron ... etc

  • Tool set.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Repeat Steps of Monthly Inspection Check List Doc. No. CP-002-r0

  2. To change oil, remove screw plug with oil strainer attached, let the oil drain off in to a container, clean out strainer with solvent, re-insert strainer and screw plug

  3. Fill compressor with new approved oil, approximately 10 liters of new oil is required

  4. After the oil change operation is completed the compressor must be started several times in succession since the suction line to the oil pump was emptied during the oil change operation

  5. Check air filter cartridge, if it is dirty, blow the cartridge out with compressed air from the inside outwards, if paper elements in cartridge are damaged, install a new filter cartridge

  6. Ensure that filter cartridge is properly seated when filter housing is re-assembled
     

  7. Check oil level in compressor crank case, but only with the compressor at rest, the oil level should never fall below the sight-glass

  8. Check for any unusual noises or vibrations

  9. Check for air leaks in piping and pipe connections

  10. Read and record gauge pressures and compressor running time indicator

  11. Clean compressor room floor
