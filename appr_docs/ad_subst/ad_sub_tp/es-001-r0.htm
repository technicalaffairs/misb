Introduction

This procedure details the Equipment Inspection I1, which are performed on an annual basis as indicated by the Y1 in the title block. 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Ensure that earthing switch compartment must be isolated and grounded it.
Tools and Equipment
  • Multimeter, blower, Gas detector, Buzzer, Screw drivers and Set of common keys

Work to be carried out

  1. Visual inspection of all outside earthing switch (ES) parts

  2. Check the main earthing connection and retighten if necessary.

  3. Check the supply voltage "220V d.c. & 220 / 380V a.c."

  4. Check the earthing switch operations (motor & hand) and observe the unusual operation noise.

  5. Check the socket (plug) terminals and ensure it is in correct positions.

  6. Retighten all terminals and check for any signs of overheating.

  7. Check the motion transmission shaft (operating rods) for all three phases and retighten all coupling bolts and joints.

  8. Check the motion drive shaft (operating rod ) of motor and retighten the bolts if necessary.

  9. Functions operation must be done on/off (local, remote and manual) and check its stroke.

  10. Check on/off indicators, ensure they are indicating the correct positions.

  11. Clean mechanism box (use blower & rag pieces) Take care not to leave any rag pieces in the

  12. Check all lock & pins of motion transmission joints (operating rods) It must be in healthy condition.

  13. Check door gasket of the earthing switch mechanism box .Gasket should be in place and making a proper seal if necessary.

  14. Gear box mechanism of transmission motion must be lubricated with silicon grease SI 31 if necessary

  15. Check gas pressure using portable pressure gauge it should be 3.4 bar.

  16. Check switch compartment for gas leaks using leak detector.

  17. Measure and record dew point nearly equal 600 ppmv.

  18. Measuring and record pressure switch operation p1 = 3.2 bar p2 = 3 bar ± 0.05 bar at 20oC?

  19. Using the end scope equipment to inspect the main contacts of the ES during opening and closing
