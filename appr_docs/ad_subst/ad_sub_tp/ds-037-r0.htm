Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1, which must be carried out every 3 months as indicated in the above title block by M3. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 8/5/02 for Matar circuit no. 1 at Sheraton El Matar 66kv Substation, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category, 

    •

  • Safety robe and precaution marks must be used

  • Wearing helmet and safety harnesses is necessary

  • Switch on the earthing switch and check mechanical interlocking with the disconnect or

  • Switch off  DC source

Environmental Precautions

  • Wear your breathing mask if you have to use gasoline because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't through oil or grease on ground used, collect it and send to the central chemical labs to recycle it.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

Tools and Equipments

  • Suitable tool set

  • Pieces of clothes and rags

  • Grease and cleaning liquid (like gasoline)

  • Safety harness, helmet, breathing mask

Work to be carried out 

  1. Clean porcelain surfaces from dust, dirties, deposits ... etc

  2. Remove the old grease out of the blades and contacts of disconnect switch and put new thin film of new grease

  3. Tighten bolts and nuts of disconnect switch

  4. Clean the gear box of disconnect switch and then grease it

  5. Clean the blade of earthing switch (that engages with disconnect switch)

  6. Clean and grease the gear box of earthing switch

  7. Inspect the marshalling box of the disconnect switch, remove dust, then clean auxiliary switch, limit switch, push buttons, etc ... with video cleaner also check its gasket to be sealed to prevent dust from going inside

  8. Repeat previous step (7) for earthing switch marshalling box

  9. Grease the horizontal axis of disconnect switch

  10. Inspect the operating arms and its hinges, and grease the hinges
