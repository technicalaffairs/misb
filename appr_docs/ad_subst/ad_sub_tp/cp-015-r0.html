Introduction

This document outlines the Equipment Inspection I1 procedures to be performed on a weekly basis as indicated by the W1 in the title block for Becker compressor type SV 250 max pressure150 discharge

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

  • Ensure compressor is properly isolated,

  • Do not wear loose clothing near the compressor while it is running,

Environmental Recommendation
  • Don't throw any remains of the paint into the sewage, because it will cause pollution inside the sewage.
  • Before using a blower to clean the air filter, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particles.
  • Using solvents such as gasoline and others for cleaning the oil filter is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very hazardous and carcinogenic chemicals. You have to wear your breathing mask.
  • Don't bum the cloth used in cleaning; collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety equipment, safety Helmet, and safety glasses

  • Cleaning material, rags, air blower, and solvent

Work to be Carried Out

1.

Check oil level in compressor crank case, but only with the compressor at rest, the oil level should never fatl below the sight glass.

2.

Check for any unusual noises or vibrations.

3.

Check for leaks in piping and pipe connections

4.

Read and record gauge pressures and compressor running time indicator.

5.

Clean compressor room floor.
