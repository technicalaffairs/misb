Introduction

    This procedure details the minor maintenance activities P1 which are performed on an annual bases Y1 as indicated in the title block.
      Note:  This procedure initiated by Cairo south 220kv substation
Safety Precautions

  • The disconnect switch must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the DS.

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Isolate the DC voltage.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower to clean the control cabinet or when cleaning the insulators, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particles.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter,

  • Air blower,

  • Gas detector,

  • Complete substation maintenance tool set,

  • Endscope equipment, and

  • Moisture tester.

Work to be carried out

  1. Complete a visual inspection of all external components of the disconnecting switch during this inspection check for any abnormal conditions, paint condition etc.

  2. Check the correct control voltage and operating voltage using the multimeter, the control voltage should be 220Vac, and the operating voltage 220Vdc

  3. Clean the mechanism cabinet using air blower, then clean and inspect the auxilary contacts.

  4. Check the enclouser earth cable to ensure it is properly fastened and making good connection.

  5. Check the condition of operating rod ( motion transmission shaft ) looking for signs of distorsion.

  6. Retighten all coupling bolts and joints on the operating rods by wrench torque.

  7. Complete a second function operation of the switch via LOCAL and REMOTE control, Check that the position indicator is operating properly, and observe any unusual operating noises.

  8. Check the SF6 pressure reading ... the value should be 4 bar .

  9. Using the leak dector check the disconnecting switch for SF6 gas leak.

  10. Measure the dew point of the insulated gas ... the value should be not more than 15ppm ref. to mpis document no. TD-SS-004-r0 using moisture tester 

  11. Check decomposition and acidity.

  12. Measure and record the operating pressures of the SF6 pressure switch. There are two settings p1 should operate at 3.5 bar and p2 at 3 bar.

  13. Check the position of the main contacts after both open and close operations using endscope equipment.

  14. Inspect the lever stopper gap of disconnect switch.
