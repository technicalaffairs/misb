Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed from month to three months as indicated by M1:3 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mahrogy Zekry Filpos, tested by inspector Abdel Hameed Meky at Karm El-Deeb 132/33kv SS, accepted by eng. Azer Abdel Nour general director, accepted by eng. Abd El-Razek Aly abd El-Meneim Head Sector, certified by eng. Ahmed Fathi quality control director, MPIS UE zone Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated and earthed.

  • The control voltage should be disconnected and switch off the DC.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set include torque wrenches, screw drivers ...etc.

  • Cleaning material (rag, cloth),

  • SF6 gas detector

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check visually all parts of CB

  2. Clean all outside compartment of CB from dust

  3. Retighten all fixation bolts if necessary

  4. Check SF6 pressure on manometers (use gas leak detector if necessary)

  5. Check operation air pressure on manometers

  6. Check the operating pressure air values (start - stop) for compressor

  7. Check (alarm for low press - cut off when high press) for compressor
