Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance Procedures P2, to be performed every ten years as indicated by Y10 in the title block The first draft initiated by eng Emad Farouk Mostafa, tested 13 July 02 at Kattamia substation 220kv, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •
  • Wear the appropriate personal protective tools, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses
  • The circuit breaker should be switched off.
  • The spring of operating mechanism must be blocked.

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common tools, keys, screw drivers, cleaning cloths, SF6 gas and machine.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Ensure that the system is left in open position.

  2. Drain off SF6 gas completely from the chamber of CB.

  3. Dismantle the arcing chamber for the three phases of CB.

  4. Check the moving contacts and rechange if needed.

  5. Check the fixed contacts and clean it.

  6. Regrease the fixed contacts with light flame of grease.

  7. Change all gas kets and reclean all parts inside and outside.

  8. Re Adjust the position for three phases and reassemble it in the reverse order to that described for dismantling.

  9. Check the screw connections and retighten in accordance with fig (4).
        - The two lock nuts 46 in, which secure the lock 45 to opreating mechanism casing 30 fig (5)
        - Use torque wrench with lux . insert sw13 at 8 Nm
       - The seven hexagon head screws for securing.
       - The cover plat 35 and bearing cap 36 to the opreating mechanism casing 30 fig (4)

  10. Lubricate points of the spring operating mechanism fig (6)

  11. Lubricate points level pinion and bolt fig (5)

  12. Lubricate points power transmission fig (5)

  13. Lubricate all ball bearings, winding handle, level grease.

  14. Make vacume for CB up to (-1 bar) for 4 hours

  15. Refill the circuit breaker with sf6 gas up to (5 bar)

  16. Recheck the circuit breaker mechanism in off-on position and check the charged spring

  17. masure and record timing on-off for CB, (make electrical test) (90 ms-40 ms )

  18. Measure and recored DC contact resistance (40 M ohm ),compare with previous test results

  19. Measure and recored the SF6 gas percentage, dew point (99%, max 50 ppm)

  20. Put the circuit breaker in service.
