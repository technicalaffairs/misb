Introduction 

This document outlines the equipment inspection steps I1, which must be performed on a weekly basis as indicated by W1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves ... etc

    •

  • Don't smoke in the battery room.

  • Don't place any tools on top of battery cells (Danger of short circuit)

  • Don't use any chemical cleaner.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard personal safety equipment.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Inspect terminals of battery and clean any salt deposit with distilled water.

  2. Clean dust from battery by using soft brush.

  3. Check for correct position of all connections.

  4. Clean battery room floor with water.

  5. Check for correct operation of battery room exhaust (ventilation).
