Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance Procedures P1, which must be carried out every 6 years as recommended & defined in Mitsubishi catalog as "ordinary maintenance" and as indicated by Y6 in the title block.

This document is updated by eng. Sherif Mahrous Omar  on 28 Aug 2004 and tested by eng. Nafad Kamel Mahmoud revised by UEEPCO Quality control general director eng. Gamal Abd El wahab and approved by eng. Mohamed Gamal Diab Head Sector of El-Kureimat power station.

Safety Precautions  

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, i.e. safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses.
  • Open the circuit breaker and open the DS's at both sides of the breaker pole. Ground the main conductor.
  • Switch off the power supply to the control circuit and exhaust the compressed air from the drain valve of the air reservoir.
  • Be sure to insert the closing or tripping preventative pins at the time of internal inspection and inspection of moving parts.
  • Use designated lubricant.
  •  Do not smear the lubricant on the insulators.
  • Renew all the O-rings at the flanges which have been opened for inspection. Handle O-rings with special care to avoid any damage.
  • Do not disassemble the slide seal assemblies.
  • Tighten each bolt with a proper torque.
  • Test the leakage of all reassembled gas seals.
  • Compare the mechanical operation data obtained before and after the inspection to insure orderly operation of the breaker.
  • Don't operate the circuit breaker by the pneumatic operating mechanism when the Sf6 gas  in the enclosures is below the lock pressure.

Environmental Recommendations

  • Fix any oil leaks from shock absorber oil to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil. Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impacts on the environment, mainly on soil and ground water and eventually on the ecosystem. "It takes only 1 liter of Hydrocarbon spilled in an aquifer to render 1,000,000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption" 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of maintenance hand tools.

  • 220V ac supply.

  • Portable pressure gauge.

  • Emergency light.

  • Timing equipment.

  • Safety helmet, shoes.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check tightness of ground terminals and other installation.

  2. Check of abnormal noise.

  3. Check of rust or damage of components.

  4. Check of blind cover for gas feeding inlet.

  5. Check of gas pressure and temperature measurement.

  6. Check of setting of gas pressure switches:

  1. Check open - close position of gas stop valve.

  2. Check of air pressure.

  3. Check of open - close of air stop valve.

  4. Check of lubrication of mechanism.

  5. Check of leakage of shock absorber oil.

  6. Check of looseness of retainer ring and nuts.

  7. Check of space heaters.

  8. Check of position of position indicator.

  9. Check of registered number of operation counters.

  10. Check of condensation and rust inside housing and water leak into housing.

  11. Check of door packing.

  12. Check of tightness of bolts in housing.

  13. Check of control circuit wiring and terminals.

  14. Check of manual opening and closing operation.

  15. Check of air reservoir capacity (from rated to lockout pressure).

  16.  Check of closing and opening time.      Closing < 90 ms. Opening < 18 ms.

  17. Measure and record: Air Content, Acidity, and dew point tests for SF6 Gas, reference to the approved MPIS document no. TD-SS-004-r0.
