Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1, to be performed on a monthly bases M1 as indicated in the above title block. The first draft initiated by Estad substation Cairo zone, tested at 1 Jan 2001 and approved at 5 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,
  • Safety fence with cautions marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet & shoes .... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Air blower, gas detector, buzzer, screw driver, and set of common keys

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check SF6  leakage using SF6 detector.

  2. Read and record the SF6  pressure 4 bar normal.

  3. Record no. of operations of the disconnect switch.
