Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I2, to be performed on a annually bases Y1 as indicated in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng M. Mahmoud M. Tawfeq and tested at 12/08/02 at Estad substation Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Ensure that the disconnect switch is isolated and earthed on both sides.

  • Change remote / local control switch to local position

Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter, blower gas detector, buzzer, screw driver and set of common keys

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Perform the monthly inspection steps as MPIS doc no. DS-023-r0

  2. Through local, check the operation off / on remote and manual for the correct.

  3. Grease the outside moving parts for position the high speed disconnect switch.

  4. Check the motion transmission  shaft and retighten coupling, bolts,  joints.

  5. Check motor, etc. circuits (tightness, cleaning).

  6. Check earthing connections of compartment (tightness).

  7. Check the operation (on-off) and looking from the glass eye.
