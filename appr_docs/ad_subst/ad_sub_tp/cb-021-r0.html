Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks I2, which must be performed every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes …. etc

    •

  • CB must be isolated and proper earthing at the two sides of CB area is required.

  • Isolate control circuit (DC voltage).

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydraulic Test gauge, SF6 test gauge, blower, dew-point equipment, acidity equipment air contoiut equipment, timing equipment.and contact resistance equipment.

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Repeat steps of annual inspection, MPIS Document no. CB-020-r0

  2. Check internal tightness of hydraulic components and take care during tightness without pressure.

  3. Check there is no condensation in the cabinets operation of the heaters.

  4. Check the condition of the electrical connections.

  5. Check leakage of gas from threshold of SF6 pressure regulators using gas detector.

  6. Measure Operating time of the CB 70 ms close, 40 ms trip, 120 ms close trip.

  7. Externally clean the control cabinet and breaker using a blower.

  8. Clean the inside of the control cabinet and inspect equipment mounting – heater operation, door gasket condition.

  9. Generally inspect the breaker for any defects including grounding electrical, conditions, deteriorating of hoses and electrical cables.
