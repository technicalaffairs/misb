Introduction

This document outlines the equipment tests I1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block. The tests will be done after any fault on CT, at capital repair, and at acceptance tests. The first draft initiated at High Dam HPS, by eng. Hossain Mahrous, tested at 10 March 2001 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes,

  • Isolate the line 15.75kv.

  • Separate the primary terminals of winding.

  • Separate the secondary terminals of winding.

  • Switch on the grounds.

  • Presence the industrial safety supervisor.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, rags, and silicagel

  • Megger: isol 5003, Doble M4000

  • DC deflection voltmeter & d.c battery.

  • Device (ATRT-03)

Checking of Winding

  1. Checked  the polarity of winding  by induction method. use equipments: d.c deflection voltmeter & d.c battery.

  2. Connect the battery on the high winding and voltmeter on low winding

  3. Check the direction of voltmeter 

  4. If the polarity is correct the direction of voltmeter in positive.

Measuring the Turns Ratio

  1. Inject current in primary winding and measured in secondary winding.

  2. Calculate the ratio Ip/Is
