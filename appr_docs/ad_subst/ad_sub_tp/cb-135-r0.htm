Introduction

This document outlines the Minor Maintenance Procedures equipment inspection P1, which must be performed every five year  bases as indicated by Y5  in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Anter Aly Bakr, accepted by general director: eng. Ibrahem M. Sayed El Ahl at Awlad Hmam 220kv substation, and approved by eng. Abdel Rahem Abdel Rahem Abdel Rahem; EHV Network Head Sector, Delta zone.

Work to be Carried Out

Adjustment work on the pressure switches must be carried out only by AEG-TFK staff

  1. Check SF6 pressure on manometer.    (8 bar at 20 oC, rated pressure=7bar))

  2. Check compressed air manometer with control instrument.

  3. Check air pressure on manometer.      (20 bar)

  4. Check SF6 manometer with control instrument.

  5. Check setting and function of manometer contacts.    
    SF6 contact manometer, temperature-compensated    F1 density controllere
    K1:  SF6-warning, 1st stage              pe    =    7.0 bar    (p1)
     K2:  SF6-operation, blocking 1         pe    =    6.5 bar    (p2)
    K3:  SF6-operation, blocking 2         pe    =    6.5 bar    (p2)

  6. Check SF6 moisture content (desired level <20oC).

  7. Check SF6 air content (desired level <2%).

  8. Check SF6 acid content (desired level <5-10 ppmg ).

  9. Check SF6 tightness at drive shafts, (sniffing drill-hole)

  10. Check for air and gas leaks.  (audible noise or frequent refilling)

  11. Before starting any work on the pneumatic system:
    - Close shutoff valves 371, 372 from the supply line and secure against unintentional opening, by removing the handle.
    - Switch off mini-circuit-breakers of the auxiliary voltage
    - Vent receiver 309 be means of valve 313. The flexible tube 317 on the monitoring block 374 may be released only after the receiver has been vented.

    12.

  12. Drain compressed air receiver.

  13. Check adjustment and function of compressed air pressure switch 376F4 Fig. 2, Adjustment work on the pressure switches must be carried out only by AEG-TFK staff 

  14. Check tightness of screw and pipe coupling.
     External diameter of pipe            Permissible moment
            10 mm                                        7 Nm
            12 mm                                      10 Nm
             15 mm                                      14 Nm
             18 mm                                      17 Nm

  15. Check refilling valve for function and tightness. Refilling valve 373 (Y0) fig. 2

  16. Check auxiliary switch S1 for function and burning of contacts.    Figure  4

  17. Check screw coupling of electrical connections if necessary re-tighten.

  18. Carry out test switching operations, (before switching check SF6 pressure in the pole columns, and the pressure of air in the compressed air receiver of the breaker):
    - 10 C-O-switching at one minute intervals.
    - Including 2 O-    switching with the manual emergency valve.

  19. Checking the pressure drop when switching as the following steps:
    a -     Produce operating pressure.   10 bar
    b -     Separate refilling valve from voltage or close shutoff valves (371/372).  Figure 2
    c - Operate the circuit breaker electrically.
    d -     Read off values on manometer (375) five minutes later.    Figure 2
         At 20 OC the difference is about 0.6 bar for opening, and about 0.3 bar for closing the breaker.
    e - Markedly increased pressure drops point to a fault in the pneumatic system.

  20. Check the mechanical On, OFF position indicators.
