Minor Maintenance Procedures (P1-Y5)

Introduction 

This procedure details the steps, which are performed every five years Y5 as a minor maintenance activity P1 on the Earthing Switch 500KV, type ELKEB, Asea Brown Boveri

Safety Precautions

  • The earthing switch must be disconnected and properly earthed using portable grounds.
  • A work permit must be issued,
  • Wear appropriate personal protective safety equipment, i.e. safety shoes and helmet
  • Appropriate warning markers must be place at the work site.
Tools and Equipment
  • Air blower,
  • Fine cleaning clothes,
  • Gas detector,
  • Complete substation maintenance tool set,
  • Fiber glass ladder,
  • End Scope equipment.
Work to be carried out
  1. Carry out a function test of the earthing switch (open and close operations), observing correct alignment of contacts and listening for unusual noise.
  2. Check and record the running time of the compression spring charging motor during a closing operation. The running time should be 4.5 seconds
  3. Inspect the centrifugal speed limiter for loss of oil, this is done by looking for signs of leaking.
  4. Check that gearing parts of the rewind mechanism are properly lubricated, re grease if necessary.
    Note:
    After 2000 close-open (c-o) cycles operations the operating mechanism must be removed from the switch and worn parts replaced in the spring assisted mechanism.
  5. Check the mechanism terminal strips for loose or damaged wires, tighten or replace as required.
  6. Clean the mechanism box using an air blower.
  7. Retighten all coupling bolts and joints on the operating rods.
  8. Perform complete electrical function tests which will check the control circuits and motor circuit.
  9. Using End Scope equipment, check the position of the main contacts after both open and close operations.
