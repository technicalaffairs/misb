Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1

The technical specification of the circuit breaker:
Rated current = 1250 & 2000 A, and short circuit current 25-31.5 KA /3 second.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc

  • Switch off the CB and withdraw it from the conduit, and ground the cable head

  • The spring of operating mechanism must be blocked

  • The opening mechanism should be secured against unintentional movement

  • The control voltage supply lines should be disconnected

  • Switch off the DC

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of mechanicals tools, troque wrenches with hex, SF6 gas

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

  • Portable air blower, metal brush

  • Set of common, drivers, and cleaning material (rag, cloth), Torque wrenches

  • Screw driver with extension spec

  • Filter's hammer, cotter pin driver

  • Benz assembly tool

  • Graphite grease, grease for lubricating 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Ensure that the system is left in open position

  2. Record the No. of operation

  3. Check SF6 gas leakage

  4. Check the tightening of bolts and nuts

  5. Check the presence of any abnormal noise

  6. Check the cable connections

  7. Clean the fingers contact by tighten all loose connection

  8. Check for operating mechanism (open - close - open)

  9. Lubricate the moving part

  10. Check the mechanical interlock

  11. Check screw connections for tightness when tightening loose connections replace washers & tension elements

  12. Clean the insulations and check for defects or cracks
