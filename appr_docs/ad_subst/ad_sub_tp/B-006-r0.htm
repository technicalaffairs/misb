Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I3, which must be performed on an annually basis as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves

    •

  • Don't smoke in the battery room.

  • Don't place any tools on top of battery cells (Danger of short circuit).

  • Never use the same tools on alkaline cells, you may have used on acid cells.

  • All tools will be used must be insulated.

    •

Tools and Equipment 

  • Standard personal safety and healthy equipment

Work to be Carried out

  1. Perform the monthly maintenance procedure from step 2 to step 9, in addition to the following steps:

  2. Disconnect AC supply to battery charger.

  3. Read and record overall battery voltage immediately after AC power has been disconnected and then after fifteen (15) minutes for the duration of one hour continue recording the voltage every hour for light (8) hours while observing at the same lime that the voltage does not fall bellow 48 volts.

  4. If voltage fall below 48 volts, place battery bank on float (high) charge for eight (8) hours.

  5. Place selector switch to trickle (normal) charge compare the readings you have taken to the reading recorded in previous years.
