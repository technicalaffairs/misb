Introduction

This document outlines the inspection activities as indicated by I1 in the above title block to be performed on annual base Y1. The first draft initiated at Demo substation, eng Abdel Tawab Ramadan. The first review at Nov 2007.

 Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with cautions marks surrounding the work area

  • CB must be isolated and proper earthingat on the two sides of CB is required.

  • Interrupt remote control and motor circuits (this achieved by receiving the multi-pin plug at the left hand side at the operating mechanism)

  • Switch breaker ON and OFF manually in order to discharge the closing and springs. 

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc".

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect top end of oil dash pot (58) through inspection window. If traces of oil are present on oil dash pot. remove and recondition as following:
             1- Remove side cover of operating mechanism.
             2- Remove oil dash pot and strip down.

  2. Measure the gas pressure with plug-in manometer and compare with gas pressure characteristic top-up SF6 gas if pressure has sunk to almost the refill pressure.

  3. Check the gas moisture content of SF6 gas and compare with the permissible moisture content limits (see table).

  4. Repeat the above steps for remaining two poles.
