Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safely category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used

  • Wearing safety helmet, shoes, masks are necessary

  • Switch off DC source

    •

Environmental Precautions

  • Before start cleaning, wear breathing mask to protect liung tissuse from dust particles and chemical solvent which, if exist, expose lings to danger
  • Don't throw any cleaning liquid on ground, collect it and send to dumb
  • Don't burn clothes used in cleaning, collect it and send to dumb

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set.
  • Pieces of clothes and rags
  • Grease and cleaning liquid (like gasoline)
  • Helmet, safety shoes and masks

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean porcelain insulators of circuit breaker from dust, dirties, deposits .... etc

  2. Check porcelain insulators to be free of cracking and scratching

  3. Inspect well the flower contacts and check it be in good condition, check its component (fingers) to be correct order and the spring compressed it is in good condition

  4. Clean flower contacts, remove the old grease from the contacts and the put new thin film of grease on it

  5. Check the contact arms to be fixed and in horizontal position to avoid bad contact between breaker contact and bas bar contact

  6. Inspect the pressure of SF6 gas and it to be in the safe area (green area)

  7. Clean the socket by video cleaner

  8. Check the mechanism of the circuit breaker, clean it with gas and grease it, check its parts for easy move

  9. Switch on the circuit breaker and off in test position mechanically and electrically

  10. Oiling the wheels of breaker tracks to make it easily move in and out of cell

  11. Measure the contact resistance

  12. Measure switching (switching off time and switching on time
