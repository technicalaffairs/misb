Introduction

This document outlines the checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases Y1 as indicated in the above titled block to minimize the possibility of error causing personal injury or damage to equipment.
The first draft initiated at Tibeen south 220kv substation, tested 14 Oct 2001, and approved 12 Jan 2002, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Isolate circuit breaker and ground it 

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Isolate the DC voltage 

  • Change remote switch to local operation position

  • Proper well fixed ladder should be used for climbing on the breaker

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete toolset, rag and air blower, Dew point, acidity equipment and contact resistance equipment

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check the door gasket of control cabinet

  2. Clean panel by blower, Bushings and connectors for dust and dirt

  3. Check control wiring for loose connections and tighten all connections as required.

  4. Check the gas leak

  5. Check the operation mechanism of the CB for loose bolts.

  6. Check gas dew point according to MPIS document no. TD-SS-004-r0

  7. Measure break and carryout the timing test for CB using time equipment.

  8. Measure contact resistance according to MPIS doc. no.CB-004-r0

  9. Check acidity contact of SF6 gas (MPIS doc no. TD-SS-004-r0)

  10. Check the pressure of SF6,  ref. value 7 : 6.3 atm
