Introduction

    This document outlines the inspection activities as indicated by I 4 in the above title block to be performed on an annual basis Y1 or after 1500 operation hours.

    Note: This procedure initiated by Cairo South 220kv substation

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses,... etc

  • Ensure that the compressor is properly isolated.

  • Don't wear loose clothes near the compressor while it is running.

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower to clean air filter, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate
  • Wear your mask if you have to use solvent such as gasoline and others for cleaning the oil filter because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals
  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.
Tools and Equipment
  • Cleaning materials, rags
  • Solvents and gasoline
  • Air blower, breathing mask.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Repeats the monthly inspection as indicated by MPIS document CP-021-r0

  2. Replace the air valves suction and delivery valves.

  3. Clean the element by gasoline.

  4. Check the diaphragm for cracks and replace if you found cracks.

  5. Check the valves seats for air leaks and replace them.

  6. Check the compressor unit for:

    • Cylinder crank shaft connect rod

    • Low Pressure piston ring mm clearance

    • High Pressure piston ring 0.6 mm clearance.

  7. Clean the compressor room floor.
