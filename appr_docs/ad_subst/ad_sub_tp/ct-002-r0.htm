Introduction 

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the titled block.

The first draft initiated by Mr. Helmut Canadian SISP, S/S specialist, and first approval at 02 Sep.1996, first review date 03 March 2002, and 2nd review date 15 Jan. 2006. The document was updated by Ataka PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, shoes, ... etc.

  • Current transformer must be isolated and proper grounding is required

  • Wear your safety belt because there is a danger of falling off the top of the CT stack when cleaning insulators or checking connections.

  • Presence the safety supervisor is required.

Caution: Secondary CT wiring could be energized through back feeds, make sure secondary circuits is short circuited.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness, Fiberglass ladder, Silicagel

  • Cleaning tools as cloth, rags .... etc

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, and safety shoes ... etc

  • Voltage indicator.

Work to be carried out

  1. In addition to the 2 monthly inspection check list, carry out the following steps:

  2. Check out the condition of the expansion diaphragm

  3. Open secondary terminal compartment, blow out compartment and check the condition of the weather seal

  4. Check that all wiring terminations are tight and securely fastened

  5. Check the condition of cables or conduits entering the secondary terminal compartment

  6. Check earth bus connections to base of CT for tightness.

  7. Do touch up painting if required.
