Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance Procedure P1, to be performed after every 5000 operations or ten years as indicated by Y10 in the title block.

Preventive maintenance is a cycle of planned inspection, testes, overhauls and servicing activities designed to keep the network equipment in good operating condition and avoid breakdowns.. 

The first draft initiated by eng Emad Farouk Mostafa at Kattamia substation 220kv and tested at 17 March 02

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

Work to be Carried Out

Instruction for dismantling and re-assembly the interrupter

  1. Drain out SF6 gas from C.B enclosure

  2. Drain off the hydraulic oil

  3. All parts which come into contact with SF6 gas should be touched as little as possible, insulators only with clean plastic gloves; when dismantled cover them with polyethylene foil to keep them clean.

Dismantling the interrupter

  1. Disconnect the operating mechanism 16700 from the three poles (fig 2/1)

  2. Dismantle the housing

  3. Disconnect the electric leads from the connector plate on the operating mechanism 16700

  4. Detach the hydraulic cylinder from the external lever 13924 by removing screw 13934 and pin 13923 (fig 2/4)

  5. Dismantle pin 16704

  6. Disconnect the auxiliary switch by unscrewing the castle ut 16686 and removing the vofaflex has

  7. Dismantle the end position inter lock 16740 from the crankcase

  8. Release the fixing screws from the mounting frame 16702

  9. Release the fixing screws from the base

  10. Remove the complete operating mechanism 16700 from the base 13965 (fig 2/1) to provide space for the with drawing deuice

  11. Assemble the over haul and with drawing device 01221 (fig 4/3)

  12. Mount support I 01222 and support II 01223

  13. Level the rails 01224 with a spirit level

  14. Mount the with drawing truck 10225 on the flange of the crank case

  15. Dismantle the flanged cover 13895 with filter 13885, with renew the gas ret 13925

  16. Undo all screws in the crankcase

  17. Carfully pull out the with drawing truck with the interrupter 17602 and on reaching the end position, hinge down wards with the crank case through 90º

  18. Lock the truck in the vertical position with two securing pine 01226

  19. Immediately it has been removed from the enclosure, free the interrupter from switching dust as far as possible, using the vacuisme cbaner

Maintenance Jobs

  1. Dismantle the exhaust casing 17610 (fig 2/3)

  2. Extract the fixed contact 17770 (fig 4/14)

  3. Remove the contact pin 17771 (fig 4/14)

  4. Replace : contact pin  17771,                       muticontact 17774

  5. Remove contact bush 17778 (fig 4/14)

  6. Replace : securing spring 17779

  7. Remove and replace the finger cage 17760

  8. Dismantle the chamber insulator 17730, blast piston 17707 and blast cylinder 17709 (fig 4/14)

  9. Remove the screws 17791, chamber insulator 17730, blast piston and cylinder 17707 / 17709 together

  10. Replace the blast cylinder

  11. To inspect the blast piston 17707, separate it from the cylinder 17709

  12. Remove the contact ring 17706, insulating no 33 le 17703 and auxiliary nozzle 17702 (fig 4/13)

  13. Replace : screns 17722 - spacer sleene 17788 - screening ring 17787 - contact ring 17706 - insulating nozzle 17703

  14. Replace : gasket 17721 - securing pin 17713 - auxiliary nozzle 17702 - securing spring 17718

  15. The auxiliray nozzle should be removed with a torque not exceeding 250 Nm

  16. Dismantle the actuating tube 17640 (fig 4/13)

Assembly    " Assemble in the reverse order to that discribed for dismantling  "

  1. Before assembling parts, check that they are clean

  2. Tighten the ouxiliary nozzle 17702 with a torque of 150-200 Nm

  3. Press the blast piston 17707 into the cylinder 17709use the pressing device 01230

  4. Insert the tube 17640 in the base casing 17790

  5. Fit the pins 17725

Adjust ment of the interrupter

  1. Move the interrupter to the stop in the open position

  2. Fit the end position inter lock in suchainay that the pin of the externel leuer is flush

  3. Push the pin of the inter lock forward

  4. Measure the clearance beween the pin and the cam of the lever and make a note of it

  5. Finally push the pin of the end position inter lock back again, move the interrupter into the closed position and lock in this position by means of the end-position inter lock.

Insert the interrupter

  1. Swing the truck of the withdrawing 01221 with the interrupter att ached to it in the horizontal position

  2. Lubricate the contacts

  3. Secure screwed joints loctite

  4. Clean the inside of the enelosure and the interrupter with the vacuum cleaner before inserting the interrupter

  5. Center the contact through the opening in the filter flange

  6. Screw up the crank - case flange
