Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I3, to be performed every 3 years Y3 as indicated in the above title block.
The first draft initiated by Estad substation Cairo zone, tested at 1 Jan 2001 and approved at 5 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required

  • Safety fence with cautions marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet & shoes .... etc

  • Change remote / local control switch to local position

Tools and Equipment

  • Air blower, gas detector, buzzer, screw driver, and set of common keys

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Perform the annual inspection steps as stated in MPIS doc no. DS-027-r0

  2. Check the alarms and adjust if needed (3.2 bar & 3 bar).

  3. Measure and record dew point.

  4. Check the interlocking system.

  5. Check contacts and replace if needed after 500 operating cycles.

  6. Check door gasket.
