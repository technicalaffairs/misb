Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every four months bases as indicated by M4 in the above title block. (Earthing transformers for the main transformers T1 & T2 at Elain El-Sokhna S/S (SOMED)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators.

  • Isolate the 11kv busbar.

  • Starting the cleaning of all porcelain surfaces of all transformer, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, and tool set.

  • Voltage indicator, portable earth for discharging on the bus bar.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes, silicagel.

  • Complete tool kit/set, including a torque wrench.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean all porcelain surfaces.

  2. Inspect all painted surface, remove any signs of rust repaint bare spots.

  3. Check for signs of oil leaks.

  4. Check oil level in tank and conservator.

  5. Check the condition of the silicagel replace it if its color changed from blue to pink.

  6. Check the condition of earthing.

  7. All bolted connections must be checked for tightness.

  8. Clean the body cables and the room of earthing transformer.

  9. Check the break point of oil.

  10. Measure the resistance for earthing transformer.

  11. Check every year the coils of the transformers (DC resistance measurement).

  12. Carry out every year megger test for coils (Insulation test).

  13. Connect all items which are disconnected.
