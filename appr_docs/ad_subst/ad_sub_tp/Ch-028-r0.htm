Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, safety Helmet

    •

  • Before start cleaning wear breathing mask to protect lung tissues from dust particles and chemical solvent which if exist expose lungs to danger.

  • Don't throw any cleaning liquid on ground, collect it and send to dumb.

  • Don't burn clothes used in cleaning collect it and send to dump.

Tools and equipment

  • Suitable tool sets.

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, and masks.

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower.

  • Pieces of soft clothes, and cleaning liquid.

Work to be carried out

  1. Change loads to the board B when work in A and vise versa.

  2. Switched off 380 draw the circuit breaker out.

  3. Blow the dust and dirt out by (using vacuum cleaner).

  4. Clean the cables and stips - electronic card - SCR and all component retighten all.

  5. Check the heaters in the cells to be switched on, clean it put the indicator of each heater at the suitable position.

  6. Check cells to be well sealed and no way for rats to get into the cells.

  7. Clean the upper parts of cells and the instruments involved in it such as, measuring instruments, etc.

  8. Put breakers in its position switch on.
