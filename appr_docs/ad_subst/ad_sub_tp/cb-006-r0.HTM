Introduction

The following procedures will be carried out by shift operation staff in substations.

Work to be carried out

  1. Drain the condensed water from high pressure air tanks

  2. Drain the condensed water from breaker tanks by opening the drainage valve (24 Fig. 1). for few seconds to let out all the condensed water. Drainage of condensed water should be done in early morning before the temperature has started to rise .

  3. Check the pressure in the breaker tanks at 17 atm.

  4. Check the function of heaters in the control cubicles, it is sufficient to observe that the glass of the position indicator window is not clouded up.

  5. Check all the tubes of the air network as well as the elimination tubes of the circuit breakers to detect any air leakage

  6. Visually check the flanges of both the hollow support insulators and the arcing chamber insulators to detect any cracks as this flanges represent a disadvantage in this type of circuit breakers.
