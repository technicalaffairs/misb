Introduction

This document outlines the inspection  procedures, to be performed on a three -month basis M3.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety belt when cleaning insulators.

    •
  • Isolation Switch must be isolated and grounded.
  • Change remote switch to local position. 
  • Isolation Switch must be isolated and grounded.
    •
Tools and Equipment

Tools: Common sets keys, cleaning material, filter, standard gauge, Torque spring key rag and scratch.

Materials: Electrical grease.

Work to be carried out

  1. Clean each isolation Switch including switch insulators and base of all accumulated

  2. Clean the switch contact surface with a cleaning solvent to remove grease and dirt.

  3. Allow surfaces to dry, regrease contact surfaces with electrical grease.

  4. Check switch operating mechanism from the switch to the control panel what are we checking for loose bolts loose linkages, ....etc..

  5. Check limits switch Cams limit switches. etc. for loose or badly worn parts.repair as required.

  6. Check for proper engagement of switch jaw and contact surfaces.

  7. Manually operate the switch and check for proper switch closing pressure.

  8. Check all bearings.

  9. Check control wiring for loose Connection and any signs of overheating.

  10. Check opening and clearance limit switches and mechanical stops.

  11. Tighten all electrical connections and make repairs to wiring as required.

  12. Check all ground connections for continuity tighten all ground connections.

  13. Check the main electrical connections for any loose bolts at the terminals.
    (tighten the bolts of the connectors as required)

  14. Clean interior of the control panel to remove any accumulated dust and dirt.

  15. Clean panel exterior, limit switch assembly unit and interlock assembly of all dust and dirt.

  16. Perform operations check both from remote and local control.

  17. Check the door gasket.

  18. Check on/off indications.
