Introduction 

This document lists the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block. The first draft initiated by Eng. Ayman Mahmoud Awad at Katamia substation, tested at 3 June 2002 Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective safety equipment, i.e. safety shoes, overall, and helmet

  • The Angle disconnect switch must be insulated.
  • Disconnect from the high voltage supply.
  • Earth both side of the Angle disconnect switch.
  • Change selector switch to local position.
Tools and Equipment

  • Set of key's, cleaning solution, piece cloth, vacuum cleaner,

  • Torque spring key and electrical grease.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check the Angle Isolator (closing & opening)

  2. Visual inspection the condition of the contact tube using sight glass.

  3. Check the gear running time with motor operated at 100% supply voltage.

  4. Check the Mechanical crank-actuated Mechanism manual operation.

  5. Check the electrical connection control circuits-motor circuit-Interlock-electrical position indicator (auxiliary switch)

  6. Correct Mechanical functioning.

  7. Synchronism between poles of the same isolator and agreement with the position indicators on the operating mechanism.

  8. Check SF6 gas pressure, humidity, percentage and decomposition

  9. Check the indication lamp.

  10. Check the contact resistance.

  11. Check  the SF6 density monitor schedule the results.
