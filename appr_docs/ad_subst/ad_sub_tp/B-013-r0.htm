Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy tools must be used  as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Smoking is prohibited in battery room.

  • Use standard personal safety equipment, safety goggles, rubber gloves and rubber apron.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set, and gloves.

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect terminals of battery and clean any salt deposits with distilled water.

  2. Clean dust off battery.

  3. Check for the correct position of all vent covers.

  4. Clean battery room floor with water.

  5. Check for the correct operation of battery room exhaust fan.
