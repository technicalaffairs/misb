Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks I1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required.
  • A work permit must be issued.
  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes…. etc.
  • Change remote/local control switch to local position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter, blower, gas detector, buzzer, screw driver, and set of common keys.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Repeat steps in MPIS Document no. ES-06-r0

  2. Check supply voltage.

  3. Check functioning of release magnet (Y 4)Ψ

  4. Check spring of piston ring (15) and blocking cylinder (13) for wear and damages. Repeat greasing after cleansing.

  5. Grease slide rods (11) at blocking cylinder (13).

  6. Check trigger path (39) for wear. Repeat greasing after cleaning 1)

  7. Check actuation ram of switch s3 for wear Repeat greasing after cleaning. 1)

  8. Check contact penetration depth. Rated value:
    Max. 151 mm/min 1.49 mm until touching/separation of contacts. Stroke of switch rod: 185 +/- 2 mm.

  Remark:     1) Requires opening of gas compartment
