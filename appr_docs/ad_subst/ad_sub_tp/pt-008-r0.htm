Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be carried out every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block

Test's of voltage transformer types HK 220 as follows:

  1. Insulation resistance test for primary winding.

  2. Insulation resistance test for secondary winding.

  3. Turn's ratio test.

  4. Capacity and dissipation factor.

  5. Testing of sample oil.

The first draft initiated and tested by eng. Khaled Ebrahim, approved by eng. Ramadan Ragab  general director, accepted by eng. Khairy Eshak Head Sector, certified by eng. Ahmed Said Abd El Aziz quality control director, MPIS ME zone Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ...etc

Tools and Equipment

  • 1000 volt megger

  • Turn's ratio equipment 

  • Tan (δ) equipment (TCB 100)

  • Breale down voltage equipment

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Isolate VT out of service.

  2. Clean the out  insulators.

  3. Isolate the end of primary winding from the earth point.

  4. Measure the  insulation resistance between (primary winding to earth & protection secondary winding to (earth + measuring SW) & measure SW to (earth + prot SW) & primary winding to (shorted SW + earth).

Notes

  1. The value of insulation resistance must be not less than 300 M ohm.

  2. Connect the end of primary winding to earth point before putting it in operation.

  3. Break down voltage for 500 kv PT must be not less than 20kv.

  4. Break down voltage for 220 kv PT must be not less than 35kv.
