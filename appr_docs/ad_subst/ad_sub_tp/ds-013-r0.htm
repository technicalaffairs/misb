Introduction 

This procedure details the inspection activities I1 which are performed every five years Y5 as indicated in the title block.

The first draft initiated at Pyramid 220kv substation, tested at 29 Oct 2001, and approved 12 Jan 2002, Cairo zone

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • The rotary disconnect switch must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Isolate the DC voltage,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses,

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Common set of screw drivers, set of keys,

  • Cleaning materials (rags, cloth), soft cloth, mask,

  • Timing instrument, Indicator glass, and cleaning fluid,

  • Endscope equipment, mask, electrical grease, and petroleum jelly.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check the contacts for good condition.

  2. Smooth out uneven spots and burn marks and lightly coat the contact strips with petroleum jelly.

  3. Test the contact force, the ref. value should be 600 N in the midway position.
