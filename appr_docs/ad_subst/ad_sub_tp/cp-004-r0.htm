Introduction

    This document lists the Equipment Inspection I4, which must be performed every week as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

  • Switch off 380 Volt AC supply to compressor

  • Switch off 220 Volt DC control power

Work to be Carried Out

*Repeat Steps of 6 Monthly Inspection CheckList Doc. No. CP-SS-003- -r0

  1. It's recommended to change the oil at intervals of 2000 operating hours, or every 2 years which ever comes first.

  2. The valves of all compressor stages must be inspected and cleaned from all contamination and oily carbon deposits every 6 months. It is good practice to have a spare set of compressor valves available to speed up the overhaul procedure

  3. Remove air filter and take out the round filter cartridge, use compressed air to blow out the filter element from the inside to the outside

  4. Remove first stage cylinder head, inspect and clean/replace suction valve and outlet supply valve, take great care that the valves are properly seated and the valve retainers are correctly placed and tightened

  5. Remove second stage cylinder head, inspect and clean/replace suction valve and outlet supply valve

  6. Remove third stage cylinder head, inspect and clean/replace suction valve and outlet supply valve

  7. Remove fourth stage cylinder head, inspect and clean/replace suction valve and outlet supply valve

  8. Remove unloading valve block, clean and inspected.

  9. Properly clean out oil and water traps below the unloaders

  10. Switch on compressor to blow out supply lines from carbon deposits for each successive stage

  11. In the same way blow out the oil cooling lines from oil/water deposits

  12. Check fan wheel and blades, oil pump and all pipe coupling connectors

  13. When all suction and supply lines are connected, test run the compressor, observing the correct gauge pressure for each stage

  14. Check the oil pressure monitor switch, momentarily close the ball cock the compressor must shut down after about 10 seconds

  15. Check the oil pressure gauge, and check for the correct oil level in the reservoir but only when the compressor it at rest

  16. Check the operation of compressor room thermostat, controlling the room's exhaust fan

  17. Check the non return valves from the main tank to the compressor unit

  18. Check the compressed air main tank, momentarily opening the bottom drain valve to expel any water
