Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng Mervat Mahmoud El Said, tested at 12 March 2004, by eng. Hamdi Mahmoud Ismael at Heliopolis substation, accepted by eng. Ahmed Faisal Shelabi General Director, and approved by eng. Samir Saleh Ismael ;Extra high voltage Cairo Sector, Cairo Zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate personal protective tools, safety helmet, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker and ground it.

  • Change remote/local control switch to local position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common tools, keys, screw drivers,

  • Cleaning cloths, SF6 gas and machine.

  • Air blower.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean all insulators and mechanism.

  2. Clean local cabinet by dry air, retighten all connections.

  3. Lubricate the moving parts of the breaker, which connected the mechanism parts.

  4. Inspect the contact surface "lower & upper" and replace warm parts (if necessary).

  5. Check for any corrosion at the mating contact surface.

  6. Check the charging spring of the breaker is recharged automatically after each ON operation.

  7. During charging spring, observe if there is any noise or spark in the charging motor.

  8. Check the auxiliary switch function by its correct position through position indication and check mechanism position indicators.

  9. Check the position indication of the earthing switch.

  10. Inspect the vacuum bottles.

  11. Check the contact resistance and timing switch of the circuit breaker.

  12. Record the number of operations from CB counter.

  13. Close the work order and inform shift engineer that the circuit breaker is ready to be in service.
