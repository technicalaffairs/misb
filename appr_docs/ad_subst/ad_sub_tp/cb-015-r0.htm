Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses, use only fiberglass ladders,

  • Circuit breaker must be isolated and grounded it.

  • Before using a blower to clean all compartments of the circuit breaker from dust, wear your breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

  • Fix any oil leaks from connections, safety valve, bleeding washers, and motion transmission shaft to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil.

  • Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impacts on the environment, mainly on soil and ground water and eventually on the ecosystem.

  • "It takes only 1 liter of Hydrocarbon spilled in an aquifer to render 1,000,000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption".

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set, SF6 gas detector.

  • Dew point, acidity, and air content equipment.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check visually all parts of circuit breaker.

  2. Check AC supply source.

  3. Check DC supply source.

  4. Record on/off operations counter

  5. Record the oil pump operations counter.

  6. Check the on/off indications.

  7. Visual outside inspections to separate insulator cone.

  8. Earth connection must be check.

  9. Clean all compartment of circuit breaker from dust, oil and other things use blower, cloth.

  10. Measure SF6 pressure of circuit breaker.

  11. Check gas dew point reference to MPIS Approved document no. TD-SS-004- -r0.

  12. Check the contact resistance for circuit breaker & all compartment.

  13. Check on/off timing.

  14. Retighten all bolts and earth connections of foundation and fixation of circuit breaker.

  15. Check gas pressure switch (for all stages).

  16. Clean all mechanism parts.

  17. Check oil level of main tanks for three phases.

  18. Check oil leaks for all connections.

  19. Check oil leak for safety valve.

  20. Check oil leak for bleeding washers.

  21. Check the heater operation.

  22. Check oil leak for motion transmission shaft (Ram).

  23. Check oil pump operation   
        - Start & stop operation.  
        - Accumulator pressure.   
        - On lockout pressure.  
        - Automatic switching lockout pressure.

  24. Check all terminals connections also the pump terminals.

  25. Oil bleeding must be done.

  26. Check auxiliary switch terminals.

  27. Check on/off coils.

  28. Check earth resistance.

  29. Check SF6 humidity content air content < 2% acid content < 10 PPM.

  30. Check door gaskets for all panels.
