Introduction

This document lists the Inspection check I1 which must be performed on a Weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, hearing protection, rubber, gloves ... etc.

    •

  • Selector switch must be in test position

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Test equipment level "dip sticks", multimeter, common sets keys, cleaning material "rags, cloth" and screw driver set

Work to be carried out

In addition to the daily inspection steps do the following:

  1. Change the operation position from automatic to local control.

  2. Check the hydraulic governor oil level.

  3. Check the coolant system coolant level.

  4. Check for air cleaner restriction.

  5. Check the fuel level, ensure it is at least 1/2 full.

  6. Check the battery charging system including water level in batteries

  7.  Start the unit up and run it at rated RPM and Check for unusual vibration.

  8. Check start switch in automatic.

  9.  Perform operation load test by switching on main CB

  10. Make overall visual inspection.

  11. Change the operation position from local to automatic.
