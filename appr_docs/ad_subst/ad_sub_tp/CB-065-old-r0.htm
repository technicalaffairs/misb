Introduction

This document lists Equipment Inspection Checklist I1, which must be performed from on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block. The first draft initiated by Pyramid 220kv substation.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • CB must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the CB.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses …

  • Control Power disconnected.

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydraulic test gauge, SF6 test gauge meter.

  • Micro-ohmmeter, dew point equipment, Blower.

  • Acidity equipment air content equipment and timing equipment.

  • Hydraulic oil, gease, and SF6 gas if needed.

Work to be Carried Out

1.      Check the gas density of each density monitors:
- An alarm contact giving an electrical signal in case of abnormal leakage.
- Alarm level 1 gives signal and alarms level 2 locks out.

2.      Clean the insulators of the circuit breaker from salt and contaminants.

3.      Lubricate the bearing in the circuit breaker with grease on delivery and need normally not lubricated other then at major overhauls.

4.      Lubricate plain bearings in parts of the mechanism such as arms links and link gears on delivery. These bearings should be lubricated regularly with a few drops of oil.

5.   Test operate the circuit breaker a couple of times
