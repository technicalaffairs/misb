Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Anter Aly Bakr, accepted by general director: eng. Ibrahem M. Sayed El Ahl at Awlad Hmam 220kv substation, and approved by eng. Abdel Rahem Abdel Rahem Abdel Rahem; EHV Network Head Sector, Delta zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • SF6 gas detector.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check SF6 pressure on manometer.   (8 bar at 20 oC, rated pressure = 7bar)

  2. Check air pressure on manometer.   (20 bar)

  3. Check for air and gas leaks.     (audible noise or frequent refilling)
