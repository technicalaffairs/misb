Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses, rubber apron, eye rinsing device is required.

  • No smoking is permitted in battery room.

  • Do not place any tools on top of battery cells (danger of short circuit).

  • Never use the same tools on these cells, you may be used before on lead acid cells.

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Standard screwdrivers set, density meter, voltmeter, brushes, distilled water, and rags.

Work to be carried out

  1. Read overall voltage of bank and record on battery maintenance card.

  2. Clean the battery.

  3. Check the electrolyte level and add distilled water (if necessary).

  4. Check the pole and connectors and clean any salt deposits with distilled water.

  5. Check that connections are tightened.

  6. Lubricate the pole and connectors with vaseline including the disco and nuts.

  7. Read voltage per cell and record on battery maintenance card.

  8. Take the electrolyte density and record on battery maintenance card.

  9. Again read overall voltage of bank and record on battery maintenance card and compare the reading from step no.1

  10. Compare the present reading with the last reading.

  11. Check the temperature inside the battery room not exceeding 45°C maximum and 20 degree min.

  12. Check the ventilation of the battery room to ensure their correct operation.
