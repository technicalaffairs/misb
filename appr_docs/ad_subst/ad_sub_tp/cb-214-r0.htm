Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated and properly earthed at both sides, it should be in ON position.

  • Disconnect DC Voltage.

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded on both sides

  • Control power disconnected

  • Isolate the DC voltage

  • Change remote switch to local operation position

  • Proper well fixed ladder should be used for climbing on the breaker

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes, .... etc"

  • Air Blower,

  • Gas detector

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Read the number of operations

  2. Check the door gasket of control cabinet

  3. Check the ventilation inlets are properly adjusted : they must not be blocked nor be directly splashed by water

  4. Check that the anti-condensation and heating elements in the control cabinets are operating properly

  5. Check for signs of corrosion in the control cabinets

  6. Check control wiring for loose connections and tighten all connections as required

  7. Check SF6 pressure on density switch, check the gas leak

  8. Clean panel by blower, porcelain insulators and connectors for dust and dirt
