Introduction

This procedure details the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance st off should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required
  • Wear your safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety glasses, breathing mask …. etc.

  • The Disconnect switch must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides .

  • Isolate the DC voltage. (Switch off the control circuit and Insert closing and tripping preventive pins.)

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set (screwdrivers, keys…..etc. ) .

  • Voltage indicator.

  • Cleaning materials ( Pieces of soft clothes and rags ).

  • Soft sand paper and cleaning liquid ( like gasoline ).

  • Carbon grease and silicon grease.

  • Air blower.

Work to be carried out

  1. Complete a visual inspection of all external components of the disconnect switch, during this inspection check for any abnormal conditions.

  2. Check for signs of overheating at terminals and retighten if necessary.

  3. Check the motion transmission shaft for three phase and retighten all coupling bolts and joints.

  4. Check the motion transmission shaft of motor and retighten the bolts if required.

  5. Check the operation stroke through the disconnect switch operation ( ON and OFF ) through remote and local ( hand and motor ) and observe any unusual sound .

  6. Clean mechanism box ( use blower and clothes ) .

  7. Check the earthing connection of mechanism box and retighten if necessary.

  8. Check to ensure there are no locks or pins missed from the motion transmission joints.

  9. Check the gasket of mechanism box door.

  10. Gear box mechanism of motion transmission must be lubricated with silicon grease.

  11. Check the bearings of the four driver levers and replace it if necessary.

  12. Check the silver layer at main contacts and that it have no erosion or rust.

  13. The disconnect switch functions should be checked after every short-circuit has occurred.
