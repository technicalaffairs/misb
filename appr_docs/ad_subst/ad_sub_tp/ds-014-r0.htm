Introduction


This procedure details the inspection steps I2 which are performed from five to eight years Y5:8 as indicated in the title block, The first draft initiated at Pyramid 220kv substation, tested at 10 Oct 2001, and approved 12 Jan 2002, Cairo zone.
 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • The rotary disconnect switch must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides, and isolate the DC voltage.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses, don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Common set of screw drivers, set of keys,

  • Cleaning materials (rags, cloth), soft cloth,

  • Timing instrument, Indicator glass, Cleaning fluid,

  • Complete substation maintenance tool set,

  • Endscope equipment, and

  • Electrical grease.

Work to be carried out

  1. Repeat the 2 years inspection steps.

  2. Check the greasing and easy operation of the swivels, regrease if necessary.

  3. Remove cleaner, grease and assemble all linkage bolts.

  4. Check that there is no oil on the diaphragm.

  5. Check the position of the membrance.
