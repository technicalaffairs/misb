Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block for motor operator for outdoor disconnect switch.

The first draft initiated by Eng. Saleh El-Sayed Saleh and revised by Operation and maintenance's General Director: eng Mohamed Reda Abd El-Azez

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of tools

  • Motor oil, Gaskets

Work to be Carried Out

  1.  Check motor drive chain. A light film of Motor Oil is sufficient, don't over-oil.

  2.  Check for excessive wear pitting or burning for Electrical contacts

  3. Check for sealing effectiveness for door gasket and replace gasket if worm or damaged.

  4. Check the stroke of the disconnect switch mechanical and electrical.

  5. Check the operation of the circle from manual position.

  6. Check the operation of the circle from remote position

  7. Check the interlocks for operation of the disconnect switch.
