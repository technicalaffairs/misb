Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1, to be performed on a monthly bases M1 as indicated in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Ensure that the disconnect switch is isolated and earthed on both sides.

  • Change remote / local control switch to local position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Air blower, gas detector, buzzer, screw driver, and

  • Complete set of common keys

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Visually inspect all external components of the disconnect switch.

  2. Check SF6 leakage using SF6 detector.

  3. Clean the mechanism cabinet using air blower.

  4. Check SF6 pressure (4 bar normal).

  5. Check the enclosure earth cable to ensure it is properly fastened.

  6. Retighten all cable terminals inside and outside cabinet.

  7. Check the heater, door gasket, clean gear box, grease thin layer of gear box if required.

  8. Record no. of operations of disconnect switch.
