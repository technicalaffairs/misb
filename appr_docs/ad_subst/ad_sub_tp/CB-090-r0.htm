Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every three years Y3 or after1000 cycles. The first draft initiated by eng. Mostafa Abd_Alla Beni Suef East 220kv Substation and approved at 15 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded on both sides

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety equipment.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc".

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check tightening of bolts and fasting nuts

  2. Check SF6 pressure on density switch (add SF6 if it is necessary)   reference value 10 bar

  3. Monitor the density switch pressure thresholds, replace the density switches if it is out of tolerance.

    As a precaution, before replacing the density switches, we recommend you to repeat the check in a room with a temperature stabilized at around 20 OC
    Tolerances at 20 OC

    "Filling top-up" signaling                            CR = 6.2 + 150 mbar
    "Low pressure" signaling                           BP =    6  + 150 mbar
    Minimum difference between CR and BP      d =   150 mbar
