Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 on low voltage air circuit breaker 380 v which must be performed on yearly bases or 1000 operations.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Kamal Oshba, tested by eng. Reda Ragab Karam, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam , approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia: WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, bearthing mask, safety glass... etc

  • Switch off the CB and withdraw it from its cell.

  • Switch off the DC power supply for this CB.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and helmet tools as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Cleaning materials (cloth - kerosene), tool set..

Work to be carried out

  1. Clean the carbon deposit and metal splashes, especially on the insulating parts.

  2. Remove humps on contacts surface by filling.

  3. Examine secondary and arcing contacts, and replace them when necessary.

  4. Lubricated all friction parts with grease.

  5. Test the breaker manually several times.
