Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I2, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 18/8/02 for Saker Qoraish Circuit no. 2 at First Urban Community 66kv Substation, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions 

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety rope with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, mask and safety harness

    •  is necessary

  • Switch on the earthing switch and check mechanical interlocking with the disconnect switch.

  • Switch off  DC source

Environmental Precautions

  • Wear your breathing mask if you have to use the gasoline because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't through oil or grease on ground used, collect it and send to the central chemical labs to recycle it.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

Tools and Equipments

  • Suitable tool set

  • Pieces of clothes and rags

  • Grease and cleaning liquid (like gasoline)

  • Safety harness, helmet

Work to be carried out 

  1. Repeat the maintenance procedures that have to be done according to MPIS document no. DS-035-r0

  2. Clean porcelain surface from dust, dirties, deposits ....... etc.

  3. Inspect well the insulators to insure if there is unseen crack, current traces, or alike

  4. Check dis connector contacts for pitting and corrosion

  5. Clean contact pieces and contact fingers and remove the old grease using cleaning liquid (e.g. Gasoline), then grease them with 1 mm - thickness film of suitable grease (such as Termalene grease 64444, or alike grease)

  6. Check earthing switch contacts for pitting and corrosion, if there is a corrosion of material to a depth of more than 0.5 mm, the earthing contacts must be replaced

  7. Clean and grease the earthing switch contacts as you did with disconnect switch

  8. Check motor and gear box of disconnect switch, clean and then grease them, and clean limit switch, relays, contactors, etc ... with video cleaner

  9. Inspect the mechanical interlocking between the earthing switch and disconnect switch

  10. Inspect movement - transmission arms, hinges, and joints for for disconnect switch, and grease it

  11. Clean the blade of earthing switch (that engages with disconnect switch) and then put a thin film of grease on it

  12. Check earthing switch motor, clean and grease the gear box

  13. Inspect marshalling box of the disconnect switch (the marshalling box is common for disconnect switch, earthing switch, circuit breaker) and clean it

  14. Check flexible connection of earthing switches

  15. Check all screwed joints for tight fit
