Introduction 

This procedure details the minor maintenance activities P1, which are performed on an annual basis as indicated by the Y1. This procedure document is the first level of maintenance performed on this type of disconnect switch.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance st off should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety rope with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,
  • Wear your safety helmet and safety harnesses is necessary.
    •
  • The isolator switch must be disconnected and properly earthed.
Tools and Equipment
  • Complete substation maintenance tool set.
  • Multimeter, Air blower,
  • Gas detector, Moisture tester.
  • End Scope equipment,
Work to be carried out

  1. Complete a visual inspection of all external components of the disconnect switch, during this inspection check for any abnormal conditions, paint condition etc.

  2. Using the multimeter check for correct control voltage and operating voltage, the control voltage should be 220VAC, and the operating voltage 220VDC.

  3. The mechanism cabinet must be cleaned using the air blower, then auxiliary contacts inspected and cleaned.

  4. Check the terminal connections for signs of overheating.

  5. Check the enclosure earth cable to ensure it is properly fastened and making good connection

  6. Check the condition of the operating rod (motion transmission shaft) looking for signs of distortion.

  7. Retighten all coupling bolts and joints on the operating rods.

  8. Complete a second function operation of the switch via LOCAL and REMOTE control, checking that the position indicator is operating correctly, also observe any unusual operating noises.

  9. Check the Sf6 gas pressure this value should be 5.4 bar.

  10. Using the leak detector check the entire disconnect switch for Sf6 gas leak.

  11. Using moisture tester, measure the dew point of the insulating gas it should be no greater than 15ppm as indicated in MPIS document TD- SS- 004-r0.

  12. Measure and record the operating pressures of the Sf6 pressure switch. There are 2 settings, P1 should operate at 4.5 bar and P2 at 3.9 bar.

  13. Using end scope equipment, check the position of the main contacts after both open and close operations.
