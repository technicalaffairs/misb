Introduction

This document lists the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block or 500 switching operation which come first.

The first draft initiated by eng Gamal Soliman Assuit 500 substation ME zobe.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • CB must be fully isolated.

  • Thoroughly exhaust comprised air from the CB air reservoir.

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of CB to ensure it is properly grounded

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, Helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses)

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on a work place, safety rope around a work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set including screw driver set torque wrench. and Blower.

  • SF6 gas leak detector.

  • 220 Vac source.

  • Portable pressure gauge standard.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect visually all parts of circuit breaker.

  2. Using SF6 gas leak detector to detect any leakage from piping joints and sealed parts .

Electro-magnetic Valve in the Operating Mech. Box

  1. Clean up the hook and apply a thin coat of turbine oil to it. When the lever is depressed with fingers, the link is disengaged.

Air Valve

  1. Measure the discharged Air (normal discharge 200-400 cc/min at free air), to do that connect a hose to the outlet and use a measuring cylinder to measure the discharge under water .

Auxiliary Switch

  1. Check each part for normal tightening and check the marks of open and closed state.

  2. Check positive contact.

Operating Cylinder

  1. Clean and apply a thin of HITALUBE 280 to the piston rod of .

Air Pressure Gauge

  1. Check the  pressure gauge by  using a standard or calibrating apparatus for calibration.

Pressures Switch

  1. Check the setting pressure by  using a standard gauge.
     
    Setting values as follows:

                a. Alarm and closing lock out ON 13.0 ± o.2 ,OFF 14.0 ± 0.2

                b. Alarm and opening lock out ON 12.5± o.2 ,OFF 13.5 ± 0.2

                c. Reclosing condition ON 15.0 ± o.2 ,OFF 14.7 ± 0.2

 

Anti-Pumping Contactor

  1. Check the contact, Clean and correct them if they are rough  with smooth cut file or very fine sandpaper.

Oil Dashpot

  1.  Remove the plug of the oil dashpot and check the oil level (oil level should be as high as the plug level oil). 

Operating Rod

  1. Check nuts and lock plates for looseness, Check split pins and joint pins of the rod system.

  2. Apply grease to joint pins.

Operating  system

  1. Confirm that the stroke of hinge piece must be 104mm( + 0mm , - 4mm), to do that Prepare the spindle and ratchet spanner delivered with GCB proper. 

  2. Attach a spindle to the operating cylinder and use  a ratchet spanner to measure the stroke.

Test after Checking

  1. Make the Operating Tests (timing, micro ohm test) for the CB.
