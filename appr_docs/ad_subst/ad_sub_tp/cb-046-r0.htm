Introduction

This document outlines the inspection procedure I1, to be performed on a monthly basis M1, these activities are in addition to the daily inspection performed by station operators and would also be performed at this time.  

Preventive maintenance is a cycle of planned inspection, tests, overhauls and servicing activities designed to keep the Network equipment in good operating condition and avoid breakdowns.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •

  • Circuit breaker isolated on both ends and earthing is required.

  • Change remote switch to local position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Gas detector, Multimeter, blower, set of common keys, buzzer, and manometer.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check Supply Voltage AC and DC.

  2. Clean the local cabinet by dry air.

  3. Check gas leek.

  4. Check SF6 gas pressure.

  5. Check oil leak

  6. Check level of oil.

  7. Clean mechanism and switch by dry air.

  8. Check the auxiliary switch functioning by its correct position

  9. Lubrication for all movement connection for hydraulic system such as position indicator

  10. Check the DC voltage for the pump motor.

  11. Check the heater operation in the mechanism.

  12. Check the earthed connection.

  13. Retighten all connection terminals of remote and local cabinet.

  14. Check unusual noise in the system.
