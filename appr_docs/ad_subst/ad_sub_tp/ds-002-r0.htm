Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I1, which must be performed annually as indicated by Y1 in the title block

Safety Precautions 

  • Disconnect switch must be isolated and grounded it.

  • Work permit must be issued.

  • Wear safety equipment, helmet, shoes...... etc.

  • Cautions marks must be hung on workplace.

Tools and Equipment
  • Test equipment level "dip sticks", multimeter, common sets keys, cleaning material "rags, cloth" and screw driver set.

Work to be carried out

  1. Visual inspection for all outside disconnecting switch parts.

  2. Check the supply voltage."22OVdc. & 380 / 22OVa.c."

  3. Check the disconnecting switch operation "hand, motor and observe the unusual operation sound.

  4. Check the socket (plug) terminals and it must be in correct position.

  5. Check for signs of overheating at terminal and retighten if necessary.

  6. Check the earthing connection of the compartment and retighten it if it is necessary.

  7. Check the motion transmission shaft for three phase and retighten all coupling bolts and joints.

  8. Check the motion transmission shaft of motor and retighten the bolts if it required.

  9. Functions operation must be done off/on through local remote and manual and check

  10. Check on/off indicators to ensure it is in correct position.

  11. Clean mechanism box (Use blower & cloth)

  12. Check to ensure there are no locks or pins missing from the motion transmission joints.

  13. Check disconnect mech. box door gasket.

  14. Gear box mechanism of motion transmission must be lubricated with silicon grease SI 3 1.

  15. Check the pressure relief valve for three phase.

  16. Read and record the gas pressure value using portable pressure gauge
    "acceptable value 3.4 bar"

  17. Check switch compartment for gas leakage by gas detector.
