Introduction 

This procedure details the minor maintenance procedure P1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety glasses, .. etc.

  • Wear breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particles ( Before using a blower to clean the control cabinet.

    •

  • CB must be isolated completely and the earthing switches must be connected on both sides of the CB

  • Isolate the DC voltage. (Switch off the control circuit and Insert closing and tripping preventive pins.) 

  • Ensure that the CB grounded on both sides .

  • Replace absorbent whenever enclosures are opened. Replacement should be made immediately before evacuation.

  • Never use clothes or wipers used previously on metal parts to clean insulating parts.

  • All O rings must be replaced and lubricated. ( Discard old O rings )

  • Only use denatured alcohol to clean interior insulators.

    •

Work to be carried out

  1. Check visually all external component of the CB for any abnormal conditions and abnormal noise.

  2. Check any rust at Metallic frame and remedy it.

  3. Check the cabinet heaters.

  4. Check the door packing, replace it if necessary.

  5. Retighten the earth terminal.

  6. Clean the porcelain from dirties and check for cracks.

  7. Check registered number of operation counters and annotate it.
          
    Warning:     don't operate the CB unless it is filled with SF6 gas and the pressure at 5 bar.

  8. Check the settings of gas pressure gauge, refer to gas reference values:
    Normal working pressure    5     bar at 20°C
    Test:  Low pressure alarm  4.5  bar  at 20°C   ( Reset : 4.7 - 5.0  bar )
    Test:  Low pressure lockout 4     bar  at 20°C  ( Reset : 4.2 - 4.5  bar )
       

  9. Calculate SF6 gas leakage per year             £  1  %

  10. Check open operation time                          £  28   ms  every 6 years .

  11. Check close operation time                          £ 100  ms  every 6 years .

  12. Check the reclosing operation trip coil         <  300  ms  every 6 years .

  13. Check spring charging time limit                  £  10  Sec.

  14. Check closing coil resistance                       =  ( 33  ±  5 % ) W  at 22°c

  15. Check opening coil resistance                     =   ( 20  ±  5 % ) W  at 22°c  

  16. Measure contacts resistance.                       £  100  m W ( according to Doc. no. CB-004-r0 )

  17. Check pressure switch test.

  18. Check pressure switch settings.

  19. Calibrate pressure gauge.

  20. Clean and re-lubricate different parts of mechanism.

  21. Replace shock mounting rubber if aged

  22. Inspect and measure ARC contacts erosion.

  23. Inspect Nozzle erosion.

  24. Inspect the absorbent bag.

  25. Check the spring motor.

  26. Check valves position.

  27. Check the auxiliary switches assembly.

  28. Re-torque all loose external hardware.

  29. External visual inspection of energized breaker

  30. Replace contacts or mechanism parts , if necessary , according to:
