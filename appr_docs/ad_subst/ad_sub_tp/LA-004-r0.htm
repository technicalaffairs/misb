Introduction

This document lists the inspection check I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Nashwa Hussein Mohamed, tested by eng. Atef Mohamed Abd El Rahman, accepted by eng. Samir Mohamed El Azab: general director, and approved by EETC Cairo Zone coordinator eng. Ahmed Shelaby Faisal Head Sector of EHV networks.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses,

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the arrester, so you must wear your safety belt when cleaning insulators or checking connections

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required.

Tools and Equipment 

  • Safety helmet, safety belt,

  • SF6 gas leak detector

  • Air blower

Work to be Carried out

  1. Read and record the discharge counter reading if fitted

  2. Check for loose caps and tighten if necessary

  3. Check the SF6 gas leakage

  4. Check the SF6 pressure value it should be 4.6 bar

  5. Check the earthing wire connected and retighten each screw.
