Introduction

This document outlines the equipment tests I1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block. The first draft initiated at High Dam HPS, by eng. Hossain Mahrous, tested at 16 April 2002

The tests will be done after any fault on CT, at capital repair, and at acceptance tests.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, safety belt.

  • Switch off the line 132kv.

  • Separate the primary terminals of winding.

  • Separate the secondary terminals of winding.

  • Switch on the grounds.  

  • Presence the industrial safety supervisor.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, rags, and silicagel

  • Megger: isol 5003, Doble M4000

  • Microhmmeter CA10, Device (ATRT-03)

  • DC deflection voltmeter & d.c battery.

Checking of Winding    use dc deflection voltmeter & DC battery

  1. Polarity of winding are checked by induction method.

  2. Connect the battery on the high winding and voltmeter on low winding

  3. Check the direction of voltmeter.

  4. If the polarity is correct the direction of voltmeter in positive.
