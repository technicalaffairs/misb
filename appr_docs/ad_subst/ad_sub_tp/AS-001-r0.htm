Introduction

This document out lists the inspection check list I1 , to be performed on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng Gamal Sayed Soliman tested at 15 Sep 02, at Assuit 500 ME,

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Air compressor must be isolated.

  • Main air tank must be isolated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the indication of pressure gauge.
  2. Drain the main air tank.
  3. Check the frequancy of the automatic operation of the air compressor unit by checking the air leakage.
  4. Check the oil level of the compressor.
  5. Check the indication of compressor's first stage pressure gauge.
