Introduction 

The purpose of this document is to describe the various inspection /maintenance procedures (General Inspections), which must be performed on weekly W1, monthly M1, and semi annual M6 intervals inspections are included in this document.

The aim of these instructions is to recommend a minimum requirements for checks and inspections in order to maintain the reliability of the fire fighting systems in the transformer yards. The detailed procedures should be determined by the site engineers and / or the persons authorized. All results of checks and inspections should be documented and / or reported in formal sheets as indicated at the end of this document.

The first draft initiated by eng Reda Mohamed Ahmed ElRous at 12 Nov 2002 Assuit 500 substation, Middle Egypt zone

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective safety equipment, i.e. safety shoes and helmet

  • Never perform any work on the transformer before isolating the fire protection system.

  • You must carry portable fire extinguisher when entering the transformer room after the fire.

  • Vent the flooded transformer room before entering it after the fire.

  • Any person inside the transformer room must immediately leave it after hearing the fire alarm. 

  • Any doors leading to the room must be kept closed.

  • To prevent the accidental discharge of the system, do not touch the pilot cylinder weighing device inside the transformer room.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet, Safety Shoes.

 

Work to be carried out

Weekly Check

Mechanical confirmations

  1. Confirm the valving conditions (opening and close) of the water supply line by actually tracing the water lines and comparing with the system diagram (Dwg nos. FW – 86012 - 003 and FW-86012-004).

  2. Confirm the water condition at the level gages provided at each tanks

  3. Confirm the valving condition (opening and close) of the compressed air line by actually tracing the compressed air line and comparing with the system diagram mentioned above

  4. Confirm the value of the pressure of the water line "ref. value 2kg/cm2" & compressed air in the tanks "ref. value 7.5 : 9 kg/cm2" by observing the pressure gages provided per each tanks 

  5. Confirm if the water storage tanks are filled with water

Electrical Confirmation

  1. Check the conditions of the lamps and switches on the fire control panels

  2. Confirm the electricity supply (“POWER SOURCE” lamp lights)

  3. Confirm the power source voltage with the voltmeter (apprx. 380v)

  4. Push the lamp test push button “PB-L” and make sure of the availabilities of all lamps on the  panels (all lamps will light)

  5. Confirm that all line break lamps and system fault lamp (s) do not light (orange lamps – OFF)

  6. Under normal condition, confirm that all fire alarm lamps and water discharge lamps are not lit (red lamps – OFF)

  7.  Confirm that the high level lamp (for water storage tank filling condition) lights, and the low level is unlit.

  8. In case that the low level lamp is lighted, fill the tank through the water supply valve by using the water wagon until the high level lamp is lit

  9. The select switch “COS” is normally set to “AUTO” position, except when manual operation at control panel is adopted.

  10. Confirm that the AUTO lamp is lit (WL – 2 ON)

  11. Confirm that the fault lamps for water pumps and air compressors are not lighted (orange lamps for pumps and compressors – OFF)

  12. When no compressor nor pump is under operation, confirm that all green lamps (for pumps and compressors) are lighted and all red lamps are unlit

  13. Confirm that the red lamps for compressor and / or pump will be lighted, when operated.

    Note: These pumps and air compressors are designed automatic operation, that will automatically start up when the water level and the pressure in the pressure tank decrease to – predetermined values, and will stop when the level and pressure reach the predetermined values, automatically.
    The operating pump (NO. 1 or NO. 2 ) and the operating air compressor (N. 1 or NO. 2)
    can be alternated by switching the selector switches for pumps and for air compressors.

  14. Confirm if the lamps on all local manual starting boxes are normally lighted.

Monthly Check

Air Line

  1. Visually check the air lanes and the associated valves for any cracks, un-sufficient interference to the lines. Remove and / or repair the defective parts, if any

  2. Confirm the correct setting of the valves on the air lines. (Normal open, Normal closed)

  3. Confirm the adequate pressure of the air line

Water Pipe Lines

  1. Visually check the water line, the associated valves for any cracks, water leakage or unsufficient interference to the line.

  2. Remove and / or repair the defect, if any.

  3. Confirm the correct setting of the valves on the water lines.   (Normal open, Normal closed)

  4. Confirm the sufficient pressure of the water line

Electrical Equipment

  1. Visually check all electrical equipment and the accessories for any harmful defect, breakage and for insufficient interference against the equipment, their operating parts and accessories.

  2. Remove and / or repair all defects and obstruction, if any.

  3. Confirm the tightness of the wiring connecting conditions at all wiring terminals in the control panels, local manual starting boxes, terminal board boxes and junction boxes, refasten all loosing parts

  4. Check the installation conditions of all electrical equipment; refasten all loosened parts, if any

Minor Instruments

  1. Visually check and confirm that all indicating gage ( pressure gages, level gages ) and minor switches ( pressure switches, level switches ) are in good conditions without any defects such as cracks, deformation or error indication ect … Release and / or replace the defective parts.

  2. Confirm that all manual valves and safety vavles are able to be handled smoothly

Semi-Annual Check

Water Supply Pumps

  1. Confirm the sufficient of the water supply pumps by isolating the inlet valve of the pressure  tank, opening the test valve (1”) at the pumps outlet, upstream of the orifice.

  2. Then, turn the selection switch “AUTO –OFF-MANUAL” to “MANUAL” Position, make sure that the pumps are operating smoothly without any abnormal symtoms (i.e abnormal sounds, abnormal temperature raising etc ...), the pumps can be test alternately by switching the selection switch “No.1 – No.2” for pumps. (on the front of the control pannel)

  3. Repair and / or release all abnormality, if any

  4. After confirming of the sufficient operations of the pumps, restore the water line as scheduled and turn the switches to normal positions for ready automatic operation 

  5. For further check and inspections, refer to the instructions of individual instructions per each equipment, if any, and the submitted “Inspection Procedure”
