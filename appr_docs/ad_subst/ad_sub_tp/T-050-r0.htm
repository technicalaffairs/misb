Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I1, which must be performed every six years as indicated by Y6 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Aziz El Bagoury, tested by eng. Doaa Anis, revised by quality control eng. Adel Abdel Qader El Nagar at EDEPC, accepted by general director eng. Mahmoud Saker and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talkha PS

Safety Precaution 

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding on all its sides is required.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes,

  • There is a danger of falling into the oil containment pit when working near large power transformers.

  • There is a danger of  falling off from the top of transformer

  • Wear safety belt when cleaning Insulators.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and tool set

  • Blower, Digital voltmeter, Mugger devices..

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes,

Work to be Carried out

  1. Check for signs of oil leaks.

  2. Check oil levels in tank conservator.

  3. Check oil temperature and winding temperature indicators

  4. Make a visual inspection of all cooling fans

  5. Remove  any debris from fan screens.

  6. Switch on cooling fans manually and listen for abnormal noise.

  7. Inspect  the Transformer control cabinet check the condition of the door seal blow out the inside of the control cabinet with compressed air using moderate pressure.  

  8. Perform the same inspection as in step 7 on the control cabinet of the tap changer.

  9. Check the condition of the silica gel breathe.

  10. Check for correct level of sealing oil in lower glass cup

  11. Check for correct oil level in all high voltage bushings.

  12. Clean all porcelain surfaces of all bushings.

  13. Check break down voltage of insulating oil .

  14. All bolted connections must be checked for tightness .

  15. Use water pressure to clean radiators and transformer tank .

  16. Clean neutral CT,  earthing switches , check that all bolted connections are tight .

  17. Clean contacts of all neutral disconnect switches . greasing all bearing points

  18. Open bleed valve on buchholz relay until a small  amount of oil Escapes  test alarm and trip circuit using the test button next to the vent valve , check all electrical connections for tightness .

  19. Take oil samples for laboratory analysis and gas analysis  for power transformer

  20. Check all protection devices by protection group  .

  21. Take  and record insulation  resistance reading  

  22. Check tan delta ( capacitance and  dissipation factor ) .

  23. Take and record winding resistance reading .

  24. Check turns ratio test .

  25. Leakage current monitor test for lightening arrestor 

  26. Check the pressure relief device .

  27. Check oil temperature gauges indicator.

  28. Check oil level gauges indicator.

  29. Check winding temperature indicator

  30. Be sure that all tools have been taken off the top of the Trans former.

  31. Place transformer bank into normal operation condition
