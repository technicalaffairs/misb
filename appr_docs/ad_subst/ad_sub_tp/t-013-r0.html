Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I1, which must be performed monthly as indicated by M1 in the title block.

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounded the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators.

  • Isolate the auxiliary transformer,

  • Taking out the breaker toward 11KV out of busbar 11KV.

  • Disconnect supply of D.C.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, and tool set

  • Voltage indicator, Portable earth for discharging,

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes, Silicagel,

  • Complete tool kit/set, including a torque wrench.

Environmental Recommendation

  • Before starting the cleaning of all porcelain surfaces of all transformer wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning. Collect it and send it to the dump.

  • Fix any oil leaks to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil.

Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impact on the environment, mainly on soil and ground water and eventually on the ecosystem. "It takes only I liter of Hydrocarbon spilled in an aquifer to render 1,000,000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption".

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Clean all porcelain surfaces.

  2. Inspect all painted surface, remove any signs of rust and repaint bare spots.

  3. Check for signs of oil leaks.

  4. Check oil level in tank and conservator .

  5. Check the condition of the silicagel, replace silicagel if it’s color changed from blue to pink.

  6. Check the condition of earthing.

  7. All bolted connections must be checked for tightness.

  8. Clean the body, cables and the room of auxiliary transformer.
