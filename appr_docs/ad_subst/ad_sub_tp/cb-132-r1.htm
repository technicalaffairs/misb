Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every three years or 500 operations as indicated by Y3 in the above title block.

  2. Insert the closing spring lock pin (76) to the hole of each closing spring guide (77) (see Figure 10,11)

Measurement of Insulation Resistance

  1. Check the insulation resistance between in-phase terminals.

  2. Wipe the insulator surface by wet cloth.

  3. When excessively dirty, apply insulator cleanser to the surface.

  4. Confirm that insulation resistance satisfies the following table.
