Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block The first draft initiated by eng. Emad Farouk Moustafa at El-Katamia substation Cairo Network

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety belt when cleaning column of the arrester.

  • The arrester is to be disconnected and put to earth.
  • Local earth must be used.
    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of common keys, cleaning clothes,

  • Blower, and PVC ladder.

  • safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety belt when cleaning suspension Insulator

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the discharge counters

  2. The number of operation is to be read off and recorded regulorly

  3. Clean the insulators of the arrester (for arresters installed in polluted areas like kattamia) we recomended cleaning of the insu lators from time to time

  4. Check that the control ring is in central position

  5. It is to be noted that the complete arrester unit must be evenly and gradually cleaned

  6. Check the three units of the arrester, the bottom-the center, and the top unit for any cracke in insulators

  7. Check the earthing wire connected and retight each screw

  8. Check for any loosen screws on the cover or any place, and it must be retightened each loosen screws.

  9. The arresters are to be connected in the shortest possible way to the system earth, at any rate never to a separate earth

  10. If we want spraying the arrester under voltage, special precautions have to be taken.

  11. Proper earthing of the jet

  12. The water must be sprinkled in a way so as to avoide concentric jet of water.

  13. An earthed wire - mesh screen between the jet and the arrester increased the safety

  14. The water used must be clean and non - conduction

  15. Spraying the dirt off in parts is to be avoided as this can lead to flash - over of the cleaner section

  16. The fill voltage is then across the cleaner section resulting in flash - over and an eventual destruction of the arrester unit.
