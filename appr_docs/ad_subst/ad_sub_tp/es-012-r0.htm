Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P2, to be performed after 5000 switching operations as indicated by 5000 in the above title block 

The first draft initiated byeng. Emad Farouk Moustafa at Katamia  substation Cairo Zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with cautions marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet & shoes .... etc

  • Change interlock to maintenance position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, blower, cleaning cloths

  • Multimeter, set of common keys

  • Loctite, grease, screw drivers

Works to be Carried Out

Dismantling

  1. RemoveSF6 gas from the chamber of the earthing switch

  2. Detach the connecting tube31992 from rotary lever 31916

  3. Release the screws joining the earthing switch to the main item of switchgear and left of the earthing switch

  4. Adjust the tubular contact 31928 to its central position

  5. Unscrew the bracket 31934 and remove from the enclosure of the earthing switch, the toggle is thereby freed

  6. Dismantle the operating shaft 31931

  7. Release the screen 31942, unscrew the contact socket31941 and remove from the earthing switch 31928.

  8. Dismantle the sliding block 31928

  9. Clean and lubricate the parts dismantle or to be replaced

  10. Cover the openings in the enclosure with anti static plastic foil

  11. The following parts should be cleaned and their sliding surfaces marked lubricated:
        - Inside of the earthing switch 31927
        - Contact socket 31941
        - Sliding block 31930
        - Screen 31942, after cleaning only touch with clean gloves

  12. Re grease the new tublar contact 31982

  13. Renew the gaskets 31946, 31947, 31948

  14. Only wip dry so that the coating of talcum powder  is not removed

  15. Grease the contact fingers of the fixed earthing contact

  16. Grease the lower threads and apply Loctite only to the upper thread.

Re Assembly

  1. The earthing switch can only be re assembled in the position off O.

  2. Insert a new gasket 31947.

  3. Center the earthing switch in the flange of the main item of switchgear.

  4. Secure the flange screws and tighten up.

  5. Make vacuum for the chamber of earthing switch up to  (-1bar) for 3hours

  6. Refill the chamber of earthing switch with SF6  up to 5 bar

  7. Measure and record SF6 %  (at rated 99%)

  8. Measure Acidity and dew point (max 50 PPM) for the SF6 gas.

  9. Check the mechanical function of the earthing switch by closing and opening several times.

  10. Put earthing switch in service.
