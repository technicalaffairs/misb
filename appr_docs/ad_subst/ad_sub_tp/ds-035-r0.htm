Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1, which must be carried out every 3 months as indicated by (M3) at the above title block. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 4/5/02 for Saker Qoraish Circuit no. 1 at First Urban Community 66kv Substation, Cairo zone

Safety Precautions 

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable safety category.

    •

  • Safety rope with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used,

  • Wear your personnel safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, and safety harness,

  • Switch on the earthing switch and check mechanical interlocking with the disconnector

  • Switch off  DC source

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower for cleaning purposes, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Also wear your breathing mask if you have to use any chemical solvent for cleaning purposes because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipments

  • Suitable tool set, safety helmet,

  • Safety shoes, breathing mask, and safety harness

  • Pieces of clothes and rags

  • Grease and cleaning liquid (like gasoline)

Work to be carried out 

  1. Clean contact pieces and contact fingers of disconnectors from dust and dirt

  2. Remove old grease and put new (choose suitable grease such as tremalene grease 64444 or non - acid Vaseline as manufacturer recommends)

  3. Clean contacts of earthing switch and remove old grease and put new

  4. Check disconnector joints

  5. Check the earthing switch joints

  6. Check flexible connection of earthing switches

  7. Check all screwed joints for tight fit

  8. Clean insulators from dirt and dust

  9. Clean auxiliary instruments (contactors, relays, limit switch) with video cleaner
