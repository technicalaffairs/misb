Introduction  

This document lists the inspection checks I1, which must be performed on weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves

    •

  • Don't smoke in the battery room.

  • Don't place any tools on top of battery cells (Danger of short circuit).

  • Never use the same tools on alkaline cells, you may have used on acid cells.

  • All tools will be used must be insulated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard screwdriver set, brushes, distilled water, rags.

  • Safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Clean dust of battery.

  2. Check that connection are tight and clean any salt deposits with distilled water.

  3. Check for the correct position of all vent covers.

  4. Check the electrolyte level and add distilled water if necessary.

  5. Check the ventilation of the battery room to ensure their correct operation.

  6. Clean battery room floor with water.
