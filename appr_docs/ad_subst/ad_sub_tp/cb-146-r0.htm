Introduction

This document outlines the checklist I1, to be performed every four years as indicated by Y4 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ibrahem Ibrahem Shehata, tested by eng. Saber Abed Elkader at 05 Jan 2003 on Bustie coupler Sues Cement 220kv substation, and revised by quality control eng. Mohamed Omran, accepted by general director eng. Sohair Nageb Zaki and approved by eng. Farg Aly El-Azab Head Sector of West Canal Network, Canal Zone, ETEC.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes safety harness

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, Safety shoes

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visually check SF6 pressure and, if the values don't tally, test the pressure gauges and check the breaker for leaks.

  2. Read and record the counter reading.

  3. Examine paint finish and touch up damaged sections.

  4. Check the oil level.

  5. Examine all pipe joints for correct sealing.

  6. Measure the provisional nitrogen pressure on the oil side.

  7. Carry out the test operations.

  8. Tighten all terminals.
