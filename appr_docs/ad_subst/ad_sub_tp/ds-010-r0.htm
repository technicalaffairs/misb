Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I2, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated in the above title block by Y1. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 2/8/02 for El-Matar circuit no. 2 at Sheraton El Matar 66kv Substation, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance st off should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety rope with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used
  • Wear your safety helmet and safety harnesses
  • Switch on the earthing switch and check mechanical interlocking with the disconnect switch
  • Isolation and proper earthing is required.
  • Switch off the DC source

Environmental Precautions

  • Wear your breathing mask if you have to use gasoline because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't through oil or grease on ground used, collect it and send to the central chemical labs to recycle it.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety helmet, grease, suitable tool set

  • Pieces of cloths and rags

  • Cleaning liquid (like gasoline)

Work to be carried out 

  1. Clean porcelain surface from dust, dirties, or any deposits ....... etc.

  2. Inspect well the insulators and check if there is unseen crack, current traces, or alike

  3. Remove the old grease out of the blades and contacts of disconnect switch and clean them with gasoline then put a new thin film of grease

  4. Tighten bolts and nuts of disconnect switch

  5. Inspect the mechanical interlocking between the earthing switch and disconnect switch

  6. Clean the gear box and then grease it

  7. Check disconnect switch motor - drives, clean it.

  8. Clean the blade of earthing switch (that engages with disconnect switch)

  9. Check the earthing switch motor, clean and grease the gear box

  10. Clean the blade of the earthing switch and softly grease it

  11. Inspect the marshalling box of the disconnect switch, remove dust, then clean auxiliary switch limit switch, push buttons, etc ... witch video cleaner

  12. Inspect the gasket of marshalling box and check if the box is closed tightly to prevent dust from going into the box

  13. Grease the horizontal axis of Disconnect switch

  14. Inspect the operating arms and its hinges, and grease the hinges

  15. Inspect the marshalling box of the earthing switch, remove dust, then clean auxiliary switch, limit switch, push buttons, etc... with video cleaner
