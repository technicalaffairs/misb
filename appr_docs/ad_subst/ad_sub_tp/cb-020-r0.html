Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks I1, which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes …. etc

    •

  • CB must be isolated and proper earthing at the two sides of CB area is required.

  • Isolate control circuit (DC voltage).

Tools and Equipment

  • Hyd. Test gauge, SF6 test gauge, blower, dew-point equipment, acidity equipment air content equipment timing equipment and contact resistance equipment

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check oil level.

  2. Check tightness of hydraulic pipes to ensure there is no oil leak.

  3. Check tightness of hydraulic components.

  4. Check internal tightness of hydraulic components.

  5. Check there is no condensation in the cabinets and you have to ensure that the heater is operating.

  6. Check the condition of the Electrical connections.

  7. Ensure the SF6 filling pressure of the CB compartments (6 bar)

  8. Check the values of operation setting for the thresholds of hydraulic drive using portable pressure gauge.
    - Normal values of hydraulic pressure=336 bar,
    - Pump will operate at 317 bar,
    - Blocking for closing at 270 bar,
    - Blocking for on/off at 250 bar.

  9. Ensure Pre-charge pressure of accumulators at 180 bar.

  10. Check oil purity by sending it to control chemical lab.
