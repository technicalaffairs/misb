Introduction  
 

This procedure details the Equipment Inspection activities I1, which are performed  month as indicated by the M1 in the title block.
 

Safety Precautions 

  • Work permit must be issued,

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Change remote/local control switch to local position.

Tools and Equipment 

  • Multimeter, blower, gas detector, buzzer, screw driver and set of common keys.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check supply voltage. "220 vdc and 220/380 vac

  2. Check SF6 pressure and air pressure manometers

  3. Check for air and gas leaks.

  4. Clean mechanism auxiliary switch by dry air (use blower).

  5. Check the aux. switch functioning by its correct position through position indication and

  6. Check mechanical position indicators and observe the three poles

  7. Clean the local cabinet by dry air (use blower).

  8. Check the heater of local cabinet

  9. Retighten all connection terminals of remote, and local cabinet

  10. Drain off moisture from the air reservoir tank by draining a small amount of compressed air

  11. Check the earthed connections

  12. Check the unusual noise in the GIS Compartment
