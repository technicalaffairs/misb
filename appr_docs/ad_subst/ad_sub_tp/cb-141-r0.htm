Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Mahmoud Mohamed Tawfeq at Estad 220kv substation, tested at 21 Jan 2004, accepted by general director eng. Moustafa Mohamed Hamed Shaker, and approved by eng. Samir Saleh Ismaiel; Head Sector of extra high voltage Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes .... etc

    •

  • Circuit breaker must be isolated & proper earthing at the two sides of CB area is required

  • Isolate control circuit (DC voltage)

  • Change remote/local switch m to local position.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet & safety shoes

  • Portable air blower, SF6 gas detector, buzzer,

  • Screw driver, and set of common keys.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the oil level in the hydraulic cabinet.

  2. Check tightness of hydraulic pipes to ensure there is no oil leak..

  3. Check tightness of hydraulic pipes.

  4. Check internal tightness of hydraulic components.

  5. Check oil purity.

  6. Replace the protective mastic beads, Repaint the CB.

  7. Clean the cabinet from any oil or dust

  8. Check there is no condensation in the cabinets & you have to ensure that the heater is operating.

  9. Check the flexible earthing wire.

  10. Check the tightness of earthing conductors.

Verification

  1. Measure the SF6 filling pressure of the compartments by untied the cover of the SF6 valve then connect the calibrated pressure gauge to the SF6 valve to make sure that the reading is 7 bar.

  2. Check oil purity.

  3. Replace the protective mastic beads.
