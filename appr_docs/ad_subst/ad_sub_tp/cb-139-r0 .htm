Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P2, which must be performed every ten years or after 3000 operations as indicated by Y10 is the above title block.

The first draft was initiated at El Moatamadeia  220kv Substation at 25 Feb. 2003 Cairo Zone. 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear your safety shoes and helmet.

    •

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides i.e. earthling switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker to censure it is properly grounded.

  • Change remote switch to local position.

  • Hydraulic system without pressure.

  • Interrupt the control circuit DC volt.

  • Gas compartments need not to be opened.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety shoes, helmet, and dry wiper

  • Complete tool set and air blower 

  • Gas detector 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Read the operation counter.

  2. Check the SF6 filling.

  3. Check for any gas leaks using gas detector.

  4. Check the tightness of the hydraulic system.

  5. Check the corrosion on the hydraulic storage cylinder.

  6. Check of lever for closing and opening solenoid for auxiliary switch box

  7. Check the level of oil and refill if necessary

  8. Replace rubber seals on covers of oil tank and valve unit

  9. Check the priming pressure of nitrogen

  10. Check the opening and closing pressure of the safety valve

  11. Check the inner sealing of the hydraulic system in both switching states

  12. Check of the operating values of the SF6 density monitor

  13. Vent the hydraulic system and raise the pressure to the nominal level.

  14. Check of the operating values of the oil pressure switch.

  15. Measure all gas tests. (dew point, SF6 contents, ...etc)

  16. Check the lock out relay of CB

  17. Check the timing and contact resistance of CB

  18. Check the paint finish of CB for damage
