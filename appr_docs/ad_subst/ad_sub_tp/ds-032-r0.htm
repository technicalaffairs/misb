Introduction 

This document lists the major maintenance procedure P2, which must be performed after 5000 mechanical operation. The first draft initiated at Katamia substation, Cairo Zone by Eng. Ayman Mahmoud Awad, tested at 3 July 2002

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective safety equipment, i.e. safety shoes, overall, and helmet

  • The Angle Isolator must be insulated.
  • Disconnect from the high voltage supply.
  • Earth both side of the Angle isolator.
  • Change selector switch  to local position.
Tools and Equipment

  • All special tools shown in figures 3. 4

  • Set key's, cleaning solution, piece cloth, vacuum cleaner, torque spring key and electrical grease.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check for wear.

  2. The O rings and seals must be renewed.

  3. The remaining parts are to be renewed when this becomes necessary only.

  4. Dismantling is always carried out with the isolator open and in the following order seem from the operating side fig 2

  5. Set the safety lock 25682 to overhauls.

  6. Remove the operating mechanism cover 25681 and after undoing  the screws 25685 take off the mechanism.

  7. Detach the operating mechanism cover 25650 from the enclosure of the isolator.

  8. Remove the insulating shaft 25625.

  9. After undoing the screw 25607  .take off the long screen 25606.

  10. unscrew the short screen 25604 with the aid of the extension 02038 by real sing screws  25605 and place on a suitable support (rubber sheet) on the bottom of the enclosure.

  11. Support the intermediate section 25638 on the enclosure (flange) by means of the device 02032.

  12. Release the tulip attachment 25637 from the tubular contact carrier by means of the special ratchet 02040 release screws 25637. and with the tulip contact in the tubular conductor push up to the stop.

  13. Remove the screw from the fixing of the tubular contact 25615.

  14.  Screw the holding device 02034 to the fixing of the tubular contact and enclosure flange with screws 25603 .

  15. Detach the tubular contact carrier  25618 from the base carrier (undoing screws 25641 by means of the special ratchet and then removing them)  then remove the screwed joint 25603 between the holding device and the enclosure flange.

  16. Push the tubular contact carrier, including the holding device out of its centering and remove altogether then the short screen can be taken out.

  17. Release the fixed contact 25660 with box spanner 02036 and remove

  18. Clean and lubricating parts

  19. Reassembly the parts in correct position.

  20. Refill the isolator according to condition of SF6.

  21. Check the isolator (open-close)

  22. Make contact test and scheduled the result.
