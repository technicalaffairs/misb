Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P2, which must be performed every six years as indicated by Y6 in the above title block or/and as shown in the table below:

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes safety harness

    • ... etc

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety harness

    • ... etc

  • Gas detector, Air blower.

  • Grease, paint, thinner, brush

  • Lifting adapter, closing spring charging handle, draw out handle, closing spring lock pin.

Evacuation and gas filling

For evacuation and gas filling, order to the nearest Hitachi sales office, but only user who prepares for gas filling unit supplied by Hitachi ltd, may carry out according to the following procedure under severe working management

The typical piping system for evacuation and gas filling is shown in figure 8.

  1. Remove the front cover and remove the cover of stop valve (81) (see figure 9)

  2. Connect the gas supplying adaptor (82) to body of stop valve (83) when high pressure SF6 is filled in the grounded tanks (20) start following procedure after exhausting if according to step 2,3 in the above procedures, and confirm the pressure switch (25) operates between 3.0 and 3.2 kg/cm2

  3. Turn the nut (84) clockwise to the red line of this adaptor (82)

  4. Close the stop valve (85),(86) and the stop valve of cylinder (87), and open the stop valve (88), then operate vacuum pump (89)
    After reduced the pressure of grounded tank (20) to 1mm hg, keep operating the vacuum pump (89) still more 1 hour

  5. Close the stop valve (88) and release the leak valve of the vacuum pump (89), then stop the operation of the vacuum pump (89)

  6. Decide replenishing pressure referring to the SF6 gas pressure-temperature characteristic curve as shown in fig 10

  7. Open the stop valve of cylinder (87) and stop valve (85) SF6 gas is filling to the piping of the breaker

  8. During gas filling, confirm switching of the pressure switch (25) between 3.84.2kg/cm2
    When replenishing pressure is lower than switching pressure of the pressure switch (25), fill SF6 gas to switching
    pressure, confirm its switching operation, and exhaust SF6 gas to replenishing pressure.
