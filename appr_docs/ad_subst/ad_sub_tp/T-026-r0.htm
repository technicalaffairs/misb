Introduction         

This document lists the equipment Testing Procedure checklist, which must be performed for Power Transformers. According to each manufacturer Instructions.

Safety Precautions

  • Isolate the transformer from service and earthed

  • A word order permit must be issued.

  • The qualified test staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Disassemble HV and LV leads of the TRA

  • All bushing must be clean otherwise errors reading could be happened

  • Don’t touch test leads during test, take care of the applied voltage 5kv dc

Tools and Equipment

  • Test equipment type BM11 D
    AVO - International, 4651 Single Westmorland TX 75237 - 1017 USA

Work to be Carried Out

  1. For all tests make short circuit onto the 3 bushings of HV side and LV side

  2. Be sure that the bushing connections are well cleaned before you apply the megger test cables

Test # 1    HV -------- LV + E

  1. Connect the S.C. LV bushing with the earth point of the TR

  2. Apply the + ve probe of megger to the S.C. HV bushing

  3. Apply the - ve probe of megger to the S.C. LV bushing

  4. Turn on megger and record the reading after 15 sec.(R15)and record anther reading after 60 sec. (R60)

  5. Turn off megger and calculate the ratio.(R60/R15)

 

Test # 2      LV -------- HV + E

  1. Connect the S.C. HV bushing with the earth point “E“ of the TR

  2. Apply the + ve probe of megger to the S.C. LV bushing

  3. Apply the - ve probe of megger to the S.C. HV bushing

  4. Turn on megger and record the reading after 15 sec.(R15) and record anther reading after 60 sec. (R60)

  5. Turn off megger and calculate the ratio.(R60/R15)

Test # 3      HV + LV ------------ E

  1. Connect the S.C HV bushing with the S.C.. LV bushing

  2. Apply the + ve probe of megger to the S.C. LV& HV bushing

  3. Apply the – ve probe of megger to the earth point “E“

  4. Turn on megger and record the reading after 15 sec.(R15) and 

  5. Recocrd anther reading after 60 sec. (R60)

Test # 4       HV -------- LV

  1. Remove any earth.

  2. Apply the + ve probe of megger to the S.C. HV bushing

  3. Apply the - ve probe of megger to the S.C. LV bushing

  4. Turn on megger and record the reading after 15 sec.(R15)
    and record anther reading after 60 sec. (R60)

  5. Turn off megger and calculate the ratio.(R60/R15)
