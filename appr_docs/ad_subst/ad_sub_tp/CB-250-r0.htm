Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be carried out every two years as indicated by Y2 in the title block.

The first draft initiated and tested by eng. Sahar Aly Rashwan at Ahliat Abu Zaabal SS, accepted by eng. Maher Ibrahem Farag general director, approved by eng. Ahmed Shelaby Faisal EHV North Network Head Sector, Cairo zone MPIS Coordinator control, and certified by eng. Mohamed Amin; head sector of substations 66/11

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc.

  • Isolate the breaker and ground it.

  • Proper well fixed ladder should be used for climbing on the breaker.

  • Isolate the DC voltage.

  • Change the operation position from remote to local.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, and Breathing mask ... etc.

  • Complete tool set, rag, air blower.

  • SF6 gas detector, dew point, air content and acidity equipment.

  • Contact resistance equipment.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean panel exterior, bushings bus bars, and connectors for dust and dirt.

  2. Check control wiring for loose connections and any signs of overheating and tighten all connections as required.

  3. Check all the bolts of the main connectors at the circuit breaker terminals for looseness.

  4. Tighten all loose bolts of the connection as required make note of any signs of overheating.

  5. Check and tighten all ground connections as required repair any damaged ground connections.

  6. Check control devices such as relays limit switches .... etc in control panel for proper operation, repair or replace faulty devices check hydraulic system for proper operation including motor, motor starter and control circuit, replace blown fuses with the same type and rating.

  7. Check the operation mechanism of the circuit breaker, inspect for loose bolts must or linkage (tighten or replace parts as required).

  8. Check gas dew point according to MPIS document no. TD-SS-004-r0.

  9. Measure break and make time using time equipment.

  10. Measure contact resistance according to MPIS document no. CB-004-r0.

  11. Measure earth resistance of circuit breaker.

  12. Check air content acidity content of SF6 gas according to MPIS document no TD-SS-004-r0

  13. Check for gas leak of the circuit breaker the gas gauge.

  14. Check the circuit breaker indications and correct its position if it is necessary.

  15. Check the close springs charged indicator.

  16. Check the trip and close springs visually make note of any remarks.

  17. Through the operators check the circuit breaker functions from remote and local.

  18. Check the door gasket.

  19. Check the operation counter of circuit breaker.

  20. Check closing spring recharge time.

  21. Check the condition and setting of operating mechanism.

  22. Check the leakage of SF6 by halogen leak detector around the crank case core and poles of circuit breaker
