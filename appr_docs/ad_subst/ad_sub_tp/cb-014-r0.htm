Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection as a first level of inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses, use only fiberglass ladders,

  • Circuit breaker must be isolated and grounded it.

Environmental Recommendation 

  • Before using a blower to clean the outside compartment of the circuit breaker from dust, wear your breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particulate.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set, SF6 gas detector, Blower and rags.

Work to be carried out 

  1. Visual inspections for all parts of circuit breaker.
  2. Check AC supply source.
  3. Check DC supply source.
  4. Record on/off operations counter and compare with the previous month.
  5. Record the oil pump operations counter.
  6. Earth connections must be checked.
  7. Clean all outside compartment of circuit breaker from dust, oil and other things using blower and rags.
  8. Retighten all bolts and earth connection parts if necessary.
  9. Visual inspections of foundation and fixation of circuit breaker.
  10. Complete cleaning for mechanism parts.
  11. Check oil level of main tanks for three phases.
  12. Check oil leaks for all connections.
  13. Check the heater operation.
  14. Check door gaskets for all panels.
