Introduction
    This document lists the inspection checks I1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.
Safety Precautions
  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required. 
  • A work permit must be issued. 
  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes etc. 
  • Change remote/local control switch to local position.
Tools and Equipment
  • Multimeter, blower, gas detector, buzzer, screwdriver and set of common keys.
Work to be carried out

  1. Checks supply voltage. 

  2. Check number of switching operations, through the counter. 

  3. Check screw joints of the electric connections by pulling, retighten if necessary. 

  4. Check pull-relief's at cables 

  5. Check mechanical connections at drive or auxiliary switch by visual control for tightness and wear. 

  6. Check mechanical position indicator. 

  7. Clean protective pipe of the position indicator by means of a cleansing a gent containing soap. 

  8. Grease threaded spindle and pain bearing of auxiliary switch (27) 

  9. Check functioning of torque clutch. rated value of slip friction torue Max 10 Nm, min. T Nm 

  10. Check density of shaft seap in housing cover (2) by sniffing, 

  11. Check functioning of auxiliary- switch contacts. 

  12. Check functioning of heating resistor. 

  13. Check contact penetration depth rated valve. Max. 138 mm/min.146 mm/min 134 mm. Until touching/separation of contacts stroke of switch rod 185+1-2 mm. Using Dople Test Equipment. 

  14. Check wear of annular contacts and switch rod 1) 2) 

  15. Check threaded spindle (10) with universal joints for damages and grease if necessary 1) 2) 

  16. Check slides rod (11) for damages; clean and grease. 

  17. Execute test switching operations 

  18. Check electrical position signals (position indicator) 

  19. Check running time of drive. Compare with reference valves from commissioning

Note:
1- Requires opening of the gas compartment. 

2- Random test at equipment having undergone highest load.
