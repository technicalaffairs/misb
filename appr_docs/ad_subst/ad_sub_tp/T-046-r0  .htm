Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I3, which must be carried out  on an annually bases as indicated as indicated by Y1 in the above title block. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 18 Sep 2002 on T1 at First Urban Community 66kv Substation, Cairo zone

Safety Precaution 

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety robe and precaution marks must be used,

  • Wear safety helmet, safety show, and safety belt

  • Switch off transformer from tow sides

  • Putting portable earthing is necessary

  • Switch off DC source.

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower for cleaning purposes, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Wear your breathing mask if you have to use any chemical solvent because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't through oil or grease on ground used, collect it and send to the central chemical labs to recycle it.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

  • Measure the Noise Level and Vibration for the cooling fan. Be sure that it does not exceed the standard levels (40-60 dB in industrial workshop). Fix any problem to prevent the noise pollution. 'For any question refer back to the manufacturer.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool sets

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower

  • Brush, Pieces of soft cloths

  • Suitable grease

Work to be carried out

  1. Repeat steps of 3- Month inspection according to MPIS document No. T-045-r0

  2. Inspect external surface of transformer, possible rust damages are removed, use primer and finish-paints

  3. Inspect joints, retighten 

  4. Check oil seals, treat any oil leakage if exist

  5. Inspect bushings, and check if there are unseen cracks in porcelain, current traces, and oil leakage

  6. Check oil level in bushings

  7. Retighten bushing belts and screws

  8. Inspect the arc - horn, retighten its tow arms, and check distance between the tow parts

  9. Check fan functions, clean operating box, clean contactors with video cleaner, also inspect and clean heater, inspect also the gasket

  10. Lubricate fan motors bearings

  11. Check oil dielectric strength

  12. Check oil moisture content

  13. Check chemical analysis in insulation oil

  14. Check gas analysis in insulation oil

  15. Check the dielectric loss tangent (tg Õ) for winding insulation

  16. Check D.C. resistance at all tap-changer steps

  17. Check transformation - ratio at all steps for the three phases

  18. Check R 60 and R 15 for winding

  19. Inspect control cabinet of tap - changer to discover eventual damages

  20. Inspect auxiliary instruments (relays, contactors, limit switch, etc ...) in operating box of tap-changer and clean them with video cleaner

  21. Inspect the condition of grease in the power transmission box for tap-changer, add new grease if necessary

  22. Check movement of arms engaged with power transmission box for tap-changer

  23. Check steps of tap-changer by changing steps from step 1 to step 17 and vise-versa, this helps also for cleaning contacts

  24. Check the heater and clean vents and filters in tap-changer motor-dive mechanism cubicle

  25. Check the interlocking contact in control circuit to prevent electrical operation during manual operation

  26. Check mechanical end stops

  27. Read the counting device to determine when overhaul is due

  28. Inspect and clean the auxiliary current transformer for winding temperature indicator (it is at the S  phase bushing at 22 kv side)

  29. Check buchhulz relay float and contacts action and check the SIGNAL and TRIP function of the relay by pressing on the push-button (which occurred on the cover of buchholz relay and used for mechanical test of the circuit), firstly pusf gently on the push - button until signal happen, then continue pushing until tripping of transformer circuit breaker at the tow sides

  30. Inspect the contacts of the thermometer for oil temperature

  31. Check function of winding temperature indicator

  32. Manually actuate oil temperature thermometer contacts to check if it gives signals, trips or not

  33. Manually actuate the contacts of thermometer of ventilation system temperature control

  34. Inspect thermometer pockets, the presence of required quantity of oil in pockets

  35. Check correct installation of active elements of thermometers

  36. Inspect the earthing resistance, clean its components, clean and grease its contacts, retighten bolts and screws

  37. Inspect and clean the current transformer and lightning arresater of earthing resistance switch    

  38. Inspect the earthing system

  39. Inspect silica-gel in the breather, change it if necessary

  40. Inspect oil in breather, quantity and condition, it is preferable to change it

  41. Provision should be made for pressure relief device, manually actuate the device by movement of valve disk and then reset it manually by its arm, also proper clearance of the operating indicator should be done.
