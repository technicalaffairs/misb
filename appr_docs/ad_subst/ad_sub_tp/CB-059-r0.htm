Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection, which must be performed on an annually bases bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

This checklist for the operating mechanism of the Power/Vac vacuum circuit breaker type: VB 13.8-1000, rated voltage 11.5kv, maximum voltage 15kv, nominal interrupting capacity: 1000MVA.

The first draft initiated by eng. Saleh El-Sayed Saleh and revised by Operation and maintenance's General Director: eng Mohamed Reda Abd El-Azez,

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy tools must be used as safety Helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Generator isolation and proper earthling is required

  • Circuit breaker disconnects and secondary disconnects and ground shoe

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • Digital voltmeter, Megger, Brushes.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the mating surfaces of the metal-clad.

  2. Check the racking mechanism to make certain that it is in the disconnect position.

  3. Check the voltage control at the source of power and line drop between the power source and breaker.

  4. Check closing and opening times.

Note:      a. Initiation of trip signal to control parting:            

                        - 5 cycle breaker                        0.35 to 0.50 Sec.

                        - 3 cycle breaker                        0.25 to 0.30 Sec.    

        b. Initiation of close signal to control closing:
            - Standard breaker                        0.60 to 0. 1 Sec.
            - Fast bus transformer breaker       0. 60 to 0. 1 Sec.
            - Instantaneous reclose time           0. 128 to 0.221 Sec

  1. The primary circuit insulation on the breaker may be checked phase to phase to ground using a 2500V megohmeter.

  2. A resistance check of the primary circuit may be made with the breaker-closed use a low resistance-measuring instrument, which measures microhms.

  3. Vacuum interrupter integrity test .

  4. Check the erosion indicator and wipe gap.

  5. Lubricate the breaker operating mechanism.

  6. Check the electrical operation using test cabinet ( if available ) or the test position in the metal clad switchgear.

  7. Examine the movable contact rod of the vacuum interrupter with the breaker open and wipe the lubricant off the rod and examine the silver surface.
