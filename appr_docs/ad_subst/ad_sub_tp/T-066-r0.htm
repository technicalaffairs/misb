Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding on all its sides is required,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety belt when cleaning suspension insulator,

  • There is a danger of falling into the containment pit when working near large transformer,

  • It is recommended to use only fiberglass (PVC) ladders when working in the switchyard.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and tool set

  • Blower, voltmeter, screwdriver set and fiberglass PVC ladder

  • Voltage indicator

  • Portable earth for discharging

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes, silicagel, 

Environmental Recommendation

  • Before starting the cleaning of all porcelain surfaces of all transformer wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning collect it and send it to the dump

  • Fix any oil leaks to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil

  • Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impact on the environment, mainly on soil

Ground water and eventually on the eco-system. "It takes only I liter of hydrocarbon spilled in an aquifer to render 1.000.000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption"

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Thoroughly clean all porcelain surfaces of all bushing.

  2. Check for signs of oil leaks (repair if possible).

  3. Check oil temperature and winding temperature.

  4. Make a visual and audible inspection of all fans, and its screen.

  5. Check transformer control cabinet and swing blower clean it.

  6. Using blower to clean the oltc cabinet and click it.

  7. Check doors gasket of OLTC and control cabinet.

  8. Check oil level in main tank, OLTC tank conservator and oil bushing (top up).

  9. Check condition of silica-gel breather.

  10. Perform a complete tap changer operation from highest to lowest step and back to the position it was found in to clear all internal contacts of tap changer (this step should be done every 3 months).
