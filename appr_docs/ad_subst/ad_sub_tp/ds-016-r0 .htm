Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, must be performed every 5 years Y5 as indicated in the above title block.

Note: The first draft document initiated by Cairo North substation 220kv, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the DS.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Isolate the DC voltage.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Before using a blower to clean the control cabinet or when cleaning the insulators, wear breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particles.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks.

  • Tool set of  drivers, screw drivers, cloth, air blowers, cleaning material (rags, cloth) timing equipment,

  • Indicator glass (end scope), cleaning fluid, electrical grease.

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Open the cover and cleaning by Blower.

  2. Check the functional (closing and opening).

  3. Check the gearing running time with motorized operating mechanism (at 100% supply voltage) max 2 sec.

  4. Check of slip coupling torque if necessary to adjust:   (figure 1)
    a -     Remove slips coupling 25667, T10248 & removes screws T10381.
    b - To adjust, turn adjusting ring T10384 with adjusting wrench ZN4051.
    c - Note (clockwise to increase torque counter clockwise to reduce it) until nominal slip torque 
         according to the following table:

    Part no.

    Nominal Slip Torque

    25667

    8 Nm +20%

              - 10%

    T10248

    4 Nm +30%

              - 10%

    d - Align adjusting ring / locking plate bares and in screw in screw T 10381.
    e - Allow slip coupling to slip 10 rotations in each direction.

  5. Check nominal slip torque and again readjust if necessary.

  6. Remove slip coupling.

  7. Repeat the step no. 3.

  8. Clean and grease the operating mechanism.

  9. Make visual inspection for the condition of the tubular contact though indicator glass.
