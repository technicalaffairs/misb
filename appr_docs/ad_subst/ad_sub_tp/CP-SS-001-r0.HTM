Introduction

This document lists the Equipment Inspection I1, which must be performed every week as indicated by W1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet
  • Safety glasses
Work to be carried out

  1. Check oil level in compressor crank case, but only with the compressor at rest, the oil level should never fall below the sight-glass

  2. Check for any unusual noises or vibrations

  3. Check for air leaks in piping and pipe connections

  4. Read and record gauge pressures and compressor running time indicator

  5. Clean compressor room floor
