Introduction

This document lists the equipment testing procedure checklist, which must be performed for Power Transformers according to each manufacturer Instructions.

Safety Precautions

  • Isolate the transformer “out of service”

  • A work order must be issued, the qualified test group should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Disconnect the transformer from bridles

  • Connect the bridles to earth.

  • All bushings should be clean otherwise errors reading could be happened.

  • Make short circuit S.C LV side bushings, H.V side bushing and Teriery side bushing.

  • Connect the earth of the TR. To earth point of the tester.

  • Hazards could be happened.

Tools and Equipment

  • Test equipment type M4K,  Doble

Work to be Carried Out

 

Test # 1   (CH)  HV ---- E + (LV + Ter. guard)

  1. Place (UST -GST) switch at  L - GUARD

  2. Connect  HV cable to SC HV bushing.

  3. Connect  LV cable to SC LV bushing.

  4. Turn on to C position and dial switch of the tester to get balance of the pointer.

  5. Changeover the switch to OF position and select the range and dial to get balance of pointer.

  6. Record the results on the attached test sheet.

  7. Correct the reading to be at 20oC.

 

 

Test # 2           ( CL)   LV ----------  E + (HV+Ter. guard)

 

   1.    Place (UST -GST) switch at H-Guard.

   2.    Connect  S.C HV cable to S.C HV bushing.

   3.    Connect  S.C LV cable to S.C.LV bushing.

   4.    Short HV bushing to tertiary.

   5.    Turn on to C position and dial switch of the tester to get balance of the pointer.

   6.    Changeover the switch to OF position and select the range and dial to get balance of pointer.

   7.    Record the results on the attached test sheet.

   8.   Correct the reading to be at 20oC.

Test # 3          (CHL)   HV ---------- LV +(T +E  guard)

  1. Place (UST -GST ) switch at UST.

  2. Connect  HV cable to S.C HV bushing.

  3. Connect  LV cable to S.C.LV bushing.

  4. Ground the Tertiary  winding.

  5. Turn on to "C" position and dial switch of the tester to get balance of the pointer.

  6. Changeover the switch to "OF" position and select the range and dial to get balance of pointer.

  7. Record the results on the attached test sheet.

  8. Correct the reading to be at 20oC.

Test # 4         (CHTer)   HV--------- Ter+ (LV +E) guard

  1. Place (UST -GST ) switch at UST.

  2. Connect  HV cable to S.C of  HV bushing.

  3. Connect  LV cable  for S.C of  Ter  bushing.

  4. Graund the L.V  winding.

  5. Turn on to "C" position and dial switch of the tester to get balance of the pointer.

  6. Changeover the switch to "OF" position and select the range and dial to get balance of pointer.

  7. Record the results on the attached test sheet.

  8. Correct the reading to be at 20oC.

Test # 5         (CLTer )    LV ----------  Ter  (HV +E) guard

  1. Place (UST -GST ) switch at UST.

  2. Connect  HV cable to S.C of  LV bushing.

  3. Connect  LV cable to S.C of  Tertiary bushing.

  4. Ground the H.V  winding.

  5. Turn on to "C" position and dial switch of the tester to get balance of the pointer.

  6. Changeover the switch to "OF" position and select the range and dial to get balance of pointer.

  7. Record the results on the attached test sheet.

  8. Correct the reading to be at 20oC.

Test # 6        (CTer )    T ---------- E+ (HV +LV) guard

  1. Place (UST -GST ) switch at ( L-Guard)..

  2. Connect  HV cable to S.C of  Teriory bushing.

  3. Connect  LV cable to S.C of LV bushing.

  4. Short H.V bushing to L.V bushing.

  5. Turn on to "C" position and dial switch of the tester to get balance of the pointer.

  6. Changeover the switch to "OF" position and select the range and dial to get balance of pointer.

  7. Record the results on the attached test sheet.

  8. Correct the reading to be at 20oC.
