Introduction

This document lists Equipment Inspection Checklist I1, which must be performed  every 3 years (Y3) as indicated in the title block. This document was initiated at Abu-Zaabal 500kv substation, Cairo.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Turn off the power source.

  • Lock the mechanism by mechanical lock pin.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks.

  • Air Blower,

  • 500v megger

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check presence of rust outside the mechanism box.

  2. Check presence of dew condensation and rust within mechanism box.

  3. Check movement of mechanism.

  4. Check condition of position indicator.

  5. Check Lever stopper gap:
    Open position 0.5 -- 8 mm
    Closed position 0.5 -- 13 mm


                                Figure 1 Lever Stopper Gap

  6. Check the wiring terminal condition.

  7. Check the tightening parts condition.

  8. Check the insulation resistance between control circuit and ground (more than 2M ohm with 500V megger).
