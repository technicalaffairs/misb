Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every year  bases as indicated by Y1  in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Anter Aly Bakr, accepted by general director: eng. Ibrahem M. Sayed El Ahl at Awlad Hmam 220kv substation, and approved by eng. Abdel Rahem Abdel Rahem Abdel Rahem; EHV Network Head Sector, Delta zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •
  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes .... etc
  • SF6 gas detector

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check SF6 pressure on manometer.    (8 bar at 20 oC, rated pressure=7bar))

  2. Check air pressure on manometer.      (20 bar)

  3. Check for air and gas leaks.     (audible noise or frequent refilling)

  4. Check that the air valves closed and then,

  5. Drain compressed air receiver.

  6. Check tightness of screw and pipe coupling.
     External diameter of pipe            Permissible moment
            10 mm                                        7 Nm
            12 mm                                      10 Nm
             15 mm                                      14 Nm
             18 mm                                      17 Nm

  7. Check refilling valve for function and tightness. Refilling valve 373 (Y0) fig. 2

  8. Carry out test switching operations, (before switching check SF6 pressure in the pole columns, and the pressure of air in the compressed air receiver of the breaker):
    - 10 C-O-switching at one minute intervals.
    - Including 2 O-    switching with the manual emergency valve.
