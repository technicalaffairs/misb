Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I2, which must be carried out every 3 month as indicated by M3 in the above title. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 11/5/02 for T1 at First Urban Community 66kv Substation, Cairo zone

Safety Precaution 

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt

  • Safety robe and precaution marks must be used

  • Switch off transformer from tow sides

  • Putting portable earthing is necessary

  • Check the transformer ladder, fix it well

  • Switch off DC source

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower for cleaning purposes, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Wear your breathing mask if you have to use any chemical solvent because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't through oil or grease on ground used, collect it and send to the central chemical labs to recycle it.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

  • Check the sound level of fans (slandered level 40-60 decibel)

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool sets

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower

  • Pieces of soft cloths

Work to be carried out

  1. Repeat the works that have be done according to MPIS document No. T-044-r0

  2. Inspect surface of transformer and gaskets and check oil leakage, any leakage must be treated

  3. Clean the bushings with soft clothes

  4. Check oil level in bushings

  5. Check and clean control cabinet of fan motor drive, using video cleaner for connections, also inspect its gasket to be sealed

  6. Cleaning and lubricating the fan bearings

  7. Check and clean control cabinet of tap changer motor drive, using video cleaner for connections, limit switch, contactors, etc...., also inspect its gasket to be sealed

  8. Check fans to be on (at 55 C) in oil temperature gauge

  9. Check fans alarm (at 85 C) in oil temperature gauge

  10. Check fans trip (at 95 C) in oil temperature gauge

  11. Check fans to be on (at 65C) in winding temperature gauge

  12. Check fans alarm (at 95 C) in winding temperature gauge

  13. Check fans trip (at 105 C) in winding temperature gauge

  14. Check tap-changer drive shaft

  15. Clean the radiator and its accessories

  16. Check the earthing system of the transformer and its accessories (arrestors, earthing resistance, marshaling box, etc.)

  17. Clean and grease the earthing - resistance switch and links between its arms

  18. Clean equipments connecting to earthing - resistance like current transformer, arrester and porcelain insulators

  19. Check all connections and retighten them
