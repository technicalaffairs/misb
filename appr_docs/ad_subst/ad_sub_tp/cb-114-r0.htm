Introduction

This document outlines the equipment checklist I1, to be performed every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block. The first draft initiated by eng Mohamed Aly Aly Hassan ElShabab Gas Turbine Power Station and tested at 20 March 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work Order must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

Tools and Equipment

  • A complete set of tools, Motor oil, Insulating oil, screw drivers.

Work to be carried out  

  1. Clean the surface of the metal clad.

  2. Check the primary circuit insulation on the breaker phase to phase, phase to ground.

  3. Check the DC resistance.

  4. Close the CB manually

  5. Open the CB manually

  6. Check of charging and discharging of the CB spring.

  7. Measure the tripping time of the CB

  8. Measure the closing time of the CB

  9. Measure the control voltage value.

  10. Lubricate the CB mechanism, figures 1, 2

  11. Check the operating mechanism

  12. Check the oil level in the CB to be kept as near the mark as possible.           Change oil **

  13. Measure the electric strength
