Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which performed every  year as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

Note: This step by step instructions are for testing the turns ratio for the power transformers,

using test set DTR Model 8500 (AEMC INSTRUMENTS ).

The first draft initiated & tested at Elaasher 3 substation by eng. Ebraheem Hasan from Elaasher  protection management, reviewed by protection manager eng. Fawzy Barakat, accepted by general manager eng. Elsaaied Nagy, revised by quality control manager eng. Mohamed Emran, and approved by eng. Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified testing staff should carry the suitable safety categories.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • The earthing connection must be the first made and the last removed.

Tools and Equipment

  • Test equipment type DTR Model 8500 AEMC INSTRUMENTS

  • Test leads
  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

Preparations

  • Take the transformer out of service.

  • Discharge the transformer windings static charge.

  • Disassembly all clamps from bushings (High Voltage side, Low Voltage side & tertiary if exists).

  • Don't misapply the High leads and Low leads of the test equipment to the transformer bushings.
    (Misapply will cause damage to the test equipment)

  • Keep test leads apart from the bushings. 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect the earth of tester to the common earth of substation.

  2. Connect the HV leads (H): connecting red clamp to HV bushing ( R ) and black one to HV bushing (S)

  3. Connect the LV leads (X): connecting red clamp to LV bushing (r) and black one LV bushing (n).

  4. Turn on the test unit power supply then the following messages will appear:-
     AEMC  INSTRUMENTS                DTR  MODLE 8500
    VT/PT Test Mode                                HV Calibrating 
    Please Wait                                          VT/PT Test Ready
    Hence the test unit is ready for measurement process.

  5. With the previous connections , and your OLTC is in position  no. 1   press the red test switch to check continuity and stay until the reading in the LCD become fixed.

  6. Record your obtained result in a table as shown below, then press the RESET button to reset for the following test.

  7. Move the OLTC to point 2 and rerun the test , then record the obtained  result, then RESET

  8. Repeat the previous steps with OLTC in all positions and record your obtained results

  9. Change the test leads H between HV bushings (S) and (T), whereas the leads  x  between LV bushings (S) and (n)

  10. Repeat measuring the turns ratio for all positions of OLTC and record the obtained results

  11. Repeat step 9 between HV bushings (T)&(R) and LV bushings (t) & (n), then repeat step 10.

  12. Compare the obtained results with calculated values
