Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance Procedures P1, to be performed every six years as indicated by Y6 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Gamal Foad Sultan and tested at 17 Aug 02, Abu Zaabal 500kv S/S Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A word permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear the appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, and safety glasses

  • CB must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides.

  • Ensure that the earthling switch must be must be closed on both sides of the breaker

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of maintenance hand tolls
  • 220V ac supply
  • Portable pressure gague
  • Emergency light
  • Timing equipment

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check tightness of ground terminals and other installation

  2. Check of abnormal noise

  3. Check of rust or damage of components

  4. Check of blind cover for gas feeding inlet

  5. Check of gas pressure and temperature measurement

  6. Check of setting of gas pressure switches:
                          Alarm 36 GA (ON) P1 = 4.5 ± 0.3 kg/cm2
                          Block 36 GL (ON) P2 = 4.0 ± 0.3 kg /cm2

  7. Check open-close position of gas stop valve

  8. Check of lubrication of mechanism

  9. Check of leakage of shock absorber oil

  10. Check of looseness of retainer ring and nuts

  11. Check of space heaters

  12. Check the position of position indicator

  13. Check of registered number of operation counters

  14. Check of condensation and rust inside housing and water leak into housing

  15. Check of door packing

  16. Check of tightness of polts in housing

  17. Check of control circuit wiring and terminals

  18. Check of manual opining and closing operating

  19. Check of closing and opining time
    closing  < 190 ms                opening < 26 ms

  20. Measure and record; Air content, Acidity, and Dew point tests for sf6 gas, reference to Approved MPIS document no. TD-SS-004-r0
