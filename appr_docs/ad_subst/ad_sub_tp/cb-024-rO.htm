Introduction

This document detail the items, which are checked on every (12) twelve years bases Y12 as recommended and defined in Mitsubishi catalogue maintenance as a Major maintenance P2.

 

This document is updated by eng. Sherif Mahrous Omar on 28 Aug 2004 and tested by eng. Nafad Kamel Mahmoud revised by UEEPCO Quality control general director eng. Gamal Abd El wahab and approved by eng. Mohamed Gamal Diab Head Sector of El-Kureimat power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

    •

  • Appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, i.e. safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Open the circuit breaker and open the DS's at both sides of the breaker pole. Ground the main conductor.

  • Switch off the power supply to the control circuit and exhaust the compressed air from the drain valve of the air reservoir.

  • Be sure to insert the closing or tripping preventative pins at the time of internal inspection and inspection of moving parts.

  • Use designated lubricant.

  • Do not smear the lubricant on the insulators.

  • Renew all the O-rings at the flanges which have been opened for inspection. Handle O-rings with special care to avoid any damage.

  • Do not disassemble the slide seal assemblies.

  • Tighten each bolt with a proper torque.

  • Test the leakage of all reassembled gas seals.

  • Compare the mechanical operation data obtained before and after the inspection to insure orderly operation of the breaker.

  • Don't operate the circuit breaker by the pneumatic operating mechanism when the Sf6 gas  in the enclosures is below the lock pressure.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of maintenance hand tools.
  • 220 V ac supply, Portable pressure gauge.
  • Emergency light, Timing equipment.
  • SF6 Gas leak detector.
  • Contact resistance tester model WRT-100
    •

Work to be carried out

The inspection steps, which we do every 6 years, should also be done in this inspection with addition to.

  1. Replace the rupture disk.

  2. Wipe away contamination and dust with clean cloths inside enclosures.

  3. Renewal of absorbent.

  4. Measure the Contact Resistance of main circuit using the test equipment model WRT-100 as shown below

  5. Check of Gas leakage.

  6. Check of setting of air pressure switches:
                   

  7. Replace the valve seat (3128) and valve seal (3318).

  8. Check of air leakage

  9. Check of open-close of air stop valve.

  10. Check of  lubrication mechanism.

  11. Check of  looseness of retainer ring and nuts.

  12. Check of connection of space heaters.

  13. Check auxiliary switch contact.

  14. Check of position of position indicator.

  15. Check of registered number of operation counters.

  16. Check of condensation and rust inside housing and water leak into housing.

  17. Check of door packing.

  18. Check of tightness of bolts in housing.

  19. Check of    control circuit wiring and terminals.

  20. Check of insulation resistance measurements of BCT circuit wiring and terminals(100 M ohm min).

  21. Check of insulation resistance measurement of control circuit(2M ohm min).

  22. Check of lubrication on lever system.

  23. Check of manual opening and closing operation.

  24. Check of air reservoir capacity (from rated to lockout pressure).

  25. Check of closing and opening time.   Closing < 90Ms.  Opening <18Ms.

  26. Record the results at the following test sheet:
