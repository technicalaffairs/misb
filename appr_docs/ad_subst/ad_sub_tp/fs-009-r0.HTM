Introduction 

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a weekly intervals bases as indicated by W1 in the title block.

The aim of these instructions is to recommend a minimum requirements for checks and inspections in order to maintain the reliability of the fire fighting systems in the transformer yards.

The first draft initiated by Eng. Emad Farouq Moustafa at Katamia 220kv substation Cairo Zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective safety equipment, i.e. safety shoes, overall, and helmet

  • Transformer outage is required

  • After the functioning test of the system, make sure

  • That the value between compressor and main tank is closed before refilling the tank again with water

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet, Safety Shoes, and breathing mask .

Work to be carried out

Readiness for Operation

  1. The main gate valve (B1) and the main gate valves controlling the water supply, e.g. (B2), must be secured open by a pad - locked up.

  2. The alarm test cock (G) must be in " service" position the drain valve (E1) and the vent valve (E2) must be closed

  3. The air filling valve (M) must be open

  4. The handles of the pressure gauge control cocks must be directed down wards

  5. Thoroughly inspect the pilot hands (0) and the extinguishing nozzles (P) periodically and remove dust, fibers, paint, etc., if necessary   

  6. It is not allowed to hang or lay any thing from or on the nozzle pipes and incitation lines

Maintenance of the Extinguishing system

  1. Check the air pressure at the pressure gauge (K1) the pressure in the incitation pipe system shall be 6 bar and must not drop below 5 bar

  2. Check the pressure of the water supply, e.g. at pressure gauge (K2)

  3. Check the pressure of the water volume and air pressure of tank and / or the water volume of the elevated tank

  4. Check the pressure gauges (K1) & (K2) by closing thier control cocks and loosening the lateral knurled head screws.
    The indicators must go back to zero,
    At the same time, the air pressure supervising switch (L) is relived and the electric air leakage detection checked

  5. Test of fire alarm. Set alarm test cock to "test" until an alarm is sounding; then set alarm test cock again to "service"

  6. Check the pump for operation

In case of a fire

After release of only one incitation nozzle. Manual release of the value by:

  • Electric remote operation of the solenoid valve

  • Operating of the manual release lever at the solenoid valve

  • Operating hand incitation valves in the incitation piping

Stopping of the Alarm

Set alarm test cock (G) to "closed" position

After a fire

  1. After extinction of the fire, close main gate valve (B1) and air filling valve (M)

  2. Drain the valve (C) and the nozzle piping by opening the drain valve (E1) and all the drain values of the nozzle piping

  3. Replace the opened incitation nozzles by new ones from the spare stock (Attention refill the spare stock)

  4. Re establish the readiness for operation of the single - chamber remote control station Attention lever indicates the switch position.
