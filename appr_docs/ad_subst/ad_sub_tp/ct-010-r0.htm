Introduction

This document details the equipment inspection I1, which must be carried out every two years as indicated by Y2 in the title block

The tests of Current Transformers 220, 132, 66 kv as follows:

  1. Insulation resistance test for primary winding.

  2. Insulation resistance test for secondary winding.

  3. Insulation resistance test between primary and secondary.

  4. Capacity and dissipation factor. tan (δ) of oil at 20C < 1.5%    once a year (Y1)

  5. Testing of sample oil.

  6. Electrical breakdown strength of oil > 45kv

  7. Chemical analysis once every three years  (Y3)

The first draft initiated and tested by eng. Khaled Ebrahim, accepted by eng. Ramadan Ragab  general director, approved by eng. Khairy Eshak Head Sector, certified by eng. Ahmed Said Abd El Aziz; Middle Egypt Zone MPIS coordinator and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators ... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • 2500 volt megger for testing primary winding

  • 1000 volt megger for testing primary winding

  • Tan (δ) equipment (TCB 100) made in Canada

  • Break down voltage equipment

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Isolate the current transformer out of service

  2. Clean the out insulators of CT

  3. Measure the insulation resistance between primary winding and earth by 2500 volt megger

  4. After isolating secondary winding from earth point, make a short on secondary winding leads and measure insulation resistance for the secondary winding to earth. (for protection & measuring), between primary winding and secondary winding

  5. Testing capacity (CX) PF and tan (δ).

Notes

  1. The value of insulation resistance for primary winding must be not less than 1000 M. ohm

  2. The value of insulation resistance for secondary winding must be not less than 100 M. ohm

  3. All above tests must be carried yearly

  4. Connect the secondary winding with earth point after isolat it from the short before put the current transformer in operation

  5. Break down voltage for 500 kv CT less than 45 kv

  6. Break down voltage for 220 kv CT less than 35 kv

Tan δ Measurement of the Internal Insulation

  1. Open the cover of the secondary connection.

  2. Disconnect the earthing wire from the terminal earth.

  3. Connect all leads of  the secondaries and the terminal ( )

  4. Connect this link to the bridge for measuring tan (δ)

  5. The disconnection and connection of wires connected to and from the leads of the secondaries should be carried out under the supervision and agreement of the relay protection engineer who are responsible for this work.
     
    The value of tan (δ) should not be more than 1.5% at temp.= 20 oC
