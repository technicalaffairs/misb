Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block for the minimum oil Circuit Breaker 66kv:
        - Rated Voltage: 66kv,          Rated current: 2500 A,             Short circuit current: 3l.5 KA/1sec
The first draft initiated by eng. Samer Ayad at Sabtia I Cairo, tested at 24/8/02

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area, safety around the work place.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses …

  • CB must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the CB

  • Control Power disconnected

Tools and Equipment
  • Blower, complete set of tools, cleaning cloth, and hydraulic oil  if needed.
Work to be carried out

  1. Check the oil level of the circuit Breaker.

  2. Change the oil every 5 times of automatic Break due to short circuit.

  3. Check tighten, Nuts, Bolts, etc….

  4. Clean porcelain insulator surface.

  5. Check the cabinet of mechanism and operation

  6. Lubricate the axis.
