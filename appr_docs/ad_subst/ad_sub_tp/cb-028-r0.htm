Introduction
    This document outlines the inspection and maintenance steps to be performed after 10 breaking of full short circuit.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.
  • CB must be isolated and proper earthed at the two sides.
  • Isolate control circuit (DC voltage)
  • Safety fences with caution marks are required
  • Close the incoming valves at the compressed air feeding the CB tank.
  • Open the drainage valve to drain the air out of the tank and make sure that the tank is free of pressure.
  • Use only fiberglass ladders.
  • Wearing safety helmet is required.

Environmental Recommendation

  • Using solvents such as gasoline and others for cleaning the oil filter is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very hazardous and carcinogenic chemicals. You have to wear your breathing mask.
  • Don't bum the cloth used in cleaning; collect it and send it to the dump.
Tools and Equipment
  • One set of metric open end box wrench Spanner (42)
  • Fine emery paper.
  • Some clean and dry cloth.
  • Torque wrench.
  • Some of glycol and benzene.
Work to be carried out

  1. Remove the high voltage connection at the two sides of the arcing chamber.

  2. Take out the movable contacts from both sides of the chamber in the following way:

    • Disconnect the two water resistances (19 fig 4) and get it down on a piece of wood Use spanner 19
    • Unscrew the nuts at the connection spindle (55 fig 4) and the relief cover (56 fig 4) Use spanner 42
    • Unscrew the 3 nuts on the plate, and remove the plate with the spindle (55 fig 4) and with the deionizer (54 fig 4)
    • Unscrew the6 screws and carefully take out the bearer of the movable contact (57 fig 4). And put it out, take care not to break the screen (49 fig 4) Use spanner 17

  3. With taking off the relief of the chamber. (66 fig 4) at the isolator end, and disconnect the flexible connection link (68 fig 4), the rest in the same way as c and d steps. Use spanner 42

  4. It the contact surface is slightly burnt takes it to the work shop. to be cleaned by a fine emery paper.

  5. Visually check fixed contact for condition.

  6. It more than 1/3 of the contact surface is damaged it should be changed.

  7. Clean the internal surfaces of the arcing chamber with dry cloths.

  8. Inspect the cover gasket and replace if it is necessary.

  9. Replace moving contact assembly and other connections.

  10. Repeat the above steps for the remaining 2 poles.

  11. Before putting the CB in service open -close- open it again and recheck for air leaks.

  12. Perform timing test, micro ohm test, and measure the support column insulator-resistance by megger.

  13. Record all that has been done on the maintenance card.
