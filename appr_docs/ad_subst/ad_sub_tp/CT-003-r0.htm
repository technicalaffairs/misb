Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must performed on an annually bases Y1 as indicated in the above title block. The first draft initiated by eng Ahmed Ismaeel Zen El_Abdeen from Bany Sowaif west 132kv substation, Middle Egypt

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 
  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, safety belt.

  • Secondary CT wiring could be energized back feeds, ensure that circuit brcaker to secondary wiring in open.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, Rags, and silicagel

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check oil level

  2. Observe the possible oil leakage, if any leakage is detected tighten carefully the corresponding bolts and nuts

  3. Take oil sample

  4. Inspect the colour in the breather silica gel, when silica gel has grown red the unit should be removed and replaced by a new one, the breather with damp silica gel should be dismantled, silica gel should be changed.

  5. Inspect the state of contact surface for absent of dirt, dints cracks

  6. Remove and clean dust, soot, etc.
