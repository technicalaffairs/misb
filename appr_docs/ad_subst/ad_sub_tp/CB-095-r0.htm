Introduction

 

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on a monthly bases M1 as indicated in the above title block. The first draft initiated at Tibeen south 220kv substation, tested 14 Oct 2001, and approved 12 Jan 2002, Cairo zone

 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides, i.e. earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker to ensure it is properly ground.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety shoes and helmet,

  • Change remote switch to local position,

  • Hydraulic system without pressure,

  • Interrupt the control circuit DC volt.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety shoes, Helmet, and dry wiper

  • Complete tool set and air blower.

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check the pressure of SF6 gas (6 bar),

  2. Check the oil pressure (320 bar),

  3. Record counter reading of operating counter,

  4. Check the function of heater and check the door gasket of control cabinet,

  5. Inspect break insulator and supporting isolator for pollution, cracks, and clean it with dry wiper,

  6. Check all connection of the breaker,

  7. Check the oil leak,

  8. Check the priming pressure of nitrogen.

  9. Lubricate the moving connection.

  10. Check the gas leak
