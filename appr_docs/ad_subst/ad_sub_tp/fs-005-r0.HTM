Introduction 

The purpose of this procedure is to describe the steps which must be followed to put this fire protection system back into service after a fire fighting incident.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipments safety helmet, safety shoes …. etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet, Safety Shoes.

Work to be carried out

Filling the Water Tank

  1. Unscrew the plugs on the overflow outlet pipe.

  2. Open the valve in the supply line from the reserve water supply.

  3. Start filling the tank with water until water comes out of the overflow pipe.

  4. Close the valve in the reserve supply line and screw on the plug on the overflow pipe.

Removing the Discharged C02 Cylinders

  1. To reset the trigger mechanism, lift the lever on the right side of the trigger until it engages, an audible clicking noise can be heard. If the system was activated manually through the emergency station, you must also reset the mechanical lever by pulling the steel cable. This will also reset the pull handle in the emergency pull-station, the glass must also be replaced.

  2. Move the saw-toothed slide so that the releasing mechanism is in its initial position.

  3. Set the locking pins on the valve heads so the opening levers on the cylinders are in the closed position.

  4. Remove the connecting pin of the tie-rod on the saw toothed slide.

  5. Now you are able to remove the discharge device on the cylinders by loosening the screws 

  6. located around the base of the valve heads.

  7. Disconnect the connection hose between the outlet of the cylinder valves and the C02 manifold.

  8. Carefully move the cylinders out and remove the holding bracket connected to the cylinder weighing device.

  9. Screw the valve caps onto the cylinder valves and move the cylinders out to be recharged

Installing new (recharged) C02 Cylinders:

  1. Remove the transport caps from the cylinder valves.

  2. Install the weighing bracket and secure the cylinders into the bracket.

  3. Screw on the connection hose between the cylinder valves and the C02 manifold.

  4. Install the cylinder valve opening device. Make sure the lever is aligned with the slot in the valve head so it can move to the horizontal position when required.

  5. Insert the connecting pin of the tie-rods and the saw-toothed slide, ensure that the whole assembly is free to move.

  6. Remove the safety pins to release the manual valve heads. Be sure that all systems are in working order and the detection system is switched back to the ON position (to prevent accidental gas discharge ).

  7. Reset the electrical discharge trigger switch manually by pressing down the button provided at the base of this contact switch.
