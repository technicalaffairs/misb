Introduction

This document lists equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually buses as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Rahman Hassan Saoudy at Cairo North substation 220kv

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •

  • Wear safety shoes, and safety helmet.

  • Switch off the CB and withdraw it from the conduit

  • Ground the cable head

  • Switch off the DC

  • Use fiberglass ladder

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmit and safety shoes

  • Portable air blower

  • Set of common and screw drivers, cleaning material

  • Graphite grease, grease for lubricating, emery, portable fiberglass ladder

  • Metal brush

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Examine the external parts of the arc extinguish chamber

  2. Check the presence of any abnormal noise

  3. Check the presence of overheat and flashover at the arc extinguishing chamber after opening cycle

  4. Check the presence of surface defects or crack at the arc extinguishing chamber.

  5. Check the tightening of bolts

  6. Check the mechanism of operation

  7. Clean the insulation and check for defects or cracks

  8. Lubricate the axis

  9. Check the moving and fixed contacts

  10. Measure the timing test open - close cycle

  11. Check the difference between phases (not exceed 10 ms)

  12. Adjust the time for open if you need from the screw open

  13. Measure the contact resistance (must not exceed 50 micro ohm)   

  14. Check the vacuum for the arc extinguish chamber. (When CB at off position, release the split pin & remove the Benz then look at the motion of moving contact directed to inside)

  15. Apply H V to the arc extinguishing chamber 18kv for 1 min. to test the vacuum
