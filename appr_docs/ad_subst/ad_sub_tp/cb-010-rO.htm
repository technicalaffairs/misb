Introduction

This procedure details the Equipment Inspection activities, which are performed every three months as indicated by the M3 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the breaker and ground it.

  • Proper well fixed ladder should be used for climbing on the breaker.

  • Isolate the DC voltage.

  • Change the operation position from remote to local.

Tools and Equipment

  • Blower, multimeter, 

  • Gas detector, set of common keys, 

  • Screw drivers set, rags, scratch and electrical grease.

Work to be carried out

  1. Vacuum the interior of the circuit breaker control panel to remove all accumulated dust and dirt.

  2. Clean panel exterior, bushings bus bars and connector of dust and dirt.

  3. Check control wiring for loose connections and any signs of overheating and tighten all

  4. Clean all the bolts of the main connectors at the circuit breaker terminals for looseness. tighten all loose bolts of the connectors are required. Make note of any signs of overheating, smooth it if its necessary.

  5. Check and tighten all ground connections as required repair any damaged ground connections.

  6. Check control devices such as relays, limit switches, ... etc. in control panel for proper operation, repair or replace fault devices. 

  7. Check hydraulic system for proper operation including motor, motor starter and control circuit. Replace blown fuses with the same type ratings.

  8. Check the operating mechanism of the circuit breaker. Inspect for loose bolts, nuts or linkage. "Tighten or replace parts as required."

  9. Check for gas leak of the circuit breaker. "check the gas gauge"

  10. Check the circuit breaker on/off indications and adjust its position as required.

  11. Check the cabinet heaters.

  12. Check the close spring changed indicator.

  13. Check the trip and close springs indication visually make note any remarks for repairing.

  14. Through the operators check the circuit breaker functions from remote and local.

  15. Check the door gasket.

  16. Check oil leak of hydraulic pump and also oil level.
