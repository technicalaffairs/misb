Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks I2, which must be performed on monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves

    • ... etc.

  • Don't smoke in the battery room.

  • Don't place any tools on top of battery cells (Danger of short circuit).

  • All tools will be used must be insulated.

  • Never use the same tools on these cells, you may be used before on lead acid cells.

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.
  • Standard screwdrivers set, density meter, voltmeter, brushes, distilled water, and rags.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Read overall voltage of bank and record on battery maintenance card.

  2. Disconnect AC supply to battery charger.

  3. Clean the dust of battery.

  4. Check the electrolyte level and add distilled water (if necessary) to the maximum level if the cells are completely charged and to 10 mm below the maximum level if they are discharged.

  5. Check the pole and connectors and clean any salt deposits with distilled water.

  6. Check that connections are tight

  7. Lubricate the pole and connectors with Vaseline including the disco and nuts.

  8. Read voltage per cell and record on battery maintenance.

  9. Measure the electrolyte density and record on battery maintenance card (1.19 kg1)

  10. Again read overall voltage of bank and record on battery maintenance card and compare the reading from step no. 1

  11. If overall voltage is below 220 volts place bank on float (high charge for 5 hours) after which you switch the charger back on normal charge.

  12. Compare the present reading with the lost reading.

  13. Check the temperature inside the battery room not exceeding 45oC maximum and 20 degree minimum.

  14. Check the ventilation of the battery room to ensure their correct operation.
