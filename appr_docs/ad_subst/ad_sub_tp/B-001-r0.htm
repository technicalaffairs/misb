Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves ... etc
    •
  • Don't smoke in the battery room.
  • Don't place any tools on top of battery cells (Danger of short circuit)
    •
  • Never use the same tools on alkaline cells you may have used before on lead acid cells
    •
  • All tools will be used must be insulated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety goggles, rubber gloves, and rubber apron.

  • Cleaning cloth.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Inspect terminals of battery and clean any salt deposits with distilled water

  2. Clean dust off battery

  3. Check for the correct position of all vent covers

  4. Clean battery room floor with water

  5. Check for correct operation of battery room exhaust fan
