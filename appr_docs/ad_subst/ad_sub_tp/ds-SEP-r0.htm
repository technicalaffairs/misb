Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1, which must be carried out  every two years as indicated by Y2 in the above title block. The first draft has been initiated by eng.                       and tested at  /   /     for                    Circuit no.   at                      220kv Substation,         zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety rope with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used,

  • Disconnect switch must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the CB.

  • Isolate the DC voltage.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower for cleaning purposes, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Also wear your breathing mask if you have to use any chemical solvent for cleaning purposes because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Common set of screw drivers, set of keys,

  • Cleaning materials (rags, cloth), soft cloth, mask,

  • Timing instrument, Indicator glass, Cleaning fluid,

  • Complete substation maintenance tool set,

  • Endscope equipment, and electrical grease.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check all bolts connections for tighteness

  2. Clean and re-grease all moving joints.

  3. Examine the contacts for signs of burning after short circuit stresses

  4. Clean the the silver plated surface using soft cloth.

  5. Re-grease lightly the contacts using vaselin.

  6. clean the post insulators using water once a year.

  7. The bearings of the swivels are totally enclosed and greased.

  8. Open and close the disconnect switch for 5 or 6 times consecutively.
