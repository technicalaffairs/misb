Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks I2, which must be performed every two years instead of ten years according to manufacturer letter dated 12 Oct 1998 to Sakr Koresh Substation, and as indicated by Y2 in the title block..

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be disconnected and properly grounded on both sides using portable grounds.

    •

  • Circuit breaker must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides.

  • Isolate the DC voltage

  • Change remote/local control switch to local position.

Tools and Equipment
  • Multimeter, blower, gas detector, buzzer, screwdriver, and set of common keys.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check supply voltage.

  2. Clean protective tube of the position indicator with cleaning agent suitable for plexiglass.

  3. Grease threaded spindle of auxiliary switch.

  4. Check screw joints of electric terminals, if necessary retighten.

  5. Check mechanical connections on the drive and auxiliary switch part for wear.

  6. Grease right-angle gearings.

  7. Grease sliding bearings.

  8. Check shaft sealing in the cover for tightness by sniffing.

  9. Check threaded spindle for wear 1) 2)

  10. Check annular contact and contact rod for contact burn 1) 2)

  11. Check auxiliary switch contacts for correct operation and contact burn.

  12. Check carbon brushes of DC motor if necessary replace.

  13. Check operation of heating resistor.

  14. Carry out test operations.

  15. Check operation of torque clutch.

  16. Check mechanical position indicator.

  17. Check drive operating time and compare with reference values from commissioning.

  18. Open contact compartment and clean internal insulator surface thoroughly.   2) 3)

Remarks:
   1)  Requires opening of gas compartment.
   2)  Random check on earthling switch with max operating duty further check only 
        on negative result.
   3)  Parties of arc and grease on contact counter, contact are to be removed with sort cloth, 
        no greasing on the tip of the moving contact, very small grease on fixed contact
