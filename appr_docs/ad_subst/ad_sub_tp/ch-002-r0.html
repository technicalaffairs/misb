Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks I1, which must be performed every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • Charger must be isolated.

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes goggles, and rubber glove.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital DC voltmeter

  • Blower or vacuum cleaner.

  • Small screw driver.

  • Fine clothe.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the dirt and dust which accumulated.

  2. Clean an insulating parts with a soft, dry linen cloth

  3. Check lightening of bus bar connection.

  4. Check the earth cuires are firmly fastened.

  5. Check the prints are fully instead and properly fixed.

  6. Check auxiliary circuit connection on devices and terminal strips for tight fit

  7. Check with the test booton whether all lamps are in working condition.

  8. Check the data measured referring to the battery charger data sheet:
      - AC Input Voltage 3x380V, Frequency 50HZ, Max current 25A
      - Float charge 252V, Equalizing Charge 270V, Max Current 100A, Correct the adjustment. If necessary.

  9. Check battery voltage and charging current in order to recognize any irregularity in the performale of battery and charger, correct the adjustment. If necessary.

  10. Adjustment of current limiting:
      - The change is to be switched to “Boost Change”
      - The current is to be set to the rated value
      - The trim potentiometer “r5” is to be toured into clockwise until the currant starts to respond.

  11. Adjustment of Float Charge:
      - Push button (normal IU) is to be pressed.
      - Switching the control circuit to Float Change operation.
      - Turn the trim potentiometer “r3” of the voltage control unit (type Rz) to adjustment the float charge.

  12. Adjustment of equalizing change:
      - Adjust the float charge first
      - Adjustment the Float Change by trim-potentiometer.

  13. Check the charger for abnormal noise and smell.

  14. Make sure that the maximum peak local doesn’t exceed 100 A

  15. Make sure that the air intake and out let of the cubicle breath freely.

Designation: R2z basic unit with

  1. Circuit for voltage control (floating charge)
  2. Additional circuit for equalizing charge with automatic transfer
  3. With provision for manually controlled boost charge.
  4. With additional circuitry for time-delayed return from
  5. Equalizing Charge or Boost Charge to float charge operation.

Mechanical Construction

Plug-in card 125 x 100 mm with 31 pole connector, DIN 41617.
