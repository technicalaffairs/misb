Introduction

This procedure outlines the checklist I1, which must be performed on an annual bases as indicated Y1 in the above titled block. The first draft initiated by eng. Mostafa Abd_Alla Beni Suef East 220kv Substation and approved at 15 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued.

  • Isolator switch must be isolated and grounded.

  • Wear proper safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc".

  • Change remote switch to local position. 

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc".

  • Blower, tool set, Vaseline, and chlorate solvent

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Clean interior of the control panel to remove any accumulated dust and dirt.

  2. Perform operation check both from remote and local control.

  3. Quarterly operation (two 0-C cycles)

  4. Clean porcelain insulators.

  5. Cleaning the electrical contacts with a chlorate solvent followed by Vaseline coating.

  6. Check control wiring for losses.

  7. Tighten all electrical connections.

  8. Check all ground connections for continuity.

  9. Check the main electrical connections for any losses bolts at the terminals.

  10. Check on/of indications.
