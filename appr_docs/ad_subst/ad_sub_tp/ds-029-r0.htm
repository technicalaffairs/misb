Introduction

This document outlines the checklist I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block. The first draft initiated by eng Ahmed Ismaiel Zain El_Abdeen, Bany Sowaif West 132kv Substation, ME zone

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Portable grounds must be applied on both sides of the work area

  • The complete bay must be isolated and safety fence (rope) with caution marks around the work area,

  • Appropriate personal protective equipment must be work i.e. helmet and safety shoes

  • Fall protection equipment (safety belt / harness) must be used while working a loft

Tools and Equipments

  • Plastic brushes,

  • Fiber glass extension ladder,

  • Complete set of substation hand tools,

  • Course cloth (burlap) for cleaning insulators,

  • Lubricating grease,

  • Air blower.

Work to be carried out 

  1. Inspect the insulators if they show dents on the porcelain or cracks on the surface,

  2. Clean the insulator surface from dust, dirt or any deposits use pure benzene,

  3. If the contact surfaces show traces of burning replace such components by a new one,

  4. Check the contact pressure and adjust if required,

  5. Tighten the bolts and the nuts on the disconnect switch carrying conductors and parts,

  6. Check  all the mechauism for the proper operation,

  7. Check the grounding of the disconnect switch and operating mechanism,

  8. Coat with grease all friction parts of gears and transmissions,

  9. In cas of manual operation of the grounding blades engage them smoothly, Use a handle made of pipe 40 (1.5 inch) about 800 mm long,

  10. Make several trial switching on and off to check the disconnect main and grounding blades for correctly dropping into contact.
