Introduction

This document out lines the Equipment Minor Inspection Procedure P1, which must be performed from two to three years as indicated by Y2:3 in the title block for the minimum oil circuit breaker:
- Rated Voltage: 66kv    - Rated current: 2500A   - Short circuit current: 3l.5KA/1sec 

The first draft initiated by eng. Samer Ayad at Sabtia I 220kv Cairo.

Safety Precautions

  • CB must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Ensure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the CB.

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area, safety around the work place.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses, use only fiberglass ladders,

  • Isolate the DC Control voltage.

Environmental Precautions

·        Don’t burn cloth. Used in cleaning but collect and send to the dump for proper disposal.

Tools and Equipment

  • Spanner for arcing tip, ring spanner, fork spanner size 19, gasket to ventilating lid,

  • A complete set of tools, tools for checking dash pot, clamping device,

  • Hydraulic oil  if needed, silicon grease, cleaning cloth, blower,

  • Test mandrel for arc control device.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Drain approximately 2 liters sump oil through oil tap (18) in humid and hot humid atmosphere conditions,

  2. Subsequently, drain oil sample from breaker pole and test according to specification:
    - The gap 2.5 mm
    -     30 kv-rms. Clean oil. -     25 kv-rms. Oil in CB     - 15 kv-rms. Used oil.
    - 10 kv rms  limits for satisfactory operation of the CB.

  1. Remove ventilating lid by turning spanner,

  2. Top up with fresh clean oil,

  3. Smear gasket to ventilating lid (38) with silicon grease,

  4. Replace ventilating lid.
