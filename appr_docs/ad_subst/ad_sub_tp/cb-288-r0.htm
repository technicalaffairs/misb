Work to be Carried Out

  1. Read gas pressure on pressure gauge 18 figure 1, and convert it at the temperature 20oC to the SF6 gas pressure-temperature characteristic curve figure 2.

  2. Record the gas pressure, temperature and converted gas pressure. If SF6 leak shall be detected supply SF6 before pressure drops to the alarm pressure.

  3. Check and record number of operation at operating counter.

  4. Check indicators showing breaker and closing spring conditions correctly, that is, on normal service condition, the open-closed indicator shows closed and charged-discharged indicator shows charged.

  5. Check for signs of abnormal noises and contamination of insulators.
