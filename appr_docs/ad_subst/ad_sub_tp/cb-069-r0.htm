Introduction

This document out lines the major maintenance procedures P2, which must be performed from six to eight years as indicated by Y6:8 in the title block for the minimum "low" oil circuit breaker:

- Rated Voltage: 66KV    - Rated current: 2500 A      - Short circuit current: 3l. 5 KA /1sec

The first draft initiated by eng. Samer Ayad at Sabtia I Cairo

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • CB must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses …

  • Control Power disconnected.

Tools and Equipment

  • Silicon grease, Gasket to ventilating – lid, A complete set of tools.

  • Hydraulic oil  if needed. Cleaning cloth, and air blower.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Drain approximately 2 litter sump-oil through oil tap (18) in continental climate.

  2. Drain approximately 2 litter sump-oil through oil tap (18) in humid and hot humid atmosphere conditions.

  3. Drain oil sample from breaker pole and test according to specification:
    - The gap 2.5 mm.
    -     30 kV-rms. Clean oil
    - 25 kV-rms. Oil in CB
    - 15 kV-rms. Used oil.
    - 10 kV – limits for satisfactory operation of the CB

  4.  Top up with fresh clean oil.

  5. Smear gasket to ventilating – lid (38) with silicon grease. 

  6. Replace ventilating lid.
