Introduction

This document lists the Equipment Inspection I2, which must be performed every week as indicated by M1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Repeat steps of weekly inspection MPIS doc. no. CP-001-r0

  2. Check air filter cartridge, if it is dirty, blow the cartridge out with compressed air from the inside outwards, if paper elements in cartridge are damaged, install a new filter cartridge

  3. Ensure that filter cartridge is properly seated when filter housing is re-assembled

  4. Check oil level in compressor crank case, but only with the compressor at rest, the oil level should never fall below the sight-glass

  5. Check for any unusual noises or vibrations

  6. Check for air leaks in piping and pipe connections

  7. Read and record gauge pressures and compressor running time indicator

  8. Clean compressor room floor
