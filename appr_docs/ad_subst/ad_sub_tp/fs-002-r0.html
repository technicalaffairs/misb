Introduction          

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued; the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes.... etc

  • Transformer outage is required.

  • After the functioning test of the system, make sure that the compressor is isolated before refilling the tank again with water.

  • Water storage tank and air supply lines must be vented to atmosphere.

  • Before commencing any work on the compressor, it must be disconnected from the mains. If the compressor 
    has an air receiver, all pressure must be completely released.

  • Wear appropriate breathing apparatus and maintain adequate ventilation while performing steps 6 & 7.

Tools and Equipment

  •  Safety helmet, safety shoes, and breathing mask.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Perform weekly inspection as per MPIS document No. FS-001-r0

  2. Perform a functional test of the deluge system by manually pulling the pin at the solenoid valve.

  3. Observe the discharging of water from all the spraying nozzles during the test.

  4. Make sure that the storage tank is completely empty after the test by opening the drain valve on the bottom of the storage tank.

  5. Isolate the air compressor and make sure that the pressure in the storage tank and in the air supply lines is completely discharge

  6. Open the man hole of the tank and inspect the inside, clean any rust found and paint if required.

  7. Paint the piping system if required. Be careful of painting the nozzles.

  8. Reset the deluge valve. Make sure to check the seal around the clapper, replace if found deteriorated.

  9. Refill the tank with water:
    - Open the supply valve in the suction line
    - Make sure the gate valve in the filling line to the tank is open
    - Open the two hand valves on either side of the sight glass
    - Switch on the water pump
    - Stop the pump when the water level reaches the mark in the middle of the sight glass.

Air Compressor Maintenance

  1. Oil change should be done after 50 working hours. While performing this step, observe the following environmental recommendations:
    - Do not throw used oil into the sewage system, collect it in tight containers and return it to
       the vendor for recycling.
    - Avoid spilling oil on the ground, this can eventually cause serious damage to the ground water system.

  2. Wash out the compressor block with new oil before refilling it with new oil also Clean the sight glass. While performing this step, observe the environmental recommendations described in the previous step.

  3. The non-return valve over the air receiver should be cleaned after approximately 1000 working hours (pressure of air receiver must be completely released) the pressure filter should be cleaned after 100 working hours.

  4. Clean the contacts of electric pressure switches.

  5. Clean suction and pressure valves.

  6. Examine all parts subject to wear and replace if required.

  7. Clean pressure pipes and coolers regularly from dust and dirt.

  8. Check the efficiency of the safety valve regularly.
