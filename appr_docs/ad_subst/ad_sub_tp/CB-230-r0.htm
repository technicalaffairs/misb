Introduction

These procedure details the steps required to completely measure openning and closing times for one break per pole circuit breaker using CBT 400 breaker Test Set, every 6 months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

Note : This step by step instruction is for CBT 400 breaker analyzer Test Set (Hathaway-England ).

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified testing staff should carry the suitable safety categories.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Circuit breaker must be fully isolated.

  • Circuit breaker must be properly earthed on both sides of it, using earthing switch if present and temporary local earthings.

Tools and Equipment

  • CBT 400 test set unit (Hathaway, England)

  • Test leads.

  • Voltmeter.

Work to be Carried Out

Circuit Breaker

  1. Clean the bushings of each phase of the breaker (if necessary).

  2. Connect your test cables clamps to the three poles of the breaker at points cleaned in step 1.

  3. Check CB auxiliary operating voltage (opening & closing voltage).

 

CBT 400 Test Set

  1. Connect the other terminals of the cables to points A1-Com ( phase R) , A2-Com
    (phase S) & A3-Com (phase T).

  2. Connect the two terminals cable concerned with opening and closing , where one terminal is connected to positive and the other is connected to coil.

Measurement of Closing Time

  1. Check your connections according to the previous steps.

  2. Connect the two terminals of closing cable where one terminal to closing positive and the other is directly to closing coil as shown :

             

  3. Switch the test unit power supply on.

  4. From the initial screen Choose  2. Test.

  5. From the breaker test options screen  Choose  1. Simple test.

  6. Choose 3. Close , then by pressing the key Enter  the device will start measuring.

  7. Record the test results obtained in a table as shown below .

  8. Switch the test unit power supply off.

Measurement of Opening Time

  1. Connect the two terminals of opening cable where one terminal to opening positive and the other is directly to opening coil as shown below.

  2. Switch the test unit power supply on.

  3. From the initial screen Choose  2. Test.

  4. From the breaker test options screen  Choose  1. Simple test.

  5. Choose 2. Open. , then by pressing the key Enter  the device will start measuring.

  6. Record the test results obtained in a table as shown below .

  7. Switch the test unit power supply off.

Note:

  1. Beside open & close test you can do also the following tests:

         open / close        close /open            open /close /open

  2. After finishing the tests :
    Disconnect the earthing switches – if present – or remove the temporary local earthings .     return the CB into service

If one Result is not available this may be due to one of the followings:

  1. The test cable terminal may be disconnected.

  2. One of the clamps is not good fixed.

  3. The two terminals cable of opening and closing is in incorrect position.

  4. There is no negative on the coil.

  5. No paper roll is present on the test unit.
