Introduction

This procedure outlines the checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases Y1 as indicated in the above title block. 

The first draft initiated by eng. Mostafa Abd_Alla Beni Suef East 220kv Substation and approved at 15 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,
  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,
  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses, and harness …
  • Transformer isolation earthing is required.
  • A proper well-fixed ladder should be used for climbing on top of the transformer tank.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety helmet, shoes , and harness. 

  • Cloth for cleaning, blower.

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Repair any leakage.

  2. Remove rust spots and retouch the transformer with rust preventive paint.

  3. Detect unusual noises and vibration.

  4. Clean the porcelain bushing.

  5. Clean the indicator dials.

  6. Tighten the 66kv and 11kv connections.

  7. Tighten the auxiliary connections.

  8. Change the silica gel of air dryer if it is pink or white or regenerate it (the periodicity of this operation could be shorter according to the local climatic conditions).

  9. Check oil level in the dielectric trap of dryer (add if necessary).

  10. Check oil level in conservator (add if necessary).

  11. Take oil sample from the main tank and tapchanger compartment.

  12. Carryout the dielectric test.

  13. Test water content if breakdown voltage is lower than 40kv.

  14. On load tapchanger inspection:a ccording to MR leaflet no. IA95/81

    - After 3 years or after 35000tap changes for Delta connection,

    - 7000 tap changes for Star connection, which one is the earliest, and

  15. Check the setting of each protection relay.

  16. Check the good working of BUCHOLZ relay.

  17. Check protective relay of tap changer.

  18. Check the motor drive of tap changer.

  19. Check the tightness.

  20. Check working of heating resistance.

  21. Check appearance of equipments

  22. Carryout the commutation tests

  23. Test the mechanical and electrical limit switches (BA80/82).

  24. Check air dryer: change the silicagel if it is pink or regenerate it, adjust oil level in the trap of air dryer.

  25. Inspect visually for painting and oil leakage. Adjust oil level if necessary.

  26. Thoroughly clean all porcelain surfaces of all bushings.

  27. Inspect connections of all cooling fans.
