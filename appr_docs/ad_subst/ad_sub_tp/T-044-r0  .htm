Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1, which must be carried out on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the above title block. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 11/5/02 for T1 at First Urban Community 66kv Substation, Cairo zone.

Safety Precaution 

  • Work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety rope with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used.

  • Wear your safety helmet and safety shoes

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower for cleaning purposes, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Wear your breathing mask if you have to use any chemical solvent because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't through oil or grease on ground used, collect it and send to the central chemical labs to recycle it.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

  • Check the sound level and vibration of fans (standard levels 40-60 decibel )

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool sets,

  • Vacuum cleaner

Work to be carried out

  1. Observe the whole state of the transformer to discover if there is any up normal thing such as oil leakage, up normal sound emitting from transformer or from fans, etc ...

  2. Check that the following valves are opened:
             * Conservator valves
             * Radiators valves

  3. Check that the following valves are closed:
             * Filter (lower) and drain valve            * Filter (upper) and filling valve
             * Oil sampling valve                            * Oil drain valve for main tank
             * Oil drain and filling valve for     OLTC

  4. Check the oil level in conservator and its correspondence with the temperature (the part to the main tank of transformer and the part related to the tap changer)

  5. Check the earth links and earthing system.

  6. Check the condition of silica-gel and the oil in the air-dehydrator, change if necessary,

  7. Check the condition of the cooling system and fans.

  8. Observe readings of transformer thermometers for oil and winding temperature
