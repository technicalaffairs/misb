Introduction    

This document lists equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated & tested at 22 Jan 2004 by eng Abdel Rahman Hassan Abdel Rahman Seiody at Cairo North 220kv substation, accepted by eng Ahmed Shelaby Faisal General Director, and approved by eng Samir Saleh; EHV Network Head Sector Cairo zone EETCo.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes .... etc

    •

  • Switch off the CB.

  • The opening mechanism should be secured against unintentional movement.

  • The control voltage supply lines should be disconnected.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Portable air blower.

  • Torque wrenches.

  • Screw driver with extension spec.

  • Filter's Hammer, Cotter pin driver.

  • Benz assembly tool.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Recorded the No. of operation.

  2. Check SF6 gas leakage.

  3. Check the cable connections.

  4. Clean the fingers contact by tighten all loose connection.

  5. Check for operating mechanism (open - close - open)

  6. Lubricate the moving part.

  7. Check the mechanical interlock.

  8. Check screw connections for tightness when tightening loose connections replace washers & tension elements.
