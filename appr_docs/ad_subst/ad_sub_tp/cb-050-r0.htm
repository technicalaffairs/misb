Introduction

This document lists equipment inspection checklist I2, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng Samir Ayad from Sabtia 1, 220kv Substation. This document was updated at 12 Jan 2002 after the 1st approval date 8 May 2000 by adding some drawings and some necessary reference data.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses …

  • Change Remote position to be at local

Tools and Equipment

  • SF6 gas leakage detector, compete set of tools

  • Emergency Light, Blower..

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Read and record SF6 pressure on manometer.     (7 Bar)

  2. Check air pressure on manometer.                     (10 Bar)

  3. Check for gas and air leaks.

  4. Shut off air valves at control cubicle.

  5. Drain compressed air receiver.

  6. Check tightness of screw and pipe couplings.

  7. Check refilling valve function: opened and closed and tightness. (Refilling valve 373 (Y0) figure1)

  8. Open air valves at control cubicle.

  9. Fill the compressed air receiver to 10 bar

  10. Carry out test switching operations,

  11. Change local position to be at Remote.
