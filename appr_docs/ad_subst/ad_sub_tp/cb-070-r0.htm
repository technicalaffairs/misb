Introduction

This document lists the circuit breaker inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the above title block.
 

The first draft initiated by eng. Samir Ayad and tested at 26 Mar 2002 by eng Esam Abdel Razeq El-Shahawy at Sabtia 1, 220kv Substation

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded (the earthing switch connected on both sides of the CB) on both sides,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, safety glasses

Tools and Equipment

  • Gas leaks detector.

  • Complete set of tools.

  • Emergency Light.

Work to be Carried Out

1.      Read and record SF6 pressure on manometer.   (7 bar)

2.       Read and record air pressure on manometer.    (10 bar)

3.      Check for air and gas leaks.
