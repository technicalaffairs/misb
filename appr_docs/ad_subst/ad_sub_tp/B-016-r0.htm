Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1, which must be carried out on a weekly bases as indicated in the above title block by W1. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 8 April 2002 for battery no. 1 at Sheraton El Matar 66kv Substation, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category, 

  • Caution marks surrounding the work area must be used,

  • No smoking in battery room or any things leads to a fire

  • Using safety equipments, safety goggles, rubber gloves, safety shoes, and rubber apron is necessary 

Environmental Precautions

  • Wear your breathing mask if you have to use any chemical materials because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipments, goggles, gloves, and apron

  • Pieces of cloth

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check terminals of battery

  2. Clean the battery of dirt and dust

  3. Clean the battery of any deposits of salt using warm water, rags and brush

  4. Check ventilation system of battery room (must be checked daily)

  5. Clean the floor of battery room by water
