Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block. The first draft initiated by eng. Emad Farouk Mostafa at Kattamia substation 220kv

Safety Precaution 

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding on all its sides is required.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety belt when cleaning suspension Insulator.

  • There is a danger falling into the containment pit when working near large transformer.

  • It is recommended to use only fiberglass (PVC) ladders when working in the switchyard.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and tool set

  • Blower, voltmeter, screwdriver set, and fiberglass PVC ladder.

  • Voltage indicator,

  • Portable earth for discharging,

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes, Silicagel,

Environmental Recommendation

  • Before starting the cleaning of all porcelain surfaces of all transformer wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning. Collect it and send it to the dump.

  • Fix any oil leaks to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil.

  • Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impact on the environment, mainly on soil and 
    ground water and eventually on the ecosystem."It takes only I liter of Hydrocarbon spilled 
    in an aquifer to render 1,000,000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption

Work to be carried out

Mechanical Check

  1. Check all gasket surfaces on the transformer and on-load tap changer for tightness,

  2. Make sure that the oil connection on the cover of the transformer are tightened,

  3. Make sure that oil drain devices on the transformer tank are tightened,

  4. Check of the insulating oil level in the conservator for transformer oil.

  5. Inspect the oil level in the oil level gauge.

  6. Check the oil temperature on the oil level gauge.
    The oil level in the conservator must be in accordance with the rated hight depending on working temperature.

  7. Check the breather of the conservator for transformer oil.

  8. Through by clean all porcelain surfaces on all bushings.

  9. Check for correct oil level in all transformer bushings.

  10. Check the fan control.

  11. Set the selectro switch - in the cabinet for fan control - to position manual operation. this way setting the fans to operations.

  12. Check each individual fan, if its sense of rotation is correct, the air stream ought to be directed aganist the radiators.

  13. Read just the contacts of the thermal image relay:
                   Contact fan ON = + 60ºC
                   Contact fan Off = + 50 ºC

  14. Check the colour of the drying agent in the hreather (Silicagel) must be blue.

  15. Check the oil level in the vessel for oil seal.

  16. Readjust the contact of the thermameter for oil temp & winding trmp indicators according the following actuating temp values:
                     For oil temp contact warning          = + 70ºC
                                         contact tripping          = + 90ºC
                     For winding temp contact warning  = + 75ºC
                                                 contact tripping  = + 95ºC

  17. Check buchholz relay for transformer, it must not contain any air accumulation.

  18. Function test of the warning-and tripping float according to the service instruction for buchhloz-relay.

 

 

Electrical Check

  1. Check the H.V & L.V connection according to service instruction of the cable function box.

  2. Check HV, LV neutral connection, for correct phase connection & good contact.

  3. Check HV & LV earthing insulator.

  4. Check HV & LV  lighting arrestor.

  5. Check the connecting cables coming from the earthing insulator for good contact.

  6. Check short circuit connections of bushing and the earthing connection for good contact.

  7. Check the earthing connection between tank and cover, conservator and cover, terminal box, on load tap changer and tank.

  8. Check the on load tap changer and set it to operation manual for each step.

  9. Check the motor drive and set it to operation.

  10. Check the oil level in the conservator..

  11. Take an oil sample from the transform through the oil drain respectively.

  12. Check the breakdoun voltage (must be at least 45kv).

  13. Check the oil in the on - load tap changer same as the oil of transformer.

  14. Place transformer back into normal operating condition.
