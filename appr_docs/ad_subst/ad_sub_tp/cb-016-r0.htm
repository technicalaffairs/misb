Introduction

This procedure details equipment inspection checklist I2, which must be performed every two years Y2. In addition to the procedures listed, it is expected the checks, which are done more frequently, would also be completed at this time.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Mageed Mahmoud, field tested at 22 Sep 98, Zeniane substation, Cairo zone, approved at 23 Nov 98 and the document layout updated at 4 Feb 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area, safety around the work place.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses, use only fiberglass ladders,

  • Isolate the breaker and ground it.

  • Proper well-fixed ladder should be used for climbing on the breaker.

  • Isolate the DC voltage.

  • Change the operation position from remote to local.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set, rag and air blower,

  • Dew point, Air content and acidity equipment,

  • Contact resistance equipment, and Breathing mask.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Vacuum the interior of the circuit breaker control panel to remove all accumulated dust and dirt.

  2. Clean panel exterior, bushings bus bars, and connectors for dust and dirt.

  3. Check control wiring for loose connections and any signs of overheating and tighten all connections as required.

  4. Check all the bolts of the main connectors at the circuit breaker terminals for looseness.

  5. Tighten all loose bolts of the connection as required make note of any signs of overheating.

  6. Check and tighten all ground connections as required repair any damaged ground connections.

  7. Check control devices such as relays limit switches ... etc. in control panel for proper operation, repair or replace faulty devices, check hydraulic system for proper operation including motor, motor starter and control circuit, replace blown fuses with the same type and rating.

  8. Check the operation mechanism of the circuit breaker inspect for loose bolts must or linkage. (Tighten or replace parts as required)

  9. Check gas dew point, according to MPIS document no. TD-SS-004-r0.

  10. Measure break and make time using time equipment.

  11. Measure contact resistance according to MPIS document no. CB-SS-004-r0.

  12. Measure earth resistance of circuit breaker.

  13. Check air content, acidity content of SF6 gas (MPIS document no. TD-SS-004-r0).

  14. Check for gas leak of the circuit breaker the gas gauge.

  15. Check the circuit breaker indications and correct its position if it is necessary.

  16. Check the cabinet heaters.

  17. Check the close springs charged indicator.

  18. Check the trip and close springs visually make note any remarks.

  19. Through the operators check the circuit breaker functions from remote and local.

  20. Check the door gasket.

  21. Check oil leak of hydraulic pump and also oil level, and Fix any oil leaks to the ground.

  22. Check the operation counter of circuit breaker.

  23. Check closing spring recharge time.

  24. Check the trip buffer dashpot (oil level).

  25. Check the condition and setting of operating mechanism.
