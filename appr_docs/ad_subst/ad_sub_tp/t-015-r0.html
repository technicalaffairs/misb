Introduction

        This document lists the inspection checks I1, which must be performed monthly as indicated by M1 in the title block.

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounded the work area is required.

  • Transformer isolation and earthing is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety harness.

  • There is a danger of falling into containment pit when working near large power transformers.

  • A proper well-fixed ladder should be used for climbing on top of the transformer tank.

Tools and Equipment

  • Fiberglass ladder, dielectric strength oil tester,

  • Voltmeter, complete set of maintenance hand tools.

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety harness.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check cooler valves (open)

  2. Check radiator valves (open)

  3. Check conservator valves (open).

  4. Check buchloz relay valve (open).

  5. Check radiator bank main valves (open).

  6. Check oil drain valve (close).

  7. Check oil filling valve (close).

  8. Check oil sampling valves (close).

  9. Check gas sampling valve (close).

  10. Check buchloz relay by-pass valve (close).

  11. Check air bleed plugs (close).

  12. Check oil level tank.

  13. Check oil level conservator.

  14. Check oil level in buchloz relay.

  15. Check oil level in cable connection chamber.

  16. Check tank ground.

  17. Check fan/motors ground.

  18. Check control cabinet ground.

  19. Check lightning arreestors ground.

  20. Check fan on 60 oC in oil temperature gauge and off at 50oC

  21. Check fan alarm 90 oC in oil temperature gauge.

  22. Check fan trip 110 oC in oil temperature gauge.

  23. Check fan on 83  oC in winding temperature gauge and off at 50oC

  24. Check alarm 108 oC in winding temperature gauge.

  25. Check trip 123 oC in winding temperature gauge.

  26. Make air bleeding from coolers.

  27. Make air bleeding from radiators.

  28. Make air bleeding from piping.

  29. Make air bleeding from Pressure relief.

  30. Make air bleeding from buchholz relay.

  31. Make air bleeding from cable connection chamber.

  32. Check color bank radiator.

  33. Check fans operation.

  34. Check the control cabinet connections.

  35. Check the direction of rotation of fans.

  36. Lubricate fan bearing or change bearing.

  37. Check the terminal boxes connection.

  38. Check silica-gel breather and replace if the color change.

  39. Clean the cooler.

  40. Clean the radiators.

  41. Clean the bushings.

  42. Clean the cable connection chamber.

  43. Clean the control cabinet.

  44. Clean the equipment.
