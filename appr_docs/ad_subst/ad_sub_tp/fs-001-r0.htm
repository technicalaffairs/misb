Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I2, which must be performed weely as indicated by W1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued.

  • The fire detection piping is pressurized with air.

  • Do not accidentally break the glass bulb in the detector head.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check for correct air pressure in the water storage tank.    
    (min. pressure is 12 bars, max. pressure is 15 bars)

  2. Check for correct water level in the storage tank. The correct level is in the middle of the sight (inspection) glass. You must open the two hand shut-off valves on the water level indicator. It is very important to close these valves after inspection.

  3. If the water level in the storage tank is low, open the gate valve in the water supply line to the tank and switch on the tank filling pump until the water level reaches the middle of the sight glass.

  4. After the filling is completed, make sure the gate valve in the water supply line to the tank is closed.

  5. Check indicating lights in the control panel. Replace any burned lamps.

Air Compressor Maintenance

  1. Momentarily open the drain valve on the bottom of the air receiver tank to discharge any accumulated water.

  2. The suction filters must be checked and cleaned if required.

  3. Check for any air leaks in the piping system, if any leaks are detected, isolate the system and repair leak as soon as possible.
