Introduction

This document out lists the inspection I3 , to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

The first draft initiated by eng Gamal Sayed Soliman tested at 17 Sep 02, at Assuit 500 ME,

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Air compressor must be isolated and the main air tank must be isolated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the values of pressure gauges.
  2. Drain the main air tank.
  3. Check the frequancy of the automatic operation of the air compressor unit by checking the air leakage.
  4. Check the oil level of the compressor.
  5. Check the indication of compressor's first stage pressure gauge.
  6. Calibbration of the pressure gauge.
  7. Check the performance of pressure switchs and safety valves.
  8. Draining of pipe line.
  9. Check the pressure reducing device and safety valves
  10. Check the control panel.
  11. Check the air leakage and  the air compressor unit.
