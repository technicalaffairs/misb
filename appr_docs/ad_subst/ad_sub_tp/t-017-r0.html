Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks I3, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes safety harness

  • Transformer isolation and earthing is required.

  • There is a danger of falling into containment pit when working near large power transformers.

  • A proper well-fixed ladder should be used for climbing on top of the transformer tank.

Tools and Equipment

  • Fiberglass ladder dielectric strength oil tester voltmeter complete set of maintenance hand tools.

Work to be Carried Out 

  1.    Repeat steps of 3 month inspection MPIS Document no. T-016-r0

  2.    Check dielectric strength for insulating oil.

  3.    Check bukhloz relay contact.

  4.    Check bukhloz relay float action.

  5.    Check bukhloz Gas content (analysis).

  6.    Check oil level indicator with alarm contacts.

  7.    Check oil level indicator connecting link.

  8.    Check oil level indicator sight gauge.

  9.    Check control wiring cable shield ground.

  10.    Check contact breakers in control cabinet.

  11.    Check connections cooler controls in control cabinet.

  12.    Check heater in control cabinet.

  13.    Fan switch and control.

  14.    Check silica-gel breather nozzle

  15.    Check silica-gel breather gasket.

  16.    Check the corrosion and paint coat.

  17.    Check the corrosion for unpainted metal parts.

  18.    Check insulating oil power factor.

  19.    Check insulating oil interfaces tension.

  20.    Check the sludge in insulating oils.

  21.    Check moisture content in insulting oil.

  22.    Check Saponifaction number in insulating oil.

  23.    Check gas analysis in insulating oil.

  24.    Check oil levels in thermometer pocket.

  25.    Check oil thermometer contacts.

  26.    Check oil thermometer calibration.

  27.    Check oil level in pocket for oil thermometer.

  28.    Check the current transfer for winding temp indicator.

  29.    Check the oil level in pocket for winding temp indicator.

  30.    Check the auxiliary CT (shunt) for winding temp indicator.

  31.    Check the thermostat contacts for heaters

  32.    Check the thermostat calibration and adjustment if necessary.
