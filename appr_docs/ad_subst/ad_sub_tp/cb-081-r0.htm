Introduction

This document outlines the checklist I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block. The first draft initiated by eng Ahmed Ismaiel Zain El_Abdeen, Bany Sowaif West 132kv Substation, ME zone

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area, safety around the work place.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses …

  • Change Remote position to Local,

  • Shut off air valves,

  • Remove DC plugs for each pole,

  • Discharge air from tank,

    •

    Tools and Equipment

    • Silicon grease, cleaning and degreasing agent (alcohol)

    • Blower, rags, compete set of tools, Emergency Light, Gas detector,

    • Dew point, acidity and air content equipment.

    Work to be Carried Out

    1. Check visually all parts of the circuit breaker.

    2. Check AC, DC power supply sources.

    3. Clean all outside compartment of circuit breaker from dust and other things using blower.

    4. Retighten all bolts and earth connection parts if necessary

    5. Check visually the fixation of circuit breaker

    6. Check SF6 pressure on manometer.

    7. Check for gas and air leaks.

    8. Check air pressure on manometer.

    9. Check air pressure of motor compressor as will as time of operation

    10. Change oil in the motor compressor.

    11. Check the end positions of the circuit breaker

    12. Check the auxiliary current circuits, tightening of clamps and series terminals.

    13. Check the operation of signaling switches and correct operation of current circuits

    14. Tight coupling levers of signaling switch.

    15. Check the operation of pressure vacuum gauge and safety valve on the air reservoir

    16. Check the correct operation of all coils and signal.

    17. Grease all joint gears on the coupling levers, axles, and bearings with silicon grease.

    18. Clean the compressed air reservoir

    19. Inspect screwed joints and transmissions and tighten if necessary,

    20. Inspect, clean, and if necessary replace the movable of closing and opening valves (pneumatic valve, closing valve, closing pressure valve, back pressure valve, main piston, piston, and impact needle, and clean thoroughly filter of the net). and reassemble those parts carefully.

    21. Measure SF6 gas dew point.
