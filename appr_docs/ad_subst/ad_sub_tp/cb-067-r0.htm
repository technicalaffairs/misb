Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I2, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Samer Ayad at Sabtia I Cairo, tested at 7/9/02

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • CB must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Insure that the earthing switch must be connected on both sides of the CB.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area, safety around the work place.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses …

  • Control Power disconnected.

Tools and Equipment

  • Blower, safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses

  • A complete set of tools, Hydraulic oil if needed, cleaning cloth.

Work to be carried out

  1. Change the oil after 5 times of an automatic break due to short circuit.

  2. Drain the oil from the CB.

  3. Clean the arc chamber with a new oil.

  4. Refilling the arc chamber with a new oil observe for the oil level.

  5. Lubricate the moving contact of the mechanism operation.

  6. Clean porcelain surfaces.
