Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I2, which must be performed for Becker compressor type SV 250 max pressure150 discharge every four months as indicated by M4 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, helmet, and safety glasses

Work to be Carried Out

1.

Check air filter cartridge, if it is dirty, blow the cartridge out with compressed air from the inside outwards, if paper elements in cartridge are damaged, install a new filter cartridge.

2.

Ensure that filter cartridge is properly seated when Hitler housing is re-assembled.

3.

Check oil level in compressor crack case, but only with the compressor at rest. The oil level should never fall below the sight- glass.

4.

Check for any unusual noises or vibrations.

5.

Check for air leaks in piping and pipe connections.

6.

Read and record gauge pressures and compressor running time indicator.

7.

Clean compressor room floor.
