Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I2, which must be performed on a weekly bases as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by: eng. Abdel Aziz El Bagoury, tested by eng. Doaa Anis, revised by quality control eng. Adel Abdel Qader El Nagar at EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Mahmoud Saker and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talkha PS

Safety Precaution 

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding on all its sides is required.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes,

  • There is a danger of falling into the oil containment pit when working near large power transformers.

  • There is a danger of  falling off from the top of transformer

  • Wear safety belt when cleaning Insulators.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and complete tool set,

  • Voltage indicator, Portable earth for discharging,

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes,

  • Brushes, Blower, fiberglass PVC ladder.

Environmental Recommendation

  • Before starting the cleaning of all porcelain surfaces of all transformer wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

  • Fix any oil leaks to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil.

  • Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impact on the environment, mainly on soil and 
    ground water and eventually on the ecosystem."It takes only I liter of Hydrocarbon spilled 
    in an aquifer to render 1,000,000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption
