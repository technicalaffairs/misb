Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, to be performed every three years Y3 or after 1000 cycles.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Abd Ell Hady Eskander, tested by eng. Mohamed Abd El Dayem, approved by eng. Hamdy Radwan Aly general director, accepted by eng. Motawee Hassan Aly Head Sector of El Shawashna SS, certified by eng. Ahmed Said Abd El Aziz quality control general director, MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated and properly earthed at both sides, it should be in ON position.

  • Disconnect DC Voltage

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded on both sides

  • Control power disconnected

  • Isolate the DC voltage

  • Change remote switch to local operation position

  • Proper well fixed ladder should be used for climbing on the breaker

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump .

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes, .... etc"

  • Complete toolset, scratch, electrical grease, rag, and air blower

  • Gas detector, new bottle sf6 gas and filling tool.

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check that nuts and bolts are properly tightened

  2. Check SF6 pressure on density switch (add SF6 if it is necessary) reference value 7 bar at 20ºC

  3. Monitor the density switch pressure thresholds; replace the density switches if it is out of tolerance
    As a precaution, before replacing the density switches, we recommend you to repeat the check in a room with a temperature stabilized at around 20 ºC

  4. Check that the circuit breaker is operated several times per year, if this is not the case, initiate a closing and opening cycle in local and remote mode and, if the option has been fitted, under voltage tripping

Tests after 3 years or 1000 cycles

  1. Check the density switch pressure thresholds and test the operation of the density switch micro-switches

  2. Check the operating mechanism charging time
    Initiate "0" and "1" operations using the control channels :
    local and remote control and under voltage tripping
