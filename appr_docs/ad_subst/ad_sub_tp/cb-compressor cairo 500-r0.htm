Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1 (visual check + maintenance), to be performed every ten years or > 1000 operations as indicated by Y10 in the title block The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel-Mohsen Hanfy at Cairo 500 substation ME zone.and tested at

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Procedures should be done by trained maintenance staff or by AEG specialist

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your appreciated safety tools: helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.... etc

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Cleaning material, and tools set.

  • Appreciated safety tools: helmet, shoes .... etc

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check of compressor (if applicable) (ICU , CCI) with regard to:
       - Delivery volume
       - Delivery time
       - Working point of pressure maintaining valve
       - Set up pressure of safety valve
       - Oil consumption (if applicable)
       - Oil change (if applicable)
       - Replacement of air filter

  2. Check of compressor motor with regard to
       - Noise of bearing
       - Wear of carbon brush (DC-motor)
       - Condition of collector
       - Fastening of electrical connections
       - Power consumption at rated pressure and rated voltage

  3. Check of dryblock (if applicable) (ICU , CCI) with regard to:
       - Operation of control valves
       - Operation of condensate drain valve
       - Operation of heater element
       - Replacement of drying agent
       - Measurement of regenerating air
       - Measurement of dew point of delivered compressed.
