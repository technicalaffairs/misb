Introduction

This procedure details the items P1, which are checked every 5 years Y5. In addition to the procedures listed, it is expected the checks, which are done more frequently, would also be completed at this time.

Safety Precautions
  • Work permit must be issued.
  • The angle isolator's switch must be isolated and earthling at the two sides
  • Isolate the DC voltage

Tools and Equipment
  • Set of common keys, air blowers, screw driver, cleaning material (rags, cloth), timing equipment, Indicator glass
    (end scope), cleaning fluid, electrical grease

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check proper functioning of the motor

  2. Check transmission and auxiliary switches in the event of a failure or fault take the following steps:

  3. Check dismantling and greasing the transmission unit. Close the earthling switch and remove plug 6011

  4. Disconnect the wiring to the motor 6215 at the secondary terminal block 6226 of the plug

  5. Decouple the rod 6218 from the drive-actuating arm. Don't alater the length of the rod

  6. Loosen the transmission unit 6216 at the screws 6229

  7. Push out the pin 6227

  8. Pull of the actuator arm 6217

  9. Dismantle the transmission unit

  10. Loosen the screw 6013 and remove the com disc 6012

  11. Lift of the top transmission plate 6014 after loosening the 6015 and retaining ring 3989

  12. Following the work plan clean and grease the transmission

  13. Reassemble in the reverse sequence
