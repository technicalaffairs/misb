Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I2, which must be performed yearly as indicated by Y1 in the title block The first draft initiated by Mr. Helmut Canadian SISP, S/S specialist, and first approval at 02 Sep 1996.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  •  Isolation and proper earthing is required

  • Wear your safety helmet

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the PT stack when cleaning insulators or checking connections

  • Wear your safety belt

Caution: Secondary PT wiring could be energized through back feeds, make sure circuit breaker to secondary circuit is open

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety belt and safety helmet.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. In addition to the 2 months inspection check list, perform the following steps:

  2. Check out the condition of the expansion diaphragm

  3. Open secondary terminal compartment, blow out compartment and check the condition of the weather seal

  4. Check that all wiring terminations are tight and securely fastened

  5. Check the condition of cables or conduits entering the secondary terminal compartment

  6. Check earth bus connections to base of PT for tightness.

  7. Do touch up painting if required.
