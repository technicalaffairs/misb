Introduction

This document outlines the minor and major maintenance Procedure P1, P2 to be performed every five years Y5 and every 10 years Y10 as indicated in the above title block. The first draft initiated by eng. Mostafa Abd_Alla Beni Suef East 220kv Substation and approved at 15 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety equipment.

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded on both sides

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc".

  • Ammeter clamp, recorder multimeter, air pressure reference gauge

  • One set of three complete closing and opening valves, as shown in figure no. 18, with seals 211

  • One set of Allen keys one set of screwdrivers torque wrench clean rags, solvent (e.g. clorotene)

  • Anderol 495 lubricating oil (approx. 0.4 L for compressor).

  • Gasket for plugs 171, 173 fig 17, Seals for container cylinder193 fig 17, seals for condensate drain filter plug 

  • Two sets of 5.5 mm to 36mm spanners, one set of Allen keys, clean rags, and emery cloth,

  • Chronometer, tool Kit (head gasket, 2 compression ring, cylinder gasket, cap gasket, oil scraper ring, complete valve block, air pressure reference gauge)

  • One 20 L gas cylinder for topping up.
