Introduction

This document out lines the inspection check list I1 , which must be performed from month to three month as  indicated by M1:3 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng.Reda Mohamed Elroos , tested by eng. Glal Kamel Salem at 17 March 2003, revised and accepted by general director eng. Foaad Saad Abed elkany , and approved by general director eng.Ahmed Saeed Abed elazez ,Assuit 500kv substation ME zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding on all its sides is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety belt when cleaning suspension Insulator.

  • There is a danger falling into the containment pit when working near large transformer.

  • It is recommended to use only fiberglass (PVC) ladders when working in the switchyard.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and complete tool set,

  • Blower, voltmeter, screwdriver set, and fiberglass PVC ladder.

  • Voltage indicator, Portable earth for discharging,

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes, Silica gel,

Environmental Recommendation

  • Before starting the cleaning of all porcelain surfaces of all transformer wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

  • Fix any oil leaks to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil.

  • Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impact on the environment, mainly on soil and ground water and eventually on the ecosystem, It takes only 1 liter of Hydrocarbon spilled in an aquifer to render 1,000,000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the transformer tank, cover, coolers and oil level indicator glasses.

  2. Clean all porcelain surfaces of all bushings insulators.

  3. Clean the transformer control cabinet.

  4. Check oil leaks.

  5. Check oil level in main tank, and bushings add if required.

  6. Read and record the main oil temperature.

  7. Read and record the the max. winding temperature.

  8. Check all cooling fans for correct operation.

  9. Check the cooling fans for unusual noises.

  10. Check the silica gel breather and change if required.

  11. Check all bolted connections for tightness.

  12. Measure the winding turns ratio ----------- every year.

  13. Measure the winding resistance ----------- every year.

  14. Measure the insulation oil break down voltage test ----------- every year.

  15. Measure the insulation resistance for each winding and for core to ground.

  16. Check all protection devices.

  17. Check the deluge system, transformer protection by water spray.
