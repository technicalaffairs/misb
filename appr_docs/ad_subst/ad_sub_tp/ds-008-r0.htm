Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks I1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required.

  •  Wear your safety helmet, etc.

  • Change remote/local control switch to local position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter, blower, gas detector, buzzer, screwdriver and set of common keys.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check supply voltage.

  2. Check number of switching operations.

  3. Check screw joints of the electrical connections by pulling, retighten if necessary.

  4. Check pull-relief's at cables.

  5. Check mechanical connections at drive or auxiliary switch by visual control far tightness and  wear.   

  6. Check mechanical position indicator.

  7. Clean protective pipe of the position indicator by means of a cleaning a gent containing soap

  8. Grease threaded spindle and pain bearing of  auxiliary switch (41)

  9. Grease right-angle gear (8)

  10. Check functioning of torque clutch rated value of slip friction torque. max 10 Nm, min. 7Nm

  11. Check density of shaft seal in housing cover (9 and 1 or 10) by sniffing.

  12. Check functioning of auxiliary switch contacts. 

  13. Check functioning of heating resistor.

  14. Check contact penetration depth rated valve.
         Max. 138 mm/min.126 mm until touching/separation of contacts stroke of switch rod 177+1-2 mm.

  15. Check wear of annular contacts and switch rod.    1), 2)

  16. Check insulating shaft with threaded spindle (6) and universal joints far damages and grease if necessary

  17. Grease bevel gearing (13).       1), (2                                                                                                                                                  

  18. Execute test switching operations.

  19. Check electrical position signals (position indicator) 

  20. Check running time of drive.

  21. Compare with reference valves from commissioning.

Notes:
             1) Requires opening of the gas compartment.

        2) Random test at equipment having undergone highest load.
