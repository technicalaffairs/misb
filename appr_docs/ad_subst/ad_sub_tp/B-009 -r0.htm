Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves

    • ... etc.

  • Don't smoke in the battery room.

  • Don't place any tools on top of battery cells (Danger of short circuit).

  • All tools will be used must be insulated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set. grease of No-ox-ID

  • Digital voltmeter, plastic bristle brush

  • Hydrometer, Gloves, Vaseline

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect AC supply of battery charger.

  2. Inspect out side battery containers for cracks and leakage of electrolyte.

  3. Inspect contact area of inter connection, lugs, terminal connections, tightening and clean by plastic bristle brush, then coated with a thin film of No - ox - ID grease.

  4. Clean a top of battery containers with clear water and neutralized acid spilt only with a solution of sodium bicarbonate baking soda.

  5. Inspect and record electrolyte level, density for each cell ( level at 1/2" lowered from full mark level, 1.2 - 1.22 ).

  6. Lubricate terminals for cells with a thin film of Vaseline.

  7. Record voltage / cell it should be ( 2.05 ± 0.04 ) at 1. 21 , 77 ºf .

  8. If cell voltage more than 0.04 below min. float voltage put battery under equalize charge.

  9. Record overall voltage of battery it should be 2.05 x No. of cell / battery if it is below put under equalizing.

  10. Replace defective battery.

  11. Measure internal resistance per cell.

  12. Check ventilation and clean battery house.
