Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I1, which must be performed monthly as indicated by M1 in the title block The first draft initiated by Mr. Helmut Canadian SISP, S/S specialist, and first approval at 02 June 1997.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Voltage transformer must be isolated and grounded .

  • Wear safety equipment helmet, shoes, etc.

  • Secondary main circuit breaker must be isolated .

  • Caution marks must be hung on work place .

  • Place Safety rope as fence around work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter, blower, Screwdrivers and Set of common keys.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check P.T foundation to ensure it is in correct vertical position.

  2. Examine the surface of the porcelain for scratchs, chips, flash over burns, and cracks.

  3. Check and retighten if necessary all mounting bolts of voltage transformer .

  4. Check the electrical connection between voltage transformer and low voltage bushing and retighten it if necessary.

  5. Check the oil level on the oil level gauge.

  6. Check for oil leaks.

  7. Check the primary and secondary terminals, and retighten all joints.

  8. Check and clean terminals box inside and out.

  9. Check the operation of the terminal box heater and check thermostat settings .

  10. Check there has been no flash over on air gap for secondary terminals and communication.

  11. Check the communication earthing switch position it is in open position.

  12. Check the terminals box gasket, repair if required.

  13. Clean voltage transformer porcelain.

  14. The main earting connection must be check and retighten if necessary.
