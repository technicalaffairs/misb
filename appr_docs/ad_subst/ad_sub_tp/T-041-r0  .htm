Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I2, which must be carried out on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the above title block.

The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 10 April 02 for T1 at Sheraton El Matar 66kv Substation, Cairo zone.

This document will be implemented whereas the transformer is in service.

Safety Precaution 

  • Work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety rope with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used,

  • Wearing safety helmet, and safety shoes is necessary

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool sets,

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

Work to be carried out

  1. Check conservator valves (open)

  2. Check the oil level in conservator and its correspondence with the temperature

  3. Check radiator valves (open)

  4. Check oil sampling valves (closed)

  5. Check the earthing and earthing system

  6. Check the condition of silica-gel and the oil in the air-dehydrator

  7. Check thermometer recordings

  8. Check the condition of the cooling system and fans

  9. Check the main tank, oil leakage and general condition of it
