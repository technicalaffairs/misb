Introduction

This procedure details the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on a monthly basis as indicated by the M1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Nashwa Hessain Mohamed, tested by eng. Atef Mohamed Mahmoud Abdel Rahman, accepted by general director eng. Samir Mohamed, approved by eng. Ahmed Shelabi Faisal: Head sector of  EHV Cairo zone EETC.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear appropriate personal safety equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, harness

  • Voltage transformer must be isolated and grounded .

  • Secondary main circuit breaker must be isolated .

Tools and Equipment
  • Complete substation maintenance tool set.

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Multimeter, air blower, rags

  • Voltage indicator.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check PT foundation to ensure it is in correct vertical position.

  2. Examine the surface of the porcelain for scratchs, chips, flash over burns, and cracks.

  3. Check and retighten if necessary all mounting bolts of voltage transformer.

  4. Check the oil level on the oil level gauge.

  5. Check for oil leaks.

  6. Check and clean terminals box inside and out.

  7. Check the terminals box gasket, repair if required.

  8. Clean voltage transformer porcelain.

  9. The main earting connection must be check and retighten if necessary.
