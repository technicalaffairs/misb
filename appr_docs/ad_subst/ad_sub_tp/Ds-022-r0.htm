Introduction

This procedure outlines the checklist I1, must be performed on an annually bases as indicated Y2 in the above titled block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mostafa Abd_Alla Beni Suef East 220kv Substation and approved at 15 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belt, breathing mask, and safety glasses ...,etc

  • Isolator switch must be isolated and grounded.

  • Change remote switch to local position. 

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc".

  • Grease, blower

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check control wiring for loose connection and any signs of overheating.

  2. Perform operation check both from remote and local control.

  3. With the command in the "closed" position verify the alignment of the blades in each pole.

  4. Check on/of indications.

  5. Check all ground connections for continuity.

  6. Check opining and clearance limit switches and mechanical steps.

  7. Lubricate the command and transmissions rotation points.

  8. Check the contact condition. Replace it if the parts in a bad condition.

  9. Check the contact dirtiness and replace the grease, removing the remaining one.

  10. Tighten all electrical connections.

  11. Check the insulators for breaking or fault, then clean, in case of adverse atmospheric the cleaning shall be done at shorter intervals.
