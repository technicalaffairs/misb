Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

The technical Specification of the circuit breaker: Rated current =1250 & 2000A, and short circuit current 25KA /3 second.

The first draft initiated by eng. George Phielep and tested by Hassan Abdel Baqy Aly at  25 Feb 2001 at Heliopolice substation,.Cairo zone. The same type at Cairo North 220kv substation. This document updated by eng. Abdel Rahman Hassan Saoudy at 04 Aug. 2003.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ...etc

  • Switch off the CB and withdraw it from the conduit, and ground the cable head.

  • Switch off the DC.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet and safety shoes

  • Portable air blower, Metal brush

  • Set of common, screw drivers, and cleaning material (rag, cloth).

  • Graphite grease, grease for lubricating,

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Examine the external parts of the arc extinguishing chamber.

  2. Check the presence of any abnormal noise.

  3. Check the presence of overheat and flashover at the arc extinguishing chamber after opening cycle.

  4. Check the presence of  surface defects or cracks at the arc extinguishing chamber.

  5. Check the tightening of bolts and nuts.

  6. Check the mechanism of operation

  7. Clean the insulations and check for defects or cracks .

  8. Lubricate the axis.

  9. Check the moving and fixed contacts
