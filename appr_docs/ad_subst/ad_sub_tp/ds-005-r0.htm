Introduction 

This procedure details the minor maintenance activities P1, which are performed on a 4-month basis as indicated by the M4. 
This procedure document is the first level of maintenance performed on this type of disconnect switch.

Safety Precautions
  • Isolate the disconnector switch whether bus bar disconnect switch or cable head disconnect switch or earthing disconnect switch,
  • Put portable earthing on the three phase of the circuit breaker
  • Disconnect supply and motor voltage.
Tools and Equipment
  • Voltage indicator,
  • Fine cloth, burnishing tool, cleaning material,
  • Insulated ladders,
  • Complete tool set (screwdriver set,...etc),
  • Grease, oil
Work to be carried out

  1. Cleaning of the surface of the isolator.

  2. Tightening of all bolted connections.

  3. Control if necessary, touch-up of the varnish.

  4. Cleaning and renewed greasing of the contacts with molykate grease BR2

  5. Under normal conditions maintenance is required only every 10 (ten years)

  6. Under heavy conditions or when the number of operations is increased (after approx. 1000 operations) as well as after heavy short circuit of the disconnector becomes necessary. 

Test operations:    Should the main contacts show the signs of consumption, the complete contact block can be replaced.
