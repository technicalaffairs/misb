Introduction

This procedure details the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on a monthly basis as indicated by the M1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Nashwa Hessain Mohamed, tested by eng. Atef Mohamed Mahmoud Abdel Rahman, accepted by general director eng. Samir Mohamed, approved by eng. Ahmed Shelabi Faisal: Head sector of  EHV Cairo zone EETC.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective safety equipment as safety shoes and helmet

  • The isolator switch must be disconnected and properly earthed.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete substation maintenance tool set

  • Multimeter, air blower, gas detector

  • End scope equipment, moisture tester.

  • Voltage indicator.

Work to be carried out

  1. Complete a visual inspection of all external components of the disconnect switch, during this inspection check for any abnormal conditions.

  2. Clean the mechanism cabinet using the air blower.

  3. Check the enclosure earth cable to ensure it is properly fastened and making good connection

  4. Retighten all coupling bolts and joints on the operating rods.

  5. Complete a second function operation of the switch via LOCAL and MANUAL control, checking that the position indicator is operating correctly, also observe any unusual operating noises.

  6. Check the SF6 gas pressure this value should be 4.6 bar at 20 oC

  7. Check the entire disconnect switch for SF6 gas leak using gas leak detector

  8. Using end scope equipment, check the position of the main contacts after both open and close operations.
