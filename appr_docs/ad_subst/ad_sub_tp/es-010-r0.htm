Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P2 for the earthing switch, which must be performed every ten years or after 2000 operation, as indicated by Y10 in the above title block.

Note: The first draft document initiated by Cairo North substation 220kv, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • All equipment before the earthing switch and after it should be isolated

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Isolate the DC voltage for the earthing switch,.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Before using a blower to clean the control cabinet or when cleaning the insulators, wear breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particles.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks.

  • Tool set of  drivers, screw drivers, cloth, air blowers, cleaning material (rags, cloth) timing equipment,

  • Indicator glass (end scope), cleaning fluid, electrical grease.

Work to be Carried Out

    1.    Check the main contacts,

    2.    Check the current path,

    3.    Close the earthling switch and draw the plug 6011,

    4.    Dismantle the earthling switch from the switchgear,

    5.    Check the fixed contact 6027 of the switch,

    6.    The silver plating of the contact fingers must be at least 2 mm. the silver plating of  the contact tip 6016 and 
           contact tube  6017 must be flawless,

    7.    Contact in good condition are cleaned and greased,

    8.    Remove the earthling switch and tighten the screw using a new O- ring seal 3223,

    9.    Replacing the main current path,

  10.    Dismantle the earthling switch,

  11.    Unscrew the current connection 6047 from the contact enclosure 6044,

  12.    Unscrew the contact casing 6044 from the earthling switch enclosure 6018,

  13.    Unscrew the guide and damping 6046 from the drive housing 7018,

  14.    Draw out the current path 6020 and replace with a new one,

  15.    Clean and grease the current path,

  16.    Fit the new contact enclosure 6044 and tighten the screws using a new O – ring 6021,

  17.    Replace the fixed contact 6022 with a new one,

  18.    Screw earthing switch on to the switchgear flange using a new O – ring and make electrical connection.
