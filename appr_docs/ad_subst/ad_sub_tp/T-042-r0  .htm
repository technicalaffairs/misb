Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I2 that must be carried out every 3 months as indicated by (M3) at the above title block. The first draft has been initiated by eng. El-Sayed Abdel Moneam El-Geddawy and tested at 15/5/02 for T1 at Sheraton El Matar 66kv Substation, Cairo zone.

Safety Precaution 

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable safety category.

    •

  • Safety rope with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, safety belt, breathing mask,

  • Transformer isolation and earthing on all its sides is required.

  • There is a danger of falling into the oil containment pit when working near large power transformers.

  • Be sure that transformer ladder is well fixed for climbing on top of the transformer tank.

  • Switch off from two sides and the 11kv CB out of its location

  • Putting portable earthing is necessary.

  • Switch off the DC supply for the transformer.

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower for cleaning purposes, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Wear your breathing mask if you have to use any chemical solvent because it is dangerous, it will expose lungs to very dangerous and carcinogenic chemicals

  • Don't through oil or grease on ground used, collect it and send to the central chemical labs to recycle it.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

  • Check the sound level of fans /////////////////review environmental document

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool sets

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower

  • Brush and Pieces of soft cloths

Work to be carried out

  1. The works that have be done according to MPIS document # T-041-r0

  2. Clean the bushings

  3. Check oil level in bushing

  4. Check oil leakage on upper surface of the transformer

  5. Check oil level in buchultz relay

  6. Check the oil seals over tapchanger container and tighten bolts

  7. Check the earthing system of the transformer and its accessories (arrestors, earthing resistance, marshaling box, etc)

  8. Check fans to be on at 57 oC in oil temperature gauge      

  9. Check fans alarm at 80 oC in oil temperature gauge        

  10. Check fans trip at 90 oC in oil temperature gauge       

  11. Check fans to be on at 67 oC in winding temperature gauge     

  12. Check  fans alarm at 85 oC in winding temperature gauge      

  13. Check  fans trip at 105 oC in winding temperature gauge          

  14. Cleaning and lubricating the fan bearing

  15. Check the operation and connections of local control cabinet

  16. Check the operation of  control cabinet of tapchanger and clean it

  17. Check tapchanger drive shaft

  18. Clean the radiator and its accessories
