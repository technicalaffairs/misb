Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must performed on an annually bases Y1 as indicated in the above title block. The first draft initiated by eng Ahmed Ismaeel Zen El_Abdeen from Bany Sowaif west 132kv substation, Middle Egypt.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet and safety belt.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, and Rags, and silicagel

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check oil level.

  2. Observe the possible oil leakage, if any leakage is detected tighten earefully the corresponding bolts and nuts.

  3. Take oil sample.

  4. Inspeet the colour in the breather silica gel, when silica gel has grown red the unit should be removed and replaced by a new one, the breather with damp silica gel should be dismantled, silica gel should be changed.

  5. Clean porcelains insulators.
