Introduction 

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every two months as indicated by M2 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by Mr. Helmut Canadian SISP, S/S specialist, first approval at 02 Sep.1996, first review date 03 March 2002, and 2nd review date 15 Jan. 2006. The document was updated by Ataka PS.

Safety Precautions  

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, shoes, ... etc.

  • Current transformer must be isolated and proper grounded is required

  • Wear safety belt because there is danger of falling off the top of the CT stack when cleaning insulators or checking connections.

  • Presence the safety supervisor is required.

    Caution: Secondary CT wiring could be energized through back feeds, make sure secondary circuits is short circuited.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness, Fiberglass ladder, Silicagel

  • Cleaning tools as cloth, rags .... etc

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, and safety shoes ... etc

  • Voltage indicator.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check primary bus connections for tightness
  2. Check for any signs of oil leaks
  3. Check porcelains for chips, cracks, cementing, and fastenings
  4. Thoroughly clean all porcelain surfaces
  5. Check for signs of overheating or flashovers.
