Introduction

This document outlines the equipment checklist P1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block The first draft initiated by eng Emad Farouk Mostafa at Kattamia and tested at 16 June 02 substation 220kv

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off.

  • The HV & LV terminals must be correctly disconnected and grounded.

  • The spring of operating mechanism must be blocked.

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of mechanicals tools, torque wrenches with hex, SF6 gas

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Carryout the procedures stated at the MPIS document # CB-117-r0

  2. Check the screw connections for tightness (Figure 4) the two locknuts 46, which secure the lock 45 to the operating mechanism casing 30. Figure 5, using torque wrench with hex, insert  SW13 (tightening torque 8 Nm)

  3. Check the screw connections for tightness (Figure 4) the seven hexagon-head screws 34, for securing the cover plate 35 and the bearing cap 36 to the operating mechanism casing 30 ( figure 4) to the operating mechanism casing 30. Figure 5, using torque wrench with hex,  insert SW10 (tightening torque 10 Nm) 

  4. Check the screw connections for tightness (Figure 4) the foure cheese-hand screws 47, for securing the operating mechanism 30 to the quenching unit housing 2 ( figure 4), using torque wrench with hex,  insert SW 8 (tightening torque 45 Nm) 

  5. Lubricate the points of the spring operating mechanism for detail lubricate points bevel pinion and bolt (figure 6)

  6. Lubricate the points of power transmission (figure 7)

  7. Lubricate all ball bearings and winding handle and level gears after 5000 operation or every 5 years
