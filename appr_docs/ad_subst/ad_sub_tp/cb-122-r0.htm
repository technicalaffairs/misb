Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block The first draft initiated by eng. Emad Farouk Moustafa, tested 07 July 02 at El-Katamia 220kv substation Cairo zone

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •
  • Wear the appropriate personal protective tools "safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses"
  • The CB switched off and earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker
  • Change selector switch to maintenance position.
    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common keys, blowers, cleaning clothe,
  • Personal protective tools
  • Mechanical oil type ESSO  J13 if needed to added

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the oil level for the circuit breaker mechanism in the main tank through the gauge, add if needed.

  2. Open the box of mechanism and take a look for any oil leake through the system, if found try to prevent it.

  3. Switch off the pump16670 from local panel, and open the stop valve 16613 fig 4/16

  4. Check the oil pressure through pressure gauge, it will be dropped from 34.2MPa to 20MPa slowly, after that it will drop to zero quickly.

  5. Check the indications for each stop of pressure gauge
                      at 29.7 MPa            oil pressure low
                      at 28.2 MPa            close circuit inoperative
                      at 26.6 MPa            trip circuit inoperative

  6. Close the stop value and switch on the pump, the motor pump will start and oil pressed again to normal pressure 34.2MPa.

  7. Observe that the indications will be disappeard one by one.

  8. Check that the motor pump will stop when the pressure of oil reach 34.2MPa.

  9. Check all pipes and screws for any leakage, retight the screws if needed.

  10. Check the heater under hydrogen tank (accumulator) and be sure it work

  11. Check all electric connections, auxilairies circuits

  12. Check the denisty monitor for each step of SF6 gas pressure. ( 7.0bar at 20ºC )

  13. Check supply voltage.

  14. Check the earthed connection.

  15. After finishing observation be sure that the door of mechanism is closed correctly.
