Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance (P2) procedures to be performed every six years (Y6) or after 3000 cycles. The first draft initiated by eng. Mostafa Abd_Alla Beni Suef East 220kv Substation and approved at 15 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded on both sides

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety equipment.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc".

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Measure the operating times.    (Operations requiring the de-energizing of the circuit breaker)

    Opening time at rated voltage          36+5 msec
    Closing time at rated voltage            75+5 msec

    Opening simultaneity difference between poles       3 msec
    Closing simultaneity difference between poles         5 msec
    Drive resetting time at nominal voltage                    10 sec

    If the operating times are out of tolerance, Check:

    - on the electromagnet supply voltage, the electrical circuit resistors.
    - The electrical circuits resistor drive.
    Contact Merlin Gerin in case of mechanical fault.

    Please call MG, to determine the service program for Reconditioning, when reaching one of the following limits that must not exceeded:
     




        - 10.000 CO operations without current,
        -   2.000 CO operations rated current,
        -        20 CO operations at 20KA breaking current,
        -        10 CO operations at 25KA breaking current,
        -          5 CO operations at 31.5KA breaking current,

  2. Monitor the drive setting time.

  3. Lubricate the drive Fig 1.    You must use low temperature grease ISOFELEX L - 152
