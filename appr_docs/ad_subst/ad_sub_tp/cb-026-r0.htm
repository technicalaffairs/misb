Introduction 

The following procedures will be carried out by shift operation staff in substations. This procedure details the activities first level of inspection I1, which must be performed on a daily basis as indicated by D1 at the above title block

Safety Precautions

  • Please don't cloth too much to the energized area.

Tools and Equipment 

  • No need for any tools.  
Work to be carried Out
  1. Drain the condensed water from high-pressure tanks.
  2. Check the pressure in the breaker's tanks at 15 atm.
  3. Check the functions of heaters in the control cubic.
  4. Check all the tubes of the air net to detect any air leakage.
  5. Check the liquid level in water resistance (19 fig.1).
  6. Visually check the arcing chamber insulators to detect any cracks.
  7. Visually check the position of the isolator blades and shock absorbers.
  8. Record any marks.
