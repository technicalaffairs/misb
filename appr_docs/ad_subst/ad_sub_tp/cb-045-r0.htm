Introduction

This procedure details the steps of preventive maintenance to fill hydraulic oil of GIS, ABB (BBC) Circuit Breaker 220KV to keep the equipment in good operating condition and avoid breakdowns.

This procedure details the steps of preventive maintenance to fill hydraulic oil of GIS, ABB (BBC) Circuit Breaker 220KV to keep the equipment in good operating condition and avoid breakdowns.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Circuit breaker isolated on both ends and earthing is required.

  • Change remote switch to local position.

  • Springs must be unloaded.

  • Hydraulic spring drive in OPEN position.

  • Breathing musk

  • Hydraulic pressure must be at 0.

Tools and Equipment

  • Vacuums pump, set of common keys, blower, oil filter, oil receptacle.

  • Clean cloth,

  • Hydraulic oil as per manufacture's recommendation.

Work to be carried out

  1. Wear breathing musk, clean the circuit breaker and hydraulic system by air.

  2. Connect vacuum pump to oil refill opening figure (1).

  3. Via a filter connect the oil receptacle to oil drain valve.

  4. Open pressure relief screw by turning to the left.

  5. Keep the drain valve closed.

  6. Draw a vacuum for approximately 30 sec (less than or equal 25 m bar)

  7. Keep pump running and draw in oil by opening drain valve until oil is in the center of oil level indicator.

  8. Close drain valve.

  9. Again draw a vacuum of 15-m bar for approximately 5 minute, while operating manually and laterally the pilot valves (close) and (open).

  10. Close pressure relief screw

  11. Apply pressure to hydraulic spring drive and switch on and off several times to remove any air in the hydraulic system.

  12. Check oil levels, with the spring are loaded and the mechanical spring drive is in open position.

  13. Correct if necessary the level oil by pumping or draining oil by means of drain valve.

  14. Unscrew vacuum pump and close oil refill opening.

  15. After completion of the oil filling and evacuation the first 5 close/open operations shall be taken into account for the evaluation of the running behavior.

  16. It is necessary to evacuate the hydraulic spring drive from the air, which penetrated from the above operation.

Note:

  1. The vacuums pump provided for the SF6 gas chambers can be used for this purpose, however a separate flexible tube and an oil filter between drive and vacuums pump shall be used for this purpose.

  2. Risk of due voids air to changing of position during the transport.
