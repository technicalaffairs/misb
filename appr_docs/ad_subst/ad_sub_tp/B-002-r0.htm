Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block..

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves ... etc
    •
  • Don't smoke in the battery room.
  • Don't place any tools on top of battery cells (Danger of short circuit)
    •
  • Never use the same tools on alkaline cells you may have used before on lead acid cells
    •
  • All tools will be used must be insulated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard personal safety equipment, goggles, rubber gloves, nibbler apron and tool set.

  • Maintenance Record Data Sheet: MRDS.

Work to be carried out

  1. Read overall voltage of bank and record on battery maintenance card.

  2. Disconnect AC supply to Battery Charger.

  3. Inspect the outside of cells for cracks and leakage of electrolyte.

  4. If you discover a defective cell use the recommended procedure to replace with a new cell.

  5. Inspect electrolyte level and record density for each cell, add distilled water if level is below normal.
    Correct level for above VARTA cell type is 35 mm above cell plates.

  6. Inspect inter-cell connectors and terminals of cells and clean any salt deposits with distilled water.

  7. Check terminal connections for tightness.

  8. Lubricate terminals of cells with a thin film of Vaseline.

  9. Clean top of cells.

  10. Read voltage per cell and record on battery maintenance card.

  11. Again read overall voltage of bank and record on battery maintenance card and compare the reading from step No. 1.

  12. If overall voltage is below 225 volts, place battery bank on float (high) charge for 4 - 5 hours after which you switch the charger back on trickle (normal) charge.

  13. Compare the present readings with the last readings.
