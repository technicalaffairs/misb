Introduction

This document details the special Inspection, which must be performed after I sc > 500KA,  

The first draft initiated by Eng. Samer Ayad Bahga, at Saptia1 220kv Substation, Cairo

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and grounded on both sides,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses …

  • Change Remote position to Local,

  • Shut off air valves,

  • Remove DC plugs for each pole,

  • Discharge air from tank

Tools and Equipment

  • Silicon grease, combination pliers, telephone pliers, mechanics pliers, adjusting pliers, machinist’s pliers, 
    side-cutting pliers,

  • Special tin snip, lacquer scraping tweezers/32 ribs, tweezers, cross leg soldering tweezers, screw drivers, 
    voltage tester, screw starter, watch maker’s screw drivers, off set screw drivers, drip oiler, three square reamer, 
    dust brush, hacksaw,

  • Measuring tape, hexagon sockets, electric soldering iron, hydraulic crane, regulating unite, heavy current unit.

  • Compete set of tools, emergency light,

  • Gas detector, dew point, acidity and air content equipment.

    •

    Work to be Carried Out

    1. Check SF6 pressure on manometer.    (7 bar)

    2. Check air pressure on manometer.      (10 bar)

    3. Check compressed air manometer with control instrument.

    4. Check SF6 manometer with control instrument.

    5. Check adjustment and function of manometer contacts.  

    6. Check SF6 moisture content (desired level <20 o C).

    7. Check SF6 air contents (desired level <2%).

    8. Check SF6 acid content (desired level <5-10 PPmg )

    9. Check adjustment and function of compressed air pressure switch.  

    10. Check fixed main contacts and if necessary replace (1,2).

    11. Check movable main contacts and if necessary replace (1,2).

    12. Check insulating nozzle and if necessary replace (1,2).

    13. Replace molecular sieve filter (1).

    14. Carryout test switching operations.

    Remarks

    1. Require opening of gas compartment.

    2. Random check on circuit breaker with max operating duty further check only on negative result.
