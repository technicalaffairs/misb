Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance inspection I1, which must be performed on yearly bases or every six months as indicated in the title block.

Technical Specification:

6.3kv / 2000 A - 50 KA

The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Kamal Oshba, tested by eng. Reda Ragab Karam, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station  and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia: WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the quatified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used.

  • Wear safety helmet, safety shoes, masks..

  • Before start cleaning wear breathing mask to protect lung tissues from dust particles and chemical solvent. which if exist, expose lungs to danger.

  • Don't throw any cleaning liquid on ground, collect it and send to dumb.

  • Don't burn clothes used in cleaning collect it and send to dumb.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set.

  • Safety helmet safety shoes and masks.

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower.

  • Pieces of soft clothes and cleaning liquid.

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Switch off the transformer which feeds the bus bar and isolate the bus bar from the two sides.

  2. Switch off all feeders at the bus bar and switch on their earthing switche.s

  3. Insure that all 3 phases of each feeder at that busbar don`t pass a current through it before draw the circuit breaker`s out of the busbsar.

  4. Switch off DC source.

  5. Connect B.B earthing device.

  6. Draw circuit breakers out of bus bar.

  7. Dismantle upper and lower shutters.

  8. Blow the dust abd dirt out of bus bar (using vacuum cleaner)..

  9. Clean the upper contact (related to bus bar) and lower contact (related to cables).

  10. Check current transformers to be well fixed retighten bolts and screws and clean it.

  11. Retighten bolts and screws for bus bar parts.

  12. Check and clean potential transformer and its cell and clean.

  13. Check the heaters in the cells to be switched on clean it put the indicator of each heater at the suitable position.

  14. Check earthing switch of each feeder cell to be in good contact (as it switched on) check also that it moves is easily.

  15. Check cells to be well sealed and no way for rats to get into the cells.

  16. Clean the upper parts of cells and the instruments involved in it such as protection apparatus timers measuring instruments etc.

  17. Clean shutters well and put it in its places in the cells.

  18. Clean the passages that tracks across it in and out the cells so breaker could get in and out of cells easily.

  19. Switch off earthing switches for all feeders cells.

  20. Disconnect the B.B earthing device.

  21. Put breakers in test position switch on and off the breakers electrically and then put them into their cells.
