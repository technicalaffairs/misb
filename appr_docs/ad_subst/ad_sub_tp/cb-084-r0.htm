Introduction

This procedure details the equipment inspection I1, for the minimum oil circuit breaker 66kv, rated current: 2500A, short circuit current: 3l.5KA, which should be carried out on an annual bases Y1 or after 5 times of an automatic break due to short circuit, as indicated in the title block.

The first draft document initiated at Sabtia 220k substation Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated completely and properly earthed from both sides,

  • Isolate the DC voltage for the earthing switch,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • DC control power should be disconnected.

  • Before using a blower to clean the control cabinet or when cleaning the insulators, wear breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particles.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks.

  • Air blower, cleaning materials as rags

  • Time equipment,

  • Micro Ohmmeter test unit,

  • Power cord heavy current cables.

  • Suitable clamps.

Works to be Carried out

1.      Check all internal parts of are chambers concerning the fixed and moving contacts change it if needed.

2.      Change the oil as before and clean all internal parts and ventilate the gases.

3.      Inspect all parts of mechanism operation and lubricate all moving parts.

4.      Clean the surface and insulation of the breaker.

5.      In case of changing the internal contacts you must adjusting the breaker again.

6.      Measure the opening & closing time opening time =1/20 second.

7.      Measure the difference between phases.  (Must be 10 ms)

8.      Measure the resistance of contacts.   Must not exceed 100 MΩ.   ref. CB-004-r0
