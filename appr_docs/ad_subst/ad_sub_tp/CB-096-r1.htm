Introduction

This document outlines the visual inspection activities I1, to be performed on an annually bases Y1 as indicated in the above title block. The 1st draft initiated by Demo substation, eng. Abd Eltawab Ramadan from ME. The first review at Nov. 2007

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • CB must be isolated and proper earthing at the two sides of CB is required.

  • Interrupt remote control and motor circuits (this achieved by receiving the multi-pin plug at the left hand side at the operating mechanism)

  • Switch breaker ON and OFF manually in order to discharge the closing and springs. 

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes, ...,etc".

Work to be carried out 

  1. Determine the SF6 gas pressure with the plug-in manometer and compare with value from the gas pressure  curve-top-up with SF6 gas if the pressure has dropped to about the level of tapping-up (refilling) pressure.

  2. Trip dash-pot (damper); check the oil level in the dash pot with the piston in the "OFF" position. If there are traces of oil at the top end of the dash pot the dash pot must be replaced.

  3. Read the operating counter read-off and record the number of switching operations.
