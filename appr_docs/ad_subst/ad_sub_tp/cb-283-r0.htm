Introduction

This document lists major maintenance procedure P2, which must be performed every twelve years as indicated by Y12  in the title block

The first draft document initiated by eng. Ibrahim Ibrahim Shehata at El-Manaief substation 220/66/11kv, revised by eng. Mohamed Emran: quality control manager, accepted by eng. Ahmed EL-Ebrashy: networks manager, and certified by eng. Ali Atiia Eldeeb: head sector of Canal western network & MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • CB must be isolated completely and the earhing switch must be coected on both sides of the CB.

  • Isolate the DC voltage, switch off the control circuit and insert closing and tripping preventive pins.

  • Ensure that the CB grounded on both sides.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective tools as safety, helmet, shoes, safety glasses, breathing mask... etc.

  • Discharge closing spring.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable common tool set (keys, screw drivers, ... etc)

  • Torque wrenches

  • Micro-ohmmeter

  • Standard pressure gauge

  • SF6 gas tester (dew point, moisture content, air content, decomposion, acidity)

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower

  • SF6 gas detector

  • SF6 gas cylinder (if needed)

  • SF6 gas refilling machine (if needed)

  • Pieced of cleaning clothes and rags

  • Grease and cleaning liquid (like gasoline)

  • Contact resistance tester

  • Operating time measuring device

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Read and annotate the CB general data: type, serial number, year of manufacturing, umber of operations, number of short circuit interruption

  2. Check of supply voltage (heater voltage, control voltage, auxiliary voltage)

  3. Clean and  relubricate all parts of drive mechanism

  4. Retighten and retorque all loose external hardware (bolted connections)

  5. Do equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months according to document no. : CB-167-r0

  6. Check the SF6 gas quality (according to technical bulletin i document
    no. : TB-SS-008-r0):
    Dew point                                           ≤ 41 °C
    Humidity                                                 ≤ 16 PPMw
    SF6 %                                                ≥ 98 %
    Decomposition products (SO2+SOF2) ≤ 15 PPMv

    Note: new SF6 gas should have a moisture content less than 125 ppmv equivalent to 15 ppmg.

    Used gas should have a moisture content less than 500 ppmv (62 ppmg), and if it is more, the gas should be exchanged with new gas or in the case of GIS equipment (a very large quantity of gas) it is more economical to use the gas truck to re-condition the gas

  7. Check breaker function (measurement of operating times) according to document no. : CB-230-r0
