Introduction

This document lists the equipment Inspection checklist I1, which must be performed from one month to 3 months as indicated by M1:3  in the title block. The first draft initiated by the Canadian SISP specialists. The first revision date 06 March 2002.

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety belt when cleaning bushings / insulators.

  • Transformer isolation and proper earthing is required.

  • There is a danger of falling into the oil containment pit when working near large power transformers.

  • It is recommended to use only fiberglass(PVC) ladders when working in the switchyard.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, PVC ladders, and tool set

Work to be carried out

  1. Clean all porcelain surfaces of all transformer & wall bushings / insulators.

  2. Clean neutral CT, earthing switches, check that all bolted connections are tight.

  3. Clean contacts of all neutral disconnect switches, lubricate all bearing points.

  4. Inspect the transformer control cabinet, check the condition of the door seal, blow out the inside of the control cabinet with compressed air using moderate pressure.

  5. Perform the same inspection as in step 4 on the control cabinet of the tap changer.

  6. Check for signs of oil leaks.

  7. Check oil levels in tank, conservator and all oil filled bushings.

  8. Check oil temperature and winding temperature indicators, record maximum temperature indication from drag hand before resetting drag hand.

  9. Make a visual inspection of all cooling fans, remove any debris from fan screens, switch on cooling fans manually and listen for abnormal noise.

  10. Check the condition of the silicagel breather, replace silicagel if one or more sections are discoloured, check for the correct oil level in bottom oil cup.

  11. Check breakdown voltage of insulating oil (every one year).

  12. Return transformer bank to normal service.
