Introduction

This procedure outlines the checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases Y1 as indicated in the above titled block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Saleh El-Sayed Saleh, and revised by Operation and maintenance's General Director: eng Mohamed Reda Abd El-Azez

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding on both sides is required.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "Belt, Shoes, Hemet ..., etc".

  • Complete tool kit, rags
