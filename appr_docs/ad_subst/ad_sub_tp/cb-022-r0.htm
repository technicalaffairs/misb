Introduction

This document lists the inspection checks I3, which must be performed every six years as indicated by Y6 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes …. etc

    •
  • CB must be isolated and proper earthing at the two sides of CB area is required.
  • Isolate control circuit (DC voltage).

Tools and Equipment
  • Hydraulic test gauge, SF6 test gauge, blower, dew-point equipment, acidity equipment air contoint equipment, timing equipment and contact resistance equipment.

Work to be Carried Out

1.

Repeat steps of three years inspection, MPIS Document no. CB-021-r0

2.

Monitor the degree of moisture of the SF6 using dew-point equipment

3.

Measure thresholds of hydraulic drive using portable pressure gauge.

4.

Measure pre-charge pressure of accumulators using portable pressure gauge.

5.

Change oil and filter.

Note:

Degree of moisture
SF6 analysis:
Air 2%, Acidity 5 ppm, and Moisture 62 ppmg
Replace the arc contacts after:
20 times on trip 220 KV
30 times on trip 66 KV
2000 times normal operation
