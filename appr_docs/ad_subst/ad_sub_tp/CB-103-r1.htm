Introduction
    This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1 as indicated by P1 in the above title block to be performed every 5 years Y5.  The first draft initiated at Demo substation, eng Abdel Tawab Ramadan. The first review at Nov 2007.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • CB must be isolated and proper earthing at the two sides of CBn is required.

  • Interrupt remote control and motor circuits (this achieved by receiving the multi-pin plug at the left hand side at the operating mechanism)

  • Switch breaker ON and OFF manually in order to discharge the closing and springs. 

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc".

Work to be carried out 


 The following inspections are to be carried out in addition to the annual inspections at MPIS doc # CB-102-r0

  1. Densimeter :- reduce the pressure and determine the operating point. Compare value with that of the gas pressure diagram if the specified tolerance is exceeded reset densimeter or replace 

  2. Gas moisture :- measure the moisture content of sf6 gas. If the moisture content of sf6 gas is too high open-up the circuit breaker and replace the adsorber.

Densimeter adjustment

Pressure adjustment

The densimeter has 2 or 3 independently adjustable microswitches (item 3) with adjusting screws (item 2). Pressure adjustment is reduced when adjusting screws are turned clockwise see figure 1. During this operation, bimetal strips (item 4) may not be touched.

                                Figure 1

Densimeter Replacement

Disassembly

  1. Prepare new densimeter and seal gasket (10),

  2. Unscrew cable connection,

  3. Unscrew the 4 M8 mounting with an Allen wrech,

  4. Remove densimeter,

  5. Remove seal gasket (10).

Assembly

  1. Insert new seal gasket (10),

  2. For assembly, proceed in reverse order of disassembly,

  3. Check gas pressure and correct adjustment if necessary

Note:    The densimeter of the switchgear supplied after January 1978 can be replaced under pressure. (copnnectio to gas compartment through nozzle position 13)
