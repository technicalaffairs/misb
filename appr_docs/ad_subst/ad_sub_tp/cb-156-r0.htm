Introduction

This documents out lines the check list I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 at the titled block.

The first draft was initiated by eng. Alsayed Abdel Monem AlGeddawy and tested on the feeder no. 15 at Fifth Urban Community Substation at 20 March 2002 Cairo Zone. 

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, and masks are necessary

    •

  • Switch off DC source

Environmental Precautions

  • Before start cleaning, wear breathing mask to protect lung tissues from dust particles and chemical solvent which, if exist, expose lungs to danger

  • Don't throw oil or grease on ground, collect it and send to central labs to recycle it.

  • Don't burn clothes used in cleaning, collect it and send to dumb  

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set

  • Pieces of clothes and rags

  • Grease and cleaning liquid (like gasoline)

  • Helmet, safe shoes and masks

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean porcelain insulators of the circuit breaker from dust, dirties, deposits ....etc

  2. Inspect well the flower contacts and check it to be in good condition

  3. Check contact arm to be fixed well and in exact horizontal position

  4. Inspect the pressure of SF6 gas and check it to be in the safe area (green area)

  5. Clean the Socket by video cleaner

  6. Check the mechanism of the circuit breaker, clean it with gas and grease it

  7. Retighten bolts and screws

  8. Switch on and off, the circuit breaker in test position mechanically and electrically

  9. Oiling the wheels of breaker tracks to make it easily move
