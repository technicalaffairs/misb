Introduction 

This document lists the major maintenance procedure P2, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5  in the title block. The first draft initiated by Eng. Emad Farouk Mostafa at Katamia substation Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • The rotating disconnect switch must be isolated and grounded..

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety belt when cleaning suspension Insulator.

  • Ground both sides of the Angle disconnect switch.
  • Change position of interlock to maintenance position.
Tools and Equipment

  • Dry cloths, graphite oil, screw drivers, and blower,

  • Torque set keys, grease and set of common keys.

Work to be carried out

  1. Clean the disconnect switch with dry cloths and remove dust by blower, remove the old grease and clean all parts.

  2. Check the middle isolator which carries the movable current conducting section. 

  3. Check the swivel type bearing which the disconnect switch mounted on it.

  4. Check that the 3 pole frames of the insulator are mounted on to the support frame.

  5. Using torque set check torque per pole, measured on the lever of the rotary post bearing, must lies between 200-250Nm

  6. Check that the contact blades are properly centre-aligned when they enter the contact pot.

  7. Coat the contacts with graphite oil

  8. Check the position of the motor drive

  9. Readjust the isolator by means of hand crank in position (ON) the dead center position must be acheved

  10.  Check the exact position of the pole blades, all blades should pressed aganist the stops with 20-30 N pressures

  11. If pretensioning is too high the rotary insulators could be over stressed, which in turn could result in breakage.

  12. Readjust the position of the blades in off (70 ºC ± 5 ºC) by means of the connection pieces

  13. Check the motion drive shaft (operating rod) of motor and retight the bolts if necessary .

  14. Re grease the bearings and bronze bushes

  15. Re oil the contact surfaces of the main contacts

  16. Re grease the motor drive spindle in extended position use a graphite oil

  17. Observe the motion of the isolator in on and off position for 10 times.
