Introduction

This document outlines the first level of inspection I1, to be performed on an monthly bases M1 as indicated in the above title block. The first draft initiated by eng. Mostafa Abd_Alla Beni Suef East 220kv Substation and approved at 15 Jan 2002.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety equipment.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety equipment "belt, shoes,...,etc", 6 mm Allen key, 13 mm spanner

Works to be Carried Out

Compressor

  1. Check the level of lubricating oil for the compressor, correspond to the mark on the dipstick.

  2. Visually check that the automatic drainage valve drains the condensate at the end of pumping.

  3. Read and record the number of the operating hours (if provided with hour counter).

  4. Manual drainage gradually of tank condensate to check that the system pressure remains more or less constant

  5. Loosen the manual condensate drain plug by the Allen key until all the air begins to comes out. Leave to drain until all the condensate has come out.

Note:  If the ambient temperature is below zero, this operation must not be carried out. During drainage of the condensate, the compressor may start again, the circuit breaker may open due to low air pressure (or lock if preset to do so) 
 

Operating Mechanism and Pole

  1. Read and record the number of operations carried out

  2. Read and record the pressure and density meter and record the pressure of the SF6 gas in the chart
