Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated in the above title block by Y1. The first draft was initiated by eng. Shelaby & Helmut and tested at 12 April 1997 at Bahtem 220kv Substation, Cairo zone.

Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance st off should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety rope with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,
  • Wear your safety helmet and safety harnesses is necessary.
  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required. 
  • Change remote to local position.

Tools and Equipment  

  • Multimeter, blower, Gas detector, Buzzer, Screw drivers, 
  • Set of common keys, micro-ohmmeter, dew point and timing equipment.

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check supply voltage (220 Vdc and 220/380 Vac).

  2. Check SF6 gas pressure manometers. (7.0 bar at 20 Co - use gas leak detector if it necessary).

  3. Check operation air pressure (Max 28bar-min. 22bar).

  4. Drain condensed water from air pressure tank.  

  5. Check the operating pressure air values (start-stop) for compressor. 

  6. Retighten all fixation bolts if it necessary. 

  7. Check all connection terminals for remote and local cabinet-retighten if it necessary.

  8. Check the earthed connections

  9. Clean pneumatic mechanism and local control cabinet. (Use dry air)

  10. Check aux. switch contacts and terminals.

  11. Check the mechanism position indicator, ensure they are synchronized with the breaker position.

  12. Check the operation of mechanism cabinet heaters.

  13. Check tightness of air and gas pipes

  14. Measure and record timing On-Off for CB (40m.s. - 90m.s).

  15. Measure and record contact resistance compare with previous test results, report/investigate significant changes

  16. Measure and record the SF6 dew point, max 50ppm.

  17. Measure and check drive mechanism force.

  18. Measure and record the earth resistance.

  19. Check the GIS compartments for unusual sound.

  20. Measure and record the SF6 dew point max 50ppm

  21. Make trip operation by protective relays.
