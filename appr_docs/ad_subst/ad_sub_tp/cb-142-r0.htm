Introduction

This document outlines the visual inspection list I1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel-Mohsen Hanfy at Cairo 500 substation ME zone and tested at 24/4/02 for circuit of October 2 .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your appreciated safety tools: helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.... etc

  • Circuit breaker isolation and earthing is required

Tools and Equipment

  • Gas detector.

  • Cleaning material.

  • Appreciated safety  tools: helmet, shoes .... etc

Work to be Carried Out

General Works

  1. Identify the general data like: substation serial-no, E-no, type, year of manufacturing of circuit-breaker

  2. Identify the operating conditions:
       - Number of Co-operations (see operation counter).
       - Number of short-circuit interruptions.
       - Sum of short-circuit interruptions.

  3. Check SF6 tightness (look at scale of densimeter)

  4. Check the tightness of compressed air system of circuit-breaker, ICU (if applicable) and compressed air reduction of central compressor unit (if applicable).

  5. Starts of compressor

  6. Check the running time of compressor (if applicable).

  7. Check the number of refillings at central compressor unit (if applicable).

  8. Check of drain valve

  9. Check of paint finish and touch up where necessary.

  10. Visual check of porcelains (general condition, damages, pollution).

  11. Check of exhausts of the valves.

  12. Check of supply voltages, check heater voltage, check the current input of the anticodensation heaters, heating effect.

  13. Check of control voltages.

  14. Check of auxiliary voltage
