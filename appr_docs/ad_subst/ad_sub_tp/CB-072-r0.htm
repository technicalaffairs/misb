Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P1, to be performed every ten years Y10 as indicated in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by Eng. Ayman Mahmoud Awad, tested at 17 April 2002 by eng Emad Farouq Mostafa at Katamia Substation, Cairo

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Ensure that the CB isolated completely and proper grounded on both sides,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety Helmet.

  • Isolate the DC control voltage, the door of the local CB must be opened.

Tools and Equipment

  • 1 set of fitter's tools, 1 torque wrench, and some of clean and dry cloth,

  • 1 industrial vacuum cleaner, special tools, and lifting gear (fork lift).

  • Exhaust tube - operating rod, 1 socket spanner - locking tool

Works to be Carried Out

The sequence for maintenance as shown in figure 9

  1. Clean the breaker pole within one hour of opening gas compartmemt

  2. Switch off the campressor motor and remove electrical connection

  3. Vent compressed air opening drain valve

  4. Remove SF6 gas

  5. Remove breaker pole releasing the mechanical connection to the base frame and dissonnect coupling link from bolis

  6. Remove push and pull rod

  7. With draw the breaker from its cell

  8. Push the breaker into the maintenance room

  9. Remove the air receiver tank

  10. Unscrew the air pipe

  11. Unscrew SF6 gas pipe

  12. Undo nexagonal head screw 2705 and remove with washers fig 1

  13. Draw out bolt 2704 with the inside treade M8 off the lever 2703

  14. Attach crame rope and s-hook to lifting eye on cover fixed contect and raise crane rope slightly

  15. Undo hexagonal head screw 0118 fig. 1 and lift breaker pole

  16. Remove the pneumatic drive

  17. Remove the center breaker pole

Dismantling the breaker pole

Before dismantling plug contacts their position should be marked   

  1. Remove the insulating shell

  2. Remove plug contacts

  3. Undo socket part 2500 fig 2

  4. Unscrew chees head screws and remove socket part 2500 from guide flange

  5. Using the vacuum cleaner remove any dust produced by switching which may be present in the socket part 2500

  6. Remove fixed contact fig 3

  7. Attach crane rope and shook to lifting eye on cover 1107 and raise crane rope slightly

  8. Undo chees head screws 0102 and carefully lift extinction chamber insulator 1001 with fixed contact out of insulator column 2000 and set vertically on one side fig 4

  9. Undo cheese head screw 0101 and taxe fixed contact 1100 out of extinction chamber insulator

  10. Remove moving contact

  11. Replace (contact pin 1109 - arcing finger 1222 - contact ring nozzle 1211 - multicontact 1215 - Auxiliary nozzle 1223 bearing 2511

  12. Inspect (fixed contact - cover 1107 - contact flange blast cylinder 1213 - moving contact - extinction chamber insulator - supporting insulator - operating system)

  13. Check (SF6 monitor - Compressed air system)
