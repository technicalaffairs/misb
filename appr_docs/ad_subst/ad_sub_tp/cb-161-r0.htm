Introduction

This document lists major maintenance procedure P2, which must be performed every six years as indicated by Y6 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Gamal Soliman, tested by eng. Galal Kamel Salem at 16 Nov.03, accepted by general director eng. Reda Mohamed Ahmed Al_Roos at Assuit 500kv substation, and approved by Middle Egypt Zone Quality Control general director eng. Ahmed Saied Abel Azez.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes .... etc

  • CB must be fully isolated, and ensure it is properly grounded.

  • Turn off the control circuit "DC" power supply.

  • Exhaust operation compressed air.

  • Take care of flanges and gasket.

  • Don't allow to dust to adhere to flanges and gaskets.

  • Don't dismantle the shaft seal.

  • Don't dismantle the gas density.

  • Don't expose absorbent to open air.

  • Confirm that O2  % concentration exceed 18% before starting inside checking

  • Confirm tighten of bolts and nuts, those removed then mark tightens.

Tools and Equipment

  • Gas detector, Air blower, SF6 gas detector

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes .... etc,

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the stationary arcing contact through looking at inspection window.

  2. Replace the contact if the wear more than 3 mm.

  3. Apply thin coat of grease.

  4. Remove the moving contact.

  5. Measure the inner diameter.

  6. Replace if wear more then 2 mm.

  7. Apply thin coat of grease.

  8. Clean both insulation nozzle and cover.

  9. Measure the inner diameter of them, replace if the wear 1 mm

  10. Change absorbent with a new one quickly just before vacuum.

  11. Remove operating cylinder cover then inspect gaskets,

  12. Apply grease to piston guide main value and operating piston.

  13. Supply new oil to the piston of oil plug.

Control Mechanism

  1. Check pins links levers.

  2. Check nuts for possible loosen.

  3. Apply then layer of grease.

Closing Link Mechanism

  1. Clean up hook pins and roller.

  2. Check state of hook engagement.

  3. Apply them layer of grease.

Electro Magnetic  Valve

  1. Clean up and apply a turbine oil to hook.

  2. Check nuts bolts and split pins.

Airing Value

  1. Connect a hose to the discharge of port.

  2. Measure discharge using measuring cylinder ( 300 - 700 ) cc/min.

Auxiliary Switch

  1. Check contacts.

  2. Check terminal for tightening.

  3. Apply thin coat of grease to pin.

Pressure Gauge 

  1. Calibrate the gauge using standard one.

  2. Don't allow ingress or dust into pressure gauge.

Pressure Switch

  1. Check the pressure setting.

 

Anti-Pumping Contact 

  1. Check contacts, repair if needed.

Operation tests

  1. Carry out timing test.

  2. Carry out DC resistance test.

SF6 Gas Test

  1. Check sealed parts for SF6 gas leak

  2. Carry out dew point test

  3. Carry out SF6 purity test

  4. Carry out SO2 content test

  5. Carry out SF6 decomposition product test
