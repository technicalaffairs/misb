Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block for Power Transformer:

Type     : SL core frame step up transformer, Class: OA/FA - 55 C rise, 3 phase, 50 Hz

Rating   : 46.5 MVA - 62 MVA, Voltage : 229 Y - 11.5 KV, I.B MNM  5279 - 12 
 

The first draft initiated by Eng. Saleh El-Sayed Saleh and revised by Operation and maintenance's General Director: eng Mohamed Reda Abd El-Azez

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued. 
  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding is required  
  • Wear your safety helmet.  
  • There is a danger of falling into the oil contain ment pit when working near large power transformers.  
  • There is a danger of falling off the top of the transformer when cleaning insulator, wear safety belt.

Tools and Equipment
  • Complete tool set.  
  • Digital DC voltmeter.  
  • Megger device.  
  • Blower or vacuum cleaner.  
  • PVC Ladder - Brushes..

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Insulation Resistance

    2. a- Each winding to ground and to other winding at 1000 To 2500 V

    b- Core to ground at 1000 volts.

  2. Insulation Power Factor.

    3. a- Each winding to ground and to other windings.

    b- Bushing (when equipped with a test connection)

  3. Winding Ratio (not.: LTC transformers may require extra test source capacity because of
    preventive auto exciting current on add numbered positions)

  4. Winding Resistance.

  5. Dielectric strength, Power Factor, Water Content, Interfacial tension, Gas Content and
    neutralization number of insulating oil.

  6. Oxygen content, Dew point and combustible gas content of gas in the transformer.

  7. Check the pressure relief device.

  8. Check temperature gauges indicator.

  9. Check liquid level gauges indicator.

  10. Check pressure gauges indicator.

  11. Check all protection devices.

  12. Check tan Delta ( Capacitance and dissipation Factor )

  13. Check of tap changer.

  14. Excitation Current Test

  15. Leakage current monitor test for lightening arrestor.
