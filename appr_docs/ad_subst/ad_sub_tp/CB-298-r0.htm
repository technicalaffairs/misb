Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 on 6.3 kv C.B which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Saad Mahmoud El-Nashaar, tested by eng. Reda Ragab Karam, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam ,approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia: WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety healthy shoes breathing mask safety glasses is required.

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated completely and properly earthed from both sides.

  • DC control power should be disconnected.

  • Before using a blower to clean the control cabinet or when cleaning the insulators, wear breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particles.

  • Don`t burn cloth used in cleaning collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Hydraulic test gauges, hydraulic oil, and SF6 gas if needed.

  • SF6 test gauge, micro ohmmeter, and air blower.

  • Dew point equipment, acidity equipment, air content equipment, and timing equipment.

Work to be carried out

 

Before putting the circuit - breaker into service carry out the following

  1. Check the tightness of the electrical connections on the circuit - breaker terminals.

  2. Check that the value of the supply voltage for the auxiliary circuit is in the correct value.

  3. Check that no foreign body, such as packing has got into the moving parts.




    Note :
    To gain access to the operating mechanism safety discharge the closing springs (by closing and opening the circuit -breaker), then remove the cover of the operating mechanism

    Check that air circulation in the circuit - breaker installation site is adequate so that there is no danger of overheating.

  4. Check that the gas pressure is correct is (green lamp on), if not, reset the correct pressure.

  5. Carry out checks indicated in the following table.

  6. Grease the C.B mechanism.

Manual operating mechanism

  1. Carry out a few closing and opening operations to ensure if the operations and relative signals occur correctly.

Motor operating mechanism

  1. Supply geared motor for spring charging at the relative rated voltage to see if the springs are charged correctly if the signal are correct the geared motor cuts off when the springs are charged.

  2. Carry out a few closing and opening operations, the geared motor recharges the springs after each closing operation.

Under voltage release

  1. Supply the under voltage release at the relative rated voltage and carry out the circuit - breaker closing. operation if the circuit - breaker closes correctly the signal changes over.

  2. Disconnect the power supply from the release the circuit - breaker opens the signal changes over.

Auxiliary contacts in the operating mechanism

  1. Insert the auxiliary contacts into suitable signaling circuits (test position).

  2. Carry out a few closing and opening operation of the circuit - breaker to see if signals occur correctly.

Insulation resistance

  1. With a 2500 V megger, measure the insulation resistance the phase and between phases and exposed conductive part of the medium voltage circuit. The insulation resistance should be at least 50 MΩ, and in any case. Constant in time if values are different, find out the cause.

  2. With a 500 V megger (installed equipment permitting) measure the insulation resistance between the auxiliary circuits and the exposed conductive part. The insulation resistance should be a few MΩ and, in any case. Constant in time.
