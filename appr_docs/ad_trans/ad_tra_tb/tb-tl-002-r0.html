Introduction

An anchor hook made of heat treated sheet aluminum is used as part of the temporary fall protection system for towers.

Information

The Anchor Hook has been designed to be used with switch sticks and to be connected to a rope via a small karabiner.

The holes on the hook to accommodate the lock pin of the switch stick and the karabiner have been made with very little tolerance. It may be necessary to ream the holes out with a rat tail file for proper fit of a larger than usual lock pin or karabiner.

Reaming to increase the diameter of the holes by up to 1/16 inch is permissible.
