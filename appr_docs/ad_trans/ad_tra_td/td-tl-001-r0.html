Introduction

This directive covers the requirements for fall protection equipment for work on transmission towers.

Directions

  1. All transmission line maintainers must wear full body harness while working on towers.

  2. All transmission line maintainers must be continuously attached to a fall protection system when they are over 3 m (10ft) above ground while working or climbing on towers.

  3. All the equipment used in the fall protection system must be of an approved type.

  4. All transmission line maintainers must have received prior training in the use of the various pieces of equipment in the fall protection systems before using them in work applications.

  5. All fall protection training on tower must be done by a competent trades person who is knowlegeable in the various systems and procedures. The supervisor must be satisfied that the training given to the transmission line maintainers before they work on towers is adequate for their safety. The zone are responsible for keeping records of the fall protection training given to transmission line maintainers.

  6. All supervisors must be satisfied that transmission line maintainers who have not climbed towers for over a year still have adequate knowledge of how to use the various pieces of equipment that make up temporary fall protection systems.
