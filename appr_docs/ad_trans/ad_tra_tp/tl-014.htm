Introduction

The purpose of this procedure is to provide detailed instructions on a safe method for replacing insulators on 500KV Suspension Russian towers type P.

Safety Precautions

  • An authorized work permit must be issued

  • Use approved fall arrest procedures

  • Use approved temporary earthing procedures

  • Use personal protective equipment (flash glasses, safety helmet, safety boots and gloves…etc).

  • Line workers uniforms must be colored to indicate the disconnected circuit that they will be working on.

  • All equipment must be rated for the loads that they will carry and be checked and approved prior to the beginning of the work.

  • Communication between the control unit, the supervisor and the crew members must be ensured.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. The circuit must be disconnected according to EEC rules.

  2. Line workers must climb the 2 towers on either of side of the tower to be worked on and apply temporary earths according to approved earthing procedures after verifying isolation of the circuit by using a potential tester.

  3. Two line workers may then climb the tower to the cross-arm above the conductor and install the hand-line in an appropriate position.

  4. Raise and install the rope ladder.

  5. Raise and install the wire rope slings, shackles and 6 ton chain hoists and suspension clamps as shown if (Figure # 1).

  6. Raise and install the wire rope safety sling as shown in (Figure # 1).

  7. Take up tension on the two 6 ton chain hoists to slacken the insulator strings.

  8. Disconnect the insulator string from the conductor clamp so it is left suspended from the tower arm by the “L” joint and 6 ton shackle only.

  9. Using the hand-line, install the winch cable in the leg snatch block and the cross-arm snatch block and attach the winch cable to the “L” joint with a shackle.

  10. Raise the insulator string gradually using the winch cable until the shackle attaching it to the cross-arm becomes slack.

  11. Release the pin of the shackle connecting the “L” joint to the cross-arm.

  12. Attach the 40 meter hand-line to the insulator string to control the strings movement and prevent it from hitting the tower steel as it is being lowered to the ground.

  13. Lower the insulator string slowly to the ground on the winch cable.

  14. Assemble the new insulator string on the ground and check all cotter keys.

  15. Attach the new insulator string to the winch cable and raise it to the suspension point on the cross-arm.

  16. Connect the new insulator string to the cross-arm and release the winch cable.

  17. Connect the new insulator string to the conductor clamp and release the two 6 ton hoists.

  18. Disconnect the chain hoist assemblies and safety sling from the cross-arm and lower to the ground on the hand-line.

  19. Lower the winch cable and snatch blocks to the ground.

  20. Remove the temporary earths after the circuit is cleared of all men and equipment.

  21. Close the work permit according to EEC Electrical Safety Rules.
