Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abd El-Satar, EEHC technical inspection & quality control head sector, and from TL instructions document, this document certified by by eng. MPIS zone coordinators

Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the personal protective equipment must used as safety shoes, safety helmet, earplug, and gloves pairs...etc

  • The transmission lines must be isolated and grounded on the work area.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety harnesses, cotton suit,... etc

Work to be Carried out

The following compression fittings are suitable for galvanised steel copperweld or alumoweld earthwires joints and dead ends are of stainless steel to UNI X 3 CN 1911 standard corresponding to A I S I 304 L dead ends are drop forged and finished by machining joints are wholly made by machining dead ends can be of two types

  1. With long eye terminal

  2. With tongue terminal

Same recommendations given for aluminium compression fittings apply for assembly of these fittings

Bolted type Tension Clamps for Earth Wires

They are suitable for galvanized steel alumoweld or copperweld earth wires (with optional liners or pure aluminium or copper) body and keeper are made of galvanised drop forged steel tightening is assured by means of a variable number of bolts according to the clamp size

Following the diameter and composition of the earth wire they have a minimum slipping strength variable from 70 to 90 % of the earth wire rated breaking strength

Please note that for aluminium alu alloy or alu steel earth wires hexagonal compression dead ends or U - bolt type tension clamps 1600 A or B series can be used
