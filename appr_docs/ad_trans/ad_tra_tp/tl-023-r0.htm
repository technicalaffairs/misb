Introduction

This procedure is used for Inspection of Russian Power-washing equipment prior to start washing Live Transmission Lines 500kv Suspension and Tension Insulators.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued including the Towers numbers, the working site, the date and Time as well as the names, numbers of the working teams and degree of safety rate for every one of them.

  • Ensure the pressure of water pump, must not be less than 10 atmospheric pressure (BAR).

  • Ensure the specific dielectric resistance of water used in the process must not be less than 400 ohm/cm and ensure the meter is calibrated.

  • Ensure the wind speed meter is calibrated and should be established at a distance of 20 m from the tower on a high spot free of obstacles, given that the wind speed must not exceed 10 m/sec.

  • Ensure the adequacy of means of communication with control center must be ensured.

  • Work should stop immediately in case of any change in weather conditions such as storms, mist or rain.

  • The supervisor must make sure that there are no broken or damaged insulator disks in the insulators strings. The cleaning process must not take place where there are less than 17 desks of good condition.

  • Work should stop immediately if the circuit becomes de-energized because of the washing process or any other reason.

  • Ensure all Equipment should be provided with an earth cable of cross section not less then 150 mm2 or 2 cables not less than 75 mm2.

Tools and Equipment

  • Telescopic tower car (26 meter height) branch with 17mm nozzles, fixed on a special support in the basket of telescope and covered by protective shield-cage, rubber hose rated at 10 atmosphere pressure and 40 meter long.

  • A pump to draw waters from the tank and drives it through the hose with a pressure not less than 10 atmosphere pressure (BAR).

  • A vehicle equipped with a water tank of a capacity about 5 m3.

  • Meter to measure the specific dielectric resistance of water.

  • A waterproof coat, A wind meter,

  • Field glasses (Binoculars), Goggles.

  • All Equipment should be provided with an earth cable of cross section not less then 150 mm2 or 2 cables not less than 75 mm2.

  • Non conductive gloves for the working individuals.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Place the vehicle and the equipment used at a distance not less than 25 meters from the outer phase of the line where they are prepared for work.

  2. Check the telescope proper operation.

  3. Check the specific dielectric resistance of water used should be at least 400 ohm/cm.

  4. Check the speed and direction of wind should be detected at a distance of 10 to 20 meters away from the outer phase of the tower where work takes place, the speed of wind must not exceed:

    a) 10 m/sec in the case of suspension tower.

    b) 8 m/sec in the case of normal tension towers} and anchor angle towers of inclination from 0° to 20° .

    c) 6 m/sec in the case of anchor angle towers with inclination from 20° to 45° .

    d) 4 m/sec in the case of angle towers of inclination more than 45° .

  5. Check the pressure of water pump, must not be less than 10 atmospheric pressure (BAR)

  6. When the wind still stable and calm, it is still essential to detect the wind direction using a wind meter in order to identify the necessary in procedures and the sequence of work on the tower phase insulators.

  7. Before the work begins the supervisor on the washing process should make sure that the equipment, are properly prepared and that the workers are ready to start .He should also inform them about the working site, the procedures to be taken, and the different conditions for using the equipment. Workers must be warned not to touch any equipment during the process and to stay away from wet ground.

  8. The work supervisor must check the adequacy of means of communication between the work site and the nearest station capable of performing communication with the NECC.

  9. Before the washing process begins, the vehicle and all the other equipment should be connected to previously cleaned tower earth using an earth cable with a cross section not less than 150 mm2 or two earth cable not less than75 mm2, that procedure is considered extremely significant . and the earth cable used must be double- checked again before starting the washing process and good connection to the tower must be insured.
