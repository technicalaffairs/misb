Introduction

This procedure outlines the steps required to use trolley on double conductor over head transmission line to do some maintenance on the conductor.

Safety Precautions

  • Obtain an authorized work permit

  • Always use approved fall arrest equipment and procedures.

  • Always use approved temporary earthing equipment and procedures

  • Use personal protective equipment.

  • Maintain limits of approach from all circuits that have not been de-energized

  • Ensure that the insulators, hardware and conductor in the span to be worked on are in satisfactory mechanical condition to safely support the extra weight of the conductor trolley and line man.

  • Inspect the cart and correct any defects.

Tools and Equipment

  • Hand Line

  • Double conductor trolley

  • Earthing cable and climbing tools

  • Pulling rope about 20 meter (L.L.RO)

  • Rope Ladder

  • Fall protection equipment

  • New spacer

Work to be Carried Out    

  1. Visually check the insulators in the work location from the Ground.

  2. Check the wheels of the trolley. See Fig.1

  3. Two linemen must climb the tower and detect the volt using voltage detector.

  4. Temporary earthing cables must be installed on both sides of the work area.

  5. Install the hand line on the tower cross arm above the work level to make it easy to install the equipment.

  6. Raise the ladder and install it to the cross arm above the work area.

  7. Raise the trolley to the level of the phase in which the work will be done.

  8. One lineman descends on the ladder and the another lineman stands at the end of the cross arm and helps the other lineman to install the trolley on the conductor.

  9. Raise the pulling rope to the lineman on the rope ladder to attach one end to the trolley while the other end Remain on the ground to use it as pulling rope.

  10. Raise the new spacer to the lineman on the ladder.

  11. The lineman on the ladder moves from the ladder to the trolley using his safety rope the lineman attach his pole strap to the trolley then release his fall arrest.

  12. By using pulling rope pull the trolley to the defected spacer location on the conductor and use the brake of the trolley to stop the trolley. See Fig. 2

  13. The lineman on the trolley can exchange the defect spacer by the new spacer.

  14. When he finished the work the trolley must be go back to the initial suspension point.

  15. Lower the trolley and tools to the ground.

  16. Remove the ground cables from the conductor.

  17. Complete and finish the work permit.
