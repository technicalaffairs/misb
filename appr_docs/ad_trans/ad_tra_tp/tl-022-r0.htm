Introduction

The purpose of this procedure is to provide detailed safe method for transmission lines maintenance staff to minimize the possibility of error causing personal injury or damage to equipment for measuring earthing resistance - Circuit Energized - by using Earth Tester.

EEC TL engineers in head office and zones using their background field experience and EEC documentation to create this procedure.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Inspect the used cables before use.

  • Wear safety shoes and rubber gloves.

  • Don’t touch any bare conductor during measuring operation.

Tools and Equipment

  • Earth tester, Two probes.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Put the jumper between the earth lead and the tower leg then separate the earth lead from the tower leg .

  2. Release the jumper from the tower.

  3. Connect the system (as in fig 1) by putting the two probes and the earth at a straight line perpendicular to the direction of the line to prevent any error due to induction.

    Note    :
    In case of angle tower put the probes in the line that bisects the out side angle. If it is not possible, we can put them on the line that bisect the inside angle.

  4. Check for the battery of instrument and calibrate the instrument.

  5. Switch on position for RC and record the value of the resistance for scale.

  6. Connect a jumper across the tower leg and earth lead then connect the earth lead to the tower then disconnect the jumper from the two sides.
