Spacers Change Using Russian Telescoping Truck		 Approved Date:    12 Sep 1997

Introduction

This procedure outline the steps required to change spacers on 500kv over head transmission line, bundle conductor using Russian telescoping truck.

Safety Precautions

  • Obtain an authorized work permit

  • The set - up location for the telescope truck must be level

  • Local temporary earthing must be installed on both sides of the work location

Tools and Equipment

  • Flash glasses, Safety Helmut

  • Safety boots, Gloves

Qty

Item
1 Telescopic tower truck capable of extending to 26 meters
1 Tool bag
1 Clamp stick
2 Earthing cables
1 Steel conductor cleaning brush with universal fitting
1 Insulated operating stick with universal fitting
1 Potential Tester / Voltage Indicator
1 Insulating Gloves
1 Flash glasses for each worker

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Climb the towers on both sides of the work location using approved fall arrest procedures and maintaining the minimum limits of approach to the conductor

  2. Check the circuit to ensure isolation using a potential tester.

  3. Install temporary earthing cables according to approved earthing procedures

  4. The work supervisor shall determine the appropriate location to set up the telescopic truck to change the spacer.

  5. Lower and fix the jacks of the telescopic truck to ensure stability.

  6. The line worker climbs into the basket with the tool bag and replacement spacers.

  7. Raise the telescope ensuring that that the vertical axis of the telescope tower does not deviate greater than 3 degrees from vertical according to the angle indicator on the truck

  8. The basket is raised to a suitable height to enable the line worker to work comfortably on the conductor.

  9. The old spacer is then removed from the conductor.

  10. The conductor must be cleaned with a metal brush at the position where the spacer was originally installed.

  11. Install the new spacer on the cleaned conductor and tighten completely.

  12. After finishing the work, the telescopic tower is lowered and the line worker descends from the basket.

  13. Lower the jacks of the truck and prepare to move.

  14.  Remove the temporary earthing cables from both adjacent towers and lower to the ground.

  15. Complete the work order to connect the line in accordance with EEC Electrical Safety Rules.
