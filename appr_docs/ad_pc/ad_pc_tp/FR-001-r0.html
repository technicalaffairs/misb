Introduction

This document details the test steps I1, which are performed on the daily bases D1 as indicated in the titel blockfor the Fault Recorder type TPE 2000, manufacturer Enertic.

Safety Precautions
  • Work order must be issued.
  • The fault recorder should be in service,

Tools and Equipment
  • Complete set of screwdrivers

Work to be Carried Out

Visual Inspection of the Operation Lamps for Master unit UR 2000 and Acquisition unit UA ‎250

  1. Check that the lamp no. 7 at UR2000 and lamp no. 9 at UA250 are lighting that is mean the power supply is connected to them

  2. Check that the UR lamp no. 4 is flashing and the other lamps are off, that mean the master correct and if any one of other lamps are lighting, make rest from push button no. 6 and if the position steal, this mean that:

    • # Lamp no. 1 means that there is no connection between the station and the NCC        (not used)

    • # Lamp no. 2 means that the link between the master and Acquisition

    • unit is fail from master side.

    • # Lamp no. 3 means that the printer is fail.

    • # Lamp no. 5 means that the wash dog is fail.

  3. Check that the lamp no. 2 at UA250 is off, that mean that there is a connection between the memory (mm1) and the Acquisition unit

  4. Check that the UA250 lamp no. 1 is:

    • Off mean that there is no events.
    • Flashing mean that there are some events

    • Light means that memory (mm1) is full.

  5. Check that the lamp no. 4 at UA250 is off, that mean that there is a connection between the memory (mm2) and the Acquisition unit

  6. Check that the UA250 lamp no. 3 is:

    • Off mean that there is no events.
    • Flashing mean that there are some events

    • Light means that memory (mm1) is full.

  7. Check that the lamp no. 5 & 7 are flashing,

    • # Lamp no. 5 means the unit is correct.

    • # Lamp no. 7 mean the link between the Acquisition unit UA ‎250 and the master is correct.

  8. Check that the lamp no. 6 is off that is mean the unit at no recorded state.

  9. Check that the lamp no. 8 is off that is mean synchronous between the UA and UR2000 is correct.
