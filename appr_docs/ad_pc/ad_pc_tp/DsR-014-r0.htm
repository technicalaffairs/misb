Introduction

The RAZFE is a three-phase, static distance relay, which measures all phases and zones simultaneously to provide complete three-zone protection for all types of phase and ground faults.

Characteristic of the impedance measuring function in the line protection RAZFE 

This document lists the testing processes for RAZFE Relay by using XS92a tester, the first draft initiated by eng  Mohamed Soliman and tested by eng Mahmoud Abd Allah El Attar  at Sues 500 Canal zone

Work to be Carried Out

Steps to Prepare for Relay Testing

  1. Review technical reference material manufacturer's manual

  2. Obtain copy of relay setting from field maintenance file.

  3. Obtain outage for line protected by this relay.

  4. Check that the bus and line disconnect switches are opened togged

  5. Block protection signals to  Breaker trip coils and transfer trip schemes.

  6. Go to the switchgear and inspect the CT 's, and VT Miniature circuit breaker (M.C.B's) and all cabling supplying the relay.

  7. Check that the cables are in good condition and that all wire connections are tight and clean.

  4. When the trip occur the value of automatically stopped by free contact then read the value of the time of zone I in the terminal

  5. Repeat the above step to measuring the time of zone II , III , IV

Notes

How to Find "Free Contact " For Relay RAZFE

  1. Select any contact and measured DC (Not Found)

  2. Measured (Buzzer) by avometer (Not Found)

  3. Make trip (this contact-measured buzzes by avometer).

  4. Connect trip free contact to set test XS92 a.

    5.

End To End Test For Relay RAZFE

  1. Repeat the above test for zone II, and make sure that the trip occur at time of  Z2.

  2. Make bridge on receive signal.

  3. Make sure that the relay RAZFE trip zone II at time of zone I

    4.

Switch into Fault (SOIF) Test:

  1. Repeat the above test for zone IV but ( SQ = 0F1) in the set test XS92 a

  2. Make sure that zone IV trip at time of zone I.

Return Protection  Scheme And Associated Equipment to Service

  1. Return relay to service by with drawing the test plug handle From the relay.

  2. Make sure CT secondary are opened.

  3. Return Relay to service by restoring supply Form :Voltage Transformer (VT) miniature circuit breaks (MCBS) is closed

  4. Place the associated Breaker failure (B.F) protection Scheme back in service.

  5. Restore the previously blocked disturbance alarms and fault recorders associated with this protection .

  6. Return the relay power line carries (PLC) links to their normal position.

  7. Turn on the associated auto-reclusxe scheme.

  8. Turn on the powers supply DC to relay.

  9. Take crossed readings of all AC currents and voltages supplied to the relay and compare them to those for normal load conditions.

  10. Check that these readings agree with station Meters; MW, MVAR, Power factor, amperes, voltages.

  11. Place a copy of the test results in the field maintenance file and test sheet (report) .
