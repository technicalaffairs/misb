Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This document initiated by eng. Hoda Mohamed Ahmed at Alx zone, tested by eng. Heba Abdel Meniem at Dekhaila II - Sedi Kirir PS, accepted by eng. Madiha Al Nagar general director, and certified by eng Esam Sadeq general director of Alx network and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions              

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used (safety helmet , safety shoes ... etc).

  • Disconnect the DC supply and Insert the test socket

  • Connect the current terminal of the relay on the test socket to the testing equipment and be sure that the remaining current circuit is closed.

  • Connect the voltage terminal of the relay on the test socket to the testing equipment.

  • Connect the tripping output of the relay on the test socket to the stop contact of the testing equipment.

  • Remove the output from the relay, which initiate breaker failure.

  • Remove the output from the relay, which initiate event recorder and fault recorder.

Requirements for Relay to be back to Service

  1. Close the CB for testing the circuit

  2. Test the relay with any zone to check the trip circuit

  3. Remove the test terminal (voltage and current )

  4. Call the dispatching center to put back the feeder into service and after that check the following

  • The current circuit with the clamp meter for all phases

  •  The voltage circuit for all phases
