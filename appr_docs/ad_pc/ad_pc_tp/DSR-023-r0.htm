Introduction

This document lists the testing procedures P1 for L8b distance relay, which must be performed on an annually bases Y1 as indicated in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Raheem Salem Mohamed Ez, tested by eng. Khaled El-Etreby & eng. Atef Farh, revised by quality control eng. Tawfik Elabasery at EDEPCo., and approved by eng. Monear Mahmoud El-Wakeel: Head Sector of Damietta Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • Line Isolation and work permit required.

  • Make sure CT secondary circuits are shorted.

  • Voltage Supply to the relay may be alive even after the line is isolated.

  • Isolate DC supply to relay, when doing any adjustments or maintenance on the relay itself

  • Ensure all CT path elements are secure after any test disturbance.

Tools and Equipment

  • BBC Relay Test Set Type Siemene,

  • Ammeter 5 amp, Phase rotation meter,

  • Small slot screwdriver, Test leads

  • 5 amp. clip-on ammeter (optional)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Prepare the siemens tester.

  2. Disconnect the DC supply by pull the hand.

  3. Disconnect the miniature circuit breaker (m.c.b) for the potential transformer PT

  4. Make a short for the points (1,2,3,4,01,02,03,04) from down.

  5. Make a short for the point (01,02,03,04) from up 

  6. Remove the terminal "a" of the ssx 117.

  7. Turn the links from "01 to 11" and from 16 to 18 to horizontal position instead of the vertical position.

  8. Connect the DC supply by push the hand.

  9. Connect the voltage leads UR1 US1 UT1 UN to terminal (6,7,8,5).

  10. Connect the timer leads "11,22" to terminal x 1(411,511).

Test  Phase R

  1. Connect the current leads IR1 IN to terminal (2,1)

  2. Inject 100V & current 0.5A and adjust the angle to 287º then raise the current value to:
    1 amp. for long transmission lines and to 3:4 amp. for short transmission lines (10km)

  3. Disconnect the current injection and lower the voltage valve to the calculated valve of 3rd zone.

  4. Inject the current the notice the time if it is 3rd zone you must raise the voltage if it is start you must lower the voltage until obtain the end of the zones.

  5. Repeat 4.5 to obtain the end of 1st & 2nd zones.

Test Phase S

  1. Connect the current terminals between tester & 18b as follow:(Sto3),(Nto1)

  2. Repeat steps 2-5 in ph-R test.

Test Phase T

  1. Connect (T to 4)(N to1)

  2. Repeat steps 2-5 in ph-R test.
