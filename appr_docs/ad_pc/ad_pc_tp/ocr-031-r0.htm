Introduction

IKT-943 (Definite time over current relay). The instantaneous and definite time over current relays of the series IKT9 ...  have been designed for the phase and fault protection of electrical plant and networks, there are two basic types available type IKT 941-single-phase unit.

IKT943-Measurment in three phases or two phases and neutral current. These relays are solid state unit which can be. Used universally to per from protection and check functions in relation to electrical plant and networks. Its fields of application include

  • Phase and earth fault protection in general

  • Graded protection of lines in radial systems

  • Back up protection

  • Lower high current check for enabling or block other protection or regulating circuits.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety Helmet.

  • Ensure that the Line isolation is done.

  • Make sure CT secondary circuits are shorted before isolating the relay's current circuit and injection the test current

Tools and Equipment

  • Test unit XS92a (secondary injection unit)

  • Voltmeter, clip on ammeter, and test leads

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Preliminary steps to Prepare for Relay Testing

  1. Review technical reference material, manufacturers manual

  2. Obtain copy of relay setting from field maintenance file and compare it with the setting on the relay

  3. Obtain outage for line protected by this relay

  4. Check that the bus and line disconnect switches are opened and tagged

  5. Inspect CT secondary circuits, the VT miniature circuit breakers (MCB`S) and all cabling supplying the relay

  6. Use the station drawing to isolate the following

  •  Breaker trip coils

  • Breaker failure scheme associated with this relay

  • Disturbance alarms and fault recorder associated with this relay.

Testing the Relay and Protection output

  1. Carefully follow these test procedure steps from page 5 to page 10

  2. Verify the results match the relay setting if not consult technical services

  3. Test trip the breaker associated with the relay

Prior Starting the Testing Procedure(s) ensure the Following steps:

  1. This procedure is used to perform off load relay testing

  2. Check that the panel nameplate agrees with the EEHC designation of the line that has been taken out of service for maintenance

  3. Check there is no AC current from CT by using clip - on ammeter

  4. Check there is no AC voltage from VT by using voltmeter

  5. Check all external connections to the relay are in good condition and check that the terminal block wires are tight

RMX-913: Directional relay

The RMX-913 directional relay are designed primarily for use in directional phase fault. protection schemes in conjunction with either the definite time over current relay IKT 943 or the (e) IDMT relay IKC 913. They can determine the direction of all kinds of phase faults, providing the fault current is distinctly higher than the load current

Main Features are:

  1. Current and voltage signals evaluated digitally

  2. Uses positive - sequence components of current and voltage

  3. Low burden

  4. Setting for characteristic angle and width of operational sector

  5. Harmonic filters incurrent and voltage inputs

  6. Comprehensive self-monitoring

Principle of Operation

The three phase-to-phase system voltages are connected to the two input p.t,s, the circuit, which follows derives the positive - sequence component u1 similarly, two phase to - phase current are obtained from the input c.t,s with their two primary windings each, and the positive sequence current I1, filtered out. u1, and I1, are both referred to R phase
