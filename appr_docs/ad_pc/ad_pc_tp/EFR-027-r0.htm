Introduction

The RSF5 relay is current relay, 5A, provided to circuits to be earth fault protection relay. This procedure details the steps required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of the relay

The first draft initialed and tested by eng. Tarik Mohamed Ahmed, at Maghagha SS, accepted by General director eng. Aemad Makalaa, and approved by eng. Yahya Mohamed Mahmoud head sector..

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety shoes, safety helmet ... etc

  • Line isolation and work permit required

  • Make sure CT secondary circuits are shorted before isolating the relay current circuits for current injection

  • Outage arrangement must be done.

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection tester

  • Voltmeter,Ammeter

  • Test leads

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Make sure CT secondary circuits are shorted at the incoming test blokes before isolating the relay current circuits for testing

  2. Look at field maintenance file and compare the position of the setting switches on relay with those shown on the setting sheet

  3. Make sure relay contact was untied before testing

Relay Test

  1. Switch of the dc mcd

  2. Untie terminals to relay`s contacts from terminal blocks and connect them to stop contact sockets of the tester

  3. Clean the relay`s contact and inspect tying

  4. Connect current leads from the tester to current terminals of the relay (4-6)

  5. Test operation value of the current setting for the relay by increase injected current and record the Iop, and record Ir reset value by decrease injected current

  6. Compare between the results and the setting

  7. Record results in this table
