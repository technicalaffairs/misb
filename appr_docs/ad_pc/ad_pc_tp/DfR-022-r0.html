Introduction

This document details the testing procedure P1, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

This procedure required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of differential protection of tap changer which in type (∏ Z T -11/2-T) from "USSR"

The (∏ Z T-11/2 - T) relay function to protect the tap changer of the main transformer differentially.

The differential relays of all types designation consist of a service element which is an electromagnetic relay. This work which will be carried out at this document is divided into two sections.

The first section indicate the preliminary steps to prepare for relay testing describes the activities required prior to testing some of these steps could be done before the a actual test

The second section is the preparation for testing descrides the steps to setup the equipment for performing the actual test

The first draft initiated by eng. Abd El Hakeem Mohamed Ahmed, tested at 07 March 2005 at Cairo 500 by eng. Ibrahim Frage, approved by general director eng. Ramadan Rageb and certified by eng. Ahmed Saied Abd El Azez general director of quality control and MPIS ME zone coordinator, EETC.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses..etc

  • Be sure that the main transformer is switched off from all sides 500,220,11kv

  • Disconnect the terminals in the row terminals of differential relay panel which isolate all sources of DC and AC

  • Check the absence of DC and AC voltage at the rear of all elements using AVO meter

  • Be sure that the CT secondary  circuits are shorted and isolated from switch yard at the current test block

  • Disconnect DC supplies which feed the control circuit of all circuit breakers 500,220,11kv

  • Disconnect DC supply of differential protection.

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection equipment for AC and DC injection

  • Portable test set type (Y ∏ Z-1) and (Y ∏ Z -2)

  • Multi-meter for measuring voltage and current

  • Clip on ammeter, test leads

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect the MCB's for dc protection signal.

  2. Short circuits for C.T secondary terminals

  3. Open circuits for VT secondary terminals

  4. Checking Dc for all relays and disconnect any terminals has dc supply.

  5. Make mechanical checking for all the relays in the panel.

  6. Prepare all required test equipment type (Y ∏ Z-1) and (Y∏ Z -2)

  7. Switch on supply of test equipment

  8. Injection AC current by portable test set

  9. Take reading point (voltage and current) for each injection by (AVO)

  10. Record all operating values in the test sheet

  11. The tables of operating value should be verified as appear in sheet
