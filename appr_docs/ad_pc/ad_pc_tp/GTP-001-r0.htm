Introduction

The scheme protection system for gas turbine is manufactured by siemens and this Scheme is solid state type. Scheme protection protect generator, step up transformer and unit auxiliary transformer. There is table for protective relays.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Raheem Salem Mohamed Ez, tested by eng. Khaled El-Etreby & eng. Atef Farh, revised by quality control eng. Tawfik Elabasery at EDEPCo., and approved by eng. Monear Mahmoud El-Wakeel: Head Sector of Damietta Power Station.

 Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •
  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Gas turbine out of service and work permit required.

  • Make sure CT secondary circuits are shorted.

  • Voltage transformer miniature circuit breakers (m.c.b) is opened

Tools and Equipment

  • Siemens test set which consists of two units

  • Current unit type 7VP48.

  • Voltage unit type 7VP49.

  • Voltmeter, Ammeter, Test leads.    

Work to be Carried Out

Preliminary Steps to Prepare for Relay Testing

  1. Review technical reference material manufactures / manual.

  2. Obtain copy of relay setting from field maintenance file.

  3. Obtain outage for unit protected by this relay

  4. Check that the bus and unit disconnect switches are opened and tagged.

  5. Inspect the secondary circuits of CT , VT miniature circuit breakers.

  6. (MCB) and all cabling supplying the relay.

  7. Block any disturbance alarms or fault recorders associated with this protection.

Preparation of Test

  1. Place the two cases one on the top of other (the voltage unit on the top of current unit)

  2. Connect the two units together by the cable connector 7 XV6201 between test plug "X2" in current unit and test plug "X1" in voltage unit.

  3. Connect the power supply cable 7XV6200 (3*380 V) between the plug "X1" in the current unit and the source (careful the positive sequence).

  4. Connect an ammeter with the socket "17 ,18" in current unit and voltmeter with the socket "5,6" in voltage unit.

  5. Connect the timer leads on the tester (11,12) to CHA12X02 :( 448,449 ) .

Generator Differential Protection

  1. To permit the relay operate the current must be pass through the operating coil.

  2. Inject the current to the relay for each phase of the two sides the current leads put as the follows:
                               CHA 12-X01        neutral side        Output side
                                 PH-R                    128.136            142.136
                                 PH-S                     131.136            144.136
                                 PH-T                     134.136            146.136

  3. Determine the pick up valve and drop out value and time of pick up value 

Reverse Power Protection

  1. Disconnect the m.c.b. for P.T. F24, F25 in cabinet BAB 36

  2. Disconnect the links CHA11-X01 (203,215,225,232,233,234)

  3. Bridged (232,233,234)

  4. Connect the voltage leads UR, US, UT, UN to terminal (203,215.225.232)

  5. Connect the current leads IR, IN to terminal CHA11-X01(97.98)

  6. Inject 3-Ø voltage UL = 100v

  7. Inject small current to adjust the angle at 180º then increase it until lamp light (Ipu =   mA) them determine the time and angle zone, at 180º again decrease the current until lamp distinguish (Id.o)

  8. Determine the angle zone for different values of current

  9. Connect the current leads Is, In to terminal CHA11-X01 (101,102) then repeat the 3 pervious steps.

  10. Connect the current leads It, In to terminal CHA11-X01 (104,105) then repeat the previous step.

Under Impedance Protection

  1. Disconnect the m.c.b. for P.T. F25 in cabinet BAB 36.

  2. Disconnect the links for CHA11-X01 (201,202,211,212,221,222,231)

  3. Connect the current leads between tester PH-R and CHA11-X01 (81,82)

  4. Connect the voltage between tester (UR, US, UT, UN) and terminal CHA11-X01 (201,211,221,231) and bridged both of (201,202) (211,212) (221,222)

Under Voltage Test

  1. Apply 3-Ø voltage by tester 100 v

  2. Decrease the voltage until lamp light (Upu)

  3. In crease the voltage until lamp distinguish (Ugo out) 

Over Current test

  1. Increase the current until lamp L1 light (Ipu) and time t3

  2. Decrease the current until lamp L1 distinguish (Ido)

  3. Connect the current leads between Is, In PH-T tester are terminal CHA11-X01(89-90) and repeat as the previous step

Under Impedance test 

  1. Inject current equal the Ipu

  2. Inject voltage to 100 v and decrease the voltage until lamp T1 light for each angle of (0º,90º,180º,270º)

  3. Repeat the pervious steps to test PH-S & PH-T

Second Fault PH-PH

  1. Connect the current terminal between the IR,Is tester and CHA11-X01 (81,85) and bridge between CHA11-X01 (82,86)

  2. Inject the P.U value Ipu

  3. Decrease the voltage until lamp t1 light for each angle of (0º,90º,180º,270º) then record the voltage value .

  4. Measure the time by connect terminal tester No (10,21) and terminal point CHA12-X02 No.(448,449) spare contact.

Negative Phase Sequence

  1. Put current leads to ph-s CHA11-X01 (69,70)

  2. Put current leads to ph-t CHA11-X01 (73,74)

  3. Determine p.u value and d.o value.

  4. Determine the definite & inverse trip time from CHA12-X02(448-449) while bridged C.B on condition CHA11-X02 (504,532)

100 % Stator Earth Fault

  1. Bridge the each two points in CHA 12-X01 (282,288),(284,290),(286,292)

  2. Connect the self test switch (stand still position)

  3. Put variable resistance between the out put phases in cabinet BAB 35 or 36 or 37 and earth

  4. Make R=α not connect to earth determine the following current by inserting ammeter leads in two socket of the relay and removing the pin to out position and measure this value and record it .

  5. Make R = 0.0 earth directly and determine the current by the same ammeter

  6. and measure this value and record it .

  7. Determine the Rpu by decreasing the resistance until the relay operate lamp light record it , determine the current at the Rpu record it.

Under excitation

  2. Disconnect the links for CHA 11-X01 (213,214,223,224)

  3. Connect the voltage leads (Us,Ut) to terminal CHA11-X01 (213,223) and bridged (213,214), bridged (223,224)

  4. Connect the current leads Ir,In (tester) to terminal CHA11-X01 (93,94)

  5. Remove the two wires from CHA11-X01 (262,263) and connect there to injection DCV devices where 263 is (+) ve and 262 is (-) ve

Rotor E/F

  1. Put variable resistance between the slip ring or brush and the earth.

  2. Connect the self test switch (stand still position)

  3. Decrease the resistance until lamp II (alarm) light Ralarm

  4. Decrease again until the lamp III (trip) light Rtrip

  5. Measure the two values Ralarm, Rtrip and record it

Dead Machine Relay 

Relay Type : RAGUA, ABB

  1. Disconnect the m.c.b for P.T. F 24, F 25 in BAB36 cabinet

  2. Disconnect the link for point CHA 11 x 01 (288,269) & (265,266)

Under voltage element test

  1. Connect the voltage leads to points CHA 19 x 01 : (268,269) pro, tec

  2. Increase the voltage to 100 v to release the under voltage element

  3. Wait 4 sec. the fuse failure flag is appear

  4. Decrease the voltage until fuse failure is release, record this value as P.U value

  5. Increase the voltage until ?

  6. Repeat the steps from 3.7 but the voltage leads connected to terminals CHAU x 1 : (265,266)

Over Current Element

  1. Connect the current leads to the terminals CHA 11, x 01 : (98,106) ph.R

  2. Increase the current until the trip flag is appeared, record this pu value

  3. Decrease the current until the flag red ease, record this d.o value

  4. Repeat, the steps from to 3 for ph.s & ph.T but with connecting the current leads to terminals CHA 11, x 01 : (102,107) ph.s & (105,108) ph.T

Functions of Dead Machine Test

  1. Connect the voltage leads to the terminal CHA 11, x01 :(268,269) & (265,266)

  2. Connect the current leads to the terminal CHA 11, x01 :(98,106)

  3. Connect the timer to free contact

  4. Inject the pu. value of current at voltage element is released and not released

  5. Measure the time of trip, record this voltage

  6. Repeat the steps from 2-5 to the other phases

Back up function

  1. Inject the voltage 100 v as pervious

  2. Inject the pu. current for ph. R as pervious

  3. Decrease the voltage to p.u. value, trip command appear

  4. Measure the time as pervious

Trip cct test

  1. Adjust the current to p.u. value, while the voltage equal zero

  2. Close the unit C.B s without isolators

  3. Inject the current, the unit C.B s will tripped

 

 

Over and Under frequency

  1. Disconnect the m.b.c. of the secondary circuit of the potential transformer.

  2. Disconnect the links of CHA11-X01(206,208).

  3. Connect the voltage leads to points terminals CHA11-X01(206,208).

  4. Inject the voltage, increase the voltage to Un. (normal)

  5. Increase the frequency until the relay picks up, record this value F > pu, then decrease the frequency until the relay drop off, record this value F > do.   (do: drop out)

  6. Measure this time from free contact where the relay picks up.

  7. Again adjust the frequency to Fn. (n: nominal)

    8.

  8. Decrease the frequency until the relay picks up, record this value F < pu, then increase the frequency until the relay drop off, record this value F < do.

  9. Measure this time from free contact where the relay picks up.

  10. When the relay operate, decrease the voltage until the relay block, record this value U block, then increase the voltage until the relay ready, record this value U ready.
