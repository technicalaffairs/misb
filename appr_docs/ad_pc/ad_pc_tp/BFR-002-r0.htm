Introduction

This procedure details the steps required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of the relay. It is recommended this procedure be carried out on an annually bases. The document is formatted the way to minimize the possibility of error causing personal injury or damage to equipment.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Raheem Salem Mohamed Ez, tested by eng. Khaled El-Etreby & eng. Atef Farh, revised by quality control eng. Tawfik Elabasery at EDEPCo., and approved by eng. Monear Mahmoud El-Wakeel: Head Sector of Damietta Power Station.

 Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • CB and the equipment off i.e. out of service,

  • Isolated trip sequel for distribute base bar

Tools and Equipment

  • Test set Siemens type 7VP48 & 7VP49  

  • Screw driver  

Work to be Carried Out

How B.F. Relay Operate

(Over Current or Auxiliary Contact from CB close) + protection trip lead to BF, Signal trip

  1. Ensure that the CB and its equipment out of service.

  2. Ensure that the BF trip for BB was isolated.

  3. Remove the plugs of matrix for t 2 (BB equipment in service which locate in the same bay)

  4. Check equipment of that breaker must be out of service.

  5. Connect the current leads to
              Breaker on BB1 X11  (12,14) & (22,24) & (32,34)
              Breaker on BBII X12  (12,14) & (22,24) & (32,34)
              Breaker coupler X13   (12,14) & (22,24) & (32,34)

  6. Increase the current value until lamp light then record the (Ipu) and then decrease the current until lamp distinguished then record (Id.o)

  7. Inject the pick up value of current while the trip signal points:
    X (11 or 12 or 13) (702,712) or (703,713) are bridged for breaker which be tested C.B.X BB1 X11., C.B.X BBII X12, C.B.X Bus coupler X13

  8. Trip signal from B.F t1, t2 are status. Make abridge between CB auxiliary contact closed (701,711) while the trip signal points (702,712) or (703,713) are bridged also.

  9. In each case of steps 8, 9 we notice the lamps t1, t2 are light and record the phoenix relay which is energized, measure the operating time from free contact.

  10. Putt t2 to setting infinity then close the CB without isolators and repeat the pervious steps 7, 8
