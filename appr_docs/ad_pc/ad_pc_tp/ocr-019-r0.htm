Introduction

This document lists the testing procedures P1, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The MCGG63 relay is designed specially for over current with time delayed element the MCGG63 manufactured by GEC

The first draft initiated by eng. Fahmy Abdel Khalek, tested by eng. Hanan El_Badrawy at 18 June 2003, revised by quality control eng. Adel Abdel Kader El-Nagar at EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Mahmoud Saker and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of  TAlkha PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

Tools and Equipment

  • Sverker 650 test set ( secondary injection current )

  • Current test plug.

  • Clamp ampere or Avo meter .

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Make sure C.T secondary circuits are shorted at the incoming terminal blocks before isolating the relay current circuit for test

  2. Review the manufacture manual, obtain a copy of relay setting from field maintenance file, and compare it with the setting on the relay.

Testing Procedures

  1. Inject the current from the plug test on first phase (R), 2nd phase (S) and 3rd phase (T) until the green led lights then record I reset in test sheet, continue injection the current until the red led lights then record I pick up and calculate the reset factor of the relay ( I reset /I pick up ).

  2. From contact trip or lock out relay take the time delay and test the tripping OFC.B of this relay and recording the value in test sheet

  3. Insert the plug test after setting the Sverker on position off

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service

  1. Return relay to service by removing the plug test

  2. Make sure CT and VT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  3. If any equipment associated with the relay has been isolated, it should be returned to service by the following restoration steps:
           - Breaker trip coils.
           - Breaker failure scheme associated this relay.
           - Disturbance alarm and fault recorder associated with this relay.     Make sure CT and VT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  4. Return the work permit .
