Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

MCT 139 is normally used as a back up protection for the circuit breaker when the circuit breaker fails disconnect when other protection operates this instrument is called (circuit breaker failure protection).

The first draft initiated by: eng. Fahmy Abe El Khalek and tested by eng. Hanan El Badrawy and revised by quality control eng. Adel Abd Alkdar El Nagar. at EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Mahmoud Saker and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talka p.s

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Sverker 650 Programma test set.
  • Avometer or clamp ampere

Work to be Carried Out

Note: This system is one and half CB and the CB "QF2" is open

  1. Set the test plug type (MMLG) in its location

  2. Make two Jumper on the test plug between points (13-14 & 15-16) to transmit the DC to the relay

  3. Make Junction between the points (F14) on the test plug and points (A13) on the relay

  4. Remove the trip relays type (MVAJ13) and (MVAJ23) when the test occur

  5. Connect the timer with the Sverker and the points (B1,B3) on the timer type

  6. Inject the current between 24,22 phase (R) & 26,22 phase (S) & 28,22 phase (T) on the test plug

  7. Record the relay I pickup value when green and red lamps lights at the same time

  8. Record test time at I pickup at test sheet

  9. Decrease I injection until the green lamp off

  10. Record the I reset

  11. Make a final check that all switches on the relay are set according to the setting

  12. Remove the test plug

  13. Return back the relays MVAJ13, MVAJ23 to its normal position.
