Introduction

This procedure details the steps required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of the 220kv busbar differential relay I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1in the title block.

The document is formatted the way to minimize the possibility of error causing personal injury or damage to equipment.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Raheem Salem Mohamed Ez, tested by eng. Khaled El-Etreby & eng. Atef Farh at 10 July 2002, revised by quality control eng. Tawfik Elabasery at EDEPCo., and approved by eng. Monear Mahmoud El-Wakeel: Head Sector of Damietta Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, Helmet).

  • Disconnect the DC supply (F001, F002) .

  • Remove the trip cards 7TR29004 .

  • Connect the DC supply (F001, F002) .

  • Connect the terminal socket between the tester and the relay .

  • Normal operation of station two busbar in service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Current test unit 7VP48, voltage test unit 7VP49

  • Safety Helmet and safety shoes.

Work to be Carried Out

First test during test socket 

                All equipment in service, two BBs energize.

Test during socket “7VX6642-4H” & selection switch “S11”  which select the selection (BorA1orB1)  For test and isolate its trip signal as follow:

                  B   normal operation

                 A1  test and isolate trip of BBI  + check

                 B1  test and isolate trip of BBII + check

 

Test BBI + CHECK

  1. Select the section “A1” by “S11”  to test the BB1 and check.

  2. Select L1 & N in the tester and inject current through the test socket increase the current until the lamp lights { I --- trip&  II ---- start   &  III  ---- block  } for BBI. Record the values of I trip , I start & I block .

  3. Measure mv Dc for operating coil through Bu1, Bu2 and measure mv Dc for Restraining coil through Bu3, Bu4 at lamp light , record these values .

  4. Select L2 & N in the tester and inject current through the test socket increase the current until the lamp lights { I ---- trip  &  II ---- start   &  III  ---- block  } for check . Record the values of I trip , I start & I block .

  5. Measure mv Dc for operating coil through Bu1, Bu2 and measure mv Dc for Restraining coil through Bu3, Bu4 at each lamp light , record these values.

Test BB II + CHECK

  1. Select the section “B1” by “S11”  to test the BBII and check.

  2. Select L1 & N in the tester and inject current through the test socket increase the current until the lamp lights { I ---- trip  &  II ---- start   &  III  ---- block  } for BBII. Record the values of I trip , I start & I block .

  3. Measure mv Dc for operating coil through Bu1, Bu2 and measure mv Dc for Restraining coil through Bu3, Bu4 at each lamp light , record these values .

  4. Select L2 & N in the tester and inject current through the test socket increase the current until the lamp lights { I ---- trip  &  II ---- start   &  III  ---- block  } for check . Record the values of I trip , I start & I block .

  5. Measure mv Dc for operating coil through Bu1, Bu2 and measure mv Dc for Restraining coil through Bu3, Bu4 at each lamp light , record these values

2nd test during terminals :

Through disconnected equipment:

  1. Disconnect the DC supply (F001, F002) .

  2. Remove the trip cards 7TR29004 .

  3. Connect the DC supply (F001, F002) .

  4. Put the time ( t2 ) of B.F for that equipment = ∞ and remove the plug pin from matrix for this BB.

Ph- L1 :

  1. Connect the leads L1 – N on tester siemens and the terminals D….X810  ( 1, 8 ) .

  2. Inject current and increase the current until the lamp lights { I ---- trip  &  II ---- start   &  III  ---- block  } for this BB and Check.

  3. Record the values of I trip , I start & I block .

  4. Measure mv Dc for operating coil through Bu1, Bu2 and measure mv Dc for Restraining coil through Bu3, Bu4 at each lamp light for this BB, record these values

  5. Measure mv Dc for operating coil through Bu1, Bu2 and measure mv Dc for Restraining coil through Bu3, Bu4 at each lamp light for Check, record these values .

 

Ph- L2:

  1. Repeat the steps from 5 – 8 .

Ph – L3 :

  1. Repeat the steps from 5 – 8 .

  2. Insert the trip card for this equipment

  3. Adjust the trip value and inject it the flag of this card is appear

  4. Close the C.B. for this equipment without isolators and inject the trip value to trip the C.B

  5. Return the state of relay .
