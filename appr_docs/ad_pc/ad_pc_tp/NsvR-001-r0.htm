Introduction

The PHΦ-1M-T relay is negative sequence voltage filter relay 100V provided to circuits to be sure that any under voltage because of fault not for other reasons. This procedure details the steps required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of the relay

Ahmed Saied AbdelAzez: MPIS and QC General Director at ME zone and approved by eng Attala Fahem Attala: ME zone Protection Head Sector

Safety Precautions

  • Line isolation is required.

  • Work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

    •

  • Make sure VT secondary circuits are opened before testing the relay

  • Make sure relay contacts are isolated from circuits

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection tester

  • Voltmeter

  • Ammeter

  • Test leads 

Work to be Carried Out

Preparations

  1. Make sure VT secondary circuits are opened and isolated from the relay

  2. Look at field maintenance file and compare the position of the setting switches on relay with those shown on the setting sheet

  3. Make sure relay contact was untied before testing

 

 

Relay test

  1. Switch off the dc mcb

  2. Untie terminals to relay contacts from terminal blocks and connect them to stop contact sockets of the tester

  3. Clean the relay contact and inspect tying

  4. Connect voltage leads from the tester to voltage terminals of the relay on terminal block as following : Make bridge between terminal of phases R&S of the relay on terminal block and connect the voltage test leads on terminals of phase (R-S) and phase T, increase voltage value to open the relay contact and record Vr reset value, so decrease the voltage value until the relay contact close then record the value Vop

  5. Repeat 4 steps for phase (S-T) and R.

  6. Repeat 4 step for phase (T-R) and S.

  7. Compare between the results and the setting

  8. Record results in this table
