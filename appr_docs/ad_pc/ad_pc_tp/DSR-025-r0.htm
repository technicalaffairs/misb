Introduction

This document lists the testing procedures P1 for SD135A distance relay, which must be performed on an annually bases Y1 as indicated in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Raheem Salem Mohamed Ez, tested by eng. Khaled El-Etreby & eng. Atef Farh, revised by quality control eng. Tawfik Elabasery at EDEPCo., and approved by eng. Monear Mahmoud El-Wakeel: Head Sector of Damietta Power Station.

 Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Line Isolation.

  • Make sure CT secondary circuits are shorted.

  • Voltage Supply to the relay may be alive even after the line is isolated.

  • Isolate DC supply to relay, when doing any adjustments or maintenance on the relay itself

  • Ensure all CT path elements are secure after any test disturbance.

Tools and Equipment

  • Relay Test Set Type 7VP48  & 7VP49  Siemens,

  • Ammeter  , Phase rotation meter

  • Small slot screw driver, Test leads

Work to be Carried Out

Distance Relay Main (SD135A)

First Test  (By socket"PSN 1000")

  1. Test during socket "PSN 1000" & selection switch (service or test position).

  2. The line is out of service.

  3. Disconnect the miniature circuit breaker (m.c.b) for the potential transformer PT

  4. Connect the voltage leads UR, US, UT, UN (5,6,7,8,) in socket to UR, US, UT, UN  in tester.

  5. Connect the current leads IR, IS, IT ,IN (1,2,3,4) to socket to IR,  IS, IT, IN in tester 

  6. Inject 3-طphase voltage UL =100V & 1-phase current

  7. Inject 100V voltage and determine the P.U. value of current and determine the zone and measure the time in socket (9,11) to the timer point in tester( 11,22 ).

  8. Inject the Ipu. and determine the P.U voltage at the angles (0,90)

  9. Turn the socket in service position.

Second Test

  1. Disconnect the miniature circuit breaker (m.c.b) for the potential transformer PT

  2. Disconnect the voltage terminal F-X1 (61,71,81,91)

  3. Connect the voltage leads UR, US, UT, UN to terminal F-X1(61,71,81,91)

  4. Connect the current leads IR,IN to terminal F-X1 (12,44)

  5. Inject 3-ط voltage UL = 100 & 1-ط current

  6. Inject 100V voltage and determine the P.U. value of current and determine the zone and measure the time (402,502) (spare contact).

  7. Inject the Ipu. and determine the P.U voltage at the angles (0,09)

  8. Repeat the previous steps for Is, In (44.22) and It, In (44,22)

  9. Connect the voltage terminal F-X1 (61,71,81,91) & connect the miniature circuit breaker (m.c.b) for the potential transformer PT
