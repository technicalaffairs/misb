Introduction

Denver Instrument is easy to use, easily adapted to most operating  environments through simple adjustments from the keyboard. Denver Instrument will display the weight directly in grams.

Safety precautions

  • The  weighing table should be solid and free from vibration¸ drafts (such as opening doors or windows) and as level as possible.

  • Corners of rooms are best as they are less prone to vibrations.

  • Don’t install the balance near heaters or air conditioners.

  • Don’t install the balance in  direct sunlight.

  • Don’t use the balance near other equipment which produces magnetic fields.

  • Suitable room dry and clean

Accuracy

To ensure that you get the most from your balance:

  1. Warm-up (plug-in) the balance for at least one hour.

  2. The weighing room should be kept clean and dry.

  3. Make each weighing quickly to avoid errors due to changes in environ conditions.   

  4. Close the breeze break doors to keep out drafts.

  5. Don’t drop things upon the weighing pan¸ or place a weight  beyond the range balance  on the weighing pan. .  

Tools and Equipment

Worked to be carried out

Display ON : OFF & Power Errors 

The balance does a self check when you  connect the AC adapter or press the ON: OFF key. If there is a problem, you will get an error display. To clear this error press the Tare  key.

Calibration 

  1. Warm up the balance for at least one hour with nothing  on the weighing pan, Turn the display on.

  2. Press and hold the Tare key the display will read zero.

  3. Place known calibration mass on the pan ( for Exp 100, 200 gm).

  4. Press the auto. Cal  key.

  5. The balance will display the calibration  mass( Cal –100-)–(Cal -200-). Then the balance measures the calibration mass value.

  6. Remove the calibration mass from the pan, The balance will return to normal weighing mode automatically.

Simple Weighing

  1. Turn the display on using the  ON:OFF key. After a moment “ zero “  will be displayed if (zero) not be display press the tare key,(zero) will be displayed.

  2. If you are using a tare container, place it on the weighing pan, The display will show the container weight.

  3. Press the tare key  to cancel the weight.

  4. Place the object  to be weighed in the container.

  5. Wait for the stabilization and read the weight.

  6. Remove the object from the pan.

  7. Turn the display OFF using the  ON:OFF key.
