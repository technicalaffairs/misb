Safety Precautions

In case of stopping work put the probe in demi. water.

Function and Operational Safety

The smooth functioning and operational safety of the instrument can only be guaranteed under the climatic conditions specified.

If the instrument was transported from a cold environment to a warm environment, the formation of condensate can lead to the faulty functioning of the instrument. In this event, wait until the temperature of the instrument reaches room temperature before putting the instrument back into operation.

Safe Operation

If safe operation is no longer possible, the instrument must be taken out of service and secured against inadvertent operation.

Safe operation is no longer possible if:

  1. The instrument has been damaged in transport.

  2. The instrument has been stored under adverse conditions for a lengthy period of time.

  3. The instrument is visibly damaged.

  4. The instrument no longer operates as described in the manual.

Note: Why determine/set up the cell constant?

  1. Due to ageing, the cell constant slightly changes. As a result, an inexact measured value is displayed. Calibration determines the current value of the cell constant and stores this value in the instrument. Thus, you should calibrate at regular intervals.
