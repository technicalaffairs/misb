Introduction

The proposed demineralization plant consists of two indentical trains, to supply demineralized water to power station.

Each train mainly consists of the  followings:

  1. one cationic vessel          capacity   700   liter.

    2.

  2. one anionic vessel           capacity    750  liter.

  3. one mixed bed vessel     capacity   250  liter cationic resin and 300  liter of anionic resin.

Cations Resin Type RELITE   CF/CR

RELITE  CF/CR  is a gel type strongly acidic cation exchange resin characterized by a high DVB content.

This resin is supplied in a calibrated screen grade and in regenerated form (H+) to permit the direct use in counter-current systems. Its composition complies with the existing food processing rules and regulation.
