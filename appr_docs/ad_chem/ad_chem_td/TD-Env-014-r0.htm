Introduction

This document covers the oil separation system in Abu Sultan Power Plant ( waste water treatment ).

The “Rotating  oil drum skimmer” "industrial model"  is a continuous and selective device for the recovery of decanted hydrocarbons on the surface of oily water decantation  tanks. It is designed to use for the treatment on predeoilers,  on ApI tanks, on food industry  and on any surface of  water  where a film of oil is present. This device, continually rotating on the surface of the water, picks up the hydrocarbons without water and any oleophilic compounds which are then recovered  using a fixed-position scraper.

Information

The originally of this process is that the amount of water remaining in the recovered oil is very low thus making it unnecessary  to dehydrate the residue through heating or centrifugation.

The continuous  recovery of hydrocarbons wake it possible to reduce loss through evaporation, often high when dealing with volatile compounds.

Environmental speaking, the disappearance of smells near the deoiling tanks is noticeable.

As supernatant oil are continually recovered, the quality of the water coming from the decantation tank is drastically improved.  Finishing treatment is this easier due to a greatly reduced in flow of oil

From a safety point, the recovery of hydrocarbons with reduced evaporation of volatile compounds makes the deoiling operation inert as far as the risk of fire is concerned.

Motor Reducer

  • Lubricated air motor,

  • Reducer, worm and gear-wheel driven, the rotation speed transmitted to the drum is 5 to 60 r.p.m.

Oil Recovery Unit

This unit consists of two elements

  • A scraper made up of 500 mm long elements; the section in contact with the drum is a bevelled blade made of hydrocarbon-resistant polymer.

  • A gutter in 3 mm sheet-metal; this gutter with a bottom on a slant is equipped at one end with an opening to allow hydrocarbons outlet in an existing pipe through the medium of a funnel.

Paintwork and  Covering

The metallic parts are protected against damp, corrosive surroundings.

Directives

Mechanical Directive

Drum Maintenance

Oil Recovery Unit

Cleaning the Scraper Every 800 h of Service

Clean the scraper when the solids start to prevent a just recovery of oil on the generating cylinder. The cleaning process  may be carried out using a hose–pipe ( preferably with hot water ) under pressure.

Changing the Scraper after 3000 h of Service

when the attrition caused by the elastomer scraper is not uniform or when the scraper no longer touches, it is time to change the scraper bands to ensure that the drum continues to work at its maximum efficiency.   

Skimmer Casing

As the quality of the skimmer casing will not deteriorate in time, it is only necessary to check periodically that it has not been damaged by sharp solids, and that it continually covers the cylinder.

Note: The cleaning or changing will depend on nature of the product to treat. Mainly concerning hydrocarbons viscosity and sediments content of the  mixture.

Motor Reducer Maintenance

This equipment is monobloc, put together  in factory and fixed by reducer's body.

The motor is built rotary type, the vanes take up their own wear and will last 5000–15000 hour depending  upon speed, method of oiling operating pressure, the type of shaft seal used does not lend itself to operating pressure above 100 p.s.t (6.89 bar-metric), the air motor is cooled by expanded air.
