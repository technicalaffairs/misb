Introduction

This document covers the typical plan for the chemical cleaning of a boiler from the investigation to the final reporting of the cleaning results.

This document was initiated by MDEPC inspection & quality control chemist: Saad Sadeek; and revised by EEHC, quality control head sector chemists: Mohamed Tawfik and Safaa Mamdouh.

This document is accepted by eng. Samir Abd El-Galil: MDEPC inspection and quality control general director & MPIS coordinator, and certified by engineer; Mervat Ezz Eldeen Mahmoud; MDEPC inspection and Quality Control Head Sector.

Information

Investigation of Boiler

  • Specification and construction materials.

  • Steam generation rate, pressure, operation time, etc.

  • Condition of water treatment, e.g., feedwater and boiler water qualities and the treatments.

  • Kind of fuel.

  • History of operation and cleaning.

Scale Investigation

  • Scale analysis.

  • Determination of the amount of scale.

  • Determination of the scale formation rate.

  • Investigation of the deterioration degree of material strength.

 

 

 

 

Dissolving Test of Sample Scale

  • Scale dissolving test.

  • Test of material strength.

  • Investigation of environmental condition such as regulations for wastewater discharge.

  • Availability of wastewater treatment facilities at cleaning site.

  • Wastewater treatment test.

Determination of Cleaning Specifications and Procedures

  • Determination of cleaning method.

  • Determination of the wastewater or gas treatment method.

  • Confirmation of power, feedwater and heat sources.

  • Preparation of cleaning flow chart and operation procedures.

Inspection

  • Visual inspection.

  • Determination of corrosion rate by test coupons.

  • Determination of amount of removed scale.

  • Confirmation of the operating condition after cleaning.

Report

  • Reporting for the cleaning operation.

  • Consulting for the maintenance and water treatment method.
