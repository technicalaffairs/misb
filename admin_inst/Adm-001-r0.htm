  1. This test its reagent (Brucine) is carcinogenic, and this method doesn't become used at modern ASTM after 1981. Note: We Highly Recommended not to use this method but use Ion Chromatograph Method for the determination of Nitrate Ion in Water which will be Wtr-006-r1.
