Introduction

This document out lines the instrument inspection steps I1, required for Auma Control Valve, type: SAREX   10.1 – F10, Com No.: 604749, Ip  /  67  16   1/Min, No.: 1194MD50205, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

    •

  • Close manual isolating valves after and before control valve.

  • Disconnect power supply.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes, … etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Ammeter.

  • Screw Driver.

  • Open end Spanner set.

  • Dc power supply.

Work to be Carried Out 

Steps to Prepare for test

  1. Review technical reference material and manufacture’s manual.

  2. Obtain copy of valve field maintenance file.

  3. Remove cover and disconnect wires for and isolate the wires using tape feed back card connect 24 VDC power supply to feed back card terminal.

  4. Connect ammeter to one terminal of feed back card and the other terminal to power supply.

Start Test

  1. Close valve manually and record ammeter reading (check Zero feed back = 4 ma) and  check close limit switch

  2. Open valve manually and record ammeter reading (Check Span feed back =20 ma) and check open limit switch.

  3. Compare the ammeter reading with the field maintenance file if there is any error in Zero or close limit switch repeat step 1 and adjust zero and close limit switch.

  4. Compare the ammeter reading with the field maintenance file if there is any error in Span or open limit switch repeat step 2 and adjust zero and open limit switch.

  5. Repeat steps 3, and 4 until reach the required ammeter reading.

Return Control Valve to Service

  1. Return control valve to service by removing the ammeter leads and power supply from the feed back card terminal.

  2. Reconnect the wires of feed back card.

  3. Connect power supply.

  4. Open manual isolating valves.

  5. Place a copy of test result in the field maintenance file.
