Introduction

This document outlines the auxiliary equipments inspection steps for Gas Turbine I1, which must be performed every 150 hours (what steps at 150 hours), 1000, and 2000 operating Hours

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety helmet, shoes, and rags

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check differential pressure of filter for lube and control oil, clean filter if requiered

  2. Check differential pressure of filter before shaft lift oil pump, clean filter if required

  3. Check oil level in oil tank throngh sight, add oil if required

  4. Extract oil vapor ........... how ???

  5. Check differential pressure for fuel oil filter and clean if required

  6. Clean the filter in fuel gas line, if rquired

  7. Lubricate movable parbs for all points in miscellaneous control device (after 1000 operabing hours or 6 months)

Combustion Chamber           Should be carried out every 2000 operating hours

  1. Check the stones and holders for the flame tube.

  2. Check visually the mining chamber and inner casing and flame.

  3. Check auxiliary air gates for fuel oil and fuel gas burners.

  4. Check visually the fuel oil mozzle and return line pipes and anial and diagonal swirler.
