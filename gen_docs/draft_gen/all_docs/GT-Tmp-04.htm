Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection activitis, which performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, and safety shoes.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety equipment "belt + shoes,... ect

Work to be carried out 

  1. Inspect tank area and equipment site daily

  2. Drain water or sediment from pumps, flow meter, and filter monthly

  3. Clean filter baskets monthly

  4. Visually check hoses

  5. Clean all casing of equipment and repaint if necessary

  6. Rotate monually all motors and check for bearing failure

  7. Check mechanical seals and replace if necessary

  8. Check countinuity if earthing cables

  9. Check for leaks and tighten

  10. Check electrical installation

  11. Check cathodic protection potential
