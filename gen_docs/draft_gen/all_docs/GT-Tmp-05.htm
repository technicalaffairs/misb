Introduction

This document out lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every M6 as indicate in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety Helmet, belt, shoes, .... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet, belt, shoes, .... etc

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check oil level every week

  2. Change oil every 1000 hours or 6 months

  3. Lubricate the individual parts suehus valves, tools ...etc every 1500 hours or 6 months

  4. Check gasket bowl, neck bearing spring and spring pistons every 1500  hours or 6 months

  5. Check tension and condition or .... belts

  6. Clean inside and remove bowl of frame every 2500 hours or 6 months

  7. Replace ball bearings on the spindle for every 4000 hours
