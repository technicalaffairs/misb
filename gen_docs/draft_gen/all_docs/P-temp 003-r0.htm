Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly

  1. Disconnect the discharge & suction lines.

  2. Remove cap head screws and nuts.

  3. Press spring dowels.

  4. Screw in forcing set screw to break seal between casings.

  5. Lift top half casing off studs and clear of impeller.

  6. Check clearance between case wear rings and impeller and compare it with data sheet.

  7. Remove bearing cap.

  8. Remove both T.E bearing retaining nuts.

  9. Tap bearing housing with T.E bearing.

  10. Slide gland lantern ring.

  11. Remove T.E case wear ring

  12. Pull sleeve with seal off end of shaft.

  13. Remove the packing.

  14. Remove impeller and remove the remaining case wear ring.

  15. Remove impeller key.

  16. Reassembly by performing the above reverse order.

  17. In the case of change the bearings. The bearings must be heated in oil bath at temp. 80C.

Checks before Reassembly

  1. Check clearance of impeller.

  2. Check the condition of bearing.

  3. Check the condition of shafts.

  4. Check spacer.

  5. Axial play of the rotor 0.1 – 0.3 mm

After Reassembly when the Pump Operates

  1. Check the direction of rotation before coupled.

  2. Check the Vibration.

  3. Check the Pressure and quantity of discharge according to characteristic.

  4. Check the Motor Ampere.

  5. Check the Temperature of oil.

  6. Check the Abnormal noise.
