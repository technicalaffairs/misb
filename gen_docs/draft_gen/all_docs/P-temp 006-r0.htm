Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly

  1. Remove the motor together with the semi – coupling.

  2. Remove the semi – coupling from motor.

  3. Extract the other semi – coupling and V- ring.

  4. Unscrew adjusting ring nut and remove washer.

  5. Remove the oil distribution plate and oil beater plate thus freeing the bearings.

  6. Remove the space coupling and lantern.

  7. Unscrew the nut and remove the gland and extract the packing and unscrew the packing sleeve from the shaft.

  8. Disconnect all main and auxiliary connection pipes.

  9. Unscrew the nut of baseplate and extract the pump from the tank.

  10. Set the pump on wooden supports on the floor.

  11. Remove the lubricator pipe.

  12. Unscrew the nuts and extract the suction body.

  13. Extract the impeller and diffuses and intermediate body and the sleeve.

  14. Continue until the complete disassembly of the pump.

  15. Reassembly by performing the above reverse order.

Checks before Reassembly

  1. Check clearance of impeller.

  2. Check the condition of slide bearings.

  3. Check the condition of shafts.

  4. Check the condition of thrust bearing.

After Reassembly when the Pump operates

  1. Check the vibration.

  2. Check the pressure and quantity of discharge according to characteristic

  3. Check the motor ampere.

  4. Check the temperature of oil.

  5. Check the abnormal noise.
