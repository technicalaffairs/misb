Introduction

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly

  1. Disconnect the power cable from motor.

  2. Disconnect the electric motor from the pump and remove it.

  3. Lean the motor on wooden blocks.

  4. Disconnect the hydraulic and electric connections.

  5. Drain of the oil from thrust house.

  6. Disconnect the frame from foundation base plate.

  7. Sling the pump and lift it till taking it out from the sump.

  8. Lean the pump in horizontal position with the discharge hole on the ground on wooden blocks.

  9. Sling the bell mouth.

  10. Sling the lock washer and unscrew the impeller locking screw and remove the impeller hub cap and split ring.

  11. Sling the wear plate from propeller bowl and remove it.

  12. Sling the impeller and remove its key.

  13. Put the shaft on suitable height wooden.

  14. Sling the support and loosen the stuffing box.

  15. Sling the hollow shaft, and unscrew the shaft nut and remove the rigid half coupling from the column pipe.

  16. Sling the rigid coupling.

  17. Disconnect motor stool.

  18. Sling the column pipe and unthread it along shaft.

  19. Sling the intermediate shaft.

  20. Disconnect the pump shaft from intermediate shaft and intermediate coupling and sling the shaft keeping it as horizontal as possible.

  21. Loosen Stop dowel, split ring and then remove the coupling and shaft.

  22. Reassembly by performing the above in reverse order.

Check before Reassembly

  1. Check clearance of impeller.                 0.5 : 1.25 mm

  2. Check clearance of slide bearing.          0.24 : 0.48 mm

  3. Check the condition of thrust bearing.    For wear and tear

  4. Check the condition of shafts.                Deformation pending

After Reassembly when the Pump Operates

  1. Check the vibration.

  2. Check the pressure and quantity of discharge according to characteristic.

  3. Check the motor Ampere.

  4. Check the temperature of oil.

  5. Ensure that there is no. abnormal noise.
