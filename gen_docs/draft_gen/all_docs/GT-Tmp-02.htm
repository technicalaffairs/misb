Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed after 4000 operating hours as indicated at the above titled block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ahmed Fathi from Dimiatta power station 

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety helmet, shoes, and rags

Work to be carried out 

  1. Measure clearance between flame tube and mixing chamber.

  2. Measure clearance between mining chamber and inner casing.

  3. Measure radical clearance for first stage compressor and turbine last stage of turbine blades.

  4. Check the stones.

  5. Check fuel oil burner.

  6. Check penet rated test.

  7. Perform penetrated metal test for the 1st stage turbine rotor blade.

  8. Wash the compressor.

  9. Clean fuel gas scrubber.

  10. Check insert plastic coupling for transmission unit.

  11. Clean main oil filters, air filter, dust collector filters, generator air filters, and oil cooler.

Transfer Pumps

  1. Clean the doubles filter when ∆ P {0.8 ÷102} bar

  2. Lubricate gate valve every 2 months

  3. Lubricate baring every 6000 hours or 6 months.

Fuel Oil Treatment Plant

  1. Lubricate a sludge pump every 10000 hours

  2. Lubricate a fresh water booster pump every 2000 hours

  3. Lubricate an air compressor every 2000 hours

  4. Lubricate an inhibitor dosing pump every 4000 hours

  5. Grease the vertical electric pump every 200 hours

Forwarding Pumps

  1. Lubricate the bearing of for warding pump every 6000 or 6 month

  2. Clean the doubles filter when ∆ P {0.8 ÷102} bar

  3. Lubricate gate valves every  2 months

Injection Pumps

  1. Lubricate the bearing of injection pumps every 12 months

  2. Clean the doubles filter when ∆ P {0.8 ÷102} bar
