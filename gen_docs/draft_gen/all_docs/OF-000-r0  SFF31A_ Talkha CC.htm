Introduction

This document lists the testing procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

The type SFF31 relays are static under frequency relays that provide a single set point for under frequency tripping if the system disturbance such that the load exceeds the generation the system is in danger of collapse, then this relay will trip the system.  

The first draft initiated by eng. Fayza El-Sharkawy, tested by eng. Shirin Hamdy & eng. Azza Salama, revised by EDEP Co. Quality Control eng. Adel El-Nagar, accepted by General Director eng. Mahmoud Saker and approved by eng. Moustfa Alam Head Sector of Talkha Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • The generator must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Test Plug handle.                                 

  • Test Leads.

  • PCR 500L Test Set.

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Make sure PT secondary circuit are opened at the incoming terminal blocks before isolating the relay voltage circuit for test

  2. Look at field maintenance file and compare the position of the setting switches on relay with those shown on the setting sheet.

Testing the Relay

  1. Connect the relay studs ( 7 , 10 ) to the D.C source 125 VD.C where stud 7 is the + ve and stud 10 is the – ve .

  2. Connect the test leads between the trip contact ( 1 – 2 ) .

  3. Connect test leads to line to line terminals ( 4 – 5 ).

  4. Increase the voltage at 115v.

  5. Adjust the frequency to a normal value.

  6. Decrease the frequency slowly until the relay trips instantaneously.

  7. Record the frequency value in the test sheet.
