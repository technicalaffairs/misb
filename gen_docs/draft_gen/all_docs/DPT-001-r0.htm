Introduction

        This document outlines the checklist I1 for Rosemount pressure transmitter (Flow Transmitter) 0-1250 mm H2O, 24           Vdc, 4 - 20 ma, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated  by Y1 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • Close manual valves for Transmitter lines.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety helmet, shoes,

  • Digital Ammeter.

  • Pressure tester.

  • Adjustable open end Spanner.

Work to be carried out

Steps to Prepare for test

  1. Review technical reference material and manufacture’s manual.

  2. Obtain copy of Indicator field maintenance file.

  3. Remove transmitter indicator and connect the ammeter leads.

  4. Open high vent to atmosphere and connect the pressure tester to high vent of transmitter

Testing Procedure

  1. Record the ammeter reading for 0 mm H2O.

  2. Using pressure tester inject 1250 mm H2O and record the ammeter reading.

  3. Vent out the pressure and record the ammeter reading.

  4. Inject 25% (312.5 mm H2O ) , 50% ( 625 mmH2O), 75% ( 937.5 mmH2O) and 100% (1250 mmH2O).

  5. Compare the ammeter reading with the field maintenance file if there is any error repeat step 3 to adjust Zero and step 4 to adjust Span .

  6. Repeat steps 3,4, and 5 until reach the required ammeter reading.

Return Transmitter to Service

  1. Return transmitter to service by removing the ammeter leads from the indicator terminal and connect the indicator.

  2. Remove the pressure tester .

  3. Open the manual valves for transmitter lines.

  4. Vent out air from transmitter lines.

  5. Close low and high vents.

  6. Place a copy of test result in the field maintenance file.
