Introduction

This document outlines the equipment tests I1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the titled block

The first draft initiated at High Dam HPS, by eng. Hossain Mahrous

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, safety belt.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, rags, and silicagel.

  • Break down voltage tester type ANN-70 Russian tester.

  • Dissipation factor tester: diel test type DTL ,2A Company: baur.

  • Flash point Pensky mortens closed, APM-6, Tanaka Japan.

  • Total aced no: tester

Works to be Carried Out

Tests: 

  1. Break down voltage.

  2. Dissipation factor test:

  3. Flash point.

  4. Total acid no.

  5. Water content.

  6. Impurities.
