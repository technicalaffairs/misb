Introduction

This document out lists the equipment inspection I2 , which must be performed on an annual bases as indicate Y1in the above above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety equipment, belt, shoes, .... etc

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check oil level every week

  2. Change oil every 1000 hours or 6 months

  3. Lubricate the individual parts suehus valves, tools ...etc every 1500 hours or 6 months

  4. Check gasket bowl, neck bearing spring and spring pistons every 1500  hours or 6 month

  5. Check tension and condition or .... belts

  6. Clean inside and remove bowl of frame every 2500 hours or 6 month

  7. Replace ball bearings on the spindle for every 4000 hours

  8. Replace neck bearing springs, inspected of bowl and check threads of lock rings for errosion and corrosion every year
