Introduction

This document lists the maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed annually base as indicated by Y1 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes,  etc.

  • The boiler must be out of operation.

  • The boiler pressure must be zero.

  • Be sure the boiler is drained.

  • Be sure the boiler vents must be opened.

  • Isolate the safety valve mechanically and electrically.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Suitable lifting device.

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Be sure the boiler out of operation, and the pressure inside the pipe lines connected to the safety valve must be zero

  2. Be sure the pipeline is drained.

    3.

  3. Be sure the air is isolated

Partially Disassembly of Safety Valve 

  1. Screw off the nuts no. 8         

  2. Screw off the nuts no. 9

  3. Screw off the nuts no. 11.

  4. Screw off nuts no. 12

  5. Take up the safety valve.

Inspection and Repair of All Parts 

  1. Check the safety valve cone (4) and regrind the sealing surface of it if needed.

  2. Check the safety valve seat (3) and regrind it if needed. When grinding the seats of the movers, attention must be paid to maintaining the angles of the sealing surfaces. In order to secure the mover function it is not allowed to reduce the seat parts by more than 0.8 mm in total (not more than 0.4 mm for one surface).

  3. Repair the safety valve cone (4) and valve seats (3) in a specialist workshop if grinding can not repair the wear in there.

  4. Check the spindle (7), repair or replace if needed.

  5. Check the spring no. 10, replace if needed.

  6. Check the tightening screw 13 for super heated steam SV

    7.

Assembly of Safety Valve

  1. Clean every part well before any assembly.

  2. Assembly the safety valve parts in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  • If you need any help please go to the operating instruction for repairs and maintenance D-4.

  • After successfully assembly coordinate with the shift operation engineer to test the valve to check that every thing is OK .

Trouble Shouting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

1.    Leakage

-        Sealing surfaces seat damaged by foreign object or by flowing substance

-        Seat grinding

-        Replacement of damage part.

2.    Broken packing cover
(stuffing box follower)

-        Squeezing of soft seal ring due to fluctuating temperatures.

-        Replacements of the packing cover.

-        Loosened bolts of the cover due to piping vibration.

-        Retighten the bolts.

3.    Leakage packing

-        Worn packing

-        Adequate tightening of bolts.

-        Packing dried up.

-        Replacement of the packing.

4.    Defect in electric servomotor

-        Defect in limit switches

-        Readjust limit switches.

-        Defect in moment switches.

-        Readjust moment switches.
