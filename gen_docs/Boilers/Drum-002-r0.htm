Introduction

These document lists the maintenance procedure P1, which must carried on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block of boiler drum which used for feeding walls with water and super heaters with saturated steam.

The first draft initiated and tested by eng. Fawzy Ahmed Bsiouny, accepted by general director pre. eng. Saad Mostafa, revised by eng. Samir Abed El Gelil at EDEPC, and approved by eng. Awad Mohammed Fathy Soliman Head Sector of Talkha power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes . etc

  • Drum must be out of operation.

  • Pressure in the drum should be zero.

  • Drain the drum and vents open.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Suitable lifting device.

 

Work to be Carried Out

Before Isolated the Drum Mechanically

  1. Check any leakage from the manholes.

  2. Check any leakage from the level glass valves.

  3. Check any leakage. From the pressure gauge.

  4. Check any leakage from the Mecca (plastic strips)

  5. Check the drum during operation.

  6. Check any leakage from the safety valve of drum

After Isolated the Drum Mechanically

  1. Be sure the boiler out of operation and the pressure inside the drum must be zero

  2. Be sure the drum is drained..

  3. Be sure the vents opened.

  4. Be sure the temperature inside the drum (35 ° C )

Inspection and Repair Out Side the Drum

  1. Screw off the nuts (1) to (13) 0f the level glasses.

  2. Screw off the nuts (1-1) to (1-13) of the level glasses.

  3. Screw off the bolts (14-15)

  4. Screw off the bolts (16-17)

  5. Change the Mecca if needed

  6. Screw off nuts for gland valves before and after the level glasses.    

  7. Take up the gland follower

  8. Talk up the baking of gland to change it

  9. Check the pressure gauge and change it if needed.

Inspection and Repair Inside the Drum

  1. Screw off the nuts (1) of the manholes

  2. Screw off the nuts (2) of the manholes

  3. Open manholes.

  4. Talk up the metalloplastic sealing of manholes and change it.

  5. Check the tubes for drain inside drum and change if needed.

  6. Check the vent of tubes and tubes from outside for chemical treatment and change it if needed

  7. Clean the cyclone properly.

Note: If  you need any help please go to the operating instruction for repairs and maintenance D-3.
