After isolating the re-circulation gas fan mechanically and electrically

Disassembly of the Rear and Front Cooling Disk              Figure (1)

  1. Dismantle the safety cover (1) of cooling disk.

  2. Screw off the nuts (2) of bolts which tighten the two halves of cooling disk around the fan shaft (3)

  3. Lift the two halves of cooling disk (4)

Disassembly of the Coupling              Figure (2)

  1. Dismantle the safety cover of coupling.

  2. Screw off the bolt (1), which connected the coupling cover (2) with its coupling half.

  3. Remove the two covers of coupling away from both.

  4. Take out the two halves of coupling spring (3).

Disassembly of the Rear and Front Bearing House              Figure (3)

  1. Take out the temperature sensor.

  2. Drain the oil from the bearing house.

  3. Disassemble all lubricating oil hoses. 

  4. Screw off the bolts (3) which connected bearing house cover (2) with upper / bottom halves of bearing house (1/1&1/2).

  5. Screw off the bolt (4), which connected two halves of bearing house (1/1&1/2).

  6. Take up the upper half of bearing house (1/1).

Disassembly Loading Mechanism

  1. Open the main hall of the fan.

  2. Screw of the bolts which tighten the upper pin with loading blades one by one

  3. Screw of the bolts which tighten the lower pin with loading blades one by one

  4. Take out the loading blades one by one out side the fan casing  

  5. Inspect and repair or exchange the defected parts

Coupling

  1. Clean every part well.

  2. Check the Coupling springs from any mechanical wear.

  3. Check the two halves coupling from any mechanical wear.

  4. Repair or exchange any part if needed.

  5. Re-grease the coupling springs

 Bearing House & Bearing

  1. Clean every part well.

  2. Check the bearing from any mechanical wear.

  3. Check the two halves of bearing house from any mechanical wear or damage.

  4. Repair or exchange any part if needed.

Loading mechanism

  1. Clean every part well

  2. Check the upper guides pins and bushes from mechanical wear and exchange it if needed

  3. Check lower guide pins with loading levers and pin bushes from mechanical wear exchange if needed

  4. Check the loading blades from mechanical wear and repair any defect

Impeller, shaft and foundation

  1. Clean every part well.

  2. Check the shaft from any defect (Cracks, deflection…) and repair or exchange it if needed.

  3. Check the impeller from any defect (mechanical wear or corrosion,….) and repair or exchange it if needed.

  4. Check the foundation from any defect and repair it if needed.

Assembly of all parts

  1. Clean every part well.

  2. Assemble every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

Re-Lubricating

  1. Use the recommended/ or equivalent oil type.

  • After successful inspection, operate the fan again, read all the available record value and putted in table (1) to compare between the values before and after inspection.

Trouble Shooting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

1.  Fan dose not reaches the prescribed output.

Increased resistance of the equipment.

 

Opening of closing organs

Checking of the perfect permeability of the piping.

Additional suction of the false air.

Closing of the checking manhole, rep. checking of joints and other spots where the suction in could take place.

Wear of blades on the impeller wheel.

Fan stopping and checking of its wear, replacement of the impeller wheel.

Pneumatic values of the equipment do not coincide with planned ones.

The pneumatic measurements on the equipment are performed, the defect being removed according to instruction of the designer.

2.  Excessive fan vibration

 

Unleveled or worn impeller wheel.

Check the leveling of the impeller wheel , level it or replace

Incorrect assembly of the coupling or its wear

Checking of the correct fitting and adjust of the coupling, of its proper axial clearance, correction and leveling of the coupling, rep. replacement.

Loosened screws, fixing the impeller wheel, bearing, el. Motor etc.

Checking of tightness and fixing of screws.

Damaged bearing

Replace bearing if need.

Damaged shaft.

Checking by using an indicator. If damaged replace it.

3.  Bearing heats up.

Higher ambient temp. or significant increase of the temp. of the conveyed air material.

Checking of attachment of cooling disks, reps. function of lubrication station (If used) , temp. Reduction of conveyed air material.

Defective or unsuitable oil.

Clean the bearing, bearing ring box and replace the oil.

Impurities in the bearing.

Dismantle the bearing, clean and fill new.

Defective bearing, incorrect radial clearance.

Dismantling and inspection of the bearing measure the radial clearance, replacement of the defect bearing.

Defective assembly of the bearing

Correct assembly.
