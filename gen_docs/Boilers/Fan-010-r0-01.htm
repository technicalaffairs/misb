Work to be carried out

 

FD FAN  Assembly Sequence

  1. Lower the base assembly (pedestals, fan housings, inlet boxes, and/or base frame) over the anchor bolts and set on the shims.

  2. Remove any temporary bracing.

  3. Add shims so that all rocking motion is eliminated.

  4. Level assembly in the axial and radial direction by replacing or adding shims to bring the base assembly level.

  5. Apply appropriate shim packs as necessary to obtain the proper elevation

  6. Shim and level the bearing support assemblies on their foundations. The bearing pedestals and sole plate, Surfaces must be level within 0.010 inches per foot [0.833 mm per meter].

  7. After tightening all anchor bolts, check each shim pack to assure equal loading.

    NOTE: By using the thickest shims possible, all shim packs should have the least number of shims, Ideally, there should not be more than five (5) shims in each pack.

  8. Clean the bearing and shaft seal machined surfaces of the rotor.

  9. Add the inlet cones over shaft ends and tie the cones to the wheel with straps or cables (not chains).

  10. Inlet cones should be inserted as far as possible into the wheel. These cones are temporarily strapped so that Shaft and wheel assembly can be lifted into the housing as a single assembly.

  11. Install the rotor shaft with the inlet cone(s), IVC(s) , and shaft bearing assemblies into the lower fan housing. Snug the bearing bolts but do not torque.

  12. Install the remainder of the fan housing and the inlet box(es). Be sure to remove any temporary bracing.

  13. Install the remaining inlet cone bolts, while maintaining the concentricity of the inlet cone

  14. Set and align the main motor for final running position. Be sure to check for magnetic center.

  15. Alignment of the motor must be set with the motor shaft at its magnetic center

FD FAN Disassembly Sequence

The FD FAN should be disassembly in the reverse order of assembly
