Introduction

This document  outlines  the  major maintenance procedures P1, which performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Mazout forward Pump ((CB324A -275)) used for moving the fuel oil from daily tank to Burner Pumps, and

Mazout storage tank recalculating pump ((CB324A- 300)) used for drawing the Mazout through the heater and pumps it to the top of main tank. Which must be Performed on an annually bases as Indicated by Y1 in the title block or at any troubles occurs

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area  is required,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for motor of this Pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable bearing of the motor of this pump

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves and drain it.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, and suitable lifting device,

  • Manual puller, feeler gauge

  • Lessen set screws, and grease

Work to be Carried Out

 Disassembly of the Pump

  1. Remove Coupling Hub: remove Check nut (22), set screw (23) And Remove the key (27)

  2. Remove Cover of Packing (Drip cup 16): remove nut (12) and Washer (13), Bolt (14) to Remove the Packing cover (16)

  3. Remove Packing (15): use packing puller to remove the five installed packing ring (15)

  4. Remove the inboard cover (25): remove bolt (2) and gasket (6), inboard cover includes plug (8), check valve (26) Washer (17) Bushing (18) inboard collar (37) and shim (21) slide all parts from (inboard cover (25).

  5. Remove the out Board Cover (24): remove bolt (4) , cover (39 ) , ring (32) and Remove gasket (36) then,

  6. Remove bolt (3) and remove outboard cover (24), gasket (5), slide bushing (34), outboard collar (38) and shim (31) from outboard cover  

  7. Remove main Shaft  (10): remove main shaft (10) from housing (9) and idelar rotor (11) from housing (9) then,

  8. Remove plug (7) and stop pin (35) from case (1) slide housing (9) from case (1)  

Inspection of the Pump Parts

  1. Check Pump case (1) and renewed if it needed

  2. Check rotor housing (9) and renewed if it needed

  3. Check Main rotor (10) and renewed  If it needed  

  4. Check Idler rotor (11) and renewed if it needed

  5. Check inboard collar (37) and renewed if it needed

  6. Check outboard collar (38) and renewed if it needed

  7. Check the check valve (26) and renewed if it needed

Assembly of the Pump

  1. Clean every parts well

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After finishing assembly of the pump it mast be rotated by hand.

  4. Realignment of the pump and motor according to the table:

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK and record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration,  according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.
