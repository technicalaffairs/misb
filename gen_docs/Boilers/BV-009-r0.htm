Introduction

This document out outlines the minor maintenance procedure as indicated by P1 in the above title block for the manual gate valve depending on external / internal leakage

This valve to be used in case of using two positions, open or closed completely.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, belt, shoes, .... etc

  • Isolate valve mechanically.

  • Drain the pipeline, which connected to this valve. 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid

  • Suitable lifting device.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Isolating the Valve Mechanically

  1. Check any leakage from valve gland. If it found, retighten on gland bolts.

  2. Check any leakage from gasket between yoke cover and valve body. If it found retighten the bolts.

  3. Check any internal leakage.

  4. Check any leakage from the valve bodies (cracks or mechanical wear). Remark the point of leakage.

  5. Check any leakage form flange end for flange type.

  6. Check the operation of the valve drive unit during closing / opening of the valve.

After Isolating the Valve Mechanically

  1. Be sure the valve was out of operation and the pressure inside the pipeline connected to the valve must be zero from the two end of the valve.

  2. Be sure the pipeline is drained.

Disassembly the Valve 

  1. Screw off nut (10) of bolt (11), which connected drive unit (14) with yoke seat (13).

  2. Lift up drive unit (14) by using suitable lifting device.

  3. Screw off nut (5) of bolt (6), which connected yoke (4) with valve body (1).

  4. Take out con (3) from spindle (2).

  5. Screw off nut (7) of gland bolts (8).

  6. Withdraw spindle (2) from yoke (4) & gland chamber.

  7. Take out the gland follower (9).

  8. Take out gland packing (15) from gland chamber.

  9. Screw off nut of bolts for flange end in case of flange type.

Inspection and repair of all Parts

  1. Check the valves cone (3) and regrind the sealing surface of it if needed.

  2. Check the valves seat (17) and regrind it if needed.

  3. Repair the valve cone (3) and valve seat (17) in a specialist workshop if grinding can not repair the wear in there. ( in weld type the valve end must be cut from the pipe line , in flange type it is easy which flange bolts screwed off only)

  4. Check the Spindle (2) and repair it if needed or replace it.

  5. Check the Gland follower (9) and repair it if needed or replace it.

  6. Clean every connection face.

  7. Change gasket (16).

  8. Check the body (1) from any cracks or wear by using penetration fluids and repair it if found by using suitable filler material or its equivalent acc. to table (1).

  9. Check the coupling (12) between drive unit shaft and valve spindle and repair it if needed.

  10. Change gasket for flange end in case of flange type.

Assembly of Valve

  1. Clean every part well before any assembly.

  2. Assembly the valve parts in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. Readjust the limit &moment switches.

Replace the Packing of Valve Gland

  1. Clean the stuffing box chamber and the cover well.

  2. Lubricate the gland bolts and nuts.

  3. Use suitable size of packing.

  4. Wire the suitable packing size on a part of shaft with the same diameter of valve spindle.

  5. Cut all packing rings along the shaft by using sharp knife.

  6. Fit the rings in the stuffing box chamber with the cut turned 180°, starting at 9 clock.

  7. Tighten the gland bolts.

  8. Open and close the valve and be sure that the valve operation is correct.

  • If you need any help please go to the operating instruction for repairs and maintenance D-4.

  • Put the valve into operation and check the list of checking before isolated.

Trouble Shouting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

1.   Leakage

Sealing surfaces – seat damaged by foreign object or by flowing substance

Seat grinding

Replacement of damage part.

2.   Broken packing cover

(stuffing box follower)

Squeezing of soft seal ring due to fluctuating temperatures.

Replacements of the packing cover.

Loosened bolts of the cover due to piping vibration.

Retighten the bolts.

3.   Leakage packing

Worn packing

Adequate tightening of bolts.

Packing dried up.

Replacement of the packing.

 4.   Defect in electric servomotor.

Defect in limit switches.

Readjustment of limit switch.

Defect in moment switch

Readjustment of moment switch.
