Introduction

This document outlines the inspection steps I1 for the textile expansion joints, which must be carried out when it consumed.

The technical data:

There are two types of joints ( wk1 at the frame after HRSG dampers & wk2 at the frame before HRSG chimney & wk3 under the drums of evaporators ), Wk1 : 7 layers & wk3 : 5 layers & wk2 : 5 layers

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdo Abdo El  Hendawy, tested by eng. Abdel Qader Fath Allah at 28 April 2003 on Boiler no. 4 and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gilel, accepted by general director eng. Sobhy Mohamed El Bakry and approved by eng. Moneer Mohamed El Wakeel Head Sector of Demiatta PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • HRSG out of service

  • Disconnect the power supply of damper actuators and tagged it.

  • G .T. out of service and ( excitation  C.B  & unit aux .C.B & low voltage trans .C.B ) is rack out

  • Mack scaffolding at the joint if need

  • All safety precaution must be followed by safety department.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Drilling M/C & hook & keys kit

  • Special tools (needle & thermal thread & iron & thermal adhesive tap)

  • Air hammer, Bolts & washers

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Lose the bolts of the joint.

  2. Release the plates which on the joint.

  3. Clean the tow flanges of the joint after remove the consumed joint.

  4. Install the new joint and tightening it.
