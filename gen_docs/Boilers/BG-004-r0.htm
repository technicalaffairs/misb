Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Shutdown the burner and the unit must be out of service

  • Isolate the burner mechanically as follows:

    • Isolate  the Main Gas valves and isolate  etch valve Burners level

    • Isolate  the Main Mazout valves and isolate  etch valve Burners level

    • Isolate the Main Solar valves and isolate  etch valve Burners level

    • Isolate  the Main Atomizing Steam valves drain and isolate  etch valve  Burners level

  • Cleaning fluid, and Suitable lifting device.

Work to be Carried out

Dismantle the Solar Burner Gun from Burner Guide Tube 
 Inspection and check for Igniter Gun   Figure 4

  1. Inspect clogging orifice for Igniter gun.

  2. Change coupling gasket and change tip not gasket when there is leakage solar or air

  3. Clean internal guide tube of igniter gun passing with compressed air

  4. Rotate guide tube to align limit switch with actuator

  5. Adjust actuator and check for operation switch

  6. Check spark gap between the Narrowest points of ignition rods at 5/32 inch

  7. Check retraction mechanism of igniter gun

Dismantle the Gas Cones Burner from burner guide tube 

Inspection and Check for Gas Burner  Figure 1

  1. Gas cone position readjusted:  reference to the air swirler and rotation of the canes .

  2. Clean the gas ring by compressed air after open the drain of the gas ring  .

  3. Inspect clogging of the gas canes tips and clean it .

Dismantle the Oil Burner from Burner Guide Tube 

Inspection and Check for Oil GUN  Figure 5

  1. Oil gun position readjusted position
  2. Inspect clogging orifice plate distributor plug for gun .

  3. Change coupling gasket and change tip not gasket when there is leakage oil steam .

  4. Clean internal guide tube of oil gun passing with compressed air .

  5. Rotate guide tube to align limit switch with actuator .

  6. Adjust actuator and check for operation of limit switch .

  7. Check the mechanism of retraction of oil gun .

Inspection and Check of Air Register   Figure 3

  1. Check  the performs the sleeve damper function of on / off control of the combustion air to the air frame .

  2. Check  the sleeve damper for any binding and check rollers for free movement .
  3. Check  retrain mechanism of air damper

Inspection and Check of Swirler   Figure 2

  1. Check the welding of the air swirler plates.
  2. Visually inspection for any deterioration due to heat.
  3. Check swirler if there is over heating is observed.

Trouble  Shooting

Trouble

Possible  Reasons

Igniter NO Light

  1. Check all valves in oil and air lines to Igniter are open.

  2. Check oil and air Pressure are correct.

  3. Check Igniter assembly. Inspect and clean disassembly and

  4. Check spark gap between narrows poit = 5/32"

Oil  Burner NO Light

  1. Check all valves in oil and steam to oil Burner are open .

  2. Check oil Burner assembly and clean oil gun tip .

  3. Check oil and steam Pressure are correct.

  4. Check oil distance Between the swirler  and oil gun tip equal 19 mm

Boiler Vibration

    1.   Adjustment the rotation of the gas canes

Flame Shape NO Good

  1. Check Pressure gas or Pressure of oil.

  2. Check the atomizing steam Pressure.

  3. Check the rotation of the gas comes.

  4. Check the air damper is completely open Not semi open.

  5. Clogging the gas canes. if firing with gas. clean it if found.

  6. Clogging the oil gun tip if firing with oil  clean it if found.

  7. Check the distance Between the swirler and oil gun tip 19mm

  8. Check retraction Mechanism of oil gun.

  9. Check retraction Mechanism of air damper.
