Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be carried out when the bearing temperature > 80 o C as indicated in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdo Abdo El  Hendawy, tested by eng. Abdel Qader Fath Alla at 02 April 2003 on Boiler no. 4 and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gilel, accepted by general director eng. Sobhy Mohamed El Bakry and approved by eng. Moneer Mohamed El Wakeel Head Sector of Demiatta PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • Be sure the seal air blower is stopped

  • Disconnect the power supply of the seal air fan  motor

Tools and Equipment

  • Hex. keys, Alin kit, Screw driver, Dig hummer

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle the all components

  2. Check the impeller for (crakes, wear, and deformation) and change it if necessary

  3. Check and repair or change the shaft and shaft packing

  4. Check the coupling flexible intermediate ring change if it bad

  5. Check the roller bearing (noise-temp.) normal temp. 50-80oC & visual test check

  6. Clean the bearing and add new grease

  7. Check the alignment of the shaft coupling 

Fitting a new flexible intermediate ring

  1. Unscrew and remove the guard

  2. Detach the motor and push it backwards

  3. Replace the intermediate ring

  4. Pull coupling and motor back in place and screw tight

  5. Re-mount guard and screw tight.

Return the Seal Air Fan to Service

  1. Return the work order to the shift engineer to prepare the procedures

  2. Prepare the seal fan for operation

  3. Record the readings at the MRDS
