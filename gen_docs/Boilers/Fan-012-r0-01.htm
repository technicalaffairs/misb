Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this GR fan.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this GR fan.

  • Isolate the GR fan mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Cleaning fluid, Feeler gauge, Manual puller

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Suitable lifting device.

  • Hammer, Dial indicator, Torque wrench

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Disconnect the fan side half coupling from the rotor and inspect both the fan side and the motor side half couplings for wear

  2. Clean all parts and re-grease.

  3. jacking fixtures are required to remove the coupling hubs. In addition to these Fixtures,the hydraulic jacks are required.

  4. Torquing requirements for bolts .

  5. Check the alignment of the motor, intermediate shaft (if applicable), and the couplings .

  6. Wear or damage may affect the fan balance.

  7. Check all lube oil and hydraulic system hoses, piping, and fittings for leaks.

  8. Check ROTOR & BLADE LINERS

  9. Check and repair as required HOUSING & INLET BOX

  10. Check and adjust as required COUPLING WEAR & ALIGNMENT

  11. Check and adjust as required DAMPER BLADES & LINKAGES

  12. Check and adjust as required ROTOR BODY BOUND BOLTS

  13. Check and adjust as required SHAFT BEARINGS & SEALS

  14. Inlet cone clearances
