Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,                                                                                

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes.... etc

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding the burners.

  • The burners must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller,

  • Suitable lifting device, Feeler gauge.

  • Hammer, Dial indicator, and Torque wrench

Work to be carried out

  1. Inspect the secondary air zone spin vanes for bending

  2. Inspect burner for blockage by foreign material

  3. The sliding air damper should also be checked for proper adjustment

  4. Final positions of the vanes and sliding damper should be marked on the positioner for each burner.

  5. Replace the rope packing or secured in place if necessary.

  6. Inspect the slip seal between the burner throat and wall opening

  7. Ensure the burner support legs are properly supported and able to expand.

  8. Check the burner barrel assemblies for signs of overheating

  9. Check the lighter, atomizer and gas elements for damage or blocked.

  10. Check the fuel flexible hoses of burners for as leakage,

HEMI Gas Elements

  1. Inspect gas elements for accumulated foreign matter and possible cracks or damage from overheating

  2. Inspect the spud tip for damage and check the spud hole sizes

  3. Replace the spud if holes are out of round, or if the holes differ more than 0.9 mm. relative to the original drilling (14.3 mm)

  4. Inspect the body of the gas element for damage and repair or replace as necessary

  5. Replace gun body gaskets and rings to maintain tight seals.

Oil Atomizer

  1. Check for accumulated foreign matter, possible cracks

  2. Clean atomizer assemblies should be available for quick installation in the event that flame appearance shows this to be necessary.
