Introduction

This document  outlines  the maintenance procedures P1, which performed on an annually bases or at any troubles accrues as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The function of Mazout (screw pump) fuel oil unloading pump: transferring Mazout from pipes to storage tanks.

  • Safety fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area isrequired,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for Motor of this Pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable bearing of the motor of this pump

Work to be Carried Out

 

Disassembly of the Pump

  1. Remove pump from driver coupling, mounting bracket remove bottom plug (45) from out board geas cover (43) and drain lubricating oil remove plugs (46) and drain pump.

  2. lock drive shaft (12) at in board end  of pump to avoid rotation of shafts.

  3. Remove drive (44) gear cover subassembly (43) and gasket (42).

  4. Remove drive gear bolt (24) with lock washer (23) and out board washer (38)
    Note:     Insert a soft metal rod copper or brass between gear teeth to prevent shaft rotation.
     

  5. Using a gear puller remove drive gear (35) key (34)

  6. Remove drives gear bolt (24) with lock washer (23) and out board washer (38)

  7. Use a gear puller remove drive gear (36) with bushing (37), washer (39), lock washer (40), and bolt (41) remove key (34).

  8. Slide gear spacers (33) from drive and driven shaft (12) and (13) remove retaining rings (32) from intermediate out board cover (7) .       

  9. Remove packing from shaft (12) and (13) in free end.

  10. Remove must (11) with washers (10) insert two jacking bolts into intermediate out board cover (7) and Remove cover (7) and also remove roller bearing (35) and lip seals (15) .

  11. Remove bolts (29) and washers bearing retainer (28) with O – ring (25) and bearing retainer (27) with O – ring (26).

  12. Remove lip seal (30)form bearing retainers (27) Remove O – rings (25) and (26) bearing retainers (27) ,(28).

  13. Slide sleeve (21) form drive shaft (12) remove retaining ring(20) and shim(48) form drive shaft (12) .

  14. Remove bolt (24) with lock washer (23) and in  board washer .(22) form drive shaft (13) .

  15. Remove packing form shaft(12)and (13) in the drive end.

  16. Remove nuts (11) with washers (10) form in intermediate in  board cover (6) insert two (2) jacking bolts into cover (6) and  remove cover (6) remove bolt bearing(17) and (18) shim (16) and wash support (19) and lip seal (15) from shaft (12) and (13) .

  17. Slide O – rings (14) from shaft (12) and (13) .

  18. Remove cap screws (5) and spacers (3) with rabbet ring (4) form in board out board ends of pump remove gasket (2) from case (1)

  19.  Remove shaft (12) , (13) from case (1)

Assembly of the Pump

  1. Clean every parts well

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After finishing assembly of the pump it must be rotated by hand.

  4. Realign the pump and motor according to the following  table:

    Lube WT ( I b )

    0.19

    Operating Alignment Limits  ( Inch )

    < 0.005”

    Gap Of Coupling  ( Inch )

    0.125”

    Cover Bolt Torque ( I b – In )

    200

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK and record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration,  according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.

Trouble Shooting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

1. Loss of Flow

System component malfunction

Inspect all system components and correct malfunction make sure all valve are in their proper position and suction and discharge line are open

 

Incorrect pump rotation

Shut down immediately. Imo Pump are unidirectional and extended reverse rotation can cause damage. Reveres the direction of the driver

 

Loss of prime or boost pressure

Shut down the pump repine the pump and restart repeat until pump primes. Check boost system for malfunction

 

Air leak in suction

Symptoms are form in the fluid and noise. Reseal and tighten all joints in suction line

 

Low oil level in reservoir

May be accompanied by noise and air in the fluid fill reservoir to proper level be sure intake is for enough below the minimum fluid level to prevent vortex

 

In sufficient inlet

May be accompanied by noise may be caused by high fluid viscosity on cold start. Check suction line for blockage, closed valve or foot valve, clogged strainer. Check for plugged breather in tank

 

Mechanical trouble

Loss coupling, broken shaft, failed pump, may be accompanied by noise or vibration locate and correct cause

2. Noisy or vibration Pump

 

 

 

Insufficient inlet pressure

May be pump speed excessive

Air in fluid

Check for leak in suction line low fluid level vortex at inlet to suction line, or return lines above fluid level

Pump not aligned

Realign the pump. Misalignment may also be caused be temperature variation

Mechanical problems

Loose or mis-positioned coupling broken shaft, bad bearing

3. Excessive Power

    Consumption

 

 

Pump speed excessive

Reduce pump rotational speed

High fluid viscosity

Reduce viscosity by temperature control of fluid

High pressure

Checks for blockage of discharge line from the pump

Mechanical

Bent shaft, tight pacing pipe strain, failed pump locate and correct cause
