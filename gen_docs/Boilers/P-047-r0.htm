Introduction

This document  outlines  the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six monthes as indicated by M6 in the title block.

Mazout Forward Pump CB324A-275 function: Moving the fuel oil from daily tank to Burner Pumps.

Mazout Storage tank re-circulating Pump CB324A-300 function: Drawing the Mazout through the heater and pumps it to the top of  main tank.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for motor of this pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable bearing of the motor of this pump

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves and drain it.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, and suitable lifting device,

  • Manual puller, feeler gauge.

  • Lessen set screws, and grease.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the direction of the Pump

  2. Be sure there is no any abnormal noise or vibration and prevent it if found

  3. Be sure there is no any leakage from any Gasket and prevent it if found.

  4. Check pressure in delivery of the pump.

  5. Check quantity delivered of the pump.

  6. Check the temperature of bearing house

  7. Check alignment of the pump according to table.

Before Isolating the Pump Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check any noise or vibration in pump.

  2. Check the direction of the motor rotation.

  3. Check leakage from any gasket.

  4. Check pressure in delivery of the pump's delivery.

  5. Check the temperature of bearing.

After Isolating the Pump Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check  the impeller and casing from mechanical wear and measure the clearances by dismantle the Pump.

  2. Check the bearing in the Pump.

  3. Prevent the leakage from gasket by renewed it.

  4. Check the coupling's gear and change grease of coupling.

  5. Retighten all bolts connected pump and motor with foundation.

  6. Check the alignment between pump and motor.

Operation of the Pump

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK and check the direction of rotation, record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration, according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.
