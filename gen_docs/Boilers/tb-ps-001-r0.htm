Introduction

 This document outlines the forming machine for metal expansion joints because of increasing the consumption rate of textile expansion joint at the inlet of hot gases to boiler (Heat recovery steam generator), so; metal expansion joints become necessary for long life and best utility.

Metal Expansion Joint is not saving in our store.
