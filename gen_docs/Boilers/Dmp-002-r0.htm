Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be carried out when the gas turbine have major maintenance after 40,0000 workhour.

The technical Data:
- Stober double damper type ( E ) electrical actuator drive,
- There are 8 actuators ( 4 isolate & 4 control ).

The first draft initiated by eng. El Kehlawy Mostafa Mousa, tested by eng. Abdel Qader Fath Allah at 02 April 2003 on Boiler no. 4 and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gilel, accepted by general director eng. Sobhy Mohamed El Bakry and approved by eng. Moneer Mohamed El Wakeel Head Sector of Demietta PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes

  • HRSG is out of service.

  • Disconnect the power supply of damper actuators and tagged it.

  • Isolat the air tanks and depressing it.

  • G .T. out of service (excitation CB & unit auxiliary CB &low voltage trans. CB) is rack out.

  • Mack scaffolding down the bypass dampers.

  • All safety precaution must be followed by safety department person.

Tools and Equipment

  • Air hammer & riveting tool                                                                                      

  • Kit of key and aln

  • Seal cord, center bearing & flanged bearing & rivet & bolts & packing

  • Welding filler & welding machine

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check shaft wear at center bearing {welding and grinding }if need.

  2. Check flanged bearing {replacement the defect and greasing).

  3. Check center bearing replacement the defect.

  4. Replace the bad packing for stuffing box and tight it.

  5. Change the bad seal cord for blades.

  6. Weld the cracks of the frame and repair the deformation parts.

  7. Change the mechanical drive defects for actuators.

  8. Repair or change the actuators gear box if defect.

  9. Check the air (lines & valves & tanks & hoses & pistons) and repair or change if necessary.

  10. Check all (bolts &nuts) for damper blades and change the defect.

  11. Check linkage rod and repair or change the defect .

  12. Adjust the damper blades at( close and open )states mechanically arranging with instrument department.
