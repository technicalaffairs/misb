Introduction

This document outlines the high pressure feed water pump inspection steps I1, which must be carried out every 6 months as indicated by M6 in the title block 
The pump technical data:
           Type:   centrifugal pump multistage           R.P.M      :       2980 
        No of  stage        :        (1+8)                       Power       :      789 K W
        Suction press.     :        1.8  bar                   Max .flow  :      266 m3/ h
        Discharge press.  :       88 bar

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • Shut down the pump

  • Disconnect the power supply & rack out the C.B

  • Isolate the inlet & outlet valves for circulation circuit of mechanical seal.

  • Put red tag on the pump.

  • The safety deppartment. must check the isolation and surrounding the work zone.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Grease gun Type of grease (aluminum complex grease)

  • Complete maintenance tool set, Cleaning fluid

  • Buchlite for ELEC. isolating & alignment devices

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the coupling {tooth flanges for cracking – O rings for oil leakage – sealing faces} replace the damaged one.

  2. Change the grease in the coupling by new grease. (0.5 kg  of aluminum complex grease)

  3. Clean the filter of circulation circuit for mechanical seal

  4. Align the coupling hubs and check the axial move ability of casings intermediate sleeves 

  5. Change oil as chemical analysis every year.
