Introduction

This document outlines the Fan inspection list I1, which must be performed every six month, as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The forced fans to supply boiler with combustion air, and gas recirculation fans to heat combustion to rise the efficiency of boiler.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves ... etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the fan motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of fan motor

  • The fan must be isolated mechanically by  closing delivery damper

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer straight edge.

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check alignment for flange of coupling of fans and ensure that is OK

  2. Measure vibration level for bearing as MRDS  then analyze it and determine what to do (if it is needed).

  3. Check bolts fixation, tighten if  needed.

  4. Check visually the bearings oil for  color and viscosity, add or change oil if needed.

  5. Check the tightness of the coupling.

  6. Check the damper linkage.

  7. Check that the connecting flanges between the fan and the ducting are good.

  8. Check the oil level of the bearing.

  9. Check that no water in the bottom of the casing.

  10. Return back all mechanical and electrical Fan positions to the normal by coordinating with shift engineer

  11. Operate the Fan and ensure that it is OK
