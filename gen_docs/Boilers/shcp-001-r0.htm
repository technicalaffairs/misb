Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller,

  • Suitable lifting device. Feeler gauge.

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly of the pump:

  1. Be sure the suction and delivery valves are closed.

  2. Open the plug (19) to drain any liquid inside the pump.

  3. Disconnect the motor from foundation and moved it for away the pump.

  4. Pull the pump’s coupling by using manual puller.

  5. Take out couplings key (26) from the shaft (10).

  6. Disconnect the cooling water connection (23).

  7. Screw off the nuts (28) to disconnect bearing bracket (5) with lantern (4) from spiral box (2).

  8. Screw off bolt (27) & (20) to Disconnect foot of bracket (9) from bearing bracket and foundation.

  9. Take out bearing bracket with lantern from spiral box.

  10. Screws off the nut (14) pull impeller (1) from the shaft.

  11. Take out impeller’s key (26/1) from shaft (10).

  12. Screw off the nuts (25) and disconnect gland flange (8).

  13. Take out spiral cover (3) from the shaft (10) by hand with turning.

  14. Take out packing and cooling ring (24) from stuffing box.

  15. Pull shaft protection bush (11) from shaft (10).

  16. Take out oil throw ring (13) from shaft (10) and drain oil by open plug (19).

  17. Screw off bolts (21) to disconnect bearing cover with oil seal.

  18. Screw off safety nut (17).

  19. Hammer the shaft at coupling side by using polyamide hammer. To take out the shaft with front bearing and without back bearing which will be inside the bearing bracket.

  20. Pull the front bearing from the shaft by using bearing puller.

  21. Take out back bearing from bearing bracket by hammering.

Inspection and renewed of pump’s part

  1. Check the impeller (1), packing ring (12) from mechanical wear or chemical corrosion and renewed if needed.

  2. Check spiral box (2) from pitting or mechanical / chemical wear and repair it if needed.

  3. Check the shaft protections bush (11) and renewed it if needed.

  4. Check the cooling ring and renewed if needed.

  5. Check the gasket (29) and renewed if needed.

  6. Check the shaft (10) and repair or – renewed it if needed.

  7. Check the front and back bearing and renewed it if needed.

  8. Check gasket and oil seal of bearing covers (6) and renewed it if needed.

  9. Check the oil throw rings and renewed it if needed.

Assembly of the pump

  1. Clean every part well.

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After finishing assembly of the pump it must be rotated by hand.

Re-alignment of the pump and motor          According to MPIS doc # cpl-001-r0

 

Change the packing of the stuffing box

  1. Clean the surfaces of the stuffing box and shaft well.

  2. Wire the suitable packing size on apart of a shaft with same diameter of the pump’s shaft.

  3. The number of turns equal to number of rings needed.

  4. Cut all packing rings along shaft by using, sharp knife.

  5. Fit the rings in the stuffing box with cut turned 180 o, starting at 9 o’clock.

  6. The packing should never be knocked as they may become damage .

  7. Remember that in soft packing there must always be as light leakage of fluid from gland .

  8. Do not tightened the gland hard to prevent the rise in temperature of stuffing box .

Re. Filling the pump by lubricating oil:

  1. Prepare the suitable lubricating oil in a clean bottle.

  2. Open plug (30).

  3. Fill some lubricating oil and turn the pump by hand many times to clean the bearing house.

  4. Open plug (19/1) and drain the oil, which used to clean.

  5. Close plug (19/1) again. And filling the pump with oil until 2/3 of oil side glass.
