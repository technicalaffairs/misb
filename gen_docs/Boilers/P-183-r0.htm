Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This pump is used to transfer the coolant water from the river nile to the condenser for condensing the steam from turbine to condensate water.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the CB of the pump motor

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery values

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Rages, Grease

  • Filler

  • Vibrometer

  • Dial indicator

    •

Work to be Carried Out

Pump in Operation

  1. Measure vibration level for bearing and compare it with the setting values, then analyze it and determine what to do (if it is needed)

  2. Check discharge, pressure and compare it with the setting values then replace the worn-out components (if it is needed)

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check the alignment and compare with the setting values.

  2. Check bolts fixation, then re-tight it (if it is needed).

  3. Grease the bearings of the pump.

  4. Check the leakage from the glands and flanges and treat it.

  5. Change the packing seal between the shaft and the wear ring

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint.
