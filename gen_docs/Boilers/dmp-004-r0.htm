Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mamdoh Anber, tested by eng. Abd El-Azeem Abdin and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abd El Gelil, accepted by general director eng. Yousry Abdel Maqsood, and approved by eng. Awad M. Fathay Head Sector of Talkha Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes

  • HRSG is out service.

  • Put damper selector on close position.

  • Disconnect the power supply of damper actuator and tag it.

  • Gas turbine out of service.

  • All safety precaution must be followed by safety dep.

Tools and Equipment

  • Spanners.

  • Cleaning fluid.

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Drain the oil from damper gear box.

  2. Check  gears wears & damage.

  3. Check leaking.

  4. Change the damage  parts.

  5. Add new oil.
