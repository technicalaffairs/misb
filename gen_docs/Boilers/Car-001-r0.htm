Introduction

This document outlines the Isusu car pick up inspection steps I1, which must be carried out on daily.

The car technical data:
     Type: Isusu Car Pickup, No. of Engine: 432, Chassis : 7156784,  Wight : I,46  Ton 

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Alaa Eldin, tested by eng. Aiman Mohamed Shabana, revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gilel, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Nageb and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talkha PS.

Safety Precaution 

  • Safety Belt must be on.

  • Fire Chimney mast be on.

  • Jake for tires.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set .

  • Cleaning fluid, Rag wipe,

  • Screw drivers.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Check wheel nuts for looseness.

  2. Check operation of lights.

  3. Check for any oil, water, fuel and brake fluid leakage.

  4. Check parking brake lever travel.

  5. Check operation of horn 0.

  6. Check operation of instruments and indicator light.

  7. Check level of fuel in the fuel tank.

  8. Check the setting angle of the rear view mirrors.

  9. Check engine oil level.

  10. Check fan belt tension.
