Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

The function of the GRF is to maintain the reheated steam temperature at the rated value over the whole control range  in order to allow the NOX reduction.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belt, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the fan motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of fan motor

  • The fan must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery damper

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set

  • Cleaning fluid manual puller

  • Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer straight edge

  • Oil: DTE 27 Grease: Mobilplex

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check bearing temp. max = 85°C, so change the oil or inspect the bearing then repair or change

  2. Check for abnormal noise define the cause of this noise if it is from the bearing or the motor or the internal parts (Repair or change)

  3. Check bearing vibration (Max 4.8 mm/s) after repairing  the cause of vibration make balance or alignment

  4. Check oil levels lubricate over running

  5. Check shaft alignment if there are any abnormal reading make a new alignment

  6. Check fan flexible connections and joints for damage and repair or change the defected parts

  7. Check coupling clearance 6-8 mm

  8. Check inboard and outboard bearing clearance

  9. Change the oil and regrease
