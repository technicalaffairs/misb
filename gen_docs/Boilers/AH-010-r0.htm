Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

In order to preheat the air and to avoid acid corrosion on the cold layers of the regenerative air preheater when firing fuel oil two steam oil air heaters are arranged in FD fans delivery ducts they are designed to utilize auxiliary saturated steam at 7Kg/cm2 press. (Steam air heater: 2 per unit)

As heating medium the steam coil preheater consisting of series of finned tubes connected to headers and are fed by steam coming from auxiliary steam heater the steam air preheater are dimensioned so that the air entering to the regenerative air heater is heated 80 AS to maintain const the cold end arrange temp. Intended as the arithmetic mean between the flue gas exit temperature {Uncorrected for air leakage} and the air inlet temperature.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves earplugs … etc.

  • Isolate the dampers.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard mechanical tool set fire shoes

  • Special equipments for pumping water to check the tubes of the steam air-heater

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect steam coil for leakage by hydrostatic test by pressurized water into tubes & steam air heater, repair if there is leak electrodes 101B enconel – NICR1B2

  2. Clean tube fine if dust by pressurized water or by air ~ 6-7 kg/Cm2

  3. Change all gaskets

  4. Inspect all steam traps and change the internal parts if needed.
