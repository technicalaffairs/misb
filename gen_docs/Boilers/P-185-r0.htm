Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This pump transfers the condensate from the hot well of condenser to the condensate pre-heaters then to the deaerator then to the feed tank.

Technical Specification:

Type of pump 

Multi stages vertical centrifugal (VB 310–12) (8 stages)

Type of fluid

Condensate water.

Type of bearing

Ball bearing (7316).

Flow rate

6150 lit / Min.

Discharge pressure

15.3 kg/ cm2.

Speed

1475 RPM.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the CB of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Grease, Rags

  • Feeler gauge & dial indicator.

  • Dial indicator, Vibrometer.

Work to be Carried Out

Pump in Service

  1. Measure vibration level for bearing and compare it with the setting values, then analyze it and determine what to do [if it is needed].

  2. Check discharge, pressure and compare them with the setting values then replace the worn-out components [if it is needed].

 Pump out of Service

  1. Check the alignment and compare with the setting values.

  2. Check bolts fixation, then re-tight it [if it is needed].

  3. Grease the bearings of the pump.(type of grease is multi-purpose grease).

  4. Check the leakage from the glands and flanges and treat it.

  5. Change the packing seal between the shaft and the wear ring.

  6. Repaint the casing against rust.
