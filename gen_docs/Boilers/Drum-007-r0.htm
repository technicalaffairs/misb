Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified stuff should be carry the suitable safety category .

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety glass ... etc.

  • Boiler out of service and pressure should be zero.

  • Drain the drum and vents open

  • Drain all boiler walls and super heaters, re-heaters and economizer.

  • Provide the working area by a good lighting.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid

  • Suitable lifting device

 

Work to be Carried out

 

Before Isolated the Drum Mechanically

  1. Check for any leakage from the manholes

 

After Isolated the Drum Mechanically

  1. Be sure the boiler out of operation and the pressure inside the drum must be zero.

  2. Be sure the drum is drained.

  3. Be sure the vents opened.

  4. Be sure the temperature inside the drum 35 ºC

  5. Open the two manholes

  6. Check the thickness of the chemical layers

  7. Check the fixation of bolts and nuts for the drum internal parts.

  8. Check on cyclones the first and the second scrubbers

  9. Check the feed tubes opening.

  10. Clean the chemical studies on the drum internal surface .

  11. Replace manhole gaskets.
