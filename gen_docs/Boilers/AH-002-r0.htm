Introduction

This document lists the medium maintenance procedure P2 of vertical rotary regenerative air heater, which must be, performed every 6 month base as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Adel Mahmoud Al-maadawi, reviewed by Maintenance director eng. Saad Mostafa, revised by eng. Samir Abdel-Gelil at EDEPCo., accepted by maintenance general director: Ali Mostafa Al-lithy and approved by Eng.: Mostafa Alam Head  Sector of TALKHA steam power station 2 x 210 MW.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.gas mask, suitable gloves  … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this regenerative air heater.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this regenerative air heater.

  • Be sure the oil pump is stopped.

  • Be sure the cooling water of the upper bearing is clos

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.
  • Cleaning fluid, Feeler gauge.
  • Suitable lifting device

Work to be Carried Out

Adjustment of rotor sealing

Basic sealing adjustment in cold state

During the preparation of the regenerative air heater for the first starting and after excessive repairs, the sealing should be adjusted in cold state according to table 1. This basic adjustment screws the assumed clearance of the sealing elements for the hot operation of the heaters. The sealing is adjusted in cold state according to schematic sketches with the heater shut down and the drive secured – disconnected from the electrical network.

Adjustment of sector boards                                                              

The sector boards are adjusted in setting the clearances A,B,G and F according to table no. 1 and to schematic fig. no.1

Adjustment of axial boards

The axial boards are basically adjusted in setting . The clearances” C” and “E” according to table 1 and the schematic figure no.2.    

Adjustment of peripheral sealing and sector board sealing

The peripheral sealing are accessible from the flue gas channels and air channels of regenerative air heater only . their clearances “H” and “J”/ table 1 can be adjusted in partial bending the sealing sheets and checking them with a feeler gauge such adjustment may be done on a shut down heater.  The same is true for the sector board sealing. For adjustment that sealing see the schematic figure.3 .

Adjustment of clearance in hot state       

  • When setting the regenerative air heater into hot state you should cautiously its run and function to prevent the sealing from damage or destroying and/or the rotor from jamming due to thermal dilatation of its parts, which could cause a crash of the drive or motor. Above all the motor ammeter and its loading should be checked. For that purpose it is recommended to connect a precise and sensitive checking ammeter easy to inspect from the service place. the basic current consumption will be established during the cold run of the regenerative air heater after adjusting the clearances to ref. table no. 1.  The check of the basic current consumption for the drive of the regeneration air heater under cold run should be done twice a year at last – namely, it could be partly changed due to running in the plant and to other operating influences.

  • The clearances of the sector and axial boards will be adjusted by means of control elements as late as under full operation of the heater for the optimum heater tightness to be maintained, it is recommended to adjust all clearance to minimum values, to such however, that prevent the boards from wearing through rubbing by the rotor rotation. The preset clearance of sector board ends and the axial boards can be observed visually through the door on the axial sealing branch.

Attention

  • There is a danger of hot flue gas or air escape during such inspection, therefor a face shield or a gas mask should be used and the hands should be protected with gloves.

Instructions for clearance adjustment in hot state :  

  • The adjust of clearance of sector and axial boards under full operation – i.e. in hot state of the regenerative air heater must be done so as to prevent the extraction flow increase of the drive electric motor towards the value established under cold run. However the clearance should be reduced to such value to secure the combustion air leakage as low as possible and to secure full safety and perfectness of the whole plant .

  • In case of crack of the regenerative air heater - rotor deformation fire, etc. The rotor dimensions may change due to uneven thermal extension to such measure that it causes excessive friction between the rotor and sealing. It manifests itself in excessive and unnatural sounds in the area of axial and sector boards. In such cases the regenerative air heater should be shut down and disconnected from the flue gas and air channels in closing the respective valves, then the cause of the failure should be found out and removed.

Maintenance of blowing and washing device

Following maintenance should be done on the blowing and washing devices of the regenerative air heater, figure (4)

  1. Check regularly if both the steam and water closing fittings are tight and if the jets of the blowing and washings arms are not attacked by corrosion or clogged.

  2. If you find out that the fittings leak, repair or exchange them immediately, because the leaking steam or water are sources of corrosion damaging the rotor heating elements.

  3. The dirty or corroded jets must be cleaned , the damaged and worn ones exchanged.

  4. Check the terminal switches limiting the swings of the blowing arms.

  5. Check the tightness of the blower joints and of all their motion parts.

  6. Check regularly the oil state in blower gearboxes and if necessary, refill for exchange the oil.

  7. Check and lubricate the motion screw bearings, the motion screw and the nut regularly, do that in harmony.

Fire extinguisher equipment

  1. Check the fire extinguisher pipe and prevent any leakage if found.

  2. Check the fire extinguisher jet and clean it if clogged.

  3. Repair any part if needed.

  4. Operate the system manually and to tested it .

Lubrication

In General

  1. It is necessary to lubricate all rotating parts of the regenerative air heater. It works under hot operating conditions, which requires special caution in selecting suitable lubricants.

  2.  The maintenance worker should be entrusted with regular lubrication, being responsible for checking all lubrication spots. He should have a lubrication chart at disposal with lubricating instructions for the regenerative air heater.

  3. The lubrication chart should include the quality of the lubricants, time interval of the checks and the lubricant exchange in harmony with the lubrication chart. If the lubricant of required quality is not on store, it is necessary to settle on a suitable substitute lubricant with the special technical service of the supplier.

  4. The lubricants must be refilled and exchanged according to the lubrication charge may not be admitted no more is desirable super charging the bearings with grease and mixing various kinds or lubricants.

Instructions for lubricant exchange

  1. Before the first filling or the bearings or gearboxes with a lubricating charge / grease or oil / all parts and surfaces being in contact with the lubricant must be cleaned orderly having cleaned the surfaces, wash them with kerosene or with special flushing oil.

  2. Don't use cloth or rage releasing fibers for cleaning the bearings and sliding surfaces, during the bearing assembly observe the possibly highest cleanness – leave the bearings in the original packing till using them for the assembly.

  3. Apply always the lubricants of the same quality for the given lubricating spot ! if it is necessary to apply some other lubricant quality to be settled with special technical service of the lubricant supplier remove first all residues of original lubricant , and wash the whole lubricating space orderly.
