Introduction

This document out lines the major maintenance procedures  P1, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The induced fans function: pulling the combustion gases from the furnace through the heat transfer surface in the superheaters, reheaters, economizer, and gases side preheater into the stack.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is requires,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the forced draft fan.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid feeler gauge, anti dust mask

  • Suitable lifting device.

Work to be Carried Out
After Isolating the Induced Draft Fan 89TVAFG Figure (1),(1a)

Disassembly of the Rear and Front Bearing type (Dodge RT Sleevoil 5.44)

  1. Take out the temperatures sensor

  2. Drain the oil from the bearing house

  3. Isolate the cooling water supply

  4. Disassemble all cooling water house

  5. Screw off the nuts (1) connected with bearing house cover (2)

  6. Screw off the plunger screw (3)

  7. Remove cover top half from bearing (2)

  8. Remove sleeve top half (3) from shaft

  9. Lift the shaft  by Jack

  10. Remove dust seal (5)

  11. Check the thrust collar for the fixed bearing (front bearing )

  12. Check the Babbitt face for any scratches and renewed if it needed

  13. Check the alignment of  shaft wheel and motor

  14. Check the Babbitt face thrust plates (6)

Assembly of the of the Rear and front Bearing

  1. Clean every part well

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly

  3. After finishing assembly of the bearing, the shaft must be rotated  manually.

  4. Realignment of the fan and motor according to Table (1)

    5.

 

 

Disassembly of the coupling type Zurn SERIES ( F ), Figure (2)

  1. Disassemble the safety cover of coupling

  2. Screw off the bolt (1) of coupling sleeve (2)

  3. Remove the tow sleeves from hubs (3) and check hubs and sleeves teeth for any mechanical wear after clean every part well

  4. Check the O-ring in grooves seal (5)

  5. Check gap with feeler for hub separation at 905C points  min. gap = 0.155" max. gap = 0.250

  6. Chang the gasket between tow sleeve (4)

Assembly of the Coupling

  1. Clean every part well

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly

  3. After finishing assembly of the bearing it mast be rotated the shaft manually

  4. Grease the coupling according to Table (1)

  5. Realignment of the fan and motor according to Table (1)

Impeller, Case, Shaft and Foundation

  1. Clean every part well

  2. Check the Shaft from any defect (cracks deflection …) and repair or exchange it if needed

  3. Check the Impeller from any defect (cracks deflection …) and repair or exchange it if needed Check the foundation from any defect and Repair it if needed

  4. Check all anchor points from any defect (cracks deflection …) and repair or exchange it if needed

  5. If the Bolts are found to be lease tighten it and change the Bolts are Broken

  6. Inspected for mechanical interference between fan and Components

  7. Upper Clearance = 5/8 inch and lower clearance = 1/4 inch

  8. Check the shaft seal clearance after change it upper clearance = 5/16 In and Lower clearance = 11/16 Inch,
