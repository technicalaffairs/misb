Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

Steam transformer system also knows as reboiler system is used for Mazout oil heating purposes by steam.

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued; the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipments

  • Two complete maintenance tools sets

  • Cleaning fluid, manual pullers

  • Straight edge, Rages

  • Small wire brush, Filter gauge.

  • Chain, lifting hock, dial gauge

Work to be Carried out
Disassembly

  1. Chick alignment and compare it with the setting values

  2. Dismantle all attached pipe to the pump .

  3. Unscrew motor bolts and take it out .

  4. Lift pump from its place to the work place .

  5. Remove all auxiliary tubing and piping .

  6. Drain the lubricating oil from the bearing housing (240) .

  7. Match the punch mark of the coupling halves.

  8. Disconnect the two halves coupling and remove coupling spacer (399).

  9. Remove the hex nuts from casing studs holding the lantern (248) bracket to pump casing (105) .

  10. Screw the release bolts provided in casing L turn bolts evenly through a quarter turn at both side.

  11. Slightly pull out the driving unit till impeller (153) clear the pump casing (105) .

  12. Remove casing gasket (511).

  13. Unscrew the impeller nut (330).

  14. Remove the gasket between impeller and impeller nut (582) and take out the impeller.

  15. Pull shaft sleeve (515) under mech. Seal.

  16. Remove mech. Seal from shaft sleeve and keep it in clean place.

  17. Take out the casing cover along with the mech. Seal cover from casing (220).

  18. Take out liquid deflector (236).

  19. Remove the bearing with puller.

Inspection

  1. Inspect the bearings and replace them if necessary.

  2. Replace the worn out parts.

  3. Check all parts for any worn out.

  4. Check all parts for cracks.

  5. Inspect the run out of shaft.

  6. Change mech. Seal surfaces for wear and tear for moving and fixed parts.

Reassemble

  1. Before reassembly all parts should be cleaned .

  2. Mount the pair of angular contact ball bearings back to roller bearing (264) at non driving end.

  3. Tighten bearing lock nut (336).

  4. Inspect shaft along with bearing.

  5. Mount bearing cartridge (241) on bearing housing at driving end and tighten it.

  6. Mount mech. Seal (315).

  7. Mount impeller on shaft and keep the cleaner between impeller worn plant.

  8. Mount the electrical motor.

  9. Mount the two haves coupling.

  10. Adjust alignment between pump and motor

  11. Mount all auxiliary tubing and piping.

  12. Mount suction and delivery tubes.

  13. Fill bearing house with oil to suitable level

 
Stating of Pump

  1. Chick the direction of rotation of motor before starting.

  2. Open the inlet and outlet valves of mech. Seal the cooling water.

  3. Open the delivery valve after the pump has been started to operate at its full speed.

During Operation

  1. Check vibration, noisy, oil level in bearing.

  2. Check temperature of cooling water.
