Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for motor of this pump

  • Isolate the electrical cable bearing of the motor of this pump

  • The pump must be is isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves and drain it.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, suitable lifting device

  • Manual puller, feeler gauges.

  • Lessen set screws, grease.

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly of the Pump

  1. Remove tubing (27) from inboard end (3) and outboard end (2) of pump.

  2. Remove packing (21) from packing cover box (4).

  3. Remove bolts (7) from each cover (2) & (3) for remove outboard end ,inboard cover (2) & (3), and gasket (6).

  4. Remove bolts (30), cover (8) and gasket (5) from outboard cover (2)

  5. Remove cap screws (18) internal tooth washers (19) and shim (16) from outboard bracket (11).

  6. Remove bracket (11) from housing (10) by remove bolts (14) for each side.  

  7. Remove spring pin (15) to slide bushing (26) from (12) for each side.  

  8. rotor each inboard and outboard Idler (four Idlers) from housing (24),(23) slide power rotor form housing (22)

  9. Remove capscrews (12) and internal tooth washers (13) to remove cover (17).

Inspection of the Pump Parts

  1. Check the Main rotor (22) and renewed if necessary.

  2. Check inboard Idler (23) and renewed  if necessary.

  3. Check outboard Idler (24) and renewed  if necessary.

  4. Check Bushing (26) and renewed if necessary.

  5. Check shin (16) and renewed if necessary.

  6. Check sprint pin (15) and renewed if necessary.

  7. Check housing of pump (10) and renewed  if necessary.

Assembly of the Pump

  1. Clean every part well.

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After finishing assembly of the pump rotate the main shaft by hand.

  4. Realignment of the pump and motor according to the following table:

    Lube WT ( I b )

    0.19

    Operating Alignment Limits ( Inch )

    < 0.005”

    Gap Of Coupling  ( Inch )

    0.125”

    Cover Bolt Torque ( I b – In )

    200

Operation of the Pump

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK and record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration, according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.

Trouble Shooting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

1. Loss of Flow

System component malfunction

Inspect all system components and correct malfunction make sure all valve are in their proper position and suction and discharge line are open

 

Incorrect pump rotation

Shut down immediately. Imo Pump are unidirectional and extended reverse rotation can cause damage. Reveres the direction of the driver

 

Loss of prime or Boost pressure

Shut down the pump repine the pump and restart repeat until pump primes. Check boost system for malfunction

 

Air leak in suction

Symptoms are form in the fluid and noise. Reseal and tighten all joints in suction line

 

Low oil level in reservoir

May be accompanied by noise and air in the fluid fill reservoir to proper level be sure intake is for enough below the minimum fluid level to prevent vortex

 

In sufficient inlet

May be accompanied by noise may be caused by high fluid viscosity on cold start. Check suction line for blockage, closed valve or foot valve, clogged strainer. Check for plugged breather in tank

 

Mechanical trouble

Loss coupling, broken shaft, failed pump, may be accompanied by noise or vibration locate and correct cause

2. Noisy or Vibration Pump

Insufficient inlet pressure

May be pump speed excessive

 

Air in fluid

Check for leak in suction line low fluid level vortex at inlet to suction line, or return lines above fluid level

 

Pump not aligned

Realign the pump. Misalignment may also be caused be temperature variation

 

Mechanical problems

Loose or mis-positioned coupling broken shaft, bad bearing

3. Excessive Power

Pump speed excessive

Reduce pump rotational speed

    Consumption

High fluid viscosity

Reduce viscosity by temperature control of fluid

 

High pressure

Checks for blockage of discharge line from the pump

 

Mechanical

Bent shaft, tight pacing pipe strain, failed pump locate and correct cause
