Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as  indicated by M1 in the above title block.

The soot blowers used to clean Long Retractable, Multi-Nozzles Blower, Ljungstrom

Technical Specification

No of Soot blowers

12 for Long Retractable Blower

16 for Multi-Nozzles Blower

 2 for Ljungstrom (air heater)

Pressure of Steam

  35 Bar

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this soot blower.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of the soot blower.

  • The soot blower must be isolated mechanically by closing the main valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller,

    •

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the front rollers torn correctly.

  2. Check the shape parking of the carriage (not leakage of steam).

  3. Check the rollers of the carriage turn correctly.

  4. Check the Bearings of chain tensioner.

  5. Check the bearing of the bevel gear of the chain.

  6. Check the shape of the packing of the valve (not leakage of steam).

  7. Check the oil level at the gear box of the carriage.
