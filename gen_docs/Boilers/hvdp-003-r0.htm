Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Suitable lifting device.

  • Manual puller, Feeler gauge.

Work to be Carried Out

Removal of the pump with pump’s gearbox

  1. Check the closing of the suction, delivery. Heating steam, and condensate valve.

  2. Prepare a tank for catching liquid from disconnected piping and from the pump at which drain plug is screwed off.

  3. Disconnect suction and delivery piping from the pump.

  4. Screw off the nuts of the coupling bolts.

  5. Screw of the bolts (30) between the pump brackets and foundation.

  6. Lifting the pump with pump’s gearbox to the maintenance workshop.

Disassembly of the pump

  1. Remove the coupling from the shaft of the pump’s gearbox by using suitable puller and take out key (17).

  2. Screw off nuts (31) to remove pump cover (26) from the pump body (20).

  3. Screw off nuts (24) of the rotating pistons.

  4. Screw off the nuts of gland flange (29).

  5. Screw off nuts of the bolts (32) which connected hydraulic part of the pump with pump’s gearbox.

  6. Pull the hydraulic part with rotating pistons along the two shafts.

Inspection of the pump parts

  1. Check the rotating piston (23) from mechanical wear and renewed if it need.

  2. Check the pumps cover (26) and hydraulic part (20) from the mechanical wear and repair or renewed if it need.

  3. Check the fixation of the stuffing box to the hydraulic part.

  4. Check the gasket (25) and renewed if it need

  5. Chang the gland packing with the suitable size.

Disassembly of the pump’s gear box

  1. Drain the lubricating oil from the gearbox by screw of plug (33).

  2. Screw off nuts (35) to remove gearbox cover (14) and take out oil seal (13).

  3. Screw off the nuts (12) from the two shafts of the gearbox (2)&(3).

  4. Pullouts ring (16) and remove oil seal (15).

  5. Pullout the gearwheel (10) and gearwheel with bevel (8)&(9).

  6. Take out the supporting ring (7) from shafts (2)&(3).

  7. Hammering on the shaft (3) of gearbox but it must be put a wooden piece between the hammer and the shaft.

  8. Take out the driver and driven shaft of the gearbox.

  9. Pullout the inner race of the bearing (6) from shafts.

  10. Take out the distance pipe (5).

  11. Pullout the bearing (4).

  12. Remove the outer race of the bearing (6) from the bearing house of the gearbox.

Inspections of the pump’s gear box parts

  1. Check the gear wheel (10) and gearwheel with bevel (8) and renewed if it needed.

  2. Check the two shafts (2)&(3) and repair or renewed it if needed.

  3. Check the bearing and renewed if it needed.(NU2209B &30209E)

  4. Check the oil seal (13&15)) and renewed if it needed.

  5. Check the gasket (36) and changed it if needed.

Assembly of the pump’s gear box parts

  1. Clean every part of the pump’s gearbox

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After assembly the gearbox it must be rotated by hand by using coupling in the driver shaft (2)

Assembly of the pump to the pump’s gearbox

  1. Clean well every part of the pump.

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After finishing the assembly of the pump to the pump’s gearbox we must be
    rotating the driver shaft (2) easy by using the coupling.

Installing the pump with the pump’s gear box to the foundation

  1. Install the pump in reverse sequence of the removal sequence.

Re. Alignment      According to the MPIS doc # cpl-001-r0

Change the packing of the stuffing box

  1. Clean the surfaces of the stuffing box and shaft well.

  2. Wire the suitable packing size on a part of shaft with the same diameter of the pump shaft

  3. The number of turns is equal to the numbers of rings needed in the stuffing box.

  4. Cut all packing rings along the shaft by using a sharp knife.

  5. Fit the rings in the stuffing box with the cut turned 180o, starting at 9 clock.

  6. The packing should never be knocked as they then may become damaged.

  7. Remember that in a stuffing box there must always be as light leakage of the fluid.

  8. Do not tighten the gland hard to prevent the rise in temperature of the stuffing box.

Refilling the pump’s gear box by lubricating oil

  1. Prepare the suitable lubricating oil in a clean bottle.

  2. Open the plug (34) and close plug (33).

  3. Fill some lubricating oil and turn the gearbox by hand many times to clean gearbox.

  4. Open the plug (33) and drain the oil, which used to clean.

  5. Close the plug (33) again and open plug (34) and then filling the gearbox with oil until 2/3 of oil side glass.

Trouble shooting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

Pump does not supply
any amount

Suction  line trouble

Check suction valve it must be opened.

Check air tightness of suction piping.

Pump not primed

Prime the pump.

Opposite sense of rotation

Check the rotation direction.

Leakage in relief valve.

Check the relief valves seat and also its spring.

Excessive suction lift.

Check the recommended specification.

Excessive worn in rotating pistons and pump casing.

Chang the rotating piston and repair pump casing or changed it.

Delivered amount is low

Suction line trouble

Check suction valve and it must be opened.

Check air-tightness of suction piping.

Pump speed are too low.

Check recommended specifications.

Clearance between rotating pistons, bore or covers is too great

Change the worn parts.

Mechanical impurities in the liquid.

Check the filter before pump.

Pump doses not reach the prescribed pressure.

Pressure gauge gives low pressure

Recalibrate the pressure gauge or renewed it

Clearance between the rotating pistons, body and covers is too great

Check or change worn parts of pump

Direct connection of suction with delivery happened in the pump.

Dismantle the pump and check it.

Excessive noise in pump

Air in pump casing.

Check air-tightness of pump casing.

Misalignment.

Realignment the pump with motor and retighten all bolts of pump and motor with foundation.

Discharge pressure too high

Check the recommended discharge pressure.

Bearing damage.

Change the bearing.

Excessive power consumption

Excessive discharge pressure.

Check the pressure gauge.

Uncorrected assembly of pump

Mount the pump correctly.

Cold liquid or high viscosity

Measure the viscosity and temperature.

Gland is tightening too much and heated.

Check the stuffing box and it must be leakage some drops of liquid to cooling gland.

Bearing damage.

Check the bearing and exchanged it if needed

Pump speed higher than rated.

Measure the pump speed.
