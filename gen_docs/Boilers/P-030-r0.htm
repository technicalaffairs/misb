Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should cany the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets shoes, gloves, … etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of tills pump

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this pump

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller.

  • Suitable lifting device feeler gauge.

  • Hammer, torque wrench.

  • Dial indicator.
