Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category                         

  •  Safety fence  with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  •  Wear your safety helmet, shoes.... etc

  •  Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this jockey pump.

  •  Isolate the electrical cable feeding the motor of this pump.

  •  The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller.

  • Suitable lifting device, feeler gauge.

  • Hammer, Torque wrench.

  • Dial indicator.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the main bearing temperature.

  2. Check the pump for smooth operation.

  3. Check all anchor bolts and support steel fasteners bolts, and tighten if necessary.

  4. Check pump packing leakage and adjust as required.

  5. Examine pump housing for signs of wear.

  6. Examine bearings and couplings and make adjustments or replacements where necessary. Give particular attention to bearing seals.

  7. Regrease pump bearing with mobilux 2 ( 0.2 kg )

  8. Check pump packing and replace as required.

  9. Check bearing seals and replace as required.

  10. Tighten all hold down bolts in pump housing.

  11. Check coupling alignment.

  12. Check impellers for wear and cracks.

  13. Check alignment between pump and motor.
