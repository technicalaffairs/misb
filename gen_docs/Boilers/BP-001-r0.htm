Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1  (to check leakage from pipes), which must be carried out when the gas turbine have minor maintenance after 4000 workhour.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Abdel Meniem Fahmy  tested by eng. Abdel Qader Fath Alla, at 04 May 2003 on Boiler no. 2 and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gilel, accepted by general director eng. Sobhy Mohamed El Bakry and approved by eng. Moneer Mohamed El Wakeel Head Sector of Demietta PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • The HRSG is out of serves & depressurized the required section.

  • Drain the the required section after opening the vent valve.

  • The inlet dampers are closed & locked mechanical when the gas turbine in service.

  • Disconnect the power supply for the damper actuators.

  • Seal air fan is in service when the gas turbine in service.

Tools and Equipment

  • TIG welding machine. 

  • Grinder & grinding wheel.

  • Selected filler metal for TIG welding.

Work to be Carried Out

High Pressure Super Heaters Pipes


The technical data:

  • Plain tubes, 8 rows  (7 finned tube & 1 without finns) and two inlet headers and two outlet headers.

  • Tube material (15mo3 & 13crMo44 &10crMo910), fins material ST4

  • Heating surface 3108 m2

Procedure (s)

  1. Fill the hp section by filling water pump

  2. Inspect and adjust the leakage

  3.  Drain the hp super heater.

  4. Weld the cracks after grinding (TIG welding) 

  5. Make hydraulic pressure test for the hp section by the hp fwp to operating press.

High Pressure Evaporator

The technical data:

  • Natural circulation type.

  • Evaporator part 1 parallel tube (9x190) ST35.8 III &

  • Evaporator part 2 parallel tube (10x190) ST35.8 III & Finns material ST4

  • Heating surface 20562 m2

Procedure (s)

  1. Fill the HP section by filling water pump after close the drain and open the vents.

  2. Inspect and adjust the leakage.

  3. Drain the HP evaporator.

  4. Weld the cracks after grinding (argon welding).

  5. Make hydraulic pressure test for the HP section by the HP.FWP to operating press.
