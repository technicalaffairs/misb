After Isolating the Re-circulation Gas Fan Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check the front /rear bearing and replace the defect bearing.

  2. Check the operation   of loading mechanism and change any defect part

  3. Dismantle the safety cover of the cooling disk.

  4. Check the tightness of cooling disk of the front and rear –bearing house.

  5. Dismantle the safety cover of the coupling.

  6. Dismantle the coupling covers and check the two half springs, exchange if it needed.

  7. Check the alignment of the coupling half’s and re –alignment it if needed.

  8. Re- grease the coupling springs

  9. Check and retighten all bolts

  10. Check every part well.

  • If you need any help please look at the major maintenance procedure MPIS doc. no. Fan-004-r0

  • After successful inspection, operate the fan again, read all the available record value and putted in table (1) to compare between the values before and after inspection.
