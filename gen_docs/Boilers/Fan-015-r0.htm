Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be carried out every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block.

The forced draft fan function: pushing atmospheric air through the air preheater dampers through various air ducts and burners into the furnace.

These fans are installed upstream of boiler to supply the required amount of air for combustion,

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is requires,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the forced draft fan.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning Fluid Feeler gauge, anti dust mask

  • Suitable lifting device.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Isolating the Forced Draft Fan Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Measure the vibration  (3: 6 Mils)

  2. Listen and check any abnormal Noise.

  3. Check the Lubrication oil Level in front and rear bearing housing

  4. Check the Temperature of rear and front bearing 800 oF

  5. Check any leakage of oil Lubrication from front or rear bearing.

  6. Check any leakage of water cooling from front or rear bearing.

  7. Read and check the quantity of air according lead.

  8. Check any oil leakage from dust seal.

After Isolating the Forced Draft Fan Mechanically and Electrically 

  1. Check the front / rear bearing and replace the defect bearing.

  2. Check the shaft seals and replace it if needed.

  3. Dismantle the safety cover of the coupling .

  4. Check any leakage from o-rings or gasket

  5. Check gap with feeler for hob separation at 905C    points = 1/8 ".

  6. Check hubs alignment with straight edges 905C    points 

  7. Lubricant hub and sleeve teeth with new grease before cleaning all parts

  8. Check the alignment of the coupling half's and re-alignment it if needed.

  9. Clean the pipes of water cooling by opening the drains.

  10. Check and clean the check valve of water cooling.

  11. Check and retighten all bolts.

  12. Check every part well.

  13. After successful inspection, operate the fan again read all available record values and put them in table (2) and compare between the values before and after inspection
