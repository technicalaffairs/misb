Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety Helmet, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of the pump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual pullers .

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

 

Before Isolated the Pump mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check any noise or vibration in pump or motor.

  2. Check any leakage from the pipe connection to the pump.

  3. Check the amount of leakage from mechanical seal, < 50 drop/sec.

  4. Check the safety valve of the pump.

  5. Check the pressure of the pump delivery.

  6. Check the temperature of bearing house.

After Isolated the Pump mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check the coupling and flexible part.

  2. Check the mechanical seal leakage.

  3. Re – grease ball bearing.

  4. Check alignment between pump and motor.

  5. Retighten the bolts connected pump and motor with foundation.

  6. Clean the pump
