Introduction

This document out lines the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

The pump function is provided with the boilers required fuel and with required pressure.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety Helmet, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of the pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Isolate the steam heating valve and condensate valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual pullers.

  • Straight edge, rages.

  • Small wire brush, filler gauge.

  • Chain, lifting hock, dial gauge.

Work to be Carried Out
Dismounting of Screw Pump

  1. Prior to commencing the dismounting.

  2. The following operations must be performed:

  3. Pinch off power supply cable to the motor.

  4. All valves (supply, delivery, steam and condensate) must be closed.

  5. Drain fluid by opening drain plug (use collecting tank)

  6. Let screw pump cool down to ambient temperature.

  7. Dismount motor if need, manometers and manometer lines and take their out.

  8. Dismount protection against accidental contact from the base plate.

Dismount of the Cartridge Unit

  1. Screw off bolts (52) to dismount pumps cover non drive side (4) from pump casing (1) and take gasket (21) out.

  2. Screw off bolts (51) to dismount the cartridge unit pump from the pump casing (1) and then take it out.

  3. Remove gasket (21) and "O" ring (29).

Dismounting of the Bearing Cover and Bearing

  1. With draw coupling half from the driving spindle (12) use manual puller.

  2. Remove key (41) from the driving spindle (12).

  3. Remove cir clip (35) and spacer bush (16) from the driving spindle (12).

  4. Screw off bolts (54) to dismount bearing cover (5) from pump cover – drive side (3).

  5. Pull bearing cover (5) over the spindle (12) by using manual puller. Then take gasket (22) = Note: stationary seal ring (83) remain in the bearing cover (5)

  6. Dismount circlip (37) from the bearing cover (5)

  7. Dismount groove ball bearing (34) from the bearing cover (5) by using manual puller and take care to avoid any damages to the stationary seal ring (83)

  8. Dismount supporting washer (36)

Dismount of the Mechanical Seal

  1. Dismount stationary seal ring (83) and "O" ring from the bearing cover (5).

  2. Dismount rotating part of mechanical seal(83) which consisting of rotating seal ring, casing, retaining profile washer, spring and valve spring plate over the driving spindle (12)

  3. Dismount spacer ring (80) and junk ring (81).

Dismounting of the set of Spindle

  1. Screw off bolts (55) and dismount pump cover – drive side (3) over the driving spindle (12).

  2. Remove gasket (24) and clean sealing surface.

  3. With draw set of spindle consisting of drive spindle (12) and two idler spindle (13) with the balance bushes (8) from the drive spindle (12).

Inspection and Changing of all part for the Pump

  1. Check all gaskets (21) & (22)  & (24) and change damage gasket.

  2. Check "O" ring (29) and change it if need.

  3. Check all circlips (35) & (37) and change it if need

  4. Check the deep groove ball bearing (34) and change it if need

  5. Clean and repair the bearing house

  6. Check the stationary ring, rotating ring and "O" ring for mechanical seal (83) and change damage ring.

  7. Check the pump casing insert and spindle for mechanical wear and chemical corrosion and renew it if there is big clearance between spindles and insert.

  8. Check the two halfs of the coupling and its flexible parts and change it if need. 

 Assembly of the Pump

  1. Clean very well all parts of pump.

  2. Assembly every parts in reverse sequence of dismounting.

  3. After finishing the assembly of the pump, make alignment the pump with motor and rotating by hand from the coupling.

  4. Re- grease the ball bearing from the lubricating nipple.

  5. Return work permit and put it in a portion.

 Starting of Pump

  1. Chick the direction of rotation before starting.

  2. Open the inlet and outlet valves.

  3. Open tracing steam for heating the pump.

  4. Check the alignment of the pump after heating.

During Operation

  1. Check the vibration and noisy

  2. Check the temperature of bearing

  3. Check the discharge pressure
