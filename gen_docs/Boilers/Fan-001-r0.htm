Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be carried out every two months as indicated by M2 in the title block.

This Fan is used for heating combustion air to raise the efficiency of the boiler.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, and safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the re-circulation gas fan and its lubrication system.

After isolating the re-circulation gas fan mechanically and electrically

  1. Check the front & rear bearing and replace the defect bearing.

  2. Open the manhole of fan and check the impeller from corrosion or mechanical wear.

  3. Check the clearance between the impeller and fan casing.

  4. Check the internal sealing and changed it if needed.

  5. Dismantle the safety cover of the cooling disk.

  6. Dismantle and check the cooling desk of the rear &front bearing house.

  7. Check the rear & front outside sealing and exchanged it if needed.

  8. Dismantle the safety cover of the coupling.

  9. Dismantle the coupling covers and check the two half springs, exchange if it needed.

  10. Check the alignment of the two coupling half and re-alignment it if needed.

  11. Re-grease the coupling’s springs.

  12. Check and retighten all bolts.

  13. Clean the oil cooler and check the oil cooler fan.

  14. Check the lubricating oil pump and repair it if needed.

  15. Check the oil level in the tank and bearing house / or refill it if needed.

  • If you need any help please look at the major maintenance procedure MPIS doc. no. Fan-002-r0

  • After successful inspection, operate the fan again, read all the available record value and putted in table (1) to compare between the values before and after inspection.
