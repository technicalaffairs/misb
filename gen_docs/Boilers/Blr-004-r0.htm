Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.    Quantity: 1/Unit

The primary function of boiler is to convert water to steam – the design and size of boiler varies widely according to their intended use, while the type of boiler auxiliary including fuel burning equipment differs with the manufacture and proposed installation area boiler can also be called steam generators or steam generating units

The radiant heat natural convection box type boiler described and is constructed to Babcock & Wilcox design

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

Work to be Carried Out

  1. In accordance with annual boiler outage schedule tasks refer to document no. AA001-12

  2. Check all drain line and valves from the steam generator and associated piping

  3. Ensure that all steam threads on manual and motor operated drain valves are clean and lubricated and repair or change any of the valves that do not work perfectly.
