Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection steps I1, which must be performed every 6 months as indicated by M6  in the above title block.

Pump function is to:

a)      Transfer of clear water to chair coal filter.

b)      Transfer of acid water to anion.

c)      Transfer of demi water to make up water tank.

d)      Transfer of drinking water to drinking water tank.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Use safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

    •

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, suitable lifting device

  • Manual puller, feeler gauges.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check any noise for vibration.

  2. Check any leakage from the piping connection to the pump.

  3. Check the leakage from stuffing box and prevent it by retightening the gland bolts.

  4. Check leakage of lubricating oil from bearing house.

  5. Check the oil level.

  6. Check the pressure in pump delivery.

  7. Check the temperature of bearing house.

After Isolating the Pump (mechanically and electrically)

  1. Check the coupling pins.

  2. Change the packing if there is more leakage.

  3. Prevent the leakage of lubricating oil.

  4. Add lubricating oil to pump to level less than 2/3 side glass.

  5. Retighten the bolts connected to the pump and motor with foundation.

Return Pump into Service

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK, check the direction of rotation, record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration, according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.
