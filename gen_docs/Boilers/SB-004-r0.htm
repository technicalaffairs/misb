Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.     

The purpose of the soot blowing system (1/unit) is to clean the soot's deposits from the exterior surfaces of the boiler, super heater, reheaters, economizer and air preheatrs blowing frequency is determined by visual inspection of boiler surface by monitoring steam temperature

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence like ropes with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of P/P motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the motor

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard mech. tool set

  • Grease gun

  • Grease Mobiplex 47

  • Oil Mobil gear 624

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check mobil pipe contact with rollers adjust for contact

  2. Tighten gland bolts for sealing joint between fixed pipe and mobil piping to prevent leak – change it if necessary

  3. Adjust flow rate and press. After poppet V/V

  4. Regrease all grease fittings for rollers and carriage

  5. Clean driver chains for carriage with solvent and relubricate and coat with film liquid parffing – molykote (using chemical or organic material)

  6. Change oil driver gear box and carriage gear

  7. Tighten flange bolts of poppet V/V to prevent leak

  8. Check alignment of source shaft

  9. Check sealing air flow or adjust it.
