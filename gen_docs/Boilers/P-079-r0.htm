Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Air preheater wash water pumps (2 per unit) are supplied from the service water system to the two P/Ps any of the two P/Ps is used to wash any of the two air preheater as the discharge piping of the two air heaters wash P/Ps is common

The technical specification of the condensate pump:  
R.P.M = 1450            Suction pressure:304.8 mm H2O
T = 94/25 °C               Discharge pressure: 6096 mm H2O   

HP = 60                      Model = LG P018 A,B

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of P/P motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of P/P motor

  • The P/P must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery V/V

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard mech. tool set.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check pump for abnormal noise, inspect the internal parts and change if needed

  2. Check pump capacity and discharge press. to determine if new rings seals … ETC may be required

  3. Change oil – mobil DTE  heavy adjust gland leakage.
