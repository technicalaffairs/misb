Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection steps I1, required for H.P mazot pump, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Hesham M. Hassan, reviewed by director eng. Saad Mostafa, and revised by quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gelil at EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Ali Al-Lithy and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam: Head Sector of Talkha Steam Power Station 2x210MW

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Feeler gauge.

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

 

 

Before isolated the pump mechanically and electrically

Steps Work to be carried out Setting
(reference value )

 Test results
1 Check any noise or vibration in pump or motor. OK – NOT OK  
2 Check any leakage from the piping connections to the pump OK – NOT OK  
3  Check the amount of leakage from mechanical seal and it must not more than 50 drop / min <50 drop / min  
4 Check the bearing by using SPM OK – NOT OK  
5 Check the safety valve of the pump OK – NOT OK  
6 Check the pressure in the pump’s delivery. 3.8 Mpa  
7 Check the temperature of the bearing house. < 80 ºC

Steps Work to be carried out Setting
(reference value )		  Test results
1 Check the coupling and flexible part OK – NOT OK  
2 Check the mechanical seal if there’s heavy leakage OK – NOT OK  
3 Re-grease ball bearing  OK – NOT OK  
4 Check alignment between pump and motor. OK – NOT OK  
5 Retighten the bolts connected pump and motor with foundation. OK – NOT OK  
6 Clean the pump  OK – NOT OK
