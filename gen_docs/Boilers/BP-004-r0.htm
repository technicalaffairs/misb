Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every two months and depending on external leakage as indicated by M2 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified staff should be carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety glass ... etc.

  • Boiler out of service and pressure should be zero.

  • Vents opened.

  • Drain all boiler walls and super heaters, re-heaters and economizer.

  • Provide the working area by a good lighting.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Grind and grinding wheel.

  • Selected filler metal for TIG welding (Argon) and suitable weld wire.

  • Welding machine.

  • Suitable lifting device.

 

Work to be Carried out

 

Before Shut down Boiler

  1. Check quantity of feeding water and quantity of steam on chart of control room

  2. Check any leakage for pipes of super heater, reheaters, suspension tube, walls and economizer.

  3. Check drum water level on chart at control room.

 

After Shut down Boiler

  1. Be sure the feed water out operation

  2. Check the pressure in side boiler (all walls) equal zero.

  3. Be sure walls drum, super heaters and re-heaters drained opened.

  4. Vents opened.

  5. Be sure the forced drought fan and recercule.

  6. Ensure that the tion fan out of service and isolated

 

Inspection the Defected Pipes

  1. Clean every part wall before any inspection.

  2. Operate the feed pump to increase pressure boiler from 0 to 10 bar and check any water leaks.

  3. Check all pipes to find damage and remark on it.

 

Repair the Defected Pipes

  1. After finding out of the place of failure pipes.

  2. Remove the fixing part as the suspension and bonds.

  3. Cut out the damage pipes.

  4. Grind the edges to be ready for welding.

  5. New pipes should be prepared to be replaced.

  6. The length of replaced pipe must not be less than 250mm in the straight part of the part.

  7. The weld must be chosen in the way ensuring the distance from the part welded to the pipe min 20mm.

  8. After success fully welding, repeats the pressure again and check again.
