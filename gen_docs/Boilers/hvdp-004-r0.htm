Introduction

       This document out lines the details equipment inspection steps I1, required for horizontal volume displacement pump with rotating pistons, which must be performed every 2 and 6 month as indicated by M2, M6 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes, … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Feeler gauge.

  • Suitable lifting device.

Inspection every 6 month
Before isolated the pump mechanically and electrically

Steps Work to be carried out

Setting value

 Test results

1

 Check any noise or vibration in pump , pump’s gear box and main gear box OK – NOT OK  
2 Check the amount of leakage from stuffing box and minimized it by retighten the gland bolts and keep some drops for cooling. OK – NOT OK  
3 Check any leakage from the pipe’s connections to the pump OK – NOT OK  
4 Check leakage of lubricating oil in pump’s gearbox OK – NOT OK  
5 Check leakage of lubricating oil in main gearbox. OK – NOT OK  
6 Check the oil level in pump’s gearbox 2/3 oil side glass  
7 Check the oil level in main gearbox. 2/3 oil side glass  
8 Check the pressure in the pump’s delivery 0.6 Mpa  
9 Check the temperature of all bearing house, it must be less than 70 C. < 70 c°
