Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every 3000 hours of operation. This procedure was applied in ABU QIR power plant since 1985 and the final draft is issued in May 2005.

The first draft is initiated and tested by eng. Mohamed El-Touny,  accepted by eng. Mohamed Abd El-Sadek general director of Abu Kir PS, approved by eng. Aly Anwar El-Fayoumy Head Sector of  Abu Kir PS, and certified by eng. Mona Ahmad El Sharkawy: WDEPC Inspection and Quality Control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety helmet, safety shoes

  • Disconnect the 6 kv circuit breaker and tagged it out

  • Put or switch the earth cable for motor

Tools and Equipment

  • Torque wrench

  • Adjustable wrenches - with different sizes

  • Grease gun

  • Turbo oil T46

  • Alvania R3 , R2

  • Telious oil 32

    •

Work to be Carried Out

 Maintenance of fan
  1. General cleaning and inspection of metal fixed parts and painting with anti oxide clean the rode & blades using hard brush and paint it with anti oxide paint

  2. Change the oil for both outlet fan bearing and inlet fan bearing ( using telious oil 32 ) as follows:
          * Dismantle the outer cover of the bearing
          * Dismantle the lower plug to remove the old oil
          * Dismantle the upper part of the bearing and inspect for roller Iub ring and assemble it
             again
          * Cleaning the oil indication level glass
          * Dismantle the upper plug and fill the new oil, tighten and fit lower plug
          * Measure the winding isolation

               

Maintenance of coupling between motor and fan

  1. Dismantle the outer covers of bearing and coupling
  2. Dismantle the two half casings covers of the coupling
  3. Remove the two spring layers. And remove the old grease
  4. General cleaning for the coupling
  5. Measure the distance between the hubs and readjust if necessary
  6. Measure the misalignment and readjust if necessary
  7. Fill up the grooves and space between hubs with grease
  8. Position the two spring layers and fill up with grease
  9. Assemble the coupling two half casings with their gasket over the spring and hubs and assembly of the half casing
  10. Assembly of the outer cover of the coupling and bearing

Maintenance of suction air damper (control damper)

  1. Inspect the blades
  2. Clean & inspect the protective air screen and change if necessary
  3. Grease the bearing dampers axis (Grease darnia quantity 30 gm / bearing)
  4. Grease the bearings of dampers blades main axis
  5. Grease the dampers blades axis
  6. Grease the dampers opening connecting rods and pins

Maintenance of the discharge damper

  1. Grease the bearing of the dampers axis
  2. Inspect the oil of the dampers piston glasses
  3. Adjust the damper opening / closing indicators position

Fan back in service

Test the fan back into service after maintenance procedures on the motor, the control and protection circuits, and the 6 KV Circuit Breaker in coordination with the shift engineer.
