Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 which must be performed every year as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Boostering (increasing) the F.D.F air to suitable press. for cooling the flame detectors

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence lie ropes with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the fan motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of fan motor

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set
  • Gasket material

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantel the fan

  2. Inspect the fan for cracks or erosion and clean of foreign matters then repair the cracks

  3. Inspect fan casing and repair it if necessary

  4. Inspect fan shaft and key for damage requiring and change any of the damaged parts
