Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must performed every two months as indicated by M2 in the title block.

The Ljungstrom air heater absorbs waste heat from flue gases, then transfers this heat to incoming cold air to increase the temperature of the incoming combustion or air process.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

    •

  • The regenerative air heater must be isolated mechanically and electrically

  • Be sure the cooling water of the upper bearing is closed.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Feeler, gauge, Hammer

  • Suitable lifting device, Manual puller, Torque wrench

Work to be Carried Out

 

Before Isolating the Air Heater Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check any abnormal noise or vibration.

  2. Check any air or flue gases  leakage from manholes.

  3. Check the oil level of upper guide bearing.

  4. Check the oil level of lower support  bearing.

  5. Check the oil level of drive unit.

  6. Check the temperature of the upper and lower bearing, temp. should be ≤ 50

  7. Check the oil level of drive unit for blowing equipment.

  8. Check auxiliary drive in service for a few minutes

    9.

After Isolating the Air Heater Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Vent the water cooling system for Guide Bearing in the top end and clean it if needed.

  2. Check sealing clearance according to table No. 1 and  figure No. 6

  3. Check alignment limits for coupling.
