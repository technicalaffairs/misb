Introduction

This document  outlines  the  equipment inspection I1, which performed every six month as indicated by M6 in the title block. The same procedures will be applied for all pumps have the same technical specification mentioned at the last page of this document.

Mazout (Screw pump) transfer pump function to transfer mazout from main tank to another main tank or to transfer mazout from main tank to daily tank.

Aly Mostafa Al Lethy EDEPC inspection & quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for Motor of this Pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable bearing of the motor of this pump

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves and drain it.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, and Suitable lifting device.

  • Manual puller, Feeler gauge, oil type WT = 0.19 LB

  • Lessen set screws, and grease.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Isolating the Pump Mechanically and Electrically  

  1. Check the direction of the motor rotation.

  2. Check for any unusual noise or vibration and prevent it if found.

  3. Be sure there is no any leakage from any gasket or any O ring and prevent it if found.

  4. Check pressure in the pump's delivery.

  5. Check quantity delivered of the pump

After Isolated the Pump Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check the impeller and casing from mechanical wear and measure the clearances by dismantle the pump.

  2. Check the bearing in pump.

  3. Prevent the leakage from gasket or O ring, change it if any.

  4. Check the couplings pins and change grease of coupling.

  5. Retighten all bolts connected pump and motor with foundation.

  6. Check the alignment between pump and motor.

  7. Change lubrication of coupling (WT = 0.19 LB).

  8. Change the packing if leak is heavy and didn't prevented by tightening the gland bolts.

  9. Check the temperature of packing house it should be < 100 F

Return Pump into Service

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK, direction of rotation is correct and record the readings of pressure, temperature, vibration, according to the technical specification table, and compare with the previous reading.
