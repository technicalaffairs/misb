Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • The filter must be isolated mechanically by closing :-

  • De-aerated filter by opening de-aeration valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

Work to be Carried Out

 

 Dismantle the filter sieve insert from the filter body

  1. Check closing of the input and output mazot valve

  2. Check opening the de-aeration valves (5).

  3. Wait until the pressure inside the filter is decreased to zero .

  4. Screw off nuts (3) to remove cover (2) of the filter

  5. Take out the filter insert from spindle(12).

Disassembly of the filter sieve insert

  1. Screw off nuts (6)then take out the spindle (8) with spindle (12) and top & bottom covers (7) & (8)

  2. Screw off spindle (12) from nut (11)

  3. Screw off nuts (13) to remove spring (14).

Clean and repair filter insert parts

  1. Clean every part of the filter insert specially filters sieves.

  2. Check the sieves (9) and repair damage part.

  3. Check spring (14) and renewed it if needed

  4. Check gasket (15) and renewed it if needed

Assembly of the filter sieve insert

  1. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

Assembly of the filter's insert inside the filter body

  1. Put the filter insert after assembly it inside the filter body.

  2. Check gasket (14) and renewed it if needed.

  3. Close filter body by cover (2) and tight nuts (3) in cross sequence .

  4. Close de-aeration valves (5) and open equivalent valve to check any leakage .

  5. Take the air inside the filter by open the valves (5) and close it
    directly when mazot come out.
