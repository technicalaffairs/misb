Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1 of vertical rotary regenerative air heater, which must be, performed every 2 month base as indicated by M2 in the title block.  

The FIRST BRNO MACHINE WORKS, National Enterprise, BRNO C.S.S.R., offers designs construes, manufactures and delivers the regenerative air heaters type Ljungstrom (Ljs) on the base of the license contract with the Swedish firm SVENSKA rotor MaskinerA, B, STOCKHOLM.

Saad Mostafa, revised by eng.: Samir Abdel-Gelil at EDEPCo., accepted by maintenance general director: Ali Mostafa Al-lithy and approved by eng.: Mostafa Alam Head  Sector of Talkha steam power station 2 x 210 MW.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Feeler gauge.

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Be sure there is no any abnormal noise or vibration and prevented it if found.

  2. Be sure there is no any air or flue gases leakage from manholes and prevented it if found.

  3. Check the operation of the oil lubricating pump and repair it if there is any abnormal condition (noise – vibration -leakage – Pressure drop ).

  4. Check the oil level of oil lubricating tank and add oil if the level less than 2/3 of oil side glass.

  5. Check the oil level of upper guide bearing and be sure there is no any leakage and add oil if the level less than 2/3 of oil side glass.

  6. Check the oil level of drive unit and be sure there in no any oil leakage and add oil if the level less than 2/3 of oil side glass.

  7. Check and record / measure the temperature of the upper and lower bearing ( its about 40 – 50 °C ).

  8. Clean the intake oil filter inside the oil tank.

  9. Read and record the pressure of the oil-lubricating pump (350:380 kpa).

  10. Check the oil level of drive unit of blowing equipment and add oil if the level less than 2/3 of oil side glass.

  11.  Be sure there is no any leakage in the oil and cooling water pipes and correct it if found.
