Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety glasses,... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipments

  • Two complete maintenance tools sets

  • Cleaning fluid, manual pullers

  • Straight edge, rages

  • Small wire brush, filter gauge.

  • Chain, lifting hock, dial gauge

 

Work to be Carried Out
Disassembly

  1. Remove the delivery pipe connection.

  2. Disconnect the motor unscrew the bolts and take out the motor.

  3. Remove the lubricating pipes (562.1, 562.2). 

  4. Remove the coupling nuts and pins (404) alone with the coupling bushes (400) and coupling washer

  5. Remove the coupling (390) by using suitable puller.

  6. Remove the bearing cover (270-1) by unscrewing the bolts.

  7. Unscrew the two bearing nuts (335/336).

  8. Remove thrust bearing .

  9. Loosen the foundation bolts of motor stool and support plats.

  10. Unscrew the pipe nut (338) take out the support plate (383).

  11. Life the entire pump unit with the help of bolts and fit clamp to the top column pipe (132) and pipe (142.2)

  12. Remove strainer (216)

  13. Disconnect the bend (280) and lubricating pipe (562.1)

  14. Loosen the nut holding casing cover (220).

  15. Take out casing cover from the impeller and shaft assembly.

  16. Hold other end of the shaft by spanner and unscrew the impeller screw (669) by using Allen key.

  17. Pull out the impeller (151) remove impeller key.

  18. Disconnect bell month.

  19. Remove impeller shaft sleeve (311) alone its key.

Inspection

  1. Inspect the bearings and replace them if necessary.

  2. Replace the worn out parts.

  3. Check all parts for any worn out.

  4. Inspect the run out of shaft.

Reassembly

  1. Put the impeller shaft slave (311) on pump shaft (185) .

  2. Slide impeller on shaft and tighten the impeller screw (669) with its gasket (682) by using Allen key

  3. Fix bearing covers (271) with oil seals to casing cover .

  4. Fix bell mouth (211) on pump casing (107) and tighten diametrically opposite nuts one by one .

  5. Insert casing cover.

  6. Inspect the whole assembly so far mad into pump casing with gasket in proper position .

  7. Mount the top column pipe on casing cover.

  8. Connect pipe nipple.

  9. Make further pipe connection up to nipple.

  10. Connect bend and rising pipe to pump casing.

  11. Lift the unit vertically.

  12. Mount strainer (216) on bell mouth.

  13. Lift the motor stool (290) and fix it to the top column pipe (132) at the same time fix the lubricating pipe.

  14. Fix the support plate (383) on the foundation.

  15. Tighten the foundation bolls of motor stool.

  16. Check the level on the machine surface of motor stool.

  17. Fix the cover for bearing bush alone with oil seal (270-2) , (500.2) to the thrust bearing (247) .

  18. Fit the thrust bearing carrier to motor stool.

Starting of Pump

  1. Chick the direction of rotation of motor before starting.

  2. Open the delivery valve after the pump has been started to operate at its full speed.

  3. Check the vibration and noisy oil level in bearing

  4. Check temperature of bearing

  5. Check the discharge pressure.
