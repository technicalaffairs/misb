Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for motor of this Pump when work on the Pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable Bearing of the Motor of this Pump

  • The Pump must be is Isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, suitable lifting device.

  • Manual Puller, feeler gauge

  • Lessen set screws

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly of the Pump

  1. Remove drain Plugs (3) and drain the fluid from the Pump. Remove Pump from drives.

  2. Remove Bolts (26)  inlet head (2) and gasket (7)
     Note: in Pumps type A6DB – 137 Remove retaining ring (6)
      

  3. Remove the thrust Cage (16), Remove oil balance tube (13), with O - rings (15) from either thrust cage (16) from rotor housing  (11) in two side.

  4. Remove Idler balance Piston housing (10) From Idler rotor (8) and Remove Idler rotor (8).

  5. Remove Bolts (21) and bearing retainer (19).

  6. Remove Bolts (26) and inboard cover (20) From case (1) and remove gasket (7) From case (1).

  7. Remove main shaft (24) with inboard cover (20)

Disassembly Main Shaft (24)

  1. Remove outer truce (18) Press Ball Bearing (23) of main Shaft.

  2. Remove inner truce ring (18) and spacer (22). 

  3. Remove Mechanical seal (12) from main shaft (24)
     Note: in type A3DHB Remove spacer (29) in type A6DB Remove spacer (28).

  4. Remove stop pin (4) and Dyane seal (5) Form case (1).   

  5. Remove Idler housing (11) with O- ring (27) From case (1) 

  6. Remove O- ring (27) From groove of Idler housing (11).

Inspection of the Pump Parts

  1. Check ball bearing (23) and renewed if it needed

  2. Check Mechanical seal (12) and renewed if it needed

  3. Check Idler Balance Piston Housing (10) and renewed    

  4. If it needed Check Idler Housing (11) and renewed if it needed

  5. Check Idler rotor (8) and renewed if it needed

  6. Check main shaft (24) and renewed if it needed

  7. Check oil Balance tube (13) and renewed if it needed

  8. Check thrust Cage (16) and renewed if it needed

  9. Check Idler Balance Piston Housing (10) and renewed if it needed

Assembly of the Pump

  1. Clean every parts well

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After finishing Assembly of the Pump it, mast be rotated by hand.

Trouble Shooting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

1. Loss of Flow

System component malfunction

Inspect all system components and correct malfunction make sure all valve are in their proper position and suction and discharge line are open

 

Incorrect pump rotation

Shut down immediately. Imo Pump are unidirectional and extended reverse rotation can cause damage. Reveres the direction of the driver

 

Loss of prime or Boost pressure

Shut down the pump repine the pump and restart repeat until pump primes. Check boost system for malfunction

 

Air leak in suction

Symptoms are form in the fluid and noise. Reseal and tighten all joints in suction line

 

Low oil level in reservoir

May be accompanied by noise and air in the fluid fill reservoir to proper level be sure intake is for enough below the minimum fluid level to prevent vortex

 

In sufficient inlet

May be accompanied by noise may be caused by high fluid viscosity on cold start. Check suction line for blockage, closed valve or foot valve, clogged strainer. Check for plugged breather in tank

 

Mechanical trouble

Loss coupling, broken shaft, failed pump, May be accompanied by noise or vibration locate and correct cause

2. Noisy or Vibration Pump

Insufficient inlet pressure

May be pump speed excessive

 

Air in fluid

Check for leak in suction line low fluid level vortex at inlet to suction line, or return lines above fluid level

 

Pump not aligned

Realign the pump. Misalignment may also be caused be temperature variation

 

Mechanical problems

Loose or mis-positioned coupling broken shaft, bad bearing

3. Excessive Power Consumption

Pump speed excessive

Reduce pump rotational speed

 

High fluid viscosity

Reduce viscosity by temperature control of fluid

 

High pressure

Checks for blockage of discharge line from the pump

 

Mechanical

Bent shaft, tight pacing pipe strain, failed pump locate and correct cause
