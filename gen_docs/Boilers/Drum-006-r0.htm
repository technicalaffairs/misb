Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified stuff should be carry the suitable safety category .

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc.

  • Close the isolating valves for water and steam this drum level sight glass.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller

  • Torque wrench. .

       

Work to be Carried out

      

Before Isolated

  1. Check any leakage from the level glass valves

  2. Check any leakage from the Mecca (plastic strips).

After isolated mechanically

 

Disassembling Port

  1. Shut gauge valve and gage line valves. Open drain valve.

  2. Determine port cap screws (10) and remove. (Hex wench size 5/16" ).

  3. Remove cover (2) with its spring cone or cones (4), washer (5) and cushion gasket (6) held in place by retaining spring (8). Discard the gasket.

  4. Remove old glass mica-gasket clip ring assembly (7, 11, 9, and 3), held in retaining spring

  5. Clean gasket sealing surface in the gage body by use of a stainless steel wire brush (Yarway part no. 301160) attached to an electric drill. Avoid damaging the seating surface.

  6. Clean contact surfaces of cover, gage bode and washer (5). Remove traces of cushion gasket (6) that may be adhering to washer for cleaning. Inspect condition of spring cones, discard and replace if damaged or corroded.

  7. Apply a light coating of high temperature anti-seize thread compound to cap screw threads. Avoid any build-up of anti-seize compound on all threaded surfaces, internal or external.

Reassembling Port

  1. Place spring cone or cones (4), correctly oriented, into cover.

  2. Place flat washer (5) on top of spring cone or cones.

  3. Reinstall retaining spring (8) Locate in groove in cover.

  4. Place cushion gasket (60 on top of flat washer.

  5. Insert repair port, glass-mica-sealing gasket-clip ring, as an assembly into the retaining spring. The retaining spring holds the repair port in the cover assembly. Be sure sealing gasket is firmly held in place by clip ring. To prevent premature failure, keep moisture from entering between the mica and the glass.

  6. Reinstall cover assembly with vent hole horizontal and away from normal access. Tighten cap screws uniformly using hex wrench (do not use power wrenching) until metal to metal contact is made between cover and body. Further tightening may damage cape caps screws and body.

  7. It is recommended that each port be repaired completely before proceeding with another.

  8. Do not touch glass and mica with fingers or dirty clothes.

  9. It is recommended to change the spring cones (part 4) at every second glass renewal.

Heating Up and Restoring Gage to Service Follow Start up Procedure

New Parts

  1. Replacement parts are furnished as repair port assembly in a package unit of five items of glass (3), mica (9), sealing gasket (11), held together in clip ring (7), plus cushion gasket (6). This assembly insures greater ease of maintenance. Cleanliness of parts is most important, use care to maintain cleanliness.

  2. When ordering parts, identity the gage serial number of figure number in order that correct repair parts are furnished.
