Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,                                                                                

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes.... etc

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding the burners.

  • Shut off compressed air to burner actuators, lighter actuators, and aspirating air connections

  • Shut off instrument air to burner front control equipment

  • The burners must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller,

  • Suitable lifting device, Feeler gauge.

  • Hammer, Dial indicator, and Torque wrench

Work to be carried out

  1. Check the slip seal ring on the front plate of the wall box and welded within ± 3 mm concentricity with the wall tube throat opening.

  2. Check the position of support tubes for each burner inside the wind box using the lifting lugs 

  3. Check the correct position and level of the burner.

  4. Replace rope packing and bend slip seal tabs over to hold the packing in place

  5. Check the welding of seal plates at the wind box casing to the extension flange.

  6. Remove protective wrap, and inspect l-Jet atomizer for each burner

  7. Adjust the end of atomizer to fire position.

  8. Check the fuel flexible hoses of burners for as leakage,
