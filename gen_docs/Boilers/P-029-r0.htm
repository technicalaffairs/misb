Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, suitable lifting device.

  • Manual puller, feeler gauges.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump and driver.

  2. Check the oil level in gearbox or fill it to the maximum height with the recommended grade of lubricating oil.

  3. Check that the mechanical seal circulation pipe (if fitted) is clear and not blocked.

  4. Check that if the suction and discharge pipes have been secured to the pump no strain has been imposed it must be possible to turn the coupling by hand without feeling tight spot.

  5. Check that suction pipe flange joint is air light the entrainment of air must be avoided.

  6. Check the temperature of bearing houses.

  7. Check any noise or vibration in pump.

  8. Check any leakage from the piping connection to the pump.

  9. Check the pressure in the pump delivery.

  10. Check the alignment between pump and motor.

  11. Retighten all bolts connected pump and motor with foundation.

  12. Return back all mechanical and electrical pump positions to the normal by coordinating with shift engineer

  13. Operate the pump and ensure that it is OK and record the readings.

Operation of the Pump

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK and check the direction of rotation, record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration, according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.
