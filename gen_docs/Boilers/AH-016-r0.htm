Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The boiler plant is equipped with two regenerative ljungstrom air heaters.

The ljungstrom air preheater absorbs waste heat from flue gas then transfers this heat to uncoming cold air by means of continuously rotating heat transfer elements of specially formed metal plates.

Thousands of these high efficiency elements are spaced and compactly arranged within sector–shaped compartments of radially divided cylindrical shell called the rotor.

The housing surrounding the rotor is provided with duct connections at both ends and is adequately sealed by radial and circumferential sealing members – forming an air passage through one half of the preheater and a gas passage through one half of the preheater and a gas passage through the other.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the main motor

  • Be sure that the air preheater is stopped completely visually

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set, special tools.

  • Dial indicator.

  • Oil drain pan, Oil can, Feeler gauge.

  • Oil Mobil gear 630.

  • Grease Mobiplex

  • Air motor oil  DTE 629.

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check all drive unit coupling and gears for unusual noise oil leakage excessive vibration, and temperature, so change the defected parts from O-seals, bearings ... etc

  2. Check PH lubrication system for oil leakage, pressure and temperature.

  3. Check MW load, air and gas temperature in and out differential pressure across the air preheater.

  4. Inspect axial radial and circumference seal condition change if required.

  5. Adjust axial, radial and circumferential seal clearances, or change any of the defected seals in outage and readjust the clearance.

  6. Inspect shaft seals and change if required.

  7. Check heat transfer and elements and change if required.

  8. Check soot blowing lance and nozzles and change if required.

  9. Change oil in air preheater – bearing mobil extra hecla super cylinder oil.

  10. Clean oil filter with air and organic substances.

  11. Restart lubricating system and check the air pressure.

  12. Change oil lubrication shaft bearings for Reduction gear – QSV 320

  13. Change oil, clean air filter, top oiler (Vactra heavy medium) for the Air motor.

  14. Check Automatic friction coupling for bushing wear and change if needed.

  15. Check free of oil or grease.

  16. Hydrodynamic coupling check and restart the air preheater.
