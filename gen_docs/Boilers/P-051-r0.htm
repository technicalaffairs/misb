Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly of the Pump

Removing the Screw Spindle Assembly from the Drive end

  1. Withdraw pump half coupling by means of extractor gear.

  2. Remove coupling key from the driving shaft.

  3. Release cap screw and remove cartridge towards the drive end. (Use jacking screws).

  4. Remove circlip on drive spindle.

  5. Remove bearing cover by means of jacking screws. Ball bearing and stationary seal ring remain the bearing cover.

  6. After removal of the complete bearing cover .the stationary seal ring can be easily dismounted.

  7. Remove circlip from bearing cover.

  8. The ball bearing and the grease guide ring of design “EG” are removed from the bearing cover inside by means of a doily.

  9. Now the mechanical seal parts fitted on the drive spindle are stripped off with particular care release cap screw . Remove drive end pump cover from casing insert.

  10. Release cap screw, remove drive and pump cover from casing insert.

  11. Dismount complete screw – spindle assembly consisting of drive spindle with shrunk on shaft collar the two idler spindles  and the balancing bushes fitted thereon by pulling it out of the casing insert  towards the drive end. Remove idler spindles and balancing bushes from the drive spindle.

Assembly of the Pump

  1. Before assembling the pump. Care must be taken that all components are free from dirt and abrasive particles. To re-assemble the pump, proceed in reverse order than described for the disassembly.

  2. During assembly and installation of the screw spindle assembly care must be taken that the color coding of the three spindles are in one direction. The color coded drive spindle tooth must engage in the color coded tooth gaps of the idler spindling before the installation. The screw spindle assembly must be well oiled with acid-free oil.

  3. When mounting the mechanical seal. Special care must be taken that for cleaning the components only clean rags and no cotton wool is used and that seal faces are not damaged. The seal rings must never be placed on the seal faces, the seal faces must be clean and should poor to assembly, be slightly oiled with clean oil. Prior to fitting on the drive spindle. Slightly oil shaft and attend to proper sequence of components

  4. Assemble the remaining parts of the pump in the reverse order of the disassembly.

Return Pump into Service

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK, check the direction of rotation, record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration, according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.
