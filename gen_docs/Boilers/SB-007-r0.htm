Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed on an annually bases indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

The soot blowers used to clean Long Retractable, Multi-Nozzles Blower, Ljungstrom

Technical Specification

No of Soot blowers

12 for Long Retractable Blower

16 for Multi-Nozzles Blower

 2 for Ljungstrom (air heater)

Pressure of Steam

  35 Bar

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this soot blower.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of the soot blower.

  • The soot blower must be isolated mechanically by closing the main valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller

Work to be Carried Out

The Carriage

  1. Isolate the installation (from steam and electricity)

  2. Remove the upper cover plates.

  3. Remove the tipping over stud from the valves opening yoke (mk.5, docket N004).

  4. Remove the flange of the feed tube ( mk 33 docket N004)

  5. Slightly move the feed tube forward to clear it from the valve assembly ( take care of rings mk 29 docket N004).

  6. Remove the lower housing of the carriage (mk.10 docket N004) while holding the lance in place.

  7. Slightly lower the lance and clear it and the feed tube from the wall crossing tube.

  8. Remove the front support of square tube ( mk.69 docket N004).

  9. Remove the rear bolt of the square tube (mk.37 docket N004).

  10. Remove the four screws (mk.12 docket N004) from the chains fastening.

  11. Actuate the puller to lower the assembly until the bushes ( mk.48 and 59, docket N004) and their bearings.

  12. Remove the four bolts ( mks 7,8,9, docket N004) attaching the carriage to the cross pieces.

  13. Remove the carriages upper housing (mk.10 docket N004) the Lancel carriage input tube assembly can now be lowered from the blower.

  14. After finishing disassembly of the carriage, clean all parts, change packing, gears chain and change oil gear box.

  15. Assemble the parts of the carriage in the reverse order of the disassembly.

  16. Check the reversible limit switch.

Steam Valve

  1. Cut off the steam supply.

  2. Remove recall spring ( mk.28, docket 550 Noo1).

  3. Remove upper pin ( mk.22, docket 550 N001) to this effect remove circlip ( mk.24, docket 550 N001)

  4. Remove the feed tube’s flange ( mk.33, docket N004).

  5. Remove the studs tightening the valve unit against the piping counter flange.

  6. Remove the nuts attaching the valve unit to the rear plate ( mk.14, docket 550 N001)

  7. Check the valve unit and change any defected parts. Figure1.

  8. Change the packing thrust flange ( mk.5, docket 550 N001)

  9. Remove valve (mk.6,12, docket 550 N001) by the bottom.

  10. Change the old packings.

  11. Grind the valve and its set.

  12. Clean and grease the packing housing.

  13. Assemble the packing thrust flange and the valve unit

  14. A play of about 0.5 mm must remain between the valve stem and the tappet.
