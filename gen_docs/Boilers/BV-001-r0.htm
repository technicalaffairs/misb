Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1 for the boiler valves, which must be carried out when the gas turbine have minor maintenance after 4000 workhour.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mamdooh ElSayed Ghazy, tested by eng. Abdel Qader Fath Allah at 19 Jan 2003 on Boiler no. 1 and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gilel, accepted by general director eng. Sobhy Mohamed El Bakry and approved by eng. Moneer Mohamed El Wakeel Head Sector of Demietta PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • HRSG must be out of service.

  • Depressurized if need or isolate if possible

  • Drain line if need

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Spanner & gasket & spacer ring

  • Driver & alin keys & cleaning liquid

  • Blanco & anti seize

Work to be Carried Out

Gate Valves         There are size from ( 2” to 24”)   VELAN-Canada

  1. Clean from foreign material

  2. Lap the gate wear or cracks or change the defect gate.

  3. Lap the seat wear or change the defect seat if possible.

  4. Make machining for stem or replace the defect.

  5. Retighten for gasket leakage or replace the defect

  6. Retighten the gland if leakage or replace

Globe Valves        There are size from ( 1/2” to 24”)      NUOVO PIGNONE-ITALY

  1. Clean from foreign material

  2. Lap the plug wear or cracks or change the defect plug.

  3. Lap the seat wear or change the defect seat if possible.

  4. Make machining for stem or replace the defect.

  5. Retighten for gasket leakage or replace the defect.

  6. Retighten the gland if leakage or replace.

Ball Valves        There are size from ( 1/2” to 14”)       VELAN-Canada

  1. Clean from foreign material

  2. Lap the plug wear or cracks or change the defect ball.

  3. Lap the seat wear or change the defect seat if possible

  4. Make machining for stem or replace the defect

  5. Retighten for gasket leakage or replace the defect

  6. Retighten the gland if leakage or replace.

Check Valves        There are size from ( 1/2” to 24”)    VELAN-Canada

  1. Clean from foreign material

  2. Lapping the disc wear or cracks or change the defect.

  3. Lapping the seat wear or change the defect seat if possible

  4. Check the pin replace if defect

  5. Retighten for gasket leakage or replace the defect

Safety Valves        Safety valves of {ECO, DRUM, SUPER .}      DESCO-ITALY

  1. Measure the adjust span before opening.

  2. Clean from foreign material.

  3. Check the spring – replace the defect.

  4. Lap the plug wear or cracks or change the defect plug.

  5. Lap the seat wear or change the defect seat.

  6. Make machining for stem or replace the defect.

  7. Retighten for gasket leakage or replace the defect.

Butterfly  Valves                VELAN-Canada


Note:
Generally used in low pressure system application where the leakage is relatively unimportant, normally used in large diameter lines  

  1. Clean from foreign material.

  2. Lap the disc wear or cracks or change the defect disc.

  3. Change the bearing if it bad.

  4. Make machining for stem or replace the defect.

  5. Replace the pin if it have wear.

  6. Retighten the gland if leakage or replace.
