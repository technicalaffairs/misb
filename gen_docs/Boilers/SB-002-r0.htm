Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every two years as  indicated by Y2 in the above title block.

 

The soot blower used for cleaning superheater, reheater, economizer tubes, and regenerative air heater baskets.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses,... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this soot blower

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this soot blower

  • The soot blower must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller,

  • Suitable lifting device, feeler gauge

  • Hammer, and air wrench.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly

  1. Remove four cap screws from the top cover.

  2. Remove the top cover assembly.

  3. Remove rollers and drive pinions to check for wear or cracks change if needed.

  4. Remove the retaining ring, oil seal and change them, and check bearing and housing is OK or not.

  5. Remove the packing and the solid gland and change them.

  6. Remove the poppet valve to check all inside components and turn on.

  7. Remove the lance and tubes of steam and air, check for cracks

  8. Remove carriage rollers check for wear, change if needed

  9. Check and grease the chain if necessary at figure 1

  10. Support the carriage by means of a hoist through the access hole in the top of the beam Remove the two short carriage support angles at the rear of the beam and lower the carriage

Carriage Reassembly

The carriage should be reassembled in the reverse order of disassembly. This order of assembly will eliminate interference problems with each previously assembled group of parts see figure 1.
