Introduction

This document lists the major maintenance procedure P2, of vertical rotary regenerative air heater, which must be, performed every annually base as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Adel Mahmoud Al-maadawi, reviewed by Maintenance director eng. Saad Mostafa, revised by eng. Samir Abdel-Gelil at EDEPCo., accepted by maintenance general director: Ali Mostafa Al-lithy and approved by Eng.: Mostafa Alam Head  Sector of TALKHA steam power station 2 x 210 MW.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  •  Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this regenerative air heater.

  •  Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this regenerative air heater.

  •  Be sure the oil pump is stopped.

  • Be sure the cooling water of the upper bearing is closed.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.
  • Cleaning fluid, Feeler gauge, anti-dust  mask

Work to be Carried Out

Adjustment of rotor sealing

Basic sealing adjustment in cold state

During the preparation of the regenerative air heater for the first starting and after excessive repairs, the sealing should be adjusted in cold state according to table 1. This basic adjustment screws the assumed clearance of the sealing elements for the hot operation of the heaters. The sealing is adjusted in cold state according to schematic sketches with the heater shut down and the drive secured – disconnected from the electrical network.

 

Adjustment of sector boards                                                                        

The sector boards are adjusted in setting the clearances A,B,G and F according to table no. 1 and to Fig.1

Adjustment of axial boards                                                                                                        

The axial boards are basically adjusted in setting. The clearances” C” and “E” According to table 1 and Fig 2

Adjustment of peripheral sealing and sector board sealing

The peripheral sealing are accessible from the flue gas channels and air channels of regenerative air heater only . their clearances “H” and “J”/ table 1, can be adjusted in partial bending the sealing sheets and checking them with a feeler gauge such adjustment may be done on a shut down heater. The same is true for the sector board sealing . For adjustment that sealing see the fig No.3 .

Adjustment of clearance in hot state

  • When setting the regenerative air heater into hot state you should cautiously its run and function to prevent the sealing from damage or destroying and/or the rotor from jamming due to thermal dilatation of its parts, which could cause a crash of the drive or motor. Above all the motor ammeter and its loading should be checked. For the purpose it is recommended to connect a precise and sensitive checking ammeter easy to inspect from the service place. the basic current consumption will be established during the cold run of the regenerative air heater after adjusting the clearances to table 1.

  • The check of the basic current consumption for the drive of the regeneration air heater under cold run should be done twice a year at last – namely, it could be partly changed due to running in the plant and to other operating influences.

  • The clearances of the sector and axial boards will be adjusted by means of elements as late as under full operation of the heater for the optimum heater tightness to be maintained, it is recommended to adjust all clearance to minimum values, to such however, that prevent the boards from wearing through rubbing by the rotor rotation. The preset clearance of sector board ends and the axial boards can be observed visually through the door on the axial sealing branch.

Attention: There is a danger of hot flue gas or air escape during such inspection, therefor a face shield or a gas mask should be used and the hands should be protected with gloves.

 

 

Instructions for clearance adjustment in hot state

  • The adjust of clearance of sector and axial boards under full operation – i.e. in hot state of the regenerative air heater must be done so as to prevent the extraction flow increase of the drive electric motor towards the value established under cold run. However the clearance should be reduced to such value to secure the combustion air leakage as low as possible and to secure full safety and perfectness of the whole plant .

  • In case of crack of the regenerative air heater –rotor deformation, fire, e.t.c – The rotor dimensions may change due to uneven thermal extension to such measure that it causes excessive friction between the rotor and sealing. It manifests it self in excessive and unnatural sounds in the area of axial and sector boards . In such cases the regenerative air heater should be shut down and disconnected from the flue gas and air channels in closing the respective valves, then the cause of the failure should be found out and removed .

Disassembly of guide bearing  figure 5

The process of disassembly of the upper – guide bearing for its revision, maintenance or exchange is following:

  1. Disconnect the drive motor from the net work and secure it against an uncompetented start.

  2. Support the rotor in three points at least so as to prevent it from banking after the guide bearing disassembly.

  3. Remove the guide bearing axlebox cover (1)

  4. Discharge all oil from the guide bearing axlebox in out bolting the plug (6) of the discharge hole .

  5. Dismantle the covering board (2) and the distance tube (5).

  6. Pull down the keyed on bush (4) from the pin (3) together with the bearing (7) by means of a pull down jig.

  7. In exchanging the guide bearing you should pull down the old bearing from the bush and to fit on a new one on it. Leave the new bearing in the old packing up to the assembly start. Before fitting it on the bush (4) heat the bearing to 70 ºC in the oil bath.

  8. For reassemble apply the converse process observe a perfect cleanness and check the accuracy of the assembly.

  9. During the re-assembly on the pin of the rotor exchange the sealing cord in the sealing (8)

  10. The assembly being finished remove the rotor supports and charge the axlebox of the guide bearing with lubricating oil .

  11. Before putting the regenerative air heater into operation, find out if the rotor supports have been removed after the assembly finished and if the oil charge of the axlebox has been restored.

Disassembly of step thrust bearing   (figure 6)

Two hydraulic jacks for lifting the rotor from the bearing are delivered with regenerative air heaters as their accessories for the disassembly of the thrust bearing the process of disassembly of the step thrust bearing for its inspection, maintenance or exchange is following:

  1. Disconnect the drive motor from the network and secure it against incompetent starting.

  2. Discharge oil from the bearing.

  3. Disassemble all pipe connections, axlebox cover and thermometers.

  4. Insert the hydraulic jacks (10) under the axlebox .

  5. Lift the rotor 4 –5 mm with both jacks at the same time.

  6. Insert distance pieces (2) between the lightening ring of the axle (1) and the rotor support (3).

  7. Remove the support plates (11) under the carrying bearing and force out the tapered pins (9) from the board supporting the axlebox in horizontal position.

  8. Relieve both jacks (10).

  9. Dismount the intermediate flange (6) from the shaft flange (1).

  10. Shift out the axlebox including the bearing in the direction between the air channel and flue gas channel on a suitable support plate.

  11. For the bearing maintenance it is sufficient to remove its upper part with the rollers and with the roller carriers.

  12. Apply removers for the bearing exchange.

  13. Apply the converse process for re-assembly. Observe perfect cleanness and check the accuracy of the assembly.

  14. Before putting into operation find out if the supporting plastes (2) and the jacks have been removed and if the bearing has been charged with oil. Exchange the packing cord in the sealing (12).

  15. When exchanging the bearing , clean and wash the axlebox , too , with flushing oil . Fill the sealing (4) with grease.

Exchange of heating elements

    Heating elements of the rotor hot end

  1. Disconnect the drive motor from the network and secure it against incompetent starting.

  2. Open the hole in the feed socket for flue gas and set the rotor through hand turning so as to place the suitable        section of heating elements under the hole dismantle the radial sealing .

  3. Remove gradually the basket with the old heating elements by lifting mechanism installed in the groove over the heater. Having removed them check and if necessary , repair the supporting partitions and drop down the baskets with new elements by the lifting mechanism .

  4. Having exchanged the elements in one section turn the rotor partly so as to position the next section and go on exchanging them.

  5. Having finished the exchange of the heating elements of the whole hot end of the rotor , mount on the radial sealing . Seal and close the hole in the flue gas feed channel.

Heating Elements of the Rotor Cold End

  1. Disconnect the drive motor from the network and secure it against incompetent starting .

  2. Open the hole in the stator housing and set each rotor sector with the hole against it by hand turnin .

  3. Remove the door on the hole of the respective sector shift out the baskets with the old heating elements inspect and if necessary , repair the supporting grillage and shift in the baskets with the new elements.

  4. Put on the door and turn th rotor by hand with the next sector to the hole and go on exchanging.

  5. Having finished the exchange of the elements of the whole rotor cold end , close and seal the hole in the stator housing .

Attention: For the work inside the rotor – both on the hot end and on the cold end – use the assembly lamp with 24 v connecting voltage. Work with anti-dust  mask!

Exchange of toothed wheel or the drive  figure 7                                       

The driving toothed wheel (1) of the rotor or the regenerative air heater with worn or damaged teeth must be exchanged. The process is following:

  1. Disconnect the drive motor from the network and secure it against incompetent starting .

  2. Dismantle the rotor drive including the bracket from the stator housing with the use of a pulley block.

  3. Draw off the toothed wheel from the gearbox shaft with the use of a draw – off jig.

  4. Clean the shaft end, lubricate it with graphite and fit on a new toothed wheel on it. Apply slip-on flanges and screws screwed in the tapped hole in the shaft face .

  5. Secure the toothed wheel on the shaft with a washer and screws.

  6. Mount the drive with the bracket back on the stator housing so as to enable the toothed wheel meshing into the rotor pin gearing. The clearance between the toothed wheel foot and the pin of the rotor pin gearing should be 15.5 mm in cold state. Adjust the clearance by rotor hand turning according to figure 7 along the whole circumference of the pin gearing it should be checked by means of a gauge in several spots of the circumference of rotor pin gearing , the rim of the pin gearing is not accurately circular.

  7. Check the oil charge of the gearbox ; If necessary refill of exchange to the oil .

  8. Having finished the drive assembly , checked the oil charge and adjusted the clearance “x” connect the drive electric motor and make a check under cold rum of the regenerative air heater.

Maintenance of blowing and washing device    figure 4         

Following maintenance should be done on the blowing and washing devices of the regenerative air heater:

  1. Check regularly if both the steam and water closing fittings are tight and if the jets of the blowing and washings arms are not attacked by corrosion or clogged.

  2. If you find out that the fittings leak , repair or exchange them immediately , because the leaking steam or water are sources of corrosion damaging the rotor heating elements .

  3. The dirty or corroded jets must be cleaned , the damaged and worn ones exchanged.

  4. Check the terminal switches limiting the swings of the blowing arms.

  5. Check the tightness of the blower joints and of all their motion parts.

  6. Check regularly the oil state in blower gearboxes and if necessary , refill for exchange the oil

  7. Check and lubricate the motion screw bearings, the motion screw and the nut regularly, do that in harmony.

Fire extinguisher equipment

  1. Check the fire extinguisher pipe and prevent any leakage if found.

  2. Check the fire extinguisher jet and clean it if clogged.

  3. Exchange any part if needed.

  4. Operate the system manually and to tested it .

Lubrication

In General

  • It is necessary to lubricate all rotating parts of the regenerative air heater . It works under hot operating conditions, which requires special caution in selecting suitable lubricants.

  •  The maintenance worker should be entrusted with regular lubrication, being responsible for checking all lubrication spots. He should have a lubrication chart at disposal with lubricating instructions for the regenerative air heater.

  • The lubrication chart should include the quality of the lubricants, time interval of the checks and the lubricant exchange in harmony with the lubrication chart. If the lubricant of required quality is not on store , it is necessary to settle on a suitable substitute lubricant with the special technical service of the supplier.

  • The lubricants must be refilled and exchanged according to the lubrication charge may not be admitted no more is desirable super charging the bearings with grease and mixing various kinds or lubricants.

Instructions for lubricant exchange

  1. Before the first filling or the bearings or gearboxs with a lubricating charge / grease or oil / all parts and surfaces being in contact with the lubricant must be cleaned orderly having cleaned the surfaces , wash them with kerosene or with special flushing oil.

  2. Don't use cloth or rage releasing fibers for cleaning the bearings and sliding surfaces during the bearing assembly observe the possibly highest cleanness – leave the bearings in the original packing till using them for the assembly.

  3. Apply always the lubricants of the same quality for the sieve lubricating spot ! if it is necessary to apply some other lubricant quality to be settled with special technical service of the lubricant supplier remove first all residues of original lubricant , and wash the whole lubricating space orderly.

 Trouble Shooting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

 Remedy

1. High pressure loss

(The air pressure indicator of the outlet of the regenerative air heater shows a low value )

 

-Considerable sediments on the heating elements.

-Blow the regenerative air heater with steam blowers.

When the blowing is not efficient, put the air heater out of operation and wash it with water.

-Big clearance of sector boards and sealings causing air leakage into the rotor flue gas part.

-Check the clearances of sector boards and sealings and if necessary to adjust so as to decreases the air leakage.

-Sealing sheets worn or broken

-Exchange the defective sealing sheets for new ones.

  2.  Low CO2 content in flue gas.

(There is a great difference between the CO2 contents before and after the heater)

-Great clearance of sector boards and axial boards and air leakage into flue gas.

-Check the clearance adjustments and the state of sector and axial boards and to remove the faults discovered

Delivery slide valve is closed.

-Open the slide valve.

  3.  Failure of drive . 

- Fall – out loss of supply net work. 

- Failure in feeder supply – feed cable interrupted or fuse melted.

 

-Discover the cause of drive failure as soon as possible to try to remove it in possibly shortest time and put the heater into normal operation

-If the failure cannot be removed and normal operation of the heater reset into operation in 5 minutes, the steam boiler or some other power plant should be put out of operation.

-Check all electrical connection.
 

- Mechanical failure on the drive – failure of electric motor , gearbox , connection between rotor and gearbox or between gearbox and toothed wheel , failure of toothed wheel or pin gearing, slackening of drive anchorage on the bracket and on the cladding .

-Align the suction piping and ensure uniform gradient.
   

-The rotor must be maintained in rotary movement by permanent or interrupted manual turning so long till the inner temperature sinks to 150-100 °C.

-In case the axial boards or the sector boards start rubbing against the rotor, the clearances must be increased .

-The failure found on the drive being removed to set the air heater into normal operation

4.Temperature rises of step thrust bearing and guide bearing.

(The remote thermometer indicator for oil temperature in the step thrust bearing indicates higher temperature than 80 °C signaled both acoustically and visually)

 

- A defect in the thermometer

-Check the function of the remote thermometer.

-Insufficient oil charge in the bearing

-Check the amount of the oil charge in the axlebox – to refill, if necessary.

- Axlebox leakage.

-Check the tightness of the axlebox – to remove possible failures.

-A failure in the cooling system

-Check, if sufficient volume of cooling water or cooling and lubricating oil passes through the cooling system .

- Bearing failure.

-Exchange the bearing.
