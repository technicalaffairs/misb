Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every 8000 equivalent operating hours EOH as indicated in the title block

The air filters filler the air of compressor form dust.

Technical data

144 Cartridges,  24''×24''x12"

Pressure drop

190 Pa to 650 Pa

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks to surround the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs ... etc.

  • Turbine must be shut down

 Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid ( WD 40 )

  • Rags

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Loosen the thumb screw, and rotate the latch.

  2. Remove the Renu prefilter  frame, don't discard.

  3. Remove the cartridge filter.

  4. Insert clean Duracel with air flow arrows and pleats in the proper orientation.

  5. Install the Renu prefilter frame, rotate the latches into position and tighten the thumb screw.
