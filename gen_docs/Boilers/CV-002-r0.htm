Introduction

This document outlines  the equipment inspection I1 which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The flow control valves and and pressure control valves types and function as follows:

Valve

Type

Function

Flow Control Valve

667 (70) ET

(one valve per unit) is to minimize flow valve low capacity used during start up the unit

Flow Control Valve

Flow Control Valve

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate air instrument

  • Purging lines by nitrogen and confirm the vents is hydrocarbon free

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect  internal parts (plug, cage, piston rings, seats) and replace damage parts if there is high wear or cracks in this parts.

  2. Packing inspection and change if needed.

  3. Stroke the V/V with IMD by readjusting the stroke from 0% to 25% to 50% to 100%

  4. Re-grease the isolation V/Vs and check the gear box from wear or cracks and change the defected parts.
