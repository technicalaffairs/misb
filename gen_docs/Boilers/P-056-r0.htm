Introduction

This document out lines the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block

The pump function: transfer the fuel oil (Mazout & Solar) to fuel oil storage tanks and can be recirculated from one Mazout oil storage tank similary solar oil.

Technical Specifications

Discharge Pressure

5 Bar

Capacity

80 M3/Hr

Motor Power

27 KW

Full load speed

1450 RPM
Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipments

  • Two complete maintenance tools sets

  • Cleaning fluid, manual pullers

  • Straight edge, Rages

  • Small wire brush, Filter gauge.

  • Chain, lifting hock, dial gauge

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Mark should be punch on pump

  2. Empty the gear casing and take of gear casing (19) after having unscrewed lock nut.

  3. Remove hex. Screw of gear (114).

  4. Pull of gear (54,55) remove keys and bushes.

  5. Remove hex. Nut screw (83), washer (84) from the casing on run drive end side bearing housing (6).

  6. Take of the bearing housing ( NDE ) from the pump casing together with roller casing.

  7. Press the roller out of bearing casing (6).

  8. Take out stationary carbon ring of mech. Seal and take out mech. Seal.

  9. Take out the roller bearing from the casing.

  10. Pull the coupling half from drive shaft (30).

  11. Remove the bearing cover (9).

  12. Check mech. Seal surfaces for wear .

Inspection

  1. Inspect the bearings and replace them if necessary.

  2. Replace the worn out parts.

  3. Check all parts for any worn out.

  4. Inspect the run out of shaft.

  5. Change mechanical seal surfaces for wear.

Assembly

  1. Insert two shafts inside pump casing.

  2. Put back moving mech. Seal on the shaft.

  3. Put back mech. Seal cover including stationary carbon ring.

  4. Push back bearing.

  5. Mount bearing cover.

  6. Mount the two gear.

  7. Put back gear cover.

  8. Mount the coupling.

  9. Adjust alignment between pump and elec. Motor.

  10. Fix the two halves of the coupling.

  11. Open the heating valves.

Starting of Pump

  1. Chick the direction of rotation of motor before starting.

  2. Open the inlet and outlet valves of mech. Seal the cooling water.

  3. Open the delivery valve after the pump has been started to operate at its full speed.

During Operation

  1. Check the vibration and noisy oil level in bearing

  2. Check oil level in bearing

  3. Check temperature of cooling water.

  4. Check the discharge pressure.
