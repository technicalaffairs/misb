Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The pumps function: transferring Mazout from tank to gun and transfer solar from tank to igniter.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for motor of this pump when work on the Pump.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Feeler gauge, cleaning fluid.

  • Dial indicator, grease.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the direction of the pump.

  2. Be sure there is no any abnormal noise or vibration and prevent it if  found

  3. Be sure there is no any leakage from any gasket or any O-ring and prevent it if found.

  4. Check pressure in delivery of the pump.

  5. Check quantity delivered of the pump.

  6. Check alignment of the pump according to the following table:

    Lube   Wt  (l b)

    0.19

    Operating Alignment Limits (inch)

    < 0.005"

    Gap of coupling  (inch)

    0.125"

    Cover Bolt Torque  (1b – in)

    200

  7. Should be change lubrication of coupling (WT= 0.19LB)

  8. Cheek the Temperature of Bearing house:
         - Temperature in type H less than   250     5F
         - Temperature in type B less than   160     5F

Before Isolated the Pump Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check any noise or vibration in pump.

  2. Check the direction of the motor rotation

  3. Check leakage from any gasket or any O-ring

  4. Check pressure in delivery of the pump's delivery

  5. Check the temperature of bearing house it must be less than 250 5F in type H and in type B less then 160 5F

After Isolating the Pump Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check  the impeller and casing from mechanical wear and measure the clearances by dismantle the Pump.

  2. Check the bearing in the pump.

  3. Prevent the leakage from gasket or O-ring by renewed it.

  4. Check the coupling's pins and change grease of coupling.

  5. Retighten all bolts connected pump and motor with foundation.

  6. Check the alignment between pump and motor.

Operation of the Pump

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK and record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration,  according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.
