Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the fan motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of fan motor

  • The fan must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery damper

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller,

  • Feeler gauge, dial indicator

  • Vibrometer straight edge

Work to be Carried Out

Dismantling

  1. Disconnect the electric motors.

  2. Remove all the different guards.

  3. Uncouple the electric motor.

  4. Remove all the auxiliary equipments installed on the bearings pyrometric probes.

  5. Discharge the bearings of their oil.

  6. Break away the shaft tighness from the intel vane control.

  7. Disconnect the shaped inlets from the inlet vane control.

  8. Remove the upper part of the fan casing and the 2 inlet vane control upper.

  9. Sling the rotor

    REMARK : Normally all the bearing compounds are marked in order to avoid any mistake during the reassembly.

  10. Unscrew the two side parts of each bearing taking care to not damage the gaskets.

  11. Remove the upper part of each bearing taking care to not damage the gaskets.

  12. Sling up the rotor and put it down on the maintenance area.

    REMARK: We advise to put the rotor on wooden made supports in order to avoid to damage the shaft or the impeller.

  13. Remove the half coupling from the shaft.

  14. Remove the backstop from the shaft.

  15. Remove the central bolt of each bearing

  16. Slide the outer side of each bearing to the shaft tip.

  17. Slide the outer spacers to the shaft tips.

  18. Remove the bearing using a hub puller.

    REMARK: an already used rolling bearing can not be reused after dismantling.

Reassembly of a New Roller Bearings

  1. Check that the shaft has no damage

  2. Install the rolling bearings on the shaft as indicated hereunder :

  3. The rolling bearings are installed tightened on the shaft.

  4. Check that rolling bearing have no foreign substances, in this case, clean them white spirit or petrol

  5. Heat the rolling bearing in an oil bath at a temperature of 100 ºC to 120 ºC without exceeding 120 ºC

  6. Check that the oil rings are well installed on the outer spacers stayed in place

  7. Slid the rolling bearing on the shaft and lock them against the inner spacers

  8. Assemble the brake washers

  9. Screw the central bolts and brake washers

  10. Assemble the half coupling

  11. Sling the rotor

  12. Sling up the rotor and install it on its place on the casing

  13. Assemble the upper parts of bearing  housings

  14. Fill the bearing with oil .

  15. Assemble the remaining parts of the fan in the reverse order of the disassembly
