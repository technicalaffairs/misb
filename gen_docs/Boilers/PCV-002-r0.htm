Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection  I1, which must be prerformed every year as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The function of the V/Vs is reducing station for fuel gas which receiving from gas company by press. = 7 bar to 4 bar working parallel durning normal operation

The first draft initiated by eng. Ali El-Safoury, tested  by eng. Mohamed Anwar Ghazy, approved by eng. Gamal El-safoury, accepted by eng. Mohamed El-Tahawy Head sector of Shoubra El-Kheimah P.S., certified by eng Nageh Soliman Shousha Inspection and Quality Control Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate air instrument

  • Purging lines by nitrogen and confirm the vents is hydrocarbon free

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set, grease no: Mobilplex 47, packing φ= 19 mm

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect the  internal parts (plug, cage, piston rings, seats) for wear, damage, erosion.....etc

  2. Repack the valve

  3. Stroke the V/V with IMD (instrument mait – department)

  4. Re-grease the isolation V/Vs and check the gear box
