Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Use safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

  • Close the Mazout valves from outlet and inlet filters.

  • Open the Mazout valve vent.

  • Clean the filter vessel.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, gasket

  • Complete inside sets of new filters.

 

Work to be Carried out

  1. When the differential pressure drop across the filter reaches the predetermined differential (5 PSI suggested), replace the filter after taken out of service and the individual element replaced,

  2. Mount the elements in the vessel on supports and held in place by wing nuts. (it is not necessary to remove the supports to change the elements). In general, cleaning of fibrous filter elements is not considered practicable.

  3. Replace the elements, by removing the wing nuts by turning counter clockwise, and after removing them, with drawing the element.

  4. Reverse the procedure to install the new element.

  5. Always compare the old and news elements to ascertain that the proper replacement is being made.
