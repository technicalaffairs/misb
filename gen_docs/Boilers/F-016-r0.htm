Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection, which must be performed depending on ∆P as indicated in the above title block.

The function of filter is cleaning mazout oil from impurities before pumps and burners.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety Helmet, safety shoes, ...etc.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. The duplex filter show in fig "1" has two sections. One in service & the other at standby.

  2. The hand "2 is used to change over between the two sections.

Dismantle the Filter Strainers insert from Filter Body

  1. Turn of hand "2" in fig 1 to isolate mazout inlet and mazout outlet to the filter.

  2. Close steam and condensate valves 6,7.

  3. Open vent valve 5.

  4. Open drain valve 8.

  5. Screw off nuts 4 to remove filter cover 3.

  6. Take out the filter insert from the hand 9.

Disassembly of the Filter Strainers

  1. Clean the outside surface of the strainers by using steam or clean solar.

  2. Screw off nuts 14 to separate the inside and outside strainers 13,12 from upper and lower flanges 10,11.

  3. Clean the outside and inside strainers and all parts by clean fluid.

Inspection

  1. Check upper flange seals and lower flange seals and replace the damage seal

  2. Check the gasket between the filter cover 3 and filter flange 15 and renewed it if needed.

Assembly the Filter Strainers insert inside the Filter Body

  1. Put the inside and outside strainers between the upper and lower flange and tighten the nuts (14)

  2. Put the filter strainers inside the filter body.

  3. Tight the nuts 4 in cross sequence.

Filter Operation

  1. Close the drain valve 8.

  2. Open steam and condensate valves 6,7.

  3. Open a balance pressure valve between two sections up to the mazout come out the vent pipe then close vent valve 5

  4. Turn off  hand 2 to put this filter section in service and isolate the other section by coordination with operation shift eng.

  5. Check ∆P on this filter.

  6. You can put this filter section in service after checking the ∆P on this filter and Return the work permit by coordination with operation shift engineer.
