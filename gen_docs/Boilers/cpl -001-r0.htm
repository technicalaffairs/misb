Introduction

This document out lines the required procedures for Alignment of any coupling size procedure.

The first draft initiated by eng. Hesham M. Hassan, reviewed by director eng. Saad Mostafa, and revised by quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gelil at EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Ali Al-Lithy and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam: Head Sector of Talkha Steam Power Station 2x210MW

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • The equipment, which needed alignment, must be isolated mechanically and electrically.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Steel ruler, Feeler gauge, Venire calipers

 

Work to be Carried Out


 

Before the work starts it is important to do the following steps

  1. Make sure that the driver of equipment is loosen.

  2. Make sure that the driver of equipment is lower than the main equipment.

  3. Make sure that the foundation is clean and the main driver of equipment brackets is clean.

  4. Adjust the driver of equipment with the equipment by eye-sight and place shims under the driver of equipment brackets.

 

Important

  1. Three things shall be corrected : height, side alignment and angle.

  2. Move the driver of equipment toward the equipment to position where the lower part of the coupling halves has half the recommended distance. If the recommended distance between the two halves is 4 mm then the distance should be 2 mm.

  3. Measure the distance between the vertical line from the coupling half on the equipment to the center of both driver of equipment brackets .(F1&F2) as in figure 1

  4. Place steel ruler on top of the coupling and measure between the ruler and the equipment’s driver couplings highest point by using feeler gauge (y).

  5. Measure the distance between couplings at the top (A).

  6. Turn both coupling halves 180° and measure the distance between them at the bottom (B).

For example
     

           If        A =2.75 mm                     B =2.15
                     F1=275mm                      F2=475mm
                     Y =0.3mm
                   Coupling diameter             ( D )                =130    mm

- First calculate      X =  A – B =  2.75 -  2.15  =  0.6  mm

           Then calculate        V =   X  / D= 0.6  /  130      =0.00462

- The thickness of shims  S1

                            S1=Y + ( V  ×F1 ) = 0.3 + ( 0.00462 × 275 )
                                = 1.57                                      mm
- The thickness of shims S2
                                                         S2 = Y + ( V × F2 )
                                                              = 0.3 + ( 0.00462 × 475 )
                                                              =  2.49         mm
- Then the shims under the brackets is :
                             S1(shim under front bracket ) =  1.57     mm
                             S2 (shim under back bracket) =  2.49    mm
 

 

Important:    Use few shims as possible

  1. After shims is in position adjust the side misalignment by using steel ruler.

  2. Keep the ruler on one side of the coupling halves.

  3. If there is gap between the ruler and the coupling .You must move the equipment’s driver sideways until the gap disappears.

  4. Use feeler gauges and measures the gap and it must be the same.

  5. The equipment’s drive brackets has to be tightened before these measurements are done.
