Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

    •

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and .... etc

  • The filter must be Isolated mechanically by :

  • Closing  Input and output valves of  Mazout 

  • Closing Steam and condensate valve .

  • Drain the filter by opening the sidement valve

  • Deaerated filter by opening deaeration valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance set

  • Special fixture for pulling off the strainer.

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags etc).

    •

Work to be Carried Out

 

Before Isolating the filter mechanically  

  1. Check isolate the selected chamber from the few thus fully sealing off the basket to be clean.

  2. Check the handle (8) hard against the drop nose bolt (5)

  3. Check closing equalizing pressure valve.

  4. Check  opening the drain of the filter.

  5. Check  opening the vent of the filter.

Disassembly of the Filter

  1. Remove the drop nose bolt (5) and the bridge (3).

  2. Lift the cover (2) from the filter body (1).

  3. Remove the basket (12) by basket handle.

  4. Place a clean rag or other material over the filter to prevent ingress of dust or dirt during the basket cleaning.

  5. The basket should be washed down using suitable solvent and immersing in a large tank then dry by air.

  6. Remove the O ring seal (10) from the body and clean it.

Clean and Repair Filter insert Past

  1. Examine the basket (12) and mechanical seal and renew it if needed

  2. Check the basket O ring seal (14) and renew it if needed

  3. Check the cover O ring seal (10) and renew it if needed

  4. Check the cover (2) and renew it if needed

  5. Check the drop nose bolt (5) and renew it if needed

  6. Check the bridge (3) and renew it if needed

Assembly of the filter's insert inside the filter body

  1. Clean every part well.

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.
