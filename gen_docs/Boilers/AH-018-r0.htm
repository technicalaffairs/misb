Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

  • The air heater must be isolated mechanically by closing inlet and outlet valves.

  • Be sure the cooling water of the upper and lower bearing is closed

  • Be sure air heater must be completely drainage

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge hammer, torque wrench .

Work to be Carried Out

Before Isolating the Air Heater Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Check any abnormal noise or vibration.

  2. Check any air or flue gases leakage from manholes.

  3. Check the oil level of upper guide bearing.

  4. Check the oil level of lower support bearing.

  5. Check the oil level of drive unit.

  6. Check the oil level of drive unit for blowing equipment.

  7. Check auxiliary drive in service for a few minutes.

After Isolating the Air Heater Mechanically and Electrically

  1. Washing air pre-heater at the cold end and hot end to cleaning it in out the unit until the PH of drain water equal washing water PH= 8

  2. Vent the water cooling system for guide supporting bearing in top bottom end and clean it if needed .

  3. Check sealing clearance according to table No. 1 and figure No. 2

  4. Check the basket and changing it if needed

  5. Check the soot blower system in every air heater and spray all  nozzle if needed

  6. Check t5he fir fitting system in every air heater and spray all nozzle if needed.

  7. Check the washing system upper and lower every air heater and spray all nozzle if needed.

  8. Check alignment limits for coupling
