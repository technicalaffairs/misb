Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2 which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The function of the F.D.F is to provide the combustion air to meet the boiler demand under varying conditions quickly and efficiently.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the fan motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of fan motor

  • The fan must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery damper

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set

  • Cleaning fluid manual puller

  • Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer straight edge

  • Oil: DTE 27

  • Grease: Mobilplex 47

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check static parts for deteriorated paint and for scaling

  2. Inspect impeller for deposits and wear and wash if there are deposits and change the blades or make balance in case of wear

  3. Check damper and vane control assemblies for grease and protect unpainted surfaces with rust ban or sprayed fuel oil

  4. Check blader for freedom of movement

  5. Check fan flexible connections and joint for damage

  6. Check coupling clearance 6-10mm

  7. Check inboard and outboard bearing clearance and change the bearing if required

  8. Check shaft alignment

  9. Re-grease and change oil
