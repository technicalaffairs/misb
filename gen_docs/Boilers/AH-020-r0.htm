Introduction

This document lists the major maintenance procedure P1, to performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

This low pressure heater (4 pcs) are used for increasing the temperature of main condensate steam of turbine.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

  • Stopping the condensate water circuit of low pressure heater.

  • The low pressure heater must be isolated mechanically by closing inlet and outlet valves.

  • Be sure low pressure heater must be completely drainage

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Tube cleaner flexible shaft.

  • Tube expander.

  • Cupper plugs.

  • Rags.

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly

Low Pressure Heater in Operation

  1. Measure temperature of main condensate at inlet and outlet water in Low pressure Heater and compare it with the setting value, then record them, and determine what to do.

  2. Measure pressure of Low pressure Heater and compare it with the setting value then record them.

Low Pressure Heater out of Operation

  1. Check the low pressure heater completely drainage .

  2. Check the air service for cleaning is available

  3. Dismantling the cover of the low pressure heater to check tubes leakage

  4. Cleaning the tubes by flexible shaft (first cleaning)

  5. Cleaning the tubes by compressed air about 6 bar (sec. cleaning)

  6. Cleaning the tubes by compressed water about 6 bar (finally cleaning)

  7. Filling the Low pressure Heater condensate side with the demi-water and determine the leakage tubes and blind them by copper plugs

  8. Recording the number of tubes which blind them not more than 5 ٪.

  9. Assembling the cover of the low pressure heater

  10. Check bolts fixation, then re-tight it (if it is needed).

  11. Check glands of valves for leaking.

  12. Check flanges for leaking.

  13. Check the casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed) and repack isolated against temperature looses.

Return Regenerative Air Heater into Service

  1. Operate the low pressure heater by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that low pressure heater is OK and record the readings of clearance, temp., vibration, ... etc, and compare with ref. values.
