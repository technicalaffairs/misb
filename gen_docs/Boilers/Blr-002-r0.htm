Introduction

This document outlines major  maintenance procedures P1 which  must be performed every 24000 operating hours on the hot gas path .

The medium EXH Gas, material 15 M03, Capacity 48.5 steam per hour

The first draft initiated by eng. Fouad Abd El Aziz Fayed, tested by eng. Khamis El Sherif, accepted by general manager eng. Samir Ebed Barghous, approved by eng. Mahmoud Saad Mahmoud Balbaa Mahmoudia PS head sector, and certified by eng. Mona Ahmed Al Sherqawy WDEPC inspection & quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety healthy, safety shoes

  • Shut down the Boiler.

  • Disconnect the power supply & rack out the CB.

  • Put red tag on the Boiler.

  • The safety dep. must check the isolation and surrounding the work zone.

Tools and Equipment

  • Kit of Alean and Spanner.

  • Alignment devices.

  • Special tools, cleaner fluid.

  • WD 40, Crane.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

Diverter damper

  1. Inspection of bearing elements

  2. Check sealing elements

  3. Check seal - air system

  4. Change the lamella

  5. Check of welding point

GT exhaust flow side

  1. Check expansion joints leakage

  2. Check soundness layer expansion joints

  3. Inspection of the tube sheets

  4. Check fins tubes

Duct boiler outside

  1. Check exhaust gas leakage

  2. Check boiler / drum fixation

  3. Check insulation

  4. Inspection of expansion joints headers

  5. Inspection of pressure points connections

Pumps

  1. Cleaning the magnetic filter HPCP

  2. Cleaning the cooler (HPCP)

  3. Check leakage mechanical seal

  4. Check leakage glands

  5. Change lubricating oil

  6. Cleaning suction filters

  7. Check cooling water flow indicator

Valves

  1. Check internal leakage for level control valve

  2. Check packing

  3. Change of grease

  4. Check leakage of steam packing

Chemical dosing

  1. Check oil

  2. Check dosing pump.

Inspection boiler stack

  1. Check bearing shaft wear

  2. Change the bade seal cord for blades
