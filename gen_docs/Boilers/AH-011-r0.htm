Introduction

This document outline the equipment inspection checklist I1 which must be performed every two years as indicated by Y2  in the title block .

The Condensate exits the atmospheric blow down tank through 3 way V/V where it normally routed through the blow down heat exchanger on its way to the flow blow down sump.

The blow down heat exchanger is a single pass tube and shell type exchanger using service water as a cooling medium on the tube side.

The blow down sump contains two vertically mounted submerged P/Ps which discharge to the waste treatment system.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence like ropes with marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of P/P motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the motor

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set
  • Contact thermometer

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect for corrosion visually or by dye-penetrant, especially around connections under insulation clean and repaint by Alomineed (thermal paint).

  2. Check inlet and outlet temperature  for shell side and tube side to know if there is founding or not

  3. Check for abnormal noise to know baffle condition and readjust the steam flow and cooling water and weld any cracks.

  4. Check steam flow rate to know if there is tube leak or not

  5. Check steam trap condition

  6. Retighten cover bolts if there is leakage
