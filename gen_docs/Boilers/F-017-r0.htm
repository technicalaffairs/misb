Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection steps, required for fuel oil filter (this filter is used for cleaning the fuel oil from any impurites).

This inspection performed depending on pressure difference across the filter as indicated in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, eye glass, nose mouth "dust cover" .... etc

    •

  • The filter must be isolated mechanically by closing:
            * Input and output valves of Mazout.
            * Steam and condensate valve.
            * Drain the filter by opening the drain valve.
            * De-aerated filter by opening de-aeration valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid

Work to be Carried Out

         The filter element has to be cleaned or the cartridge changed at a differential pressure of 0.8 bar

Procedure for Dismantling and Cleaning the Filter

  1. Open the air vent of the isolated filter, this relieves the liquid pressure.

  2. Dismantle the casing cover of the isolated filter.

  3. Open the drain.

  4. Drain the liquid down to the strainer base.

  5. Remove the filter element vertically upwards.

  6. Clean the filter element by cleaning fluid and by compressed air.

Procedure for assembling the filter after Cleaning

  1. Install the filter element after cleaning.

  2. When mounting the cover, check that the seal is in perfect condition and seated properly; if necessary, replace seal. Screw cover on, the air vent remains open.

  3. Close drain.

  4. Slowly start up plant. Open the inlet shut-off valve slightly close air vent as soon as the air has escaped and liquid emerges.

  5. Open both shut-off valves fully.
