Introduction

This document out lines the details equipment inspection steps I1, required for horizontal volume displacement pump with rotating pistons with a supporting bearing.which must be performed every 2 and 6 month as indicated by M2, M6 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Hesham M. Hassan, reviewed by director eng. Saad Mostafa, and revised by quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gelil at EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Ali Al-Lithy and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam: Head Sector of Talkha Steam Power Station 2x210MW

Technical specification of Unloading Mazot PUMP

No. of pump

5        (OPD11,12,13,14and15) D001

Pump type

SIGMA  150-RPP-4000-101-LN-17

Gear pox type

TSA – 031301 – 03 – 3 – 5.6  x 1500
Delivered quantity (L/S) 17.3
Pressure in delivery( Mpa) 0.6
Pump speed (rpm) 260
Recommended oil type for pump’s gear box DTE 105
Equivalent oil type for pump’s gear box Misr compressor oil 50
Quantity of oil for pump’s gear box (L) 3
Exchange interval for pump’s gear box (hr) 2000
Recommended oil type for main gear box Mobil gear oil 630
Equivalent oil type for main gear box Misr industrial gear oil p - 220
Quantity of oil for main gear box (L) 4
Exchange interval for main gear box (hr) 2000

Flexible coupling type

 BKN-250 (Between pump’s shaft and out gearbox shaft)
BKN – 160 (Between motor shaft and in gearbox shaft)
Pump, gear box, motor shaft Diameter (mm) ( 63 , 85 – 42 , 55 ) H7
Pump, gear box, motor weight (Kg) 510 , 120 , 430

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

    •

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Feeler gauge.

  • Suitable lifting device.

Work to be Carried Out

Inspection every 2 month
Before isolated the pump mechanically and electrically

Steps

Work to be carried out

 Setting
(reference value )

Test results

1

Check any noise or vibration in pump, pump gearbox and main gear box.

OK – NOT OK

 

2

Check any leakage from the piping connections to the pump.

OK – NOT OK

 

3

 Check the amount of leakage from stuffing box and prevented it by retighten the gland bolts but it must leaks some drops (50 drop/min) to cool the gland. 

OK – NOT OK

  

4

 Check any leakage of lubricating oil in pump’s gearbox

OK – NOT OK

  

5

 Check any leakage of lubricating oil in main gearbox.

OK – NOT OK

  

6

 Check the oil level in pump gearbox. 2/3 of oil side glass  
7 Check the oil level in main gearbox 2/3 of oil side glass  
8  Check the pressure in the pump’s delivery. 0.6 Mpa  
9 Check the temperature of all bearing houses. < 70 ºC
