Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection steps I2, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

Pump function is used to:

a)      Transfer of clear water to chair coal filter.

b)      Transfer of aced water to anion.

c)      Transfer of demi water to make up water tank.

d)      Transfer of drinking water to drinking water tank.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

    •

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, suitable lifting device

  • Manual puller, feeler gauges.

 

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly of the Pump

  1. Isolated suction and delivery valves are closed

  2. Disconnect the motor from foundation and move it far from the pump.

  3. Pull the pump coupling by using manual puller

  4. Look out (withdraw) the coupling key from the shaft.

  5. Disconnect the cooling water connection.

  6. Screw off the nut to disconnect the impeller from shaft.

  7. Withdraw the impeller key from shaft.

  8. Screw off the gland flange

  9. Take out packing.

  10. Pull shaft protection.

  11. Take out the shaft with front bearing and without back bearing.

  12. Pull the front bearing from the shaft by using bearing puller.

  13. Take out the back bearing from bearing bracket by hammering

Assembly of the Pump

Inspection and Assembly of Pump Parts

  1. Check the impeller packing ring from mechanical wear or chemical corrosion and renewed if it needed

  2. Check the shaft protection bush and renewed it if it needed

  3. Check the shaft and repair or renewed it check the front and back bearing and renewed it if it needed.

Assembly of the Pump

  1. Clean every part well

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly

  3. After finishing assembly of the pump it must be rotated by hand.

  4. Fixed the pump and motor on foundation alignment of pump and motor

  5. Change the packing of stuffing box filling the pump by lub oil

Return Pump into Service

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK, check the direction of rotation, record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration, according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.
