Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Suitable lifting device.

  • Manual puller, feeler gauge, Forcing screw.

Work to be Carried Out

 

Disassembly of the pump

  1. Check the closing of the suction, delivery. Heating steam valves.

  2. Let pump cool down to the ambient temp.

  3. Dismount protection against accidental contact from the base plate.

  4. Screw off the bolts connected motor with foundation and moved motor faraway the pump.

  5. Withdraw coupling’s half from spindle (12) by using manual puller and remove key (41).

  6. Prepare tank for catching liquid drain from pump by open plugs (84)&(85).

Dismount of the cartridge-unit pump from the pump casing

  1. Screw off bolts (52) to dismount pump’s cover – non driver side (4) from pump casing (1) and take out gasket (21).

  2. Screw off bolts (51) to dismount the cartridge-unit pump from the pump casing (1) and then we can take out the cartridge-unit pump from the pump casing (1)

  3. Remove gasket (21) and “O” ring (29).

Disassembly of the bearing cover and bearing

  1. Remove circlip (35) and spacer bush (16) from the driving spindle (12).

  2. Screw off bolts (54) to dismount bearing cover (5) from pump cover – drive side (3).

  3. Force off bearing cover (5) from the pump cover – drive side (3) and dismount the driving spindle (12) by using forcing screw and then take out gasket (22)

  4. Dismount circlip (37) from the bearing cover (5).

  5. Dismount deep groove ball bearing (34) from the bearing cover (5) by using bearing puller.

  6. Dismount supporting washer (36).

Disassembly of he mechanical seal

  1. Remove the stationary ring (86) of the mechanical seal from bearing cover (5) and take out “o” ring (87) from this unit.

  2. Dismount the rotating part of the mechanical seal (83) consisting of rotating seal ring, casing, retaining ring, profile washer, spring and valve spring plate over the driving spindle (12).

  3. Dismount spacer ring (80) and junk ring (81).

Disassembly of the spindles set

  1. Screw off bolts (55) and dismount pump cover – drive side (3) over the driving spindle (12).

  2. Remove gasket (24).

  3. Withdraw set of spindles consisting of driving spindle (12) and two idler spindle (13) with balance disks (8) from the pump casing insert (2).

 

Inspection and renewed of the cartridge-unit part

  1. Check gasket (21) and renewed it if needed.

  2. Check “o” ring (29) and renewed it if needed.

Inspection and renewed of the bearing cover part

  1. Check all circlips (35)&(37) and renewed it if needed.

  2. Check gasket (22) and renewed it if needed.

  3. Check the deep groove ball bearing (34) and renewed it if needed.

  4. Check the bearing house and repair it if needed.

Inspection of the mechanical seal parts

  1. Check the stationary ring (86) and it “o” ring (87) and renewed it if needed.

  2. Check all parts of the rotating part of the mechanical seal (83)and renewed any part if it needed .

Inspection and renewed of the pump casing insert and spindles

  1. Check the pump casing insert and spindles from the mechanical wear and chemical corrosion and renewed it if there is big clearance between spindles and insert.

  2. Check gasket (24) and renewed if it needed.

Inspection of coupling

  1. Check the two half of the coupling and renewed it if needed.

  2. Check the flexible parts of the coupling and renewed it if needed.

Assembly of the pump

  1. Clean well every part of the pump.

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After finishing the assembly of the pump it must be rotating by hand from the coupling.

Re-alignment of the pump to the motor

  • According to the MPIS doc # cpl-001-r0

Re-greasing the ball bearing

  1. You must use the suitable type of grease.

  2. Quantity of grease is 35 gram.

  3. Re-grease the ball bearing from the lubricating nipple (50).
