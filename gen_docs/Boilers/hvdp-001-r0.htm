Introduction

This document out lines the major maintenance procedure P1, required for horizontal volume displacement pump with rotating pistons with a supporting bearing, which performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Hesham M. Hassan, reviewed by director eng. Saad Mostafa, and revised by quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gelil at EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Ali Al-Lithy and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam: Head Sector of Talkha Steam Power Station 2x210MW

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this pump.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set,

  • Cleaning fluid, Suitable lifting device,

  • Manual puller, Feeler gauge.

Work to be Carried Out

Removal of the pump with gearbox

  1. Check the closing of the suction, delivery, heating steam and condensate valve.

  2. Prepare a tank for catching liquid from disconnected piping and from the pump at which plug (28) is screwed off.

  3. Disconnect suction and delivers piping from the pump.

  4. Screw off the nuts of the coupling bolts. (Between input shaft of the pump’s gear box and the out put shaft of the separate gearbox).

  5. Screw of the bolts (37) between the pump frame and foundation.

  6. Lifting the pump with gearbox to the workshop.

Disassembly of the pump

  1. Remove the coupling from the shaft of the pump’s gearbox by using suitable puller.

  2. Screw off nuts (1) to remove pump cover (15) from the pump body (26).

  3. Screw off bolts and nuts (36).

  4. Remove the pump body (26).

  5. Screw of the bolts of splice (35) and take them off.

  6. Remove rotating piston (14) by means of a puller. Which removed the bearing (34).

  7. Remove the gland body (16) even with the gland packing (18) even with the flanges (20).

Inspection of the pump parts

  1. Check the bearing (34) and renewed if it need.

  2. Check the rotating pistons from mechanical wear and renewed if it need.

  3. Check the pump body from the mechanical wear and renewed if it need.

  4. Check the gasket between the pump cover (15) and pump body (26) and renewed if it need

  5. Check the gasket between the pump body (26) and gland body.

  6. Check the two shaft of the pump and renewed if need.

  7. Check the gland bush and renewed if need.

  8. Chang the gland packing with the suitable size.

  9. Check the sealing insert (13) and renewed if it needed.

Disassembly of the pump’s gearbox

  1. Drain the lubricating oil from the gearbox by screw of plug (23).

  2. Dismantle the cover (25) from the gear box body (10) by screw of bolts (38).

  3. Screw off the nuts (39) from the two shafts of the gearbox.

  4. Remove the bearing cover (4).

  5. Hammering on the input shaft (22) of gearbox but it must be put a wooden piece between the hammer and the shaft.

  6. Take out the driver and driven shaft of the gearbox.

  7. Pull out the bearing from the two shafts (21, 22).

  8. Pull out the gear wheels (8) by using puller.

Inspections of the pump’s gearbox parts

  1. Check the spur gear wheel (8) and renewed if it needed.

  2. Check the bearing and renewed if it needed.

  3. Check the oil seal (11) and renewed if it needed.

  4. Check the gasket between cover (25) and gearbox body (10) and changed it if need.

Assembly of the pump’s gearbox parts

  1. Clean every part of the gearbox

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After assembly the gearbox it must be rotated by put the coupling in the driver shaft (22) and rotated it.

Assembly of the pump

  1. Clean well every part of the pump.

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly of the pump to the gear box.

  3. After finishing the assembly of the pump to the gearbox we must be rotating the driver shaft (22) easy by using the coupling.

Installing the pump with the gearbox to the foundation

  1. Install the pump with the gearbox in reverse sequence of the removal sequence .

Re-Alignment of the pump to the separate gearbox

  1. According to the MPIS doc # cpl-001-r0

Change the packing of the stuffing box

  1. Clean the surfaces of the stuffing box and shaft well.

  2. Wire the suitable packing size on a part of shaft with the same diameter of the pump shaft.

  3. The number of turns is equal to the numbers of rings needed in the stuffing box.

  4. Cut all packing rings along the shaft by using a sharp knife.

  5. Fit the rings in the stuffing box with the cut turned 180o , starting at 9 clock.

  6. The packing should never be knocked as they then may become damaged.

  7. Remember that in a stuffing box there must always be as light leakage of the fluid.

  8. Do not tighten the gland hard to prevent the rise in temperature of the stuffing box.

Refilling the pump's gearbox by lubricating oil

  1. Prepare the suitable lubricating oil in a clean bottle.

  2. Open the plug (7).

  3. Fill some lubricating oil and turn the gearbox by hand many times to clean gear box.

  4. Open the plug (23) and drain the oil, which used to clean.

  5. Close the plug (23) again and filling the gearbox with oil until 2/3 of oil side glass.

                                                                      Trouble Shooting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

Pump does not supply amount

Suction  line trouble

- Check suction valve it must be opened.

- Check air tightness of suction piping

Pump not primed

Prime the pump.

Opposite sense of rotation

Check the rotation direction.

Leakage in relief valve

Check the relief valve seat and also its spring.

Excessive suction lift

Check the recommended specification.

Excessive worn in rotating pistons and pump casing

Chang the rotating piston and repair pump casing or changed it.

Delivered amount is low

Suction line trouble

- Check suction valve and it must be opened.

- Check air-tightness of suction piping.

Pump speed are too low

Check recommended specifications.

Clearance between rotating pistons, bore or covers is too great.

Change the worn parts.

Mechanical impurities in the liquid

Check the filter before pump

Pump doses not reach the prescribed pressure

Pressure gauge gives low pressure

Recalibrate the pressure gauge or renewed it.

Clearance between the rotating pistons, body and covers is too great.

Check or change worn parts of pump

Direct connection of suction with delivery happened in the pump

Dismantle the pump and check it.

Excessive noise in pump

Air in pump casing

Check air-tightness of pump casing.

Misalignment.

Realignment the pump with motor and retighten all bolts of pump and motor with foundation.

Discharge pressure too high

Check the recommended discharge pressure.

Bearing damage

Change the bearing

Excessive power Consumption

Excessive discharge pressure

Check the pressure gauge.

Uncorrected assembly of pump.

Mount the pump correctly.

Cold liquid or high viscosity

Measure the viscosity and temperature.

Gland is tightening too much and heated.

Check the stuffing box and it must be leakage some drops of liquid to cooling gland.

Bearing damage

Check the bearing and exchanged it if needed

Pump speed higher than rated

Measure the pump speed.
