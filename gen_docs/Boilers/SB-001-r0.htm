Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six month as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

 

The soot blower used for cleaning superheater, reheater, economizer tubes, and regenerative air heater baskets.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this soot blower

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this soot blower

  • The soot blower must be isolated mechanically

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller,

  • Suitable lifting device. Feeler gauge

  • Hammer, Air wrench

    •

Work to be Carried Out

 

Monthly

  1. Check the gear box oil level and adjuste as required

  2. Check the lube oil leakage

  3. Check the soot blower for smooth operation

  4. Check all bolts and support steel fasteners bolts, and tighten if necessary

  5. Check the bearing oil for contamination

Every 6 month

  1. Check the soot blower holes tip

  2. Check the poppet valve seat and link

  3. Check the seal air lines

  4. Check the lance tube for deformation

  5. Check and inspect the air relief valve

  6. Check the roller and carriage

  7. Inspect and analyse the oil of the gear box
