Introduction

This document details the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually bases or at any troubles occurs as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Function of the pump: Drawing Mazout through the heater and pumps it to top of daily tank. The heater will hold the tank contents at 195  5F

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for motor of this pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable bearing of the motor of this pump

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves and drain it.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, and Suitable lifting device,

  • Manual puller, Feeler gauge

  • Lessen set screws, and grease

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly of the Pump 

  1. Remove inlet head

  2. Remove cap screws (4) and pull off inlet head (24)

  3. Remove Idler rotors

  4. Remove Bolts (21) Remove thrust plate (9) and spacers (20)Remove Idler balance piston housing (18) roll out Idler rotors (17)

  5. Remove in board cover

  6. Remove Bolts (4) with draw the in board cover (9) and O-ring (7) from the case (1).

  7. Remove Power rotor assembly

  8. Remove Bolts (16), bearing retainer (26), bearing (14), bearing retainer (26), bearing spacer (13) and mechanical seal (23).

  9. Remove rotor housing

  10. Push the rotor housing (2) and O ring (6) out of inlet end of the case (1)

Inspection of the Pump Parts

  1. Check ball bearing (14) and replace if it is necessary.

  2. Check mechanical seal (23) and replace if it is necessary.

  3. Check thrust plate (19) and replace if it is necessary.

  4. Check rotor housing (2) and replace if it is necessary.

  5. Check in board cover (9) and replace if it is necessary.

  6. Check power rotor (11) and replace if it is necessary.

  7. Check Idler rotor (17) and replace if it is necessary.

  8. Change housing O ring (6) with a new one.

  9. Change cover O ring (7) with a new one.

  10. Change Gasket (8) with a new one.

  11. Change spacer ring (25) with a new one.

Assembly of the Pump

  1. Clean every parts well

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After finishing Assembly of the Pump it, must be rotated by hand.
