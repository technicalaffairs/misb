Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mamdoh Anber, tested by eng. Mohamed Abd El Aal and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abd El Gelil, accepted by general director eng. Yousry Abdel Maqsood, and approved by eng. Awad M. Fathay Head Sector of Talkha Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes.

  • HRSG out of service.

  • Disconnect the power supply of damper actuator and tag it.

  • G.T. out of service and ( excitation CB & unit aux. CB & low voltage trans. CB ) is rack out.

  • Make scaffolding if need,

  • All safety precaution must be followed by safety dep.

Tools and Equipment

  • Welding machine.

  • Grinder m/c & grinding disc.

  • Filler metal for welding, Spanners.

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Repair the deformations.

  2. Check leaking.

  3. Change the damage parts.

  4. Check base metal for cracks & deformation.

  5. Change the damage base metal.

  6. Maintain the base cracks if available.

  7. Check bolts for loose & retighten the loosen bolts.
