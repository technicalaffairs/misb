Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P2, which must be carried every 48000 operating hour.

The medium EXH Gas, material 15 M03, Capacity 48.5 steam per hour

The first draft initiated by eng. Fouad Abd El Aziz Fayed, tested by eng. Khamis El Sherif, accepted by general manager eng. Samir Ebed Barghous, approved by eng. Mahmoud Saad Mahmoud Balbaa Mahoudia PS head sector, and certified by eng. Mona Ahmed Al Sherqawy WDEPC inspection & quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc.

  • Shut down the Boiler.

  • Disconnect the power supply & rack out the CB.

  • Put red tag on the Boiler.

  • The safety dep. must check the isolation and surrounding the work zone.

Tools and Equipment

  • Kit of Alean and Spanner.

  • Alignment devices.

  • Special tools, cleaner fluid.

  • WD 40, Crane.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

HP Steam Drum

  1. Chemical injection spray nozzle, check pro holes for tears & wears.

  2. Check bolts & plates.

  3. Check soundness of steam drier.

  4. Change main hole gasket.

  5. Inspection of level gauges.

  6. Check of safety valve.

  7. Check gasket leakage and drain flange.

Feed Water Tank Dearator

  1. Check for obstructions or extensive wear to drum sliding plates.

  2. Check manhole gasket leakage.

  3. Check leakage from level gauges.

  4. Spray nozzles check bolts and soundness nozzle.

  5. Steam distributor pipe, check dorthles for wear and tear.

  6. Wear plate L.P drum, check thickness.

Blow Down Tank

  1. Inspection nozzle connections.

  2. Change manhole gasket.

  3. Check for corrosion or abnormalities.

Water Tank

  1. Tank external painting, check for corrosion.

  2. Check manhole gasket leakage.

Diverter Damper

  1. Inspection of bearing elements.

  2. Check sealing elements.

  3. Check seal - air system.

  4. Check the lamella.

  5. Check of welding point.

GT Exhaust Flow Side

  1. Check expansion joints leakage.

  2. Check soundness layer expansion joints.

  3. Inspection of the tube sheets.

  4. Check fins tubes.

Boiler Duct Outside

  1. Check exhaust gas leakage.

  2. Check boiler / drum fixation.

  3. Check insulation.

  4. Inspect the expansion joints headers.

  5. Inspection the pressure points connections.

Pumps (FWP - HPP - LPP)

  1. Cleaning the magnetic filter HPCP.

  2. Cleaning the cooler (H.P.C.P).

  3. Check leakage mechanical seal.

  4. Check leakage glands.

  5. Change lubricating oil.

  6. Cleaning suction filters.

  7. Check cooling water flow indicator.

Valves

  1. Check internal leakage for level control valve.

  2. Check packing.

  3. Change of grease.

  4. Check leakage of steam packing.

Chemical Dosing

  1. Check oil.

  2. Check dosing pump.

Silencers

  1. Check corrosion casing.

  2. Check of the silencers internal absorption.

Tube Finned

  1. Inspection of L.P evaporator.

  2. Inspection of H.P economizer.

  3. Inspection of H.P evaporator.

  4. Inspection of super heater.

Boiler Stack

  1. Check shaft wear at center bearing (welding and grinding) if need.

  2. Check flanged bearing (replacement the defect and greasing.

  3. Check center bearing replacement the defect.

  4. Replace the bad packing for stuffing box and tight it.

  5. Change the bad seal cord for blades.

  6. Change the mechanical drive defects for actuators.

  7. Change the gear box if defect.
