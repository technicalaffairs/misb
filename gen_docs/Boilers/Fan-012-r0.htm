Introduction

This document out lines the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

The forced draft fan is used for pushing atmospheric air through the air preheater dampers various air ducts and burners in to the furnace.

This fans are installed upstream of boiler to supply the required amount of air for combustion

Safety Precaution

  • A work Permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your Safety helmet and safety shoes and etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the forced draft fan.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, feeler gauge

  • Suitable lifting device

 

Work to be Carried Out
After Isolation the Forced Draft Fan 73TVAFG Figure (1), (1a)

Disassembly of The Rear and Front Bearing type (Dodge RT Sleevoil 4.44)

  1. Take out the Temperatures Sensor

  2. Drain the oil from the Bearing house

  3. Isolate the Cooling water Supply

  4. Disassemble all Cooling water house

  5. Screw off the nuts (1) Connected with Bearing house cover (2)

  6. Screw off the Plunger Screw (3)

  7. Remove Cover Top half from Bearing (2)

  8. Remove Sleeve Top half (3) from shaft

  9. Lift the Shaft  By Jack

  10. Remove Dust Seal (5)

  11. Check the thrust collar for the Fixed Bearing (front bearing)

  12. Check the Babbitt Face for any Scratches and renewed if it needed

  13. Check the alignment of Shaft wheel and motor

  14. Check the Babbitt Face thrust Plates (6)

Assembly of the of the Rear and Front Bearing

  1. Clean every part well.

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

  3. After finishing assembly of the Bearing it mast be rotated the shaft manually.

  4. Realignment of the fan and motor according to Table (1).

Disassembly of the Coupling type Zurn Series ( F ):  Figure (2)

  1. Disassemble the Safety Cover of Coupling

  2. Screw off the bolt (1) of Coupling Sleeve (2)

  3. Remove the Tow Sleeves from hubs (3) and check hubs and sleeves teeth for any mechanical wear after clean every part well

  4. Check the O-rig in grooves Seal (5)

  5. Check gap with feeler for hub separation at 905C points min. gap = 0.155" max. gap = 0.250

  6. Chang the gasket between tow sleeve (4)

Assembly of the Coupling

  1. Clean every part well

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly

  3. After finishing assembly of the bearing it mast be rotated the shaft manually

  4. Grease the coupling according to table (1)

  5. Realignment of the fan and motor according to table (1)

Impeller, Case, Shaft and Foundation

  1. Clean every part well

  2. Check the shaft from any defect (cracks deflection ) and repair or exchange it if needed

  3. Check the impeller from any defect (cracks deflection ) and repair or exchange it if needed

  4. Check the foundation from any defect and repair it if needed

  5. Check all anchor points from any defect (cracks deflection ) and repair or exchange it if needed

  6. If the Bolts are found to be lease tighten it and change the bolts are broken

  7. Inspected for mechanical interference between fan and components

  8. Upper clearance = ⅝ inch and lower clearance= ¼ inch

  9. Check the shaft seal clearance after change it Upper clearance = 5/16 inch and Lower clearance = 11/16 inch

Trouble Shooting

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

Excessive Fan Vibration

1- No alignment Between the driver and driven

- Make alignment Between The driven and driver

 

2- Loose anchoring devices

on the fanõs foundation

- Fixed the fanõs very well by fixing of screws

 

3- Vibration result of aero-dynamic  forces

- Caused By duct obstructions ether up stream or down stream of the fan

 

4- The wheel unbalances caused by erosion of the Blades and end plate or side plates.

- Make balanced for the wheel or re-placed if needed

 

5- Improper Lubricant viscosity may be cause of vibration

- Check to Make sure the Lubricant used in the Bearing has the properties recommended

Bearing Wear

1- Inadequate Lubrication

- The Amount of Lubrication Must Be Verified

- The correct of Oil Type is Used

- Check the specification of oil

 

2- Oil contamination

1.    investigated the oil contamination

2.    Change the dust seal if it needed

3.    Check any internal coolant leak

 

3-  Misalignment

Check alignment  if it  needed

High Bearing temperature 

1-  Non existent lubrication

- Check the percentage of lubricant and add oil if it needed

 

2- lubrication used does not meet the manufacture's specification

- Check the viscosity of oil and change the oil for specification oil

 

3- Insufficient water cooling

- Check the quantity of cooling water

- Clean the cooling piping

- Clean and check (check valve ) of cooling system

- Check the pressure of  cooling system

 

4- Alignment of bearing with other components is not correct

- Disassemble of bearing and inspect clearance of components

 

5- Bearing is worm or defective

- Change the bearing with new bearing

 

 6- Impurities in the bearing

- Disassemble the bearing clean and fill new oil

- Change the dust seal
