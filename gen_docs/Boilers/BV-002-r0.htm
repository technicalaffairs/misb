Introduction

This document lists the check list I1 of all valve types, which must be performed every two months as indicated by M2 in the title block.

The first draft initiated and tested by eng. Fawzy Ahmed Basuoni at 20 Dec 2003, accepted by General Director eng. Saad Mostafa, revised by EDEPCo. eng.. Samir Abdel-Gelil, and approved by eng. Awad Mohammed Fathy Soliman Head  Sector of TALKHA power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes, … etc.

  • Isolate valve mechanically and electrically.

  • Drain the pipeline, which connected to this valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Suitable lifting device.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check any leakage from valve gland. If it found, retighten on gland bolts or renew the gland packing.

  2. Check any leakage from the pressure tight cover. If it found retighten the cover nut.

  3. Check any internal leakage. If there is any internal leakage it must be carried out the maintenance procedure of this type to prevent the leakage.

  4. Check any leakage from the valve bodies (cracks or mechanical wear). Remark the point of leakage, and welded it with suitable electrode.

  5. Check the operation of the valve drive during closing/ opening of the valve.

  6. Check the electrical drive of the valve for electrical type.

  7. Check the oil level of the gear- motor for electrical drive.

  8. Re-grease the valve spindle.
