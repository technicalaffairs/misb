Introduction

This document lists the major maintenance procedure P1, to performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

This low pressure heater (4pcs) are used for increasing the temperature of main condensate steam of turbine.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

  • Stopping the condensate water circuit of low pressure heater.

  • The low pressure heater must be isolated mechanically by closing inlet and outlet valves.

  • Be sure low pressure heater must be completely drainage

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Tube cleaner flexible shaft.

  • Tube expander.

  • Cupper plugs.

  • Rags.

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly

Low Pressure Heater in Operation

  1. Measure temperature of main condensate at inlet and outlet water in Low pressure Heater and compare it with the setting value, then record them.

  2. Measure pressure of Low pressure Heater and compare it with the setting value then record them.

Low Pressure Heater out of Operation

  1. Dismantle the outer insulation for upper cover for Low pressure Heater.

  2. Dismantle the screws of two flanges ( inlet and outlet of water side ) .

  3. Dismantle the studs of upper cover .

  4. Remove the screws of two flanges and the studs of upper cover to be cleaned .

    5.

  5. Lift the upper cover about 1 mm by using two depressing screws .

  6. Remove the upper cover by using the main lift.

  7. Test the leakage of tubes and mark the defected tubes.

  8. Make a test to Low pressure Heater from steam side and then notice around the welding tubes to the tubes plate from leakage.

  9. Take the tube bundle out of the Low pressure Heater jacket.

  10. Remove all the tubes which blinded.

Inspection

  1. Notice the level of any tubes of L. P. H. E for decreasing the level and set the Coordinates of this tubes ( X , Y ) .

  2. Check all parts for any worn-out and replace them by new ones.

Assembly

  1. Assembly the new tubes in the position of the defective tubes by beading and welding.

  2. Take the tube bundle in the Low pressure Heater jacket after cleaning it.

  3. Make finally test for Low pressure Heater to insure no leakage happened in tubes.

  4. Put the gasket of upper cover Low pressure Heater.

  5. Set the upper cover of Low pressure Heater carefully and notice the flanges of inlet and outlet water side .

  6. Put the flange gasket and tightening of the flanged joints ( cover and two flanges of water side ) adhere to the uniform tighten cross wise , i.e converted to a clock dial tighten in the sequence of 3-9-12-6 and then round and round .

  7. Excess stresses to the screws to the screws and studs .

  8. After we put the Low pressure Heater to working operation about 24 hours we retighten the screws of two flanges ( water side ) and studs of the cover .

Starting of Low Pressure Heater

  1. Check the inlet and outlet main condensate valves of Low pressure Heater are opened.

  2.  Check the by pass valve of Low pressure Heater is closed.

  3. Check the steam valve of Low pressure Heater is opened.

  4. Check whether the inlet for flooding the stuffing box is open.

Attendance and checking during Operation

  1. The temperature of the inlet and outlet condensate of the low pressure heater must be different 25°C at least.

  2. The steam condensate level in low pressure heater is in middle indicator glass.

  3. The performance of regulating valve of outlet steam condensate in low pressure heater is correcting.

Return Regenerative Air Heater into Service

  1. Operate the low pressure heater by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that low pressure heater is OK and record the readings of clearance, temp., vibration, ... etc, and compare with ref. values.
