Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this FD FAN

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this FD FAN

  • The FD FAN must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller,

  • Suitable lifting device, Feeler gauge

  • Hammer, Dial indicator, Tourqe wrench 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect the fan side half coupling from the rotor and inspect both the fan side and the motor side half couplings for wear. Clean all parts and re-grease. jacking fixtures are required to remove the coupling hubs. In addition to these fixtures, the hydraulic jacks are required. Torquing requirements for bolts. Check the alignment of the motor, intermediate shaft (if applicable), and the couplings.

  2. Wear or damage may affect the fan balance

  3. Check all lube oil and hydraulic system hoses, piping, and fittings for leaks

  4. Check ROTOR & BLADE LINERS

  5. Check and repair as required HOUSING & INLET BOX

  6. Check and adjust as required COUPLING WEAR & ALIGNMENT

  7. Check and adjust as required DAMPER BLADES & LINKAGES

  8. Check and adjust as required ROTOR BODY BOUND BOLTS

  9. Check and adjust as required SHAFT BEARINGS & SEALS

  10. INLET CONE CLEARANCES
