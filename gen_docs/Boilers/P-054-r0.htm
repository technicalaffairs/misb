Introduction

This document out lines the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block

The pump function: transferring boiler drain to waste water retention basin treatment.

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is requires,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage .

 
 
Tools and Equipments

  • Complete maintenance tools sets

  • Cleaning fluid, manual pullers .

  • Straight edge, rages

  • Small wire brush, Filler gauge

  • Chain, lifting hock, dial gauge.

 

Work to be Carried out
Dismantling 

  1. Remove the delivery pipe connection.

  2. Disconnect the motor .

  3. Unscrew the bolts and take out the motor .

  4. Remove the coupling nuts and pins (404) alone with the coupling bushes (400) .

  5. Remove the coupling (390) by using suitable puller alone coupling key (321) .

  6. Remove the bearing cover (270-1) by unscrewing the bolts .

  7. Unscrew the two bearings nut (335/336) and then remove the thrust bearing and change if needed .

  8. Loosen the foundation bolts of motor stool and support plate (383) .

  9. Disconnect the lubricating pipe (562-1) from motor stool .

  10. Remove the bolts holding motor stool .

  11.  Take out the motor stool (290) and coupling (395) .

  12. Remove the lubricating pipe .

  13. Lift the complete unit vertically .

  14. Disconnect the bend (280) .

  15. Take out casing cover from impeller and shaft assembly .

  16. Pull out the impeller (151) .

  17. Remove impeller key (320) .

Inspection 

  1. Insect the bearing and replace it if necessary.

  2. Inspect the worn out parts.

  3. Check all parts for any worn out .

  4. Inspect the run out of shaft .

 

Reassembly

  1. Put the impeller shaft sleeve (311) on pump shaft (180) .

  2. Slide impeller on shaft and tighten the impeller screw (669) with its gasket .

  3. Fix bearing covers (271-1) with oil seals (500-4) to casing cover (220) already felted with bearing bush (353) . 

  4. Fix bell mouth (211) on pump casing (170) .

  5. Inspect casing cover (220) on pump shaft (186) .

  6. Insert the whole assembly into pump casing (107) with gasket in proper position .

  7. Mount the column pipe (132) on casing cover .

  8. Connect pipe nipple with shaft parts of union joint to bearing cover .

  9. Connect bend (280) to the pump casing .

  10. Lift the unit vertically and mount strainer (216) on bell mouth .

  11. Lift the motor stoon (290) and fix it to the column pipe at the same time fix the lubricating pipe to motor stool .

  12. Fix the support plate on foundation .

  13. Tighten the foundation bolts of motor stool .

  14. Check level of motor stool .

  15. Fix the cover bearing alone oil seal (270-2) , (500-2) to thrust bearing carrier (247) .

  16. Fit the thrust bearing (262) in the thrust bearing carrier .

  17. Push the thrust bearing adapter (272) on the pump shaft (180) .

  18. Fit bearing cover alone the oil seal .

  19. Fix the coupling key (321) and fit the flexible coupling pump shaft on the head shaft .

  20. Tighten the foundation bolts of the support plate .

  21. Mount the motor half coupling (391) on motor shaft .

  22. Mount the motor on motor spool . there should be a gap of (6mm) between the two coupling face .    

 
 
Starting of Pump

  1. Chick the direction of rotation of motor before starting.

  2. Open the delivery valve checking during operation.

  3. Vibration and noisy

  4. Temperature of bearing

  5. Discharge pressure.
