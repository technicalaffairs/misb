Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. The burner will be only dismantled after purging after closing of manual and automatic fuel valves.

  2. When closing of the manual and automatic fuel valves when the dismantling air is in service and when the burner is in the back ward position.

  3. Let the burner cool before cleaning it by immersing the head in a gas oil bath.

  4. Completely dismantle the burner head and carefully clean each of the elements.

  5. Check the condition of inside hoses and changing it if needed plates (No. 5)

  6. Check the numbers of the spray plates and distributors (No. 1)

  7. Measurement of holes and changing it if needed (No 1.3)

  8. Correctly reassemble the spray parts and re-assembly tighten the tip nut (No. 2).

  9. After tightening the burner long purging of the (steam or air).

  10. Check the condition of the bearing faces of yoke and if necessary lap them.

  11. After cleaning as described above store the guns as a rack and immerse the lance tip in a gas oil bath.
