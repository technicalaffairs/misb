Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc.

  • The HRSG are out of service & depressurized the high pressure section

  • Drain the high press. drum and put red tag on the feed water pumps

  • The inlet dampers are closed & locked mechanical

  • Disconnect the power supply for the damper actuators.

Tools and Equipment

  • TIG welding machine    

  • Grinder & grinding wheel

  • Selected filler metal for TIG welding

  • Cleaning tools & keys kit

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Internal inspection for evidence of corrosion or scaling.

  2. Internal inspection for cyclones, repair the defects.

  3. Clean internal parts from the foreign bodies.   

  4. Check the feed water pipe clean and repair the defect.

  5. Check manhole gasket if there is leakage.

  6. Check blow down parts clean and repair the defect.

  7. Inspect the perforated plate repair the defect.

  8. Inspect all piping connections for evidence of corrosion.

  9. Inspect insulation repair the defect.

  10. Inspect the opening for down comers 

  11. Inspect the passivition layer for inner surface   

  12. Check water level glass and change if needed

  13. Check valves and repair the defect
