Introduction

This water pump used to transfer feed water from law press-drum to high press-drum.

This document out outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mamdoh Anber, tested by eng. Mohamed Abd El3aL and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abd El Gelil, accepted by general director eng. Yosry Abd El Maqsood, and approved by eng. Awad Fathay Head Sector of Talkha Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as Helmet, belt, shoes, .... etc

  • HRSG is out service.

  • Put damper selector on close position.

  • Disconnect the power supply of damper actuator and tag it.

  • All safety precautions must be followed by safety dep.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet, belt, shoes, .... etc

  • Spanners, Cleaning fluid, and Rags

Work to be Carried Out

 

Disassembly

  1. Drain pump case and bearing housing.

  2. Remove the constant level oilers to prevent breakage.

  3. Remove coupling cover.

  4. Disconnect pump – driver coupling.

  5. Remove the coupling spacer and coupling spacer and coupling hub.

  6. Disassembly pump bearing ( Driver side ).

  7. Disassembly pump thrust bearing ( end side ).

  8. Disassembly mechanical seal ( driver side & end side ).

  9. Remove cooling water pipes.

  10. Unbolt pump casing.

  11. Lift out upper casing half.

  12. Lift out pump shaft.

  13. Remove the old gasket (from casing two halves).

  14. Check pump base fixation  .

  15. Check driver shaft run out .

  16. Check bearings housing for wear and cracks.

  17. Check ball bearing condition (Change if needed).

  18. Change lube. oil.

  19. Check alignment between pump and motor.

  20. Check upper & lower halves of pump casing for wear and cracks.

  21. Check all impellers for wear and cracks.

  22. Check wear rings – sleeves - keys for wear and cracks.

  23. Check stage piece – center stage sleeve for wear , cracks and damage.

  24. Check pump rotor for wear, damage and deformation.

Assembly

  1. Apply good cleaning for all pump parts.  

  2. Reinstall pump rotor in the lower casing half.

  3. Put new gasket.

  4. Reinstall upper casing half.

  5. Rebolt upper and lower casing halves.

  6. Reinstall mechanical seal in both sides.

  7. Reinstall front and rear bearings .

  8. Using heating reinstall coupling hub (pump&motor shafts).

  9. Do alignment check between pump & motor shafts.

  10. Reinstall coupling sleeve and reassemble pump – motor coupling.

  11. Reinstall cooling water pipes.
