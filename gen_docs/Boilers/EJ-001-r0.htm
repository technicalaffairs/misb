Introduction

This document outlines the inspection steps I1 for the metal expansion joints, (zigzag under the evaporator (HP & LP) & blows at the frame of the boiler and at GT stack, Material: 15MO3), which must be carried out when  the gas turbine have major maintenance after 4000 work hour.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdo Abdo El  Hendawy, tested by eng. Ashraf Abdel Moniem Fahmy at 28 April 2002 on Boiler no. 4 and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gilel, accepted by general director eng. Sobhy Mohamed El Bakry and approved by eng. Moneer Mohamed El Wakeel Head Sector of Demiatta PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • HRSG out of service

  • Disconnect the power supply of damper actuators and tagged it.

  • G T out of service and (excitation CB & unit aux. CB & low voltage trans. CB) is rack out

  • Mack scaffolding at the joint if need

  • All safety precaution must be followed by safety department.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Argon and TIG welding machine    

  • Grinder m/c & grinding discs

  • Filler metal for argon and TIG welding.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Modify the zigzag under HP evaporator to bellows

  2. Repair the deformations

  3. Weld the cracks after grinding
