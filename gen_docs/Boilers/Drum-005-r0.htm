Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc.

  • Boiler out of service.

  • Drain the drum to zero level.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller,

 

Work to be Carried out

  1. Open the two manholes.

  2. Check the thickness of the chemical layers.

  3. Check the fixation of bolts and nuts for the drum internal parts.

  4. Check on cyclones, the first and the second scrubbers.

  5. Check the feed tubes opening.

  6. Clean the chemical sludges on the drum internal surface.

  7. Replace manhole gaskets.
