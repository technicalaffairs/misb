Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets shoes, gloves, … etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this pump

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this pump

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valve

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller, Torque wrench

  • Suitable lifting device feeler gauge

  • Hammer, Dial indicator

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly of the Pump  (see fig 1 & 2)

  1. Check the closing of the suction delivery. Heating steam valves.

  2. Let pump cool down to the ambient temperature.

  3. Prepare tank for catching liquid drain from pump by open plugs.

  4. Dismount protection against accidental contact from the base plate.

  5. Screw off the bolts connected motor with foundation and move motor faraway.

  6. Remove coupling with removal device and remove key (866) also.

  7. Move pump vertical and remove pump casing (131) upwards.

  8. Mark position of idler spindle and (661) and remove them.

  9. Press main spindle (669) with ball bearing out of flange cover (074)

  10. Remove circlip (869) and spacer (063)

  11. Press out ball bearing

  12. Check flat gaskets and o-rings whether they are damaged. If necessary clean and polish the running surface of the main spindle in the range of the o-rings and seal lips

  13. The coupling end should be covered with an installation sleeve to protect the o-rings and the seal lips. For an easy installation it is useful to grease the surface slightly

  14. To dismantle the mechanical seal, remove the slide ring and the spring from the main spindle by twisting it clockwise. Press counter ring with a plastic sleeve out of the casing

Inspection and Renewed of any defect Parts as needed
Assembly of the Pump

  1. Clean well every part of the pump

  2. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly

  3. After finishing the assembly of the pump it must be rotating by hand by hand from the coupling

  4. Re-alignment of the pump to the motor

  5. Re-grease the ball bearing from the lubricating nipple
