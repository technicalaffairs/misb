Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures check listed P1 which must be performed for steam drum every 3 years as indicated by Y3 in the above title block .

The steam drum separates the water from the steam and returns the water by way of the down comers.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified staff should be carry the suitable safety category .

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes, .. etc.

  • The HRSG are out of service and depressurized the high pressure section.

  • Drain the high pressure drum and put red tag on the feed water pumps.

  • The inlet dampers are closed , locked mechanical.

  • Disconnect the power supply for the damper actuators.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Rages.

  • Grinder and grinding wheel.

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Remove front and rear manholes doors insulation.

  2. Open front manhole door.

  3. Remove old gasket.

  4. Clean internal parts.

  5. Inspect the following parts and repare if needed.

  6. Drum doors for pits, cracks and damage.

  7. Drum internals for pits, cracks and damage.

  8. Drum body for pits, cracks and damage.

  9. Check water level glass and change if needed.

  10. Reinstall new gaskets.

  11. Close drum manholes doors.

  12. Reinstall manholes doors insulation.
