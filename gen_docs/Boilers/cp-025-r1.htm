Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this compressor.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this compressor.

  • The compressor must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery valve.

  • Close the cooling water supply.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller, Forcing device

  • Feeler gauge, Suitable lifting device

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check of tightening of all bolted connections, namely connections of the crankshaft with a flywheel and a coupling bolts of frame bearings and of connecting rod bolts, tightening of piston nuts and of piston rods. (see figure 1)

Check of wear of piston rings - exchange when necessary

  1. Check of piston rings is carried out visually, when they are mechanically damaged, exchange them

  2. Worn piston rings result in a drop of the delivered medium. In this case it is necessary to exchange a complete set of piston rings.

Attention

  1. Before starting mounting the piston ring on a piston

  2. Check clearance in a lock after fitting into cylinder.

  3. A small clearance after heating to opr. temperature may cause a bearing of both ends of piston rings and may cause an excessive wear , resp. seizing in cylinder .

  4. A big clearance may cause non tightness and decrease of the capacity . ( see FIG 2 )

  5. Check also side clearance between piston rings and a groove of piston ring should move freely.

  6. A big side clearance causes tilting of rings and its wear, a small clearance and thus non- movability of rings in a groove cause a seizing.

  7. Rings are to be exchanged also when maximum wear reached the values mentioned in records. ( see FIG 2 )  

  8.  Clearances in locks of piston rings are also mentioned in records. ( see FIG 2 )

Inspection of working valves in all stages and of non-return valve resp. exchange of plates and springs

  1. Take out and dismount working valves and clean their individual parts in kerosene by fine brush – remove sediments and impurities.

  2. Individual parts of valves are to be protected against damage and deformations.

  3. Try to prevent their mutual change.

  4. Keep an uniform tightening of holding down screws and of screws in valve covers in order to prevent a crossing of valve lanterns and of valves.

 

 

 

 

External inspection of shaft in parts of the main bearings

Check the function of safety valves

  1.  Check there is no interval leakage.

  2. Check spring function

  3. Recalibrate the safety valves according to pressure 1st stage safety valve P=0.33 Mpa & 2nd stage safety valve P= 1.59 Mpa

Check function of pressure gauges and thermometers, recalibrate when necessary

  1.  Clean thermometer pockets.

Maintenance of glands of shut- off control elements.

  1. The shut – off and control fittings are to be maintained in a perfect condition.

  2. Check tightness of control spindles and tighten the gland when necessary, Resp. exchange the packing.

Cleaning of oil filter and of slot filter.

  1. Cleaning of oil filter in frame is carried out by trichloro-ethylene or by technical benzene.

  2.  The washed filter should be dried and remounted.

  3. Cleaning of slot filter after a certain operation period , stop the oil supply ,remove filter and wash in benzene .

  4. Possible oil escape round a spindle can be prevented by tightening of gland bolt and exchanged of packing or by exchanged of packing ring.

  5.  The ring will be exchanged on a dismounted filter after taking down a handle.

  6. Before putting on a new ring, we recommend to apply a thin layer of grease on a spindle and slide it.

  7. Lips and lamella must not be damaged during handling. ( see FIG 1 )

Cleaning of suction air filter

  1. Maintenance of suction filter requires namely cleaning and wetting of cylinder filter inserts and repainting.

  2. Dirty inserts are washed in worm water with detergent.

  3. Clean dry insertion pieces are wetted in low – congealing oil.

  4. After oil dripping – approx. after 2-3 hours the inserts remounted. ( see FIG 1)

Check of wear of all piston rods and of glands

  1. When the piston rod is scratched, the scratched may be reground and polished.

  2. Worn piston rod (with a smaller diameter than by 0.3 mm) is to be exchanged.

  3. Worn seal leaks oil from a frame into intermediate frame.

  4. Check and modify the size of inclined cross sections between individual segments of a bronze gland.

  5. The cross section should be 0.3-0.35 mm . active surface of gland are treated by scraping on an arbor or on a piston rod , try to keep sharp edges on a lower part of scraping segments .

Check of wear of packing glands.

  1.     Worn seal is indicated by whistling / blowing during the set run.

  2.      Always exchange complete seal as an unit.

Check of control valve and of pressing – off device

  1. Regulating valve and depressing device of suction valves must be maintained in a perfect function condition.

  2. Check movability and tightness of pistons in the bush of control valve casing and in bushes of covers of suction valves. When exchanging the bush in a control valve bush remill inner diameter 10 H7 after pressing and drill two by-pass holes – Ф 5 mm . Bushes of depressing pistons are to be remilled. A new piston is to be ground manually in body bush to ensure its tightness. 

 External check of reduction parts of connecting rods and of bolts.

Check of wear of cylinder bushes – cross-head guide.

  1. Measurement is carried out by a micrometer to inside diameter.

  2. When the clearance of a cross-head line exceeds values in check record exchange the respective detail.

  3. When scratches appear on an active surface, regrind them and polish.

Check of function of valves, which are permanently open or closed during operation

Check, resp. exchange of functional elements incl. Water fittings

Cleaning of oil piping of circulating system

Check of brasses in frame bearings, connecting rod brasses and of bushes

  1. Wear of brasses of the main and connecting rod bearings and bushes of piston pins results in an increased knocking in a lower part of the set , namely during idle run .

  2. When radial clearance (oil clearance) exceeds recommended values in a check record , exchange the brasses and bushes .

  3. At the same time check quality of the surface of pins of crankshaft resp. of connecting rod pins. Damaged surface is to be finished or reground.

  4. Oil clearance is measured by a feeler gauge .

  5. Spare brasses are delivered with a fitting allowance, the brasses are to be scratched to tolerance site in a check record. Connecting rod brasses and bushes are to be mounted in a connecting rod and both openings are to be milled to tolerance mentioned. Keep the alignment of both openings.    

Check of cross-head pins

  1.     Measure the  ovality of cross-head pins.

  2.     When the ovality exceeds a value mentioned in a check records, exchange the pins .         

Check of connection bolts - connection flywheel – crankshaft –piston rod – crank - connecting rod and frame bearing bolts from cracks by using penetration fluids

 

Check and inspection of complete piping for corrosion and erosion with a pressure test including fittings.

  1. Pay proper attention to dimensions of parts due to erosion and corrosion.

  2. When reassembling the piping try to prevent additional stress by non-uniform tightening of bolts of flange joints.

Check of tightness all gas discharge and water fittings  *

  1.  The maintenance itself and pressure test are to be carried out in accordance with documentation of the suppliers.

Check and Inspect the intermediate cooler, resp. of after-cooler with pressure and tightness water test   **

  1. By press water in every water tube from one end and blind the other ends and see if there is any leakage in the air space.

Check of water space of cylinders head and of intermediate cooler resp. of after cooler  ***

  1. In order to make the cleaning of cylinders easy, they are provided with cleaning openings blinded.

  2. After having removed respective covers, clean water space from sediments and mud.

  3. In bundle intermediate cooler, resp .

  4. After-cooler check passage of tubes and tightness of beading in tube plates.

  5. When the tube is broken, blind it on both sides and exchange when necessary.

 Attention:                  *, **, ***            at least once a year

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Trouble shooting  

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

1.Oil pressure in circul. Lubrication drops below 120 Kpa (over pressure ) and can not be set by a regulating screw .

- Choked suction oil filter

-Stop the set , clean the filter

- Non- tight or broken oil piping

- Stop and test the piping.

2. Sudden increase of oil pressure

- Choking of oil lubricating lines

-Stop , test the set and clean the oil lines

3. At the first starting or after repair – excessive vibration of compressor and of el. Motor, impacts in a coupling.

-Ill mutual center-ring of compressor and el. motor, resp. considerable squeezing of coupling inserts ( In case of long operation )   

-Stop and check the coupling, resp. exchange inserts of the coupling

4.Knocking in a cylinder

- Loose all broken valves – a part of valve plate has got into a working space

-Stop , check the valves resp. the space above the pistons .Exchange the plates or valves and tighten valves

-Big clearance in crank mechanism ( It is increasing by wear )

-Stop and check the clearance , worn  parts are to be repairs or exchanged.

5.Sudden drop of out put

-Worn piston rings

-Stop and check the rings , exchange worn one’s , mount the new one’s.

-Faulty valves are leak.

-Check , repair and exchange

-Incorrect function of compressor regulation , full output is not achieved , rusty and choked valve

-Clean and adjust regulating system.

6.Pressure in a delivery side increases over oper. Value.

-Faulty or dirty suction or delivery valve or faulty valve plate, defective packing under the valve. 

-Stop , exchange or clean faulty valve , packing .faulty valve is recognized acc. To increased temp. of the valve cover.

-Ill – adjusted regulation. Safety valve does not work. Shut-off valve in a delivery side, shut-off valve in supply of air regulator.

-Clean and adjust the regulation. Test safety valve, resp. adjust by 15% above operating pressure. Open the valve.

7.Pressure in a delivery is dropping – it dose not reach required oper. Over pressure.

-Consumption is bigger than output of compressor.

-Set consumption in accordance with output of compressor, put the next unit into operation.

- Non- tightness in piping .

-Check and seal distr. piping.

-Worn piston rings

-Stop and check the rings, exchange worn one’s, mount the new one’s.

-Faulty valves are leak.

-Check, repair and exchange

-Incorrect function of compressor regulation, full output is not achieved, rusty and choked valve. 

-Clean and adjust regulating system.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Trouble

Possible Reasons

Remedy

8.Temp. in a delivery side is rising

-Faulty. Loose or dirty suction or del. valve, valve plate or sealing under the valve.

-Check and repair the valve or exchange the plate or packing

-Blowing round the piston due to worn piston rings.

-Exchange of rings and fit them.

-Low or none passage of cooling water through the set.

-Increase water flow or stop the machine at once

Attent.:

When the machine overheated due to water loss, leave the machine without cooling water and let it cool.

9.White waste water by air bubbles

-Pressure air enters the cooling circuit from a machine, broken cylinder or bushes, faulty packing between head and cylinder.

-Stop, check the cylinder or bushes of cylinder, resp. pressure test.

10.Regul. Valve will switch on regulation at a lower or higher pressure than oper. one.

-Bad setting of weight, loosening of set screw..

-Adjust valve to correct pressure by shifting the weight on a longer or shorter arm and fix it.

11.Regulation does not work.

-Weight  regulator does switch off due to:

Ill connection of supply piping of pressure air to regulator. 

-Clean , open and seal the piping 

·Choked , closed or slightly dimensioned ( very long supply piping of pressure air )

 

·Rusty resp. loose piston of regulating valve.

-Dismantle the valve, resp. exchange the piston.
