Introduction

Oil Burner Gun  use for automiszing  oil to combustion chamber 
This document out lines the major maintenance procedure steps P1 required for oil burner gun,.which must be performed on an annually bases or with any default in burner as indicated by Y1 in the title block .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Shut down the oil burner.

  • Isolate the oil burner gun mechanically as follows:
    - In cooling re-circulation position.
    - Slowly close the shut off valves to all three oil inlet/ outlet in the following sequence (II–III–I)
    - In operation position.
    - Slowly close the shut off valves to all three oil inlet / outlet in the following sequence (II–I–III)
    - Shut down pressure air for oil burner gun.
    - Isolate limit switch of oil burner gun.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Vernier caliper, Cleaning fluid.

 

Work to be Carried Out


Dismantle the oil burner gun from burner guide tube

  1. Check the closing of valves inlets and outlets of the oil burner gun .

  2. Before dismantle the oil burner gun you must measure the distance (L) between burner gun tube edge and the end of the oil burner gun as in fig. (2).

  3. Check the Mazot pressure gauge ( It must be zero) .

  4. Prepare a tank to catch Mazot leaks during disconnecting of the oil feed pipes.

  5. Screw off the air connection (62) .

  6. Disconnect the hoses socket of the inlet / outlet mazot which connected to I,II,III

  7. Screw off bolts (B) which connected oil burner gun with oil burner guide tube (A).

  8. Screw off bolts ( C ) and release the oil burner gun sealing (D) from the burner guide  tube (A).

  9. Take out the oil burner gun from the burner guide tube ( You must use special gloves )

Disassembly of the oil burner gun body parts

  1. Disconnect the two pipes (33) , (34) of feeding and return oil.

  2. By use air service in inlet (62) the piston will be in operation position and we can loose cap nut (12) easy.

  3. Shut off the air service.

  4. Screw off bolts (58) which connected oil burner gun body (5) with needle guide body (4).

  5. Screw off the counter nut (56) and adjustable nut (57) .

  6. Screw off the cap nut (12) and take out nozzle( 51) and atomizer (50) .

  7. Release the “ O “ ring (55) which sealed between atomizer ( 50 ) and inside tube ( 10 ) .

  8. Take out the needle ( 47 ) from  the oil burner gun body .

Disassembly of the needle guide body parts

  1. Screw off hand (17)

  2. Screw off the bolts (31)

  3. Screw off the bolts (59) which connected needle guide body flange to gate valve body flange (3)

  4. Take out guide rod (63), guide needle (15) and spring (14) .

  5. Release “O” ring (46), (43), (45/1), (45/2).

Disassembly of the five – way gate valve parts

  1. Screw off the bolts (60) which connected five-way gate valve flange (3) to pneumatic part flange (2)

  2. Take out the distribution gate valve (8) with guide rod (64) from five – way gate valve body (3)

  3. Removes pin (28) which connected piston rod (7) & guide rod (64) to the distribution gate valve (8)

  4. Release “ O “ rings (45/3).

Disassembly of the pneumatic parts

  1. Screw off cover (1) and take out spring (13).

  2. Take out the piston (6) with piston rod (7) from piston liner (2) .

  3. Screw off nut (29) and remove counter ring (32).

  4. Take out piston rod (7) from piston (6).

  5. Release “ O “ ring (43/2) from piston liner body (2)

  6. Release “ O “ ring ( 44 ) from piston inside bore.

  7. Release “ O ” ring (42) from outside diameter of piston.

Inspection and renewed of all parts of oil burner gun

 

Oil burner gun body parts

  1. Check semispherical needle end (47) and polish or renewed it if needed.

  2. Check the atomizer (50) and nozzle (51) and renewed it if needed.

  3. Renew “ O” ring (55) , (45/1).

Needle guide parts

  1. Checks spring (14) and renewed it if needed.

  2. Renew “ O “ ring ( 46 ) , ( 43/1 ).

  3. Renew “ O “ ring ( 45/2 ).

  4. Check guide rod (63) , (64) from mechanical wear and renewed it if needed.

Five – way gate valve parts

  1. Check five–way slide valve (8), body (3), and renewed it if there is big clearance between them.

Pneumatic parts

  1. Check pneumatic cylinder body (2) and renewed it if needed.

  2. Check piston (6) and renewed it if needed.

  3. Check spring (13) and renewed it if needed.

  4. Renewed “ O “ ring (45/3) , (43/2) , (42).

Assembly of all oil burner gun parts

  1. Clean well every parts.

  2. The assembly of every part will be done in reverse sequence of disassembly of every parts.

Adjustment of oil burner gun needle

  1. After assembly every part except cap nut (12) and nozzle (51).

  2. Measure the distance (x) as in fig (1) and it must be 5mm when air service is in operation to air inlet (62)

  3. If the distance is not correct, we do the following :

          * Shut off air service,          * Loosen bolts (58),              * Adjust from nuts (57), (56).

  4. After final adjust we must light every bolts and nuts in cross sequence well.

Test of the oil burner gun

  1. Test the oil burner gun from leakage by pressure air service in inlet (II) and check leakage by using foam forming agent and it forming bubbles if there is leakage.

Important:   (In operation)

* If there is oil leakage from part (16 ) this indicates that the piston rod sealing ring (43/2) is probably defective.

* If there is leakage of pressure air through of the opening (65) of the pneumatic cylinder body (2). This indicates that the sealing ring of pneumatic cylinder (42) is defective.
