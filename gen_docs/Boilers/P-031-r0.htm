Introduction

This water pump used to transfer feed water from low press-drum to high press-drum.

This document out outlines the equipment inspection check list I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety Helmet, belt, shoes, .... etc

  • HRSG is out of service.

  • Put damper selector on close position.

  • Disconnect the power supply of damper actuator and tag it .

  • All safety precautions must be followed by safety dep

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet, belt, shoes, .... etc

  • Spanners, Cleaning fluid, Rags

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Drain pump case and bearing housing

  2. Remove the constant level oilers to prevent breakage

  3. Remove coupling cover

  4. Disconnect pump – driver coupling

  5. Remove the coupling spacer and coupling spacer and coupling hub .

  6. Disassembly pump bearing ( Driver side )

  7. Disassembly pump thrust bearing ( end side )

  8. Disassembly mechanical seal ( driver side & end side )

  9. Check pump base fixation

  10. Check driver shaft run out

  11. Check bearings housing for wear and cracks

  12. Check ball bearing condition ( Change if needed )

  13. Check oil condition ( Change if needed  )

  14. Check alignment between pump and motor
