Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be carried out every 3 months as indicated by M3 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdo Abdo El  Hendawy, tested by eng. Abdel Qader Fath Alla at 02 April 2003 on Boiler no. 4 and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gilel, accepted by general director eng. Sobhy Mohamed El Bakry and approved by eng. Moneer Mohamed El Wakeel Head Sector of Demiatta PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • Be sure the seal air blower is stopped

  • Disconnect the power supply of the seal air fan  motor

Tools and Equipment

  • Hex. keys, Alin kit,

  • Screw driver,

  • New grease, Grease Gun

Work to be Carried Out

  1.  Inject new grease into both bearings by a grease gun until the used grease comes out of the side as suitable bearing grease such as (comparable R3)

  2. Check the coupling flexible intermediate ring, change it if necessary.
