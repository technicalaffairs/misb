Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The function of this valve (one valve per unit) is to minimize flow valve low capacity used during start up the unit

The first draft initiated by eng. Ali El-Safoury, tested by eng. Mohamed Anwar Ghazy, accepted by eng. Gamal El-Safoury, approved by eng. Mohamed El-Tahawy Head sector of Shoubra El-Kheimah PS, and certified by eng Nageh Soliman Shousha; MPIS coordinator and inspection & quality control director for CEPC.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate instrument air

  • Purging lines by nitrogen and confirm the vents is hydrocarbon free

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Grease Mobilplex 47

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect the internal parts (plug, cage, piston rings, seats) and replace damage parts

  2. Repack for leak

  3. Stroke the valve with IMD

  4. Regrease the isolation valves and check the gear box
