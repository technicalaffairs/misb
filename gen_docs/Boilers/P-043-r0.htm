Introduction

This document details the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

Mazout unloading Pump is used to transfer Mazout from pipes to storage tanks

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for Motor of this Pump when work on the Pump.

  • Isolate the electrical cable Bearing of the Motor of this Pump

  • The Pump must be is Isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves and drain it.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, suitable lifting device.

  • Manual puller, feeler gauge

  • Lessen set screws, grease

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the direction of the Pump

  2. Be Sure there is no any abnormal noise  or  vibration and prevent it if  found

  3. Ensure there is no any leakage from any gasket or any O-rig and prevent it if found.

  4. Check pressure in delivery of the pump.

  5. Check quantity of the pump.

  6. Check alignment of the pump according to table no.  2.
                             Table No. 2  (Alignment limits for Coupling)

    Lube Wt (l b)

    0.19

    Operating Alignment Limits (inch)

    < 0.005"

    Gap of coupling  (inch)

    0.125"

    Cover Bolt Torque  (1b – in)

    200

     

  7. Change lubrication of coupling (WT= 0.19LB)

  8. Cheek the temperature of bearing house (300-4005 F) and grease

  9. Change the lubrication oil and grease from externally lubricated timing gears and bearing.

    - Lubrication oil:   Mobil gear 629 03 or Gulf lube HD 30
    - Grease:               Mobil grease no. 2 or Gulf crown grease 2

Before Isolated the Pump mechanically and electrically  

  1. Check any noise or vibration in Pump  

  2. Check the direction of the Motor rotation.

  3. Check leakage from any Gasket or any O-ring.

  4. Check Pressure in delivery of the Pump's delivery.

  5. Check the Temperature of Bearing house it must be less than 400 5F

After Isolated the Pump mechanically and electrically fgvhgf

  1. Check  the impeller and casing from mechanical wear and measure the clearances by dismantle the Pump.

  2. Check the bearing in the pump.

  3. Prevent the leakage from gasket or O  ring by renewed it.

  4. Check the couplingوs pins and change grease of coupling.

  5. Retighten all bolts connected pump and motor with foundation.

  6. Check the alignment between pump and motor.

  7. Change the packing if leakage is heavy didn't prevented by tighten the gland bolts.

Operation of the Pump

  1. Operate the pump by coordination with the shift engineer.

  2. Ensure that the pump is OK and record the readings of pressure, temp., vibration,  according to the technical specification table at introduction, and compare with the previous reading.
