Introduction

This document outlines the Isusu car pickup inspection steps I3, which must be carried out every 5000 km.

The car technical data:
   Type: Isusu Car Pickup,    No. of Engine: 432,    Chassis : 7156784 ,    Wight : I,46  Ton 

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Alaa Eldin, tested by eng. Aiman Mohamed Shabana, revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Samir Abdel Gilel, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Nageb and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talkha PS

Safety Precaution 

  • Safety Belt must be on.

  • Fire Chimney mast be on.

  • Jake for tires.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set .

  • Cleaning fluid, Jake for tires,

  • Rag wipe, screw drivers.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Change oil filter

  2. Check front disc brake pads and discs.

  3. Check rear brake lining and dram wear.

  4. Check that the fan belt gives a deflection 10 mm when the middle part of the beltis depressed with a force of 10 kg.

  5. Check that the brake and clutch fluid level

  6. Check clutch paddle free play and height
    a) Free play 5  to 15 mm,   b) Height 178  to 188 mm

  7. Check brake pedal free , height and allowance
    a) Free play 6  to 10 mm,   b) Height 174  to  184mm,    c) Allowance  75  mm

  8. Check the battery and apply thin coat of grease into the battery terminals to prevent corrosion

  9. Clean turbo charger.

  10. Check inflation pressure for tires:    - Front 28 psi,    - Rear 60 psi

  11. Clean nozzles.
