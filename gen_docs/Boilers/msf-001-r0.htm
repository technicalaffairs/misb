Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes, … etc.

  • The filter must be isolated mechanically by closing:
           - Input and output valves of mazot.
          - Steam and condensate valve.
          - Drain the filter by opening the sidement valve.
          - De-aerated filter by opening de-aeration valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

 

Work to be Carried Out

 

 Dismantle the filter sieve insert from the filter body

  1. Check closing of the input and output mazot valve of the filter (A, B).

  2. Check closing of steam and condensat valve (20) & (21).

  3. Check opening the de-aeration valves (19).

  4. Check opening the drain valves (22).

  5. Check the pressure in the differential pressure gauge and it must be Zero.

  6. Screw off nuts (16) to remove cover of the filter (17).

  7. Take out the filter insert from the hand (15).

Disassembly of the filter sieve insert

  1. Clean the outside surfaces of the filter insert by using steam.

  2. Screw off nuts (13), (14) to take out the part (4), also take out the internal sieve (8), it covers (2), and spindle (5).

  3. Screw off nuts (23) to dismantle the top cover (L) of the filter insert and then you can take out the intermediate sieve (7)

  4. Screw off nuts (11) to dismantle the intermediate cover (3) from bottom cover (10) and the take out the outside sieve (6) and spindles (9).

Clean and repair filter insert parts

  1. Clean every part of the filter insert specially filters sieves.

  2. Check the sieves and repair any damage part.

Assembly of the filter sieve insert

  1. Assembly every part in reverse sequence of disassembly.

Assembly of the filter's insert inside the filter body

  1. Put the filtert inside the filter body.

  2. Check the gasket (18) and renewed it if needed.

  3. Close filter body by cover (17).

  4. Tight the nuts (16) in cross sequence.

  5. Close de-aeration valves (19).

  6. Close drain valve (22).

  7. Open the steam and condensate valve.

  8. Open the mazot input valve and check any leakage.

  9. Take the air inside the filter by open the valves (19) and close it
    directly when mazot is come out.

  10. Open the out put valve of mazot and then the filter is in operation.
