Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the personal protective equipment must used as safety shoes, safety helmet, earplug, and gloves pairs...etc

  • The motor must be out of service.

  • Circuit breaker of the motor in test position .

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set, Air Blower, Megger device

  • Greasing machine, Multimeter.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

  • Brushes, (soft brushes)

Work to be Carried out

  1. Measure and record the insulation of motor winding, repair if required

  2. Clean the body of the motor and ventilation holes using air blower and brushes

  3. Check all the tightens of the power and control circuits connections .

  4. Grease the roller bearing or replace it if need .

  5. Measure and record the dc resistance of motor winding.

  6. Measure and record at the isulation of motor winding after maintenance and befor running the motor .

  7. Connect the power source to the motor

  8. Run the motor and check the ball bearing or roller bearing housing temperature not more than 80oC

  9. If the motor has be changed its roller bearing we should check lubrication and alignment if temperature exceed this limit .

  10. Check vibration or noises, tighten the bolts if required

  11. Check operation of the motor from local and remote operation on  test  position

  12. Check all protection of the motor by protection group ( O.C. , S.C., U.V. )
