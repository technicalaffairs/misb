Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must performed on weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block.

This motor is used for the following equipments: 

Boiler feed water pump, desalination water supply pump, fire fighting motor, closed cooling motor, service water pump, circulating water pump, condensate extraction pump

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet ... etc

  • Motor is out of service for some works if necessary

  • High temperature may arise under operating conditions on the motor surfaces so that touching should be prevented or avoided.

Tools and Equipment

  • Kit, safety and healthy equipment

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check that there is no abnormal noise or vibration

  2. Check the oil level for upper bearing

  3. Check the air filter is cleaned
