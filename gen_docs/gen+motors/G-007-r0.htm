Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The technical specification of the main generator: Rated voltage is 17.5 kv & rated stator current 7560 A  rated frequency 50 HZ & rated speed 100 rpm rated power 175/206 MW/MVA

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes,...etc

  • Switch off the CB (B-1G) 15.75kv

  • Switch of the unit isolator (P-1G) 15.75kv

  • Switch off the VT of (main generator - Aux.) for the unit.

  • Switch off the automatics F 403 (D.C control of the start / stop unit operation), 72D2 - 7 (DC auto. for control of CB, unit filed, BF protection) 72 A-11, (AC auto. control supply circuit for auditing rotor of Aux. generator insulation device), 72 A - 13 (AC auto. for control supply circuit of auditing rotor of main generator insulation device), 14AG (AC auto for control circuit of for the unit disconnector)

  • Switch of the AJB (on/of switch for Aux. generator)

  • Shunt rheostats (the resistance for control valtage of Aux. generator) in max. resistance value position

  • Switch on the earthling for main generator (Z - 1G), Aux.G(Z-1BG) for the unit.

  • Stoppers on (blocking for servo motor), close valve no.7 (controller in the entrance pressure in two direction open/close gates)

  • Switch on the two supplies of (high pressure pump)

  • Presence of industrial safety supervisor

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable air blower, plastic brush

  • Set of common, screwdrivers, and cleaning material (rag, cloth)

  • Benzene

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle one iron plate above aux. generator insulation

  2. Dismantle bus bars of Aux. generator and put insulation wedges between the terminals

  3. Dismantle the brushes of (main & aux. generator & exc. generator) for the unit

  4. Dismantle bus bars of VT for main generator and (isolator & earth) of the reator

  5. Perform HV test for main and aux. generator.

  6. Take sample oil for chemical test

  7. Check the brushes for exciter gen and measure the air gap for exciter generator.

  8. Clean the filters by used air vacuum and clean the slip rings

  9. Clean the main and Aux. poles for exciter generator.

  10. Check the brushes and slips rings for main & aux. Generator

  11. Clean the surface of the rotor for the main generator and clean surface of poles from top.

  12. Check the connection tight between the poles.

  13. Retighten all the bus bars for the unit.
