Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, gloves pairs, breathing mask, safety glass … etc .

  • Take care that the unit at service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

    •

  • New brushes

  • Blower

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the brush and slip ring of exciter.

  2. Check for correct operation of exciter room air condition and ventilation fan.

  3. Clean exciter room floor.

  4. Check air filter of exciter panels and clean it.

  5. Check all abnormal cases inside exciter room.

  6. Record current and voltage value of exciter as in table (1).
