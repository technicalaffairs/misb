Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be carried out every five years as indicated by Y5 in the titled block

The generator of the steam turbine as any generator is used for generating the electricity. The generator type is brushless (self – induced).

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs … etc.

  • Be sure that the turbine is out of service and turning gear is off.

  • Be sure that the all electrical & instrumentation cables are completely isolated.

  • Be sure that the fire fighting system is isolated.

 

           

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Set of wires.

  • Cleaning articles.

  • Fillers, Vibrometers, dial gauges and slip gauges.

  • Special tools for clamping and extracting the generator rotor and the exciter.

  • Hydraulic pump for unbolting the LP / Gen coupling.

Work to be Carried out

Before disassembly, you must record all clearances and measurements of the unit during normal operation.  

Disassembly

  1. Uncouple the LP / GEN coupling. Use a hydraulic pump to unbolt the coupling bolts.

  2. Measure the length of the coupling bolts.

  3. Check the alignment between the LP / GEN coupling and record them (loosen coupling).

  4. Sling and lift the four coolers to be cleaned.

  5. Unbolt and raise the upper and lower part of the bearings No, 5 & 6 cover.

  6. Measure the clearances of the BRGs No, 5 & 6 seals and record them.

  7. Measure the clearances between the blades of the stator diaphragms.

  8. Check the clearances between the rotor and the stator for exciter.

  9. Extract the exciter using special fixture for that.

  10. Remove the screws & bolts fixing the bearings No, 5 & No, 6 bearing –block caps on the lower bearing –block.

  11. Disassemble the two bearings blocks caps fitted with the rotor  groundings.

  12. Remove the screws &bolts fixing journal – bearing.

  13. Dismantle the journal - bearing No, 6 as follow:

    1. Remove the journal bearing strap.

    2. Dismantle the thermocouple.

    3. Remove the upper half journal – bearing.

  14. Measure the clearances of the journal bearings and record them.

  15. Dismantle the oil supply connection from the rotor jacking.

  16. Remove the lower half journal – bearing.

  17. Disassemble the bearing no. 5 as mentioned for bearing no. 6

  18. Dismantle the lower part of the bearing no. 6 pedestal as follow:

    1. Sling the lower part of the bearing by using two eyebolts in the distinct position. 

    2. Lift the lower part of the bearing.

  19. Dismantle the lower part of the bearing No, 5 as mentioned for the bearing No, 6.

  20. Extract the generator rotor using special fixture for that.

Inspection

Cleaning

  1. Degrease bearing blocks with non corrosive product and rags.

  2. Clean carefully the GEN inside area with adequate products and solvents.

  3. Clean the four coolers by chemical agents.

  4. Clean all the bolts.

Checking the Bearing Blocks

  1. Check the journal bearings clearances.

  2. Check the sliding planes condition.

  3. Check the journal bearings surface condition.

  4. Check the journal bearings mating - faces.

Assembly

  1. Insert the generator rotor using its fixture (special tool) till it rests on its place.

  2. Make the blue test between the bearings pedestals (No, 5 & 6) and the generator body.

  3. Fix the lower part of the bearings No, 5 & 6 pedestals in the body.

  4. Assemble the lower part of the bearing No, 5 as follow:

    1. Pull down the lower part of the bearing in its grooved place.

    2. Tight the screws fixing the lower part with the pedestal.

  5. Assemble the lower part of the bearing No. 6 as mentioned for the bearing no. 5.

  6. Measure the clearances of the journal bearings and record them.

  7. Put the upper part of the journal bearing.

  8. Tight the screws fixing the two halves.

  9. Fix the journal bearing straps.

  10. Mantle the oil supply connection from the rotor jacking.

  11. Mantle the thermocouple

  12. Assemble the upper part of journal bearing no. 6

  13. Assemble the upper part of the bearing no. 5 casing as follow:

    1. Put the bearing upper casing on the lower half.

    2. Tight the screws that assemble the two halves of the casing.

  14. Assemble the upper part of the bearing No, 6 casing as mentioned in the bearing no. 5.

  15. Check the alignment between LP / Generator shafts with slip gauges and dial gauges (loosen coupling).

  16. Tight the LP / GEN coupling bolts.

  17. Measure the length of the coupling bolts to determine the elongation.

  18. Check the rotor runout.

  19. Insert the exciter using special fixture for that.

  20. Measure the clearances between the blades of the stator diaphragms.

  21. Measure the clearances between the rotor and the stator for exciter.

  22. Mount new oil seal stripes (upper & lower parts) on BRG, 5 , 6.

  23. Measure the clearances of the oil seal stripes.

  24. Bolt the upper and lower part of the bearings No, 5 & 6 cover

  25. Put down the four coolers in their places.
