Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required

  • Before starting any work on the machine, check that it is switched off and blocked up for switching on again.

  • The unit should be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness, cleaning tools as cloth.... etc

  • Lifting device, press-on and detaching device.

  • AVO meter and insulation test device

    • .

  • Oil for bearing.

Work to be Carried Out

Changing the Bearings (A)

Disassembly of Bearings (Drive end side) (A1)

Removal in vertical Motor Position

  1. Draw off coupling.

  2. Remove grease- slide.

  3. Disconnect bearing thermometer.

  4. Dismantle lubrication pipe.

  5. Loosen bearing cap screws and remove cap.

  6. Loosen surplus grease ejection disc locking pin and unscrew disc.

  7. Loosen bearing bracket bolts.

  8. Push off together bearing and bearing bracket by means of two bracket bolts via threaded holes in bracket and remove it. Inside bearing race of roller bearing and inside bearing cap remain on shaft. Inside bearing race draw off only if bearing will be renewed.

  9. Remove inside bearing cap.

  10. Heat up inside bearing race to about 80-120 oC and pull off race.

  11. Push out bearing from bearing bracket.

Bearing Assembly (Drive end side) (A2)

Assembly in vertical Motor Position

  1. The mounting of the bearing has to be done with the most possible care. Pay attention to particular tidiness. The fittings of bearings, spigot, surplus grease ejection disc and bearing caps have to be cleaned before mounting. Grease the fittings of bearing with molycote and fill the bearing up to 2/3 with fresh grease according to the grease instructions before mounting.

  2. Press the roller bearing into the bearing bracket 

  3. Mount the inner bearing cap on shaft .

  4. Mount inside bearing race on the shaft. Heat up bearing race in an oil bath to about 70-90 oC and place it on shaft.

  5. Assembly bearing and bearing bracket very carefully.

  6. Mount bearing bracket bolts.

  7. Mount surplus grease ejection disc and fix locking pin.

  8. Mount outside bearing cap.

  9. Place grease slide.

  10. Connect bearing thermometer.

  11. Mount lubrication pipe and fill with grease.

  12. Grease the bearing.

Disassembly of Bearings (Thrust Bearing Side) (A3)

Removal in vertical Motor Position

  1. Support motor shaft axially.

  2. Disconnect bearing thermometer

  3. Loosen bearing cap bolts and remove cap.

  4. Unlock and unscrew slotted nut.

  5. Remove locking washer.

  6. Loosen bearing bracket bolts.

  7. Push off together bearing and bearing bracket by means of two bracket bolts via threaded holes in bracket and remove by lifting device.

  8. Loosen bearing cap bolts and remove inside bearing cap, surplus grease ejection disc and grease guide ring.

  9. Push out bearing from bearing bracket.

Bearing Assembly (Thrust Bearing Side) (A4)

Assembly in vertical Motor Position

  1. As per (A2 point 1)

  2. Mount inside bearing cap to bearing bracket.

  3. Assembly bearing bracket on stator housing and mount bearing bracket bolts.

  4. Place surplus grease ejection disc on shaft.

  5. Place grease guide ring.

  6. Mount angular-contact bearing individually. Use a suitable size pipe able to press inner and outer race of the bearing at once and use the threaded hole in the center of the shaft as support for a threaded bolt for proper mounting.

  7. Place locking washer.

  8. Mount and lock slotted nut.

  9. Mount outside bearing cap.

  10. Connect bearing thermometer.

  11. Remove axial support of motor shaft.

  12. Grease the bearing.

Disassembly and Assembly (B)       

Disassembly of Motor Uncoupled (B1)

Disassembly in Vertical Motor Position

  1. Remove flange type bearing bracket and roller bearing as per (A1).

  2. Remove thrust bearing bracket and thrust bearings as per (A3).

  3. Loosen fan bolts and remove fan.

  4. Loosen air guide bolts and remove air guide.

  5. Lift rotor carefully out of stator. Use a proper eye bolt screwed in the threaded hole at the center of the shaft. Take particular care for the stator winding when rotor is handled.

Assembly (B2)

Assembly in Vertical Motor Position

  1. The assembly of motor is done in the reverse order to the sequence of disassembly described as per (B1) observing the bearing assembly instructions as per (A2) and (A4).

  2. Clean the heat exchanger.

  3. Measure the insulation resistance for stator winding.

  4. Clean the inside terminal box, and retighten bolts& earthing bolts.
