Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The technical specification of the main generator: Rated voltage 15.75kv & rated stator current 7560A. Rated frequency 50 Hz & Rated Speed 100 r.p.m. Rated power 175 / 206 MW / MVA

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass

  • Switch off the CB (B-1G) 15.75 kv figure (1).

  • Switch off the unit isolator (P-1G) 15.75 kv

  • Switch off the VT of (M.G-AUX.G) for the unit

  • Switch off the automatic`s (F403 DC control of the start / stop unit operation), 72 D2-7(DC auto. for control of CB, unit filed and B.F protection), 72A111,(A.C auto. control supply circuit for auditing rotor of AUX. G insulation device), 72A-13 (A.C auto. for control supply circuit of auditing rotor of M.G insulation device), 14 AG (AC auto. for control circuit of for the unit. Disconnector)

  • Switch off the AJB (on/off switch for AUX. G)

  • Shunt rheostats (the resistance for control voltage of AUX. G) in max. resistance value position

  • Switch on the earths for (M.G (Z-1G), AUX.G(Z-1BG) of the unit

  • Stoppers on (blocking for servo motor), close valve no. 7(controller in the entrance pressure in two direction open / close gates)

  • Switch on the two supplies of (high pressure pump)

  • Presence of industrial safety supervisor .

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common, screwdrivers, and cleaning material (rag, cloth)

  • Portable air blower, plastic brush, Benzene

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle one iron plate above aux. generator

  2. Dismantle bus bars of aux. generator and put insulation wedges between the terminals

  3. Dismantle the brushes of (M.G & AUX.G & exc. G) for the unit

  4. Dismantle bus bars of V.T, reactor isolator and reactor earth for M.G.

  5. Perform H.V test for (M.G. & AUX. G)

  6. Dismantle bus bars of (M.G) and star point

  7. Dismantle four-iron plate, for (M.G) plus two plates for carastavena and two plates above M.G windinga

  8. Take sample oil for chemical test

  9. Check the brushes for exciter gen. and measure the air gap for exciter gen.

  10. Clean the air filters by used air vacuum and clean the slip rings

  11. Clean the main and aux. poles for D.C gen.

  12. Check the brushes and slips rings for (M.G & AUX.) gen.

  13. Clean the surface of the rotor for the (M.G) and clean upper parts of poles surface.

  14. Check the connection tight between the poles

  15. Measure the air gap between the rotor and stator for five poles for each quarter

  16. Clean the surface of stator of M.G

  17. Dismantle two M.G poles for good cleaning and mantle them after that

  18. Perform blackout and prob. test for stator of M.G.

  19. Retighten all the bus bars for the unit
