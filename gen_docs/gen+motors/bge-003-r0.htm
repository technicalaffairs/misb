Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The Brush Dimension 25x32x64 mm,     Rated Current Density 9.5 A/cm2,     Brush Holders R2532

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc.

  • The unit must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • New brushes, megger device, clamp ammeter, and digital voltmeter.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Remove the brushes of generator, exciter and earthing brushes from their holders

  2. Remove brushes holders

  3. Clean brushes slip ring contact

  4. Before and after cleaning brushes slip ring contact measure the insulation resistance between slip ring and earth by using megger device 500 V.D.C approximately ≥ 10 MΩ

  5. Clean brushes holders.

  6. Before and after cleaning brushes holders measure the insulation resistance between holders and earth by using megger device 500 V.D.C "approximately ≥10 MΩ "(recording in table 2)

  7. Put holders in their position and tightening it

  8. Check the clearance between brushes holders and slip ring "approximately 3 m.m"

  9. Replace the brush by a new one if its height falls to 25 mm.

  10. Check brushes must move freely in the holders and must sit to the rings by their full surface.

  11. Check brush pressure in permanently given by the holding-down system of the holders.

  12. Check connection of cables of brushes must be tightened.

  13. Put the unit in service.

  14. Inspect the brush vibration.           

  15. Inspect the sparking brushes.

  16. Measure the current in each brush and recording it in table (1) :if the current in any brush less than 10 A (according to the recommendation from specialist), replaced it"

  17. Measure the leakage AC voltage between bearings of generator as fig (1) "approximately ≥ 2 V.A.C.
