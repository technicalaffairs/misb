Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I3, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

The exciter data: 2.42 MVA, 525 Vac, Y connection, 2420 A, PF=0.5, 50HZ, 3000 RPM

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Aziz El Bagoury, tested by eng. Enas Azmy and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Saad Bassuony accepted by eng. Aly Abdel Whab general director and approved by eng. Fathy Ragab head sector of Talkha PS

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, gloves pairs, breathing mask, safety glass … etc .

  • The unit must out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • New brushes.

  • Blower, Megger device and digital voltmeter.

  • Spray.

  • Fine cloth and brushes for cleaning purposes.

Work to be Carried Out

Exciter

  1. Check for correct operation of exciter room air condition and ventilation fan.

  2. Clean exciter room floor.

  3. Clean air filter of exciter panels.

  4. Check interconnection terminals.

  5. Remove thyristor units clean it and measure its insulation resistance by using megger device 500 V D.C  approximately ≥ 1 M Ω to be sure the results of cleaning .

  6. Change polarity of alternator (Q6).

  7. Check push buttons, signal lamps, fuses, CB, and auxiliary relay.

  8. Check signals of warning, limitation 1st, limitation 2nd, and shut down at control room and JVP panel (open circuit test)

  9. Check operation of regulator, start stop logic, phase control, diagnostic, RG3 and RG4.

  10. Clean the insulators of output of exciter “ Y  3Ф  525 V A.C”.

  11. Check the protection device.

Rotor :

  1. Remove both rotor and stator of exciter.

  2. Before and after cleaning slip ring of exciter measure the insulation resistance between slip ring and earth by using megger device 500V D.C approximately ≥ 3 MΩ  table (1).

  3. Measure the deeps of slip ring according the reading in table (2) will state for grading or not.

  4. Clean the surface , guid of air and the winding of rotor.

  5. Check any cracks in rotor by using ultra sonic test.

  6. Measure the ohmic  resistance of the winding by using battery 12 V as in figure (1) compare with the standard value.

  7. Check the bearing insulation.

  8. Check the protection device.

Stator

  1. Check the measuring sensors located in the winding.

  2. Measure the insulating resistance of the winding by using megger device 500 V D.C as in table (3).

  3. Test the tightness of the winding and the wiring of the machine.

  4. Check the condition of the stiffeners in the end parts and the outlets.

  5. Check the bushings of the winding replacement of their packing.

  6. Check the wedging in slots ; re-wedging if necessary recording as in table (4).

  7. Check on a part of the distribution system of cooling water in the machine and be sure that cooling is suitable.

  8. Check the protection device.
