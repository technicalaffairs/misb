Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedure for boiler motors which must be performed in :
* Comb, Inspection             (every 12000hr.)
* Hot gas path inspection     (every 24000 hr)
* Major overhaul inspection (every 48000 hr)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety, healthy, shoes

  • Circuit breaker must be disconnected

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger (500) volt

  • Voltmeter

  • Complete maintenance tool set

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect circuit breaker and rack it out

  2. Turn off terminal voltage

  3. Dismounting motor from base & coupling

  4. Remove the fan cover

  5. Dismantle the rotor from the motor

  6. Check bearing & lubricate bearing, remove it if this important

  7. Check 3 phase coils & clean it

  8. Measure insulation between coils & coils with earth

  9. Lubricate bearing & mount 3 phase coil, cooling fan

  10. Mount cooling fan cover

  11. Mount the terminal voltage & rosemount

  12. Test the operation of motor with no load (the amb. of motor must be less than half)

  13. Check the protection devices
