Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Safety fences with caution marks surrounding the work area must be used.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes earplug, and gloves pairs.

  • The motor must be out of service.

  • Circuit breaker in test position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set, Air blower.

  • Greasing machine.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

  • Brushes, (soft brushes).

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Ensure that all fixing bolts are secured.

  2. Ensure that electrical connection are tightened.

  3. Check the isolators and clean them.

  4. Check all protection devices of the motor by protection group.
