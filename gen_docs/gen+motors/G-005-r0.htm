Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet, earplug  .... etc

  • Generator must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Megger 5000V, Grease injector

    •

Work to be carried out

Every Six Months

  1. Check exciter end generator bearing and shaft seal insulation.

  2. Re-greasing devices of vapor exhauster shaft seals with grease.

  3. Drain liquid from compressed air filter.

  4. Check slip ring surface. (At rotor standstill)

Annually

  1. Measure insulation resistance.

  2. Check shut off valve of gas supply for function and free movement.

  3. Check gas supply line, shut off valves and solenoids on gas tightness.
