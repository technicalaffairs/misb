Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required

  • Before starting any work on the machine, check that it is switched off and blocked up for switching on again.

  • The unit should be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness, cleaning tools as cloth.... etc.

  • Crane, press ON, detaching device, properly fitting spanners.

  • AVO meter and insulation testing device.

  • Oil for bearing.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visual examination of lower bearing sleeves, change oil every 2.5 years if no analysis is available.

  2. Visual examination and measure insulation resistance for stator winding.

  3. Clean interior, retighten screws for terminal boxer earthing.

  4. Retighten bolts for complete motor.

  5. Change the bearing oil.

  6. General cleaning.
