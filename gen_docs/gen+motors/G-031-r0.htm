Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I4, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

The generator data: 277MVA, 15.75KV, 10.173KA, PF=0.8, 50HZ, 3000 rpm, Y connection

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Aziz El Bagoury, tested by eng. Enas Azmy and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Saad Bassuony, accepted by eng. Aly Abdel Wahab general director and approved by eng. Fathy Ragab head sector of Talkha PS.

This document accepted by eng. Samir Abd El Gilil: EDEPC inspection & quality control general director, MPIS coordinator, and certified by accountant Mahmoud Soliman EDEPC admin & financial inspection & quality control's general director and supervisor of inspection & quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment.

  • The unit must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • New brushes, Air blower, and Spray

  • Digital voltmeter, megger, clamp ammeter

  • Fine cloth and brushes for cleaning purposes

Work to be carried out

Rotor

  1. Remove both rotor and stator of generator.

  2. Before and after cleaning slip ring of rotor measure the insulation resistance between slip ring and earth by using megger device 500Vdc approximately ≥ 3 MΩ, table 1.

  3. Measure the deeps of slip ring according the reading in table 2 will state for grading or not.

  4. Clean the surface, guid of air and the winding of rotor.

  5. Check any cracks in rotor by using ultra sonic test.

  6. Measure the ohmic  resistance of the winding by using battery 12V as in figure 1, compare with the standard value.

  7. Check the bearing insulation by using megger device 500Vdc.

  8. Check the protection device.

Stator

  1. Check the measuring sensors located in the winding.

  2. Check on a part of the distribution system of cooling “water + hydrogen” and be sure that the pressure is suitable.

  3. Measure the insulating resistance of the winding by using megger device 5000 V D.C as table (3).

  4. Test the tightness of the winding and the wiring of the machine.

  5. Check the condition of the stiffeners in the end parts and the outlets.

  6. Check the bushings of the winding replacement of their packing.

  7. Check the wedging in slots ; re-wedging if necessary recording as in table (4).

  8. Check the protection device.

  9. Measure the leakage current of the stator winding by inject high voltage 32 KV D.C for 60 second and record the results as in table (5).

  10. Record the time and date with remark.
