Introduction

This document outlines the equipment maintenance inspection on the 6 kv CB and 380v CB which must be performed every 1500 hours of operation. This procedure was applied in ABU QIR power plant since 1985 and the finial draft is issued in August 2004    

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Hassan Toto,  accepted by eng. Khalil Aly Khalil general director of Abu Kir PS, approved by eng. Ahmed Taha Hashim Head Sector of  Abu Kir PS, and certified by eng. Mona Ahmad El Sharkawy: WDEPC Inspection and Quality Control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety healthy, safety shoes

  • Insure that the motors are switched off

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger

  • Injection test set

  • Compressor for cleaning

Work to be Carried Out

6 kv Pump Motor

  1. General cleaning

  2. Clean the cooling paths using the compressed air or brushes

  3. Inspection and tightening

  4. Check the earth cable is in good fixation

  5. Check the connection of the motor is in good fixation state

  6. Measure the temperature of the stator

  7. Check the protection devices

  8. Measure the winding isolation

380 volts Motor

  1. Check the connection tightening

  2. Check the earth cable is in a good holding

  3. Check the connection cobles of the motor

  4. Measure the temperature of the stator

  5. Check the protection devices

  6. Measure the winding insulation

  7. General cleaning

6 KV Cell

  1. Clean the internal parts

  2. Grease the earth breaker

  3. Lubricate mechanical parts of the cell foundation including the following:

  • The cell internal automatic connecting dampers

  • The earth connecting switch control

  • Internal axis - rods - arms

  • Stopping part of movable contact  

380 V Cell

  • Ensure that the 380 V cell is disconnected and out in test position .
