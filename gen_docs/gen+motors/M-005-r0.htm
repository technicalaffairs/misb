Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The technical specification of the motor: Rated power 40 kw, Δ/Y 220 / 380 v

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass

  • Switch of the AC power supply

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • Cleaning materials (cloth - benzene), grease for bearing

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect the leads of the motor and label them

  2. Clean the motor from dust, clean it by benzene

  3. After dismantling the motor, clean the inner parts from all foreign matters

  4. Blow the motor with compressed air of 2-3 atm pressure

  5. Inspect the guide bearing and replace them if necessary

  6. Measure the resistance of the insulation of motor winding

  7. Check the DC resistance and compare it with the reference one

  8. After assembling the motor, put it into work and check its rotation
