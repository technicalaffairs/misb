Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, breathing mask, ... etc.

  • The unit must be out service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger (500 – 5000) volt.

  • Multimeter, Air blower, Cleaning solvent 

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Contact resistance tester.
