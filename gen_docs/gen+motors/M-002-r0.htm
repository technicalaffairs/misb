Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every two months as indicated by M2 in the title block

The motor is check while being running in service needing no motor stoppage or isolation

The motor working condition indication parameters as current and temperature is monitored to predict and record performance change and take any necessary corrective action as iubrication, bearing check or change.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ahmed Fawzy Badry, tested by eng. Nabeel saad Ibrahem at 01 May 2003, accepted by general director eng. Shawqy Mahmoud Attala, and approved by eng. Mohamed Mahdey Rezq Alnshar Head Sector of Sidi Kirir 1&2 Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

    •

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as helmet, and safety shoes ...etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Dry cloth, thermometer

  • Electrical equipment kit, device to detect noises

  • New filters if necessary

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Record the ambient temperature.

  2. Read and record motor temperature at bearing side and fan side and compare with recommended values

  3. Check for unusual noise from the motor.

  4. Clean the outside motor surface.

  5. Read the current of the motor and ensure it is within normal range

  6. Clean or change the filter if there is a signal of ∆ P or as the manufacturer recommendations,
