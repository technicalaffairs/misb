Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained , qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc).

  • The operator, who is inside the stator, is deprived from any metallic or magnetic object (kees, phones, tape measure, chain …).

  • Protect the inferior part of endwinding and core, with a rubber carpet or plastic film which will move with a make progress expertise .

  • Supply of necessity, by intermediate of separate cubicle, the eventual appliance, and lighting put on work inside the core stator.

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger (500 – 5000) volt,  

  • Complete maintenance tool set,  

    • Air blower, Cleaning solvent

  • Contact resistance tester,

  • Multimeter
