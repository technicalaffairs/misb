Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I2, which must be every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block .

The exciter data   2.42 MVA,  525 Vac, Y connection, 2420 A , PF=0.5 , 50HZ, 3000 RPM

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, gloves pairs, breathing mask, safety glass … etc .

  • The unit must be out of service .

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

    •

  • New brushes

  • Blower, Megger device and digital voltmeter.

  • Spray.

  • Fine cloth and brushes for cleaning purposes

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check for correct operation of exciter room air condition and ventilation fan.

  2. Clean exciter room floor.

  3. Clean air filter of exciter panels.

  4. remove brush holders and cleaning slip ring contact.

  5. Before and after cleaning slip ring of exciter measure the insulation resistance between slip ring and earth by using megger device 500 Vdc approximately ≥ 3 MΩ  table (1) to be sure the results of cleaning.

  6. Check interconnection  terminals.

  7. Remove thyristor units clean it and measure its insulation resistance by using megger device 500 Vdc approximately ≥ 1 M Ω .

  8. Change polarity of alternator (Q6).

  9. Check push buttons, signal lamps, fuses, and auxiliary relay.

  10. Check signals of warning, limitation 1st , limitation 2nd , and shut down at control room and JVP panel (open circuit test).

  11.  Check operation of regulator, start stop logic, phase control, diagnostic, RG3 and RG4.

  12. Clean the insulators of output of exciter “ Y, 3ph, 525 Vac”.
