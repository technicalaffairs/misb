Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety Helmet ... etc.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required

  • Before starting any work on the machine, check that it is switched off and blocked up for switching on again

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

    •

  • Tool set for tightness, cleaning tools as cloth.... etc

  • Press-on and detaching device.

  • AVO meter.

  • Air blower.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Inspect the electrical cubical and general cleaning by air blower: Air pressure less than 3bar.

  2. Check the electric motors.

  3. Clean the hydrogen unit.
