Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2 which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The technical specification of the turbine KVA = 2155, F = 50, V = 3150, A=395, Speed=1000 rpm, Vexci = 60 v, Iexci = 6.4A

The first draft initiated by eng. Abd El Mohsen Mohamed; Manager of Nag Hammady Hydraulic PS, and certified by eng. Kamal Ragheb Haleem HPGC "MPIS Coordinator & QC general Director"

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

  • Stop the unit and CB out, and earthing transformer

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard personal safety and healthy equipment as Safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc

  • Set of wrenches

  • Cleaning material, and blower

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the outer generator surface

  2. Check that the earthing terminals are well connected, and check the condition of the earthing brushes

  3. Change the bearing oil

  4. Check the condition of the generator bearing

  5. Check the condition of the generator coupling

  6. Check and test vibration, bearing generator temperature 

  7. Cleaning the air gap between rotor and stator by using blower

  8. Check the tight main generator cable connections

  9. Check and test the protection equipment (REG 316)
