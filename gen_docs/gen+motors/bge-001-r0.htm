Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a daily bases as indicated by D1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc.

  • The unit must be on service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • New brushes.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect the brush vibration.

  2. Inspect the sparking brushes.

  3. Inspect brush holders and slip rings.

  4. Check brushes must move freely in the holders and must sit to the rings by their full surface.

  5. Check brush pressure in permanently given by the holding-down system of the holders.

  6. Check connection of cables of brushes must be tightened.

  7. The brush is replaced by new one if its height falls to 25 mm.
