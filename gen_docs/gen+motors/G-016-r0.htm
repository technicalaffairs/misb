Introduction

This document lists the maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft was initiated by eng. Hussein Hashem Mohamed, tested by eng. Naser El Sayed Abd EL-Aziz, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman Selim, approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel-Baset  head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc

  • Isolate the generator out of service

  • The generator circuit breaker is switched off

  • The generator field breaker is switched off

  • The field flashing AC and DC are switched off

Tools and Equipment

  • Complet kit of tools

  • Measuring instrument (Ampero-meter)

  • Dry rags, Alcholl

  • Compressed, Special marior

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check and clean excitation transformer components.

  2. Check and clean the cooling fans of thyristor and their alarm system.

  3. Check and clean the equipment of the thyristor pannels using comprissed air.

  4. Clean the air filter of thyristor pannels using compressed air

  5. Check gate terminal of the thyristor with the aid of special marrior to ensure the proper terminal connrction.

  6. Check and clean auto voltage regulator component.

  7. Check and clean filed breaker contacts and measure the clearance between moving contact and fixed contact and readjust it if necessary.

  8. Check and clean the discharge resistance contacts and measure the clearance between moving contact and fixed contact and readjust it if necessary.

  9. Check and clean field flashing breaker contact.

  10. Check all fuses for Thyristors.

  11. Check and test excitation over current relay (two stages).

  12. Check all protection signals for excitation system with the unit control board.

  13. Operate the field breaker (ON - OFF) for test while checking the performance of the discharge resistance contact synchronising with the main field breaker contacts operation.
