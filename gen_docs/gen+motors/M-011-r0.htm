Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety & healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses etc.

  • Isolate the motor: electrical and mechanical.

Tools and Equipment

  • A complete tool set.

  • Grease, lubricating oil, Plastic brush.

  • Megger, Voltmeter.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the motor by dry compressed air.

  2. Check the junction box terminals connection.

  3. Lubricate ball and roller bearing using grease, oil lubricated.
