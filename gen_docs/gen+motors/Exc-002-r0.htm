Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc

  • Switch down the generator and exciter.

  • Put the circuit breaker of the generator out of its position.

  • Put ground on the generator winding.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy tools as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Blower and brushes

  • DC 500 V megger

  • Special tools

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purpose

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the insulation for the AC exciter winding (rotating armature, magnetic poles) must be more than 1mΩ at DC 500 V megger.

  2. Cleaning the silicon rectifiers, heat sink, condenser, resistor and rectifier ring.

  3. Check if the clamping bolts of lead connections are loose.

  4. Check the silicon rectifiers under the condition that the main generator field winding is detached from the rotary rectifiers (remove the leads shown in the figure).

  5. Check the reverse resistance of each silicon rectifier (measure the reverse resistance between B and heat sink and between heat sink A and C in turn an shown in the figure 1. the normal value for the reverse resistance of each silicon rectifier is more than 0.1 MΩ by 500 V megger.

Return the Generator into Service

  1. Disconnected ground on the generator winding

  2. The circuit breaker of the generator on the service position.

  3. Lift up the safety fence with caution mark surrounding the work area.

  4. Finish the work permit.
