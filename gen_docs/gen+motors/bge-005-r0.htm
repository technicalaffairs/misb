Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified or directly supervised by any one

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes

  • Complete tool set, air blower

  • Soft brush seating stone

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Remove the cover holder

  2. Remove brusher from their holder

  3. Clean brush and holder

  4. Clean brush clip contact

  5. Replace brush before the pigtail in contact with top brush box

  6. Check the clearance between the holder and shaft at least 1/8 inch

Notice:    There are daily inspection for brushes and replaced the corrosion brusher
