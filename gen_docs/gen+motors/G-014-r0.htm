Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued before start work and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, safety clothes and breathing mask when cleaning. ... etc,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • The generator in case of shut down.

  • The circuit breaker of generator on test position.

  • Put ground on the generator windings.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes and complete tools

  • Special tools for extracting the rotor.

  • Voltage indicator, portable earth for discharging.

  • Blower and brushes.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

  • DC 1000 V megger.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Measure the insulation resistance ( > 12 MΩ )

  2. Check of silicon rectifiers

  3. Check of bearing metal

  4. Check and adjustment of alignment

  5. Check and cleaning of stator coil end

  6. Check and cleaning of exciter

  7. Check of brush holder spring pressure

  8. Check and clean the air coolers

  9. Check and adjustment of main terminal bolts connection

  10. Check and adjustment of aux. wiring terminal bolts connection

  11. Alarm test

  12. Cleaning of cooling water piping

  13. Check and adjust the clearance at each section

  14. Pressure test of air coolers

  15. Check or repair of air cooler headers inner coating

  16. Check or replacement of earthing brushes

  17. Check or replacement of seal strips for bearing

  18. Replacement of packing foe air coolers

  19. Replacement of consumable parts

Return the Equipment into Service

  1. Disconnect the ground on the generator winding

  2. Put the circuit breaker of the generator on service position

  3. Lift up the safety fence with caution mark surrounding the work area

  4. Finish the work permit with coordination with shift engineer.
