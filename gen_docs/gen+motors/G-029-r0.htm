Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I2 , which must be performed on monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block .

The generator data: 277MVA,  15.75KV, 10.173KA, PF=0.8, 50HZ, 3000 rpm Y connection

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Aziz El Bagoury, tested by eng. Enas Azmy, revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Saad Bassuony, accepted by eng. Aly Abdel Wahab general director and approved by eng. Fathy Ragab head sector of Talkha PS

This document accepted by eng. Samir Abd El Gilil: EDEPC inspection & quality control general director, MPIS coordinator, and certified by accountant Mahmoud Soliman EDEPC admin & financial inspection & quality control's general director and supervisor of inspection & quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment.

  • The unit must be on service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass …etc

  • New brushes.

  • Digital voltmeter and clamp ammeter.

Work to be carried out

  1. Inspect the brush vibration.

  2. Inspect the sparking brushes.

  3. Inspect brush holders and slip rings.

  4. Replace the brush by new one if its height falls to 25mm.

  5. Record current and voltage value of generator as in table (1) and compare the results with the standard value.
      

  6. Measure the current in each brush and record it in table (2) if the current in any brush less than 10A, according to the recommendation from specialist, replace it.

  7. Measure the leakage AC voltage between bearings of generator as in figure 1 “approximately ≥ 2Vac
