Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I3, to be carried out every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The generator data: 277MVA, 15.75KV, 10.173KA, PF=0.8, 50HZ, 3000 rpm, Y connection

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Aziz El Bagoury, tested by eng. Enas Azmy and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Saad Bassuony, accepted by eng. Aly Abdel Wahab general director and approved by eng. Fathy Ragab head sector of Talkha PS.

This document accepted by eng. Samir Abd El Gilil: EDEPC inspection & quality control general director, MPIS coordinator, and certified by accountant Mahmoud Soliman EDEPC admin & financial inspection & quality control's general director and supervisor of inspection & quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment.

  • The unit must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • New brushes, Air blower, and Spray

  • Digital voltmeter, megger, clamp ammeter

  • Fine cloth and brushes for cleaning purposes

Work to be carried out

  1. Remove the brushes of generator and earthing brushes from their holders.

  2. Remove brush holders.

  3. Measure the insulation resistance between slip ring and earth by using megger device 500 Vdc, table (1).

  4. Clean brushes slip ring contact.

  5. After cleaning measure the insulation resistance between slip ring and earth by using megger device 500 Vdc approximately ≥ 10 MΩ to be sure the results of cleaning, as in table (1).

  6. Change polarity of alternator.

  7. Put holders in their position and tighten them.

  8. Check the clearance between brushe holders and slip ring approximately 3mm.

  9. Check the protection device.

After Entering the unit into service

  1. Measure the current in each brush and record it in table 2, if the current in any brush less than 10 A, according to the recommendation from specialist, replace it.

  2. Measure the leakage AC voltage between bearings of generator as in figure 1 “approximately ≥ 2Vac
