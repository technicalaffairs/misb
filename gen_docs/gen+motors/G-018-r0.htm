Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every 6 months as indicated by M6  in the title block.

The unit generators are cynch. Generator has 11 kv generated 3937 amp 100 rpm, 50 HZ and 0.9 power factor

The generators has self excitation 240 v and 795 amp. (static exc)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, safety helmet ... etc

  • The generator unit must be isolated (unit off)

  • The generator unit circuit breaker must be racked out

  • Switch on the unit earth (G -ES)

  • Switch off the DC excitayion

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger (500 v)

  • Multimeter

  • Hand tools

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the rotor wdg insulation resistance.

  2. Check wear of brake lining and ring.

  3. Check balancing exhaust air temp.

  4. Check oil vapour separator.

  5. Check shaft current preventive insulation.

  6. Using megger 500 v and before maintenance measure the rotor winding insulation.

  7. Check the brushes, brushholders and exciter filter.

  8. Inspect under rotor, inspect for brake ring & brake equipment.

  9. Reverse water circulation in generator pit for generator coolers.

  10. Check all piping & valves.

  11. Check after maintenance the rotor wdg. insulation.
