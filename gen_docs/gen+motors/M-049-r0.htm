Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance inspection on boiler feed water pump, which must be performed every one year

This procedure was applied in damanhour unit 300 MW since 1990

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • Use safety fences with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, earplug, and gloves pairs

  • The motor must be out of service

  • Circuit breaker in test position

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set, air blower meager device

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

  • Brushes (soft brushes)

Work to be carried out

  1. Ensure that all fixing bolts are secure

  2. Ensure that electrical connection are tight

  3. Check the isolators and clean it

  4. Check for water leaks clean cooler fins (air side)

  5. Check for oil leaks

  6. Clean cooler tubes (water side)

  7. Visually inspection for winding and clean it

  8. Measure winding insulation resistance every 2 year

  9. Check all protection devices of the motor by protection group
