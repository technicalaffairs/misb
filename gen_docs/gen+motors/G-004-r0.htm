Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspections I1, I2, I3, I4, which must performed every day, week, month, and 3 months as mentioned by D1, W1, M1, M3 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet, earplug  .... etc

  • Take care: the generator in service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, earplug, and Safety shoes

  • Exhaust filter and air filter of the fan motor

    •

Work to be carried out

Daily

  1. Check sight glasses after level detectors for liquid level.

  2. Check gas consumption.

    3.

Weekly

  1. Check hydrogen cooler vent.

  2. Check shaft-grounding brushes.

  3. Check carbon brushes.

  4. Check seal oil cooler in operation for proper venting.

  5. Check seal oil supply lines and shut off valves for leakage.

  6. Check degree of contamination of seal oil filter.

  7. Check oil level and contamination of oil in vacuum pump.

  8. Drain liquid from drain traps before and after vacuum pump.

Monthly

  1. Change over seal oil pump no. 1&2  i.e. Change the selector switch of seal oil pump from 1 to 2 and vise versa

  2. Perform functional check of stand by seal oil pump. (Check for abnormal sound, vibration, excessive load current ... etc)

  3. Change over vapor exhausters no.1&2.

  4. Check intake strainer in take port of vacuum pump.

  5. Check exhaust filter at vacuum pump.

  6. Re-grease shaft seal of vapor of exhauster.

Every Three Months

  1. Check air filter at fan motor of gas dryer on dirt.
