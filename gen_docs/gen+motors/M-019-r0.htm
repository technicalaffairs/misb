Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required

  • Before starting any work on the machine, check that it is switched off and blocked up for switching on again.

  • The unit should be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness, cleaning tools as cloth.... etc.

  • Crane, press ON, detaching device, properly fitting spanners

  • Oil for bearing

  • AVO meter & insulation testing device

Work to be Carried Out

Changing the Bearings (A)

Removing Bearing Shell (A1)

  1. Remove bearing housing end cover (non-drive-end only)

  2. Remove rain protection roofing

  3. Loosen bearing assembly bolts, upper with lower section and upper section with bearing bracket

  4. Remove cover of bearing housing

  5. Loosen bearing shell bolts

  6. Remove top bearing shell

  7. Open lubricating oil ring joint and pull out ring

  8. Raise shaft using lifting device

  9. Twist bottom bearing shell free and remove

Installing Bearing Shell (A2)

  1. The installation of bearing shell is done in reverse order to the sequence of removal described in (A1). By assembling it, care should be taken not to be damage the bearing ring seals (half rings). They should be checked before installation and replace it if necessary.

  2. Change the bearing oil.

Disassembly and Assembly (B)

Disassembly of Motor uncoupled (B1)

  1. Drain bearing oil on both sides.

  2. Remove the bearing shell at the drive end as per (A1).

  3. Disconnect and remove bearing thermometer on both sides.

  4. Loosen the bolts in the lower section of the bearing housing jointed to the bearing bracket and remove the lower section of the bearing housing.

  5. Insert a sheet 0.5 mm thick between the bearing bracket ring seal and the shaft and carefully lower the rotor.

  6. Remove the bearing shell at the non-drive-end as per (A1).

  7. Proceed as per (B1 point 4).

  8. Loosen bearing bracket dowel pins and withdraw them.

  9. Loosen bearing bracket bolts and press the bearing bracket off the stator housing using 2 bearing bracket bolts via the two threaded holes.

  10. Carefully lower the rotor together with the bearing bracket into the stator bore.

  11. Remove bearing bracket.

  12. Lift rotor on the drive end side.

  13. Proceed as per (B1 point 9, 10, 11 and 12).

  14. Dismantle air guides.

  15. Lift rotor on both ends and carefully remove it from the stator. Take particular care for the stator winding when rotor is handle.

Assembly (B2)

The assembly of motor is done in the reverse order to the sequence of disassembly described as per (B1).
