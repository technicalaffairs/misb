Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I4, which must be performed every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block.

Generators (for units 1, 2) type: THDI 93/43, 187.5 MVA,150 MW, 10.3kA, 10.5kV, 50Hz, 0.8 PF

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety Helmet ... etc.

  • No smoking beside the generator is filled by hydrogen gas.

  • Never remove more than one brush from exciter (ground fault detection system).

  • The unit in service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

  • Measuring rod.

  • Cleaning fluid and clothes.

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check zero of CO2/ H2 purity meter system.

  2. Check generator bearing insulation.

  3. Check labyrinth ring insulation.

  4. Check shaft seal insulation.

  5. Check exciter insulation.

  6. Check insulation between exciter bearing and attached piping.

Rearrangement State:  Connect all items which are disconnected.
