Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This generator cooled by hydrogen gas using cooling water of closed cooling system.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, helmet ...etc

  • The unit must be shut down

  • Disconnect the main breakers of the generator

  • Ground the disconnect switch

  • Disconnect the field breaker.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety "helmet, belt, shoes" ...etc

  • Tool set, Blower, Voltmeter.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

    •

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Operating Maintenance

  1. Clean and replace the brush rigging and collector rings

    2.

  2. Clean air filters.

  3. Inspect vertical cooler.

  4. Check the shaft grounding brush.

Shut Down Maintenance

  1. Inspect and clean armature core.

  2. Inspect and test armature winding.

Rotor

  1. Clean the outside surfaces and retaining rings.

  2. Apply a  coot of brassolene to prevent rusting.

Insulating Testing

  1. Carry out the the electrical testing to determine the weakness points.

Cleaning

  1. Clean the windings using suitable  cleaning fluid.

Other Procedures

  1. Inspect the cooler section.

  2. Check the collector rings.

  3. Interchange the polarity of the collector rings.

  4. Check the protection devices by protection group.

  5. Measure RTD resistance and check the wiring.
