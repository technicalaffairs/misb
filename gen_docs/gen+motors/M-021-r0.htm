Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Before starting any work on the motor, check that it is switched off and blocked up for switching on again.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness, cleaning tools as cloth.... etc

  • Lifting device, press-on and detaching device

  • AVO meter and insulation test device

    • .

  • Vibration and noise level measuring devices.

  • Grease for the bearing.

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Measure the vibration and noise level.

  2. Clean the heat exchanger.

  3. Check the bearing visually.

  4. Grease the bearing.

  5. Measure the insulation resistance for stator winding.

  6. Clean the inside terminal box, and retighten bolts and ground them.

  7. General cleaning.
