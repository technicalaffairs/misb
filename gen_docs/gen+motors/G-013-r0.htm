Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

To avoid the degrading to dangerous insulation levels. It is advisable to perform a first inspection six months  after the putting into service, to define the inspection frequency and the maintenance schedule, taking into account only the switchboard prescription but also those of each component.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc

  • The personal shall perform the safety operation so as all the live parts are de-energized, earthed and equipped with warnings before removing the protection and reaching main circuit.

  • Wear safety belt when cleaning insulations. 

  • The generator must be in case of shut down

  • The circuit breaker of the generator on test position

  • Put ground on the generator windings

Tools and Equipment

  • Blower and brushes

  • Voltage indicators, portable earth for discharging

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purpose

  • DC 1000 V megger

Work to be Carried out

  1. Check or replace earthing brushes

  2. Check or replace seal strips for bearing

  3. Measure the insulation resistance   ( > 12MΩ )

  4. Check of brush holder spring pressure

  5. Check and clean air coolers

  6. Pressure test of air coolers

  7. Check or repair air coolers headers inner coating

  8. Check or replace earthing brushes

  9. Check or replace seal strips for bearing

  10. Replace the packing for air coolers

Return the Equipment into Service

  1. Disconnect the ground on the generator winding

  2. Put the circuit breaker of the generator on the service position

  3. Lift up the safety fence with caution mark surrounding the work area

  4. Finish the work permit with coordination with shift engineer.
