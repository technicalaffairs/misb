Introduction

This document out lines vibration transducer ( TR 76 ) maintenance procedure I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

This System consists of GT 8030 A,B,C,D,W,8030 B and + 24303 +24304+ 24305 + 24306 + 24307 + 24308 / uv 5627 b.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohmed Aly Aly Hassan Al Shahat Ahmed Mousa at 01 March 2003, revised by quality control eng. Abdel Monem Al Emam at  EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Abdel Moola Melegy and approved by eng. Yousry Mohamed Aly Salama Head Sector of  Al Shabab GT PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

    •

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Gas turbine must be out of service at cold state.

  • Wear safety helmet, and safety shoes.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set

  • Air blower

  • Multimeter, connection wire (test leads)

  • Contact cleaner spray

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Turn switch to 180 at vibration simulation in the 12  O'clock position, and potentiometer scale sitting (24301 – R2 )_74 %

  2. Check the value of measuring voltage: N12 = 4.37 V. AC  at the main unit.

  3. Adjust the measuring and calibration test re MRDS

Magnetic Pick up Vibration Sensor

  1. Clean the sensor using Air blower / Contact cleaner spray

  2. Check  DC R. approximately R=560 ohm

  3. Check the ground.  between the card terminal and the ground

  4. Disconnect the cable to give you alarm and if OK return the cable to its normal position.

  5. Short circuit the cables to give you alarm and if OK remove the short circuit.
