Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the personal protective equipment as (safety shoes, Helmet).

  • Turbine shout down & all MCC CB off (racked out).

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital AVO Meter, Frequency meter, Remover card.

  • Speed tronic calibrator set model  (151A4925)

  • Special tool to adjust the potentiometer at the cards

Work to be Carried Out

 

Turbine Speed Sensor

 

PR/A: Pulse  Rate / Analog            NHP: normal Speed

 

 

Magnetic Pickup ( 77HC1 , 77HC2 )

  1. Check DC resistance shod be (140Ω).

  2. Check wires for grounding.

  3. Check that  the sensor wires are healthy.

 Speed Control Card SSZB  (1L 1I ) 

  1. Inject frequency source from calibrator at pin (1C, 2B) at 100%, to reach NHP (5105) RPM.           

  2. Ensure that voltage at 5H,G read 10 V dc

  3. If it is not correct adjust from (R112).

  4. Pull card SFPB at 1L 1P.

  5. Make Jumper max1, max2 pin (34 & 35) at card SSKC to max. (VCE).

  6. Adjust 100% NHP until speed control light just comes on. If it is not correct adjust from (R112) according the table blow

Full Speed No Load  (FSNL)

  1. Remove card SFPB.     

  2. Set 100% NHP 5105 r.p.m.

  3. Measure VCE 7H,1G at calibrator as given in sitting (3.4 volt) if it is not correct. Must be adjusted from FSNL R207 at SFPB card.

Droop 104%

  1. Set 100% (NHP)  by 1C , 2B at calibrator

  2. Raise D.S.P to 104 at 4 volt

  3. Ensure that VCE =16.3 or 8 volt.

  4. If it is not correct. it must be adjusted from “DROOP” R209 at card SFPB.
