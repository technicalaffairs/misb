Introduction

This document outlines the equipment maintenance inspection on the 6 kv CB and 380v CB which must be performed every 1500 hours of operation. This procedure was applied in ABU QIR power plant since 1985 and the finial draft is issued in august 2004    

 the first draft is initiated in august 2004 by eng. Mohamed Hassan Toto,  approved by eng. Khalil Aly Khalil General Director of Abu Kir PS, accepted by eng. Ahmed Taha Hashim Head Sector of  Abu Kir PS.

Reviewed by eng. Ahmad El Sharkawy Inspection and Quality Control general Director of WDEPCo.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety helmet, safety shoes

  • Insure that the circuit breaker is switched off  in coordinate with the shift engineer

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger

  • Rags

  • Air Blower for cleaning

  • Grease type SKF 63

  • Tool Set

  • Avometer

 

Work to be Carried Out

6 kv Circuit Breaker

  1. Clean all the outer parts of the C.B. specially the 6 kv insulators

  2. Visual inspection of the C.O. oil level (not less than half of the glass-fill in if required

  3. Measure the insulation resistance of the oil 30 kv if not O.K change the oil

  4. Connect and disconnect  the spring  manually, using the electric circuit , using the plug and the electric circuit
     

  5. Maintain the shocks absorption cylinder by increasing the oil level till the middle and change cell 34 and its gasket
     

  6. Grease the following parts:
        * Internal gears parts 125
        * Parts 141 with grease type SKF 63
        * Main spring parts 161 by hand to be sure all parts are greased
        * Chains group part 155
        * Parts 156

  7. Lubricate the following parts:
        * Chain connecting mobile part of motor
        * Reverse movement of the CB
        * Internal holes

  8. Lubricate the 6 kv feeding sockets of the CB with puste melydal ELS

  9. Check mechanical interlock

380 v CB

  1. Withdraw the contactor and check its state

  2. Clean the internal parts from dust

  3. Tighten the bolts and check the mechanical and electrical connection parts if they are in a good condition

  4. Check the diodes in its direction

  5. Check the operation of thermal and mechanical for each one and check the on and off switch

  6. Check and measure the fuses in good state

  7. Clean the contacts and change if needed

  8. Check and adjust the limit switch for the contactor of the following types:
        * RD 80
        * RD 125
        * RD 250

  9. Check the relay fixation

  10. Check the faulty lamps

  11. Test the internal parts by using the avometer

  12. Grease the mettle parts spatially the connected parts

  13. Test the time of connection and disconnection of the thermal of each contactor according to the curve
