Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified or directly supervised by any one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Generator isolation and proper earthing is required.

  • Ensure that the circuit breaker disconnected and secondary disconnect.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety helmet, shoes

  • Complete Tool set, Brushes

  • Megger, Digital voltmeter
