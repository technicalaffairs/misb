Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed (3 times / year) bases as indicated by M4 in the title block.

The technical specification of the isolator is: rated current = 2000 A, rated voltage = 500 kv

The first draft initiated, reviewed and tested by Eng. Solimean A.Elftah on 20/6/2004, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad general director, and approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station,  Hyd.EPCo.

This document certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem MPIS Hyd.EPCo. Coordinator.

 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear safety equipment as helmet, shoes, ... etc

  • Switch off isolator

  • Switch off the AC supply, DC supply

  • Switch on the earthling at disconnector sides

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

Tools and Equipment

  • Human safety fence

  • Portable air blower metal brush

  • Human safety fence

  • Grease for lubricating (USSR grease 220)

  • Set of common, screw drivers, cleaning material and water pump

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the mechanism of operation and lubricate the gears

  2. Clean the insulation's of disconnector

  3. Lubricate the axis

  4. Check the tightening of (contacts, input bus dar, output bus bar) bolts and nuts

  5. Check the presence of overheat and flashover of contacts

  6. Examine the ear thing assembly mounted on disconnector

  7. Testing the switch (on & off) manually operation of the disconnector

  8. Check hot spots of (contacts, input bus bar, output bus bar) bolts and nuts
