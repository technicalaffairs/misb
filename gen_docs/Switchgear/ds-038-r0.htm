Tools and Equipment

  • Portable air blower, metal brush

  • Set of common, screw drivers and cleaning material (rag, cloth)

  • Graphite grease, grease for lubricating 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the disconnectors from the dust and replace the old grease

  2. Clean the surface of the insulators and porcelain rods from dust and dirt

  3. Check the insulator from cracks and replace them if necessary

  4. Check the contacts pressure conductivity of unit disconnectors

  5. Clean the contact blades that reclaim damage parts

  6. Replace graphite grease on moving and fixed contacts surfaces of dissconectors

  7. Tight the bolts and nuts on the moving contacts and fixed contacts

  8. Make sure that operation of the discorrectors is reliable by closing and opening of the circuit for several times

  9. Check the situation of grease for contacts of earth disconnectors

  10. Clean the earth disconnector contacts blades if any parts be found burn or damage replace them

  11. Check the contacts pressure of earth disconnector blades

  12. Replace the grease by new one on the friction parts of the earth disconnector and operating mechanism

  13. Make sure that operations of the earth discorrector is reliable by closing and opening of the circuit for several times

  14. Make sure that the earth disconnector blades correctly
