Introduction

This procedurs explains the steps required to check the value of DEW POINT of SF6 gas. The above model DP 19 - GEC is specialy designed for field use, the measurements are made at pressure 10 mp - 10 bar.

The measurements are based on the chilled mirror principle which guarantees direct and precise measurement of the actual humidity

The first draft was initiated and tested by eng. Naser El Sayed Abd EL-Aziz, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman Selim, approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel-Baset  head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc

  • The circuit breaker is open, isolated and properly earthed.

  • Make sure that the measuring gas outlet (no.2 fig.2) is open.

  • Make sure that the power supply is 220Vac and the voltage selector switch of the instrument is 220v

  • Make sure that the gas pressure not exceed more than "10" bar 

Tools and Equipment

  • The moisture measuring device DP19 GEC

  • Suitable wrenches for the hose connection

  • Clean clothes

Work to be Carried Out

Check the Condition of the Instrument before use and all tupes and couplings must be Flushed with Dry Gas

Check Dew Point Mirror as follow:

  1. Close the control valve completely.

  2. Open the flow meter valve.

  3. Open the control valve slowly unit gas flow of 30 - 40 L / H is indicated.

  4. Press the nains switch.

  5. Press the mode switch, now both switches are illuminated.

  6. Check dew point mirror condition as follow:
    - The indicator needle is in the middle of the red field means correct
    - The indicator needle moves towards the red field but remains in the green field means contaminated

  7. In case of contaminated mirror, it must be cleaned as follow:
    - Wait until the temprature indicator shows ambient temprature because if mirror temprature is below ambient temprature when opening the measuring head, dew will form on the mirror
    - Close the control valve.
    - Open the measuring head.
    - Clean the mirror.
    - Install the front part of the measuring.
    - If the indicator is now in the middle of the red field, the mirror is clean, if it remains with in the green field or at the edge of the red field, set the indicator needle with light intensity potentiometer (no. 9 figure1) to the middle of the red field
    - If the range of the light intensity potentiometer is not sufficient, lamp and photocell in the front part of the measuring head must be cleaned.

Measurement

  1. Close the control valve completely.

  2. Open flowmeter valve completely.

  3. Open control valve slowly until the required gas flow of 30 - 40 L/H is reached.

  4. Press the mode switch to check the mirror condition as described above.

  5. Release the mode switch (light is off), the instrument starts cooling until dew point is reached and needle moves in to the green field.

  6. Wait until the indication of the digital display stabilizes.

  7. Read the dew point.

  8. Determine the moisture contenes from the chart (fig. 3) in PPMv.
