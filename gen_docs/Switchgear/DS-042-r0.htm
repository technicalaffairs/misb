 Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes,...etc

    •

  • Open the circuit breaker on both sides of Disconnect Switch.

  • Isolate the Disconnect Switches and put grounds on both sides after ensure that the there is no voltage using voltage indicator.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes,...etc

  • Complete tool set, Air blower, corrosion cleaner,

  • Suitable grease unit, Fine cloth for cleaning purposes .

  • Voltage indicator, and digital voltmeter.

 

Work to be Carried out

  1. Disconnect the control circuit.

  2. Inspect and clean the porcelain insulation at regular intervals.

  3. Lubricate the bearing points.

  4. Adjust the full opening and closing stroke manually and electrically.

  5. Clean the moving and fixed contact and replace the worst contact.

  6. Check the tightening of electrical wiring and circuits.

  7. Check the hot spot points using X- rays or gun

  8. Check the electrically and mechanically interlock.

  9. Connect the control circuit.

  10. Check the signals of isolating switch in control room.

  11. Remove all grounds and maintenance tools and materials from work area.

  12. Return the work permit to shift engineer to prepare the DS into service
