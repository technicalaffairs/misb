Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the isolator stack when cleaning insulators or checking connections wear your safety belt.

  • Be sure that the isolator is disconnected from all electric power sources.

  • Make sure the drive mechanism is locked and the power supply for the motor drive is interrupted.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness

  • Cleaning materials (rags, cloth), soft cloth,

  • Safety belt, safety helmet, mask, ... etc

  • Voltage indicator.

    •

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check all bolted connections for tightness.

  2. Clean and re-grease all moving joints.

  3. Check porcelains for chips, cracks, cementing, and fastenings.

  4. Thoroughly clean all porcelain surfaces.

  5. Check for signs of overheating or flashovers.

  6. Smooth out any uneven spots and burn markers on scissors, earthing and fixed contacts and lightly coat them with Petroleum Jelly  (for  Z 245 (AEG))

  7. Clean the contacts and apply a thin and uniform film of molykote BR2 paste to the contact surfaces (for GSSB-245 1600A (Hapam))

  8. The bearing and gearing are totally enclosed and lubricated for life, hence are maintenance-free over protracted periods. (for  Z 245  (AEG))

  9. It is recommended to grease the main bearings and the pivots of the coupling rods using one of the following types of grease: Mobil (Mobilux 3) or Shell (Alvania R3).( for GSSB-245 1600A (Hapam))

  10. Check the state of the high tension terminals.

  11. Inspect the equipment and the operating mechanism for good working condition .

  12. Inspect and check all safety interlocks and test for proper operation .

  13. Check the corona shield for damage (for GSSB-245 1600A (Hapam))
