Introduction

This document outlines the equipment  inspection I1, which must be performed every 3000 hours of operation on the oil 6 kv CB fan

The first draft is initiated and tested by eng. Ahmed Maharem,  accepted by eng. Mohamed Abd El-Sadek  general director of Abu Kir PS, approved by eng. Aly Anwar El-Fayoumy Head Sector of  Abu Kir PS, and certified by eng. Mona Ahmad El Sharkawy: WDEPC Inspection and Quality Control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ...etc

  • The circuit breaker should be switched - off (open)

Tools and Equipment

  • Clean rags, Grease type SKF 63

  • Complete kit of tools

  • Oil Graphite

  • Vaseline

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean all the outer parts of the C.B specially the 6 KV insulators

  2. Visual inspection of the C.B oil level not less than half of the glass - fill in if required

  3. Insulation resistance of the oil 30 kv if not o.k change the oil

  4. Connection and disconnection
          * Manually by the spring
          * Using the electric circuit
          * Using the plug and electric circuit

  5. Maintenance of the shocks absorption cylinder by increasing the oil leveltill the middle and change cell 34 and its gasket

  6. Grease the following parts with grease SKF 63
           * Internal gears parts 152
           * Parts 141
           * Main spring parts 161 by hand to be sure all parts are greased
           * Chains group part 155
           * Part 156

  7. Lubricate the following parts with oil graphite
           * Chain connecting mobile part of motor
           * Reverse movement of the CB
           * Internal holes

  8. Lubricate the 6 KV feeding sockets of the C.B with paste mely dail EL 5

  9. Check mechanical inter lock

6 KV cell

  1. Clean the internal parts

  2. Grease the earth connecting switch with vaseline

  3. Lubricate mechanical parts of the cell foundation including the following
          * The cell internal automatic connecting dampers
          * The earth connecting switch control
          * Internal axis - rods - arms
          * Stopping parts of movable connect
