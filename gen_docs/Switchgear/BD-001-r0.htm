Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be carried out on an annually bases or after any severe electrical faults as indicted by Y1 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Elsaied Albiah, tested by eng. Haytham Hemdan, revised by edepc quality control eng. Adel Abd El kader, and approved by eng. Tarek Youssef Ali, Head Sector of Ayoun Moussa Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes and safety helmet... etc

  • The bus duct must be de-energized and the bars must be connected to earth.

  • The insulation resistance must be checked with a megger (at least 500 volts) prior and after maintenance to re-energize the bus duct.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Voltage Indicator.

Work to be Carried Out

 

Support Insulators

They are mounted along the bus, each support is constituted by three insulators removable from the outside of the enclosure.

  1. Check the Support insulators.

 

Bolted Connections of the Conductors

They are present in the connection between bus and transformers, PT cubicles, generator and disconnection joint.

  1. Check the Bolted connections of the conductors.

Neoprene Bellows and Sleeves

They are mounted at all the end connection of the enclosure.

  1. Verify that there are no cuts or cracks with the Neoprene bellows and sleeves.
