Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P2, which must be performed every 12:16 years as indicated by Y12:16 in the title block

The technical of the circuit breaker: Rated current = 2000A, Short-circuit = 40kA/3 second, Pneumatic mechanism.

The first draft initiated by eng. Soliman Abdel Fatah Soliman at High Dam Power Station, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad: general director, approved by eng. Abdel-Karem Abu Zaid: High Dam head Sector, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Haleem: EPGC quality control general director, MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

    •

  • Switch off the disconnectors on CB sides and grounded the CB sides

  • Switch off DC & AC supply (CB is electrically dead)

  • Switch off the charging current DC motor wound spring

  • The CB in not under pressure (all compressed air is released)

  • Electronic breaker timing analyzer (analyzer TM 1600 / MA 31) micrometer and water pump

Work to be Carried Out

Steps before maintenance

  1. Isolate the terminals of (closing, opening) coils of CB from main cabinet for all CB phases

  2. Connect the external DC supply to (closing & opening) coil for CB phases

  3. Connect the AC supply of air compressors

  4. Measurement (close - open) times, of CB

  5. Disconnect AC supply off air compressors and test the air protection devices during release the compressed air from the CB

  6. Switch of the external DC supply and AC supply

  7. Record counter reading of operation counter and no of operation hours of compressors

  8. Measurement the DC resistance of contacts of CB

  9. Test (dew point, air volume percentage & decomposition products) of the SF6 gas

  10. Suction the SF6 gas from the CB

Steps of Dismantling and Maintenance

  1. Dismantle the terminals from the both sides of CB

  2. Dismantle the twin head - dismantle the capacitors and transport the twin head to work shop

  3. Measure the capacitance of the capacitors

  4. Dismantle the parallel (preinsertion) resistors from twin head end measure the resistance

  5. Check and maintenance the mechanism and moving contacts of resistors

  6. Check the presence of overheat and flashover on breaking units components

  7. Check the presence of surface defects or cracks on breaking units components

  8. Dismantle the (moving stationary) contacts of interrupters, clean and lubricate the (puffer cyl., arcing pins, crown)

  9. Inspect the external insulator of breaking units

  10. Replace the old o-rings by new o-rings before mounting the all parts of twin head and parallel resistors

Steps before Mounting the Twin Head on C

  1. Measure the resistance of parallel resistors and compare it by the previous reading

  2. Dismantle and maintenance (actuator, closing value, opening value, solenoid valve, aux. - switch, interlock close piston) of pneumatic mechanism (lubricate all parts and new-o-ring)

  3. Transport twin head from workshop to site of CB and mantle it

  4. Mantle all parts of pneumatic mechanism of step (24)

  5. Check and inspect the insulation of support column and its tightening

  6. Repeat all steps (11 to 28) every twin head and pneumatic mechanism of all C.B Phases respectively

Steps of Maintenance of air Compressors

  1. Check the air values and chang the oil

  2. Check the air filter and safety valves

  3. Check the all air pistons

  4. Change o-rings and gaskets for air-pipelines

  5. Check the all components of control cabinet

  6. Switch on air compressore to fill the receivers by compressed air

  7. Check the pneumatic mechanism for air leaks

  8. Perform the vacuum for all breaking units and support columns of CB

  9. Fill the all phases of CB by SF6 gas and test (the gas protection stages, dew point, air volume percentage and decomposition test)

  10. Measure the (closing & opening) times for each phase

Preparation of switch on the Circuit Breaker

  1. Connect the terminals on CB sides and clean the insulation of CB

  2. Switch on DC supply control of CB

  3. Switch on the CB several times

  4. Record counter reading of operation counter and no of operation hours of compressors

  5. Measure the hot spots of all part of CB
