Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Abd Allah Elsayed Mohamed, tested by eng Abdel Latif Ahmed, revised  by EDEPC quality control eng Adel Abdel Qader El Nagar, accepted by eng El Sayed Attiah Abd El Maksood general director, and approved by eng. Fathy Mahmoud Ragb Head Sector of Talkha PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintanance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safty fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt ...etc.

  • Shut down the equipment under testing.  

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool kit of screw drivers and common keys.

  • Digital multimeter.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Test the Interlocking System according to the attached logic diagram.
