Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc.

  • Circuit Breaker must be out of service and on the open position

  • Draw out the circuit breaker.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Complete kit of tools,

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

  • Blower, Voltmeter

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visually look for a dirt grease or other foreign material on any breaker parts.

  2. Check for any loosen component of breaker or wiring

  3. Observe a few nos. of close-open operations using the operating or maintenance handle.

  4. Verify the safety interlocks (racking mechanism, positive, closing spring, disconnect position, pad locks, and optional interlocks key, door interlocks ) are properly working.

  5. Inspect the condition of the arc chutes and contacts.

  6. Look for excessive burning or leakage.

  7. Check all protection devices by protection staff.

  8. Return the breaker into service with coordination with shift engineer.
