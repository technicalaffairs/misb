Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes .... etc

    •

  • The circuit breaker under test must be out of service.

  • Circuit breaker must be isolated & proper earthing at the two sides of CB.

  • Isolate control circuit (DC voltage)

  • Change remote/local switch to local position.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet & safety shoes

  • Pressure gauge with its delivered connection

  • 20 micron filter

  • Portable air blower, SF6 gas detector, buzzer,

  • Screw driver, and set of common keys.

Work to be Carried Out

  • Put the circuit breaker in open position.

  • Reduce the oil pressure to 0 bar by using the pressure controller.

  • Empty the main tank by using the pressure controller.

  • Clean the bottom of the tank with a solvent.

  • Bleed the operating mechanism.

  • Replace the oil filter and charge the operating mechanism with oil.

  • Place the CB back into normal operation condition.
