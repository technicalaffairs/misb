Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, Helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses).

  • Switch off input and output supply.

  • Circuit breaker must be isolated and earthed.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set, Digital voltmeter. 

  • Brush, Air blower or vacuum cleaner.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the outside CB surface to remove contaminant particles.

  2. Check for loose bolts and tighten if needed.

  3. Check the SF6 pressure value.

  4. Clean mechanical parts and electrical switches (contacts).

  5. Check the closing and tripping coils (operations).

  6. Check the possibility of easy motion for the breakers.

  7. Check the operation of charging motor and charging of spring.
