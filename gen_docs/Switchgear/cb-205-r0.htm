Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist (P1) which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block

The technical specification of the CB Rated voltage is 17.5 kv - 50 HZ, rated current 8000A, rated short time current (4 sec) 74 K.A

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintanance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear safety equipment as helmet, shoes breathing mask, safety glasses

  • Switch off the CB (B-1?G) 15.75 kv

  • Switch of the unit isolator (P-1G) 15.75 kv

  • Switch off the V.T of (M.G) for the unit

  • Switch off the automatics F 403 (D.C control of the start / stop unit operation), 72D2 - 7 (D.C auto. for control of C.B, unit filed, B.F protection) 14 AG (A.C auto. for control circuit of for the unit disconnector Switch on the earths for M.G (Z - 1G), C.B 15.75 (Z - 1B)

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable air blower

  • Using rag, cloth for cleaning

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean C.B from dust and dirt

  2. Perform visual inspection for the unit C.B

  3. Drain the air storage tank to remove water build - up

  4. Check and clean of the compressor system and observe for leaks

  5. Check the function of heaters

  6. Check and clean the compressor motor system

  7.  Check the setting of pressures switches

  8. Check the tightness of the terminal connections

  9. Check the tight andsealed of C.B

  10. Record the pressure (air, SF6 gas) for the C.B

  11. Record numbers of close / open the C.B

  12. Record numbers of operation hours  of compressor

  13. Check the function of the cabinet components by operating the C.B
