Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection checklist I1,which must be performed every 6 months bases as indicated by M6 in the above title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc.

  • All Breakers are disconnected and out of service.

  • Incoming Breaker and interconnection Breaker are disconnected and out of service .

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set, grease

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes .

  • Brushes, Blower, megger device and digital voltmeter .

  • Closing and opening time measuring instrument, leakage gas test instrument sf6 pressure measuring instrument and DC resistance measuring instrument.

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Clean breaker from any dust .

  2. Check screw connections for tightness .

  3. Grease the Breaker operating mechanism .

  4. Close the Breaker mechanically

  5. Open the Breaker mechanically .

  6. Check the charging and discharging Breaker manually .

  7. Check the connection of earthing system .

  8. Check cable sockets of Breaker .

  9. Check push buttons, signal lamps, fuses and auxiliary relays .

  10. Check any gas leakage (SF6).

  11. Check control voltage value.

  12. Check charging and discharging Breaker electrically.

  13. Check operation of Breaker from local in test position.

  14. Check operation of Breaker from Remote in test position.

  15. Check the interlocks for operation of Breaker .

  16. Record counter reading (Table I).

  17. Measure closing time of breaker (Table I).

  18. Measure opening time of breaker (Table I).

  19. Measure insulation resistance of main bus-bar  (Table II)

  20. Measure insulation resistance of outgoing cubicle  (Table II)

  21. Measure D.C Resistance of breaker ( Table III ). 

  22. Check SF6 gas pressure of breaker .

  23. Check alarm and block of lower SF6 pressure .

  24. Check interconnection terminals.

  25. Check all protection devices of CB by protection group.
