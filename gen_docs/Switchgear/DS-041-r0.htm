 Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes,...etc

  • The unit must be out of service.

  • Isolating switch is disconnected from voltage supply.

  • Two earthing devices are connected.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set, grease

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes .

  • Brushes, Blower, megger device and digital voltmeter .

 

Work to be Carried out

  1. Check the main contacts do not spark or do not get over heated .

  2. Clean the insulators.

  3. Clean all contact and apply with Vaseline

  4. Check the tightening of screw joints especially on current, track, insulators and drives.

  5. Check the surface of all insulators look for a cracks on them.

  6. Check the rightness of the set-up of position of switching arms and adjustment of insulators carrying the arms.

  7. Check the motor drive chain.

  8. Check the operation of isolating switch from local and remote position .

  9. Check the operation of isolating switch mechanical and electrical .

  10. Check the interlocks for operation isolating switch .

  11. Clean and inspect isolating switch control cabinet .

  12. Check the interconnection terminals.

  13. Check the signals of isolating switch in control room.
