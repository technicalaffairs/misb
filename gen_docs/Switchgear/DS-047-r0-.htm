Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses, don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

  • The rotary disconnect switch must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides, and isolate the DC voltage.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required.

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the isolator stack when cleaning insulators or checking connections wear your safety belt.

  • Be sure that the isolator is disconnected from all electric power sources.

  • Make sure the drive mechanism is locked and the power supply for the motor drive is interrupted.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness, Common set of screw drivers, set of keys,

  • Cleaning materials (rags, cloth), soft cloth, Electrical grease

  • Safety belt, safety helmet

  • Voltage indicator.

 Work to be carried out

  1. In addition to the 6 monthly inspection check list, carry out the following steps:

  2. Check that all wiring terminations are tight and securely fastened.

  3. Check the condition of cables.

  4. Check that bolts, nuts and washers are in place and in good condition. Replace items showing excessive wear or corrosion.  

  5. Check earth bus connections to base of isolator for tightness.

  6. Do touch up painting if required.
