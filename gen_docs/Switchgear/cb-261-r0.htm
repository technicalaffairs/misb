Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed from twelve to twenty four years as indicated by Y12:24 in the above title block

Competence Requirement: To be carried out by trained staff or ALSTOM specialists. Take the circuit- breaker out of service, an opening of the pole columns is not necessary.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belts ... etc.

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated and grounded from both sides..

  • The position indicator of the closing spring shows " discharged "

  • Isolate the service voltage and control voltage.

  • The appropriate local safety regulation should be observed.

Tools and Equipment

  • Cleaning tools.

  • Safety belt, safety helmet.

  • No special tools needed.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the cable connections: (fixed inside the operating mechanisms and control cubicle).

  2. Check the securing elements of the drive rod (locking nuts).

  3. Functional check of the electrical control circuit: (perform one close – and one open operation).

Measurement of  the Operating Times

  1. Take the operating times on one "close" – and one "open" – operation at rated supply voltage.

  2. The values have to correspond to those given in the routine test certificate.

  3. The opening time is time the from energizing the opening release to contact separate .

  4. The close time is the time from energizing the closing release to contact touch.

Checking of the SF6 Gas Quality

  1. Check the gas quality with respect to water content, Sf6 content and acidity.

  2. Dew point                        ≤ -10 ° C at nominal pressure

  3. SF6 content                      > 98 %

  4. Acidity                             ≤  10 ppm
