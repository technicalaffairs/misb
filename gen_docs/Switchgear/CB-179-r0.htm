Introduction            

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated  by Y1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by: eng. Saleh Abdou, tested by eng Mohamed Abaas & eng Tawfeek Ramadan,  revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Tawfeek Elabasery, accepted by general manager eng Tarek Youssef Ali and approved by eng Mechaeal George Habashy head sector of Ayoun Moussa power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses,

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off, fully isolated, and grounded on both sides,

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker,

  • Switch off control current for CB (d.c).

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower to clean the outside compartment of the circuit breaker from dust, wear your breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

  • Fix any oil leaks from connections, safety valve, bleeding washers, and motion transmission shaft to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil.

  • Oil leaks to be ground have very bad impact on the environment, mainly on the soil and ground water and eventually on the ecosystem. “ It takes only 1 liter of Hydrocarbon spilled in an aquifer to render 1,000,000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption “.    

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, glove, and breathing mask,

  • Complete maintenance tool set,

  • Air Blower, rags, and SF6 gas detector

  • Dew point, acidity, and air content equipment.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visual inspection for all parts of circuit breaker.

  2. Check AC and DC supply source.

  3. Record on / off operations counter.

  4. Record the oil pump operations counter.

  5. Check the on / off indications.

  6. Visual outside inspections to separate insulator cone. 

  7. Earth connections must be checked.

  8. Clean all outside compartment of circuit breaker from dust, oil and other things using blower and rags.

  9. Measure SF6 pressure of circuit breaker.

  10. Check gas dew point reference to MPIS approved document no. TD-SS-004-r0.

  11. Check the contact resistance for circuit breaker & all compartment

  12. Check on / off timing.

  13. Retighten all bolts and earth connection of foundation and fixation of circuit breaker.

  14. Check gas pressure switch (for all stages)

  15. Clean all mechanism parts.

  16. Check oil level of main tanks for three phases.

  17. Check oil leaks for all connections.

  18. Check oil leaks for safety valve.

  19. Check oil leaks for bleeding washers.

  20. Check the heater operation.

  21. Check oil leaks for motion transmission shaft (Ram).

  22. Check oil pump operation:
        - Start & stop operation
        - Accumulator pressure.
        - On lockout pressure.
        - Automatic switching lockout pressure.

  23. Check all terminals connections also the  pump terminals.

  24. Oil bleeding must be done.

  25. Check auxiliary switch terminal.

  26. Check on / off coils.

  27. Check earth resistance.

  28. Check SF6 humidity content air content < 2 %,  acid content < 10 PPM. 

  29. Check door gaskets for all panels.
