Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P2, which must be performed on thirty years or after 10,000 mechanical CLOSE/OPEN operations (for major overhaul ) or  Σ n * I1.8 = 20,000 (for overhaul of breaking chambers) n = number of short-circuits I= short-circuit current, KA (RMS)

Competence Requirement:

  • Personnel authorized by ABB to carry out assembly, commissioning and maintenance work

  • Support from ABB may be necessary in certain cases.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belts ... etc.

  • Gas evacuated.

  • Complete circuit breaker including operating mechanism transported to clean workshop.

  • Closing springs discharged, circuit breaker in open position and ground both sides of CB, and isolate the control circuits i.e. de-energized

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety harnesses ... etc.

  • Voltage indicator.

  • Complete tool kit, cleaning tools.

  • Thermovision camera.

  • Electronic timer.

  • Gas filling equipment

  • Spray equipment, clothes and wrenches

  • Resistance bridge, test current min. 200A dc

  • Variable DC voltage source

  • Standard tools, close fitting dust mask, long sleeved overall, protective plastic or rubber gloves.

Work to be Carried Out

Overhaul of Breaking Chamber

Porcelain lift off, contacts and PTFE- parts replaced with new ones.

Major Overhaul of Complete Circuit Breaker and Operating Mechanism

  1. Disassembly of the poles and mechanisms, new contacts and PTFE-components are to be mounted.

  2. Exchange of outer worn out parts.

  3. If the circuit-breaker and operating mechanism are overhauled for another 2,000 operations, selected worn out parts are to be exchanged.

  4. If the circuit breaker and operating mechanism are overhauled for another 10,000 operations, in principle all moving parts to be exchanged.

Capacitor

Check of values:    C= CN + 5 % , - 0 %        Tan δ < 0.2 %
