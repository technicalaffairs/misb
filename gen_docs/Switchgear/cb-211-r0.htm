Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by  M6 in the title block.

The technical of the circuit breaker : Rated current = 1500A, and Short circuit current = is 21.2 kA, Rated voltage is 132 kv

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintanance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety equipment's as helmet, shoes

  • Switch off the C.B and discharge the compressed air

  • Switch off the connectors at circuit breaker side

  • Switch off D.C supply control of C.B

  • Connect the ground at circuit breaker sides

  • Put the suitable warning tags

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable air blower, metal brush

  • Set of common screw drivers, and cleaning material (arg, cloth)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Examine the external parts of (arc extinction, isolator) chambers

  2. Check the presence of surface defects or cracks at the (arc extinction, isolator)

  3. Check the tightening of the bolts and nuts

  4. Maintain the main cabinet (filter, non-returned valves) and air pressure manometers

  5. Dismantle and maintenance main piston and aux. Piston of mechanism of the arc extinction chamber and isolator chamber

  6. Clean the insulator of CB
