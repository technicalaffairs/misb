Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which should be carried out every 6 months as indicated by M6 in the title block .

The first draft was initiated and tested by eng. Naser El Sayed Abd EL-Aziz, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman Selim, approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel-Baset  head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ...etc

  • The circuit breaker should be switched - off (open)

  • The circuit breaker be with drawn from its enclosure

  • The closing and opening springs should be discharged by performig mechanical on - off operations until ensuring that no more operations are available

  • Caution mark should be placed on the front of circuit breaker enclosure to protect againist the live parts inside the enclosure 

Tools and Equipment

  • Clean rags, Electrical grease

  • Complete kit of tools, Gas detector

  • Voltammeter instrument

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the internal parts and devices with dry rags

  2. Clean the oxidised springs or replace them if damaged

  3. Check that the locking rings are fitted in their housing

  4. The loosen nuts must be re tighttened

  5. The wire fasteners must be reconnected

  6. Lubricate the parts using special type (WD 40)

 

11 kv Parts

  1. Clean the dust with a dry rag

  2. Check that the locking rings are not out of their housing and retighten the loosen screws

  3. Replace the distorted or cracked arts of the insulating parts after consulting the manufacturer

  4. Clean the oxidized isoating tulip contact or replace it if damaged

Earthing Isolating Contact

  1. Clean with a rough dry ragsoaked in asuitable solvent and lubricate with neutral grease

Auxiliary Circuit Supply Voltage

  1. Check the supply voltage of the operating mechanism and that the releases and electrical interlocking devices work correctly

Operating and Control Elements

  1. The springs are charged correctly

  2. The signals are correct

  3. The geared motor cuts off when he springs are charged

  4. The geared motor recharges the springs after each closing operation

  5. Check the shunt opening and closing releases

  6. Check the blocking coil

  7. Check the auxiliary

Gas Pressure

  1. Check the leakage for all junctions in the gas path

  2. Check the pressure in the normal case (green lamp is on), if not, reset the pressure according to the standared value and ambient temp rature effect

In case of changing a part of the medium voltage equipment, megger test with 2500 v must be done for the medim voltage parts, and a measurement of closing time and opening time must be done
