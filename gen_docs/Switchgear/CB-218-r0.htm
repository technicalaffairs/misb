Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified or directly supervised by any one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Generator isolation and proper earthing is required.

  • Ensure that the circuit breaker disconnected and secondary disconnect.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety and healthy equipment helmet, shoes, ... etc

  • Complete tool set, Brushes

  • Megger, Digital voltammeter

 

 

Work to be Carried out

Inspection Steps

Item

Inspection Steps

Test Results

1

Clean the mating surface of the metal clad.

 

2

Check the control voltage value.

 

3

Check the racking mechanism to make certain that it is in the disconnect position.

 

4

Close the CB manually.

 

5

Open the CB manually.

 

6

Check the charging and discharging CB.

 

7

Check the closing time of breaker.

 

8

Check the tripping time of breaker.

 

9

Check the primary circuit insulation on the breaker phase to phase, phase to ground may be checked.

 

10

Check the DC Resistance

 
11

Lubricate the breaker operating mechanism.

 
12

Paint the CB and accessories.

 
13

Clean the disconnecting switch for the auxiliary transformer.

 
14

Check the disconnecting switch for the auxiliary transformer.

 

 

Rearrangement state: Connect all items which are disconnected.
