Introduction

This document details the items, which are checked daily or weekly or monthly as recommended and defined in Mitsubishi catalogs. This inspection is visual inspection during the site touring. There is no need to open the doors or covers of the operating mechanism housing and control cabinet for this inspection. If the sf6 gas low pressure alarm is given, SF6 gas should be supplied until the gas pressure reach the rated value. Sf6 gas can be supplied even with the breaker energized.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

    •

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety Helmet, safety shoes, ...etc.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of maintenance hand tools.

  • 220V ac supply.

  • Portable pressure gauge.

  • Emergency light.

 

Work to be Carried out

 

Circuit Breaker Proper

Exterior

  1. Check of abnormal noise.

  2. Check of rust or damage of components.

 

 

 

 

Gas System

  

Operating Mechanism Housing

  1. Check of gas pressure and temperature measurement.

 

Air System

  1. Check of air pressure (15 bars)

 

Control System

Operating Mechanism Housing

  1. Check the position of position indicator.

  2. Check and record the registered number of operation counters.

  3. Check of condensation and rust inside housing and water leak into housing.
