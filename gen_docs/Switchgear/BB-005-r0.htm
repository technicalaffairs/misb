Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belts ... etc.

  • Isolate and ground the Bus bar completely

  • Check by the LV tester rod.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool sets,

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, and masks ... etc

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower.

  • Voltage indicator.

  • Pieces of soft clothes, and cleaning liquid.

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Switched off the transformer which feeds the Bus Bar, and isolate the Bus Bar from the two sides by switching off the two bus ties at the two sides.

  2. Switch off all feeders at the Bus Bar and switch on their earthing switches.

  3. Insure that all 3 phases of each feeder at that busbar don't pass a current through it before draw the circuit breaker's out of the busbsar.

  4. Switch off DC source.

    5.

  5. Draw circuit breakers out of Bus Bar.

  6. Dismantle upper and lower shutters.

  7. Blow the dust and dirt out of Bus Bar (using vacuum cleaner)

  8. Clean the upper contactors (related to Bus Bar) and lower contactors (related to cables)

  9. Check current transformers to be well fixed, retighten bolts and screws and clean it.

  10. Retighten bolts and screws at links between Bus Bar parts and at links between Bus Bar and contactors, also at links between cable ends and contactors

  11. Check and clean potential transformer and its cell, also check and fix its capsules (cartridges), check and clean arresters

  12. Check the heaters in the cells to be switched on, clean it, put the indicator of each heater at the suitable position

  13. Check automatic fire fighting with sensible pipes, check the pipes to be far from cable ends put it away from any sharp edges, check the extinguisher cylinders to be in good condition

  14. Check earthing switch of each feeder cell to be in good contact (as it switched on), check also that it moves is easily

  15. Check cells to be well sealed and no way for rats to get into the cells

  16. Clean the upper parts of cells and the instruments involved in it such as protection apparatus, timers, measuring instruments, etc, using video cleaner

  17. Clean shutters well and put it in its places in the cells.

  18. Clean the passages that tracks across it in and out the cells, so breakers could get in and out of cells easily.

  19. Switch off earthing switches for all feeders cells.

  20. Put breakers in test position, switch on and off the breakers electrically, and then put them into their cells.
