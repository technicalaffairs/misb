Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belts ... etc.

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated and grounded from both sides..

  • Isolate the service voltage and control voltage.

  • The appropriate local safety regulation should be observed.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set,

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet .... etc,

  • Cleaning cloth, dry spry cleaner,

  • Grease, and special oil for CB,

  • Labels.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Take the CB out of the bus bar.

  2. General cleaning for the CB.

  3. Retighten the bolts and nuts for the CB.

  4. Check ON / OFF circuit of CB

  5. Oil and grease moving parts of the CB.

  6. Check the performance of the limit switches.

  7. Check relay and contactors of the circuit breaker.

  8. Grease the contact points of CB.

  9. Rearrangement state: Connect all items which are disconnected.
