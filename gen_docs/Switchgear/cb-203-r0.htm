Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed every four months as indicated by M4 in the title block

The technical specification of the CB max. rated voltage =145kv rated voltage is 132kv, rated current = 1250 A  short-circuit current 31.5KA

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintanance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment, as Safety helmet and safety shoes

  • Switch off the CB and grounded its sides

  • Switch off DC supply control

  • Switch off the charging current (DC) motor wounding spring

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable air blower, metal brush

  • Set of common, Screwdrivers, cleaning material (rag, cloth), water pump, metal wires

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the presence of any abnormal noise

  2. Check the presence of surface face of effects or cracks at break and supporting insulators

  3. Check the tightening of bolts and nuts

  4. Check the mechanism of operation

  5. Measure the bending of drive rod (< 3mm)

  6. Inspect the function of heaters

  7. Record counter reading of operations counter

  8. Clean the insulations and check for defects or cracks
