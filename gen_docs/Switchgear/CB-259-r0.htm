Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category. 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc.

  • Isolate and ground both sides of CB.

  • Switch off and isolate the control circuits i.e. de-energized

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool kit,

  • Cleaning tools.

  • Safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety harnesses ... etc.

  • Voltage indicator.

  • Dew point device, contact resistance device

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the high voltage terminal.

  2. Visual check and cleaning of the insulators.

  3. Visual check for control cubical.

  4. Measure the humidity of the SF6 gas "dew point" (commissioning value 1.25mg  H2O / L at 15◦C ).

  5. Measure the contact resistance across the main contacts.

  6. Current consumption of the operating coil.

  7. Check the sealing of the SF6 gas compartments and the compressed air system.

Important Notes

  1. It is necessary to open the break chamber if these tests are negative.

  2. In the case of doubt one chamber should be opened a visual check of the contact system should be made.

Before Opening

  1. Make sure that no pressure exit in the break chamber.

  2. The SF6 gas should be removed using the service wagon.

  3. It is recommended that protective clothing rubber gloves and respirator  are used with carrying out this work.
