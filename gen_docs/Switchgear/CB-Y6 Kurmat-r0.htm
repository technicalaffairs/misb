Introduction

  • This document details the items, which are checked on every Y6 years as recommended and defined in Mitsubishi catalogs "ordinary maintenance(Or)"  
  • In this inspection we inspect and maintain the parts outside of gas space.
  •  (No evacuation of sf6 gas) the component inside the sf6 gas compartments not to be checked in this inspection.
  • The items of previous inspection (regular inspection) should also be done during this inspection.

Safety Precautions

  • Open the circuit breaker and open the DS's at both sides of the breaker pole. Ground the main conductor.
  • Switch off the power supply to the control circuit and exhaust the compressed air from the drain valve of the air reservoir.
  • Be sure to insert the closing or tripping preventative pins at the time of internal inspection and inspection of moving parts.
  • Use designated lubricant.
  •  Do not smear the lubricant on the insulators.
  • Renew all the O-rings at the flanges which have been opened for inspection. Handle O-rings with special care to avoid any damage.
  • Do not disassemble the slide seal assemblies.
  • Tighten each bolt with a proper torque.
  • Test the leakage of all reassembled gas seals.
  • Compare the mechanical operation data obtained before and after the inspection to insure orderly operation of the breaker.
  • DON'T OPERATE THE CIRCUIT BREAKER BY THE PNEUMATIC OPERATING MECHANISM WHEN THE SF6 GAS PRESSURE IN THE ENCLOSURES IS BELOW THE LOCK PRESSURE

Environmental Recommendations

  • Fix any oil leaks from shock absorber oil to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil. Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impacts on the environment, mainly on soil and ground water and eventually on the ecosystem.

"It takes only I liter of Hydrocarbon spilled in an aquifer to render 1,000,000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption"

Work to be carried out

The inspection steps, which we do every month, should also be done in this inspection with addition to.

 

1- CIRCUIT BREAKER PROPER

* Exterior

  1. Check tightness of ground terminal and other installation.

  2. Check of abnormal noise.

  3.  Check of rust or damage of components.

2- GAS SYSTEM.

            * Circuit breaker proper

1.   Check of blind cover for gas feeding inlet.

* operating mechanism housing

1.   Check of gas pressure and temperature measurement.

2.   Check of setting of gas pressure switches:

Alarm 63 GA (ON) P1= 4.5 ± 0.3 kg /cm2

Block 63 GL (ON) P2= 4.0 ± 0.3 kg /cm2

P1-P2 = 0.5 ± 0.3 kg /cm2

3. Check open - close position of gas stop valve.

 

3- AIR SYSTEM

* operating mechanism housing

1.   Check of air pressure (15 bar).

4-OPERATING MECHANISM

      * operating mechanical housing

  1. Replacement of valve seat (3128) and valve seal (3318).
  2. Check of open - close of air stop valve.
  3. Check of lubrication of mechanism.
  4. Check of leakage of shock absorber oil.
  5. Check of looseness of retainer ring and nuts.

5- CONTROL SYSTEM

            * operating mechanism housing

  1. Check of connection of space heaters.
  2. Check of position of position indicator.
  3. Check and recording of registered number of operation counters.
  4. Check of condensation and rust inside housing and water leak into housing.
  5. Check of door packing.
  6. Check of tightness of bolts in housing.
  7. Check of control circuit wiring and terminals.

6- OPERATION TEST

  1. Check of manual opening and closing operation.
  2. Check of air reservoir capacity (from rated to lockout pressure).
  3.  Check of closing and opening time.      Closing < 90 ms. Opening < 18 ms.
