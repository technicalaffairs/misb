Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every 5 years bases indicated by Y5 in the title block.

The technical specifications of the circuit breaker: rated current = 1500A. and short circuit current is 21.2 kA, rated voltage is 145 kv, pneumatic mechanism.

The first draft is initiated and reviewed by eng. Soleiman Abd Elfattah Soleiman on 17/2/2000, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad general director, and approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station,  Hyd.EPCo.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear safety equipment's as helmet, shoes

  • Switch off the C.B and discharge the compressed air

  • Switch off the connectors at circuit breaker side

  • Switch off DC supply control of C.B

  • Connect the ground at circuit breaker sides

  • Put the suitable warning tages

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable air blower, metal brush

  • Telescope crane and lifting crane

  • Set of common, screw drivers, and cleaning material (rag, cloth)

  • USSR grease (220) and water pump

  • Micrometer, electronic breaking analyzer (type TM 1600 / MA 31)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Examine the external parts of (arc extinction, isolator) chambers

  2. Check the presence of surface defects or cracks at the (arc extinction, isolator)

  3. Check the tightening of the bolts and nuts

  4. Maintain the phase cabinets (opening and closing valves) and the main cabinet (filter, non returned valves) and air pressure manometers

  5. Dismantle the terminals of C.B side

  6. Dismantle and maintenance main piston and aux. Piston of mechanism of the arc extinction chamber and isolator chamber

  7. Check and maintenance the moving and fixed contacts

  8. Measurement the d.c resistance of arc extinction chamber isolator contacts and bus bar

  9. Measurement the resistance of parallel resistors and capacitance of capacitors

  10. Measurement the (cls., Op & dead times) and test switch (on & off) of C.B

  11. Clean the insulator of C.B

  12. Check hot spots of all tightening parts of C.B
