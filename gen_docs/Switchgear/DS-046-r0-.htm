Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses, don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

  • The rotary disconnect switch must be isolated completely and grounded on both sides, and isolate the DC voltage.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required.

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the isolator stack when cleaning insulators or checking connections wear your safety belt.

  • Be sure that the isolator is disconnected from all electric power sources.

  • Make sure the drive mechanism is locked and the power supply for the motor drive is interrupted.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness, Common set of screw drivers, set of keys,

  • Cleaning materials (rags, cloth), soft cloth, Electrical grease

  • Safety belt, safety helmet

  • Voltage indicator.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check all bolted connections for tightness.

  2. Clean and re-grease all moving joints.

  3. Check porcelains for chips, cracks, cementing, and fastenings

  4. Thoroughly clean all porcelain surfaces

  5. Check for signs of overheating or flashovers.

  6. The contacts should be examined for signs of burning after short-circuit stresses and the silver –plated contact surfaces being cleaned with a soft cloth.

  7. After cleaning, the contacts must be lightly re-greased with Vaseline or molykote.

  8. The bearing of the swivels are totally enclosed and greased for life, hence are maintenance-free over protracted periods (for  SEP 245 (AEG))

  9. It is recommended to grease the main bearings and the pivots of the coupling rods using one of the following types of grease: Mobil  (Mobilux 3) or Shell (Alvania R3). (for SSB11-AM-245 1600A (Hapam))

  10. Check the state of the high tension terminals.

  11. Inspect the equipment and the operating mechanism for good working condition.

  12. Inspect and check all safety interlocks and test for proper operation
