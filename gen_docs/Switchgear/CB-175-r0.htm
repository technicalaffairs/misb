Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

This checklist for the operating mechanism of the circuit breaker type: AM-13.8-750-5, rated voltage 11.5kv

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy tools must be used  as safety Helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Switch off input and output supply

  • Generator must be fully isolated 

  • Circuit breaker for generator must be isolated and grounded.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • Digital voltmeter, megger

  • Brush, vacuum cleaner or air blower

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the mating surfaces of the metal clad.

  2. Check the racking mechanism to ensure it is in the disconnection position.

  3. Check the voltage control at the power source and check that there is no line drop between the power source and the breaker.
