Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every 6:8 years as indicated by Y6:8 in the title block

The technical specification of the CB Rated current = 2000A, Rated voltage = 500 kv, Short circuit current = 40 kA

The first draft initiated by eng. Soliman Abdel Fatah Soliman at High Dam Power Station, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad: general director, approved by eng. Abde: Karem Abu Zaid: High Dam head Sector, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Haleem: EPGC quality control general director, MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

    •

  • Switch off the disconnectors on CB sides and grounded the CB sides

  • Switch off DC & AC supply (CB is electrically dead)

  • Switch off the charging current DC motor wound spring

  • The CB in not under pressure (all compressed air is released)

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable air blower, metal brush

  • Set of common screw drivers, and cleaning material (arg, cloth)

  • Water pump

  • Micrometer

  • Elect. breaker timing analyzer (analyzer program TM 1600 / AM 31)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visual inspection of the condition of the breaker together with a check of the (gas pressure, insulators, operating mechanism and heating elements

  2. Check air compressor lubricant level

  3. Check air compressor for lubricant leaks

  4. Wash air compressor inlet filter sleeve

  5. Wash all housing air filters: main control, phase and compressor

  6. Check air compressor belt tension

  7. Drain condensation for air tank

  8. Replace inner filter on air compressor inlet if holes or tears develop

  9. Clean all parts of the compressor

  10. Change air compressor lubricant and filter

  11. Perform the test of (start - stop) operations compressors

  12. Removal of inspection locks in order that all the mechanism parts are accessible for cleaning, rust protection, and lubrication

  13. Touch up paining, if necessary

  14. Check that the functional values of the breaker still are within design limits

  15. Check air compressor valve for leakage and carbon

  16. Clean the insulators of CB
