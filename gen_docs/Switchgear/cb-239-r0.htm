Introduction

This document outlines the minor reconditioning  procedures P1,,which must be performed every 8-14 years as indicated by Y8:14 in the title block.

This minor reconditioning must be conducted by the manufacturer or by specially trained station staff.

The contact resistance of the poles must be measured taken as a guide to decide the reconditioning or not

The first draft was initiated and tested by eng. Naser El Sayed Abd EL-Aziz, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman Selim, approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel-Baset  head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Deenergise and ground the breaker

  • Interrupt the control circuit of the motor

  • Discharge the closing and opening springs by operating the manual levers in the operating mechanism until no more operations (on-off) are possible

  • Interrupt the control circuit of the close and trip coils

  • The lifting device (crane) must be earthed

Tools and Equipment

  • Contact resistance measuring device

  • Depth gauge

  • Dry rags

  • Alchohol for cleaning

  • Slow motion device

  • Lifting device (crane).

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. The control resistance is previously measured and if it is ≥ 35 µΩ the reconditioning should be performed

  2. Now the circuit breaker is "open" the springs are discharged and the control circuits of the motor and coils are interrupted as mentioned above

  3. The SF6 gas must be sucked from the breaker throw special service cart to be filtered and stored

  4. Erect the slow motion device towards "on" position till hearing the beep of the tester which is connected to the breaker terminals - this beep means that the connecting pin between the rod and the module became outside the insulator and can be dismantled

  5. Connect the crane to the upper terminal of the module

  6. Discrew the bolts which connect the module insulator with the support insulator

  7. Dismantle the connecting pin between the module and the rod by pulling the split pin

  8. The module contains all parts of contacts (parallel contact - contact crown - arcing pin - nozzel - arcing sleeve - gas flow guide), all of these parts must be cleaned several times with rage and alchohol if they are in good condition, but if they have any damage or corrosion they must be completely changed

  9. After finishing each part must be re-erected in the same way as before and according to the overal procedures

  10. Replace the humidity absorber by new one

  11. Replace all seals by new ones

  12. Perform the eccentrisity test with the aid of special tool for this the eccentricity test indicates if all module parts are correctly erected or not

  13. After ensuring that the module is ready, it must be re-erected

  14. Remove the slow motion device

  15. Evacuate the breaker for at least 1/2 hour and refill it by SF6 gas with the nominal pressure and according to the pressure - temp curve

  16. Reconnect the connections between the breaker terminals and each of busbar isolators and current transformers

  17. Operate the breaker mechanically (on - off) several times

  18. Reconnect the control circuit of the motor and closing and opening coils

  19. Operate the breaker (on - off) electrically

  20. Measure the contact resistance of the three poles while the breaker is electrically closed it should be < 35 µΩ

  21. Measure the closing time and tripping time they should be:
         * Closing time : 140 ms
         * Opening time : 25 ms

  22. Check the SF6 pressure of the breaker (alarm & trip) according to the concept of the breaker and pressure - temp curve

  23. Remove earths tools instruments etc from the breaker pit

  24. Now the breaker is ready to put into service

Appraisal of contact components

If reconditioning activities are initiated as a result of the "permissible burning of contacts" criterion or if L is < 97 mm replace the following components:

  • Arcing pin 1.36

  • Blast nozzle 1.31

  • flow member 1.34

  • Retaining ring 10

  • Retaining plate 11

  • O-ring 12

These remaining components can be reused after cleaning provided they are in perfect condition:

  • Screening cap 8

  • Pressure ring 9

  • Contact crown 1.33

Especially observe on:
Screening cap 8: deformation
Pressure ring 9 : the edge at D must be sharp
