Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, ... etc.

  • Surge Arrester out of service.

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the arrester when cleaning insulators or checking connections wear your safety belt.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required

Tools and Equipment 

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt ... etc.

  • Rags, Fiberglass ladder.

  • Voltage indicator, thermovision gun.

  • Tool set for tightness, cleaning tools as cloth .... etc

Work to be Carried out

  1. Check that there is no voltage, clean all porcelain insulators surfaces of surge arrestor.

    2.

  2. Check porcelain for cracks and chipped skirts.

  3. Check for loose caps and tighten if necessary.

  4. If fitted, read and record discharge counter reading.

  5. Put the surge arrester into service and carry out thermo vision test for hotspot for its connection with network.
