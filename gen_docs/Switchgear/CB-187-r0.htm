Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list I1, which must be performed on a year bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The technical specification of the breaker: Rated current 600 A, 33 kv, 500 MVA, 8.75 kA

The first draft is initiated and reviewed by eng. Nagy Bekheit Saleh, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad general director, and approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station,  Hyd.EPCo.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety helmet, safety shoes ..etc

  • Switch off the CB, the isolating disconnecting switch

  • Switch of the DC current.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set and cleaning materials (cloth - benzene)

  • Oil for lubbricating

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Drain off the oil, remove lower covers, with stationary contacts and take out explosion chambers spacing cylinders

  2. Repair contacts and chamber, examine and clean other parts of the breaker

  3. Clean the damaged places in the fixed contacts, wash them by benzene and wipe them dry

  4. Replace badly burned contacts with large pits

  5. Inspect and clean all insulating parts

  6. Check and clean the spring damper and then lubricate it

  7. Check and tightening all the breaker fastenings

  8. Inspects and cleans the moving contacts

  9. Lubricate the CB mechanism

  10. Check the CB adjustment. Travel inside the contacts, total travel of the moving contacts, difference between the contact rod extreme and working positions

  11. Check the operation of the CB mechanism connected with the operating mechanism at smooth operation
