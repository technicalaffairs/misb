Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six years as indicated by Y6 in the above title block

Competence Requirement: To be carried by trained staff. Adopt the relevant safety precautions; an opening of the pole columns is not necessary.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belts ... etc.

  • The circuit breaker must be isolated and grounded from both sides..

  • The position indicator of the closing spring shows " discharged "

  • Isolate the service voltage and control voltage.

  • The appropriate local safety regulation should be observed.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety belt, safety helmet.

  • No special tools needed.

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visual check for damage especially porcelains.

  2. Check of corrosion on metallic parts and piping.

  3. Check the ventilation apertures: (operating mechanism, control cubicle, linkage cover sheet).

  4. Check of the anti condensation heater.

  5. Check of the SF6 pressure: (the SF6 density monitor in the green range).
