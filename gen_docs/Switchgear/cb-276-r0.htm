Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belts ... etc.

  • The breaker should be switched off and disconnected out of its cell.

  • Disconnect the dc control voltage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool of common keys and screw drivers.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes .

  • Brushes ,grease and air blower .

  • Digital multimeter .

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the breaker from any dust.

  2. Check screw connections for tightness.

  3. Close and open the breaker mechanically.

  4. Check the manual charging and discharging of the mechanism.

  5. Check the connections of earthing system .

  6. Check the cable socket of the breaker.

  7. Check pushbuttons, singnal lamps ,fuses and auxiliarty relays.

  8. Check for SF6 leakage.

  9. Check the value of control voltage.

  10. Check the charging and discharging of the breaker electrically.

  11. Check the operation of the breaker from local in test position .

  12. Check the operation of the breaker from remote in test position .

  13. Record the counter reading.
