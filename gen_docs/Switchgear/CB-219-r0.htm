Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified or directly supervised by any one who is.

    •

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Generator isolation and proper earthing is required.

  • Ensure that the circuit breaker disconnected and secondary disconnect.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety helmet, shoes

  • Complete Tool set, Brushes

  • Megger, Digital voltmeter

Work to be Carried out

Item

Inspection steps

Test results

1

Replace the defective components.

 

2

Replace the worn parts in accordance with relevant instructions.

 

3

Check the operation of controls.

 

4

Measure the characteristic values of insulating and arc-quenching media. Tightening of screw connections. 

 

5

Lubricate and replace grease and oil.

 

6

Cleaning.

 

7

Make adjustments to ensure reliable operation.

 

8

Measurement of service characteristics; such as: 

A-Minimum pressure limit.

B-Switching times.

C-Insulation values pertaining to secondary circuits.

  

 Rearrangement state: Connect all items which are disconnected.
