Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed from three to six years or 2,000 mechanical CLOSE/OPEN operations as indicated by Y3:6 in the title block

Competence Requirement: 

Personnel authorized by ABB to carry out assembly, commissioning and maintenance work

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belts ... etc.

  • Isolate and ground both sides of CB.

  • Switch off and isolate the control circuits i.e. de-energized

  • Closing springs discharged, circuit breaker in open position and grounded.

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the CB stack when cleaning insulators or checking connections wear your safety belt.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool kit.

  • Cleaning tools.

  • Safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety harnesses ... etc.

  • Thermovision camera.

  • Electronic timer.

  • Voltage indicator.

Work to be Carried Out

Important Note:

All HPL circuit breakers are completely hose-proof, i.e. circuit breakers in operation    can be cleaned with high-pressure water without any risk of water entering the circuit breaker and causing damage to essential parts.

Ocular Inspection

  1. Clean the circuit breaker insulators from salt and other contaminant deposits. The time interval for cleaning depends on the local conditions.

  2. Check external cleaning , heater element function, gas pressure, valves and drainage holes .

  3. A new or overhauled circuit breaker should be checked for tightness by reading the density monitors after about 1 month and then at 1-2 year intervals in connection with the regular ocular inspection.

Rating Plates

Make a note of the serial number.

Operation Counter

Make a note of the number of operations.

Visual Inspection in Operating Mechanism

Look for loose bolts and nuts, oil leaks from dashpots. If leakage replace the complete dashpot

Corrosion Inspection and Rust Protection

  1. Visual inspection, rust stains polished off and treated with rust protection

  2. Certain steel parts in the operating mechanism have manganese surface treatment and are then dipped in a rust inhibiting oil. This applies to latches, gear wheels, parts of the link system, etc.

  3. The closing and opening springs are rust inhibited by painting with two-component paint .

  4. When supplied, the operating mechanism is equipped with anti-corrosion vapour phase inhibitors.These inhibitors are only for protection during transport. Despite the extensive rust inhibitor treatment, minor corrosion can occur after some years especially if the circuit-breaker is located in a strongly corrosive environment. Rust spots should be cleaned and new rust inhibitor applied with a brush or spray.

Important Note:

The recommended rust inhibitor is Grease ,,G,, or Tectyl 506, Grease ,,G,, USED for greasing sealing rings and crevice corrosion protection.  (Grease ,,G,,  Lubricants 1HSB445409-1), Tectyl 506 forms a waxy protective skin when it dries and therefore it must not be sprayed into bearings and mechanisms or on to gear-wheels and chains.

Greasing

The ball, roller and needle bearings in the operating mechanism are permanently lubricated and require no maintenance. Lubrication is therefore limited to shafts in the latches of the operating mechanism and the worm gear in the drive unit with grease ,,M,,.

Important Note:

Grease ,,M,, is Low-temperature grease for long-term and   permanent lubrication of worm gears, spur gears and other machine elements. Counteracts wear and corrosion, (Grease ,,M,, special  Lubricants 1HSB445409-1).

Thermovision Measurements

  1. To be carried out at full load before the circuit-breaker is taken out of service. The load current must be taken into consideration during the measurements as well as large variations in the load current 3-4 hours before the measurements.

  2. Check the temperature rise of the external parts of the breaker unit. The maximal allowed temperature difference between phases is 5 degrees . 

Measurement  Spring Charging

Measure the dimension between the spring bridge and frame foot .                          

Operating Test

Operating test in combination with measurement of operating times

-  Closing time < 65 ms               -  Opening time coil I  20 ± 2 ms                       

-  Opening time coil II  20 ± 2ms
