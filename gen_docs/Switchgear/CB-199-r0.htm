Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc.

  • All Breakers must be disconnected and out of service.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set, grease

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes .

  • Brushes, Blower, mugger device and digital voltmeter .

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Clean breaker from any dust.

  2. Check screw connections for tightness.

  3. Grease the Breaker operating mechanism.

  4. Clean arcing contacts by means smooth of file.

  5. Close the Breaker mechanically.

  6. Open the Breaker mechanically.

  7. Check the charging and discharging Breaker manually.

  8. Check the connection of earthing system.

  9. Check cable sockets of Breaker.

  10. Check push buttons , signal lamps, fuses and auxiliary relays.

  11. Check control voltage value.

  12. Check charging and discharging Breaker electrically.

  13. Check operation of breaker from local in test position.

  14. Check operation of breaker from remote in test position.

  15. Check the interlocks for operation of Breaker.

  16. Check interconnection terminals.

  17. Check all protection devices by protection group.
