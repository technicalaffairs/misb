Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc

  • Switch off the CB and withdraw it from its cell

  • Switch off the DC power supply for this CB

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy tools as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Cleaning materials (cloth - kerosene), Tool set

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean it of carbon deposit and metal splashes, especially on the insulating parts.

  2. Humps on contacts surface should be removed by filing.

  3. Examine secondary and arcing contacts, and replace them when necessary.

  4. All friction parts should be lubricated with grease.

  5. Test the break manually several times.
