Introduction        CB -014, 015, 048, 049   for 220kv يراجع مع مستندات باسوس      

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated  by M1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated at Ayon Moussa PS, East Delta Electricity Production Company

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses,

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off, fully isolated, and grounded on both sides,

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker,

  • Switch off control current for CB (d.c).

Environmental Precautions

  • Before using a blower to clean the outside compartment of the circuit breaker from dust, wear your breathing mask to protect your lung tissues from the dust particulate.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set,

  • SF6 gas detector,

  • Air blower, and rags,

  • Safety helmet, shoes, glove, and breathing mask,

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visual inspection for all parts of circuit breaker.

  2. Check AC supply source.

  3. Check DC supply source.

  4. Record on / off operations counter and compare with the previous month.

  5. Record the oil pump operations counter.

  6. Earth connections must be checked.

  7. Clean all outside compartment of circuit breaker from dust, oil and other things using blower and rags.

  8. Retighten all bolts and earth connection parts if necessary.

  9. Visual inspection of foundation and fixation of circuit breaker.

  10. Complete cleaning for mechanism parts.

  11. Check oil level of main tanks for three phases.

  12. Check oil leaks for all connections.

  13. Check the heater operation.

  14. Check door gaskets for all panels.
