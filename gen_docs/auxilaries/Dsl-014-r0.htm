Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks to surround the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, earplug, gloves pairs, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc

  • Be sure that the emergency diesel is out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Measuring and checking tools.

  • Cleaning articles.

  • Magnetic pick–up flexible.

  • Spray gun, Grease gun

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check engine oil, add or change it.

  2. Check oil filter and clean it.

  3. Inspect for oil leaks of connections, and treat any leakage.

  4. Check fuel oil level, and readjust it.

  5. Inspect coolant elements (water level – leakage).

  6. Check fuel strainer and fuel filter or change it.

  7. Check the fuel pump and rotation of it.

  8. Grease the fanes of cooling system.

  9. Drain water and sediment from the fuel tank.

  10. Drain water and sediment from the air tank.

  11. Check air starting motor.

  12. Clean air start screen and check the differential pressure.

  13. Check injection synchronization.
