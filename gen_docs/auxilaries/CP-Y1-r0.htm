Introduction      Should be compared with CP-007

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list PI, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The technical specifications of the air compressor. Stationary, three stage, single acting one crank, air cooled machine 180m3/h, 40hg/cm2

The first draft is initiated & reviewed by eng. Nagy Bekheit Saleh, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad general director, and approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station,  Hyd.EPCo.

 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear safety equipment`s, helmet

  • Switch off the A.C current

  • Close the outlet valve 

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet

  • Cleaning materials (cloth - kerosene)

  • Tool set

    •

Work to be Carried Out

In addition to the work done in, (P3-M6) do the following

  1. Replacement of connecting rods dig end bushing if required

  2. Replacement of bushings with subsequent boring of the piston and crosshead bosses

  3. Grind the piston pin surface

  4. Replace the gaskets

  5. Replace the cylinders, pistons, and pistons ring by new ones if required

  6. Inspect, clean the pump gears

  7. Repair and clean the cooler unit with soda

  8. Inspect the compressor foundation
