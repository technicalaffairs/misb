Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the personal protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Isolate all AC supplies, auxiliary supplies and the battery (open isolator, remove battery fuses or remove a battery link) before working on the inverter and never handle a component or assembly without personally confirming that no voltage is applied or present.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Multimeter

  • Air blower

  • Contact resistance tester

  • Cleaning solvent 

  • Megger (500 – 5000) volt

Inverter Unit, Calibration of Card IWM / IWT (AIA4)

No.

Procedure

Position

Test Result

1

Set the switch ICSC (ICSC is mounted on the lower left of the inverter) in the CAPACITORS CHARGE position

Keep it in this position until the leds on the logic cards (IPS-ISM/IST-IWM/IWT) light up, then release it.

OK – NOT OK

2

Close the input switch IEI before the leds extinguish.

Check that the INPUT ON/OFF control, located on the front panel, is OFF, and the switch on the IPS is ON.

 OK – NOT OK

3

With a tester, verify the presence between TPI (INVERTER FREQUENCY) and ground of card IWM/IWT of a 5V direct voltage (corresponding to a line frequency of 50 Hz).

OK – NOT OK

4

If this voltage is not present, adjust potentiometer A4PI to obtain the value indicated.

 OK – NOT OK

5

Enable inverter switch-on. To do this, after having checked that all the switches on the cards are ON, proceed as follows:

Set INPUT ON/OFF at ON.

Set IUI at ON.

OK – NOT OK

6

Measure, by connecting the tester to the terminals of the voltmeter located on the front panel,

The value of the output voltage (depending on the size of the inverter)

 OK – NOT OK

7

If necessary, adjust potentiometer AIA4P3 unit the desired voltage value is obtained.

OK – NOT OK
