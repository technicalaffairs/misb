Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safty category

  • Safty fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc.

  • Shutdown the power supply of the crane.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool kit of screw drivers and common keys.

  • Digital multimeter

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

  • Lubricant (  MOBILPLEX 48 )

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the rope condition.

  2. Apply the lubricant ( MOBILPLEX 48 ) to:

    Hoist rope: The condition of the wire rope is of prime importance, it should be greased little but often, avoiding the use of lubricants containing petrol.

    Rope drum:  By hand through the opening above the gearbox.
