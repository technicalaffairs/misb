Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safty category

  • Safty fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt ...etc.

  • Shutdown the power supply of the crane.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool kit of screw drivers and common keys.

  • Digital multimeter

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

  • Lubricant  MOBILPLEX 48 and MOBIL VACTRA OIL .

  • Brush or scrapper.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Apply the lubricant ( MOBILPLEX 48 ) to:

    1. Hoist rope : The condition of the wire rope is of prime importance, it should be greased little but often , avoiding the use of  lubricants containing petrol.

    2. Rope drum :  By hand through the opening above the gearbox.

  2. Remove the old grease of the teeth of the travel wheels and drive pinions by using brush or scrapper and smear on new grease ( semi fluid MOBILPLEX 48 ) not in excess.

  3. For the flat cable conductor system, remove the track end  stop at line start end and withdraw trolleys from track, then apply (MOBIL VACTRA OIL) at 50˚c between wheel and trolley frame.

  4. Travel wheels bearings should be packed with the ( MOBILPLEX 48) during wheels assembly.

  5. Inspect and grease the limit switch bar and particularly where it passes through the rope drum and plates ensuring that  it slides freely.

  6. Ensure that the push button box is properly suspended by the supporting wire or chain.

  7. Check the condition of the carriers of the enclosed conductor system.

  8. Ensure all fixing screw of the flexible cable is tight.

  9. Ensure that the moving parts of contactors moving freely.

  10. Check that all terminals and fuses of the transformer are tight.

  11. Check for proper connection and tightness of the terminal block .

  12. Inspect the wire rope carefully. It should be replaced immediately for the least suspicion in its quality (broken wires , permanent deformation and so on ).

  13. Ensure that the rope guide and pressure ring assembly in position without interfering with the passage of the wire rope and that the tension spring is effective.

  14. Ensure that the disc brake is effective.  Examine the disc for any uneven wear.
