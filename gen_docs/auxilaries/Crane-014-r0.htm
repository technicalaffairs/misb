Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safty category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt ...etc.

Personnel

  • It is forbidden to assign operation of lifting equipment to personnel with inadequate knowledge of instructions resulting in improper accomplishment of these functions and to personnel not satisfying a preliminary medical test. The same applies to personnel responsible for guiding crane movements by means of signals to the operators.

  • It is  inhibited to employ personal below the age of 18

  • The working personnel should appropriately and effectively be informed about:

    • The legal and regulator prescription for equipment safety.

    • The dangers inherent to crane utilization.

    • The precautions to be observed before, during and after utilization and / or intervention on the crane, may it be normal or exceptional.

    • Adapted and efficient individual (helmet, harness) or collective (markings, nets) protection facilities should be made available to working personnel during the course of their duty, thus ensuring them with maximum safety during utilization and/or intervention on the crane, may it be normal or exceptional.

       

  • The tests checks inspections will be carried out by duly qualified and specialized technicians either from the same establishment or from an organism which regularly carries out such specific activities

  • Maintenance operations will be carried out by a qualified person perfectly knowing the equipment and the installations. All mechanical or electrical adjustment operations should be carried out by an authorized and qualified person, in particular as concerns the safety devices

Cranes

  • The cranes and lifting accessories should be maintained in a good operating condition in compliance with the maintenance periodicities listed in the maintenance and test operations and adapted to the operating rates.

  • When a crane in perfect operating condition is stored for a period of more than 3 months, should be inspected from all points of view in accordance with the instructions given in the maintenance and check operations.

  • Following a malfunction or damage in the crane, should undergo the following operation:

    • Inspection to determine the exact cause of the incident and damage caused.

    • An adapted maintenance intervention

    • A complete correct operation check

Lifting Accessories

Cables 

  • Any cable with the following defects should be removed from service:

    • Bulge

    • Constriction

    • Deformation

    • Broken strand

    • A visible number of broken wire, counted over to setting steps, equal or greater than 20 % of the total number of wires composing the cable

Lifting Chains

  • Any lifting chain, even comprising a single link, presenting the following defects should be removed from service:

    • Deformed

    • Flattened out

    • Open

    • Elongated or worn

  • Replacement of a link or a lifting chain should be performed by a chain manufacturer

Cables, Cords, Chains

  • Cables, cords, chains should:

    • Be calculated, selected, laid out and maintained so as to prevent rupture, slippage or be cut

    • Not contain any knots

    • Lifting accessories should be clearly marked with the lifting capacity

 

 

Hooks

  • The suspension hooks shall be of a type that prevents unhooking

Crane Utilization

  • It is forbidden to allow personnel to carry out maintenance interventions during normal utilization of the crane

  • To carry out maintenance operations, access points have been provided for personnel thus avoiding dangerous movements

  • If works are executed in non-protected areas, the person concerned should be protected against risks of falls.

  • Estimate the load which should be less than or equal to the nominal load marked on the crane or recommended during the various regulatory checks or tests

  • It is absolutely forbidden to use this crane for carrying persons

  • The load should be fastened so that it doesn’t:

    • Tip over

    • Slip

    • Damage the grasping accessory

  • Only lift the load to the height required for its transport or for the regulatory test

  • The loads should not be transported above persons

  • Avoid sudden movements, shocks, simultaneous movements

  • Slowly deposit the load, ensuring its stability

  • Don't sway the loads to deposit them at a point which cannot be reached by the lifting equipment

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool kit of screw drivers and common keys.

  • Digital multimeter.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

  • Lubricant

  • Brush or scrapper.

Work to be Carried Out

Crane Traveling

  1. Check operation of the brake type: NM

  2. Check bearing efficiency

  3. Check operation of Position Switch (1)

Winch Trolley Traversing

  1. Check operation of the brake type: NM

  2. Check bearing efficiency.

  3. Check operation of Position Switch

 

 

Hoist Trolley Traversing

  1. Check operation of Position Switch (1)

Winch Lifting Group: 1Bm, 1Am

  1. Check operation of the brake (1)

  2. Check bearing efficiency.

  3. Check operation of Position Selecting Switch Inductive Detector

Hoisting

  1. Check operation of the brake (1) for  Hoist Type XL Group: 1Bm, 1Am  

  2. Check bearing efficiency.

General

  1. Check operation of radio control (1)
