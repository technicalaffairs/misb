Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued,

  • The maintenance staff must be qualified and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required..

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ear plugs, rubber, gloves, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the Diesel Engine.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

    

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Soft brush

  • Suitable lifting device, Feeler gauge

  • Hammer, Torque wrench, Manual puller

  • Dial indicator.

  • K&N air filter cleaner, K&N air filter oil

  • Engine Oil type: SAE grade 50, Quantity: 20 liter /Year

Work to be Carried out

  1. Run Engine ( per NFPA20 Specification ).

  2. Make general inspection for the engine.

  3. Check all anchor bolts and support steel fasteners bolts and tighten if necessary, Fuel tank and lubricating oil.

  4. Check cooling system, exhaust system, operating gauges, jacket water heater & coolant hose inspection.

  5. Check fuel lines.

  6. Clean battery.

  7. Check Air Cleaner- Dry type ( -03AN & -04AN ).

  8. Check Drive belts condition.

  9. Check Speed control.

  10. Check Exhaust System.

  11. Check Manual Contractors.

  12. Check Governor Run- stop Mechanism.

  13. Replace Lubricating oil.

  14. Replace air cleaner – dry type (-03 & -04 )

  15. Replace Fuel & lube oil filters.

  16. Check crank case vent system.

  17. Replace cooling system.

  18. Replace air cleaner – dry type (-03 & -04 ).

  19. Check heat exchanger electrode.

  20. Replace coolant hose.

Air Filter Service Instructions

 

1. Pre-cleaning

Tap the element to dislodge any large embedded dirt, then gently brush with a soft bristle brush.

Note: If complete cleaning is not practical at this time, re-oil the element and re-install in your vehicle.

2. Spray on cleaner

Spray K&H air filter cleaner liberally onto the entire element and let soak for 10 minutes.

 

3. PAN cleaning

Large K&H elements can be rolled or soaked in a shallow pan of K&H air filter cleaner. Remove immediately and let soak for approximately 10 minutes.

 

4. Cleaning hints

 

Use only K&H air filter cleaner.

   

No gasoline cleaning.
No steam cleaning.
No caustic cleaning solutions.
No strong detergents.
No high pressure car wash.
No parts cleaning solvents.

 

Any of these No`s can cause harm to the cotton filter media. Plus shrink and harden the rubber end caps.

5. Rinse off

 

Rinse off the element with low pressure water. Tap water is ok always flush from the clean side to dirty side this removes the dirt and does not drive it into the filter.

 

6. Drying hints

 

Always dry naturally after rinsing shake off all excess water and let the element dry naturally.

     

DO NOT USE compressed air.
DO NOT USE open flame.
DO NOT USE heat dryers.
 

Excess heat will shrink the cotton filter media..

 
Compressed air will blow holes in the element.

7. Aerosol oiling

  

After cleaning air filter always re-oil before using spray K&H air filter oil down into each pleat with one pass per pleat wall 10 minutes and re-oil any white spots still showing.

 

 

 

 

8. Squeeze bottle oiling
 

After cleaning air filter always re-oil before using squeeze K&H air filter oil down into the bottom and along each pleat only one pass per pleat let oil wick into cotton for 20 minutes re-oil any white spots still showing.

 

 

 

 

9. Oiling hints
      

 Never use a K&H air filter without oil. (The filter will not stop the dirt without the oil) Use only K&H formulated air filter oil.

     
K&H air filter oil is compound of mineral and animal oil blended with special polymers to form a very efficient tack barrier. Red dye is added to show just where you have applied the oil. Eventually the red color will fade but the oil will remain and filter the air.

     

Never use automatic transmission fluid
Never use motor oil
Never use diesel fuel
 Never use WD-40, LPS, or other light weight
