Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Wgdy Roshdy Habeb, tested by eng. Samy Shehata, accepted by eng. Hany Fahmy Madny; general manager, approved by eng. Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, ... etc.

  • Stop the Elevator and switch off the power supply.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Set of common screw drivers, wrenches

  • Cleaning material, grease for lubricating.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check up the oil in the machine and motor bearing.

  2. Adjust the machine oil level or change it if necessary.

  3. Adjust the motor bearing oil level if necessary.

  4. Check up the brake and change the brake shoes if necessary.

  5. Clean the machine parts and its items from outside.

  6. Grease the machine parts and the pulleys machine room.

  7. Clean the cabin guides and oil it.

  8. Check up the doors.

  9. Check up the rope belts on the cabinet.

  10. Check up the stop of cabinet in all elevations.

  11. Operate the elevator with full load and readjust the stopping of the cabinet in the floors.
