Introduction

This document lists the minor maintenance procedure P1 of emergency diesel engine, which must be, performed every 5000-operation hr base as indicated by H 5000 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Hesham M. Hassan, reviewed by Maintenance director: eng. Saad Mostafa, revised by eng. Samir Abdel-Gelil at EDEPCo., accepted by maintenance general director: eng. Ali Mostafa Al-lithy and approved by eng.: Mostafa Alam Head  Sector of Talkha steam power station 2 x 210 MW.

After isolated the diesel engine mechanically and electrically

  1. Dismantle the engine for inspection specified below (See major maintenance procedure)

  2. Clean and wash all the units, clean all pipes and oil channels.

  3. Inspect all functional units and check their condition.

  4. Check mainly the following assemblies and parts:

Cylinder head

  1. Remove the cylinder heads

  2. Clean suction and exhaust valves chamber for any carbon deposits

  3. Re-machine the exhaust valve seats on the milling machine and grind them. 

  4. Check Suction and exhaust valves springs from any fatigues and renew if any.

  5. Readjust Starting, pressure relief and fuel injecting valve

  6. Clean Water spaces in cylinder heads.

  7. Check Rocker arm pins, bushes and rollers from any mechanical wear and replace if the clearance w.r.t clearance table no. 1 at MPIS doc no. Dsl-009-r0

  8. Check the tightness of all parts according to MPIS # Dsl-009-r0

 

The Pistons, connecting rods, and Cylinder liners

  1. Check the  Piston pins (must fit firmly in piston) from any mechanical wear and replace if the clearance w.r.t clearance table no. 1, 2 at MPIS doc no. Dsl-009-r0

  2. Check the Piston condition (jacket and piston crown, hole for piston pin, grooves for rings)

  3. Check the  Piston pins bushes, Connecting rod bearings, and Piston rings from any mechanical wear and replace if the clearance w.r.t clearance table no. 1 at MPIS doc no. Dsl-009-r0

  4. Check for wear and replace if needed w.r.t clearance table no. 1, 2 at MPIS doc no. Dsl-009-r0

Camshaft:

  1. Check the Cams (Keying on),  Lifters, Rollers and lifters pin, Camshaft bearings from any mechanical wear and replace if the clearance w.r.t clearance table # 1, 2 at MPIS doc # Dsl-009-r0

Timing gear and governor drive:

  1. Check the Gears,  Lifters, Pin and inter mediate gear bush from any mechanical wear and replace if the clearance w.r.t clearance table # 1, 2 at MPIS doc no. Dsl-009-r0

Starting equipment

  1. Check the valves of starting equipment from any leakage, repair or replace if needed.

  2. Clean the air distributor filter.

Governor and regulation

  1. Check the governor and regulation parts for any abnormal condition, repair or replace if needed.

 Lower part of engine case and crank shaft :

  1. Check the alignment and end floats of crankshaft and realignment if needed.

  2. Check the  crankshaft, crank pins, and main bearings from any mechanical wear or cracks and replace if the clearance w.r.t clearance table no. 1, 2 at MPIS doc no. Dsl-009-r0

Front case with pumps

  1. Check the driving wheels, gear, bushes, and bearings of shaft of fuel oil pump, for any mechanical wear or cracks and replace if the clearance w.r.t clearance table no. 1, 2 at MPIS doc no. Dsl-009-r0

  2. Readjust the regulating valve. 

All the measuring, control and signaling instruments

  1. Check the end floats measuring for wear limits w.r.t clearance table no. 1, 2 at MPIS doc no. Dsl-009-r0

  2. Check the tightness of fuel oil piping and of injecting equipment (Injecting pumps, injecting valves)

  3. Carry out the tightness test mainly of valves and piping)

  4. Visually inspect for seizure, carbon deposits, corrosion, scales, etc

  5. Carry out the pumps performance for adjusting safety, control valves, gearing, etc.
