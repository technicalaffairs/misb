Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection  I3, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 as indicated in the title block.

The technical specification of gear box, power rating 1800 kw input speed 90.58 RPM, output speed 1000 RPM, gear ratio 0.9058, oil quantity 550 L

The first draft is initiated, tested and approved by eng. Abd El Mohsen Mohammed general manager of 

Nag Hamady hyd. PS

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintanance staff should carry the suitable safty category

  • Stop the crane and switch off the power supply.

  • Put suitable warning boards

  • Safety fence caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes.

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of wrenches, Screw driver

  • Grease for lubricaying

  • Cleaning material

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check up control and safety instrument

  2. Check up the noise of electrical pump and readjusted the mechanical pump

  3. Change the gear box oil

  4. Check up the bearing temperature of gear box

  5. Check up the noise at operation

  6. Clean the filter of cooling line
