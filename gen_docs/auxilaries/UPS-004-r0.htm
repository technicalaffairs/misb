Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection. which must be performed every 6 months as indicated by M6 in the title block for inverter (u.p.s) with the following data:
   - AC input: 380v, 50hz, 3ph, AC output: 380v, 23A 50hz, 3ph
   - Static By bass 380V, 23A, 50 HZ, 3PH

The first draft initiated by : Eng. Abdel Aziz  EL Bagoury, tested by eng. Doaa Anis, revised by quality control Eeng. Adel  Abdel EL Kader EL Nagar at EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Mahmoud Saker and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talkha PS 2x210 MW

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on a work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set .

  • Digital voltmeter.

  • Brush., Blower

 Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check for correct operation of  DC room air  condition.

  2. Check ventilation fan of (UPS).

  3. Read AC input voltage, AC output voltage, AC input current, AC output current,  and output  rectifier voltage DC and record.

  4. Switch off ( U.P.S ) input and output supply.

  5. Check all abnormal cases inside ( U.P.S ).

  6. Check over heating compartment of rectifier cards, inverter cards, card fram , static switch card and display unit .

  7. Inspect visually fuses, contactors, and capacitors.

  8. Check that the terminals are well tightened and cleaned .

  9. Check that the terminals of input supply switch and output supply switch are tightened

  10. Inspect terminals of battery and keep it clean .

  11. Check that the static by bass of the UPS.

  12. Read battery voltage and test the efficiency of the battery.

  13. Clean UPS from dusty air.

  14. Clean the floor of the DC room.

  15. Put UPS  in service and check input and output voltage and record  .
