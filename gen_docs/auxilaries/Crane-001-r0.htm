Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft is initiated and reviewed by eng. Nagy Bekheit Saleh, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad general director,  approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station, and certified by by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem: HPGC  inspection and quality control general director..

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required

  • Wear safety equipment as helmet, shoes etc

  • Switch of the A-C current

  • Put the suitable warning sign

Tools and Equipment

  • Tools set, cleaning materials (cloth - benzine), grease

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean commutator, slip ring, brushes for motors

  2. Check the bearing of the motors and replace them if there is any damage in them

  3. Clean and inspect the main contacts of the contactors

  4. Inspect and clean the resistors of the rotor

  5. Check the control circuit

  6. Inspect and check the brake of the motors

  7. Make revision for the motors of the supporters
