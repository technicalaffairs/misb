This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 of emergency diesel engine, which must be, performed every 150 operation hour as indicated by H150 in the title block.

After isolated the diesel engine mechanically and electrically

  1. Check the oil level in the tank and / or refill it from the storage tank up to the top mark on the level gauge.

  2. Turn the handles of the coarse (lamination type) oil cleaner fitted in the oil circuit.

  3. Check the clogging of the fine oil cleaners acc.to the differential pressure gauges data.

  4. Check the amount of leakage from stuffing box of fuel pump and prevented it by retighten the gland bolts or replace the sealing ring { it must be dropped some drops to cool the gland}.

  5. Check the temperature of the crankshaft bearings by touching the side lids of the upper engine case, thus the safety of operation is increased.

  6. At higher temperature it is necessary to dismantle the lids and to check the condition of bearing cca 10 min after engine stopping.

  7. Check the suction pipe for tightness. Imperfect tightness will manifest itself in the output of the engine And remove any leakage if found.

  8. Check the tightness of the cylinder heads – incase of untightness tighten the bolts of cylinder heads. 

  9. De-water the air vessel with head.

  10. Check the lubrication of rocker arms and valve guide and adjusted the regulation valves of lubrication if needed.

  11. Keep the surface of the engine clean.
