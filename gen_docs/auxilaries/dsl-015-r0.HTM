Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued,

  • The maintenance staff must be qualified and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required..

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ear plugs, rubber, gloves, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the Diesel Engine.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

 

   

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Soft brush

  • Suitable lifting device, Feeler gauge

  • Hammer, Torque wrench, Manual puller

  • Dial indicator,

  • K&N air filter cleaner,

  • K&N air filter oil

Work to be Carried out

  1. Run Engine ( per NFPA20 Specification ).

  2. Make general inspection for the engine.

  3. Check all anchor bolts and support steel fasteners bolts and tighten if necessary, Fuel tank and lubricating oil.

  4. Check cooling system, exhaust system, operating gauges, jacket water heater & coolant hose inspection.
