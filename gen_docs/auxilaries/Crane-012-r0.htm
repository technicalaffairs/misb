Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt ...etc.

  • Shut down the power supply of the crane.

Tools and Equipment

  • Tool set for tightness,

  • Cleaning tools as cloth.... etc

  • Press-on and detaching device, air blower.

  • AVO meter, megger…..etc

  • Grease

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Operate the crane, observe and hear the sound of main hoisting, auxiliary hoisting, trolley, and crane travel motors

  2. Grease motors bearing.

  3. Clean all motor, and electronic control panel, resistors, by air blower.

  4. Check the motors breakers for main hoisting, auxiliary hoisting, trolley and crane travel.
