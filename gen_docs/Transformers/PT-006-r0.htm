Introduction

This document outlines the equipment tests I1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the titled block  The tests will be done after any fault, at capital repair, and at acceptance tests.

The first draft initiated at High Dam HPS, by eng. Hossain Mahrous and tested at 16 Jan 2002

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area, Wear safety helmet, shoes, Presence the industrial safety supervisor.

  • Switch off the unit.

  • Separate the primary terminals of v.t (high winding).and the secondary terminals of v.t (low winding).

  • Switch on the grounds.  

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, rags, and silicagel

  • Megger: isol 5003, Doble type M4000 (Insulation analyzer),

  • Micro ohmmeter type AC 10, Ratio tester type ATRT-03
