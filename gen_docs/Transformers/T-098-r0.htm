Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • Disconnect the output transformer CB.

  • Remove the HV fuse of the transformer.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes .... etc.

  • Air Blower.

  • Complete tool set.

  • Digital Voltmeter, meager.

  • Brushes, Sand paper

  • WD40 spray fluid.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

  • HV detector, portable earth.

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check the HV fuse of the transformer is removed.

  2. Check no voltage on HV or LV by used detector.

  3. Discharge the static voltage on bushing by using portable earth.

  4. Clean the exterior, remove all rust, prime bare spots, and repaint.

  5. Inspect the bushings and replace broken bushing.

  6. Check the leaking gasket, reseal the transformer and check for leaks.

  7. Inspect the liquid level. Add liquid as needed to restore the correct level.

  8. Remove all tools and equipments.

  9. Check that no earth is connected to the transformer.

  10. Return the HV fuse of the transformer.

  11. Connect the output transformer CB.
