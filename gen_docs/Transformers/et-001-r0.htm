Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounded the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators.

  • Isolate the 6 kV bus bar.

  • Starting the cleaning of all porcelain surfaces of all transformer, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, and tool set

  • Voltage indicator, Portable earth for discharging on the bus bar.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes, Silica gel

  • Complete tool kit/set, including a torque wrench.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check for signs of oil leaks.

  2. Check oil level in tank and conservator .

  3. Check the break point of oil.

  4. Rearrangement state :Connect all items which are disconnected.
