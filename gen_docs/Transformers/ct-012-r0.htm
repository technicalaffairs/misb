Introduction

This document outlines the steps for testing turns ratio of current transformer  I1 which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block, and which is performed when entering service

The first draft initiated by eng. Sabry Shabaan Abd el-Latif, and tested by eng. Sherif Mahrous and eng. Hani Bassuonin accepted by t echnical expert eng. Hassan elbehiry and approved by eng. Fathy El sayed head sector of El-kureimat power station and general manager of inspection and quality control sector eng. Gamal Abd El wahab

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes

  • Current transformer isolation and proper earthing is required

Tools and Equipment

  • Two ammeter

  • Sverker 650

  • Test wire.

    •

Work to be Carried Out
  1. Connect ammeter A1 to the primary of current transformer see fig (1)
  2. Connect ammeter A2 to the secondary of current transformer
  3. Connected sverker 650 to the secondary of current transformer
  4. Read the transformer ratio by dividing A1/A2 gives the current transformer ratio
