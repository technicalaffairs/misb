Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed after any internal faults as indicated in the title block

Safety Precautions Figure (1)

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Switch off CB the main generator (B-1  ) for unit no.1, service transformer C.B (B-1TC) and disconnector of power transformer no.1 (P-1T)

  • Switch off the disconnectors (P-1 ) of main generator & (P-1TC) of service transformer withdraw the C.B 6 kv out of the cell - 3/1KPY

  • Connect the earthes of transformer at 15.75, 500 kv ( Z-1T, 1Z-1T), the earth of service transformer (Z-1TC) and the temperory. earth on the o/p of 6 kv C.B cell

  • Disconnect the automatic (MCB) of voltage transformer of power transformer No. 1

  • Put the gas relay jumper out of work (or on alarm position)

  • Close fire manual valves of transformer

  • Presence industrial safety supervisor during washing (if exist)

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

Tools and Equipment

  • Metal brushes, Crane

  • Screw driving, cleaning materials and wrenches

  • Grease, petrol ... etc

  • Withdraw machines and instruments

  • Suction pumps, filtering machines

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle the bushings terminals 500 kv / 15.75 kv and neutral to withdraw tr. to work shop.

  2. Check insulation resistance & tan 8 and turns ratio for the windings of the transformer

  3. Use filtering oil plant to heat transformer oil until 50 C to discharge the oil

  4. Dismantle emergency pipe - reserve tank and all bushings after discharge the oil from the transformer at workshop

  5. Dismantle the upper part of the transformer tank at workshop

  6. Perform visual inspection for the core and windings and repair any defect

  7. Mantle the cover of the transformer after check the internal of the transformer

  8. Mantle all parts of the transformer (bushings / reserve tank, ... etc)
       * Perform vacuum processing for the transformer by using vacuum machine until 10 mm Hg
       * Maintain the transformer on vacuum condition for 20 Hr & vacuum value should be
          recorded
       * Audit the transformer through vacuum process

  9. Fill the transformer with oil with the continuity of vacuum and heating programs
       * Fill the transformer with oil and oil temperature should be between 55-60 C and rate of
          filling 2 tins / hour at 10 mm Hg by using filtering plant
       * The filling process finish when the oil level cover the windings of the transformer
       * After 24 hours from the end of filling oil sample should be tested

  10. Perform h.v test for windings

  11. Dry the transformer if necessary. by using motor - generator depending on the result of above test

  12. Put the transformer on the service after close the work permit
