Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, shoes, ... etc.

  • Potential transformer must be isolated and proper grounded  is required

  • Wear safety belt because there is danger of falling off the top of the PT stack when cleaning insulators or checking connections.

  • Presence the safety supervisor is required.

Caution: Secondary PT wiring could be energized through back feeds, make sure circuit breaker to secondary circuit is open

The secondary circuits for the PT  should be opened

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt, and silicagel

  • Voltage indicator.

  • Cleaning tools as cloth, rags .... etc

  • Multimeter, Air blower,

  • Screwdrivers and set of common keys.

Works to be Carried Out

  1. Check PT foundation to ensure it is in correct vertical position.

  2. Examine the surface of the porcelain for scratches, chips, and flash over burns, and cracks.

  3. Check and retighten if necessary all mounting bolts of voltage transformer.

  4. Check the oil level on the oil level gauge.

  5. Check for oil leaks.

  6. Check the primary and secondary terminals, and retighten all joints.

  7. Check and clean terminals box inside and out.

  8. Check there has been no flash over on air gap for secondary terminals and communication.

  9. Check the terminals box gasket, repair if required.

  10. Clean PT porcelain.

  11. The main grounding connection must be checked and retightened if necessary.
