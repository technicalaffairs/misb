Introduction

This document lists steps for testing magnetisation curve test for current transformer and measuring magnetising current following for different values of applied equipment inspection check list I1 which are done frequently

The first draft initiated by eng. Sabry Shabaan Abd el-Latif, and tested by Hani Bassuoni and eng. Sherif Mahrous accepted by technical expert eng. Hassan elbehiry and approved by eng. Fathy El sayed head sector of El-kureimat power station and general manager of inspection and quality control sector eng. Gamal Abd El wahab

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes

  • Transformer isolation and proper earthing is required

Tools and Equipment

  • Ammeter (A)

  • Voltammeter (V)

  • Sverker 650

  • Test wire.

    •

Work to be Carried Out
  1. Connect ammeter to the secondary of current transformer see fig (1)
  2. Connect voltammeter to the secondary of current transformer see fig (1)
  3. Connect sverker 650 to the secondary of current transformer
  4. Test is conducted only up to knee point which is at a point where 10% increase in applied voltage results in 50% increase in magnetizing current
