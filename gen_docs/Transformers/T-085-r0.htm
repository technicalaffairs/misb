Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, and harness ....

  • Transformer isolation and earthing on all its sides is required

  • There is danger of falling into the oil containment pit when working near large power transformer

  • A proper well fixed ladder should be used for climbing on tap of the transformer tank

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger (500 - 5000) volt

  • Multimeter

  • Air blower

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning solvent

Work to be Carried Out
 Transformer oil system

 
No Maintenance Procedure Position Test result
1. Read and note down the oil temperature indicate whether the oil or winding temperature is caused by long term transformer overload or insufficient cooling take corrective measures. Ok - Not ok   
2. Check the dehydrating breather, it must be changed when the upper layer of blue active material has diminished in thickness to about 3 cm. Ok - Not ok   
3.
  • Check the oil levels in the conservator or other vessels, they must be checked by reference to the sight glasses
  • Oil level indicators depending on the transformer oil temperature
  • The oil levels must correspond to the temperature marked on the indicators
Ok - Not ok   
4.
  • Check the flanged joints and welds for oil leakage
  • All flanges seals and welds must be checked for oil leaks
  • If any leakage found we must immediately seal by retightening the screws replacing the seals or by welding
Ok - Not ok   
5.
  • Check the paint finish and clean the surface
  • If the finishing coat is touched up in time, the paint coat in general needs not be completely renewed
  • Clean the painted surface with a solvent soaked rag or a steam jet blower
 Ok - Not ok   
6.
  • Check the earthing system (protective earthing)
  • Check that the earthing screw is correctly tightened and retighten it if necessary
 Ok - Not ok   
7. Oil sampling (for oil analysis):
  • Samples of transformer oil must be taken for the purpose of measuring the disruptive discharge voltage
  • Check the chemical and physical properties of the liquid insulant
  • Oil sampling valve top or middle sampling for gas analysis oil sampling valve bottom sampling for other analyses
 Ok - Not ok

  

  
8. Treatment and filteration of oil under vacuum in closed cycle for 72 hours continuously and test dielectric isolation for oil sample after filteration Ok - Not ok   

 

 

 

 

Off load tap changer

No Maintains procedure Position Test result
1. The off load tap changer may be operated only with the transformer de-energized Ok - Not ok   
2
  • Traverse all taps for cleaning the tap changer contacts
  • The copper parts of the contacts are tarnishing when immersed in oil
 Ok - Not ok

 

  
3
  • Check that the interlock and the end positions are secured mechanically
  • When the end position is reached, tap change can only be moved in the opposite direction
 Ok - Not ok

 

  
4 Take and record final reading on tap changer operations Ok - Not ok  

Cooling system

No Maintenance Procedure Position Test result
1. Read and note down the inlet and outlet temperatures of the oil Ok - Not ok   
2
  • Check the wedge type flat slide valves or butterfly valves for correct position
  • Check position of valves according to the label "position of shut off devices"
 Ok - Not ok  
3
  • Check the flanged joints and weld for oil leakage
  • All flanges seals and welds must be checked for oil leaks
  • If any leakage found we must immediately seal by retightening the screws replacing the seals or by welding
 Ok - Not ok  
4
  • Inspect condition of all cooling fans
  • Check that screens have no obstruction
  • Check for cracks in fan blades, test all fan motors
  • Check for abnormal bearing noise
 Ok - Not ok  
5 Cleaning the coolers:
  • Use water pressure to clean radiators and transformer tank walls do not direct the stream of water directly at the cooling fans
  • The unsealed wiring must be covered by plastic covering to avoid s.c in wiring system
  • The coolers must be cleaned at least when the oil temperature tends to rise while transformer load and inlet temperatures of air or water remain constant
Ok - Not ok  

 

 

 

 

Control cabinet

No Maintenance Procedure Position Test result
1.
  • Check the heating and lighting of the control cubicle
  • The thermostat of the control cubicle heating is to be set to 20ºC
  • Check the heating for correct functioning
  • Check cubicle lighting for correct functioning (door contact)
 Ok - Not ok   
2
  • Check the enclosure for water tightness
  • Check anti corrosion coating of enclosure, door gaskets and lock
  • Clean the enclosure vent
 Ok - Not ok  
3
  • Replace the contacts in motor circuit breakers and contactors
  • Inspect transformer control cabinet and blow out dust
  • Check condition of door seal
  • Check all electrical connection for tightness
  • Check proper operation of space heaters
  • Test operation of fan control
  • Adjust temperature setting as necessary
Ok - Not ok  

Bushings

No Maintains procedure Position Test result
1.
  • Check the oil pressure in bushing
  • Check the pressure by reference to the pressure gauge on the HV bushing
  • The pressure must not exceed the limits stated in the pressure chart
 Ok - Not ok   
2
  • Check the oil level in bushing
  • The bushings may be provided with oil gauge glasses, depending on design and make
  • The oil level in the oil gauge glass must be within the tolerance markings. If required, refill oil
 Ok - Not ok  
3
  • Check for oil leaks bushings, bushing domes
  • The pipes leading to the bushings must be checked for oil leaks
 Ok - Not ok  
4
  • Clean the porcelain surfaces on all bushing
  • The intervals, which must be specified for each application, are largely dependent upon local air pollution
  • The average cleaning interval is 6 months
 Ok - Not ok
5 Clean and apply graphete grease to all contact surfaces of all neutral disconnect switches. Lubricate all bearing points Ok - Not ok  
6 Check for correct spacing of horns on all bushings Ok - Not ok
7 Clean grease and tighten all couplings of driver shaft to neutral disconnect switchs Ok - Not ok
8
  • Check the bushing terminals
  • Check the bushing terminals for light fit
 Ok - Not ok  
9 Check the terminal connections built - in current transformer must either be connected to measuring instruments Ok - Not ok
10 Check the terminal earthing, each transformer secondary winding must be earthed on one terminal Ok - Not ok

Monitoring devices

No Maintenance Procedure Position Test result
1. Checking the buchholz relay
  • The alarm and trip floats shall be operated mechanically
  • The alarm function (gassing alarm) is to be checked with the circuit breakers closed
  • Open bleed valves on buchholz relay unit a small amount of oil escapes test alarm and trip circuit using the test button next to the vent valve
  • Check all electrical connection for tightness
  • When the tripping function is checked, the circuit breakers must trip and the generator must be de-excited
  • Check the corresponding signals in the control room
  • Check for gas in the buchholz relay by means of the gas analyzer
 Ok - Not ok   
2
  • Check surge relief device
  • The protective equipment must be checked for correct functioning in accordance with the equipment description
  • Check for circuit breaker tripping generator de-excitation and signal input in the control room on operation of the protective equipment for the diverter switch
 Ok - Not ok  
3
  • Checking the telethermometers
  • Write down the temperature setting and indicate whether oil or winding temperatures are measured or not
 Ok - Not ok  
4
  • Check the temperature monitors
  • Write down the temperature setting
 Ok - Not ok  
5
  • Checking the resistance thermometer
  • Check the resistance thermometer for correct functioning
  • The resistance is set to 100 Ω at 0ºc and increases by 0.38 Ω /k
 Ok - Not ok  
6
  • Check the oil level monitors
  • Check the oil level monitors for correct functioning by operating the signalling contacts. In the case of maximum and minimum contacting
Ok - Not ok  
7
  • Check the oil level indicators
  • The oil levels in the conservator or in other vessels must be checked by reference to the sight glasses or oil level indicators
 Ok - Not ok  
8
  • Check the oil flow monitors
  • Check each oil flow indicator for correct contact making by closing one angle valve
 Ok - Not ok  
9
  • Check the pressure relief device on the transformer tank
  • Check for correct contact making
  • The pressure relief device must be set to tripping
  • The tripping elements remain in the operation position. After testing they must be brought back to the normal operating position
Ok - Not ok  
10 Checking the fire fighting system Ok - Not ok
