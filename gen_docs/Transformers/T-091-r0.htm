Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, … etc.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

  • Take care the service transformers in service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, and tool set

  • Voltage indicator ( HV tester rod ).

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes, Silica gel,

  • Complete tool kit/set, including a torque wrench.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check all painted surface for any signs of rust and repaint bare spots.

  2. Check for signs of oil leaks.

  3. Check oil level in tank and conservator.

  4. Check the condition of the silica gel, replace silica gel if it’s color changed from blue to pink.

  5. Check the condition of earthing.

  6. All bolted connections must be checked for tightness.

  7. Clean the body, cables and the area of service transformer.

  8. Check the break point for oil.
