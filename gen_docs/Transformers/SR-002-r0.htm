Introduction

The first draft initiated by: eng. Saleh Abdou, tested by eng Mohamed Abaas & eng Tawfeek Ramadan, revised by quality control eng. Tawfeek Elabasery  at EDEPCo., accepted by general manager eng Tarek yosife Ali and approved by eng Mechaeal Gorge Habashy head sector of Ayoun Moussa power station

This procedure details the major maintenance activities (2nd level of maintenance as indicated by the P2), which are performed from 2:3 years as indicated by the Y2:3  .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, helmet,

  • Reactor  out of service and work permit required.        

  • Use only fiberglass ladders.

  •  If it becomes necessary to enter the main tank, be sure that the oxygen level is sufficient to provide safe working conditions.

  • Personnel carrying out the inspection should be trained and qualified or directly supervised by someone who is.

Tools and Equipment

1.     Mugger 2000 volt.

2.     Binoculars or infrared scope.

3.    All tools needed to complete the work

 

Work to be carried out

1- Insulating oil (dielectric strength)

·        Using an oil tester, make a dielectric strength measurement of  the insulating oil in the main tank .

2- Insulation of Windings

1.     Measurement of winding insulation resistance.

2.     Measurement of dielectric loss factor (tan δ) of winding.

      3. Inspections by other insulation judging methods.

3- Insulating oil

      1.  Measurement of dielectric strength.

      2.  Measurement of neutralization number.

      3.  Measurement of water content.

      4.  Gas analysis .

4- Oil preservation system

1.     Check oil level and for proper relation with oil temperature.

2.     Check for oil leaks.

3.     Check that the oil stop valve between tank and conservator is open.

4.     Refer to separate instructions, if any

5- No-voltage Tap changer (if provided)

1.     Check for oil leaks.

2.     Checks tap position.

3.     Check that bolts are tight

6- Fan motor

1.     Check the bearings for unusual sound and Vibration.

2.     Check the oil seals for unseal sound and Vibration.

7- Breather

1.     Check the breather.

2.     Check the oil level .

8- Bushing (Porcelain type)

1.     Measurement of insulation resistance.

2.     Measurement  of tand. (If the bushing is of condenser type)

3.     Inspection of terminal and bushing top shield. Check for loose connection or change in color.

4.     Check  for contamination and damage of porcelain.

5.     Check for oil leaks.

6.     Check oil level, if provided.

9- Cooling equipment.

1.     Clean the heat exchanger surface.

2.     Check for oil leaks and rust.

10- Indicator (winding temperature ,Oil temperature) and  protective Relays

1.     Check normal indication and operation.

2.     Check the auxiliary wiring and the signaling for the indicator and protective relays.

3.     Check for looseness of terminals and lead

11- Tank

1.     Check for oil leaks.

2.     Inspection of finished surfaces. See if rainwater gets collected on the tank top and causes rusting on edges and corners .

12- Pressure Relief Device

1.     Inspection of pressure relief plate, if provided.

2.     Inspection of alarm contacts, if provided.

3.     Check for oil leaks.

4.     Check for insulation resistance.

5.     Refer to separate instructions .

13- Buchholez Relay

1.     Check whether or not any gas found in the relay.

2.     If any free gas is found in the relay. Check the gas for decomposable gases such as hydrogen, methane,         ethane, acetylene, carbon mono oxide and so forth. As for gas analysis, refer to item 1.6.

3.     Refer to separate instructions.

    14-   Cooler control cubicle

   Enclosure

1.     Check for loose bolts or bolts which have fallen off .

2.     Check for rust .

  Terminal Blocks

    Check for loose connection or change in color .

Molded case circuit breaker (MCCB)

1.        Check for loose connection or change in color.

2.        Check for crack of body.

3.        Check for breakers which have tripped.

Contactors (Mgsw)

1.     Check for loose connection or change in color.

2.     Check for damage to contacts.

3.     Check for crack of body.

4.     Check for loose connection or change in color of coil.

 Thermal relay

1.       Check for loose connection.

2.       Check for damage to heat element device

 Enclosed fuse (EF)

1.      Check for loose connection or change in color.

2.      Check for crack of tube

high voltage testing each 2 years

1.     D.C Resistance for winding.

2.     Turns ratio for windings.

3.     Insulation for windings by mugger (R60/R15)

4.     Tan delta for windings and bushings.
