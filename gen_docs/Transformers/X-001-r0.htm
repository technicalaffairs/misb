Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list I1 which must be performed an annually  bases  as indicated by Y1 in the title block. for the TL High Dam / Nag Hamady cct no. 1

The first draft is initiated by eng. Alaa el-deen Sakr, tested by eng. Gamal hassan, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad general director, and approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions     Figure (1)

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety id category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding  work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

    •

  • Switch off the CB`s (B-L1) - II, B-L2 - II)

  • Switch off the disconnector 500 kv reactor (PP-L1)

  • Connect the earth of reactor 500 kv (Z-P-L1)

  • Disconnect the automatic (MCB) of voltage transformer of reactor

  • Put the gas relay jumper on alarm position (or out of work)

  • Close fire manual valves of reactor

  • Presence industrial safety supervisor during washing

Tools and Equipment

  • Metal brushes

  • Screw driving and cleaning materials and wrenches

  • Grease, petrol ... etc

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle reactor, 500kv, neutral and pin terminals for reactor

  2. Perform chemical test for oil sample

  3. Check the function of oil level indicator and gas relay

  4. Check bushing oil pressure (500 kv) and increase it if necessary

  5. Check oil level of reactor reserve tank

  6. Take an oil sample to perform chemical test

  7. Check calibration of temperature indicators

  8. Replace the silca jel of safety pipe filter & breather

  9. Replace the silca jel of cooling circuit if test result of oil sample is not good

  10. Measure insulation resistance & tan 8 for windings

  11. Mantle the terminals 500 kv neutrals 500 kv neutral and pin

  12. Clean the reactor tank & bushing porcelain from oil leak and dirty and retighten all joints leak oil

  13. Wash transformer with water

  14. Close the work permit to enter the reactor on the service
