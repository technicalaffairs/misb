Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators, … etc.

  • It is recommended to use only fiberglass PVC ladders when working in the switchyard.

  • Transformer must be out of service, isolated and grounded from all sides .

  • Taking out the ...kv breaker of bus bar ...kv.

  • Disconnect supply of DC.

Environmental Recommendation

  1. There is danger of falling into the oil containment pit when worked near large power transformer

    2.

  2. Before starting the cleaning of all porcelain surfaces of the transformers, wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  3. Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool kit/set including a torque wrench tool.

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt

  • PVC ladder

  • Voltage indicator HV tester rod.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes, new silica-gel.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Take an oil sample and perform complete oil testing (physical , chemical, electric and gas analysis test )

  2. Check completely the whole cooling system.

  3. Check and repair the whole transformers and associated equipment for oil leak.

  4. Carry – out an operation for the transformer control circuit.

  5. Check of the grounding system for continuity.

  6. Release air or accumulated gases for all venting plugs.

  7. Check for rust formation or injuries parts and repair in the same manner as original painting (primary and finishing coats).

  8. Carry – out an operation test for all protection and monitoring devices to check the proper functioning and signals.

  9. Operate manually the off –load tap changer several times in the two directions along all the tap position   to prevent the formation of the oil –cool on the contact surface.

  10. Check the insulation of control wiring by 2000Vac megger, the insulating resistance should not less than 1000(MΩ)

  11. Check the control wiring for continuality.

  12. Retouch the paint work or completely repaint the transformer if necessary.
