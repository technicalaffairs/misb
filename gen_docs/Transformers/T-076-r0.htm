Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding on all its sides is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety belt when cleaning suspension Insulator.

  • There is a danger falling into the containment pit when working near large transformer.

  • It is recommended to use only fiberglass (PVC) ladders when working in the switchyard.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and complete tool set,

  • Blower , screwdriver set, and digital multimeter.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning , silicagel, and fiberglass PVC ladder.

  • Portable earth for discharging,

Environmental Recommendation

  • Before starting the cleaning of all porcelain surfaces of all transformer wear your breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send to the dump.

  • Fix any oil leaks to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil.

  • Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impact on the environment, mainly on soil and ground water and eventually on the ecosystem, It takes only 1 liter of Hydrocarbon spilled in an aquifer to render 1,000,000 liters of potable water useless for human consumption

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Make visually inspection for painting and oil leakage. Adjust oil level if necessary.

  2. Check oil temperature and winding temperature.

  3. Make visual inspection and audible inspection of all fans and its screen.

  4. Check transformer control cabinet and use blower for cleaning it.

  5. Check transformer OLTC cabinet and use blower for cleaning.

  6. Check door gaskets of OLTC and Control Cabinet.

  7. Check air dryer : change the silica gel if it is pink or white or regenerate it. Adjust oil level in the trap of air dryer.

  8. Check heating circuit of motor drive unit of OLTC.

  9. Keep the transformer clean together with the neutral point Earthing resistor.

  10. Check and inspect the good working of fire fighting system.

  11. Put the transformer back into normal operation
