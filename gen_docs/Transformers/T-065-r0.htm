Introduction

This document outlines the high voltage tests procedures P1, which must be performed on an an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block .

The first draft is initiated and tested by eng. Hussein Mahrous Mohamed,  accepted by eng. Mohamed Diaa El din El Gabalawy  general director, and approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station,  Hyd.EPCo.

Tests

  • Insulation resistance

  • Measuring the turns ratio

  • Measuring of D.C resistance

  • High voltage tests

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified test group staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the safety equipment as Safety helmet, safety shoes, ...etc

  • Switch off the transformer

  • Separate the primary circuits of transformer from both sides

  • Switch off the disconnect switch

  • Switch on the earth from both sides

  • The terminals of high and low winding must be shorted

Tools and Equipment

  • Metal brushes, Grease, petrol ... etc

  • Megger : isol 5003; Range (500 & 1000 & 2500 & 5000 v)

  • Screw driving and cleaning materials

  • Crane

For Insulation Resistance

Insulation resistance reading must also not deviate excessively from previously logged readings

  • The value of insulation resistance allow to less 30% from manufactures reading or 15% from last reading at the same temperature

  • R60/R15 > 13

 

 

Measuring of Turns Ratio

Used ُEquipments (ATRT -03)

Measuring the ratio of three phase transformer (Y/∆ 525/15.75)

  1. Connect cable (H) to high winding-(H1--to phase A)&(H2--to phase B)&(H3-- to phase C) (H0-- to neutral N)

  2. Connect cable (X) to low winding--(X1--to phase A)&(X2--to phase B)&(X3--to phase C)

  3. Operate the device & chose the program--(transformer test & configuration & name plate and start)

  4. The ratio measured directly from device and recorded in chart

Normal Method

Used  equipments

  • Use external AC supply 220 v & variable resistance and two voltmeter v1 & v2connect a.c supply to high winding phase (A) read (V1) and connect voltmeter (v2) to low winding phase (a) read (v2) then

  • Calculate the ratio of phase (A) = V1/V2 and repeat the same sequence to phase B and C

High Voltage Test

Measuring of Dissipation factor (% p.f ) and capacitance of two winding transformer  

using doble M4000

  1. Operate the equipment doble M4000 unit control

  2. From the program chose ... (two winding transformer)

  3. Record technical data of transformer

  4. Record test K.V for winding and bushing

  5. The terminals of high and low winding shorted

  6. Connect HV cable to high voltage winding

  7. Connect LV lead (read) to low voltage winding

  8. Connect ground cable (green) to the tank (station ground)

  9. Switch on the high voltage unit

  10. Check and review all connections and device

  11. Start the test (overall test) for H.W & L.W and bushing

Test Procedures for Three Winding Transformer

Measuring of Dissipation factor (%p.f) and capacitance of three winding transformer

Using Doble M4000 Unit

  1. Operate the equipment Doble M4000 unit control

  2. From the program chose..(three winding transformer)

  3. Record technical data of transformer

  4. Record test KV for winding and bushing

  5. The terminals of high & low and tertiary winding must be shorted

  6. Connect HV cable to high voltage winding

  7. Connect LV lead (read) to low voltage winding

  8. Connect LV lead (blue) to tertiary (tert) voltage winding

  9. Connect ground cable (green) to the tank (station ground)

  10. Switch on the high voltage unit

  11. Check and review all connections and device

  12. Start the test (overall test) for H.W & L.W and bushing
