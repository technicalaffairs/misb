Insulation Resistance Test

Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

    •

  • Switch off the transformer

  • Separate the primary circuits of transformer from both sides

  • Switch off the disconnect

  • Switch on the earth from both sides

  • The terminals of high and low winding must be shorted

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger: isol 5003, Range 500 & 1000 & 2500 & 5000 v

  • AC supply 220 V

  • Two voltmeters V1& V2

  • DC battery 12 v heavy duty , circuit breaker , ammeter 0--12 amp & variable resistance.

  • Doble (M4000)

  • Microhmmeter CA 10

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect the line terminal of megger to high winding and earthed the low winding read R 15 & R 60 in (MΩ) of H.W

  2. Repeat the item 1 to low winding and earthed the high winding read R15 & R 60 in (MΩ)

The results were record at temp: 15 C

Measuring of Turns Ratio     Used equipments (ATRT-03)

Measuring the Ratio of autotransformer (500/132/15.75)

  1. Connect (cable H) to high winding - (H1 --- to A) & (H0 --- to neutral X)

  2. Connect (cable X) to low winding --- (X1 --- to --- a1,a2) & (X0 --- to ... x1,x2)

  3. Operate the device & chose the program ... (transformer test & configuartion single phase & name plate and start)

  4. The ratio measured directly from device and recorded in chart

Normal Method

Used equipments

  • Use external a.c supply 220 v and two voltmeter v1 & v2
    connect AC supply to high winding (A&X) read (V1) and connect voltmeter (v2) to low winding (a&x) read (v2) then

  • Calculate the ratio of phase (A) = V1/V2 and repeat the same sequence to phase B and C

High voltage test

Measuring of Dissipation Factor (% p.f) & Capacitance of two winding autotransformer
 

Using equipment:    (doble M 4000)
 

Procedures

  1. Operate the equipment doble M 4000. unit control

  2. From the program chose...(two winding transformer)

  3. Record technical data of transformer

  4. Record test KV for winding and bushing

  5. The terminals of high and low winding shorted

  6. Connect HV cable to high voltage winding

  7. Connect LV lead (read) to low voltage winaing

  8. Connect Ground cable (green) to the tank (station ground)

  9. Switch on the high voltage unit

  10. Check and review all connections and device

  11. Start the test (overall test) for H.W & L.W and bushing
