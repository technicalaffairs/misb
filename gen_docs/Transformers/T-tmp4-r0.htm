Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, helmet,

  •  out of service and work permit required.  

General Precautions

  • Prepare necessary tools and spare parts after due planning.

  • Before commencing work make sure that the machine is isolated from the power supply and make sure again by making use of electric detector that the circuit is dead.

  • Ground transformer bushing terminals while working on the transformer.

  • Should further information be needed or any particular problem arises which are not covered by these instructions such as internal inspection please ask  HITACH   I LTD.

Inspection Items

Contents And Notes

1

Transformer,

temperature

and load

1.     Read and record oil and if any   winding temperature.

2.     Record ambient temperature, load (MW, MVAR) and voltage.

 

Notes:

 

1)     Transformer temperature directly affects the life of the insulating material.

2)     Maximum temperature rise limits are specified for the both oil and winding temperature.

     In the daily inspection, check not only that temperatures are held within the maximum limit, but also that the temperature lies in a satisfactory range in relation to their values in the test report, load condition and ambient temperature.

Overloading of transformer should be made only in accordance with the applicable standard. 

 

2

Oil level

Check oil level gauges

3

Noise

Chick for abnormal sounds

4

Loose connections

1.     Check for loose connections such as those found in main circuits, grounding circuits, auxiliary circuits, and in foundation bolts and others.

2.     Check valves for looseness.

5

Oil leakage

Check for oil leaks in connections such as those of valves and meters-especially in gasket joints and welded parts.

6

Gas leakage

In case of nitrogen gas sealed transformer, measure the nitrogen gas pressure and check for gas leaks.

7

Silica gel breather

1.      Pay attention to the discoloration of the        

absorbent.

2.      Check the level of the sealing oil in cup.

 

Note:

The absorbent will turn from blue into pink if its moisture content becomes in excess of 20 or 25% in the gravimetric ratio When more than 70% if its volume is found discolored replace or dry the   absorbent.

Too much sealing oil sometimes contaminates absorbent while less sealing oil causes ineffective sealing function. Check to see if the oil level is held within a specified limit at all times.
