Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes,...etc

  • The transformer must be out of service, isolated, and grounded on both sides.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Complete kit of tools

  • Blower, Voltmeter

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check for any oil leak from transformer parts and repair.

  2. Check oil level in main tank.

  3. Record max oil temperature.

  4. Record max winding temperature.

  5. Check cooling system.

  6. Test tap-changer Locally and Remote

  7. Check all protection devices by protection staff.
