Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, shoes, ... etc.

  • Current transformer must be isolated and proper grounded is required

  • Wear safety belt because there is danger of falling off the top of the CT stack when cleaning insulators or checking connections.

  • Presence the safety supervisor is required.

Caution: Secondary CT wiring could be energized through back feeds, make sure circuit breaker to secondary circuit is short circuited.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, and silicagel

  • Voltage indicator.

  • Tool set for tightness,

  • Cleaning tools as cloth, rags .... etc

  • Fiberglass ladder

Works to be Carried Out

In addition to the 2 monthly inspection check list, carry out the following steps:

  1. Check out the condition of the expansion diaphragm.

  2. Open secondary terminal compartment, blow out compartment and check the condition of the weather seal.

  3. Check that all wiring terminations are tight and securely fastened.

  4. Check the condition of cables or conduits entering the secondary terminal compartment.

  5. Check earth bus connections to base of CT for tightness.

  6. Touch up painting if required.
