Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, clothes, breathing mask, safety glass, safety belt when cleaning insulators.

  • Transformer isolation proper grounding on all sides.

  • There is danger of falling from the top of transformer.           

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and complete tool

  • Voltage indicator, portable earth for discharging.

  • Blower, fiber glass PVC lades, brushes.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

Work to be Carried out

Transformer Temperature

  1. Watch and record the oil thermometer and record max. oil temperature readings

  2. Watch and record the coil thermometer and record max. coil temperature 

Load Condition and Ambient Temperature

Notes: in the daily inspection, check not only that temperature are held within the maximum limit. But also that the temperature lies in sat is factory rang in rotation of their values to the test results

Oil Level

  1. Supervise and record the oil level in main tank conservator

  2. Check the soiled window glass of the oil level gauge

Oil Leakage

Check for oil leaks in connections such as of those valves, meters especially in gasket parts and welded parts

Breather

  1. Pay attention to the discoloration of the absorbent

  2. Notes: the absorbent will turn from blue into pink if its moisture content becomes in excess of 20 or 25% in gravimetric ratio

Pressure Relief Device

Check for marks of blowout, and oil leaks

Cooler Radiators

  1. Check for oil leaks

  2. Check for abnormal rotating sounds (fan – cooled type or forced oil – cooled type).

On – Load Tap Changer

  1. Check whether or not the tap position is correct.

  2. Record the number of tap changing operation.     Position no.       The number of tap     

  3. Check that the oil level shows between the lowest and the high test makes.    Low  / High

Bushing

  1. If the bushing is provided with oil level gauge, observe oil level and check for oil leaks

  2. Evaluate the extent of the bushing contamination

Indicators

Check for proper function

Return the Transformer into Service

  1. Disconnect the grounds on all sides of transformer (220,22,11kv)

  2. Ensure that there is no any tools or fine cloth on the top of the transformer.

  3. Lift up the safety fence with caution mark surrounding the work area

  4. Finish the work permit with coordination with shift engineer.
