Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses … etc . 

  • Transformer and the unit must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, ... etc . 

  • Complete tool set, air blower.

  • Megger, multimeter

  • AC & DC Voltmeter, Clampmeter ( AC ammeter )

  • Tan delta device.
