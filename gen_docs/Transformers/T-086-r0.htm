Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1 for step up and step down oil power power transformer, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, and harness ....

  • Transformer isolation and earthing on all its sides is required

  • There is danger of falling into the oil containment pit when working near large power transformer

  • A proper well fixed ladder should be used for climbing on tap of the transformer tank

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger (500 - 5000) volt

  • Multimeter

  • Air blower

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning solvent

Work to be Carried Out
 

Routine Tests

  1. Take and record insulation resistance readings (megger readings), high voltage winding to low voltage winding, high voltage to earth, low voltage to earth

  2. Compare readings to historical data

  3. Take and record capacitance dissipation factor percentage tan Δ

  4. Compare reading to historical data

  5. Perform transformation ratio tests in lowest, highest and neutral tap position

  6. Measure high voltage windings (phase to phase)

  7. Measure low voltage windings (phase to phase)

  8. Must be neutral point earthed

  9. Compare readings to historical data readings taken at all points of tap changer

  10. Take and record DC resistance winding readings

  11. Each high voltage phase to neutral

  12. Each high voltage phase to phase

  13. Each low voltage phase to phase

  14. Compare readings to historical data

  15. Take and record no load current readings in high and low voltage side for all pts of tap changer

  16. Compare readings to historical data
