Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes and safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Ensure that the transformer was isolated, and proper earthing on both sides is done.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multimeter (AVO)

  • Complete tool kit of screw drivers and common keys

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the feed-throughs,

  2. Check the tightness of the connections.

  3. Check the protection device for the following functions: oil level, internal pressure, temperature

  4. Touch up the paintwork if necessary.

  5. Check that the porcelain parts and the tank show no sign of damage.

  6. Check up the quality of the dielectric oil every 10 years.
