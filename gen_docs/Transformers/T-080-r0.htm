Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, clothes, breathing mask, safety glass, safety belt when cleaning insulators.

  • Transformer isolation proper grounding on all sides..

  • There is danger of falling off from the top of transformer.           

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety beet, and tool set

  • Voltage indicator, portable earth for discharging.

  • Blower, fiber glass PVC lades, brushes.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Measure the winding insulation resistance refer to figure 1

  2. Measure the dielectric strength refer to table1

  3. Measure the acid value refer to table 2.

On Load tap Changer

  1. Operator the tap – changes through its full range in order to clean the contacts.

  2. Check for oil leaks, lubricate gears.

Bushing

  1. Measure the insulation

  2. Measure tan Δ

  3. Check for damage of porcelain

  4. Inspect any oil leaks

Cooler Radiators

Check of cooling fan

Indicators

  1. Clean the inside of terminal box and loosened screws of terminals.

  2. Operation check of alarm contact

Protection and Control Circuits

  1. Alarm signal test of the relays, (Buchholz, pressure relief, temperature, oil level, cooler power supply).

  2. Operation test for cooling fan

  3. Measure the insulation resistance of wiring the value should be 1MΩ or above with 500 V or 1000 V megger

Close Inspection of the Externals

  1. Visual inspection of transformer body and oil leaks.

  2. Check main circuit, grounding circuits, auxiliary circuits foundation bolts

  3. Dusting the radiators

Return the Transformer into Service

  1. Disconnect the grounds on all sides of transformer (220,22,11kv)

  2. Ensure that there is no any tools or fine cloth on the top of the transformer.

  3. Lift up the safety fence with caution mark surrounding the work area

  4. Finish the work permit with coordination with shift engineer.

Notes:

In sampling oil, utmost care should be taken not to allow the admixture of foreign objects such as water, dust particles. Sample oil directly from the sampling cock or drain valve provided at the bottom of the transformer into the test container. The sampled oil should be tested promptly.

A sampling container must be clean and dry.

Drain some oil into the container.

Empty the container.

Draw off more oil and test this sample.
