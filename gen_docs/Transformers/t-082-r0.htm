Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes,...etc

  • The transformer must be out of service,

  • The transformer must be isolated and grounded from the two sides.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Complete kit of tools

  • Blower, Voltmeter

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean all transformer bushings

  2. Clean the heat radiating surfaces

  3. Inspect any leakage oil from transformer parts and repair.

  4. Check oil level in main tank.

  5. Record max oil temperature.

  6. Record max winding temperature.

  7. Check cooling system.

  8. Check oil pump.

  9. Check oil level in all high voltage bushings.

  10. Check all bolted connections for tightness.

  11. Clean the neutral current transformer.

  12. Take an oil sample for dielectric strength test and gas analysis

  13. Measure insulation resistance.

  14. Check tan δ (delta.)

  15. Measure winding resistance

  16. Measure turns ratio

  17. Check the pressure relief device.

  18. Check the oil temperature gauge.

  19. Check the winding temperature gauge.

  20. Lubricate tap-changer bearing points.

  21. Check the buchhloz relay.

  22. Check the gas detector alarm.

  23. Take a sample of gas for analysis.

  24. Check all protection devices by protection staff.
