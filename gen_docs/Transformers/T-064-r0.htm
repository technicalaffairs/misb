Safety Precautions Figure (1)

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Switch off CB`s (B-13T, B1-13T, B2-13T and B-2TC)withdraw the C.B 6 kv of service tr. out of cell
       * Switch off the disconnectors (PL - 13T, PL - 13T, 2PL - 13 T and P-1TC)

  • Connect the earths of auto transformer at 132, 500 kv and the earth of service transformer, put the temp earth on the o/p of 6 kv C.B cell (1Z - 13 T-1, 1Z-13T, Z-13T and Z-2TC)

  • Disconnect the automatic (MCB) of voltage transformer of auto transformer

  • Put the gas relay jumper on alarm position (or out of work)

  • Close fire manual valves of auto transformer

  • Presence industrial safety supervisor during washing (if exist)

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

Tools and Equipment

  • Metal brushes

  • Screw driving and cleaning materials

  • Grease, petrol ... etc

  • Crane

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle transformer terminals (500, 132,15.75,neutral)

  2. Dismantle pin terminal of 500 kv bushings

  3. Perform chemical test for oil sample

  4. Measure insulation resistance & tan 8 for windings

  5. Check the function of gas relay and oil level indicator

  6. Check oil pressure of bushing 500  kv & 132 kv and increase it if necessary

  7. Check oil level of reserve tank

  8. Check calibration of temperature indicators

  9. Replace silca gel of the safety pipe filter and breather

  10. Replace silca gel of cooling circuit if test result of oil sample is not good

  11. Mantle auto transformer terminals 500,132,15.75 kv neutral and pins for 500 kv bushings

  12. Clean the transformer tank & bushing porcelain from oil leak and other dirty and retighten all joints leak oil

  13. Wash transformer with water

  14. Close the work permit and put the transformer on the service
