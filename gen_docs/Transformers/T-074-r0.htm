Introduction

This document outlines minor maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Alaa El-deen Saker, tested by eng. Gamal hassan, accepted by eng. Badr Ebrahem: general director, approved by eng.  Abd Elkreem Abu zaid: head sector of Power Station, and certified by eng Kamel Raghb Atia : HPGC QC general director and MMS coordinator

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

    •

  • Switch off CB the main generator (B-1ſ ) for unit no.1, service transformer C.B (B-1TC) and disconnector of power transformer no.1 (P-1T)

  • Switch off the disconnectors (P-1ſ ) of main generator & (P-1TC) of service transformer withdraw the C.B 6 kv out of the cell -3 / 1KPY

  • Connect the earthes of transformer at 15.75, 500 kv (Z-1T, 1Z-1T), the earth of service transformer (Z-1TC) and the temperory earth on the o/p of 6 kv C.B cell

  • Disconnect the automatic (MCB) of voltage transformer of power transformer No1

  • Put the gas relay jumper out of work. (or on alarm position)

  • Close fire manual valves of transformer

  • Presence industrial safety supervisor during washing

Tools and Equipment

  • Metal brushes, Grease, petrol ... etc

  • Screw driving, and cleaning materials

  • Crane

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle transformer terminals (500, 15.75, Neutral).

  2. Dismantle primary terminal of voltage transformer.

  3. Dismantle pin terminal of 500 kv bushings.

  4. Dismantle arrestors terminal.

  5. Take an oil sample to perform chemical test.

  6. Measure tan δ "delta" and insulation resistance for windings of transformer.

  7. Check function of gas relay and oil level indicator.

  8. Check oil pressure of bushing 500 kv and increase it if necessary.

  9. Check level of oil indicator of reserve tank.

  10. Check calibration of temperature indicators.

  11. Replace silca gel of the safety pipe filter and reserve tank breather.

  12. Replace silca gel of cooling circuit if test result of oil is not good.

  13. Mantle the power transformer terminals 500, 15.75, neutral and pins for 500 kv bushings.

  14. Mantle the primary terminal voltage transformer and arrestors terminal.

  15. Clean the transformer tank & bushing porcelain from oil leak and other dirty and retighten all joints leak oil.

  16. Wash transformer with water.

  17. Close the work permit and put the transformer on the service.
