Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes,...etc

  • The transformer must be out of service,

  • The transformer isolated and earthed from the two sides.

  • The coolers of the transformer are out of service and the power supply of electrical motor of then is switched off

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete kit of tools, Dry rags

  • Measuring instrument (Ampero - meter)

  • Compressed air

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean all HV, LV, Neutral and current transformer bushings

  2. Clean the transformer bus duct using compressed air.

  3. Check oil level in each of tank, thermometer packed and bushing.

  4. Clean and check buchhloz relay (direction of flow, contracts float action, cable connection and gas content).

  5. Check silica gel breather and change it if nessary.

  6. Tighten all bolts.

  7. Inspect any leakage oil from transformer parts and repair.

  8. Take oil sample and test the (dielectric strength, power factor, inner tension, inhibitor content, sludge, moisture content, saponifaction number and gas analysis).

  9. Perform all transformer tests (HV insulation, DC resistance and turns ratio).

  10. Check all protection signals:
    (buchholz, oil level, oil flow, water flow and temperature)

Water Oil Coolers      Figure 1

  1. Remove the cover from the two side of cooler and cleaning cover at the connection water box.

  2. Clean the inside tubes (water) by using nylon brush or compressed water.

  3. Check any leakage oil from tube and repair it if found.

  4. Check oil pump (oil leakage, direction of rotation, and for noise).
