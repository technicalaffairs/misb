Introduction

The document over current relays are back - up relay for pilot - wire diff relay The document over current relays have a characteristic of (-30º) RXPE 42 The relay operates down to a supply voltage of 0.15 % of the rated voltage for a current of 1= 10 x IS. the voltage U that is completely independent of the set value on the current scale.

 

For large currents, above 1 = 50 x IS, the relay will operate even if the voltage is zero but the operation is no directional. In order that directional operation can be ensured at such large currents, the voltage must be larger than 0.1% of rated voltage.

 

When faster fault clearing is required, the directional over current relay can be used in a directional comparison scheme with a communication channel. In such a scheme the over current relay operates instantaneously but is allowed to trip only receipt of a carrier signal (permissive scheme), in this way tripping will be made only for faults on the protected line section

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued

  • The line is out of service

  • Isolate the relay from PT`s & CT`s

Tools and Equipment

  • Second. 1 - ph. injection test set (SVERKER 650 - Sweden)

  • Multimeter, Test wires, Test - plug handle (RTXH 18)

  • Variable resistance (0 - 450) ohm

Work to be Carried Out

 

 The line is out of service

  1. Connect the circuit as shown in fig (1)

  2. Increase the current gradually until reach the operating value 

  3. Ensure that the relay operates at the setting current value (1.5)A

  4. Ensure that the relay operates at the setting tine value (2) sec

  5. Repeat for phases S & T

  6. Record this values on the test sheet

  7. Take off the equipments and the wires, then return the relay to its normal position
