Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually basses as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

The first draft is initiated by eng. Mohamed Aly Aly Hassan tested by eng. Al shahat Ahmed Mousa at 01 Sep. 2003, revised by EDEPC quality control engineer Adel Al-nagar, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Abdel Mawla Meligy, and approved by eng. Yousry Mohamed Aly Salama Head Sector of Elshabab GT Power Station

The Protection Scheme consist of:

1. Differential protection Relay    { F 305 }
2. Earth Fault protection Relay    { F 317 }
3. Over current protection Relay  { F 301}

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes …. etc.

  • Step up transformer off and aux. transformer off

  • All  mcc "Motor Control Center" CB out service.

  • Isolated side protection.

              - F 305                    No. (3, 7, 11 ) High Side

                                                     (2, 6, 10 ) Low Side

              - F 301                    No   ( 1 , 3 , 5  )

              - F 317                    No   ( 136, 133 )

Tools and Equipment

  • Sverker test set.

  • Air blower.

  • Complete maintenance set.

  • Cleaning power ( 90 ) ( Video cleaner dry 90 ).

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect the relay testing units ( Sverker ) to a power source.

  2. Connect the timer to power source and its terminals to the trip terminals the relay test.

  3. Put an ammeter on current leads of testing unit to measure the current and connect the volt meter in parallel with the voltage leads testing unit to measure the voltage.

  4. Review the manual .

  5. Prepare the previous results of the relay testing.

  6. Be sure that the CB unit and the transformer are disconnected and DC  source is available .

Differential Relay F305

  1. Inject 0.4 Amp. to operating coil to terminals ( 3,1), (7,1), (11,1) 66 KV SIDE

  2. Record Results .

  3. Inject 1.6 Amp. to operating coil to terminals (2,5 ), (6,5), (10,5) 11.5KV Side

  4. Record results .

Over Current Relay  F301 type ITY192-232

  1. Inject 1.4 Amp. to operating coil to terminal (1,2 ), ( 3, 4), (5, 6) operating coil after Time lag (2.76 sec.)

  2. Record results for SC current.

  3. Inject 6 Amp. to operating coil to terminals ( 1,2 ), ( 3,4), ( 5,6 ) time direction (0) sec.

  4. Record Results.

Earth Fault protection F317

  1. Inject 86 VAC to operating coil to terminal ( 133, 136 )

  2. Record results.

Putting the Protection Relays into service

  1. Check that the CT and PT secondary connection return to its normal position as before the test .

  2. Close the terminal side protection .

      F 305  No      ( 3 , 7 , 11 ) High side
                       ( 2 , 6 , 10 ) Low side
F 317  No       ( 133, 136 )

      F 301  No       ( 1 , 3 , 5  )
