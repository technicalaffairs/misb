Introduction

The purpose of the relay is basically to prevent damage on the turbine, if the driving torque less than the total losses in the generator and the prime mover, the generator starts to work as a synchronous compensator, taking the necessary active power from the network. The total losses, as the percentage of the rated power, may vary approx, from 1 to 3 %

The reverse power relay is provide with one step. The relay shall be set at 1 % of the generator rated power and shall give an alarm and trip after a time delay of 10 sec. The relay contains one directional current unit RXPE 40 and one time delay unit.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued

  • The line is out of service

  • Isolate the relay from PT`s & CT`s

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary 1 phase injection test set (SVERKER 650 - Sweden)

  • Multimeter, test wires

  • Test - plug handle (RTXH 18)

  • Variable resistance (0 - 450) ohm

Work to be Carried Out

 

 The line is out of service

  1. Connect the circuit as shown in fig (1)

  2. The voltage across the resistor R should be (5-10)v

  3. Increase the current gradually until reach the operating value 

  4. Ensure that the relay operates at the setting current value (10)mA

  5. Ensure that the relay operates at the setting tine value (10) sec

  6. Repeat for phases S & T

  7. Record this values on the test sheet

  8. Take off the equipments and the wires, then return the relay to its normal position
