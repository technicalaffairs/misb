Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedures P1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the titled block

This patent concerns the test of protection relays of the transformer manufactured by “ AEG”. The Transformer, rated active power 310 MW, rated apparent  power 420 MVA, power factor 0.8, and voltages 21/220 KV

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc.

  • Ensure the disconnection of the exciter, generator, main transformer and home transformer.

  • Be aware of parts in service.

Works to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Review the manual.

  2. Prepare the previous results of the relay testing.

  3. Check the wires of the relay beforehand.

  4. Be sure that the unit and the transformer are disconnected and the DC source is available.

  5. Check the existence of the DC sources in panels.

  6. Disconnect the trip leads of the relay and connect the timer to them.

  7. Check all the leads used in tests.

 Preparation of testing tools

  1. Connect the test plug.

  2. Connect the timer to a power source and its terminals to the trip terminals of the relay under test.

  3. The test of almost all generator protection devices is achieved by isolating either CT circuits or PT circuits or both. This is done by using a special selector switch.

Test Steps

  1. Place selector switch on the test mode.

  2. insert the test plug  in special boord

  3. Insert timer the plug  in special socket

  4. Repeat the steps for the other three of phases.

  5. Record the results.

  6. the steps from 1 to5 are apply  for all protection relays
