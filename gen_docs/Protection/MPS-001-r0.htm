Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection on he protection relays operation, which must be performed every 3000 hours of operation for motor

This procedure was applied in ABU QIR power plant since 1985 and the final draft is issued on May 2005

The first draft initiated and tested by eng Ahmed Mhareem,  accepted by general manager eng Mohamed Abd Elsadik Ahmed, approved by eng Aly Anor El-Faumy and certified by eng.Mona A Al Sherqawy WDEPC quality control & inspection head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety Helmet, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • The transformer must be was isolated.

  • Make sure CT Secondary circuits are shorted.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • 3 Phases current injection device (I.S.a)

  • 2 Multi meters with separate clamps

  • Group of test leads

Work to be Carried Out

Relay Primary Inspection

  1. Visual inspection to ensure the cleaning and contacts tightness

  2. Cleaning the relay and wiring

  3. Be sure that the DC supply is connected

  4. Be sure that the end of the terminals are tighten well

  5. Test the mechanical movement of the reset pin.

 

 

Injection Test

Single Phase Test

Over load Element Test

  1. Record the setting values and compare it with setting in test form

  2. Inject 2 I setting and measure the trip time and compare it with the relay curve

  3. Repeat the previous steps at 4 I setting & 6 I setting

  4. Test the other phases by the same way

Short Circuit element

  1. Inject 0.9 I setting the relay will not trip

  2. Try to find the start current (I setting)

  3. Inject 1.1 I setting and measure the trip time

  4. Test the other phases by the same way

Negative Phase Sequence Element

  1. Inject 2 I setting and measure the trip time and compare it with the relay curve

  2. Repeat the previous steps at 4 I setting & 6 I setting

  3. Test the other phases by the same way

Phase Test

Repeat the previous step by inject current on two phases only

Over Load element Test

  1. Inject I setting and check the relay dose not trip

  2. Inject 1.1 I setting and check the relay trip after 1.5 hours

  3. Inject 2 I sitting with the relay in the cooled state measure the trip time and compare it with the relay curve

  4. Repeat with the hot state measure the trip time and compare it with the relay curve

Negative Phase Sequence Element

Inject 1.2 I setting and measure the trip time and compare it with the relay curve

Test the Trip Circuit of Circuit Breaker & Signals

Put the CB on the test position when the protection relay operate test the trip circuit of the circuit breaker & signals.

 

 

 

 

When the Test Finish, Check that all Setting as in Test From

Earth Fault Protection Relay Check

Voltage level :6 kv

Relay type : ITH 7111

Test procedure

 

Relay Inspection

  1. Visual inspection to ensure the cleaning and contacts tightness

  2. Cleaning the relay and wiring

  3. Be sure that the DC supply is connected

  4. Be sur that the end of the terminals are tighten well

  5. Test the mechanical movement of the reset pin

Injection test

  1. Record the different setting values of trip current and compare it with setting in test form

  2. Inject many values of trip current and compare it with the setting values

  3. Test the trip time of the relay at the trip current (with the setting value in test form)

Test the Trip Circuit of Circuit Breaker & Signals

(With the CB on test position)

When the protection relay operate check the trip circuit of the circuit breaker and signals

When the test finish, check that all setting as in test form
