This document outlines the inspection checklist for Communication System. The system is depending on a PLC control unit. The maximum capacity of the system is (96 line/36 Trunk) and the used capacity is (88 line/8 Trunk).

Safety Precautions

  • A Work Order must be issued; the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety Category.

  • Safety Fence with caution marks surrounding the work Area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, Shoes, anti static hand probe to avoid electrical choke and electrostatic effects.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multi-meter.

  • Screw Driver Kit.

  • Antistatic vacuum cleaner for dust cleaning.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visual inspection for PLC system

  2. Power Check:
         * Use the multi-meter to check the 220v AC power source
         * Open the front cover of the panel and Check the 4 Red LEDS
         * Notice the power leds that indicates the power good states.
         *     There are 4 RED LEDS for power states
                   * 220v AC           (Input Voltage)
                   * 24v   DC           (Output Voltage)
                   * 12v   DC           (Output Voltage)
                   * 5v     DC           (Output Voltage)

  3. Switch off the power source by turning off the main power switch.
  4. Use the Vacuum Cleaner to clean the following parts:Panel rack, Electronic cards, Battery bay.
  5. Retighten the wirings and cards.
  6. Switch on the power source by turning on the main power switch and wait for the program to complete its loading process.
  7. Recheck the power LEDS For 220 VAC input - and (24,12,5)VDC Outputs
