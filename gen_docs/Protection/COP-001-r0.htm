Introduction

Cut off protection protect around 10% to 20% from the line and rejoin in called dead zone for distance relay L8a.This protection have two relays 4RSF5, 5RSF5 one for phase A and other for C. Maintenance and testing done annually

The first draft initiated by eng. Abd ElHafez Farghaly, tested by eng. Hamdy Hsaneen, accepted by eng. Sleem Ahmed Aly; general manager, approved by eng. Abd El Kerim Abu Zeid Ismail Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety Helmet.

  • Isolate DC supply to relay

  • Switch off test - switches for DZW : YPOB for line

  • Switch off test - switches for NECATIVE SEQUANCE protection

Tools and Equipment

  • Test set type K 501

  • Ammeter

  • Voltmeter

  • Timer

  • Test leads

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Prepare test set to inject current suitable for setting relays value (from1A : 10A)

  2. Raise current gradually until relay 4RSF5, 5RSF5 trips

  3. Make sure Aux. relay 5pn energize

  4. Record the measurement values


     

    Relay I sett I meas. Ir. Notes
    4 RSF 5 8      
    5 RSF 5 8      

     

  1. Switch on C.D supply

  2. Switch on C.B

  3. Perform actual test for switching off C.B when cut off protection relays trips

  4. Make sure all alarms shown in DCS

  5. Return relay and associated equipment to service

  6. Make sure CT secondary circuits are in normal condition
