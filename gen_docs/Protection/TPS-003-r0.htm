Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually basses as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

The first draft is initiated by eng. Mohamed Aly Aly Hassan tested by eng. Al shahat Ahmed Mousa at 03 Sep. 2003, revised by EDEPC quality control engineer Adel Al-nagar, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Abdel Mawla Meligy, and approved by eng. Yousry Mohamed Aly Salama Head Sector of Elshabab GT Power Station

The Protection Scheme consist of:

  1. Differential protection Relay     { F 305 }

  2. Over current protection Relay  { F 307 }

  3. Single phase over current Relay  { F4 }

  4. High Impedance Relay               { F5 }

  5. Over current protection Relay     { F1 }

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category
  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.
  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes …. etc.
  • Step down transformer off ( low and high side ).
  • All  mcc (Motor Control Center) CB out service.
  • Isolate side protection must be done as follows:

(A)  Differential protection relay { F305 Type ( D 202 ) } .

                          No.   3 , 7 , 11           High side

                          No.   2 , 6 , 10           Low side

(B)  Over current protection relay { F307 Type ( ITX 192 – 232 ) }.

                          No.   1 , 3 , 5       Low side transformer

(C)  Single phase over current relay { F4 Type ( ISM2) }

                          No.   70 , 71

(D)  High Impedance  protection relay { F5 Type IM 21 qv+ YB1 L 21 }

                          No.   76 , 75

(E)  Over current protection relay { F1 Type ( RSZ 3f1 ) }      

                          No.  7 , 8 , 9

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Sverker test set.
  • Air blower.
  • Complete maintenance set.
  • Cleaning power ( 90 ) ( Video cleaner dry 90 ).

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect the relay testing units ( Sverker ) to a power source .
  2. Connect the timer to power source and its terminals to the trip terminals the relay test.
  3. Put an ammeter on current leads of testing unit to measure the current and connect the volt meter in parallel with the voltage leads testing unit to measure the voltage.
  4. Review the manual .
  5. Prepare the previous results of the relay testing .
  6. Be sure that the CB unit and the transformer are disconnected and DC  source is available .

Differential Relay F 305  type D202

  1. Inject 0.3Amp. to operating coil to terminals ( 3 , 1 ) , ( 7 , 1)  ,  (11,1) 66KV Side.
  2. Record Results.
  3. Inject 1 Amp. to operating coil to terminals ( 2 , 5 ) , (6,5) , (10,5)
  4. Record results .

Over Current Relay  F307 type ITX 192 - 232

  1. Inject 1.1 Amp. To operating coil to terminal (1,2 ) , ( 3, 4) , (5, 6) the operating coil working after 1.1 sec.
  2. Record results for s.c current.
  3. Inject 7 Amp. to operating coil to terminals ( 1,2 ) , ( 3,4) , ( 5,6 ) the operating coil  working.
  4. Record Results.

Single Phase Over current Relay   ISM2

  1. Inject 1.0 Amp. to operating coil to terminal ( 70, 71 ) the operating coil working after 1sec.
  2. Record results .

High Impedance Relay    IM 21qv + YB1 L21

  1. Inject 62 volt AC to operating coil to terminals ( 75 , 76 ).
  2. Record Results .

Over Current Relay   F1 type  RS 3 F1

  1. Inject 5.0 Amp. to operating coil to terminals  No ( 7,10 ), ( 8,11 ) , (9,12 ) the operating coil working after 1.1 sec.
  2. Record results.

Putting the Protection Relay into Service

  1. Check that the CT and PT secondary connection as before the test  
  2. Close the terminal side protection:

F305         No.  ( 2,3,6,7,10,11 )

F307         No.   ( 1, 3, 5 ) 

F 4            No.   ( 70 , 71 )  

F 5            No.   ( 75, 76 )  

F 1            No.   ( 7, 8, 9 )
