Introduction

This document outlines the feeders protection testing procedure P1 witch must be performed on an annually basses as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Aly Aly Hassan. tested by eng. Al-Shahat Ahmed Mousa. At 7 sep. 2003, revised by EDEPC quality control engineer. Adel El-Nagar, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Abd el-Moola Melegy, approved by Yousry Mohamed Aly Salama: Power Station head sector, and certified by EDEPCo. Quality Control Head Sector eng. Aly Al Lethy.

The Protection Scheme consists of:

  • Static over current relay                IKS 703 (F1)

  • Directional earth fault relay            PM2g no/90(F3)

  • Zero sequence current relay          IM3X - SSX 110 (F6)

  • Static over current relay - IKS 703 (F1) No (1,2)(3,4)(5,6)

  • Directional earth fault relay  - PM 2g no/90 (F3) No (1,2) C.T and (3,4) V.T

  • Zero sequence current relay - IM 3X - SSX 110 (F6) No (1,2) C.T

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category
  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required
  • Wear your safety helmet safety shoes ...... etc
  • Switch off the CB of the feeder
  • CB must be out service

  • Isolate the side protections must as follows:

    • Static Over Current Relay - IKS 703 (F1)  No. (1,2)      (3,4)      (5,6)

    • Directional Earth Fault Relay - PM 2g no/90 (F3)  No. (1,2) CT   (3,4) VT

    • Zero Sequence Current Relay IM3X - SSX 110 (F6)  No. (1,2) CT 

Tools and Equipment

  • Sverker test set

  • Air blower

  • Complete maintenance set

  • Cleaning power (90) (video cleaner dry 90)

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect the relay testing units (Sverker) to a power source

  2. Connect the timer to power source and its terminals to the trip terminals the relay test

  3. Put an ammeter on current leads of testing unit to measure the current and connect the voltmeter in parallel with the voltage leads testing unit to measure the voltage

  4. Review the manual

  5. Prepare the previous results of the relay testing

  6. Be sure that the CB disconnected and DC source is available

Static Over Current Relay - IKS 703 (F1)

  1. Open circuit (O/C)

  2. Inject 2.5 amp. to operating coil to terminal (1,2)(3,4)(5,6) the operating coil working after 0.54 sec

Short Circuit (S/C)

  1. Inject 10 amp. to operating coil to terminal (1,2)(3,4)(5,6) the operating coil working after 0.06 sec

  2. Record results

Directional Earth Fault Relay - PM 2g no/90 (F3)

  1. Inject 2.5 amp. on points (1,2) and inject 5 voltage on points (3,4) the operating coil working

  2. Record results

Zero Sequence Current Relay IM3X - SSX 110 (F6)

  1. Inject 0.5 amp to operating coil to terminal (1,2) the operating coil working after 0.53 sec

  2. Record results
