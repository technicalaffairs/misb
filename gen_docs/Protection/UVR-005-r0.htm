Introduction

The relays of the UM3 … series are instantaneous over and under voltage relays designed for general protection and signaling applications. Space is provided for fitting an ancillary timer.

The first draft initiated, tested by eng. Hany Bassuony Abdel Razek, accepted by Dr. Tharwat Sabry, and approved by UEEPC inspection & quality control general manager eng. Gamal Abdel Wahab.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety Helmet, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Unit or line should be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Sverker 650 test set

  • Avometer, test leads

Work to be Carried Out

Preliminary Steps to Prepare for Relay Testing

  1. This procedure is used to perform off load relay test.

  2. The test is divided into two tests

  3. The AC test to determine pick up and reset voltage.

  4. The DC test to determine the operating time.

AC Test

  1. Connect the circuit as shown in fig. 1.

  2. Isolate the wire connected to pin 1at the relay.

  3. Switch on the Sverker and adjust the voltage to100V.

  4. Turn off the Sverker.

  5. Connect the wire to pin 1 at the relay.

  6. Switch on the Sverker to apply the 100 v to the relay then check that the relay reset.

  7. Slowly decrease the voltage until the relay picks up and closes its contact.

  8. This is the pick up voltage.

  9. Slowly raise the voltage until the relay resets and open its contact.

  10. This is the reset voltage.

  11. Switch off the Sverker.

DC Test

  1. Connect the circuit as shown in fig. 2.

  2. Isolate the wire connected to pin 5 at the relay.

  3. Switch on the Sverker and adjust the DC voltage to 110 v.

  4. Turn off the Sverker.

  5. Connect the wire to pin 5 at the relay.

  6. Reset the timer of the Sverker.

  7. Switch on the Sverker to apply the 110 v  DC to the timer ssx110.

  8. Check that the timer picks up and closes its contact after the shown time on the LCD of the Sverker.

  9. This is pick up time compare it with the setting time.
