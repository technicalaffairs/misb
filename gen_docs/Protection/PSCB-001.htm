Introduction

This document details the testing procedures P1, required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of the relay, to be carried out on every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

This petition concerns the list of protection relays of 400 VCB, manufactured by (SKODA).

The CB is of type either ARV or J2UXñT of rating 630 A.

Procedures of T2UX-T CB

  1. Connect the tester with a 220 Vac source.

  2. Dismantle the CB socket ( on the left ) and all pins of CB.

  3. Connect cable (2) between the tester and the CB. socket ( left one ).

  4. Screw out all the pins of the blocks except the pin of (K) it is better to put it on the lowest value .

  5. Press the tester push button to test the (Pn) block . The green light indicates good resistors.

  6. If you test the Pn with no pin screwed in (k) then the test isnít achieved and a sign Pnn will appear on screen. During further tests this pin must be always screwed out

  7. Screw out the pin of (K)

  8. Press the push buttons to test the diodes (Pnd). The green lamp indicates good diodes.

  9. Press the push buttons to transfer to STA test in which an inner stabilized feeding voltage to the relay is measured the value of the voltage should be 6.2-7.6V

  10. A value of the voltage of CB will display on the screen.
