Introduction

This procedure details the testing procedures P1, which recommended to be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Gaber Salah Taher, tested by eng. Osama Ahmed Hassanain on unit no. 4 at 05 March 2005, accepted by eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman Salim general director, approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel Baset Rekabi Head Sector of Aswan Dam 1 Power Station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem: HPGC MPIS coordinator & inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Unit must be shut down.

  • Circuit breaker open and local.

Tools and Equipment

  • Use suitable tools

  • Instrument secondary injection test (DHF)

  • Avometer

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Isolate the secondary terminals of VT, CT

Testing the Relay

  1. Open the links on terminals X211(61,81) to isolate the VT secondary circuit

  2. Connect test leads between the voltage terminals X211(61-81) terminal is the common figure 1

  3. Open the links on terminals X02 (302,402)

  4. Increase the voltage gradually until the second stage value

  5. Record the voltage value in the test sheet

  6. Increase the voltage gradually until the first stage value

  7. Record the voltage value in the test sheet

  8. Decrease the injection voltage gradually to zero

  9. Close the links terminals X02 (302,402), X211 (61,81)

  10. Make sure CT and PT secondary circuits are in normal condition
