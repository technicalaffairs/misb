Introduction

This procedure details the testing procedures P1, to be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Gaber Salah Taher, tested by eng. Osama Ahmed Hassanain at 30 January 2006, accepted by eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman Salim general director, approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel Baset Rekabi Head Sector of Aswan Dam 1 Power Station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem: HPGC MPIS coordinator & inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Unit must be shut down.

  • Circuit breaker open and local.

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection test set type DHF

  • Suitable tool set.

  • Avometer, test leads

  • Variable Resistance

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Isolate the secondary terminals of VT.

Testing the Relay

  1. Open the links on terminals X631(61,71) to isolate the VT. secondary circuit

  2. Connect test leads between the voltage terminals X621(67,90) terminal is the common figure 1

  3. Connect test leads between the bridge Resistance terminals   X631 (601,604) terminal is the common Figure 1.

  4. Open the links on terminals X02 ( 302 ,402 )

  5. Increase the voltage gradually until the 110vac value

  6. Record the voltage value in the test sheet .

  7. Increase the gradually Variable Resistance until pick-up value  

  8. Decrease the injection voltage gradually to Zero

  9. Decrease the fault Resistance gradually to Zero

  10. Close the links terminals X02 ( 302 , 402 ) , X631 (61 , 71 )  (601 , 604)   

  11. Make sure PT secondary circuits are in normal condition.
