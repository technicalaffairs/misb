Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet safety shoes ...... etc

  • Emergency unit (diesel) must be stopped.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect the relay testing units sverker to a power source

  2. Connect the timer to power source and its terminals to the trip terminals the relay test

  3. Put an ammeter on current leads of testing unit to measure the current and connect the voltmeter in parallel with the voltage leads testing unit to measure the voltage

  4. Review the manual

  5. Prepare the previous results of the relay testing

  6. Be sure that the C.B unit and the transformer are disconnected and D.C source is available

Over Load (Fts.) 45e1

  1. Inject 4 amp. to operating thermo stating to terminal (1,2) the operating coil working after 20 min

  2. Record results

 

 

Gen. min / max Frequency FM2 - 110 (46e3)

  1. Over frequency + 5%
      - Inject the frequency to terminal (1,2) 52.5 HZ the operating coil working after 4 second

  2. Under frequency - 5%
      - Inject the frequency to terminal (1,2) 47.5 HZ the operating coil working after 4 second
      - Record results

Reverse Power PM2 g 90g - 110 45e7

  1. Inject 2.5A at terminal (1,2) and inject the voltage 15.7 AC on terminal (3,4) the operating coil working after 1 second

  2. Record results

Over Current JSM (45e3 , 45e4 , 45e6)

  1. Inject 6 amp. to operating coil to terminal (1,2) per phase or each set (45e3 , 45e4 , 45e6) the operating coil working after 3 sec

  2. Record results - current and time

Differential Protection D2 se.47e1

  1. Inject 6 amp. to operating coil to terminal (3,1)(7,1)(11,1) side gen. stat point

  2. Inject 6 amp. to operating coil to terminal (2,1)(6,1)(10,1) side gen. min current the operating coil working after

  3. Record results

Min Voltage PUM 2g 90 - 10 49e4 (for BB 380 v)

  1. Inject 80% from rated voltage 100 VAC operating coil to terminal (1,2,3) to operating coil working after 2 sec

  2. Record results

Min / Max Frequency FM2 - 110 (49e4) (for BB 380 V)

  1. Over frequency + 2 %
       Inject the frequency to terminal (1,2) 51 HZ the operating coil working after 0.2 second

  2. Under frequency - 2 %
       Inject the frequency to terminal (1,2) 49 HZ the operating coil working after 0.2 second
       Record results

Gen. min / max Voltage PUM 2 % 90 - 140 (46e1)

  1. Over voltage + 15 %
     Inject 115 VAC to operating coil to terminal (1,2,3) the operating coil working after 3 second

  2. Under voltage - 13.5 %
      Inject 86.5 VAC to operating coil to terminal (1,2,3) the operating coil working after 3 second
      Record results voltage and time
