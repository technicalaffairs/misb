Introduction

This document shows the protective maintenance which must be performed annually on the modular cone check system of turbine its function is to quietly eliminate all forms of water hammer through the valve

The purpose of this maintenance is to examine the condition of the cones and diaphragms

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet .... etc

  • Maintenance staff should be will be trained & qualified or directly supervised by some one

  • Supervised by some one

    •

  • The unit must be stop out of work and dewatered

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid manual puller

Work to be carried out 

  1. The flanges need simply be unbolted the center plate

  2.  Removed the flanges will separate readily as the pressure seal is internal and subsequently not exposed to excessive corrosion

  3. Check the pressure seal diaphragms and change it if necessary
