Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the personal protective equipment must used as safety shoes, safety helmet ...etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Special Spray for test

  • Screw Drivers

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Retighten the terminals in control cubicle.

  2. Check input voltage (220 Vac) and output voltage (24Vdc).

  3. Check the detectors for all four zones.

  4. Simulate the alarm by using special spray and record the acoustic alarm and RED LED at this zone.

  5. Repeat step (4) at every at every zone and acknowledge the alarm.

  6. Finally check there is no RED LED alarm at any zone.

  7. Check the battery, which should be changed every three years.
