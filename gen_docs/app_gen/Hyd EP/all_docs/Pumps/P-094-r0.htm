Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every  month as indicated by M1 in the title block.

The function of the unit pump is to rise or lower the intake gates.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Maintenance staff should will trained & qualified or directly supervised by some one who

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Wrenches, Safety and healthy equipment

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the oil level on the tank of oil and add mobil oil (DTE 26) if necessary.

  2. Check the condition of return line filter and replace it if needed.

  3. Check the pressure on the pressure control valves (17.1-17.2-17.3-17.4).

  4. Check air breather filter.

  5. Test run and listen for abnormal sounds from the hydraulic pump.
