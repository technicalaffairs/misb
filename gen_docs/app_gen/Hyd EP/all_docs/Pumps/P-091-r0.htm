Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc

  • The pump must be out of operation

Tools and Equipment

  • Mechanical tool kit

  • Adjustable wrenchs with different sizes

  • Torque wrench

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rage, .... etc)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check that pump flange's bolts of out and inlet oil must be tightened

  2. Check the outer bolts of the pump must be tightened

  3. Inspect the coupling of the pump and motor

  4. Clean the outer surface of the pump

The Pump in Operation

  1. Check the flow rate of the pump

  2. Check the sound of the pump

  3. Check that no leakage of oil from the mechanical seal
