Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Stop pump and switch off the power supply.

  • Close the out let and inlet valves and put suitable warning boards.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

  • Set of wrench

  • Alignment equipment

  • Grease for lubricating

  • Cleaning material

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the alignment of the pump.

  2. Operate the pump to check the noise.

  3. Stop pump and switch off the power supply and close the out let and inlet valves.

  4. Dismantle the coupling.

  5. Dismantle the shaft and impeller and protection and cooling pushing and check for defects or cracks.

  6. Check key and change if need.

  7. Check the bearings if need.

  8. Install the pump and alingment with motor.

  9. Install the coupling.

  10. Operate the pump and check the load current.
