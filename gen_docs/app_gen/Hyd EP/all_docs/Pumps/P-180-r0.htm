Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Stop pump and switch off the power supply.

  • Close the out let and inlet valves and put suitable warning boards.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

  • Set of wrench

  • Alignment equipment

  • Grease for lubricating

  • Cleaning material

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the packing of the gland of the pump add or change.

  2. Check the packing of the gland of the outlet and inlet valves.

  3. Check the bolts of the pump and motor.

  4. Check the alignment of the pump.

  5. Operate the pump and check bearing and the noise.

  6. Check the current of the pump.
