Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, to be performed every three years or every 24000 hours. as indicated by Y3 / H2400 in the title block.

There are two drainage pumps one in the North main sump tank and the other in the South main sump tank and  their function to drain the main sump tanks.

Technical Specifications:

Rated power : 30kw                       Rated speed : 1450 rpm

Rated flow   :  20 Liter/sec              Delivery head : 46 m.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

  • Pump should be out of service.

    •

  • Isolate the pump mechanically and electrically.

  • The motor should be disconnected from MCC, control panel by electric maintenance section and hang caution marks.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Special mechanical tool kit includes torque wrenches with different sizes and feeler gauge.

  • There is slinging wench upward the sump tank, used for lifting and lowering the submersible pumps in the sump tank 3 tons

  • Oil drainage pump.

  • Cleaning articles ( fluid, rags, ... etc )

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Make sure that the lifting equipment is in good condition.

  2. Get out the pump from the sump.

  3. Check the condition of carrying handle, lifting eyes, chains and wire cables.

  4. Check that the guide bars are vertical.

  5. Rinse the pump thoroughly with clean water before starting work.

  6. Clean the pass of cooling pump system.

  7. Check the pump casing and impeller ( clearance not exceed 2 mm. )

  8. Dismantle the power and control cable from the top of pump (38) by electric sections.

  9. Drainage the oil from the oil casing using a small special pump (48) and check the oil condition see figure (1)

  10. Dismantle the parts of the pump in sequences as shown in figure (1)

  11. Inspect the shaft of pump (77)

  12. Inspect the impeller (81)

  13. Inspect the wear ring , replace it with a new one if necessary (100)

  14. Replace the lower and upper roller bearing with a new one (26 , 27)

  15. Replace the lower and upper mechanical seals with a new one (57 , 66)  

  16. Paint the inner and outer side of pump.

  17. Inspect the electrical sensors by electric sections

  18. Erect the pump in opposite sequence of dismantling and usE a new group of gaskets and sealing

  19. Erect the power and control cables in the top of pump by electric sections

  20. Fill the oil casing with a new oil and plugged it.

  21. Check the cable entry and make sure that the cable clamps are tight.

  22. Check the pipes and valves.

  23. Lay down the pump in it is seat and sure that it is in proper position.

  24. Joint electrical terminals of cables by electric sections.

  25. Check the function of operation of the pump.
