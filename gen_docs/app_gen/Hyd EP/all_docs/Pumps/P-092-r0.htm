Introduction

This document outlines the major  maintenance procedures P2, which must be performed every three years or when occur hummed noise, scratch, rumble noises or excessive temperature increases during the operation or after 24000 operating hours as indicated by Y3/H24000 in the title block.

The first draft was initiated by eng. Ashraf Saber Ibrahem, tested by eng. Alsayed Ibrahim El Ashmony, accepted by general director eng. Abdel Ghaffar Osman, approved  by eng. Mohamed Abd elbaset  head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC inspection and quality control general director and MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety Helmet.

    •

  • The pump must be out of operation

  • Isolated the electrical power supply from the motor

  • Isolated the pump by closed of the inlet and outlet oil valve

Tools and Equipment

  • Mechanical tool kit

  • Adjustable wrenches with different sizes

  • Torque wrench

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rage, .... etc)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismount the electric motor (by maintenance electric stuff)

  2. Drain the remaining oil in the pump, suction and delivery pipes

  3. Draw off coupling half on the pump side by means of a detaching device

  4. Remove key (41) from the shaft end of the driving screw

  5. Slacken socket - head cap screws (51) and dismount slid - in pump towards the driving side. (Use forcing screws)

  6. Slacken socket - head cap screws (54), dismount stuffing box casing (76). (Use forcing screws)

  7. Dismount circlip (35)

  8. Slacken socket - head cap screws (55) dismount screw set with pump cover on the driving side (3) from the casing insert (2) and remove working screws with compensating bushes (8) from the driving screw

  9. Dismount driving screw with grooved ball bearing (34) from pump cover on the driving side (3)

Assembling

  1. Mounting of the screw set to be effected in reverse order of the dismounting prior to assembling the pump absolutely see to it that all components should be from dirt and abrasive particles and

  2. The ball bearing and mechanical seal must be changed with a new one

Note:

  1. Warm up coupling half on the pump side to approx. 80 ºC (oil bath) and push on to the driving screw. Avoid knocking the coupling up in a cold condition

  2. The pump must be prepared by filling with oil and deriation before its operation

  3. Check the sound of pump, leakage and the flow rate
