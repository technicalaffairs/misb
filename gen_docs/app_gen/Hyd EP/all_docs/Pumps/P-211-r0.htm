Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The pumps are centrifugal, horizontal with radial impeller and spiral casing provided with feet axial suction orifice and delivery orifice in an upward position. 

The unit block pump motor is assembled on an electro-welded steel base plate.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

  • Pump should be out of service.

    •

  • Isolate the pump mechanically and electrically.

  • The motor should be disconnected from MCC, control panel.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set includes manual wrenches, tightening hexagonal screw with a torque wrench.

  • Special tools as mechanical seal, ball bearing  ... etc.

  • Cleaning articles and brushes ... etc

  • Blanco 1 ton

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check that the shaft can rotate free by hand.

  2. Check the lightening of the section and delivery flanges.

  3. Check that the ball bearings are filled up with grease.

  4. Check the mechanical sealing.

  5. Check for presence of foreign substances which could damage the pump.

  6. Check the oil level.
