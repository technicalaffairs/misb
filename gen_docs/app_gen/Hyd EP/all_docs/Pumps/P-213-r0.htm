Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The high pressure oil system must be actuated during start up - up to approx 90% rated speed of the generator. Also during shutdown the high pressure oil system is to be activated between 90% of the rated speed until has teached standstill

  • The high pressure bearing oil pump is to be in operation whenever the turbine shaft is turning at any speed below approx 90% of rated speed.

  • The high pressure oil pump set is mounted on the bearing oil plant. The AC motor is for normal operating condition the DC motor is for stand-by.

  • The high pressure oil pump is adouble bank radial piston pump with five pistons per bank with three delivery connection.

  • The oil pressure is controlled by pressure switches.

  • The shaft can be rotated manually when high pressure oil is properly applied to the bearings.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes .... etc

  • The pump is out of service.

  • The motor is disconnected from the control panel.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set includes manual wrenches ... etc

  • Tighten hexagonal screws with a torque wrench

  • Blanco 1 ton

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Before assembly of the coupling clean the hub and sleeve teeth carefully.

  2. Erect the coupling hubs so that the inner sides close flush with the shaft ends grub screws can be screwed in carefull adjustment of the shaft increases the life of the coupling.
