Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

The technical specification of the pump

Q = 1000 M3/H         H = 100 M         P = 400 K.W.       V = 6000 V

The first draft initiated by eng. Hatem Abd El Daim and tested by eng. Hanay Fahmy Madany at 13-7-2004 High Dam hydraulic PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Stop pump and switch off the power supply

  • Close the out let and inlet valves

  • Put suitable warning boards

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

  • Set of wrench

  • Alignment equipment

  • Grease for lubricating

  • Cleaning material

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the packing of the gland of the pump ads or change

  2. Check the packing of the gland of the outlet and inlet valves ads or change

  3. Check the bolts of the pump and motor

  4. Check the alignment of the pump

  5. Operate the pump and check the noice and bearing
  6. Check the current
