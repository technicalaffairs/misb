Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Switch down the generator

  • Switch off field breaker of auxiliary generator

  • Switch off disconnector for main generator

  • Switch off AC and DC automatic

  • Switch on isolator ground of auxiliary generator

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy tools as safety helmet

  • Blower

  • Special tools

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purpose

Work to be carried out 

  1. Clean and revision the cabinet of thyristor groups

  2. Visual inspection of thyristor section and clean the filters of thyristor section

  3. Check the protection of thyristor signal and trip

  4. Clean and check the field breaker of auxiliary generator

  5. Clean and revision the panel of power section

  6. Clean and revision the panel of regulation section

  7. Check at the clamping bolts of lead connection are loose

  8. Testing signaling from system to DCS

  9. Check the protection and signaling of thyristor
