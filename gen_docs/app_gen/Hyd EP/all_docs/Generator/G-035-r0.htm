Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued

  • The generator is out of service

  • The generator fire fighting out of service

  • Generator breaker is applied.

  • Connect earth switch of generator CB

  • Disconnect the stand still heating of generator

  • Position of rotor locking bolt on position

Tools and Equipment

  • Vaccum cleaner

  • Smooth brushes

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Elin generator are designed to minimize maintenance work

Stator

  1. Keep the generator stator clean all the time there will be no major maintenance work

  2. Use a vacuum cleaner and smooth brushes for cleaning or even better if availiable dry air, not exceeding 3 bars of air pressure

  3. If the CO2-fire fighting system has been operated the generator shall be checked and cleaning of all parts must be performed

Rotor

  1. Keep the generator rotor clean all the time there will be no major maintenance work

  2. Use vacuum cleaner and smooth brushes for cleaning

  3. The slip rings have to be inspected monthly for wear and proper operation

  4. Worn brushes should be changed when wear is approximately fifty percent of the original length (use for rotor earth fault)

  5. Measure the insulation resistance and polarization index after the unit is out of network

  6. Check the operation of the stand still heating of generator

  7. Check the operation of the generator brake limits switch's

  8. Check the operation of dehumidifier

  9. Check the RTD s of hot air and cold air

  10. Clean the neutral point of generator

  11. Check the position of the rotor locking bolt

  12. Clean the over hang of the stator winding by vaccum cleaner and smooth brushes

  13. Clean the generator over hang by use alcohol

  14. Keep the generator section of the bulb always absolutely clean
