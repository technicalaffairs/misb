Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the personal protective equipment must used as safety shoes, safety helmet ...etc

  • Switch off CB of MG           

  • Switch down the generator and exciter        

  • Switch off disconnector          

  • Switch off AC and DC automatic

  • Switch ON isolator ground of (MG and AG)

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy tools as safety helmet            

  • Blower                                           

  • Special tools                     

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purpose

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visual inspection of thyristor section

  2. Clean and change the filters of thyristor    

  3. Clean and review the cabinet of thyristor groupes (WG and FG ) .                            

  4. Check the protection of thyristor signal and trip.

  5. Clean and check the field circuit of MG and AG

  6. Clean and review the panel of automatic voltage regulator MG and AG

  7. Check if the clamping bolts of lead connection are loose.
