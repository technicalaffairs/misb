Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft is initiated and tested by eng. Amr Moustafa, accepted by eng. Nagy Nageeb Saleh: general manager, approved by eng. Khalaf Allah Mahmoud Abd El-Gaber: head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman: HPGC quality control general director & MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the personal protective equipment must used as safety shoes, safety helmet ...etc

  • Neutral Generator must be isolated voltage from two sides ( IN – OUT ).

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger

  • Hand tools

  • Voltage detector

  • Volt – ohm meter

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the terminal.

  2. Check perfect tightening of all terminals.

  3. Test the protective devices.

  4. Check the painting.
