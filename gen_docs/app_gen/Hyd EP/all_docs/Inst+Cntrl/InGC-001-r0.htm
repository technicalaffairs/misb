Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

This circuit let the gates lowering under its weigh and controlled through series resistance and DC generator to break the gate at zero position.

The AC control used to lift or lower the intake gate in normal operation, this circuit consist of command apparatus limit switches and control buttons which control the operation of 4 motors rapid lowering used DC control circuit and it works according to over speed protection ( 175 RPM ), or the local push button in machine hall or in intake.

The first draft initiated & tested by eng. Mohamed Shaaban Al-Behairy, accepted by general director eng. Selem Ahmed Aly,  approved by eng. Abd El-kreem Abu Zaid: head sector of High Dam Power Station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belts, work gloves

  • Switch off the automatic ( AC – Power Supply )

  • Switch off the automatic ( DC – Power Supply )

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Portable air blower.

  • Set of common , acrew drivers and cleaning material ( rag , cloth ).

  • Digital multimeter.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Clean the contacts for push buttons.

  2. Clean the contacts for limit switches.

  3. Cleaning contacts for relays 1kt, 2kt, 1k2, 2k2, 1k3, 2k3, 3pґ3I and 3pґ3Д.

  4. Checking operating voltage and reset for relays:
