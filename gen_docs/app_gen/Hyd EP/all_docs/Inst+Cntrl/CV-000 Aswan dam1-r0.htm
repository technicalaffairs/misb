Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist I1 for Auma actuator valve type SAREX 10.1 ñ F10,  K.K.S  PGM10.AA005 & PGM10.AA006, to be carried out on an annually bases as indicated as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Zakaria Mohamed Abdel Raheem, reviewied & yested by eng. Osama Ahmed Hasanian, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman Selim, and approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel Bast head Sector of Aswan Dam 2 Hydraulic Power Station and certified by HPGC quality control general director, and MPIS coordinator eng. Kamel Ragheb.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • Ensure the unit out of service

  • Close manual isolating valves after and before control valve.

  • Disconnect power supply.

  • Disconnect the mechanical coupling

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety helmet, shoes, and rags

  • Digital Ammeter, Screw Driver.

  • Open end Spanner set.

  • DC Power Supply.

Work to be carried out

Steps to Prepare for test

  1. Review technical reference material and manufactureís manual.

  2. Obtain copy of valve field maintenance file.

Testing Procedure

  1. Put Operation selector switch to manual position.

  2. Open valve manually and record the opening indicator lamp and the digital signal as in signal list.

  3. Close valve manually and record the closing indicator lamp and the digital signal as in signal list.

  4. Simulate the Failure signal and check the failure indicator lamp and alarm signal.

Return Control Valve to Service

  1. Return control valve to service by reconnecting the mechanical coupling.

  2. Connect power supply.

  3. Open manual isolating valves.

  4. Place a copy of test result in the field maintenance.
