Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedures P1, for all types of thermosignal for 500kv lines, 132 kv lines and units, to be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

This first draft initiated & tested by eng. Ahmed Mohamed Hamada, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Diaa El-Gabalawe, approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zaid: High Dam Hydraulic PS Head Sector, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC quality control general director & MPIS Coordinator

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Test Stand

  • Oven temperature

  • Ohmmeter

Work to be Carried Out

preparation

  1. Review technical reference material and manufacture's manual

  2. Obtain copy of the setting value of thermosignal

  3. Disconnect the bulb of the meter from its thermowell

  4. Disconnect the protection socket from the meter

  5. Remove thermosignal from the barrier and take it to lab

Testing

  1. Put the bulb into the oven and hang the meter at the stand

  2. Connect stand protection socket to the meter

  3. Turn oven on

  4. Vary oven temperature gradually as stated in MRDS

  5. Record the readings of the oven and of the meter, and compare between them

  6. Adjust meter if it is neecessary

  7. At 65°C be sure that alarm contact was closed (stand green lamp lit)

  8. At 75°C be sure that trip contact was closed (stand green lamp lit)

  9. If it is necessary readjust protection contacts

Return thermocouple into service

  1. Hang the meter at the barrier

  2. Install the bulb in the well

  3. Connect protection socket to the mete

  4. Place a copy of test result in the field maintenance file
