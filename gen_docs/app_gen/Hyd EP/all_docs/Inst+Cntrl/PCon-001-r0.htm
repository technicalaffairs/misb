Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

Two pumps one in auto position the other inres position the auto one works when oil pressure in the accumulator goes down to 37 kg / cm2 and stops at 40 kg / cm2 .

The reserve pump works at 34 kg/cm2 and stops at 40 kg/cm2.

The pressure oil used through speed governor actuator to open and close two servomotors which they open and close the wicket gates.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belts, work gloves

  • Switch off the automatic ( AC – Power Supply )

  • Switch off the automatic S401D.

  • Switch off the automatic F403.

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Portable air blower.

  • Set of common, screw drivers and cleaning material ( rag , cloth ).

  • Digital multimeter.
