Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

Leakage oil pump at level 112, works ( Man-Auto) it pumps the leakage oil from the servomotor to the accumulators tank.

The leaking oil collected in a tank on the turbine cover and this tank has a float when the level of oil in the tank reaches 220mm height the float goes-up, and the mercury contact closes and the relay pn-25-T energized and the pump start to work, when the level of oil reach 60wm hight the mercury contact opens and the pump stop if the level reaches 240mm and the pump did not work a mercury contact closes to give alarm.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belts, work gloves

  • Switch off the automatics:

    • Ac ( Control of leakage oil pump )

    • 5401E ( for Alarm )

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Portable air blower.

  • Set of common, screw drivers and cleaning material ( rag , cloth ).

  • Digital multimeter.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Clean and check contacts for relay ( 5pn) and check at operative voltage (160v) and reset (80v) .

  2. Adjust mercury Contact.

  3. Clean and check contacts for key position.

  4. Checkthe oil pump (Man, Auto).
