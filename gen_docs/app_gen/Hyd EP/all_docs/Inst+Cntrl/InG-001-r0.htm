Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed on a  monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block

Technical specifications:

Maximum lifting distance:   20.7 m by hydraulic telescopic servomotor.

Safety Precautions

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • The maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Turn off electric power to crane before maintenance proceeding.

Tools and Equipment

  • Special mechanical tool kit includes torque wrench, wrenches with different sizes,

  • Pressure grease gun and feeler gauge, hoses

  • Mobilplex 47 grease, Mobilux Ep4 grease and Esso surrett Nx 5k gears

  • Cleaning articles ( fluid, rags ... etc )

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Examine and cleaning of servomotor and grease it with Mobilplex 47 grease

  2. Check the servomotor seals every three months for excessive leakage

  3. Grease the chain roller wheel every six months with Mobilux Ep4 grease

  4. Examine the paint work and renew if necessary

  5. Clean the filter cloth and excessively dirty filters should be renewed .

  6. Check fuses and gate position indicator every month

  7. Check all terminals and screws for tight seat in control cubical
