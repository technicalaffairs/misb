Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The hydraulic system, control circuits consists of group of relays control the sequence of operation of closing and opening the gate

The irrigation demand is the factor affects the operation of the station. If we can not meet this demand by generating electricity we have to open a segment gate to pass much water to meet the irrigation demand.

  • Force for gate lifting in tons 40.

  • Force for gate pressing in tons 15.

  • Force for eccentric tightening in tons 75.

  • Force for eccentric releasing in tons 5.

  • Angle of turning of the ecceutric lever 90.

  • Angle of gate turning from the loaer 31.

  • Gate lifting time in minutes 16.

  • Gate lowering time in minutes 17.

The first draft initiated & tested by eng. Mohamed Shaaban Al-Behairy, accepted by general director eng. Selem Ahmed Aly,  approved by eng. Abd El-kreem Abu Zaid: head sector of High Dam Power Station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belts, work gloves

  • Switch off the automatic ( AC – Power Supply )

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Portable air blower.

  • Set of common , screw drivers and cleaning material ( rag, cloth ).

  • Digital multimeter.

 

 

Work to be Carried out

  1. Clean the contacts for limit switches.

  2. Clean the contacts for relays and checking operating voltage and reset for relays:
