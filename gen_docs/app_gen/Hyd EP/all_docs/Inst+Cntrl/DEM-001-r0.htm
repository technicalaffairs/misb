Introduction

This document lists the testing procedure P1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

The testing procedure for all types of energy meters for 500kv, 132kv units and lines

The first draft  initiated & tested by eng. Ahmad Mohamed Hamada, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Diaa El-Gabalawe, revised by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC general director quality control, and approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zaid Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station,

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Maintenance staff should be trained well, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety shoes, safety helmet ... etc

    •

  • The Unit or Line meters under test must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multimeter

  • Meter test PTS 1.3

  • Test stand

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Review technical reference material and manufactures manual.

  2. Obtain copy of attached test certificate

  3. Short circuit the current terminals and open voltage terminals which feed the meter

  4. Be sure there is no voltage at meter terminals using multimeter.

  5. Disconnect meter wires and isolate them with tape.

  6. Remove the meter from the panel and take it to the lab.

Testing Procedures

  1. Put the meter at test stand.

  2. Measure the meter insulation at 100v.

  3. Connect the meter terminals to the meter test PTS1.3 as shown at figure 1

  4. Connect the scanning head between the meter and meter test.

  5. Switch power on to the meter test.

  6. Adjust meter test to measurement function and select "error measure' mode.

  7. From sub menu:

    1. Select 4 wire measurement.

    2. Select watt  measurement.

    3. Enter meter constant.

    4. Enter number of pulses

  8. Adjust the voltage at 100v.

  9. Inject current and adjust the scanning head.

  10. Adjust pf =1.0

  11. Adjust the current at 10, 20, 40, 60, 80, 100% of load current.

  12. Measure the error.

  13. Record the readings at the attached test certificate

  14. Adjust pf = 0.5

  15. Repeat steps 11, 12, 13.

  16. Be sure the error at acceptable limits.

Return the Meter to Service

  1. Switch power off.

  2. Disconnect the meter terminals from meter test.

  3. Measure the meter insulation at 1000v.

  4. Return the meter to the panel at site.

  5. Connect meter wires.

  6. Disconnect the SC and close the voltage circuits.

  7. Place a copy of test results in the field maintenance file.
