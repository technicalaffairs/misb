Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block. "There are 3 units of air conditioner"

Its function is to keep the required temperature of control room.

The first draft was initiated by eng. Ashraf Saber, tested by eng. Kamal Hassan, accepted by eng. Abd El-Ghaffar Osman: general director, and  approved by eng. Ahmed Mohamed Yonis; Head Sector of Aswan Dam 1 & 2 Power Station and certified by eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman HPGC inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • The maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

  • Stop the unit and turn off the electric supply.

Tools and Equipment

  • Special mechanical tool kit

  • Vacuum pump

  • Cleaning articles ( fluid, rags, ... etc )

  • Charging hose

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the unit ( suction and exhaust ducts , condenser, evaporator ) with compressed air  

  2. Inspect the evaporator fan, condenser fan. 

  3. Inspect the pipe connections.

  4. Turn on the electric supply and operate the unit

  5. Inspect the coolant water pumps and making maintenance for them.

  6. Inspect and cleaning for all fan cool units in the site.

  7. Drain the water from compensation water tank of the system and cleaning it and paint with a suitable paint.

  8. Refill the tank with water again.

  9. Measure the pressure from gauge points.

  10. Refill with refrigerant 22 if necessary.

  11. Finish the work permit.
