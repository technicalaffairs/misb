Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

A pump works when the unit has a starting signal but the sequence of starting the unit will not take place unless the high pressure pump works and the pressure of oil reaches 75 kg/cm2 and the sequence of stopping as well.

The main function of this pump is to make a film of oil at thrust bearing between the mirror and the bads to to preveist  the tilne bads from mechanical friction at starting and stopping the unit (for unit no10)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Switch off the automatics (AC power supply)

  • Switch off the automatics S401F

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable air blower

  • Set of common, screw drivers and cleaning material (rag, cloth)

  • Digital multineter

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the contacts for ( lA) switch

  2. Clean the contacts for selector switch

  3. Clean the contacts for relays (2PП,3PП, 1PH, 1PBП)

  4. Check the operating voltage and reset

    Relays Operating Reset
    2P П 160 V 90 V
     3PП 160 V 100 V
    1 PH 150 V 30 V
    1PB П 150 V 100 V

  5. Check the operating high pressure pump manually
