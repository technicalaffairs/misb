Introduction

There are two unwatering pumps one in the north main sump tank and the other in the south main sump tank and their type CT3230 ( used for unwatering the turbine water path in maintenance )

There are two drainage pumps one in the north main sump tank and the other in the south main sump tank and their type CP3230 ( used for drained the main sump tanks )

 This document outlines the pump inspection which performed every six months

The first draft was initiated by eng. Abd Elnabi Ibrahim , tested by eng. Al sayed Ibrahim Al ashmony, checked by general director eng. Abd elghaffar Osman and  approved by eng .Ahmed Mohamed Yonis; Head Sector of Aswan ( 1 & 2 ) Power Station

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • Safety fence ( rope ) surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes , belts and work gloves.

  • The pump have to be stopped and its electric breaker isolating before starting any maintenance procedure

Tools and Equipment

  • Cleaning articles ( fluid , rags , ….. Etc )

  • There is slinging wenche upward the sump tank using for lifting and lowering the submersible pumps in the sump tank ( 3 tons )

  • Torque wrench , wrench with different sizes  and feeler gauge

    Special mechanical tool kit

  • Oil drainage pump

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Make sure that the lifting equipment is in good condition

  2. Get out the pump from the sump

  3. Check the condition of carrying handle , lifting eyes , chains and wire cables

  4. Check that the guide bars are vertical

  5. Rinse the pump thoroughly with clean water before starting work , good clean of the outer pump surface

  6. Clean the pass of cooling pump system

  7. Check the pump casing and impeller

  8. Check the clearance between the impeller skirt and the pump casin
    ( not exeeds 2 mm. ) if it exeeds 2 mm. replacing the wear ring  ( 13 )

  9. Check the condition of oil through the oil hole screw ( 1 )

  10. Take  some of oil from the bottom of oil casing and sure that no water mixed with oil , then check that the oil reaches up to oil hole

  11. If there is water mixed with oil . the oil then became cream-like and oil must be change ( about 14 liters )

  12. Replace a new O ring (2), good tightness for the oil plug (1).  Mark : if the oil full change because there is water in the casing oil , the pump must be inspect again after one operation week

  13. Check the liquid ( water / oil ) in the stator casing :

  14. Warning : resulting from leakage , there my be over pressure in     

  15. The stator casing . Hold a rag over the inspection screw to        

  16. Prevent splatter . 

  17. Tilt the pump so that any liquid in the stator casing can run out      

  18. Through the hole

  19. If there is water in the stator casing the cause may be : 
         * That the inspection cover (4) is no tight enough       
         * That O ring (5) is damaged
         * That cable entries (11) are leaking .                           

  20. If there oil in the stator casing the cause may be :

  21. That the inner mechanical seal (7) is damaged and must be replaced

  22. That an O ring (9) is damaged and must be replaced

  23. Check the cable entry : make sure that the cable clamps are tight

  24. Check the pipes , valves

  25. Lay down the pump in it is seat and sure that it is in proper position

  26. Check the function of operation of the pump .
