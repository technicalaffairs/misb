Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

Passenger elevator is working between six floors (floor 0.1.3.4.5&6)

It's considered as an essential load so it will be supplied by emergency diesel

The car door is working automatically with the floor door.

Youssef Mahmoud Aly :  general director of Esna hydraulic power station and certified by eng. Kamel Raghib Hakem: HEPC inspection & quality control and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Wear the personal safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet, ...etc

  • Maintenance is carried out with cooperation with mechanical department.

Tools and Equipment

  • Avometer

  • Vaccum cleaner

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Turn off the supply of the passenger elevator from mcc room & from the elevators room

  2. Using vaccum cleaner to clean the motors filter and modules` cabinet.

  3. Turn on the supply of the elevator from mcc & elevator room.

  4. Change the control mode to "inspection" from the switch on the top of the car.

  5. Review all limit & magnetic switches on the car and their cams inside the elevator pit for all floors visually and adjust their position if needed.

  6. Change the control mode to "normal" from the switch on the car.

  7. Test open & close all doors in all floors automatically.

  8. Review photocell adjust and fasten it if needed.

  9. Reviewing all motors & the lighting of the car is carried out by general auxiliries department.

  10. Test calling the car in all floors.

  11. Put the elevator in service.
