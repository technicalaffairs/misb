Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The control valve modules are based on a modular design, fitting location on the oil tank module is freely selectable. The individual control valves are interchangable. The return pipes are below the oil level

The following items are fitting on the control valve module:

  1. Servovalve

  2. Double pressure filter for filtering the control

  3. Filter connection

  4. Minimess connections and armatures

  5. Lubrication pump. Relive valve, Pressure switch and pressure gauge

The two control oil circuits:

  1. Main circuit for actuation of the servomotor

  2. Control oil circuit with fine filter for the oil actuate the servovalves, proportional and direction control valves

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

  • The unit should be out of service.

  • Close the oil supply.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set includes manual wrenches ... etc.

  • Cleaning rags.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the servovalve.

  2. Change the double pressure filter.

  3. Change the special filters in the sevovalve.
