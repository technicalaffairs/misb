Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be carried out on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the above title block.

The  function of the gate shaft water level indicator is to show the level of water inside the gate shaft.

The first draft was initiated and tested by eng. Kamal Amin Gad Allah, accepted by general director eng. Atty Sefen Rizk Allah, approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel-Baset head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area are required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Wrenches

Work to be Carried out

  1. Clean the measuring shaft ,there should be no foreign object in the shaft which would hinder the float motion.
