Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months or when damages occur on any part as indicated by M6 in the title block

This crane is used for maintenance work of the intake gates and for  lifting and lowering the bulkhead gates in front of the intake gates of the turbine unites.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belts, work gloves

  • Turn off electric power to crane before maintenance proceeding

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Cleaning articles ( fluid, rags  .. etc )

  • Mobilgear 634 oil , Esso surrett Nx 5k gears and Mobilplex 47 grease

  • Torque wrench, wrench with different sizes, pressure grease gun, and feeler gauge

 

       

Work to be carried out  

  1. Check brake lining for wear and tear and if 2 mm thick above or under rivets should be changed

  2. Carefully examine for broken wire strands and any other damages

  3. Examine the ropes and grease it with Esso surrett Nx 5k grease

  4. Check oil level of gear boxes and refill if necessary with Mobilgear 634 oil

  5. Inspect and check tightness of bolts , nuts .

  6. Examine the condition of flanges and anti-friction bearing seals

  7. Examine the structure steel works for rust or corrosion and repainted if necessary

  8. Check rings and bushings for wear and tear

  9. Clean bearings and grease it with Mobilplex 47 grease

  10. Check tightness of coupling, screws

  11. Examine gears for main hoist, axial travel and cross travel which in open boxes and grease it with Esso surrett Nx 5k grease.

  12. Place a copy of test results in the crane data file.
