Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenanc staff should will trained & qualified or directly supervised by some one

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area are required

  • Personal protctive equipment must be used

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

  • Wrenches

  • Suitable oil for gear box, Grease.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Lubricate the movable parts

  2. Change oil of gear boxes

  3. Change grease of bearing

  4. Grease gears

  5. Test the break

  6. Test the crane moving

  7. Test the limit of cable stroke

  8. Test the horizontal velocity

  9. Test th lifting velocity

  10. Test the sliding velocity
