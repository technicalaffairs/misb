Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Its function is lifting and lowering the rotor of main generator, movabale parts and auxiliaries.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should will trained & qualified or directly supervised by some one.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area are required.

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

  • Wrenches

  • Suitable oil for gear box, Grease.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect lifting hook, lifting rope, rope pulleys of trolley, rope drum, hoisting machinery, cabin, steel construction, wires, break, and track.

  2. Check oil in the gear box.

  3. Lubricate the bearing.
