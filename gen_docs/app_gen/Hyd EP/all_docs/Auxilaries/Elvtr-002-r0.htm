Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Technical Specifications:

 Load 800 kg, Delta / Y,  220 / 380V, 17/4.2 kW, 1435 / 330 rpm,  60/135A, 34 /20A

The first draft initiated & tested by eng. Ashraf Loka Fikry, accepted by eng. Soliman Abdel-Fatah: general manager, approved by eng. Abdel-Karem AbuZaid: High Dam head sector, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem: HPGC quality control general director & MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc

  • Stop the elevator and Switch off the AC, DC for the elevator

  • Suitable warning boards.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Set of common screw drivers, wrenches

  • Cleaning material, grease for lubricating.

  • Avometer and clamp

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle the motor cable terminal and sign it

  2. Dismantle the coupling and motor

  3. Dismantle fly wheel and dismantle motor back cover

  4. Dismantle the motor forward cover and carryout the rotor

  5. Cleaning the stator from dust and increasing winding insulation

  6. Check motor bearing and change one if it is needed

  7. Mantle the motor after insulation drying  

  8. Cleaning motor main contacts for magnetic resistance

  9. Cheek electric brake coil

  10. Collection the motor with gears and electric cables

  11. Check elevator and make it on
