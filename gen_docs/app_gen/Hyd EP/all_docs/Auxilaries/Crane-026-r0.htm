Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

  • Switch off the AC current

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

  • Complete tool set

  • Cleaning materials (cloth – Benzene).

  • Grease.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean commutator, slip ring, brushes for motors.

  2. Check the bearing of the motors and replace them if there is any damage in them.

  3. Clean and inspect the main contacts of the contactors.

  4. Inspect and clean the resistors of the rotor

  5. Check the control circuit

  6. Inspect and check the brake of the motors

  7. Make revision for the motors of the supporters
