Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months or when damages occur on any part as indicated by M6 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belts, work gloves

  • Turn off electric power to crane before maintenance proceeding

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Digital multi-meter.

  • Hand held radio wave for communication.

  • Screw driver kit and test leads.

  • Antistatic vacuum cleaner for dust cleaning.

  

     

Work to be carried out  

  1. Switch off main CB.

  2. Check the contacts for main and aux. contactors and clean it by using spray.

  3. Check limit switch for trolley &hoist and crane movement (terminal & contacts).

  4. Check emergency limit switch (terminal & contacts).

  5. Check the contacts and terminals for joystick of trolley, hoist and crane movement.

  6. Switch on main CB.

  7. Check trolley movement and its limit switch function.

  8. Check hoist movement and its limit switch function.

  9. Check crane movement and its limit switch function.

  10. Place a copy of test results in the crane data file.
