Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belts, work gloves

  • Turn off all power to crane before proceeding

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Torque wrench, wrench with different sizes, pressure grease gun and feeler gauge

  • Cleaning articles ( fluid , rags , ….. Etc )

  • Mobilgear 634 oil , Esso surrett Nx 5k gears

    

Work to be carried out  

  1. Check brake lining for wear and tear and if 2 mm thick above or under rivets should be changed

  2. Examine for broken wire strands

  3. Examine rope securing points and rope guide.

  4. Grease rope with Esso surrett Nx 5k.

  5. Check oil level of gear boxes and refill if necessary with Mobilgear 634 oil.

  6. Inspect and check tightness of bolts, nuts.

  7. Examine the condition of flanges and anti-friction bearing seals.

  8. Examine the structure steel works for rust or corrosion and repainted if necessary.

  9. Check rings and bushings for wear and tear.

  10. Check  tightness of coupling, screws.

  11. Clean bearings and lubricat it with Mobilux Ep4 grease.

  12. Examine gears for main hoist, axial travel and cross travel which in open boxes and grease it with Esso surrett Nx 5k grease.

  13. Operate the crane for test.

  14. Place a copy of the field test results at the equipment data file.
