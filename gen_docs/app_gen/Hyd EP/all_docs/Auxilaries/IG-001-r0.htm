Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintanance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Switch off the power supply for the gate.

  • The tunnel must be empty.

  • Lowering six DS stop loges in even slot.

  • Install a fixed wooden platform

  • Wear safety equipment as safety belt, shoes ... etc.

  • Use lights 36 v.

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of wrench, Special screw drivers.

  • Cleaning material.

  • Grinding machine.

  • Welding machine.

  • Compressed airline.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check up the oil level in the tank.

  2. Check up the oil level in the hinge.

  3. Check up the oil leakage in all joins of the gate.

  4. Check up and calibrate all the manometers.

  5. Check up the mechanical parts at complete opening and complete closing and partial opening and check the time and pressure.

  6. Check up the protection.

  7. Check up the oil leakage from the backing box and servomotor shaft and tight all the bolts.

  8. Check up, clean and grease the rope of transmission moving.

  9. Check up the hinge and oil leakage from it.

  10. Check up the servomotor shaft and repair any corrosion.

  11. Install wood platform after openIing the gate in mantenace opening.

  12. Check up the water seal of the gate and change if necessary.

  13. Check up the corrosion or cavitations in the gate.

  14. Check up and clean the filters and all pipes.

  15. Check up the bolts and all the metallic parts.

  16. Check up the reduction and non-retum valves.

  17. Check up ,clean and grease the bearing.

  18. Clean and paint the gate.
