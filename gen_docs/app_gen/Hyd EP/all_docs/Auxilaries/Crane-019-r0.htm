Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months or 'when damages occur on any part of it' as indicated by M6 in the title block

This crane is used for lifting and lowering draft tube gate of main turbine unites

Technical specifications:

Carried capacity : 88 ton                         Hoist speed : 1.3 m / min.

Crane travel speed : 6.7 m / min.             Wheel base : 20 m.             Span : 5.4 m.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Saber Ibrahem, tested by eng. Mohamed Hafez Abdel-Meniem, checked by general director eng. Abdel Ghaffar Osman, revised by HPGC quality control eng. Kamel Ragheb, and  approved by eng. Mohamed Abd Elbast; Head Sector of Aswan 1 & 2  Power Station

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, belts, work gloves

  • Turn off all power to crane before proceeding.

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment, safety goggles, and rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Mobilgear 634 oil , Esso surrett Nx 5k gears

  • Torque wrench, wrench with different sizes, pressure grease gun and feeler gauge

  • Cleaning articles ( fluid, rags ... etc )

Work to be carried out  

  1. Check brake lining for wear and tear and if 2 mm thick above or under rivets should be changed.

  2. Examine the chain and grease it with Esso surrett Nx 5k grease

  3. Check oil level of gear boxes and refill if necessary with Mobilgear 634 oil

  4. Inspect and check tightness of bolts, nuts.

  5. Examine the condition of flanges and anti-friction bearing seals

  6. Examine the structure steel works for rust or corrosion and repainted if necessary

  7. Check rings and bushings for wear and tear

  8. Check tightness of coupling, screws

  9. Examine gears for main hoist, axial travel and cross travel which in open boxes and grease it with Esso surrett Nx 5k grease.
