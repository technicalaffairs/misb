Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • No smoking is permitted in the battery room.

  • Use standard personal safety equipment as helmet, safety shoes, safety goggles, and rubber gloves, and rubber apron.

  • Use the proper insulated tools.

  • It is not permitted to place any tools on the top of the battery cells avoiding short circuit.

  • It is not permitted to use the same tools on Lead-Acid battery before using on Alkaline battery and vice- versa.

  • Disconnect the power supply fuse.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard personal safety and healthy equipment

  • Brushes, distilled water, rags, safety goggles, rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • AVO meter

Work to be Carried Out

  1.  The overall voltage of bank must be measured and recorded.

  2. The battery charger must be switched –off on both sides.

  3. All cells should be inspected for cracks and leakage of electrolyte and the damaged cell(s) must be changed immediately.

  4. Electrolyte level for all cells should be inspected and if it is below the mark ”normal" distilled water should be added providing that the electrolyte density is not affected.

  5. The electrolyte density should be measured and recorded for all cells.

  6. The inter-cell connectors and terminals for all cells should be inspected and cleaned from salt deposits using distilled water and clean dry rags.

  7. Terminal connections should be checked for tightness.

  8. Terminals and inter-cell connectors should be lubricated with a thin film of Vaseline.

  9. Top of cells should be cleaned.

  10. Measure and record the voltage per cell.

  11. Measure and record the density per cell.

  12. Check the drop of voltage and density it should be within the accepted range.

  13. Compare the present readings with the previous readings to decide the battery condition.

  14. In case the battery is in good condition the battery charger should be turned back to service.
