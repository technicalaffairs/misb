Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The function is to keep the speed of the unit at 100 rpm during the unit is online and control of the wicket gate opening and runner position.

The first draft was initiated by eng. Mohamed Al-Lethy Ahmed, tested by eng. Abdel Gawad Yousef Mohamed, accepted by general director eng. Atty Sefin Rezq Alla, approved  by eng. Mohamed Abdel-Baset Mohamed Rekaby; head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc

  • Unit out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Wrenches, Indicators, Voltmeter.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Change filters, inspect and clean flow regulators and pressure regulators.

  2. Inspect, disassemble, clean, assemble and calibrate the pilot valve bushing and plunger in the distributing valve and in the transducer assembly.

  3. Check, disassemble, clean, assemble the gate limit valve plunger and the pilot servomotor.

  4. Ensure that there is no friction through the full stroke.

  5. Check and calibrate the gate position and gate limit indication.

  6. Adjust for cable stretch if necessary.
