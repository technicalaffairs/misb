Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which should be carried out every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

The technical specification of the hydro unit: Francis Turbine max. power 180MW,  max head 75m, speed 100rpm, Wooward governor.

The first draft was initiated by eng.  Mohamed Abd Allah Ismaiel, tested by eng. Atef Sadeq Sayed, accepted by eng. Ahmed Saber Gabr: general director, approved by eng. Abdel-Karem Abuaid: head sector of High Dam power station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC inspection and quality control general director & MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc

  • Completely empty the tunnel and the draft tube.

  • Secure closing intake and draft tubes maintenance gates.

  • Open the wicket gates to100%.

  • Switch off F403 automatic.

  • Close the main oil valve no.7.

  • Release pressure of the oil pressure unit MHY to zero.

  • Use the lightening 36volt

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common and special wrenches

  • Spare parts, gaskets and rubber.

  • Benzene, kerosene, cleaning material (rags, cloth)

  • Packing and grease.

  • 3 portable lambs 36vwith able 30m.

  • Rubber and klingride gasket.

  • Hand tackle.

    •

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the turbine grease plant in operation to ensure that all the objects are supplied with grease

  2. Check up the feeders operation.

  3. If it is necessary to disassemble, revise and adjust the operation of the grease plant separate elements.
