Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every three years base as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

The technical Specification of the MHY hydro unit:

Francis Turbine, maximum power: 180 mw, maximum head 75 m, speed: 100 rpm, Woodward governor. 

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Abd Allah Esmaiel, tested by eng. Atef Sadek Sayed, accepted by eng. Ahmed Saber Gabr: general director at High Dam Power Station, approved by eng. Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the personal protective equipment must used as safety shoes, safety helmet ...etc

  • Completely empty the tunnel and the draft tube.

  • Secure closing intake and draft tubes maintenance gates

  • Open the wicket gates to 100%

  • Switch off F403 automatic.

  • Close the main oil valve no.7.

  • Switch off the power supply of MHY oil pumps.

  • Release pressure of the oil pressure unit (MHY) to zero.

  • Using overhead crane 400 ton.

  • Using lightening 36 V.

  • Using 8 bar service air.

  • Use suitable warning boards.

Work to be Carried Out

 

Oil pressure unit

  1. Measure time of pressure drop for 1 atm in MHY when the hydro-unit is stopped for the reserve

  2. Determine values of oil losses from MHY vessel which is performed by measured the pressure drop and oil level drop in MHY vessel.

  3. Determine the values of air losses from MHY vessel (for performing the oil and air losses from MHY vessel it is necessary completely closed all valves connected to MHY vessel during time test.

  4. Determine oil pump capacity.

  5. Check the function of relief valve of MHY oil pump.

  6. Check the function of unloader valve of MHY oil pump.

  7. Check noise and vibration of MHY oil pumps.

  8. Basing on results of measurements, the decision about the revision of MHY elements.

  9. Make the system pressureless and drain the oil from MHY vessel , oil return tank, servomotors and pipe lines of regulating system into the oil facilities.

  10. Check and clean the elements of pressure relay block and replace the filters.

  11. Check and clean oil measuring glass

  12. Check the oil valves and replace the seals if necessary.

  13. Dismantle the air valves and change the packing and testing pressure is to be performed.

  14. Unloading, safety, non-return valve of MHY oil pump is disassembled only if some defect had been discovered while testing the pump.

  15. Dismantle the coupling of MHY pump if the electric motor is replaced and after that check up the oil pump centering.

  16. Clean the filter of return tank by blowing out dust with compressed air (8 bar).

  17. Oil vessel and return tank (oil reservoir) shall also clean and free of scale, rust, oil, grease, and other foreign material.

  18. Change the gasket of vessel hatch and close the vessel and oil return tank hatches.

  19. Fill in the vessel and drain tank with pure oil taking into account the filling with oils the servomotors and pipelines of regulating system.

Servomotors

  1. Dismantle and take the covers and cylinders out of housing of servomotor housing.

  2. Inspect ,clean and grind the inside surface of cylinders

  3. Absence of corrosion, notches, dents and other defects on surface of servomotor rod, discovered roughness should be smoothed out.

  4. If necessary, replace the piston rings.

  5. Dismantle and replace gland packing, copper sealing of air vent plug and o ring sealing cylinder.

  6. Check and clean slide block, adjusting bush and spacer.

  7. Check and revise the mechanism of stopper and indicators of stoppers positions.

  8. Eliminate all detected oil leakages.

  9. Test all the connection, which were disassembled while revision and also check up the reliability of fastening of all pipelines to the supports.

  10. Check the needle valves of draining the pressure-draft pipe lines.

Oil Leakage System

  1. Drain oil and clean the tank and filter

  2. Check the float type arrangement together with E.T.L

  3. Check the valve no.249, 250, 251of leakage pump.

  4. If necessary, check up the motor-oil pump centering.
