Introduction

This document outline the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block .

This shaft function is to transfer the motion from hub to the runner of the generator of unit

The purpose of this maintenance is to check the position of the unit.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • The unit must be stopped and out of work

  • The indicator pin must be in its place
