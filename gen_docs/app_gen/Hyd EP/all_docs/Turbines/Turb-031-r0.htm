Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which should be carried out every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

The technical specification of the hydro unit: Francis Turbine max. power 180MW,  max head 75m, speed 100rpm, Wooward governor.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Abd Allah Esmail, tested by eng. Atef Sadek Said, accepted by general director eng. Ahmed Saaber, approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zaid: High Dam Hydraulic PS Head Sector, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC quality control general director & MPIS Coordinator

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Wear safety equipment as hard hat, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ...etc

  • Completely empty the tunnel

  • Water level in draft tube must not exceed 100 m.

  • Secure closing intake and draft tubes maintenance gates.

  • Open the wicket gates to 100%

  • Switch off F 403 automatic

  • Close the main oil valve no. 7

  • Release pressure of the oil pressure unit (MHY) to zero

  • Using overhead crane 400 ton

  • Using lightening 36 V

  • Using 8 bar service air

  • Use suitable warning boards

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common and special wrenches

  • Spare parts, gaskets and rubber

  • Benzene, kerosene and cleaning material (rag. cloth)

  • Rubber and klingride gasket

  • Grinding paper

  • Filler gauge

    •

Work to be Carried Out

Means of Pumping out water from the Turbine Cover

Ejector

  1. Disassemble the ejector, to inspect the nozzle, if it necessary replace it.

  2. Clean and cover the inside part of the ejector side and filter

  3. Revise the non - return valve on ejector input assembles the ejector and the slide to connect the delivery pipeline

Drainage Pump

  1. Inspect the pump impeller

  2. Change the bearing lubricant and gland pack

  3. Check up the pump electromotor centering

  4. Revise the non-return valve on the pump input with the anticorrosion compound

  5. Clean and cover the filter with the anticorrosion compound

  6. Check up the operation of the controlling scheme in the regime "automation" & "reserve"
