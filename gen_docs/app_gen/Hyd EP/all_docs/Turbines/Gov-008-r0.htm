Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block (for unit #10)

The technical specifications of governor: rated speed, RPM = 100 the control circuit close 100 P

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Switch off the automatic:  F403 DC (control of the governor), F506 AC (control of the governor).

  • Close valve no. 7 (inlet the oil to the governor 40 bar).

  • Locked two stoppers of servomotors.

  • Transfer switch of governor at off position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common, screw drivers and cleaning material (rag, cloth).

  • Portable air blower.

  • Digital multimeter,

  • Oscilloscope two channels or more.
