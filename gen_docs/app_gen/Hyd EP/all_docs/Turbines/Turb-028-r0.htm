Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which should be carried out every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

The technical specification of the hydro unit: Francis Turbine max. power 180MW,  max head 75m, speed 100 RPM, Woodward governor.

The first draft was initiated by eng.  Mohamed Abd Allah Ismaiel, tested by eng. Atef Sadeq Sayed, accepted by eng. Ahmed Saber Gabr: general director, approved by eng. Abdel-Karem Abu Zid: head sector of High Dam power station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC inspection and quality control general director & MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc

  • Completely empty the tunnel and the draft tube.

  • Secure closing intake and draft tubes maintenance gates.

  • Open the wicket gates to100%.

  • Switch off F403 automatic.

  • Close the main oil valve no.7.

  • Release pressure of the oil pressure unit MHY to zero.

  • Use the lightening 36 Volt

  • Use 8 bar service air.

Work to be Carried Out

Guide Vane Apparatus

  1. Check up the tightening of the lock nuts of the tightening rods, if it is necessary to carry out control measurings of the length of tightening rods & to compare them with the previous lengths

  2. Carry out inspection & spot check of fasten tightening of housing of the blade bearing levers keys 70 mm

  3. Check up all shears pins

  4. If there are leakages through the blades bearings-the replacement of the rubber salig cuffs must be done

  5. If it is necessary to carry out the regulation of the blades butt gaps

  6. Drain & pour in the pure oil into he support of the regulating ring

  7. Make power characteristics of the guide vane apparatus, while the underwater turbine part is dried

  8. Eliminate other detected remarks on the guide vane apparatus

Turbine cover

  1. Clean the turbine cover completely from oil and durt to clean out the holes and the over compartments

  2. Check up the tightening of the fastening of the turbine cover by spot - check to inspect the weldings

  3. Revise the air breaking valves

  4. Check up the good order of the valve plate packing rubber, tightening of the spring lock - nuts, the absence of the valve rod corrosion

  5. Cover the turbine cover (above water part) with anticorrosion compound

Air inlet valve under the runner

  1. Inspect the valve to check it in operation

  2. The disassembling of valve servomotors is carry out in case of detecting the defects and faults on it only

  3. Check up the seat insulation

  4. Revise the control valves. Assure that there is no leak
