Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every three years base as indicated by Y3  in the title block

The technical specification of the hydro unit: Francis turbine, maximum power: 180MW maximum head 75 m- speed: 100 RPM WoodWard governor.

This first draft initiated & tested by eng. Atef Sadeq Sayed, accepted by general director eng. Ahmed Saaber, approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zaid: High Dam Hydraulic PS Head Sector, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC quality control general director & MPIS Coordinator

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued ad the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear safety equipment as hard hat. shoes breathing mask safety glass ... etc

  • Completely empty the tunnel

  • Water level in draft tube not exceed level of 100 m

  • Secure closing intake and draft tubes maintenance gates

  • Open the wicket gates to 100 %

  • Switch off F 403 automatic

  • Close the main oil valve no 7

  • Release pressure of the oil pressure unit (MHY) to zero

  • Using overhead crane 400 ton

  • Using lightening 36 V

  • Using 8 bar service air

  • Use suitable warning boards

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Open the hatches into spiral case & draft tube to organize the erection of the illumination, entrance scaffold and required ladders

  2. Carry out the inspection of the state of tunnel spiral case & draft tube lining (by knock method)

  3. Carry out the inspection of the state of turbine stator & the blades of the guide vane apparatus

  4. Carry out penetrant test on runner blades

  5. Detecting corrosion the cavitation destruction welding break good order of sealing rubber

  6. Eliminate all the detected damages on runner guide vanes foundation ring by means of welding over the cavitation damages with the next grinding cutting defect welds

  7. Dismantle rubber - holding stripes and change the damaged vertical rubber sealing of guide vanes

  8. Check up and replace the damaged end seal rubber

  9. Cover with anticorrosion compound metal & on case hardening

  10. Fill in the following formularies:
    -  No 1 - of the butt gaps of guide vane blades
    -  No 2 - of the gapes on the upper located labyrinth of the runner the gaps between the
                   runner foundation ring
    -  No 3 - of the dependence of opening of guide vanes apparatus upon the troke of servomotor rod.
