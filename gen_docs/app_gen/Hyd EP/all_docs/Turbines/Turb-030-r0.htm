Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which should be carried out every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

The technical specification of the hydro unit: Francis Turbine max. power 180MW,  max head 75m, speed 100 RPM, Woodward governor.

The first draft was initiated by eng.  Mohamed Abd Allah Ismaiel, tested by eng. Atef Sadeq Sayed, accepted by eng. Ahmed Saber Gabr: general director, approved by eng. Abdel-Karem Abu Zid: head sector of High Dam power station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC inspection and quality control general director & MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Wear safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask safety glass ...etc

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Completely empty the tunnel

  • Water level at draft tube not exceed 100m.

  • Secure closing intake and draft tubes maintenance gates.

  • Open the wicket gates to100%.

  • Switch off F403 automatic.

  • Close the main oil valve no.7.

  • Release pressure of the oil pressure unit MHY to zero.

  • Using overhead crane 400 ton

  • Using lightening 36 V

  • Using 8 bar service air

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common and special wrenches

  • Spare parts, gaskets and rubber.

  • Benzene, kerosene, cleaning material (rags, cloth)

  • Rubber and klingride gasket.

  • Grinding paper.

  • Filler gauge.

Work to be Carried Out

Turbine bearing

  1. Dismantle the waved iron round the bearing, to inspect the bath & housing of the turbine bearing and also all the pipe line connections, oil cooler to detect the leakages

  2. Drain oil first from 1 oil - cooling compartment of the turbine bearing, then from the 2-d one; to dismantle the upper bath cover and thermo controlling scheme; to make the formular no 4 of gaps on bearing & on the shaft position corresponding to the adjustment marks

  3.  Left all the segment on the butt of the bearing neck, to carry out the examination of state of babbit surface of the segment & working surface of the neck

  4. If it is necessary to carry out the scraping of the babbit surface

  5. Check up the tightening of the fastening of the upper bath, the connections of the upper located circulating pipes, the lock - nuts of the screw - down bolts

  6. If the tightening of the lock - nuts with the thread 3" is slacked, to check up the fixation of the thread bushes in the bearing housing & take the measures for their reliable fixing in the housing

  7. Check up the safety of all the pins

  8. Dismantle the lower circulating pipes, to lower the lower located bath on 4 stubs L=440 mm

  9. Clean the bath from the oil sediment & durt, to lift & assemble the bath & to connect the pipes

  10. Dismantle 2 oil - coolers of the bearing

  11. Clean the oil - coolers from the oil - sediment & durt the oil - cooler compartments

  12. Inspect the packing rubber on oil - coolers

  13. Wipe and trial the oil - coolers with pressure 8 atm during 10 min, before their placing, to erect the oil - coolers, to connect the pipelines, to trial all connections with pressure of water from the fire main (5 atm)

  14. Install the segments, to adjust the gaps & to make the formular no. 7 of gaps together with E.T.L. to check up the operation of the thermo control of the bearing

  15. Eliminate all the rest detected remarks and faults of the bearing

  16. Fill bearing with pure oil to secure the normal oil level (190 mm from the support ring of the segments)

  17. Check up together with E.T.L. the operation of oil level relay and presence of the signal

  18. Install the protective grating and waved iron round turbine bearing
