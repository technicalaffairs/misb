Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which should be carried out every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

The technical specification of the hydro unit: Francis Turbine max. power 180MW,  max head 75m, speed 100 RPM, Woodward governor.

The first draft was initiated by eng.  Mohamed Abd Allah Ismaiel, tested by eng. Atef Sadeq Sayed, accepted by eng. Ahmed Saber Gabr: general director, approved by eng. Abdel-Karem Abuaid: head sector of High Dam power station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC inspection and quality control general director & MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc

  • Completely empty the tunnel.

  • Water level at draft tube not exceed 100m.

  • Secure closing intake and draft tubes maintenance gates.

  • Open the wicket gates to100%.

  • Switch off F403 automatic.

  • Close the main oil valve no.7.

  • Release pressure of the oil pressure unit MHY to zero.

  • Use overhead crane 400 ton.

  • Use the lightening 36 Volt

  • Use 8 bar service air.

Work to be Carried Out

Turbine shaft packing

Disassemble the pack casing, bush and rubber ring

  1. Inspect the state of the rubber ring and working surface of stainless made bush, to make the formular no. 5 of the gaps between the shaft and steel ring, if it is necessary, to replace the rubber ring

  2. Flush the pipelines of water supply to shaft working pack. The water must be supplied through the big ring - shaped collector and through the reserve line to shaft working pack from the fire main. The pressure on the collector must be 5 atm. To clean the drainage pipe

  3. Cover the shaft zone of working pack and steel ring with the anticorrosion compound

Repair pack

  1. Inspect the state of the cylinders, the elements of the hand and hydraulic drive of the pack

  2. Test the lowering and lifting of the repair pack manually and hydraulically

  3. Eliminate the detected faults
