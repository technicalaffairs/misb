Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Wear safety equipment as safety helmet safety shoes breathing mask safety glass ... etc.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Completely empty the tunnel

  • Water level at draft tube not exceed 100 m

  • Secure closing intake and draft tubes maintenance gates

  • Switch off F403 automatic

  • Close the main oil valve no 7

  • Use lightening 36 V

  • Use 8 bar service air

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common and special wrenches

  • Spare parts gaskets and rubber

  • Benzene kerosene cleaning material (rags cloth)

  • Rubber and klingride gasket

  • Grinding paper

  • Filler gauge

Work to be carried out 

  1. Drain the governor oil to remove from the system undesirable impurities or compounds which have entered the hydraulic circuits

  2. Oil in the actuator sump should be drained and the sump is inspected for oil contamination of foreign material

  3. Dismantle strain body a dirty filter should be replaced

  4. Remove any of grease and dirt from all linkage pivot points and rods

  5. Lubricate the pivot points and bearings with oil

  6. Inspect all unions and pipe connection to be certain they are tightened

  7. Inspect and clean flow regulators and pressure regulators

Pilot control assembly

  1. Dismantle the pilot control assembly

  2. Unscrew the fixed bolted and clean all internal parts with benzene

  3. If necessary replace the o-rings oil sealing springs and gasket

  4. All internal surfaces and edges shall be smooth uniform clean and free from scale rust and cracks

  5. Check the smooth rotation of oil motor which is used to rotate the transducer pilot valve bushing

  6. Reassemble and joint parts of pilot control assembly

  7. Air should be removed from hydraulic system. For this purpose before filling the system with oil it is necessary to open air vent and it is to be closed when oil appears from it

  8. After reassembly it is important to recenter the transducer before being placed in operation tighten the lower centering screw 07075-75 with 7/76 Allen wrench. Then back the screw off 1-1/2 turns next the centering is done by using 1/8 inch Allen wrench to center the transducer pilot valve plunger

Main distributing valve

  1. Absence of corrosion notches dents and other defects on surfaces of distribution valve bushing plunger pilot valve bushing and pilot valve plunger

  2. Inspect the pilot valve bushing and plunger in distributing valve and should be checked to ensure there is no friction through full stroke

  3. The speed of oil motor vibrator should be set between 500 and 600 rpm by adjusting the flow regulator, the oil motor eccentric is then adjusted to produce an oscillation of the distributing valve plunger of approximately 1.5 mm

  4. Check cable stretch for gate restoring cable this indicated by a change in gate position indication in opening direction when the turbine servomotors are in the fully closed position

  5. The restoring cable is provided with a convenient cable adjustment connection inside the governor cabinet

  6. Check the gate position and gate limit indication for accuracy at the governor indicator adjust for cable stretch if necessary

Governor test procedures and adjustments

Gate limit calibration

Follow this procedure in calibrating the gate limit for proper alignment with the gate position deviation of 1% at any given point is considered satisfactory

  1. Make sure that turbine is unwatered and the gate limit is below zero

  2. Place the governor transfer switch in the "manual" position

  3. Block shutdown solenoid out of the way

  4. Set the pilot servomotor at 1/2 stroke using a 0.875" spacer block. To de so raise the gate limit to 100% and place the spacer under the connecting pin (07075-35) at the top of the pilot servomotor Then lower the gate limit control The spacer will stop the pilot servomotor at 1/2 stroke

  5. Adjust the length of the pilot valve connecting link so that the black gate indication hand on the front of the governor is at 50% tighten the connecting link lock nuts

  6. Turn the gate limit control knob clockwise until the gate position (black hand) raises above 50% remove the spacer block

  7. Turn the gate limit knob until the gate limit indicator (red hand) is at 50%

  8. Adjust the length of the vertical gate limit connecting rod (07075-28) until the gate position indication (black hand) is also at 50% tighten the connecting rod lock nuts

  9. Turn the gate limit control so that the red hand is at 10 % record the position of the black hand and note its deviation from 10%

  10. Next turn the gate limit control so that the red hand is at 90% record the position of the black hand and note its deviation from 90%

  11. The deviations recoded in step 9 and 10 will usually be high at one position and low at other If the deviations are greater than = 1% then the gate limit lever eccentric must be adjusted
    The gate limit lever eccentric is adjusted by loosening the hex head screw (07075-234) and nut (07575-229) and then the moving the hex head screw in the slot with the aid of the set screw (07075-23)

  12. The gate position travel on either side of 50% should now be almost equal also with the gate limit (red hand) at 50% the gate position (black hand) should be at 50
    If the recorded gate position travel on either side of 50% should now be almost equal with the gate limit the governor gate limit control is properly calibrated
    The gate limit is correctly calibrated when the gate position coincides with the gate limit within 1% throughout the full gate stroke
