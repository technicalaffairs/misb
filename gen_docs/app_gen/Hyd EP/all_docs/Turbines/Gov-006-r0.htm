Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed  on an annually bases as indicated  by Y1 in the title block.

The present electric hydraulic governor system represents the latest development in a continuing program – designed to provide the best equipment available for hydraulic turbine speed control --- the hydraulic turbine governor is no longer just a speed response control of the turbine control of the turbine .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained qualified or directly supervised by the some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet …. etc.

  • Unit out of service.

  • Switch off the DC power supply before inserting or withdrawing the relay

Tools and Equipment

  • Signal generator variable from 0 – 5000 Hz , and minimum 6 to 8 volt ( r.m.s )

  • Electric frequency counter to accurately set the signal generator output to the desired frequency

  • Multi -  range DC voltmeter

  • A good oscilloscope and digital voltmeter are very handly to have, although not always necessary

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Turn off governor power supply.

  2. Pull all modules out of racks.

  3. Turn on the governor power supply transfer switch to the test position.

  4. Check the main supply ( AC – DC ).

Procedures

  1. Turn the potentiometer (P1, P2, P3, P4) which are located in the power supply monitor board fully counter clockwise

  2. Adjust the +15 Vdc supply to +14.5 Vdc . Adjust P1 clockwise until +15 V  supply indicated light goes out

  3. Adjust the +12 Vdc supply to +11.5 Vdc . Adjust P2 clockwise until +12 V  supply indicated light goes out

  4. Adjust the +5 Vdc supply to + 4.75 Vdc . Adjust P3 clockwise until +5 V  supply indicated light goes out

  5. Adjust the -15 Vdc supply to -14.5 Vdc . Adjust P4 clockwise until -15 V  supply indicated light goes out .

Calibration of Crowbar Circuit

  1. Adjust the regulator to the desired crowbar set voltage , slowly turn the crowbar .adjust counter clockwise until the voltage drop suddenly , shut off the  power , and turn back the voltage 

  2. Adjust some what so the crowbar will not clamp the voltage when power supply is reapplied , the volatge regulator then may be calibrated for the specified voltage output.
