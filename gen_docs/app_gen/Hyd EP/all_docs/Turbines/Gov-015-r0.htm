Introduction

This document outlines the preliminary adjustments for rotation telemetry modules that have not been in service or the equipment inspection I1 to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The rotation telemetry module is used to provide speed indication of the turbine. It has three individually adjustable output so that three separate tachometers or speed indication can be used

The rotation telemetry module likewise produces a pulse on the tachometer at low speed. This is used to indicate that the unit is running slowly or creeping. This out put can also be used to operate a "pulsing relay"

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet .... etc

  • Turn the transfer switch to "off" position as request

  • Run the unit on line and loading at several setting as request

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common and screw drivers

  • Digital multimeter

  • Extender card

Work to be carried out 

  1. Turn the transfer switch to "off" position and put the rotation telemetry module on an extender card, turn the transfer switch to "Gov" position and reset lock-up

  2. Run the unit at normal speed 100 RPM (spinning mode)

  3. Adjust P-4 (calibration) on the rotation telemetry module (figure 1) to obtain 6.00 volts (dc) between PIN #3 (output) and PIN # 8 (CT) (figure 2)

  4. Adjust P-1 (TACH no 1) on the rotation telemetry module (figure 1) to obtain 100 RPM (50 HZ) reading on scale of the tachometer which is mounted on the actuator panel

  5. Adjust P-2 (TACH no 2) on the rotation telemetry module (figure 1) to obtain 100 RPM (50HZ) reading on scale of the tachometer which is mounted on the cubicle panel

  6. Shut the unit down turn the transfer switch "off" remove the rotation telemetry modules from the extender card and plug into the rack turn the transfer switch to "Gov" and reset the lock-up
