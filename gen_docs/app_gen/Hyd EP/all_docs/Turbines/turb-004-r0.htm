Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance Procedures P1, which must be performed every five years or when the leakage water through the shaft seal is excessive and the interval time of stop start of the turbine leakage water pump become short as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

The first draft was initiated by eng. Ashraf Saber, tested by eng. Alsayed Ibrahim El ashmony, accepted by general director eng. Abd elghaffar Osman, approved  by eng. Mohamed Abd elbaset  head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety Helmet.

    •

  • Intake and draft tube gate in closed position

  • Complete water passage is dewatered

  • Guide vane mechanism open totally

  • Hydaulic control system depressurised

Tools and Equipment

  • Mechanical tool kit

  • Adjustable wrenchs with different sizes

  • Torque wrench

  • Feeler guage (for clearance between carbon seal and the protective sleeve)

Work to be Carried Out

 

Dismantling  (DWG no: 8621720)

  1. Open spiral casing main hole

  2. Install scaffolding above turbine runner

  3. Open access (4 covers) in inner turbine cover

  4. Dismantle the water supply pipe, part no. 32

  5. Remove the screws, part no. 14 from seal casing ring 4 (top ring)

  6. Take out the two-parted casing ring 4

  7. Remove the draw spring, part no. 19

  8. Remove the segments of the carbon sealing ring, part no. 16 including pressure spring, part no. 18, and position pin, part no 19

  9. Repeat steps 5 to 8 for dismantling of sealing ring 3

  10. Remove the water supply ring 2

  11. Repeat steps 5 to 9 for dismantling the sealing ring 1 (bottom ring)

Re-erection

Proceed in the opposite direction as indicated for dismantling

Attention

Make sure, that the upper carbon sealing segments are installed in ring 3+4, and the lower carbon sealing segments are installed in ring 1

Note:

Make sure the bad carbon segments must be repair with a new segments
