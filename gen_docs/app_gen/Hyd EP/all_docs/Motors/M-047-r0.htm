Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as helmet, shoes ... etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

  • Tool set.

  • Cleaning materials (cloth, Benzene), grease for bearing.

Work to be Carried out  

  1. Disconnect the leads of the motor and label them.

  2. Clean the motor from dust, dirt, clean it by benzene. 

  3. After dismantling the motor, clean the inner parts from all foreign matters.

  4. Blow the motor with compressed air of 2-3 atm pressure.

  5. Inspect the guide bearing and replace them if necessary.

  6. Measure the resistance of the insulation of motor winding.

  7. Check the DC resistance and compare it with the reference one.

  8. After assembling the motor,  put it into work and checks its rotation.
