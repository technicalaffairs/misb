Introduction 

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

This disconnect switch is outdoor in the switchyard. It's air disconnect switch. The rated voltage 145KV. The rated current 3150 &1250 A and the short circuit current 31.5 KA. 

The first draft initiated & tested by  eng. Mamdouh Aly Hassan, accepted by general Director eng. Nagy Nageeb Saleh , and approved by eng . Mohamed Abdel Basest head Sector of Aswan Dam 2 Hydraulic Power Station and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety belt when cleaning suspension insulator.

  • The disconnect switch must be opened, isolated, and grounded from two sides is required.

  • Change position of interlock to maintenance position.
  • Portable earth must be used.
Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt ………etc.

  • Dry and fine cloth, set of common keys, screw drivers, and blower

  • Grease and graphite oil,

  • Portable earth for discharging.

  • Voltage indicator and ladder.

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Remove dust from disconnectswitch parts and mechanism cabinet.

  2. Clean all porcelain surfaces.

  3. Check insulators with regard to damage.

  4. Grease all rotating points actuating the auxiliary switch. Measure the opening – closing time

  5. Grease the chains lightly with first class oil.

  6. Grease the main bearings.

  7. Grease the bearings of vertical drive rods and coupling rods.

  8. Clean the main contacts with transformer oil and rub the contact surface with dry cloth
