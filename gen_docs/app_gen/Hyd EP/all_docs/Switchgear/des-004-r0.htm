Introduction 

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated & tested by eng. Mamdouh Aly Hassan, accepted by general director eng. Nagy Nageeb Saleh, and approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel Bast head Sector of Aswan Dam 2 Hydraulic Power Station and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the Personal safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet ,…. Etc.

  • The distribution boards must be isolated voltage from two sides ( IN – OUT ).

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt ………etc

  • Megger.

  • Hand tools.

  • Voltage indicator.

  • Digital voltmeter

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

Work to be carried out

  1. By using compressed dry air, remove dust and pollution from bus bars, CB and cabinet.

  2. Check all power cables, bus bars bolts fitting.

  3. Check all insulation parts and change it when necessary.

  4. Check the earthing for boards and good tighten.

  5. Change any wires not good.

  6. Meggring between distribution board parts and ground.
