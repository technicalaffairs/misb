Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

The technical data

SF6 circuit breaker of auxiliary switchboard is duplicated tripping coil.

12kv, 3 x 630A, Standard IEC 56

The first draft is initiated and tested by eng. Amr Mustafa, accepted by eng. Nagy Nageeb Saleh: general manager, approved by eng. Khalaf Allah Mahmoud Abd El-Gaber: head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman: HPGC quality control general director & MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the personal protective equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet ...etc

  • The circuit breaker must be racked out.

Tools and Equipment

  • Hand tools.

  • Micro ohmmeter.

  • Time meter set.

  • Scotch bright.

  • Lubricating oil.

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean and lubricate the operating mechanism & link mechanism.

  2. Clean and lubricate the two latches of the latching mechanism with petrol.    

  3. Lubricate the joints and the bearings of the latching mechanism with fluid oil.

  4. Lubricate the springs guide with low temperature grease.

  5. Lubricate motor gears & clean commentator.

  6. Lubricate all the mechanism joints of the pole – units lower part.

  7. Check the gas pressure in each pole – unit: normal pressure: 1,5 bar at 20°C

  8. Check the tightness of electrical connections.

  9. Measure the opening & closing time ( ms ).

  10. Measure the main contacts resistance by micro ohmmeter.
