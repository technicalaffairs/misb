Introduction

This document lists the minor maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed from eight to twelve 8-12 years in service to determine the max. wear of the main contacts of the breaker poles, therfore, we can decide if the equipment has to be reconditioned or not as indicated by Y8:12 in the title block.

The first draft was initiated and tested by eng. Naser El Sayed Abd EL-Aziz,, accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman Selim, approved by eng. Mohamed Abd elbaset  head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director and MPIS Coordinator..

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ...etc

  • De-energize and ground the circuit breaker

  • Interrupt the control circuit of the motor

  • Discharge the closing and opening springs by operating the manual levers in the operating mechanism until no more operations (on-off) are possible

  • Interrupt the control circuit of the close and trip coils 

  • The circuit breaker should be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Contact resistance measuring device

  • Depth gauge, Ampero-meter

  • Slow motion device

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Now the circuit breaker is "open" the springs are discharged and the control circuits of the motor and coils are interrupted as mentioned above

  2. Close the circuit breaker by the slow motion device(fig2)

  3. Connect the tester to high voltage terminals (fig 1)

  4. Move the circuit breaker towards "off" by the slow motion  until the instance of stopping the "beep" of the tester and measure the distance "Xa" the distance Xis measured from outside edge of chassis to left end of linkage "viewed from motor wound spring operating mechanism (fig 1)

  5. Repeat the same procedures of poles b,c to measure Xb, Xc

    Definitions:  Xa,Xb,Xc = the distances in service status,        
                       Xa0,Xb0,Xc0 = the distances in new status

  6. Calculate the value X-X0 for the three poles
    This value (X-X0) must equal or less than 6 mm and if not the breaker must be reconditioned

State of Arcing Contacts

The state of the contact material respectively the wear caused by arcing can be checked from the outside. Thus unnecessary opening of the circuit breaker respectively of the gas compartments can be avoided before the date of recondtioning

To determine the maximum admissible wear proceed as described below

Figure 1

Dimension X at the moment of electrical contact separation has been determined for poles a,b and c of each circuit breaker on completion of erection and recorded on the label in motor wound spring operating mechanism 70.00 (see chapter "Final inspections")

During service period xa , xb and xc can be checked from time to time. If dimension x at one or more poles differa from its new-condition value by 6 mm or more the equipment will have to be reconditioned
