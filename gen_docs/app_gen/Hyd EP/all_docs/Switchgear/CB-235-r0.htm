Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 for generator air circuit breakers, which must be performed every every 3 years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

The technical data of the circuit breaker:

Air circuit breaker rated voltage 17.5 kv rated current 5000 A and rated braking capacity 40 kA

This breakers associated type GM electrical stored energy operating mecanism supplied at 110 Vdc, shunt closing release 110 Vdc and shunt voltage operating release .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the personal protective equipment must used as safety shoes, safety helmet ...etc

    •

  • The generator must be isolated

  • The generator circuit breaker racked out

  • Switch on the earth generator (G - ES)

Tools and Equipment

  • Hand tools

  • Micro ohmmeter

  • Time meter set

  • filter (o mm - 10 mm)

  • Scotch bright

  • Lubraction oil & vasline

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Remove dust from pole units and clean insulators with dry cloth

  2. Clean and lubricate the operating mechanism and the linkages lubricate joints

  3. Clean arcing contacts and main contacts and lubricate with vasline

  4. Clean the tip witch enganges the ringers of visible - brake blades

  5. Inspect the arc-chutes

  6. Measure with ashim the gap between the moving arcing contact and the center portion of the fixed arcing contact the gap should be (6 mm ± 1 mm) if the gap has decreased to less than 2.5 mm replace thearing contacts

  7. Check the opening, closing times. opening time = 53 m sec closing time = 168 m sec

Checking the gap:

after clean the main contact and arc contacts close the circuit braker and  check the gap        

2.5mm  < gap   <  6 mm as a reference result

Test the time (closing - opining) and contact resistance

  1. Measure the resistance of main contacts

  2. Measure the opening and closing time
