Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

Technical specifications of the compressor Stationary, three stage, single acting one crank, air cooled machine, with w-shaped cylinder arrangement. 180m3/h,40hg/cm2

The first draft is initiated and reviewed by eng. Nagy Bekheit Saleh, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad general director, and approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station,  Hyd.EPCo.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc

  • Switch off AC current

  • Close the outlet valve 

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Cleaning materials (cloth - kerosene)

  • Tool set

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Drain the oil from crankcase

  2. Check the connecting rod bolts for tightening

  3. Check the condition of the compressor valves

  4. Check the clean the cooler unit

  5. Clean the oil filter and breath

  6. Check the condition of the crank shaft sealing

  7. Check the condition of the connecting rod bearing and piston pin

  8. Check the cylinder and pistons for wear

  9. Check the clearance in piston ring and piston grooves, if required replace the rings by new ones

  10. Check the cylinders, cylinder covers, crosshead for cracks

  11. Inspect, clean the compressor valves, seats, and plates

  12. Check, clean the oil pump

  13. Clean and inspect the crank case

  14. Check the fan drive mechanism

  15. Check the foundation for proper tightening and their boots and nuts
