Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The first draft is initiated and reviewed by eng. El-Sayed Sobhy Saad, tested by eng. Osama Ahmed Hasanain, accepted by eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman Salem: general director, and approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel-Bast; Head Sector of Aswan Dam 1 & 2 Power Station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC quality control general director & MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes .... etc

  • Switch off the AC current

  • Close the outlet valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Digital ammeter

  • Complete tool set includes screw drivers

  • Cleaning materials

  • Open end spanner set

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Review technical reference material and manufacturer’s manual.

  2. Obtain the compressor field maintenance file.

  3. Clean and inspect the control panel.

    4.

  4. Retighten the terminals in the control panel.

  5. Reduce the pressure in a air vessel and record the operation set point for each compressor at MRDS

  6. Check the priority function.

  7. Place a copy of test results in the field maintenance file.
