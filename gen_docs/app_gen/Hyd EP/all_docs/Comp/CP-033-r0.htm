Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list I1, which must be performed on a weakly bases as indicated by W in the title block

The technical specifications of the air compressor 40AT, Stationary - three stage, single acting one crank, air - cooled w-shaped cylinder arrangement. 180 m3/h, 40hg/cm2

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required

  • Wear safety equipment as helmet, safety shoes .... etc

  • Switch off the AC current

  • Close the outlet valve 

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet.

  • Cleaning materials (cloth - kerosene)

  • Complete tool set

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check oil in crank case

  2. Check the compressor covers, flanges and other for proper fastening

  3. Check the safety valves and blow - off valves

  4. Check the fan belts for correct tension

  5. Check operation of automatic control circuit
