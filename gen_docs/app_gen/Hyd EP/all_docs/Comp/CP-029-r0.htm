Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block

The technical specifications of the rotary screw air compressor model 13.9 H.H-S.S.R 2000, 1480rpm, single stage, 8 bar, 3 phase, 380 volt, 117.9 kw, 50 Hz

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses,... etc

  • Switch off AC power supply

  • Close air outlet valve

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of wrenches and screwdrivers

  • Wiping material and cleaning liquid

  • Coolant - lubricant oil

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the oil level of the separator tank

  2. Check pressure gauges

  3. Check for correct operation

  4. Clean and wipe
