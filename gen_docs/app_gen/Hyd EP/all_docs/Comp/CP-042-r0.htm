Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed  every one year or 2000 hours operation as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This compressor serves in compressing air for cleaning mechanical parts and different parts during maintenance

The first draft was initiated by eng. Ashraf Saber, tested by eng. Alsayed Ibrahim El ashmony, accepted by general director eng. Abd elghaffar Osman, approved  by eng. Mohamed Abd elbaset  head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director.

Technical specifications

Type of compressor : sigma-la-as/35 Kaser - Germany

Free air delivery from package : 3390 L/min        Working pressure: 8 bar

Power: 22 k.w                                    Speed: 3000 r.p.m

Used oil: compressor oil S 46

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety healthy, shoes ...etc.

  • Check the pressure before and after the oil separator (14) at points (28) while the compressor in operation and mark the differential pressure (must not exceed 0.8 bar)

  • Shut off the compressor (start stop switch and main disconnect switch)

  • Shut off the down stream compressor valve

  • Use a vent socket at point (28) to losses the pressure in the oil separator tank (10)

  • Discharge compressed air line if necessary

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Compressed air hose for cleaning

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags, ...etc)

  • Vent socket, pressure gauge with socket

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Replace air inlet filter after one year or 1500 hours as required

  2. Change oil filter (13) after one year or 2000 hours operation. this done by vent the air pressure (point 28) in the oil separator (10) and dismantling the oil filter and replaced

  3. Discharge the oil by special procedure: the oil drain from the oil separator, air cooler and oil cooler

  4. Check air cooler (18) every one year and if there are excessive temperature in oil circulation, the air cooler must be dismounted and cleaned by solvent liquide, dry and mounting again

  5. Pure a new oil to the oil separator until required level (about 15 L.used compressor oil S 46)

  6. Put the compressor ready for operation
