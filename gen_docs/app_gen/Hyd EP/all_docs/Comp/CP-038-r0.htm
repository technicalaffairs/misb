Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 which must performed on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block.

This compressor serves in compressing air for the governors and breaks of the main turbine units 3-9

The first draft was initiated by eng. Ashraf Saber, tested by eng. Alsayed Ibrahim El ashmony, accepted by general director eng. Abd elghaffar Osman, approved  by eng. Mohamed Abd elbaset  head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) power stations and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director and MPIS Coordinator..

Technical specifications

Type of compressor : E22.5 - 15/Se/Sz - 3 stages - BAUER - Germany

Air flowrate (Q) : 850 L/min        Max. pressure (P) : 40 bar

Revolution (N): 1150 I/min

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc

  • Disconnect the power supply of the compressor

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Compressed air hose for cleaning

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags, ...etc)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check function of automatic drain valve system and manual valves for OQF 12 AT. 101, 102 and 103. Drain the separator water

  2. Check compressor oil sump level and Top-up if required

  3. Clean the compressor parts by air jet and using rags for the outer surfaces
