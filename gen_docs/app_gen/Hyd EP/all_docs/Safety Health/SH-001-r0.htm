Introduction

This document out lines the equipments inspection and withstand test kv and leakage current which must be performed periodically and before using & review the values at last test regard to the table below.

The first draft initiated &  tested by eng. Hesain Mahrous Mohamed, accepted by eng.  Dia Eldeen Elgabalawy general director, approved by eng. Abd El Kerim Abu Zeid Ismail Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem: HPGC technical inspection general director, and MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear the shoes and gloves.

Tools and Equipment

  • HV transformer

  • Ammeter to measure the DC leakage current mA

  • Kilo-voltmeter kv dc

  • Input supply 0  ... 220v

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Put the voltage to the equipment according the table no. 1

  2. Measure the leakage current

  3. Record the withstand voltage with time
