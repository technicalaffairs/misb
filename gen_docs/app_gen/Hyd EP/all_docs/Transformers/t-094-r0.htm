Introduction 

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The transformer cooling type is OFAF

The first draft initiated & tested by eng. Mamdouh Aly Hassan, accepted by general Director eng. Nagy Nageeb Saleh, and approved by eng . Mohamed Abdel Basset head Sector of Aswan Dam 2 Hydraulic Power Station and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb: HPGC MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the personal safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet ,…. etc.

  • Transformer is isolated and earthed from three sides is required .

  • Portable earth connected to high voltage side  .

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt ………etc

  • Complete tool set ( meager ,dielectric test ,T.R. test and DC .R test )

  • Portable earth for discharging

  • Voltage indicator and ladder

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

  • Grease treatment plant

Work to be carried out

  1. Tack oil sample for oil analysis laboratory.

  2. Clean all porcelain surfaces on all bushing and check the oil level in transformer bushing and tank.

  3. Check the transformer for oil leakage.

  4. Check the bushing for oil leakage and porcelain damage.

  5. Check the color for silica gel and change it when necessary.

  6. Perform the transformer oil test and if the dissolving gases is higher or laboratory results is low ,we must use  m/c filtering to filter and make vacuum to the transformer oil .            

  7. Check the oil level in the conservator.

  8. Check the oil level in the top housing of the H.V bushing.

  9. Inspect the paint work.

  10. Check function of buchholz relay.

  11. Check the seating flange bolts for tightness

  12. Check the four oil coolers

  13. Check the transformer earthing circuit

  14. Check the transformer fire circuit

  15. Place the transformer into normal operation condition with annual schedule coolers.

Tests and Results

Dissipation Factor Test

by using HV tester, measure and record capacitance, dissipation factor % and compare these values with historical data.

Turns Ratio Test

Perform ratio test in high voltage coil to LV1&LV2and compare reading to historical data

Winding Resistance (D.C.R.)

  1. Take and record winding resistance (D.C.R.) each high voltage coil phase to neutral

  2. Each low voltage coil phase to phase and compare reading to historical data

Winding Insulation Resistance (megger)

Measure insulation resistance by using megger 2500V at 15&60 sec. (R15&R60)
