Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

The oil treatment plant is 3 phase 380V, 50 HZ, 170 KW      

The VH equipment is suitable for conditioning, inhibiting transformer oil, and drying transformer insulation or oil / paper insulation in general, this type of plant can be used to regenerate both oil and insulation, this increasing the lifetime of the transformer, the conditioning process eliminate the following components from the oil:

- Solid dirt

- Free and dissolved water 

- Dissolved air

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles, and rubber glove. ...etc

  • Put the equipment ( mica file ) out of service.

  • Power supply is switched off.

  • All valves are switched off.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete kit of tools, dry rags

  • Measuring instrument ( Ampere - meter )

  • Compressed air

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the exterior surfaces of the equipment plant

  2. Check all fixing screws are tightened

  3. Check all fuses.

  4. Check oil level for vacuum pumps and change if necessary   .

  5. Clean coarse filter and change if necessary.

  6. Check function test of all valves: (gear pump valves, safety valve, non return valve, throttle valve) 

  7. Check function test of safety thermostat.
