Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the tilte block.

The technical specification of the main transformer, rated power = 2000 KVA, H.V = 11 KV, current = 105 A, LV = 3.15 KV, current = 366.5A

The first draft initiated & tested by eng. Abd El Mohsen Mohammed general manager of  Nag Hammadi Hyd. PS,  and certified by by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem HPGC  inspection and quality control general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, breathing mask, and safety glasses,

  • Wear safety belt when cleaning bushing or insulator

    •

  • Transformer isolation and proper earthing is required

  • It is recommended to use only fiber glass PVC ladders when working in the switchyard.

  • There is a danger of falling into the oil containment pit when working near large power transformers

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, PVC ladders, tool set and safety shoes

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean all porcelain surfaces of all transformer & wall bushings / insulators

  2. Clean neutral CT, earthing switches, check that all bolted connections are tight

  3. Clean contacts of all neutral disconnect switches, lubricate all bearing points

  4. Inspect the transformer control cabinet check the condition of the door seal, blow out the inside of the control cabinet with compressed air using moderate pressure

  5. Check oil level, oil temperature, winding temperature indicator, cooling fans & silica gel breather

  6. Check breakdown voltage of insulating oil, and filter the oil if need

  7. Return transformer bank to normal service
