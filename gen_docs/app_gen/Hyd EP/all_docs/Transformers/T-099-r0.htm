Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

The technical specification of the auxiliary transformer:
11/3.3 KV, 3000 KVA, ON load tap changer, ONAF, delta /Y11

The first draft initiated by eng. Hessain Hashem, tested by eng. Hasseb Hessain Bahboh, accepted by eng. Mohamed Osama Soliman, certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem; HGPSC inspection general director, and approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel Baset: head sector of Aswan Dam 1,2 PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses is required.

  • The transformer must be out of service

  • The transformer isolated and earthed from two sides

  • Isolate auxiliary CB

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tools kit

  • Dry rags

  • Compressed air supply

    •

Work to be carried out  

  1. Clean exterior surfaces of transformer

  2. Clean all bushing (HV, LV)

  3. Tighten all bolts

  4. Inspect any leakage oil from transformer parts and repair

  5. Check oil level in tank

  6. Check silica gel breather and change it if necessary

  7. Inspect and clean the tap changer

  8. Test electric circuit for tap changer

  9. Inspect and clean the cooling fans motor

  10. Test automatic operation for cooling fans

  11. Clean and check buchhloz relay
    (direction of flow, contracts floatation, cable connection and gas content)

  12. Every year: take oil sample and test the (dielectric strength, sludge, moisture content )

  13. Check all protection signals (buchholz, oil level, and temperature )
