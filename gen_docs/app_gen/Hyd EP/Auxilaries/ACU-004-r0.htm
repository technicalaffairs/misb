Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed  annually  bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

In ventilation and air condition system chilled water system carries out the function to release in to the cooling water heat absorbed by air conditioning units in to air conditioned rooms and building heat removal function is performed all the year around

Chilled water system is divided in to two section :- section one produces and distributes chilled water to air conditioning units as flows :

  • Ventilated areas

  • Control room air conditioner

  • Relay room air conditioner

  • Offices room air conditioner

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet .... etc

  • Main supply disconnected switch in open position

  • Maintenance is carried out with cooperation with electrical department

Tools and Equipment

  • Mechanical tools ( complete set of manual wrench - brushes - etc)

  • Blanco 1 ton

  • Tighten hexagonal screw with atorqe wrench

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check that the characteristics of the pump (flow and head) suit the capacity of the units

  2. Check provide piping to allow condensers cleaning

  3. Check the level of the oil into the compressors through the relevant sight glass (at least on the half of the sight glass)

  4. Check eventual refrigerant gas leaking by means of suitable equipment and the refrigerant flow must be continuous liquid line and without bubbles after the solenoid valve

  5. Check that the on-off valve of the manometers are opened

  6. Refrigerant adding on low pressure side if need it

  7. Adding oil to compressor by :

    • Empty the low pressure circuit

    • Connect the oil pump fitted on the compressor sump after a dearator filter

    • Discharge the connection pipe and when the oil is on the sump connection pump the oil into the sump as long as oil level reaches the half position of the sight glass

    • Take out the pipe and close the connection

  8. Clean slide and butterfly dampers

  9. Clean air filter by jet of air or water for H.A unit

  10. Check the condition and tension of fan and motor driving belts

  11. Make sure that the fan blades have not suffered any damage
