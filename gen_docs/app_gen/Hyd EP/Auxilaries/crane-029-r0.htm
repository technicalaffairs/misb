Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The gantry cranes for power house erection and maintenance service have the follow Main lifting capacity of the main hoist : 900 K.N
Main lifting capacity of the auxiliary hoist : 320 K.N
Vertical lift of the main hook : 37.7 m
Vertical lift of the auxiliary hook : 38.25 m
Main hoisting speeds  :1/0.2 m/min abt
Auxiliary hoisting speeds: 3/0.6 m/min

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet .... etc
