Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet .... etc

  • Stop pump and switch off the power supply

  • Close the out let and inlet valves

  • Put suitable warning boards

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

  • Set of wrench

  • Alignment equipment

  • Grease for lubricating

  • Cleaning material

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check the alignment of the pump

  2. Operate the pump to check the noise

  3. Stop pump and switch off the power supply and close the out let and inlet valves

  4. Dismantle the coupling

  5. Dismantle the shaft and impeller and protection and cooling pushing and check for defects or craks

  6. Check key and change if need

  7. Check the bearing if need

  8. Install the pump and alignment with motor

  9. Install the coupling

  10. Operate the pump and check the load current
