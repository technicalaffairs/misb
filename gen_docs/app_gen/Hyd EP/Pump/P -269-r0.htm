Introduction

This document shows the protective maintenance which must be performed annually on the thrust bearing oil circulation pump as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This pump function is to pumped the oil through the oil cooling system the technical specification of the oil pump

capacity Q : 300 L/m
pressure p : 5.2 bar
speed N   3000 rpm
power : 7.5 kw

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet .... etc

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained & qualified or directly

  • Supervised by some one

  • Switch off pump

  • Close the inlet and out let valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Set wrenches and screwdrivers

  • Kerosene and cleaning material (rag. cloth)

Work to be carried out 

  1. Vent all pressure from pump housing

  2. Disassembly the inlet and out let junction

  3. Revision pump from driver motor and remove coupling from pump

  4. Disassembly the pump parts

  5. Inspect O-rings, mechanical seal, ball bearing and packing

  6. Replace when the parts have been disturbed from their original installed position

  7. Clean and coated all internal and rotating parts with lubricated oil before assembly

  8. Assembly the pump and mounted on its place

  9. Assembly the inlet and out let junction and the coupling on the pump

  10. Align the pump .
