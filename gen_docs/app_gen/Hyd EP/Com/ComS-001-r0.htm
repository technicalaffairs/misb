Introduction

The SX-50 communication system is an integrated communication system able to send and receive both voice and data signals it is a compact wall-mounted system. Using stored program control and advanced digital techniques its modular design allows easy fault finding and circuit card replacing with out disturbing the system.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Switch off the power supply.

    •
Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter Fluk – 79.

  • Set of common screw drivers.

  • Air blower

  • Dry cleaning spray and cleaning cloth.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Turn off the power through the switch on power unit of the system.

  2. Remove the cover of the sys.

  3. Plug out the cards of the sys respectively.

  4. Clean the cards and clean the contact points on them.

  5. Clean the mother board by using of air blower and clean the board contact points with the cards by dry cleaning spray.

  6. Turn on the power feeding and measure the following volts (± 5 , ± 12 , - 32 , - 48 ).

  7. Turn off the power again.

  8. Plug in the cards as before and erect the systems cover.
