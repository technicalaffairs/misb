Introduction

The neax12sa electronic systems is a fully stored program controlled which can send and receive both voice and date singles. It can be handeled as 96 telephone lines up to 384 lines maximum.

Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter Fluk  79.

  • Set of common screw drivers.

  • Air blower

  • Dry cleaning spray and cleaning cloth.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Turn off the power through each case feeding off individually Then tern off the main power.

  2. Plug out the cards respectively and clean the cards and contact points.

  3. Clean the mother board by using of air blower and clean the board contact points with the cards by dry cleaning spray.

  4. Turn on the power and measure the following volts (± 5 , ± 12 , - 58 , - 48 ).

  5. Turn off the power again.

  6. Plug in the cards respectively as before and in stole the systems cover.

  7. Turn on the power and test the sys- through the program review.
