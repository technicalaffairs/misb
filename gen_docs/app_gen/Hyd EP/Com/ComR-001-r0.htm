Introduction

The CR-310 is a communication recorder with audio tape recorder which records data onto VHS video cassette tapes, it can record continuously for 24 hours on 10 channels with automatic control of recording levels

The first draft is initiated & tested by eng. Abdel-Aleem Ahmed, accepted by eng. Sleem Ahmed  Ali general director, approved by eng. Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Switch off the power supply.

    •
Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter Fluk 79.

  • Set of common screw drivers.

  • Air blower

  • Dry cleaning spray and cleaning cloth.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Turn off the power through recorder switch .

  2. Open the front cover of the device through two screw polts .

  3. Dismantle the mechanical drive and replace the rolls by another new .

  4. Clean the mechanical drive by using dry cleaning spray .

  5. Plug out the cards respectively and clean the contact points .

  6. Clean the recorder from inside by using air blower .

  7. Clean the mother board contact points .

  8. Plug in the cards respectively as before

  9. Install the mechanical drive and test the recording to pe operation with measure the volts on it

  10. Install the hole device and tested again
