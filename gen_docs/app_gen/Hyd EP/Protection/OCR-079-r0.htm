Introduction

This document outlines the inspection checklist for over current and cut off for aux. 11 k.v protection which must be carried out on an annually

The first draft initiated and tested by eng. Hatem ahmad Shafea, at 8 July 2008, and accepted by General Manager Eng. Usama Ahmed Hsaneen and approved by Eng. Khalf Alla Mahmod Head Sector of Aswan Dam 1  station HPGC.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, and rags

  • Digital ammeter

  • Screw driver

  • Set of secondary injection type DHF

Work to be carried out 

  1. Review technical reference material and manufacture's manual

  2. C.B 11 k.v for this feeder must be off and out

  3. MCB for DC F1 turn off

  4. Disconnect terminal on SPAJ 140c for currants 1-3,4-6, 7-9 and 66 for D.C trip

  5. Prepare secondary injection set and terminal for connection current and time

  6. Connect the currant to 1-3 and time for 66 and 65

  7. MCB for DC F1 turn on

  8. Raise the currant slowly to reach limited currant for trip O.C

  9. Stop injection by Kay for time S1 (S1 off) on set of secondary injection and make time zero on display

  10. Put S1 On

  11. Return step no 9 and record currant and time for trip O.C in the record sheet

  12. Raise the currant slowly to reach limited currant for trip O.C cut off

  13. Stop injection by Kay for time S1 (S1 off) on set of secondary

  14. Put S1 on

  15. Return step no 13 and remark currant and time for O.C cut off

  16. Turn off secondary injection set

  17. Disconnect terminal from 1-3

  18. Connect currant terminal to 4-6

  19. Return steps from 8 to 16

  20. Disconnect terminal from 4-6

  21. Connect currant terminal to 7-9

  22. Return steps from 8 to 16

  23. MCB for DC F1 turn off

  24. Connect terminal 1-3,4-6,7-9 and 65 on SPAJ 140c

  25. Recheck for connection

  26. MCB for DC f1 turn on
