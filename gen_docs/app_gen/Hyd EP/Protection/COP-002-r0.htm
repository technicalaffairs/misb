Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes goggles and rubber glove .... etc

  • The section should be isolated from both sides

  • The main 6 kv circuit breaker of the switched off and racked out

  • The sub circuit breakers of the distribution board (out going0 should be switched - off and racked out

  • The control voltage 220 v dc should be switched - off

  • The switched - off test switches

Tools and Equipment

  • Test set K 501

  • Ammeter

  • Hand tools

  • Fine cloth & contact cleaner (spray) for cleaning purposes

 

Work to be carried out 

  1. Clean all of contacts relay with special type of spray

  2. Check all of control circuit terminals for tightness

  3. Make the connection as cutoff. Current protection relay with a current transformer test set K501

  4. Slowly increase the current and to stop when the contact monitor senses a closed contact

  5. After finishing the following tests should be done by using special test equipment

  • Indicating lamps and their circuits are healthy

  • Trip the operations and their remote signals by certain faults

  • Checks all signals of tests indicated by DCS & SOUND & IGHT

Restoring the relay into service

  1. Return relay to service by removing and switch on test switches

  2. Checks relay configuration
