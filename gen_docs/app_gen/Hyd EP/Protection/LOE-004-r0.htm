Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated  by Y1 in the title block.

The YTM 7111 relay are designed to bring generator out of service when a field loss is detected by the relay and the phase currents on phases R&T of generator stator circuit exceed a pre - determined setting

The first draft initiated & tested by eng. Mohamed Abdel Morred, accepted by general director eng. Nagy Nageeb Saleh and approved by eng. Khalaf Allah Mahmoud Abd El-Gaber head sector of Aswan Dam (1&2) Hydraulic Power Stations and certified by eng. Mohamed Usama Soliman HPGC MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Maintenance staff should carry the ID Safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used  as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • The generator must be isolated.

  • Switch off the DC power supply before inserting or withdrawing the relay.

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection test set T/I & T/V

  • Test leads.

  • Multi-meter.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check the minimum operating currents.

  2. Check the time delay.

  3. Check the trip signal to generator circuit breaker.

  4. Check the signal alarm.

  5. Check the indication flag.

 

 

Preparation

  1. Connect the secondary injection test set to AC supply.

  2. Withdraw the relay from its case.

  3. The relay connections must be in accordance with the connection diagram supplied with the relay as in attached drawing.

  4. Review the test sheet for required settings.

Checking the tripping element

  1. Connect the current source to terminals 2 & 3 (ph- R) or 6 & 7 (ph-T), and the voltage source to terminals 22&23.

  2. Inject a current equal to setting values, the indicator lamp should go out and the output unit should operate to trip the circuit breaker and signaling an alarm.

    3.

Checking the operating time

  1. Inject the current source on one of the phases that in turn simultaneously starting the  timer and the timer must be stopped by the change of state of the contact ( C ) on the output unit.
