Introduction

The RXEL - 22 over voltage relay is generally used in protection, control and supervision applications where accurate and repeatable operation at the set operating value and a high continuous voltage rating are required and where a high resetting ratio is not an important consideration

The RXEL-22 is especially recommended for applications where substantial voltage harmonic components are present

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, (safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc).

  • Issue work permit including the team work name.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • The transformer is out of service

  • Isolate the relay from PT`S

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary 1-ph. injection test set (sverker 650 - sweeden)

  • Multimeter

  • Test - plug handle (RTXH 18)

  • Test wires

  • Variable resistance (0-450)ohm

Work to be carried out 

The Transformer is out of service

  1. Connect the circuit as shown in fig (1)

  2. Increase the voltage gradually until reach the operating value

  3. Ensure that the relay operates at the setting voltage value 6 v

  4. ensure that the relay operates at the setting time value 0.5 sec

  5. Record this values on the test sheet

Restoring the relay into service

  1. Return relay to ts normal position by Take off the equipments and the wires
