Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed  on monthly  bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

Power : 560 KW
Bore : 135 mm
stroke : 152 mm
Cubic capacity : 26.11 liters
Compression ratio : 14.5/1

The perkins CV12 is four stroke turbocharged direct injection compression ignition liquid cooled engine with air charge cooling the 12 cylinders are arranged in two banks in a 60 deg (V) configuration the diesel generator in normal operating condition of the power unit is automatically started and connected to switch board item CJ-QP01 CJ-QP02 in case of lack of power voltage on its bus bar the diesel generator is rated to cover the electric power demand of the auxiliaries that are to be kept operating

The main systems of engine

  1. Cooling system : radiator pack engine pipe work thermostat

  2. Fuel system : injection pump feed pump fuel injectors fuel filters

  3. Lubrication system : sump, oil pump, pressure relief valve, oil filters

  4. Induction system : air cleaners, turbocharger

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet .... etc

  • The unit should be off position

  • Maintenance is carried out with cooperation with electrical department

Tools and Equipment

  • Mechanical tools ( complete set of manual wrench - brushes - etc)

  • Blanco 1 ton

  • Tighten hexagonal screw with atorqe wrench

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check the (coolant water lubricating oil fuel) level with the correct grade (upper mark on the dipstick)

  2. Check each air cleaner restriction indicator if red sleeve is visible the air cleaner element has reached the limit of fouling and must be serviced immediately reset the indicator by pressing the button in its top

  3. Check the control linkage for freedom and range of movement

  4. Check the induction and exhaust system and the radiator are free from obstruction

  5. Check the oil pressure at rated speed

  6. Check the condition and tension of fan and alternator driving belts

  7. Check the turbocharger securing nuts tightness and check for freedom of rotation and listen for sound of rubbing may indicate bearing wear cleaning and bearing checks
