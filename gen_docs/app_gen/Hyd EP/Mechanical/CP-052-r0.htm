Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed  annually  bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

Esna power plant contain three groups of different compressors:

  • Two compressors high air pressure (60 bar) to feed the high air pressure to go mechanical governor speed and reduced to 32 bar to feed breaking units to stop

  • Two compressors high air injection compressor (60bar) to produce compressed air for turbines at
    (Emergency shut down load rejection slice mode)

  • Two compressors low air pressure system (7 bar) to feed (seal water , water in take screen blowing service, water pressurization vessels various available fast connection at elevation 78,73.5,70,56.5)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy protective equipment as safety shoes, helmet .... etc

  • Main supply disconnected switch in open position

  • Maintenance is carried out with cooperation with electrical department

Tools and Equipment

  • Mechanical tools ( complete set of manual wrench - brushes - etc)

  • Blanco 1 ton

  • Tighten hexagonal screw with a torque wrench

 

 

 

Work to be carried out 

  1. Manually drain condensate traps if the unit is being operated during periods of high

  2. Check oil level in sump and refill as required

  3. Drain oil sump and renew oil and replace oil filters

  4. Inspect clean and refill with oil

  5. Clean and inspect for damaged fins

  6. Check belts tension

  7. Check cooling cycles for all stages of compressors
