Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes and safety belt when cleaning insulators.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding is required.

  • The unit must be out of service.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt

  • Digital voltmeter and clamp ammeter.

  • Dielectric tester, megger

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check that the glass of level indicator is not dirty.

  2. Check the level of the sealing oil in oil cup.

  3. Check visually the extent of any contamination on the bushing.

  4. Check for any loose connection.

  5. Check for oil leakage at any connection.

  6. Measurement of dielectric strength by dielectric tester (more then 40 KV/2.5mm).

  7. Measurement of acid value of oil by chemical staff according to MPIS less than 0.2.

  8. If the dielectric strength and acid value are below standard to be make the test specified of oil according IEC296.

  9. Measure the insulation resistance of cooling fan motor (more than 2MΩ).

  10. Check the operation of tap changer.

  11. Check the operation of all (switch) annunciators and lamps for control panel.

  12. Check the oil tank leakage and coating damage.

  13.  Check that all connections are good.

  14. Check the discoloration of silica gel if the quantity of silicagel changed into pink "qty changed ≥ two third' then you must change all silicagel with a new".

  15. Measure the insulation resistance between windings and between winding and ground with 2kv Megger.

  16. Check for any oil leakage of bushing.

  17. Check for any damage to the bushing.

  18. Clean the bushing.

  19. Check the construction of protective relay.

  20. Check the operation of all devices.

  21. Check the insulation resistance for control cables more than 2MΩ.

  22. Check the water tightness and the tightness of all connection for local panel.

  23. Check the insulation resistance of control panel and terminal box  more than 2MΩ.
