Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounding on all its sides is required

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes

  • There is a danger of falling into the oil containment pit when working near large power transformers

  • There is a danger of falling off from the top of transformer

  • Wear safety belt when cleaning insulators

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and tool set

  • Blower, digital voltmeter mugger devices

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Check for any oil leaks

  2. Check oil level in main tank conservator

  3. Check oil level bushings

  4. Check oil temperature and winding temperature indicators

  5. Record max oil temperature

  6. Record max winding temperature

  7. Check all cooling fans for correct operational

  8. Clean and inspect transformer control cabinet and tap changer control cabinet

  9. Check silica gel breather

  10. Clean all porcelain surfaces of all bushings

  11. Be sure that all tools have been taken off the top of the transformer
