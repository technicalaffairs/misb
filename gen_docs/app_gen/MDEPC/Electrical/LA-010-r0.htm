Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • Circuit breaker must be fully isolated

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off and ground the CB on both sides.

  • Isolation and proper earthing is required.

  • There is danger of falling off the top of the arrester when cleaning insulators or checking connections, wear your safety belt.

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Tester (digital Avometer).

  • Set off spanners and screw drivers .

  • Oscillograph.

  • 500/1000 V Megger .

  • Vacuum cleaner.

  • Safety belt, and safety Helmet

  • Rags

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check porcelain for cracks, and chipped skirts.

  2. Check for loose caps and tighten if necessary.

  3. Thoroughly clean all porcelain surfaces
