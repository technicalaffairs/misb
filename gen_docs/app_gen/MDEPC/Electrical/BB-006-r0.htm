Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every 2 years or during outage of service as indicated  by Y2 in the above title block., The first draft initiated by Eng. Amged Abd El Aziz and revised by maintenance General Director: Eng .Wageh Khalil   revised by MDEP Co. Quality control Eng. Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • Circuit breaker must be fully isolated

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off and ground the CB on both sides.

  • Switch off control current for CB (D.C)

  •  

Tools and Equipment

  • Tester (digital Avometer).

  • Set off spanners and screw drivers .

  • Oscillograph

  • Silicon grease .

  • 500/1000 V Megger .

  • Contact resistance meter .

  • Vacuum cleaner.

  • Grease.  

Work to be carried out 

  1. Dry cleaning of all bushings.

  2. Retiting the terminal blocks and operation control panel.

  3. Clean the insulators of the earth connectors and wave traps.

  4. Grease the contact points of bus bar isolator.

  5. Carry out visual check of the bays, where applicable also check parts outside the building, e.g. overhead-line termination modules.

  6. Record any special events (as requested by system management).

  7. Check gas filling using a precision pressure gauge. (All gas compartments with the exception of the circuit breaker module; 
    Top with gas, if necessary.

  8. Trace and eliminate leak, if necessary.

  9. Check lock nuts of operating linkages for disconnectors, earthing switches for tightness.

  10. Motors assembly ,Check control section and test heater output (without taking readings).

  11. Check operating linkages for disconnectors, earthing switches for easy movement. 
    To do this watch the On-Off  indicators: Their movement must be uniform and smooth.

  12. Test-operate, i.e. open and close, each disconnector and earthing switch five times by its motor assembly (not by hand).

  13. After test-operating disconnectors, earthing switches and high-speed earthing switches check their mechanical indicators for the 
    On and Off positions and note any discrepancies.

The following steps depending on the condition:

  1. Check all remade joints (covers and flanges) for any signs of leakage (final check). 

  2. Measure moisture content of gas in all gas compartments (final check). 

  3. Paint finish and anti-corrosion protection to be touched up by customer's personnel.

  4. Bushings of overhead-line termination modules to be cleaned by customer's personnel.
