Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block

Technical Specification:

Batteries 2 x 6124 OPzS 3000 2V - 3000 Ah

D : 1.215 kg/I

C 10,vf = 1.8 V

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued

  • Wear safety gloves and clothes

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set

  • Digital voltmeter

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower brush

  • Hydrometer for electrolyte density measurement

Work to be carried out

  1. Check the cells electrolyte level

  2. Clean it from air dust and dirty

  3. Check the ventilation system

  4. Measure the cells electrolyte density & voltage and record

  5. Check the voltage of battery set 1 & 2
