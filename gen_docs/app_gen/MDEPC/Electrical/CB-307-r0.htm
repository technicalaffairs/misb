Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • Circuit breaker must be fully isolated

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off and ground the CB an both sides.

  • Switch off control current for CB (DC)

Tools and Equipment

  • Tester (digital Avometer).

  • Set off spanners and screw drivers.

  • Oscillograph .

  • Silicon grease .

  • 500/1000 V Megger .

  • Contact resistance meter .

  • Vacuum cleaner.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check the value of the Capacitor.

  2. Check 220 kv CB control panel (retightening & cleaning).

  3. Grease mechanical parts.

  4. Check operating mechanism.

  5. Open the earthing switch on both sides of the breaker

  6. Switch on control current for CB (DC)

  7. Close the work permit.
