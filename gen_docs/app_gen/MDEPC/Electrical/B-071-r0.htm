Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.         

  • No smoking is permitted in battery room.

  • Use standard personal safety equipments .

  • Water source must be available in battery room.    

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydrometer.

  • Digital multimeter .

  • Thermometer.

  • Complete set of common keys.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be carried out 

  1. Make sure the cell are clean and dry , externally clean the caps .

  2. Check that connections are properly tightened, lubricate them and fully screw them

  3. Check the temperature of the cell : constantly high temperature will increase the consumption of the water and reduce the life of the battery whereas, constantly low temperature will lower discharge performance.

  4. Read the voltage, density and temperature of each cell

  5. Check electrolyte level and top them up with distilled water if necessary

  6. An equalizing charge is needed in the following cases

  • After some water has been added

  • If voltages or densities are not homogeneous

  • In case of low density
