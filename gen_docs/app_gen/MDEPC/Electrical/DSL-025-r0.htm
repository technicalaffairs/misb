Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses)

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified .

Tools and Equipments

  • Hydrometer .

  • Tester (voltmeter, ammeter) .

  • Cleaning cloths .

    •

  • Cleaning brushes tool .

  • Check the ventilation system .

     

Work to be carried out

  1. Check batteries current .

  2. Measure room temperature .

  3. Visual inspection for cables bolts and joint .

  4. Check battery charger indication lamps.

  5. Check battery charger input and output voltage .

  6. Check connection and disconnection in case of fault in power supply .

  7. Check battery charger alarm .
