Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Circuit breaker must be fully isolated.

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker.

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off and ground the CB an both sides.

  • Switch off control current for CB (DC)

Tools and Equipment

  • Tester (digital Avometer).

  • Set off spanners and screw drivers.

  • Oscillograph .

  • Silicon grease .

  • 500/1000 V Megger .

  • Contact resistance meter .

  • Vacuum cleaner.

Work to be carried out

 

Routine Inspection & Maintenance

  1. Measure the contact resistance by contact resistance tester.

  2. Measure the insulation resistance by megger 1000 V DC.

  3. Calibrate gas pressure devices.

 

Hydraulic System

  1. Check solenoid valve.

  2. Check bolts.

  3. Check the operating indicator, joint link , etc.

  4. Inspect & clean the operating rod surface.

  5. Check the accumulator.

  6. Check the relays contact .

  7. Check oil pressure gauge.

  8. Check oil pressure switch.

  9. Check oil pipe.

  10. Check the oil pump unit

  • Measure initial charging pressure.

  • Measure motor operation duration from 0 to 314 bar.

  1. Check oil leakage.

  2. Control wiring  power off.

  3. Check all relay contacts.

  4. Check auxiliary linkage.
