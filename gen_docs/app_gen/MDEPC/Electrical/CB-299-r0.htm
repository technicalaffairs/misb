Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The unit must be out of service.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipments.

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • Digital voltmeter.

  • Vacuum cleaner.

Work to be carried out 

Visual inspection of the condition of:

  1. External screwed fittings

  2. Operating linkage

  3. External connections

  4. Check for corrosion

 Visual and manual inspection of the condition of all wiring.

  1. Check all terminal boxes.

  2. Check the circuit and connection points by continuity method.

Miscellaneous checks:

  1. Read and record  the operations counter P1(Fig 1) of the three-pole circuit-breaker Q0.

  2. Read and record of pump start-up counter P2(Fig 1) of drive.

  3. Check for correct function of the various heaters

  4. Investigate for unexplainable noises and smells.

  5. Check  regularly the circuit-breaker SF6 gas pressure.

The pressure gauge serves only for visual monitoring of the SF6 gas pressure.
