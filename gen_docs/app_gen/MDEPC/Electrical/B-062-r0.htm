Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1in the title block for battery charger

Technical Specification:

DC input : 220 V No. of cells : 36
   DC output : 220 V No. of cells : 36

The first draft is initiated by eng. Mohamed Fouad, tested by eng. Wageh Khalil, revised by MDEPCo. Quality control eng. Adel El Naggar, accepted by eng. Ali Zahran, general manager & eng. Samir Abd El Gillil MDEPC. quality control general director, approved by eng. Ahmed Sawwan head sector of Nubaria power station and certified by eng. Mohammed Nageb quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued

  • Wear safety gloves and clothes

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set (battery hydrometer)

  • Digital voltmeter

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower brush

Work to be carried out

  1. Measure voltage and density (specific gravity) for every cell

  2. Clean it from air dust and dirty

  3. Check the ventilation system

  4. Measure the total voltage for the batteries

  5. Measure positive voltage with earth

  6. Measure negative voltage with earth

  7. Check the liquid level inside the batteries

  8. Measure compartment temperature
