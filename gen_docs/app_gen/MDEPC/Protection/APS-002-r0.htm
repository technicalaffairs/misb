Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

This patent concerns the test of protection relays for the indoor 6.3 kv circuit breakers manufactured by ABB ARAB. The CB`s are of types HB 07, 12.40 V, HC 07, 25.40 V, HD4 12.16.40 and HD4 12.15.40 rating currents 1250A, 2500A, 1600A, 2500A respectively and short circuit capacity 40 KA , 3 sec

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified test staff should carry the suitable safely category

  • Safety fence with caution marks and no smoking signs surrounding the work area must be used

  • Wearing safety helmet, shoes, cloths, and masks are necessary

  • Be aware of parts under voltage

  • Disconnect the cell of work

  • Draw the CB of the concerned cell in the 6.3 kv hall

  • Put the CB at test position

  • Check the indicating lamps of the cell

  • Check the existence of DC of the cell

Tools and Equipment

  • A bag of screws and nail

  • Two testers units of type Z11

  • Some wires

  • A timer TM - 2 (program)

  • A voltmeter and an ammeter

The Protection Devices for Motors

  • Under voltage device.

  • Multi – function motor protection device  ( S.C. – O.C. current – negative phase sequence earth fault ).

 

The Protection Devices for Transformer

  • Under voltage.

  • Earth fault.

  • Short circuit + Over current.

  • Differential.

  • Zero sequence relays.

  1. Measure the 220 V DC control voltage on the terminals (61,62)

  2. Disconnect the D.C.

  3. Loosen phase R(13, 14 ) phase S(16,17) and phase T (19,20) of the device and pit Z11 leads, see figure 1

  4. Put timer leads on ( 65, 66 ) .

  5. Reconnect the D.C.

  6. Inject a voltage through Z11 till the reset value to remove the fault sign of the device ( yellow one )

  7. Decrease the voltage again to record the pick up (operating) value

  8. The timer starts counting at this moment and closes after the rated time. The  trip sign (red one) shows up.

  9. Record the results for three phases and compare them with the previous ones at table 1

Short Circuit Current

  1. Measure the 220 Vdc. control voltage.

  2. Disconnect D.C. switch. see figure 2

  3. Put the leads of Z11 on (X2.1) Phase R(1,2) and phase T (5,6) of the device

  4. Disconnect the trip leads either on (X4,1) or (16,17) or (18,19), and connect the timer leads on them.

  5. Connect the D.C. again.

  6. Inject the current by Z11.

  7. Observe the device screen till it flashes indicating a certain mode.

  8. At a certain time the timer stops and the screen displays the operating mode (I>>) .

  9. Connect phase S(3,4) instead of phase R (1,2) see figure 1

  10. Connect phase T (5.6) instead of phase S (3.4)

  11. Record the results for phase R and phase S and phase T at table 3

Differential Protection (SPAD 346 C)    (Figure 5)

 

Type SPAD 346 C Insulation test voltage for 1 min : VP 2 KV EFF
Function : F 87 Differential Frequency fn : 50/60 HZ
Manufacturer : ABB (finland) (In) rated current 1 A
Setting 0.2 In Auxiliary voltage: 220 V dc

  1. Measure the 220 Vdc, control voltage.

  2. Inject current by Z11 in(13,14)&(16,17) & (19, 20) on the rosette for the incoming cubicles [low tension side ].see figure 5

  3. Inject current by Z11 in  (13,14) & (16,17) & (19, 20) on the rosette for the transformers    [low tension side ].

  4. Increase the injected current till the red lamp of the device switches on. The relay trips instantaneously in each case.

  5. Inject current by Z11 in(1,2)& (4,5) & (7,8) on the rosette for the incoming cubicles [high tension side].

  6. Inject current by Z11 in  (1,2) & (4,5) & (7,8) on the rosette for the transformers [high tension side ].

  7. Record the results for both sides at table 6

 

Finally –Maintenance Procedures

  1. Disconnect all the testing leads and estrange the testing tools.

  2. Put the  C.B. in its original state to be on service any time.

  3. Press the “RESET” button for the devices and check the relay setting .

  4. Connect the D.C. after the limit switch connection.

  5. Make the place clean of work signs.
