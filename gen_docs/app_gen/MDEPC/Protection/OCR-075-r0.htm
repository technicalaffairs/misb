Introduction

This procedure details the testing procedures and calibration I1, which recommended to be carried out every year as indicated by Y1 in the title block..

The type IJCV51A relay is draw out induction disc time over current relays having voltage restraint and inverse time characteristic.

The relay will pick up at tap value when rated voltage is applied to the restraint circuit with zero voltage on the restraint circuit , the relay will pick up at 25% tap value.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Issue work permit including the team work name.

  • The generator must be out of service.

  • Put C.B of  unit at test position.

  • Disconnect secondary terminals of voltage transformer ( PT1).

  • Disconnect excitation system for A.C. generator (switch off "41 AC" breaker).

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection test set.

  • Clamp meter, Test leads

  • AVO meter

  • Complete tool set.

  • Air blower.

  •  Contact cleaner (CRC).

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be carried out

Preparation            

  1. Look at field maintenance file and compare the setting on relay with those shown on the test sheet.

  2. Check DC. Circuit for Over Current Relay.

Testing the Relay

First test   ( with voltage restraint  V = 110 v )

  1. Connect test leads between  the current terminals ( 5 – 6 ) on the relay.

  2. Remove voltage terminals (7-8) from the relay.

  3. Connect test leads between  the voltage terminals ( 7 – 8 ) on the relay.

  4. Connect test leads between  the trip terminals ( 1 – 2 ) on the relay to timer terminals on the test set.

  5. Increase the injection voltage to 110 v.

  6. Increase the current slowly until the relay energize and regist (P.U) value .

  7. Decrease the current slowly until the relay denergize and regist (REST) value.

  8. Increase the current again to (P.U) value

  9. Stop the test set

  10. Restart the test set ( with voltage = 110 v  &  current = P.U value ).

  11. The relay will operate , the timer will account and stop when the trip contact close.

  12. Register the time value.

  13. Record these values in the test sheet.

  14. Repeat above steps for phases (S& T).

Second test ( with voltage restraint  V = 0 v )

Repeat all above steps from (1-14) except step no.( 5 )   

with injection voltage   ( V=0).                                                                                                                               

Test C.B tripping circuit of the unit.

  1. Remove relay 52GTX .

  2. Close C.B. for the unit at test position.

  3. Trip C.B. for the unit by Over Current Relay.

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service

  1. Disconnect the test leads from the Relay.

  2. Reconnect voltage terminals (7-8) to the relay.

  3. Make sure CT and PT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  4. Make reset to Over Current relay.

  5. Reconnect excitation system for A.C generator (switch on "41 AC" breaker).

  6. Reconnect relay 52GTX

  7. Make sure the spring of the C.B on discharge position .

  8. Put C.B in service position .
