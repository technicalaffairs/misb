Introduction

This procedure details the testing procedures and calibration I1, which recommended to be carried out every year as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This is single phase sensitive over voltage relay of induction disk construction,

This is equipped with a time dial so provide adjustable time delay in their operation,

This relay have a capacitor in series with the operation coil, the effect of capacitor is to tune the relay coil circuit so that the relay pickup on third harmonic .

Earth fault relay (IAV51K) connected via distribution transformer, its provide the very sensitive voltage pickup rating, this enables the relay to detect aground fault .this relay will pick up when over voltage ground detecting over its tap value.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, (safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc).

  • Issue work permit including the team work name.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • The generator must be out of service.

  • Put C.B of unit at test position.

  • Disconnect secondary terminals of voltage transformer (PT1).

  • Disconnect excitation system of A.C. generator (switch off "41 AC" breaker).

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection test set.

  • Test leads.

  • AVO meter.

  • Complete tool set.

  • Contact cleaner (CRC).

  • Air blower.

 

Work to be carried out 

Preparation

  1. Look at field maintenance file and compare the setting on relay with those shown on the test sheet.

  2. Check DC. Circuit for Earth fault Relay.

  Testing the Relay

  1. Remove voltage terminals (5-6) from the relay.

  2. Connect test leads between the voltage terminals (5-6) on the relay.

  3. Connect test leads between the trip terminals (1 – 2) on the relay to timer terminals on the test set.

  4. Increase the voltage slowly until the relay energize and record (P.U) value.

  5. Decrease the voltage slowly until the relay not energize and record (REST) value.

  6. Increase the voltage again to (2P.U) value.

  7. Stop the test set.

  8. Restart the test set (with voltage = 2P.U).

  9. The relay will operate, the timer will account and stop when the trip contact close.

  10. Record the time value.

  11. Make sure that lock out relay operate with earth fault relay energize.

  12. Record these values in the test sheet.

  13. Make reset to lock out relay.

Test C.B Tripping Circuit of the unit.

  1. Remove relay 52GTX.

  2. Close C.B. for the unit at test position.

  3. Trip C.B. for the unit by earth fault Relay

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service

  1. Disconnect the test leads from the Relay.

  2. Reconnect voltage terminals (5-6) to the relay.

  3. Make sure CT and PT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  4. Make reset to earth fault relay, alarm and lock out relay.

  5. Reconnect excitation system for A.C generator (Switch on "41 AC" breaker).

  6. Reconnect relay 52GTX.

  7. Make sure the spring of the C.B on discharge position.

  8. Put C.B in service position.
