Introduction

This procedure details the testing procedures and calibration I1, which recommended to be carried out every year as indicated by Y1 in the title block..

The type CFD relays are used for differential protection of alternating current machines against phase to phase and phase to ground faults. For an internal fault the relay will close its contacts. If on external  fault therefore the relay will not operate.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Issue work permit including the team work name.

  • The generator must be out of service.

  • Put C.B of  unit at test position.

  • Disconnect excitation system for A.C. generator (switch off "41 AC" breaker).

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection test set.

  • Clamp meter, Test leads

  • AVO meter

  • Complete tool set.

  • Air blower.

  •  Contact cleaner (CRC).

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be carried out

Preparation            

  1. Look at field maintenance file and compare the setting on relay with those shown on the test sheet.

  2. Check DC. Circuit for high speed differential relay.

Testing the Relay

  1. Connect test leads between  the current terminals ( 5 – 6 ) figure 1

  2. Increase the current slowly until the relay energize and regist (P.U) value

  3. Increase the current slowly until the relay denergize and regist (rest) value

  4. Make sure that lock out relay operate with diff. relay energize

  5. Record these value in the test sheet

  6. Connect the test leads between the current terminals (7-6)

  7. Repeat steps (2&3&4&5) for terminals (7-6)

  8. Repeat all above steps for phases (S&T)

  9. Remove relay 52 GTX

  10. Close C.B for the unit at test position

  11. Trip C.B for the init by differential

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service

  1. Disconnect the test leads from the Relay.

  2. Make sure CT and PT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  3. Make reset to lock out relay and reset alarm indication

  4. Reconnect excitation system for A.C generator switch on "41 AC" breaker

  5. Reconnect relay 52GTX

  6. Put C.B in service position .(make sure the spring of the C.B on discharge position)
