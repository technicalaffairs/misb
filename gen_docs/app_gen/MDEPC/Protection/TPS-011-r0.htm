  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  •  Gas turbine out of service and work permit required

  • Make Sure CT Secondary circuit are shorted

  • Voltage transformer miniature circuit breakers (m.c.b.) is opened

Preliminary Steps to Prepare for Relay Testing

  1. Review technical reference materiel manufactures /  materiel

  2. Obtain copy of relay setting from felid  maintenance file.

  3. Obtain outage for unit protected by this relay

  4. Inspect the secondary circuits of CT/VT miniature circuit breakers

  5. (MCB) and all cabling supplying the relay .

  6. Block any disturbing alarm or fault Recorders associated with this protection.

 

Preparation of Test

  1. Shorted terminal of CT in Socket (X101)(X201) in banal (CHB01)

  2. Connect the POWER Supply to the device (Secondary) Tester (OMICRON)

  3. Connect the secondary injected tester (OMICRON) For inject the current (1.2.3) and Neuter (4)for two sides.

  4. Connect the timer leads.

  5. Digs program feed the deice from laptop.

  6. Event record from the device to laptop.

  7. Record the Pickup Value and drop out value and the time of Pickup Value.

Unit auxiliary transformer protection system 2 (F23)

The used spectrum of the device covers the over current earth protection for the unit auxiliary transformer BBT01 the main over excitation protection for the BAT/BBT transformer. The under frequency / over frequency protection first stage (220 kv substation decommission)

  • Over current 50/51

  • Earth fault 51N

  • Under & over frequency 81

  • Over excitation V/HZ

  • Bus ground delectation 59 N (90% stator ground fault protection)

  • External trips

  • Shorted the CT terminal in x 208.x209x204

  • Injected the current and voltage (x209)

  • Record the pick up

  • The over and under frequency protection of the device is used as first stage for is elating the power plant from the network by H.V circulate breaker opening
