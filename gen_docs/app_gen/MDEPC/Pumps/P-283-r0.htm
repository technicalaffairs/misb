Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Straight edge, rugs, cleaning fluid.

Work to be carried out 

Pump in Operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare it with the setting value

  3. Check any leakage from pump & piping flanges and re-tighten (if it is needed)

  4. Measuring bearing temperature

  5. Check glands for leakage and re-tightens it (if it is needed). 

Pump out of Operation

  1. Replace oil of pump

  2. Replace packing rings

  3. Check shaft sleeve condition and replace (if it is needed)

  4. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to be (if it is needed)

  5. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed)

  6. Check bolts fixation then re-tightens it (if it is needed)

  7. Clean external part with compressed air.
