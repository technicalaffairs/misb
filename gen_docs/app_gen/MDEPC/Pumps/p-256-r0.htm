Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Work to be carried out

Pump in operation

  1. Check pump capacity, pressure and power

  2. Check the noise of the Pump.

Pump out of operation

  1. Check the pump and piping for leaks

  2. Check the fixation

  3. Check alignment

Starting the Pump

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  1. Check the direction of rotation of motor before starting it is clockwise and adjust if needed

  2. The level of liquid in the tank must be above the minimum level

  3. The on-off valve in the suction from the tank must be completely opened while the air discharge valve closed after complete priming

  4. The control valve in delivery must be partially opened

Attendance and checking during operation of the pump, the following should be checked:

  1. Following the indication of the flow - meter and manometer installed adjusted to control valve on the plant delivery until the flow rate and deliver head reach the design value if necessary and adjusted the bypass flow rate

  2. Check of flow rate delivery head and absorbed power

  3. Check the water leakage of pump

  4. Check the pump pressure
