Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed every one year as indicated by (Y1) in the above title block.

This pump is used to suction the sludge wasting water from the DAF tank and discharged it in the sludge thickner.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes breathing mask  ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction values

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained

Work to be carried out 

Pump in operation

  1. Check pump capacity, pressure and power .

  2. Check the noisy of the pump.

  3. Measure vibration level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do

  4. Check the Temperature of bearing

  5. Check the leakage  of the mechanical seal 

Pump out of operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value

  2. Check the mechanical seal  leakage

  3. Check the pump and piping flanges for leaks

  4. Check the bolts fixation

Starting the Pump

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  1. Check the direction of rotation of motor before starting.

  2. Open the valves and vents

  3. Filling the pump casing with the medium , in case of high viscosity media fill with the liquid .pump priming is vital to ensure lubrication of the rubber stator

  4. Fill the piping on the pump suction  side in anti-clockwise rotation only: fill the pump housing

Attendance and checking during operation of the pump, the following should be checked:

  1. Check of flow rate and delivery head

  2. Check the shaft seal for leaks
