Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed every one year as indicated by (Y1) in the above title block.

This pump is used to unloading the alum from the car tank to the storage tank.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Supports and thicknesses

  • Parallel v block

  • Hexagonal socket or elbowed wrenchs (16,23,30mm)

  • Wrenches (8,13,17and 19 mm)

  • Puller for pins      3,4,6,8mm

  • Bearing extractor

  • Pair of levers

  • Monkey wrench

  • Hammer

  • Rubber hummer

  • Twist drill        3,4,6,8 mm

  • Vernier slide gauge

  • Emery cloth

  • Surface gauge.

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check pump capacity, pressure and power .  

  2. Check the Noisy of the Pump.

  3. Check the pump and piping for leaks

  4. Check the fixation

Starting the Pump

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  1. Check the direction of rotation of motor before starting; it is clockwise

  2. The level of liquid in the tank must be above the minimum level

  3. The on –off valve  in the suction from the tank ,must be completely open, while the air discharge valve after complete priming – if applicable-and the draining valve must be closed.

  4. The control valve in delivery to the pump , must be partially-but never completely closed

  5. The check must be carried out be qualified and competent  operators , starting the motor for a few second

Attendance and checking during operation of the pump, the following should be checked:

  1. Check of flow rate , delivery head and absorbed power

  2. Check the water leakage of pump

  3. Check the pump pressure

  4. Check the bolts of foundation

  5. Check  alignment and record at M.R.D.S.

  6. Check coupling .

  7. Check vibration .
