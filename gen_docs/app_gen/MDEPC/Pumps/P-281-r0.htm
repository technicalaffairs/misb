Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Straight edge, rugs, cleaning fluid.

Work to be carried out 

Pump in Operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure

  3. Check any leakage from pump & piping

  4. Measure bearing temperature

  5. Check mechanical seal for leakage and re-adjust (if it is needed). 

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check the validity of oil and add or change it (if it is needed).

  2. Check flanges for leakage and re-tighten ( if it is needed ).

  3. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tighten it (if it is needed).

  5. Clean external part with compressed air.
