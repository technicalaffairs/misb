Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed every on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

This pump is used to transfer the water from the back wash collection sump to the waste collection sump.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, straight edge, rags.

Work to be carried out

Pump in Operation

  1. Check pump capacity, pressure and power .

  2. Check the noise of the pump.

  3. Measure vibration level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyse it and determine what to do

  4. Check the ttemperature of bearing

  5. Check the leakage of the mechanical seal and piping leak . 

Pump out of operation

  1. Check alignment and adjust if needed

  2. Check the fixation

  3. Adjust mechanical seal 

Starting the Pump

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  1. Check the direction of rotation of motor before starting; it is clockwise

  2. Check the oil level in the oiler

  3. Filling the pump casing with the liquid

Attendance and checking during operation of the pump, the following should be checked:

  1. Check of flow rate and delivery head

  2. Check the mechanical seal for leaks

  3. Check the pump pressure
