Introduction

This pump is used to transfer the coolant water from Nile River to condenser for condensing the steam from turbine to condensate water.  This document outlines the major maintenance which must be performed every 5 Years as indicated by Y5 in above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued &qualified maintenance staff should carry  the suitable safety category.

  • Wear safety helmets – shoes – gloves , etc .

  • Use safety fence ( Rope ) with caution marks surrounding the work area  .

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor .

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor .

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves .

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid & rages

  • Feeler gauge & dial indicator

  • Screw driver set

Work to be carried out

Pump in service

  1. Check discharge and pump pressure then compare it with the setting value and replace the worn - out components (if it is needed)

  2. Measure vibrations level for bearing and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do (if it is needed)

Pump out of service

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value and record it

  2. Make test for bearings oil and add or change it (if it is needed)

  3. Check flanges for water leakage

  4. Check bolts fixation then re-tightens (if it is needed)

  5. Check glands for water leakage and re-pack (if it is needed)

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint it

Dissassembly refer to fig (1)

  • Before proceeding with the disassembly of the bowl assembly measure and record the end play of the shaft .

  1. Remove suction case plug from bottom of suction case.

  2. Slide suction case  over the lower end of the pump shaft ,suction case bearing will slide off with the suction case.

  3. Loosen set screw on suction case sand collar and slide sand collar and grease sea lover lower end of pump shaft.

  4. Use driver tool to drive impeller lock cullet toward top of bowl assembly .

  5. Slide impeller over lower end of pump shaft.

  6. Place blade of screw driver in the split on the cullet end slide the cullet over lower end of pump shaft .

  7. Remove bolts from bowl flange .

  8. Slide pump bowl over lower end of pump shaft.

  9. Repeat step (5) through (6) to remove remainder of bowls when top bowl is removed top bowl bearing and seal  if used , will slide off with the bowl.

  10. Slide discharge case over lower end of pump shaft when dies charge case bearing or discharge case cap will slide off with the case .

Inspection

  1. Inspect & examine impeller s for signs of rubbing or chipping .

  2. Inspect the ball bearing for damage or change it (if it is needed) .

  3. Inspect the wearing rings for wear or damage .

  4. Inspect the shaft for trueness with a dial indicator it must be true within (0.004 inches) .

  5. Check shaft run –out (not exceed 0.002 inches) .

Assembly refer to fig no ( 1)

  1. Screw the positioning plug into the lower end of the suction case and lay I horizontal position .

  2. Insert shaft fully into suction case and lock against positioning plug by tightening3/8”bolt on the positioning plug.

  3. Slide the grease seal dawn the shaft with the lip of the seal pointing away from bottom of the pump seat it firmly and evenly in the recessed housing at the top of the suction case .

  4. Slide the sand culler down the shaft and turn it back and forth until it fits fully down over the suction case bearing housing then tighten set screw .

  5. Slide impeller down pump shaft until it rests in the recess at the top of the suction case .

  6. Place screw driver blade in split in impeller cullet and slide cullet down shaft until it rests in the impeller ,work the cullet by hand as far into the impeller or as possible.

  7. Use the impeller lock cullet driver to drive the cullet securely between the impeller and pump shaft .

  8. Remove cullet driver from shaft and slide bowl down shaft until it mates with the top of the suction case.

  9. Turn pump bowl until ribbing on bowl lines up with ribbing on suction case .

  10. Insert and uniformly tighten bolts .

  11. Repeat step (5) &(8) until all bowls are in place.

  12. Slide the discharge case (16)down the shaft slowly until it mates with the top bowl .

  13. Insert and uniformly tighten bolts remove 3/8” bolt from bottom of positioning plug and screw grease fitting into the hole and inject a good grade of insoluble grease.

  14. Work shaft up and down several times to remove excess grease.

  15. Remove the positioning plug and insert the suction case plug .

  16. After bowl assembly is completely reason-pled measure end play of shaft and compare against reading obtained after step nominal end play dimensions .

Starting the pump

  • Before starting the pump the following steps should be checked:

  1. Check the direction of rotation of the  pump for starting the pump for the first .

  2. Check the quantity of oil in the thrust ball –bearing pump.

  3. Open the suction and delivery valves of the pump.

  4. Connect electric terminal of the pump motor.

  5. Put the circuit breaker of the pump motor in service.

Attendance and checking during operation

  1. Check the bearing temp.

  2. Check the pump suction & discharge pressure.

  3. Check the vibration of the pump and record it .
