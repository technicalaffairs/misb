Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 , which must be performed for condensate  pump every  six months as indicated by M6 in above title block .

This pump is used for lubricating bearings and to provide seal Oil to the generator shaft seals.

Safety Precautions

  • A work  permit  must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Wear safety helmets – shoes – gloves , etc   .

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor .

  • Use safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area  .

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves  .

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor .

  • Be sure pump  must be completely drainage.

  • Shut down the unit .

  • Generator should be isolated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set .

  • Cleaning fluid manual puller &rags.

  • Feeler gauge &  dial indicator with magnetic base.

  • Screw driver set.

Work to be carried out

Pump In Service

  1. Check discharge and pump pressure then , compare it with the setting  value and replace the worn – out components ( if it is needed ) .

  2. Measure vibrations level for bearing and compare it with the setting value , then analyze it and determine what to do ( if it is needed )

 Pump Out of Service

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value and record it.

  2. Make test for bearings oil and add or change it ( if it is needed ) .

  3. Check flanges for water leakage  .

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens ( if it is needed ) .

  5. Check glands for water leakage and re-pack ( if it is needed ) .

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint it .
