Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes breathing mask  ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction values

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid. Manual puller

  • Feeler gauge dial indicator

  • Vibrometer straight edge rags

Work to be carried out 

Pump in operation

  1. Checks pump capacity, pressure and power.

  2. Check the Noisy of the Pump.

  3. Measure vibration level for bearings and compare it with the setting value then analyze it and determine what to do

  4. Check the temperature of bearing

  5. Check the leakage of the mechanical seal

 Pump out of operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value

  2. Adjust mechanical seal  leakage

  3. Check the pump and piping flanges for Leaks and Re-tight if needed.

  4. Check the bolts fixation

  5. Check pump from rust Re-paint if needed

Starting the pump

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  1. Check the direction of rotation of motor before starting.

  2. Check the oil level in the oiler

  3. Filling the pump casing with the liquid

Attendance and checking during operation of the pump, the following should be checked

  1. Check of flow rate and pressure of pump

  2. Check the mechanical seal for leaks

  3. Check that the current absorbed by the motor
