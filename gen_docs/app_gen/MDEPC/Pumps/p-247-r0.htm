Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection of turbine oil pump, which must be performed every six  months as indicated  by M6 in above title block.

Lubrication oil pump is used for lubricate bearing 1-2-3 coupling 1-2, accessory gears and reduction gears.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Wear safety and healthy, shoes and gloves pairs...etc.

  • Unit out of service.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

Tools and Equipments

  • Complete maintenance tool set. 

  • Cleaning fluid, Rag

 

 

Work to be carried out

Pump in Service

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure of lube oil  pump and record it.

  2. Check the vibration level of the of lube oil pump and record it.

  3. Check mechanical seal for leakage.

Pump out of Service

  1. Check the alignment between the pump and the motor

  2. Check the bearing for the pump and refill by grease

 Check pump  during operation:-

  1. Check Lube oil pressure and discharge of the pump .

  2. Check Vibration for lube oil pump and record it.
