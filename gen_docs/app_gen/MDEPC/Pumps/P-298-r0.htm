Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Straight edge, rugs, cleaning fluid.

Work to be carried out 

Pump in operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure. and compare it with the setting value

  3. Check Any Leakage From Pump& Piping flanges and re-tight ( if it is needed ) .

  4. Measuring bearing temperature

  5. Check glands for leaking .

Pump out of operation

  1. Check alignment and Adjust it According to  the setting value.

  2. Check transmission components.

  3. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).

  4. Adjust glands for leaking then re-tightens it (if it is needed). 

  5. Adjust bolts fixation, then re-tightens it (if it is needed).

  6. Clean external part with compressed air

  7. Adjust greasing in bearing housing

Starting of pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump before starting the pump for the first time.

  2. Open delivery valve gradually after pump operate to its full speed.  

Attendance and checking during operation

  1. Check the temperature of the bearing

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure

  3. Check the vibration of the pump
