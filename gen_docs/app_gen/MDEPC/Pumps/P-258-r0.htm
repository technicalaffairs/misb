Introduction

This document outlines the equipment major maintenance (P1 ), which must be carried out every year which indicated by ( Y1 ) in the little block.

This pump is used to transfer hydrazine and ammonia to the boiler

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Shut down the pump.

  • Disconnect the power supply & rack out the circuit breaker.

  • Put red tag on the pump.

  • Close the shut–off valves in the  (suction & discharge).

  • Let the pumps cool down to about < 80 ºC measured at the branches, then seal off the cooling water pipes and close all other supply lines.

  • The safety department must check the isolation and surrounding the zone of work.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of tools.

  • Crane.

  • Alignment indicators, filler.

  • Grease gun.

  • Cleaning fluid.

Work to be carried out 

Pump in serves

  1. Check noise

  2. Check vibration.

  3. Check foundation.

  4. Check looses of bolts.

Pump out of serves (Dismantling)

  1. Disconnect the motor electrically.

  2. Isolate the pump ( Close the isolating valves ) and drain it as completely as possible.

  3. Disconnect the auxiliary piping ( water cooling ).

  4. Loosen all auxiliary lines, such as circulation, cooling liquid, lube oil.

  5. Uncoupling pump from motor and disconnect any safety devices as well as pressure and temperature measuring instrumentation.

  6. Disconnect pump from suction and discharge piping.

  7. Remove coupling spacer.]

  8. Drain the oil.

  9. Remove the casing – adapter frame nuts.

  10. Pull out the complete bearing housing with rotor.

  11. Carry the complete bearing housing with rotor eventually to the workshop to continue dismantling.

Steps of maintenance:

  1. Check dosing rate and tightness. In case of a plunger leakage, replace packing

  2. The gear oil should be changed (Gear oil : ISO viscosity class VG 150, mobil gear 629)

  3. All filters should be checked and cleaned

  4. The parts are broken should be replaced

  5. Check the bearing and changing if necessary

  6. Check the wear rings

  7. Check smooth slid.

Assembling

  1. The steps of assembling are the reverse steps of dismantling.

  2. Check alignment and free rotation by hand of pump and driver shaft
