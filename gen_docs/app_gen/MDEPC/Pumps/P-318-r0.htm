Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes breathing mask  ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Straight edge, rugs, cleaning fluid

Work to be carried out 

Pump in operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure. and compare it with the setting value

  3. Check Any Leakage From Pump & Piping flanges and re-tight (if it is needed)

  4. Measuring bearing temperature

  5. Check glands for leaking then re-tight (if it is needed)

Pump out of operation

  1. Check greasing bearings house

  2. Replace rubber pads in coupling

  3. Replace gland packing

  4. Check alignment and compare it whit the setting value

  5. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).

  6. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens it (if it is needed).

  7.  Clean external part with compressed air

Starting the Pump

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  1. Open the suction and delivery valves of the pump.

  2. Connect electric terminal of the pump motor.

  3. Check the direction of rotation.

Attendance and checking during operation

  1. Check the temperature of the bearing

  2. Check the vibration of the pump

  3. Check discharge quantity and pressure
