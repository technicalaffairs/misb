Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller

  • Band wrench for stator assembly

  • Chain wrench for stator assembly

  • Stator clamp for stator assembly

  • Stator assembly device

  • Set hexagon socket screw

  • Set pin extractor

  • Torque wrench

  • Caliper gauge with depth scale

  • Assembly pincers for circlip

  • Filling device for gear joints

  • Packing screw

Work to be carried out

    Pump in operation

  1. Check pump capacity, pressure and power .

  2. Check the noise of the pump.

  3. Measure vibration level for bearings and compare it with the setting value then analyze it and determine what to be

  4. Check the temperature of bearing

  5. Check the leakage of the mechanical seal

Pump out of operation

  1. Check alignment and adjust if needed

  2. Check the fixation

    Starting the Pump

    Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  1. Filling the pump casing with the medium, in case of high viscosity media fill with the liquid, pump priming is vital to ensure lubrication of the rubber stator

  2. Check the direction of rotation of motor before starting it is clockwise

  3. Open the valves and vents

 

Attendance and checking during operation of the pump, the following should be checked:

  1. Check of flow rate and delivery head

  2. Check the mechanical seal for leaks

  3. Check the pump pressure
