Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 , which must be performed for condensate  pump every six months as indicated by M6 in above title block .

This pump supply hydraulic oil for steam valves.

Safety Precautions

  • Issue work order Including the team work names .

  • Wear safety helmets – shoes – gloves , etc .

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor .

  • Use safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area  .

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor .

  • Be sure pump  must be completely drainage .

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set .

  • Cleaning fluid manual puller .

  • Feeler gauge &  dial indicator .

  • Rags

-        

Work to be carried out

Pump In Service

  1. Record pump pressure reading .

  2. Check discharge quantity .

  3. Check and record the vibration level of the pump.

 Pump out of Service

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value .

  2. Inspect of tapered roller &needle bearing oil and add or change it ( if it is needed)

  3. Check flanges for oil leakage  .

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens ( if it is needed ) .

  5. Check the piston & rotor for wear or damage.

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint it ( if it is needed ) .

  7. Inspect the end play of ball socket joint in piston and shoe for wear or damage
