Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes breathing mask  ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid. Manual puller.

Work to be carried out 

Pump in operation

  1. Check pump capacity, pressure and power

  2. Check the noisy of the pump.

  3. Check the level of oil

  4. Check pump leaks

Pump out of operation

  1. Adjust oil level

  2. Check the fixation of bolt

  3. Adjust the pump and piping for leaks

 

Starting the Pump

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  1. Open suction and delivery valve

  2. Check the direction of rotation of motor before starting it is clockwise

Attendance and checking during operation of the pump the following should be checked

  1. Check of flow rate , delivery head and absorbed power.

  2. Check the water leakage of pump

  3. Check the pump noise
