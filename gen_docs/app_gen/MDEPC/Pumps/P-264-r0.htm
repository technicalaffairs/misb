Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, dial indicator

  • Vibro meter, straight edge, rags

Work to be carried out 

Pump in operation

  1. Check pump capacity, pressure and power .

  2. Check the Noisy of the Pump.

  3. Measure vibration level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do.

  4. Check the Temperature of bearing .

  5. Check the leakage  of the mechanical seal. 

Pump out of operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Adjust  the mechanical seal  leakage.

  3. Adjust piping for leak.

  4. Check the fixation.

 

Starting the Pump

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  1. Check the direction of rotation of motor before starting; it is clockwise.

  2. The check must be carried out be qualified and competent  operators , starting the motor for a few second.

  3. Filling the pump casing with the liquid.

  4. Fill suction line with  liquid and completely prime pump.

  5. Turn rotation element by hand to make sure it rotates freely.

  6. Tighten plugs in gauge and drain taps.

  7. Check suction and discharge piping for leaks , and make sure for flange bolts are securely tighten need

Attendance and checking during operation of the pump, the following should be checked:

  1. Check of flow rate and delivery head.

  2. Check the shaft seal for leaks.

  3. Check that the current absorbed by the motor does not exceed the rated value shown on the name plate.

  4. Check the pump pressure.

  5. Install coupling guard on flexible coupled unit.

  6. Fully open gate valve (if any ) in suction line , and close gate valve in discharge line.

  7. Check discharge piping for pressure leaks.
