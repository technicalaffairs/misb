Introduction

This pump is used to make back wash flow on travel screen trays to clean it from any contaminations.  

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be carried out every month as indicated by M1 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Straight edge, rugs, cleaning fluid.

Work to be carried out 

PUMP in operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings.

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure.

  3. Check Any Leakage From Pump & Piping flanges

  4. Measuring bearing temperature

  5. Check glands for leaking

PUMP OUT OF OPERATION

  1. Check the validity of oil and add or change it (if it is needed). .

  2. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint  (if it is needed).

  3. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens it (if it is needed).

  4.  Clean external part with compressed air

  5. Adjust glands for leaking then re-packing it (if it is needed). 

 

Starting of Pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump before starting the pump for the first time.

  2. Open delivery valve gradually after pump operate to its full speed.  

 

Attendance and Checking during Operation

  1. Check the temperature of the bearing

  2. Check the oil level in bearing

  3. Check the vibration of the pump

  4. Check discharge quantity and pressure
