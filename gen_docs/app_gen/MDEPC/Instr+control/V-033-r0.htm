Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, Helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses)

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on the work place, safety rope around the work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Brushes

  • AVO meter

  • Blower

  • Cleaning fluids such as Freon - TF chlorothene

  • Allen key

  • Screws

Work to be carried out 

Inspection and maintenance of pneumatic control valve positioned

  1. Check the supply voltage (380 V AC)

  2. Check the feed back and command signal

  3. Check opening and close the valve

  4. Actuator cleaning

  5. Close the work permit
