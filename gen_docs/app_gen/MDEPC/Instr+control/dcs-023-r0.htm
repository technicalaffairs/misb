Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block.

The AB121 is an analogue acquisition module used to acquire 8 low level measurements from 100 W/0C˚ (3wire) platinum resistance temperature detectors (RTDs) It isolates and pre-processes the signals for the CE 2000

Operation

The AB121 module includes:

  • An analogue interface consisting of:

  • A switching converter

  • Eight low-level inputs (RTC0 to RTD7), each fitted with a filtering and portecting network and eight RTD power supplies

  • A 0 V test channel and a full-scale test channel

  • A channel reserved for calibration

  • A static multiplexing circuit

  • A measuring chain (voltage/frequency converter, and amplifier)

  • An intelligent unit consisting of a micro-controller with the following:

  • EPROM/RAM/EEPROM

  • A clock with a maximum frequency of 16 MHZ

  • Three independent 16-bit counters,

  • An analogue interface control circuit,

  • An interface with the 2000 I/O backplane bus, consisting of:

  • A circuit for plugging in with power on,

  • A 2000 I/O bus slave interface,

The AB120 performs the measurement acquisition, in the course of which it:  

  • Scans the inputs cyclically,

  • Detects open lines (sensor disconnection),

  • Corrects the offset or gain drift errors,

  • Linearizes the measurements

  • Converts the measurements into physical quantities (°C)

The AB 121 module

  • Tests its own circuits,

  • Converses with the higher level via the 2000 I/O bus,

 

Controls and indications on the front panel

A green LED on the front panel indicates that the module is working. It goes out in the following cases:

  • The CE2000 rack PU does not have access to the module,

  • The module has detected a hardware fault, 

  • The module has detected a user fault: loss of the process voltage, disconnection of a channel.

Protection

General protection on the process voltage +Vp by CTP with maximum tripping current at 20°C: 0.45 A.

Safety Precautions

  • Caution marks must be hanged on the work place, safety rope around the work place.
