Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block.

The LC105A is a logic output module providing 16 make contacts supplied by the positive polarity of the process voltage. The outputs are current-sourcing outputs.

Dimension:

Height: 233 mm Width: 1 slot = 25.4 mm
Depth: 220 mm Weight 0.420 kg

Operation :

The LC105A module includes:

  • A process interface consisting of:

  • 16 logic outputs based on monostable relays with make contacts, individually protected against short-circuits.

  • A process voltage distribution and monitoring circuit,

  • A short-circuit detection circuit

  • An interface with the 2000 I/O backplane consisting of:

  • A circuit for plugging in with power on,

  • A 2000 I/O bus slave interface,

  • Input-output registers,

Controls and Indications on The Front Panel

A green LED on the front panel indicates that the module is working.

It remains on if the module is operating correctly and goes off in case of faulty operation (fault detected in the configuration, hardware or use of the module).

Output Elements

The module includes 16 logic outputs built from monostable relays with make contacts. Each relay winding is energized by two transistors, one providing the +12V voltage and the other the 0V. The controls of these transistors are separated and complemented.

The risk of an untimely output is thus very low.

A protection against overloads is connected in series with each contact.

Process Voltage

The process voltage is supplied by the backplane connector (distributed voltage), or by the process connector (external voltage).

A logic input based on an opto-isolator indicates the presence of the process voltage.

Protection

General protection on the process voltage +Vp by CTP with maximum tripping current at 20°C: 0.45 A.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses)

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on the work place, safety fence around the work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set, voltammeter

- Process

  • Voltage 48 V (38.4 V to 57.6 V)
