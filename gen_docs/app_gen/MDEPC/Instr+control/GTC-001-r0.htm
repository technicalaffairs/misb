Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes

  • Turbine must be shut downed

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Ammeter

  • Screw Driver

  • PG 720 (PC)

  • Air cleaner

Work to be carried out

Steps to Prepare for Test

  1. Review technical reference materials and manufacture's manual

  2. Review the light in cabinet CJR01 (turbine control)

  3. Review the temperature of cabinet by check the fan and clean the fan

 

 

Testing Procedures

  1. Check supply voltage (24 VDC)

  2. Visual inspection of the cabinet, incl CPU modules and terminals

  3. Check cabling incl, shielding and earthing

  4. Check the 7-segment indicator for error messages

  5. Check CPU battery and change if necessary

  6. Connect PG 720 with master processor

  7. Check the required values for turbine control valves

Return Control cabinet to service

  1. Disconnect the PG 720 (PC) from master processor

  2. Close the cabinet CIR01
