Introduction

This document outlines the checklist I1 for all types of pressure switches, which must be performed every year as indicated  by Y1 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • Close manual valve for indicator line

Tools and Equipments

  • Pressure tester (druck - dpi610)

  • Screw driver

  • Set of key

Work to be carried out

Steps to prepare for test

  1. Review technical reference material and manufacture’s manual.

  2. Obtain copy of indicator setting values record it in its MRDS.

  3. Open indicator vent

  4. Connect indicator tester to indicator vent

  5. Take reading of 0% & 25 % & 50% & 75% & 100% and record in data sheet

Calibrating procedure

  1. Injection the suitable pressure value reference to setting and record at MRDS, check if the reading is correct .

  2. If there is any error, adjust the pressure indicator and repeat step 1 until reach the correct reading .

Return indicator to Service

  1. Return the pressure indicator to service by removing the pressure tester .

  2. Return the pressure indicator back to its location .

  3. Open manual valve for pressure indicator line .

  4. Place a copy of test result in the field maintenance file .
