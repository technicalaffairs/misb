Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, Helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses)

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on the work place, safety rope around the work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Hart device

  • AVO meter

  • Blower, brushes

  • Cleaning fluids such as Freon - TF chlorothene

Work to be carried out 

Inspection and maintenance of P.T. 

  1. Make calibration procedures to The pressure transmitters

  2. Check the supply voltage (24 v dc)

  3. Check the output (4-20 m AMP)

  4. Transmitter cleaning

  5. Close the work permit
