Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, Helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses).

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on the work place, safety rope around the work place.

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Brushes  measuring tool.

  • Blower.

  • Screw driver.

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check the carbon brushes.

  2. Check the carbon brushes length during operation without removing the brush holder by inserting a gage stick of insulating materials into the bore of the telescopic plunger. The gage stick is provided with several notch marks showing the useful length of the carbon brush.

  3. By visual inspection, make sure that no spark (brushes are matched with slip rings).
