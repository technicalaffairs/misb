Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 for thyristor element which must be performed on yearly bases or during outage as indicated in the title block.

Technical Specifications:

Quantity

18 (3parrallel bridges)

Type

N880C

Peak reverse voltage

3000 V

Number of parallels

3(including 1 redundant)

Cooling method

Forced air cooling (100% redundant fan)

Reverse voltage

2800 V

Surge on current

50 Ka

Junction temperature

-40Cº ~125 Cº

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses).

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on the work place, safety rope around the work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Brushes 

  • Blower.

  • Screw driver.

 

 

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check the resistance between the gate and cathode (RGK) or between the anode and cathode (RAK) and compare with criteria value

  2. Retighten the gate socket

  3. Clean around the thyristor tray

  4. Exchange between the cooling fans
