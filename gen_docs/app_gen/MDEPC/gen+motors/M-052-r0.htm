Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • Isolate the power breaker feeding the motor

Tools and Equipment

  • Tester (digital Avometer).

  • Set off spanners and screw drivers.

  • 250/500 V Megger .

  • Vacuum cleaner.

  • Air blower

Work to be carried out

During motor isolation

  1. Check motor terminal box.

  2. Check motor fan blades and fan cover.

  3. Measure the winding insulation resistance using Megger

  4. Check motor control cabinet.

  5. General cleaning the surface and air filter by blower

 

 

 

During motor operation

  1. Check motor bearing sound and lubricate the bearing as per motor manual.

  2. Check motor temperature, current, voltage and vibration.

  3. Clean motor air filters
