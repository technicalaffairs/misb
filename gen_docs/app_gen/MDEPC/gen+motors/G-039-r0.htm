Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Blower, Brushes.

  • Cleaning fluids as Freon - TF, Chlorothene.

  • Digital multi-meters.

  • Sliding contacts.

  • Brushes measuring tools.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check bearing insulation resistance

  2. Check Slip Rings for exciter current transmission on contamination and damage. Finish turn or grind out of true or non faced slip rings.

  3. Check grounding shaft brushes

  4. Inspect make up air filter for contamination

  5. Check generator slip ring brushes.

  6. Check neutral point thermometer temperature.

  7. Check operating air coolers on proper ventilation.
