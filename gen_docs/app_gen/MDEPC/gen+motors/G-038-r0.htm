Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Blower, Brushes.

  • Cleaning fluids as Freon - TF, Chlorothene.

  • Measuring brushes tool.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check grounding shaft brushes

  2. Inspect make up air filter for contamination

  3. Check generator slip ring brushes.

  4. Check neutral point thermometer temperature.

  5. Check operating air coolers on proper ventilation.
