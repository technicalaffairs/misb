Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  •  The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified ,and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety helmet  .

  • The unit must be out of service

 

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • Digital voltmeter and clamp ammeter.

  • Vacuum cleaner and air blower, brushes

  • Mugger 500 - 5000 V

  • Measuring brushes tool

Work to be carried out 

Stator

  1. Measure insulation resistance of stator winding (not less than 2 G Ω)

  2. Implementation of insulation test (high voltage test)

  3. Charge and discharge measurement

  4. Tan delta measurement

  5. Partial discharge measurement

  6. Check stator winding condition (visual)

  7. Check of flexible strips

  8. Check condition of stator core (El. CID)

  9. Check condition of stator core (visual)

  10. RTD - insulation resistance test

Rotor

  1. Check for true running

  2. Check rotor wedges and retaining (visual)

  3. Check rotor end winding area

  4. Check air escape slots

  5. Measure in solution resistance of rotor winding

  6. Voltage drop test on slip ring supply lead

  7. Check bearing contact faces

  8. Impedance test

Excitation system

  1. Dismantle collector cover

  2. Check insulation resistance of slip rings

  3. Check bearing and pipe insulation

  4. Check bearing surfaces and Babbitt bounding

  5. Check bearing clearance

  6. Check labyrinth rings and if required replace seal strips

  7. Check air filter and if required replace filter cartridge

  8. Check condition and performance of measuring devices

  9. Check condition and performance of brush carrier and carbon brushes

  10. Clean slip rings and insulation components

  11. Check condition of slip rings

Coolers

  1. Check cooling water inlet and outlet pipes

  2. Check vent pipes

  3. Check mounting of all coolers

  4. Check condition and performance of temperature measuring devices

  5. Check condition and performance of pressure measuring device

Air cooler

  1. Check and replace if required clean air cooler on waterside

  2. Check the leakage from air filter in the cooler cover

Bearing

  1. Check and replace if required bearing insulation

  2. Check bearing surfaces and Babbitt bounding (dyepenetratcandus)

  3. Check bearing clearances

  4. Check labyrinth rings and replace if required replace seal strips

  5. Check condition and performance of bearing temperature monitoring system

  6. Check condition and performance of shaft lift oil system

  7. Check condition of potential instrument wires

Generator Supervisory Equipment

  1. Check of condition and performance of temperature gauges and alarms

  2. Check condition and performance of liquid level alarm switches

  3. Check condition and performance of grounding brushes
