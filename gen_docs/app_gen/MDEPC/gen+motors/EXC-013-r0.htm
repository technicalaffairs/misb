Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, Helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses).

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on the work place, safety rope around the work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Brushes 

  • Blower.

  • Screw driver.

  • Megger (250-500 V)

 

Work to be carried out 

Check power supply for each panel

  1. Surge absorber panel.

  2. Thyristor panel.

  3. Field C.B panel.

Check circuit wiring for:-

  1. Surge absorber panel.

  2. Thyristor panel.

  3. Field CB panel

Measurement of insulation resistance of control and alarm circuit for:-

  1. Surge absorber panel.

  2. Thyristor panel.

  3. Field CB panel.

Check for any loose connection cables or parts

Clean all panels

  1. Surge absorber panel.

  2. Thyristor panel.

  3. Field C.B panel.

Check of field circuit breaker.

Check the thyristor fans

Inspection of exterior surface paint
