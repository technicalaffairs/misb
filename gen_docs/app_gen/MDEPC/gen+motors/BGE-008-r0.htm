Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment.

  • The unit must be on service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc

  • Digital voltmeter and clamp ammeter.

  • New brushes.

  • Useful brush length.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the brush vibration.

  2. Check the sparking brushes.

  3. Check brush holders.

  4. Replace the brush by a new one if its not fall below 6A/cm2 (=60A per brush) and not exceed 12A/cm2 (=120A per brush).

  5. Record current and voltage value of generator as in MRDS and compare the results with  the standard value fig (1).
