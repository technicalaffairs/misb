Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 for gas Turbine Generator excitation system, which must be performed on every yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block or during any stoppage.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used (safety shoes, Helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses).

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained and qualified.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on the work place, safety rope around the work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Brushes 

  • Blower

  • Screw driver.

  • Megger (250-500 V)

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check power supply for each panel                      

  2. Check circuit wiring

  3. Measure the insulation resistance of control and alarm circuit.

  4. Retighten the wiring and general cleaning by blower with small pressure.

  5. Clean the bars and check coil by blower.
