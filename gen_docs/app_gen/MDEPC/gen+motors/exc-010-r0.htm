Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety helmet.

  • The unit must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • Digital voltmeter.

  • Laptop.

  • Vacuum cleaner, Blower

  • Snaps.

Work to be Carried out

  1. Visual check of condition.

  2. Check of all Screw terminals.

  3. Analysis of alarms and alarm report.

  4. Check alarms and repeated alarms.

  5. Check electronics cabinet installed with voltage regulator equipment.

  6. Check all electronic cards

  7. Check Power Supply including:

    1. Incoming diode.

    2. Flow indicator.

    3. Dust filter.

    4. Clamp of circuit breaker.

  8. Check operator function from central control room.

  9. Check analog Signal inputs.

  10. The following items will done when the generator is full speed no load.

  11. Test Sequence of excitation on.

  12. Test and Record deexcitation.

  13. Test and possibly optimize step response voltage regulator.

  14. Test switch over Auto-Manual.
