Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  •  The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified ,and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety helmet  .

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • Digital voltmeter and clamp ammeter.

  • Measuring brushes tool

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check shaft ground brushes

  2. Check leak air filter in cooler cover on contamination

  3. Check operating air coolers on proper ventilation

  4. Check carbon brushes for excitation current transmission on damage or  wear, exchange  brushes if required .
