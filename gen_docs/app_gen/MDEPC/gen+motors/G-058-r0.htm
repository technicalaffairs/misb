Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  •  The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety helmet  .

  • The unit must be out of service

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • Digital voltmeter and clamp ammeter.

  • Vacuum cleaner and air blower, brushes

  • Mugger 500 - 5000 V

  • Measuring brushes tool

Work to be carried out 

Stator

  1. Measure insulation resistance of stator winding (not less than 2 G Ω)

  2. Dismantle bushing and replace gaskets between bushing and housing

  3. Dismantle stator end shields and replace all gaskets

  4. Check of flexible strips

  5. Check condition of stator core (El. CID)

  6. Check condition of stator core (visual)

  7. Replace all cable lead out plate gaskets

  8. Replace all generator flange gaskets

  9. Replace all gaskets of casing penetrations

  10. Check condition of sealing element between stator core and casing

  11. RTD insulation resistance test

  12. Implementation of insulation test (high voltage test)

  13. Charge and discharge measurement

  14. Tan delta measurement

  15. Partial discharge measurement

  16. Check stator winding condition (visual)

Rotor

  1. Check for true running

  2. Remove and check the rotor

  3. Check rotor wedges and retaining rings (visual)

  4. Check rotor ends winding area

  5. Check air escape slot

  6. Measure insulation resistance of rotor winding

  7. Voltage drop test on slip ring supply lead

  8. Check bearing contact faces

  9. Check rotor fan blades (visual)

  10. Check coupling flange

  11. Check contact pins and replace multi contact strips

  12. Check rotor alignment

  13. Impedance test

Excitation

  1. Dismantle collector covers

  2. Check insulation resistance of slip ring

  3. Check coupling flange

  4. Replace multi contact stripe

  5. Check alignment

  6. Check bearing and pipe insulation

  7. Replace bearing and pipe insulation

  8. Check bearing surfaces and Babbitt bounding

  9. Check bearing clearance

  10. Check seating of spherical portion of bearings

  11. Check labyrinth rings and if required replace seal strips

  12. Check air filter and if required replace filter cartridge

  13. Check condition performance of measuring devices

  14. Check condition performance of brush carrier and carbon brushes

  15. Clean slip rings and insulation components

  16. Check condition of slip rings

Coolers

  1. Check cooling water inlet and out let pipes

  2. Check vent pipes

  3. Check drain pipes

  4.  Check mounting of all coolers

  5. Check condition and performance of temperature measuring devices

  6. Check condition and performance of pressure measuring devices

Air cooler

  1. Check and if required clean air cooler on water side channels

  2. Check condition and if required recondition air cooler water channels

  3. Dismantle air cooler

  4. Check air cooler on water sides

  5. Check air cooler on air sides

  6. Replace gaskets

  7. Perform pressure tests

  8. Check the leakage air filters in the cooler cover

 

Bearings

  1. Check and if required replace bearing insulation

  2. Check bearing surfaces and Babbitt bonding (dye penetrate and us)

  3. Check bearing clearance

  4. Check seating of bearing sleeves on bearing saddles

  5. Check labyrinth rings and if required replace seal strips

  6. Check condition of performance shaft lift oil system

  7. Check condition potential instrument wires

Generator supervisory equipment

  1. Check of condition and performance of temperature gauges and alarms

  2. Replace gaskets at temperature measuring points

  3. Check condition and performance of liquid level alarm switches

  4. Check condition and performance of grounding brushes
