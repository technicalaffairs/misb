Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  •  The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified ,and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety helmet  .

  • The unit must be out of service

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set.

  • Digital voltmeter and clamp ammeter.

  • Vacuum cleaner and air blower, brushes

  • Megger 500 - 5000 V

  • Measuring brushes tool

Work to be carried out 

Stator

  1. Measure insulation resistance of stator winding (not less than 2 G Ω)

  2. Check stator winding condition (visual)

  3. Check the flexible strips

Rotor

  1. Check for true running

  2. Check rotor end winding area

  3. Measure insulation resistance of rotor winding (not less than 1M Ω )

  4. Voltage drop test on slip ring supply lead

  5. Impedance test

Excitation system

  1. Dismantle collector cover

  2. Check insulation resistance of slip rings

  3. Check bearing and pipe insulation

  4. Check air filter and if required replace filter cartridge

  5. Check condition and performance of measuring devices

  6. Check condition and performance of brush carrier and carbon brushes

  7. Clean slip rings and insulation components

  8.  Check condition of slip rings

Coolers

  1. Check cooling water inlet and out let pipes

  2. Check vent pipes

  3. Check drain pipes

  4. Check mounting of all coolers

  5. Check condition and performance of temperature measuring devices

  6. Check condition and performance of pressure measuring device

 

Air cooler

  1. Check the leakage air filter in the cooler cover

Bearing

  1. Check condition and performance of bearing temperature monitoring system

  2. Check condition and performance of shaft lift oil system

  3. Check condition potential instrument wires

Generator Supervisory Equipment

  1. Check of condition and performance of temperature gages and alarms

  2. Check condition and performance of liquid level alarm switches

  3. Check condition and performance of grounding brushes
