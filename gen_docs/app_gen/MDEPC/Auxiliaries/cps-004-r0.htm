Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 for cathodic protection systems and infrastructure in the area of influence of any new cathodic protection systems,  which must be performed on an annual bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • High input impedance voltmeter

  • Suitable half-cell (The standard practical half-cells are copper/copper sulphate, silver/silver chloride/seawater, silver/silver chloride/ potassium chloride and zinc).

  • Tester (ammeter).

 

Work to be carried out

 

Galvanic-anode outputs monitoring method

  1. Galvanic-anode outputs may be monitored, as can currents in electrical bonds between structures.

  2. Record the results and compare them with the previous data results.

  3. If the result is out of criteria range, we treat the damage or not good parts or replace them.
