Procedures

Check following for good function & condition

  1. Check external body and joints

  2. Check the disk & seat & leakage for the control valve

  3. Check the disk & seat & leakage for the manual valve

  4. Check the flanges & setting & pivots & glands

  5. Check the any corrode of body

  6. Check the external isolating covered

  7. Cleaning from ashes

  8. Check the all parts

  9. Assembling the element & closed the doors

  10. Check the condition (open & close)

  11. Adjusting

  12. Check after repairing
