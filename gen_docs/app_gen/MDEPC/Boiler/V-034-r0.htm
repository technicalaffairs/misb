Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes breathing mask ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the valve actuator.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the actuator.

  • Isolate the valve mechanically by closing isolating manual valves after and before (HV 001).

  • Be sure line must be completely drained.   

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set .

  • Special tools for valves Special wrench

  • Handled with lifting lugs

  • Straight edge , rugs, cleaning, fluid

  • Puller.

Work to be carried out 

   

Steps of the dismantling valve (Figure 1)

  1. Disconnect electric and( IC) terminal of the actuator.

  2. Disconnect  the actuator.

  3. Unscrew the nuts from the gland bolts and remove the gland bolts. Part no 15.

  4. Unscrew retaining nut part no 19 .

  5. Loosen  the yoke 22 to end to remove parts no 20&19&18+positoner.

  6. Unscrew body bolts part no14 &15.

  7. Remove the packing washer part no.8 and segments part no4.

  8. Lift the bonnet carefully to keep the seal ring graphite part no10.

  9. Lift the wedge and stem part no 4&5.

Inspection for Re-assembly 

  1. Thoroughly wipe all parts with solvent and clean cloth. 

  2. Examine the following parts for pitting or scratches: SEAT - Sealing surface &STEM - Packing area  &WEDGE - Sealing surface 

  3. Grease all threaded parts with molykote

  4. Change seal ring graphite.

 

 

Assembly of The Valve

  1. Insert the stem/wedge-assembly into the valve-body.

  2. Insert the seal ring in the bonnet .

  3. Insert the bonnet into the valve body (with markings )    

  4. Place the gland over the stem.  

  5. Put the yoke together with the gland-flange over the stem.

  6. Fix the gland-flange with the stud bolts.

  7. Fix the body bolts.

  8. Tighten the nuts of the studs bolts. (to compress the packing)

  9. Loosen the nuts of the stud bolts.

  10. Now again carefully tighten the nuts of the stud bolts.

  11. In case of leaking tighten the nuts of the gland bolts.  carefully, until leakage stops

  12. Insert the actuator.

  13. Connect the electric and (IC) terminal.
