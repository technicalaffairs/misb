Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every year as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Critical piping must be high dencty material and high pressure and temperature resistance & alloys high density material it is carbon steel & stainless steel & alloy steel & and must be transfer the fluid smooth resistance

Technical specification

Range of diameters (mm) 80-800
Range of pressure (bar) 2-156
Range of temperatures (Co) 102-567.7
Types of materials Carbon steel - alloy steel - stainless steel
Types of fluids Water & steam


Safety Precautions

  1. A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  2. Don't touch the lines within the operation

  3. Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask .... etc

Tools and Equipment

  1. Complete maintenance tool set

  2. Insulations tools

  3. Temperature sensor

  4. Straight edge, rags

Work to be carried out

HRSG in operation

  1. Check the pressure of critical piping

  2. Check the temperature of critical piping

  3. Check the damage insulations

  4. Check the valves and piping for leaks

  5. Check the fixation and supports

HRSG out off operation

  1. Check the pressure of critical piping

  2. Check the temperature of critical piping

  3. Check the fixation and supports
