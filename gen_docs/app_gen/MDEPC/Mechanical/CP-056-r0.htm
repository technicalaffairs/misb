Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I3, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above titled block. 

This compressor serves is compressing air for self-cleaning system which is using to clean the Gas Turbine air filters and to control opening and closing of inlet damper.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety, helmet, shoes

  • Compressor unit shutdown

  • Disconnect the power supply of the compressor

  • Isolate the compressor mechanically

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Compressed air hose

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags, soft brushes.… etc)

  • Soft brushes

  • Spray gun

Work to be carried out 

Compressor out in service

  1. Check  safety valve

  2. Inspect cooler for leaks clean it if necessary

  3. Inspect any leakage from compressor unit

  4. Check and inspect inlet and outlet valves and change any damage parts

Compressor out of service

  1. Remove small air filter clean it by air jet

  2. Remove dismantle and clean float valve of moisture trap

  3. Change the oil of the compressor

  4. Inspect tension belt and check pulleys and change it if necessary

  5. Adjust pressure of open and close safety valve

  6. Inspect cooler for leaks

  7. Remove air filter element and also small air filter, clean it by air jet inspect and change the damage one

  8. Remove dismantle and clean float valve of moisture trap

  9. Change the oil of the compressor

  10. Inspect tension belt and check pulleys and change it if necessary

  11. Grease the bearings of the motor fan and compressor motor
