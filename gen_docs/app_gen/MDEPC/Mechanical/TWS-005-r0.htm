Introduction

The Traveling water screen system is used for cleaning the Raw water from the impurities such as herbs, shells, papers and plastic bottles before

entering to the main cooling pump.

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be

 performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above titled block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmets shoes earplug and gloves pairs

  • Isolate the screen electrically and mechanically by close inlet valves. 

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor.

  • Close inlet valve for nozzle spray for this traveling screen

Tools and Equipment

  • Maintenance tool set

  • Grease (multi purpose grease)

  • Wires to raise up the screen cover

  • Cleaning fluid, rages

Work to be carried out 

Traveling water screen in service

  1. Check the validity the mesh of the screen wires.

  2. Check the sprockets of the traveling screen.

  3. Check the spray nozzles of clogging.

  4. Check the chains of roller drive from wear.

  5. Check pressure of cleaning water.

Traveling water screen out of service

  1. Check the validity of the roller ball bearings of the sprockets.

  2. Check flanges for water leakage.

  3. Check the hinges pins between screens holder.

  4. Check any wear of the washing nozzles.

  5. Check the bolts fixation to avoid the vibration.

  6. Check any wear in the drive and driven sprockets.

 

Preparing for operation

  1. Open valves

  2. Connect power for motor

  3. Operate the spray pump

  4. Operate the traveling screen
