Introduction

This compressor serves is compressing air for self-cleaning system which is using to clean the Gas Turbine air filters and to control opening and closing of inlet damper.

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the above titled block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety, helmet, shoes

  • Disconnect the power supply of the compressor

  • Isolate the compressor mechanically

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Compressed air hose

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags, soft brushes.… etc)

  • Soft brushes

  • Spray gun

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check  for oil leaks.

  2. Check the oil level and add oil if it is required.

  3. Clean unit by air jet.

  4. Remove small air filter element and clean it by air jet if necessary.
