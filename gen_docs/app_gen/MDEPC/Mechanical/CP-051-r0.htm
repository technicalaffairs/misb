Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Wear safety helmets - shoes - gloves, etc

  • Use safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the compressor motor

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the compressor motor

  • The compressor must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid

  • Feeler gauge & dial indicator & micrometer (outer & inner)

  • Screw driver set

Work to be carried out 

Compressor in service

  1. Check compressor discharge pressure then compare it with the setting value

  2. Check compressor flow rate

  3. Check compressor bearing temperature

  4. Check air temperature

  5. Check pressure safety valve for leakage

  6. Measure vibration level for compressor and compare it with the setting value

Compressor out of service

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value and adjust it if needed

  2. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens (if it is needed)

  3. Re-adjust pressure safety valve

  4. Check the compressor casing against rust and re-paint it
