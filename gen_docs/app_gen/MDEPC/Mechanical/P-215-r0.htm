Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be carried out on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the little block.

This pump is used to transfer water from feed water tank to economizer then to high pressure drum.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

    •

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses,

    •

  • Shut down the pump.

  • Disconnect the power supply & rack out the circuit breaker.

  • Put red tag on the pump.

  • Close the shut off valves in the (suction & discharge& balancing & min. flow).

  • Let the pumps cool down to about < 80oC measured at the branches, then seal off the cooling water pipes and close all other supply lines.

  • Loosen all auxiliary lines, such as circulation, cooling liquid, lube oil.

  • The safety department must check the isolation and surrounding the zone of work.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of tools.

  • Cleaning fluid, Rags.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check oil level and viscosity.

  2. Check heat.

  3. Check noise.

  4. Check vibration.

  5. Check packing.

  6. Check leakage of water.

  7. Check looses of bolts in foundation.
