Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I3, which must be performed on 8000 hr bases as indicated by W1 in the title block for air intake, cleaning air added to fuel and burnt in combustion chamber and hot gases are expanded in turbine.

Technical Specification

Quantity

4

Type

1st Stage coaleser filter (A1 Mg3_384 pcs 610X610X50 & 224 pcs 610X305X50)

2nd stage 768 pcs pulse filter ( conical CO-2612 & cylindrical CY-2612)

3rd stage 473 pcs fine filter (CAM GT - 242412-85_592X592X290)

Auxiliaries

Two pulse air compressors (atlas copco GA30)

192 double diaphragm valves VEP 416 - 24DC 2'

Alarm

12 mm bar

Trip

16 mm bar

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Close the main pulsing valve

  • Shutdown the compressor before replacing the oil

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set

  • Vacuum cleaner or blower

  • Equipments for oil filing

  • Cleaning clothes

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Monitoring of DP across each filter stage.

  2. Check leakage of double diaphragm valves.

  3. Inspection on weather entry screen for blockage of debris.

  4. Inspection of filter elements for fastening outer damages, contamination and alignment to frame.

  5. Inspection of drainage lines for damages and corrosion.

  6. Inspection of all flange connections for outer damage, tightness, and completeness of bolted joints.

  7. Inspection of doors at filter house for closing devices and sealing.

  8. Visible check of clean compressor inlet via MBL inspection windows.

  9. Change the filters according to DP.

  10. Check cleanness

  11. Change the oil and the oil filter for the pulse compressor.

  12. Change the air filter for the pulse compressor.

  13. Replace oil separator for the pulse compressor.

  14. Test safety valve.

  15. Test auto drain function for compressor and dryers.

  16. Filter check and connections check for air dryer.

  17. Leakage check on hoses, hose connection and container for oil water separator

  18. Check condensate level at sight glass for DD/PD filters.

  19. Test auto drain function for DD/PD filters.

  20. Test auto drain function for air receiver.
