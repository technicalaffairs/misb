Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the screen mechanically and electrically.  

Tools and Equipment

  • Maintenance tool set.

  • Wires to raise up the screen cover

TROUBLE SHOOTING CHART FOR DUAL FLOW TRAVELING WATER SCREEN 

MALFUNCTION

SYMPTOM

PROBABLE CAUSE

CORRECTIVE ACTION

SCREEN WILL NOT OPERATE

MOTOR RUNS BUT TRAYS DO NOT MOVE

Torque limiting coupling trips

Check path of chain and trays and remove any obstructions. Replace or repair any damaged parts torque coupling automatically resets. Restart screen

 

 

Drive belts broken or slipping.

Replace or re tension belts

 

 

Internal failure of gear reducer (input shaft turns, but output shaft does not turn)

Remove gear reducer & replace or repair

 

MOTOR STALLS

Wrong voltage applied to motor.

Have qualified electrician check motors & electrical connections

 

MOTOR WILL NOT RUN

Defective motor or improper electrical connection

Have qualified electrician check motor and electrical connections. Replace motor if defective.

 

 

Differential control malfunction

Have qualified electrician. Check differential control instruments and wiring by others.

TRAYS NOT CLEAN

CARRYOVER OF DEBRIS

Gaps in spray coverage due to clogged, worn, broken spray nozzle

Clean or replace sp ray nozzle

 

 

Misalignment of spray header

Align spray discharge by turning spray header pipe.

 

 

Insufficient volume or pressure or wash water due to clogged spray header or supply pipe

Clean spray header & supply pipe

 

 

Insufficient volume or pressure of wash water due to clogged strainer element in supply line

Remove strainer element, clean and reinstall (by others).

 

 

Insufficient volume or pressure of wash water due to defective pump.

Check wash water pump output

 

 

No wash water due to closed manual valve.

Open manual valve

 

 

No wash water due to automatic valve malfunction

Check operation of automatic valve. Replace or repair if defective (by others).

 

 

 

IMPROPER TRAY TRACKING

ERRATIC TRAY LINE MOVEMENT

Improper chain tension - too loose or too tight.

Adjust chain to proper tension

 

WEAR ON EDGE OF SEAL PLATES

Head shaft out of level

Check level of head shaft. Correct by adjust. Take -up

 

 

Frame steel out of plumb

Shim to straighten and plumb frame.

 

SPROCKET TEETH OR CHAIN SIDE BARS ON ONE SIDE

Chain not centered on sprockets

Check level of head shaft. Correct by adjust. Take -up

UNUSUAL DRIVE OPERATION

EXCESSIVE HEAT BUILD-UP

Low oil level or improper oil in gear reducer.

Add oil and refill w/proper grade & quantity of oil.

 

EFFECTIVE NOISE OR VIBRATION

Drive misalignment

Check path of chain & trays & remove any obstructions.

 

 

Defective gear reducer

Remove gear reducer & replace or repair
