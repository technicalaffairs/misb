Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be carried out on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the little block.

The high pressure evaporator is used for receiving water from high pressure circulating pump and rising its temperature & pressure (by taking heat from exhaust gases coming from gas turbine) then transfer it to high pressure steam drum

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • The unit is out of service.

  • Put red tag on the high pressure super heater.

  • The safety department must check the isolation and surrounding of the zone of work.

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Argon welding machine.

  • Grinder & grinding wheel.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Fill the high pressure section by filling water from feed water pump after closing drain and opening the vents.

  2. Inspect and adjust the leakage.

  3. Drain the high pressure evaporator

  4. Make hydraulic pressure test for the high pressure section by running the high pressure circulating pump to operating pressure (54 bar)

  5. Check that the distance between pipes is equal.

  6. Check the cleanliness of the surface of pipes.

  7. Check any cracks and use grinding and argon welding according to the manual.
