Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks to surround the work area.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as: safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs .... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Brushes

  • AVO meter

  • Blower

  • Cleaning fluids such as Freon – TF. chlorothene.

  • Allen key

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check the supply voltage(220 Vac)

  2. Check the feed back signal

  3. Clean the positioner

  4. Close the work permit
