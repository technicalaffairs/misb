Introduction

The condenser is receives the hot steam from the steam turbine and

condensate it to water using raw water. The raw water is flows through the

tubes of the condenser and the steam around it.

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed

every six months as indicated by M6  in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Unit must be shut down

  • Insulate inlet & outlet cooling water valves on the water box

  • Open drain and vent valves of water box to drain cooling water

  • Fill the hot well with demy. Water to be above tubes level .

  • Be sure the cooling raw water must be completely drained .

  • Open manholes of condenser to check any leakage .

 Tools and Equipment

  • Maintenance tool set

  • Grease (multi purpose grease)

  • Cleaning fluid, rages

  • Brushes

Work to be carried out 

 

Check, inspection and treatment:

 

  1. Fill the steam side of the condenser with water and test the condenser tubes for water leakage

  2. Leaky tubes should be plugged with copper dowels we should to take into consideration that max. plugged tubes not more than 10% of the total tubes

  3. Clean the condenser tubes with water and brushes

  4. Close inspection manholes

  5. Close drain valves

  6. Open inlet& outlet cooling water valves to fill the water box

  7. Close vent valves  

  8. Check inspection holes for leakage

  9. Inspect expansion joint for external surface damage (inlet )
