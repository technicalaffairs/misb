Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the little block.

The high pressure economizer used for receiving water from feed water pump and rising its temperature and pressure (by taking heat from exhaust gases coming from gas turbine) then transfer it to HP steam drum

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • The safety department must check the isolation and surrounding of the zone of work.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • The unit is out of service.

  • Put red tag on the high pressure economizer.

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set includes screw drivers and common keys

  • Argon welding machine

  • Grinder & grinding wheel

  • Cleaning fluid.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Fill the high pressure section by filling water from feed water pump after closing drain and opening the vents.

  2. Inspect and adjust the leakage.

  3. Drain the high pressure economizer.

  4. Make hydraulic pressure test for the high pressure section by running the feed water pump to operating pressure (60 bar)

  5. Check the distance between pipes is equal.

  6. Check the cleanliness of the surface of pipes.

  7. Check any cracks and use grinding and argon welding according to the manual.
