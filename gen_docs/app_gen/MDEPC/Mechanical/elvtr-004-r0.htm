Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure I1,which must be performed every month as indicated by M1 in the above title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • The circuit breaker should be switched off

Tools and Equipments

  • Tester (digital Avometer)

  • Set off spanners and screw drivers

  • lubricate oil or grease suitable for each part

  • Vacuum cleaner

Work to be carried out

  1. Check oil amount gears and retiring

  2. Lubricate brakes arm

  3. Check brake setting

  4. CVheck the coupling

  5. Check any abnormal sound or smell

  6. Check he primary and secondary shaft bearings and replacement, if necessary

  7. Check the bed plate of hoisting machine and insulator

  8. Check speed regulator

  9. Check ropes condition and ropes paths

  10. Check control panel

  11. Check gear tolerance

  12. Check lighting lamps and air fan
