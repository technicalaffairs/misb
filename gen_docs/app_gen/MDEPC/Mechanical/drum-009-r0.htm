Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2,which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the little block.

The high pressure steam drum functions:

  • Receiving water from high pressure economizer.

  • Acting as a reservoir for the suction of high pressure circulating pumps.

  • Receiving water and steam from high pressure evaporator.

  • Acting as the source of steam to the super heater.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, breathing mask ... etc.

  • Put red tag on the high pressure super heater.

  • The safety department must check the isolation and surrounding the zone of work.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Grease gun

  • Cleaning fluid.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Open the drum manhole

  2. Clean the drum

  3. Check bolts of manhole and repair or change if necessary.

  4. Change manhole gasket.

  5. Change insulation.

  6. Change gasket of standpipe vent and drain flanges.

  7. Check safety valve and repair or change if necessary.

  8. Check level gauges and repair or change if necessary.

  9. Check valves of level gauges and repair or change if necessary.

  10. Check portholes of chemical injection spray nozzle and repair or change if necessary.

  11. Check bolts, wire mesh and vanes of steam drier and repair or change if necessary.

  12. Check bolts and plates of return case and repair or change if necessary.
