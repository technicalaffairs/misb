Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be carried out on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the little block.

The high pressure super heater is used for receiving steam from high pressure steam drum and making it super heated by exchanging heat with exhaust gases coming from gas turbine.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask ... etc

  • The safety department must check the isolation and surrounding of the zone of work.

  • The unit is out of service.

  • Put red tag on the high pressure super heater.

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Argon welding machine.

  • Grinder & grinding wheel.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Fill the high pressure section by filling water from feed water pump after closing drain and opening the vents.

  2. Inspect and adjust the leakage.

  3. Drain the high pressure super heater.

  4. Make hydraulic pressure test for the high pressure section by running the feed water pump to operating press.

  5. Check the distance between pipes is equal.

  6. Check the cleanliness of the surface of pipes.

  7. Check any cracks and use grinding and argon welding according to the manual.
