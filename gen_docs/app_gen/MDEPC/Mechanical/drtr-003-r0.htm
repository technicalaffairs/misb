Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should be trained qualified and carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask ... etc

  • HRSG out of service

Dearator out of Operation

  1. Inspect for evidence of corrosion scaling cracking or broken or worn parts

  2. Check spray valve

  3. Check the plug for dearator

  4. Check spray nozzle

  5. Check manhole gasket and replace if necessary
