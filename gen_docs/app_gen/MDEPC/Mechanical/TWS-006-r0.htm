This equipment used for clean intake water from any deposits suspended.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the screen mechanically and electrically.  

Tools and Equipment

  • Maintenance tool set.

  • Wires to raise up the screen cover
