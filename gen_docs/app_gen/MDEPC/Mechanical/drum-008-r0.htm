Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1,which must be carried out on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the little block.

The high pressure steam drum functions:

  • Receiving water from high pressure economizer

  • Acting as a reservoir for the suction of high pressure circulating pumps

  • Receiving water and steam from high pressure evaporator

  • Acting as the source of steam to the super heater

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

    •

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask ... etc.

  • Put red tag on the high pressure steam drum.

  • The safety department must check the isolation and surrounding of the zone of work.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Outside Inspection

  1. Check fixation.

  2. Check manhole gasket leakage.

  3. Check tidiness of insulation.

  4. Check gasket leakage of standpipe vent and drain flanges.

  5. Check safety valve according vender info.

  6. Check level gauges according vender info.
