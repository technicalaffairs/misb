Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified and carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags. 

Work to be Carried Out

Pump in Operation

  1. Measure vibration level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do. Also check noise.

  2. Check pump capacity, pressure and power .if pump performance does satisfy your process requirements, and process requirements have not changed, pump should be disassembled, inspection, and worn parts should be replaced. Otherwise, a system inspection should be done.  

  3.  Check the temperature of bearing

  4. Check the leakage  of the mechanical seal 

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value

  2. Check the mechanical seal leakage

  3. Check the pump and piping for leaks

  4. Check the fixation, supports

Before Starting the Pump

  1. Check the oil level

  2. Check the cooling water

  3. Check the direction of rotation of motor before starting

  4. Check the suction and delivery

  5. Check the open suction and discharge valve  

During Operation of the Pump

  1. Check the oil temperature and oil leakage

  2. Check the water temperature and water leakage 

  3. Check the vibration level

  4. Check pump capacity

  5. Check pump pressure and power

  6. Check pump temperature of bearing

  7. If pump performance does satisfy your process requirements, and process requirements have not changed, pump should be disassembled, inspection, and worn parts should be replaced. Otherwise, a system inspection should be done.
