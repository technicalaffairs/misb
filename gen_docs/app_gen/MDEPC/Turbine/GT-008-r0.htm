Introduction

This document outlines the replacement procedure, which must be performed for main air filters when filters differential pressure is greater than 4 inch water gauge, or emergency door open. Changing filters depending on ∆P according to the weather of Mahmoudia city.

Main air filters are used to clean the air to the main compressor from dust, small particles…etc

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, eye glass, nose mouth "dust cover".... etc

  • Turbine must be shutdown

  • Fire fighting system must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Rags, cleaning fluid

Work to be carried out  

  1. Disassembly the old filters.

  2. Inspect  air diffusers and diffusers brackets for broken and cracks (replace it if needed)

  3. Assembly the new filters and tight the filters supports

  4. Measure pressure difference for main air filters
