Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The basic function of the steam turbine in a multi shaft (STAG) plant is to generate efficient electric power from heat which would other wise be wasted by the multiple gas turbine exhaust flows.

The system accepts available steam from HRSG`S which convert the waste heat energy from their associated gas turbine into usable steam energy

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Shutdown the unit

  • Lock the condensate pumps (1&2) switches, close isolate valves and isolate the power

  • Lock the vacuum pumps (1&2) switches, close isolate valves and isolate the power

  • Isolate compressed air for all valve

  • Switch off gland exhaust and oil vapor extractor

  • Switch off ventilation system

  • Generator should be isolated

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Special tools for turbine

  • Welding machine

  • Screw driver set

  • Jacks (hydraulic, screw)

  • Filler gauge

  • Telescope gage

  • Dial indicator with magnet base

  • Cleaning liquid & rags

Work to be carried out

  1. Ensure the unit shutdown

  2. Check alignment for coupling between turbine & generator axial (0.01 mm) lopes down & radial (0.02mm) and record it

  3. Inspect bolts, studs, dowel fits, dowel nuts for cracks and loosen

  4. Check and measure the vibration for bearing  no. 1&2&3&4

  5. Review the valves position
