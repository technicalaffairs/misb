Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1, on yearly bases or each 8000 equivalent operating hours indicated by Y1 or H8000 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Gomaa Farhoud, tested by eng. Ahmad Shanab, accepted by general manager eng. Omran Abd El-Hamid Shoaib, approved by eng. Ahmed Sawan Nubria power station head sector and revised by MDEPC quality control eng. Abouda El-Bana.

This document certified by eng. Samir Abd El-Gelil inspection & quality control general director, MPIS coordinator and approved by eng. Mohamed Naguib inspection and quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued.

  • The unit is out of service

  • All circuit breakers of all equipments are isolated.

  • Fire fighting equipments.

  • The maintenance staff should carry out the suitable ID safety category.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipments as safety helmets, shoes, gloves etc.

  • Generator should be isolated

  • The oil of turbine must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure turbine must be completely drained. 

  • Gas lines must be vented.

Tools and Equipment

  • C54 standard tools.

  • Combustion chamber tools

  • Base plate for  turning device

  • Compressed air.

  • Cable slings , lifting devices

  • Crane

  • Forklifts

  • Turning device for rotor

  • Scaffolding

 

 

 

Work to be carried out

Compressor

Casing

  1. Open manholes, hand holes.

  2. Visual inspection of first rows of vans by boroscope. (see figure 1)

  3. Angle check of inlet guide vanes

  4. Check of compressor vane hooks (see figure 2)

Rotor

  1. Visual inspection of first rows of blades with boroscope (see figure 3)

  2. Measuring of radial blade clearances (see figure 4)

Turbine

Casing

  1. Open manholes, hand holes.

  2. Visual inspection of first and last row of vanes with boroscope for indication of. :

  • Location and size of cracks indications and deformation .

  • Location of blocked cooling holes.

  • Location and size of areas with overheating and coating spoliation.

Rotor

  1. Visual inspection of first and last row of blades with boroscope for indication of :

  • Location and size of cracks indications and deformation .

  • Location of blocked cooling holes.

  • Location and size of areas with overheating and coating spoliation.

  1. Measuring of radial blade clearance. ( see figure 5)

Bearing

  1. Check of bearing casing and seal rings for oil leakage.

  2. Tightening of any loose connections.

Turning device

  1. Visual check , check of oil supply

 

Compressor diffuser

  1. Visual inspection

Combustion chamber

  1. Visual inspection.

  2. Inspection of ceramic heat shield.

  3. Inspection of metallic heat shield.

  4. Inspection of tile holders.

  5. Replacement of any damage parts .

  6. Visual inspection of burners .

  7. Visual inspection of diagonal swirls .

Exhaust diffuser

  1. Visual inspection of first compensator behind the turbine
