Introduction

This document outlines the Lub. Oil cooler inspection which must be performed every combustion chamber inspection, hot gas inspection and major inspection.

Cooling Water Heat Exchanger used for cooling water circuit to cool the auxiliary equipment. 

The cooling water flow through the heat exchanger is controlled by VTR-1 that maintains the correct bearing header temperature (VTR-1 bearing header temperature valve setting: 120+/-3 F)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety helmet and shoes.

  • Isolate the cooling water heat exchanger by closing the inlet and out let valve.

  • Drain the heat exchanger.

Tools and Equipment

  • Maintenance tool set.

  • Grease (multi purpose grease).

  • Wires to rise up the screen cover.

  • Cleaning fluid, Rages

  • Air Compressor.

Work to be carried out  

  1. Unbolt water boxes bolts.

  2. Dismantle the water boxes.

  3. Remove the used (old) gasket and replace with a new gasket if necessary.

  4. Clean all tubes compressed air and water.

  5. Clean water boxes by brush and water.

  6. Inspect tube leakage by pressurized the heat exchanger and blocked any pipe leaks.

  7. Inspect water boxes coating and repair if needed.

  8. Reassemble the water boxes.

  9. Test the teat exchange after putting into operation
