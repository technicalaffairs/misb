Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection II, which must be performed for steam valves every six months as indicated by M6 in above title block

A combined stop and control valve are provided in the main steam line supplying the HP steam turbine inlet

The first draft initiated by eng. Mahmoud Abo El-Fetouh Shata, tested by eng. Ali A. Elslam and revised by MDEPC quality control engineer Abouda Elbana, accepted by general director eng. Yosry Abd El-Maqsood and approved by eng Farouk Awad  head sector of  Talkha power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Shutdown the unit and be out of service

  • Isolate compressed air for all valve

  • Switch off gland exhaust and oil vapor extractor

  • Switch off ventilation system

  • Generator should be isolated

  • Drain the pipeline switch connected to this valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Screw driver set

  • Jacks (hydraulic, screw)

  • Filler gauge & torque wrench

  • Dial indicator with magnet base

  • Cleaning liquid

  • Rags

 

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Check valve stroke

  2. Check valve timing

  3. Check sealing welds

  4. Inspect valve body visually

  5. Inspect spring & bolts visually
