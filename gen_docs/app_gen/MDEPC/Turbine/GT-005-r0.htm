Introduction

 

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1, which must performed every 8000 equivalent operating hours as indicated by H8000 in the above title block

Combustion Chamber Technical Data

  • Annular combustion chamber

  • 24 hybrid burners

  • 2-flam detector

  • Walk in combustion chamber (two manholes up & down ) for inspection

  • 552 ceramic tiles (253 tiles at the hub - 300 tiles at outer shell)

  • Gas mass flow rate 15 kg/s at 100 % load

  • Fuel oil mass flow rate 18 kg/s at 100% load

  • Temperature degree 1230 °C at 100% load

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the suitable safety  category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes and gloves pairs

  • Unit out service

  • Fire fighting system must be isolated

  • Fuel system should be isolated

  • Lubricate oil & hydraulic oil system must be isolated

  • All electric switches & motor control center panel should be isolated

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Borescope

  • Torque range from "100NM - to 600 NM"

  • Special tools

Work to be carried out

Dismantling

  1. Remove the isolation around the combustion chamber manholes

  2. Dismantle the upper and lower combustion chamber manholes

  3. Open air intake system

  4. Open the exhaust system manhole

Inspection and Repair
Combustion Chamber Inspection

  1. Inspect all tiles by using template to detrain the defective tiles (broken - cracks - erosion ) and replace it if required.

  2. Visual inspection of the burners for (cracks - over heating & deposit).

  3. Visual inspection of first row of vans.

  4. Periscope inspection of first row of vans if required for (cracks - over heating).

  5. Measure the radial clearance. Of the first row of vans.

Air Intake System Inspection

  1. Replace coalescer filters and self cleaning filters at ∆P= 16 mb

  2. Check the cleaning of blonum champer

  3. Check the cleaning of inlet gaid van's (I.G.V)

  4. Measure inlet gaid vans angle at the opening case (6°→7°)

  5. Measure the radial clearance of the fist row of the compressor

 

Exhaust System Inspection

  1. Check the exhaust system for any cracks and damage

  2. Measure the radial clearance of last row of vans

  3. Visual inspection of turbine bearing no. 2 area for leakage oil

 

Chang of Ceramic Tiles

  1. Dismantle the metallic tiles (use as locking for ceramic tiles rows)

  2. Star to dismantle the ceramic tiles by remove two "Free support holders"

  3. Move the ceramic tiles to be free from the other two "Fixed support holders"

  4. Dismantle the "Fixed holders" from their slots and check the holders for cracks and change the fix bolts for the holders

Igniter Test

  1. Check spark plug for all burners on/off

  2. Check igniter by borescope for broken & cracks if required
