Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed from one month to three months as indicated by M1:3 in the title block.

A current transformer is an instrument in which the secondary current is within specified limits proportional to and in- phase with the primary current

The purpose of this current transformer is to transform the actual system current to a value suitable for supplying control devices of excitation circuits. At the same time, it serves to insulate the measuring circuit from the primary circuit

The technical data for the current transformer : 1Φ transformer, 2500/25A 1180 VA and 50 Hz frequency

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear your safety helmet and safety shoes when cleaning

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounded on all its sides are required

  • Before commencing work make sure that the machine is isolated from the power supply and make sure again by making use of electric detector that the circuit is dead

  • Presence of the safety supervisor is required

 Caution

Secondary CT wiring could be energized through back feeds, make sure secondary circuit is short circuited

 Tools and Equipment

  • Blower, voltmeter, screw driver set, and fiberglass PVC ladder

  • Voltage indicator, portable earth for discharging

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

  • Complete maintenance tool set

Work to be carried out  

  1. Remove all dust from the transformer, particularly from insulating and cast resin surface

  2. Check the cable and bolted connection of the two side of the CT and ensure that all parts are secured

  3. Check for any signs of oil leaks

  4. Parts subject to corrosion should be cleaned from time to time and their surface must be given protective treatment

  5. Check primary bus connection for tightness

  6. Check for signs of overheating or flashover
