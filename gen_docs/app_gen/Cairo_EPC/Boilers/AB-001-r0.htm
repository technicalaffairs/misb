Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

Condensate polisher air blower provides air for resin mixing and scrubbing. The blower is equipped with inlet filter and silencer

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for electrical equipments

  • Close valve delivery.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Regrease – mobil UX 47

  2. Drain flush and replace oil – delvac 1240

  3. Check inlet filter silencer and discharge silencer
