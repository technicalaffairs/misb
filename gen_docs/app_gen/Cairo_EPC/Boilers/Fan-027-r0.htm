Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which should be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Forced draft fan FDF supply air to wind box for firing and remove gases from boiler.

The  first draft initiated by eng. Kamel Abd El-Salam Abd El-Rahman, tested by eng. Mohamed Ahmad Eid, accepted by general manager eng. El-Sayed Mohamed Ahmed, approved by eng. Mohamed Abdel Hafez Al-Sharedy: head sector of Cairo West Power Station,

This document revised by eng. Nageh Soliman Shousha CEPC inspection & quality control director, and certified by eng. Afaf Ebrahim El-Dib CEPC inspection & quality control's Head Sector, MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, ....etc.

  • Unit should be out of service.

  • Disconnect the circuit breaker of the fans motor.

Tools and Equipment

  • Different sizes, types of wrench, hammer, screw driver

  • Pneumatic tools, Hydraulic jack, blank and accessories.

  • Dial indicator, oil pump.

  • Grinder, brush, air hoses, grease pump.

  • Filler, rags, lead wires, shims, clamp, rope.

  • Verneir cellipers, vibration measurement.

  • Lubricant oil as table 1.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Silencer washing (A,B).

  2. Check impeller housings and inlet damper guide vanes, blades and linkage for cracks and loose connection.

  3. Check the bearing mounting bolts.

  4. Measure the clearance of the bearings for radial and axial using a lead wire and micrometer.

  5. Change the bearing oil with type (DTE  25). figure 2

  6. Carrying out the required alignment.

  7. Put FDF (A,B) into operation and check vibration.

Alignment Procedures

  1. Coupling spacer dismantle and remove.

  2. Mount the dial indicators one at the axial direction and one on the radial direction both on half coupling motor side (fixed part).

  3. Adjust the indicator pointer to zero reading.

  4. Rotate the fan on the CWD and record the initial reading for axial and radial direction.

  5. Loose the motor mounting bolts and jack bolts.

  6. According to the initial reading shims to be added or removed under the motor legs to adjust the reading to the accepted limits.

  7. Tighten the motor mounting bolts and jack bolt.

  8. Take a final reading for the alignment check in the radial and axial direction.

  9. Reassemble the coupling spacer.

  10. Put the fan in operation.

  11. Check the vibration using the vibrometer.
