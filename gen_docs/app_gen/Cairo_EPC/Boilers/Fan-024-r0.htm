Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which should be performed on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block.

GRF (for boilers no. 5,6) supply flue gases at furnace bottom to control reheat outlet temperature and to reduce NOX. pollution

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, ....etc.

  • Fan in service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable vibration measurement

  • Rags, organic fluids.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the outside surface of fan

  2. Check the vibration of FDF bearing at 3 directions using the vibro-meter.

Bearing Vibration Level:

  • Admissible level peak to peak: up to 126 u m (4.5mm/s)

  • Stop level peak to peak: 320 um (11mm/s)
