Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed from month to 3 months as indicated by M1 : 3 in the above title block.

The technical data of the starting up transformer: 3 Φ transformer - 380 V-940 KVA with 50 Hz frequency and YD11 connection

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear your safety helmet and safety shoes when cleaning suspension insulator

  • Transformer isolation and proper grounded on all its sides are required

  • Before commencing work make sure that the machine is isolated from the power supply and make sure again by making use of electric detector that the circuit is dead

  • Should further information be needed or any particular problem arises which are not covered by these instruction such as inspection please ask (BBC)

Environmental Recommendation

  • Before starting the cleaning of transformer wear you breathing mask to protect lung tissues from the dust particulate

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning collect it and send to the dump

  • Fixing oil leads to prevent pollution and save money of wasted oil

  • Oil leaks to the ground have very bad impact on the environment mainly on soil and ground water and eventually on the eco-system " It takes only 1 litter of hydrocarbon spilled in an aquifer to render 1.000.000 litter of potable water useless for human consumption

 

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Blower, voltmeter, screw driver set, and fiberglass PVC ladder

  • Voltage indicator portable earth for discharging

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Fin cloth for cleaning purposes

Work to be carried out 

  1. Visually inspect bolted connection in the main and auxiliary circuits, foundation bolts and all other bolted connections to ensure that all parts are secure

  2. Read and record oil and winding temperature check that the maximum temperatures are not being exceeded the limits saved also check that temperature is acceptable by comparing it with past entries

  3. Listen and check for any abnormal sounds from the main tank and accessories rattling sound indicates loose bolts

  4. Checks for leakages from the transformer tank pipes gouges gasket joints welds retighten any loosen screws if needed

  5. Check the cables of transformer for H.V side and L.V. side for any abnormal position

  6. Check the oil level in the gauge of conservator tank be sure that the oil level is in same position between max and min level

  7. Check the earthing screws and retighten if needed

  8. Clean the all body of the transformer by cleaning clothes, and use the blower to remove the dust

  9. Pay attention of discoloration of the silica gel
