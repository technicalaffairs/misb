Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Unit outage is required

  • The transformer must be isolated and grounded from the two sides

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical toolbox.

  • Megger 1000V.

  • Insulation oil device.

  • Thermal test element.

  • Turns ratio device.

  • DC winding resistance device.

  • Voltmeter.

  • Air blower

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Make visual inspection for painting and leakage .

  2. Check the transformer control panel and use blower for cleaning it .

  3. Check breather , change the silica gel if the color is  pink or white and adjust oil level in the trap of air dryer .

  4. Check and inspect the good operation of fire fighting system .

  5. Check oil level in conservator (add if necessary) .

  6. Check ground wire and tighten it if necessary .

  7. Measure De-electric voltage of oil (40 Kv / 2.5 mm) .

  8. Measure acid value of oil (less than 0.2 , acceptable) .

  9. Analyze gases in oil (dissolved combustible gases less than 700 ppm) .

  10. Measure water content (Less than 40 ppm) .

  11. Measure winding insulation resistance (including bushing ) , to be in accordance with fig. (1) and table (1) .

  12. Measure the HV and LV windings resistance , in accordance with tables (2) .

  13. Measure the voltage ratio bet HV & LV, in accordance with table (3).

  14. Check No-voltage tap changer can smoothly be changed over with light cricks.

  15. Check bushing (pipe cracks  contamination including saline  oil leakage  rust formation).

  16. Check protective control circuit (tests of relays pressure setting , oil flow level , dial thermometer , cooler power supply , visually check wire) .

  17. Check pressure release device (Cracks, oil or gas leak, break of safety pin) .

  18. Check mechanical protective relay (buchhloz relay) .

  19. Check winding temp. equipment .

  20. Check cooling fans motors (bearing and megger group circuit)

  21. Check oil pumps for leakage and measure the insulation resistance of winding of their motors .

  22. Get the transformer into service.
