Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The purpose of this transmitter is to make a matching of devices with pressure output to the normal voltage input of BBC electronic devices. The converter sections are not insulated from one other.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and health equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belts ... etc.

  • Gas turbine out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Voltmeter (0 -750 volt)

  • Test leads.

  • Air Compressor

  • Pressure gauge indicator (0-15bar).

 Work to be carried out  

  1. Remove the cover of the pressure transmitters.

  2. Connect the test leads at the pressure transmitters test jacks as shown in figure (1).

  3. Inject for the compressor at the both pressure transmitters direct pressure with a pressure supply.

  4. Measure the injected compressor pressure by using the pressure gauge indicator.

  5. Increase the pressure from (6.11bar, 7ba, 8bar, 9bar, 10.19bar) and record the pressure transmitters output (10-50 mv).

  6. Compare characteristic with final setting and measurement as shown in figure (2).
