Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The purpose of this converter is making a matching of devices with current output to the normal voltage input of BBC electronic devices. The converter sections are not insulated from one other.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses is required.

  • Gas turbine out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Ammeter (0 - 20A).

  • Voltmeter(0 -750 volt)

  • Test leads.

  • Air Compressor

  • Pressure gauge indicator (0-15bar)

 

 

Work to be carried out  

  1. Inject at both pressure transmitters (R1286, R1287) direct with pressure supply (Air Compressor).

  2. Increase the pressure from (6.11 bar) to (12.1 bar).

  3. Measure the inject current (4-20 m A) at pin (04) on the transducer.

  4. Measure the output voltage (2-10 V) at pin (07) on the transducer.

  5. Compare characteristic with Final Setting at Measurements as shown in figure (1).

  6. Readjust the (I/U) transducer if necessary (zero at R6 and span at R9).

 

         The terminal of Current to Voltage Converter.

 

04 Current Input 20 mA
07 Analog Output Voltage 10 V
10 -15 Volt, 8 mA max.
