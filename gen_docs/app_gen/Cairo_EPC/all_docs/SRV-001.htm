Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every 2000 operating hours as indicated by H2000 in the title block.

The compressor is required with 3 blow – off valves to prevent any surge condition at low speed and to reduce the power required on start-up. The closing of the blow-off valves is controlled by the pressure switch R2585 set to 4.7 bar compressor discharge pressure. This switch opens at its set pressure to deenergize the solenoid valve R1582. The solenoid valve closed their air bleed part and open the supply part to allow control air pressure from the compressor discharge to close the blow off valves. When the turbine trips, low safety oil pressure allows hydraulic valve R2284 to close their supply ports and open their bleed ports thus removing air control from the blow- off valves.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes... etc.

  • Unit on the full speed no load.

Tools and Equipment

  • A water tank with the separately stored foam extract inside.

  • A  CO2 - cylinder for pressure and mixing the water with the extract by the bursting the separating membrane.

  • Various air-water foam – mixing nozzles at the above mentioned locations.

  • Manually operated valves to energize the system by CO2 and to select  the flow to the various areas.

  • The necessary pipes and one hose.   

  • The fire control- valves are located near a door in order to allow leaving the machine hall in case of emergency.

  • The foam is absolutely harmless to all machine parts &can be cleaned-off easily.        

Work to be carried out  

  1. Press manually lever of safety relay to release control air pressure of blow off valve until alarm "blow- off valves not closed is" appears, that the blow-off valves opened.

  2. Release lever immediately. Alarm "blow-off valves not closed" must disappear.

Caution:

  (Do not operate unit for more than 1 minute with open blow-off valves).
