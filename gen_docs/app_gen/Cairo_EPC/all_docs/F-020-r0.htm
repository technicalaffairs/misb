Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on  yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Each clarifier is equipped with surface skimmers to remove floating oil and scum. The function of the raw water clarifier is to clarify nile river water for use in plant

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for electrical equipments

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

  • Grease gun

  • Wire brush scraper paint brush

  • Grease-Mobil EP-2

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check for loose bolts nuts and fettings

  2. Lubricate rollers, pinion shaft bearings, track rack and pinion  mobilex EXZ

  3. Flush and clean the unit

  4. Remove scale and corrosion and repaint effected areas

  5. Inspect for broken welds and loose bolts repair or replace as required

  6. Check sweeper adjustment
