Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Circuit breaker must be out of service and on the open position .

  • Draw out the circuit breaker .

  • Discharge the closing and opening springs .

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical tool box

  • Air blower

  • Megger 500 V .

  • Avometer, Timer

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check and clean the insulation of vacuum interrupters .

  2. Check for loose bolts , nuts , terminals and retightened wherever need .

  3. Check vacuum interrupter for proper fixation and any overheat or spark traces .

  4. Lubricate the operating mechanism using oil (LT-10f Nihon molybdenum CO , ltd) .

  5. Check springs for deformation , rust and for proper engaged of operating mechanism .

  6. Check the switching indicators and counter operations for proper functioning

  7. Measure closing and opening time; Closing time 0.15 sec,     Opening time 0.04 sec .

  8. Measure the insulation resistance of main conducting parts to earth must not be less than 500 MΩ .

  9. Check closing / tripping latches for smooth operation .

  10. Draw in the circuit breaker cell
