Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • The disconnecting switch is out of service and not charged .

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical toolbox .

  • Avometer .

  • An industrial vacuum cleaner .

  • Opening & closing time measuring device .

  • Gas leak detector .

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the external bolts and nuts

  2. Check the indicated number on the operating counter

  3. Check the existence of rust

  4. Check heater (in the operating mechanism), check for the disconnection of cable

  5. Check the Aux. switch (in the operating mechanism).

  6. Check operation characteristics (manual & remote) operation , opening & closing times, speed .

  7. Lubricate operating mechanism.

  8. Check gas leak.
