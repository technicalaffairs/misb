Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I2, which must be performed every three years as indicated by Y3 in the title block.

The first draft is initiated by eng. Ahmad Al-Okabi, tested by eng. Tarek EL-kafafy, accepted by general director eng. Nady Mosaed, approved by eng. Mohamed EL-Tahawy Head sector of  Shoubra EL-Kheima PS.

This document revised by eng. Nageh  Soliman Shousha, CEPC quality control director, and certified by eng. Afaf  Ebrahim El-Dib, CEPC inspection & quality control 's  head sector and MPIS coordinator  

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • The circuit breaker is out of service

  • The CB must be isolated & grounded from both sides .

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical tool box .

  • Gas leak detector .

  • An industrial vacuum cleaner .

  • Megger 1000 V

  • Avometer

  • DC resistance measuring device .

  • Opening & closing time measuring device

Work to be Carried Out

 

  1. Check correct operation of position indicator , operation counter , and auxiliary contacts

  2. Check SF6 gas press. (6 Atm.)

  3. Check gas & oil leak .

  4. Check exterior for loose nuts or bolts and rust spots .

  5. Check the ground wire and tighten if necessary .

  6. Measure the moisture in gas SF6 (its normal value is about 40PPmv).

  7. Measure SF6 gas purity (its normal value is about 98%) .

  8. Measure decomposition gas .

  9. Check oil level of p/p unit, in accordance with fig(1).

  10. Check operation at oil low press 230 atm .

  11. Check that oil press. switch operates within 10% of the specified value

  12. Check SF6 pressure & density switch settings (alarm & blocking), in accordance with fig(2)&table (1).

  13. Measure the insulation resistance of control circuit , greater than 2MΩ .

  14. Measure resistance between main circuit and gas tank , greater than 1000 MΩ .

  15. Measure DC resistance of contacts for each phase of the CB .

  16. Measure the opening and closing time of the CB (O.T < 0.018sec,C.T < 0.1sec).

  17. Check the time required from rising the pressure from 315 bar to 335 bar .

  18. Coat the operating mechanism with silicon grease .

  19. Check the accumulator gas N2 pressure by starting up to the pump at zero oil pressure then the pressure rises at stroke from several tens to about 220 bar , the pressure which rose in this manner is the gas pressure .

  20. Check for disconnection of space heater , measure current bet the space heater terminals .

  21. Put the C.B in service
