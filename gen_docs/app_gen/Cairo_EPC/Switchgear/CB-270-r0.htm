Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • The circuit breaker in service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical tool box .

  • Gas leak detector .

  • An industrial vacuum cleaner .

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the outside surface with the industrial vacuum cleaner

  2. Tighten loose bolts or nuts

  3. Check the ground wire & tighten if necessary

  4. Check gas pressure to be 6 atm

  5. Check oil pressure to be 320 atm

  6. Check oil level  in accordance with fig (1)

  7. Check for gas or oil leak

  8. Record on/off operation counter

  9. Record the oil pump operation counter.
