Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every equivalent Operating  4000 service Hours as indicated in the title block.

The over speed trip protects the turbine against inadmissible speed increases when activated it relieves emergency oil circuit which causes an immediate closing of the turbine stop valves. The desired tripping speed can be adjusted with the over speed trip in the dismantled condition..

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses is required.

  • The unit must be at full speed and at no load.

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal safety equipment

Work to be carried out  

  1. Pull lever on testing device R4291 carefully.

  2. Verify pressure GT7119 at the moment when emergency oil pressure GT7117 drops down to 0.03-0.1 bar.

Pressure GT7119 at this moment must be 0.7-0.8 bar

  1. Alarm "emergency oil pressure" must appear.

  2. Pull lever completely up to mechanical stop (end position).

  3. Release lever on testing device R4291 carefully and slowly.

  4. This results that pressure GT7119 drops and pressure GT7117 increases to nominal value(1.1-1.15) bar.

  5. Alarm "emergency oil pressure" must disappear.

Important:

If there are any deviations in characteristics during above mentioned procedure, search immediately for the fault.
