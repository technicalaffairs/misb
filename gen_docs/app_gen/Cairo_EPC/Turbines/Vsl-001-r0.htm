Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The function of the vessel is to polish condensate water , the vessels consists of 3 polisher service vessels (cation regeneration vessel, anion regeneration vessel and resin storage vessel)

The technical specification of the Vessel:     Qty = 12

Description: Condensate polisher service vessel
Type:  Deep Bed
Rating: 7570 LPM (2000GPM)
Design pressure: 50 KSCMG (700 Psig)
Design temperature: 60 ° C (140 °F)
Manufacture: INFILCO
Bed Depth 1.06M, 36.8 LPS/SQ.M

Safety Precaution 

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves  etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for electrical equipments

 

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set.

  • Electric lights.

  • Safety and healthy equipment

Work to be Carried out

  1. Transfer the resin from vessel to storage tank.

  2. Loose guides & open the main haul of the vessel.

Inspection of the Internal Pipes:

  1. Check the tighten of laterals and threads.

  2. Check the sleeve (sleeve must be covered all holes in lateral).

  3. Check whether the sleeve has any damage or cut.

  4. Check the seal & strips (the tighten of seal must be good).

Inspection of Basket Strainer:

  1. Open the flange of strainer & rise it up by chain block.

  2. Check the basket against any damage or cut.
