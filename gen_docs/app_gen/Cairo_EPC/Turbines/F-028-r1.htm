Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block. This document updated regard to their paper at 07 May 2007

The function of drier filter is to separate water from air which come from compressor to blow out valves.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • The gas turbine is out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • Wrenches key  41.17

  • Compressed Air.

  • Suitable cleaning agents

Work to be carried out

Cleaning of filter

  1. As per the degree of dirtiness of the air to be purified, it is recommended to clean the filter (5), by the suitable cleaning agents after loosening the filter cover (6)

  2. The filter can simply be drawn off in upward direction. When mounting filter (5) again, care must be taken that the lower gasket (7) remains undamaged and that filter (5) properly engaged with the recess and does not tilt.

 

Replacement of the elastic nozzle wall

  1. After a longer period of service it can become necessary, above all if there is a pulsating flow, to replace the nozzle wall (8) After removal if the body upper part (3) Only screws (18) for the nozzle wall must be loosened (see fig. 2). When mounting the new nozzle wall care must be taken that same can move freely between the body upper part( 3) and guide (9). To check this, the elastic nozzle wall (8) should be pushed inwards.

  2. The nozzle wall (8) shall snugly but freely (movable) fit the body upper part (3) whereas a clearance of 1mm shall be between nozzle wall (8) and guide (9)

Cleaning of the body lower part

  1. Condensate containing impurities must be discharged from the body lower part (10) through the drain plug (11) if there is difficult operating conditions-(e.g) if the condensate contains much impurities or much oil.

  2. It's required to clean the body lower part (10) including float controls with cleaning gasoline the intervals between the cleaning operations must be determined on the basis of the degree of contamination of the condensate. If the condensate contains much oil, it is possible to install a light- construction float better adapted to the specific gravity of the condensate containing oil.

Dismantling of body lower part

  1. Before dismantling the body lower part, the system including the TG5501 unit must be depressurized.

  2. Loosen the body screws.

  3. Loosen the lateral condensate discharge line at the outlet (4).

  4. Draw off the in downward direction the body lower part (10) with silumin intermediate. tube (12)

  5. The body upper part (3) can remain in the piping.

  6. After removal of the intermediate tube (12) and the catch plate (13) the float control can be dismantled.

Dismantling of Float Control

  1. This is made by means of a mandrel

  2. This mandrel must be turned through thread of the bushing (19)

  3. After passing through a small space without thread, the mandrel must be introduced by 2-3 turns into the thread of the bore of the supporting body (14) Applying some slight hammer strokes against the head of the mandrel (see fig 3) the float is driven out of the conical seat when doing so, float (15) must be lifted.

* The float control comprises:

- (14) Supporting member.

- (16) Rotary side with split pin.

 -(15) Float with float lever.- 

Mounting of float control

  1. The control together with the supporting body must be introduced to the conical body seat the mounting mandrel must be turned thought the thread of bushing (19)

  2. After passing through a small space without thread. The mandrel is introduced into the thread of the supporting body (14) and turning it towards the right firmly drawn into

  3. The conical body seat (See fig 5). After this has been done. The mounting mandrel must be turned out again. The immersion tube must be directed vertically downwards.  

  4. This can be obtained with the following means.

  5. Remove the drain plug (11) and introduce a screw driver through the free threaded opening into the immersion tube.
