Introduction

In different plants, the HGC suspension ring was found to break off during operation. For this reason, the suspension ring in newly manufactured HGC's (at the vane carrier end) was widened. With installation of reconditioned HGC, the suspension of groove of the vane carrier needs widening form 23 mm to 27 mm.

This document initiated by eng. Magdi Sami Ebrahim, tested by eng. Maher Abd Elgwad Salem and eng. Mohamed El-Sayed Ahmed, and approved by eng. Mohamed Salah Eldin Abdel Ghafar: general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses is required.

Tools and Equipment

  • Vertical Lathe

  • Welding electrodes UTP 7114 diameter 2.5mm.

  • Welding electrodes Oerliken diameter 2.5mm

Work to be carried out  

  1. Bolting together vane carrier upper and lower half - measure roundness and if necessary reset roundness by using bracings to be installed in blade grooves.

  2. Build up welding according dwg HTCZ  300 068 P 2, views (C) and (D) and remark 3 electrodes to be used oerlikon spezil, diameter 2.5 mm no heat treatment required either beforehand or afterwards.

  3. Rework at GT-vane-carrier according dwg.HTCZ300 068. The groove is to widen from 23mm to 27mm. The for hard facing planned pockets need to be made on both sides. Positioning, number and size of them pockets to see on dwg. HTCZ300 068 view "A" and "B". This work is to perform local on a vertical turning mill in suitable work shop.

  4. Apply hard facing according dwg. HTCZ300 068 P3 views "A" and "B" in the pockets of sides of the groove. Turning the groove on hard faced sections to the final dimension according dwg. HTCZ 300 068 (consider remarks on dwg.). Electrodes to be used for hard facing UTP7114 dia.2.5mm.No heat treatment required either beforehand or afterwards.
