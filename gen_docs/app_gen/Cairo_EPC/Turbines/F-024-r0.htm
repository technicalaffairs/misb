Introduction

The maintenance procedures should be done on the power oil filter when the pressure drop across the filter is about 0.5 kg/cm2.

Twin filter are used in case the regeneration of the dirty filter element must be carried out without shutting down the plant. The red segment of the indicator disk is visible over a pressure range equal to 20 -100% the first read constant is actuated at 75% and the second at 100%

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses is required.

Tools and Equipment

  • Wrench 24,19,17,10

  • Air Compressor.

  • Cleaning Fluid

Work to be carried out  

  1. Set the change- over device at its mid- position both filter chamber in use.

  2. Open the air vents of both chambers.

  3. Start the plant up slowly.

  4.  Close the air vents when the air has escaped and liquid has started issuing.

  5. Operate the change - over device to isolate one filter chamber while one of the filter chambers is being used for filtering the isolated chamber is in reserve until the maximum permissible differential pressure has built up by the operating filter becoming clogged when the differential pressure reaches 0.8 or 1.2 Kg/cm2 for filter mesh and 2 Kg/cm2 for micro-cartridge filters, the change-over is made as follows to the clean filter chamber.

  6. If there is pressure equalizing cock open it by briefly opening the air vent, check whether the reverse chamber is full.

  7. Change- over.

  8. Shut the pressure equalizing cock.

  9. Open the air vent on the isolated filter this replaces the pressure of the liquid and this possible to check whether the change –over device has stopped the flow to the filter chamber in contrast to the change – over using valve or segment, when there is a change – over by plug- cock, complete sealing is not possible: by opening the sludge outlet, liquid leaking during the cleaning process, can be drawn off.

  10. Take the cover off the casing to the isolated filter chamber.

  11. Remove the filter element vertically upwards, in doing so it may be necessary, to reduce the liquid level by opening the sludge outlet.

  12. Clean the filter element, or fit new micro- cartridges. Clean the filter mesh with a suitable cleaning agent assisted by blowing through the mesh from the clean side.

  13. Fix the cleaned filter element.

  14. Put the cover back on the casing, leaving the air vent open. Before putting back cover, make sure that the seal is in good condition and is properly seated, if necessary renew the seal.

  15. If there is a pressure equalizing cock, open it until the air has been vented from the filter half. If there is no pressure equalizing cock, set the change over- device to its mid- position until the air has been vented.

  16. Close the air vent. One of the filter halves is then again in reserve. This procedure, from paragraph 6 on, is repeated as required.
