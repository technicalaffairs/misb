Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

Generator air cooler function is to cool in closed loop circuit by exchange heat between the generator air and water. Two coolers water/air are used for cooling the air of the generator indirectly by water and the hot water return back to the cooler to cool by forced air using fan.

Work to be carried out

Cleaning The Exterior (Air Side)

Normally it is possible to clean the heat exchanger fins during operation. The cleaning method depends on the degree of soiling. The exterior part of the heat exchanger is cleaned mechanically or by hydraulic method.

Hydraulic method

If the soiling of the fins is of more resistant nature, a cleaning application with a high pressure water jet shall be used. If it is a matter of oily or sooty deposit, a cleaning agent should be added to the water. Be careful not to deform the blades when applying the water jet.

Mechanical Method

  1. If the dirt deposit is of slight, dry or dusty nature, the HE fins can be cleaned by means of a jet of compressed air.

  2. If the soiling and accumulated fibrous material is mainly around the fins inlet area, it is to be eliminated with a soft brush.

Inside Cleaning (water side)

If a rather heavy soiling is to be expected, the heat exchanger should be designed with removable collectors. The elimination of dirt deposits or product remnant from the tubes or collectors is an easy task. According to the degree of soiling, the tubes can be cleaned in different ways

Hydraulic Cleaning

For the inside cleaning, just as for the exterior, a cleaning appliance with a high pressure water jet can as well be used (required pressure: up to 500 atm.). Through a nozzle head at the end of a tube plain water is being pulverizes under very high pressure against the soiled inner side of the heat exchanger tube. The cleaning effect result from very sharp water jets, which breaks up the incrusted layer without damaging the tube.

Mechanical Cleaning

The accumulated dirt deposits can be removed with a special brush. As to the material and hardness, these brushes are matched to the used heat exchanger tubes. Rinse the tubes thoroughly at the end of the cleaning.
