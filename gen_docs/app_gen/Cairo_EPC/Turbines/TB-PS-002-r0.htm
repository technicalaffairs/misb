Introduction

Type G Cuno Auto Klean Filter is of all metal construction operating on the principle of edge filtration, it is suitable for flows up to eight gallons per minute & will remove all solids larger in dimension than the thickness of specified spacing. Type G Cuno Auto Klean filter is used to filter the low pressure power oil to feed the temperature control valve.

Information

  1. Filter element is cleaned without interruption of service, by one complete turn of the filter element. 

  2. The positive combing action of the cleaner blades frees it of all solids collected on the filtering surface.

  3. Each filter is equipped with a vent as standard, a built in relief valve arranged to bypass the filter cartage, should a predetermined  pressure drop across the cartridge be exceeded maybe substituted for the vent, where a relief valve is required the differential relieving  pressure must be specified.
