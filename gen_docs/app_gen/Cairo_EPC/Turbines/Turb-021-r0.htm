Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2 to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The technical specification of the turbine:  

Guaranteed KW  315000
Initial pressure 170 ATM
Initial temperature 537.8°C
Reheat temperature 537.8°C
Exhaust pressure 63.5 mm Hg A
Percent make up 5
RPM 3000

Units SN:13A6501 (unit 1), 13A6531 (unit 2), 13A6541 (unit 3),  23A6731 (unit 4)

This unit is a two cylinder tandem compound double exhaust, condensing reheat turbine, designed for high operating efficiencies.

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for electrical equipments

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Complete measuring tool set

Work to be carried out

  1. Check all drain lines and V/Vs from the turbine and the isolated piping.

  2. Check all orifices and traps by measuring the pipe temperature upstream and down stream.

  3. Internal inspection, cleaning, and maintain of one valve from each turbine critical valves types, if any problem detected, check all valves of the type.

  4. Grease governor end pedestal, onboard cylinder supports and sole plate, grease cylinder outer supports and seating plate.

  5. Grease all steam threads on manual V/Vs.
