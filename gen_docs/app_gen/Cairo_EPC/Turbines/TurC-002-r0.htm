Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves  etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for electrical equipments.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

 

Work to be Carried out

  1. Check condenser for sediment and debris, clean if necessary

  2. Check steam drains for impingement on condenser tubing or supports

  3. Check for broken pipes or supports
