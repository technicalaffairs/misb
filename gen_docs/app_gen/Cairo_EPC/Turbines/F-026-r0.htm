Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

In machines with a closed air circuit,  some air is bound to leak, it must be replaced by air drawn in through filters, the two leakage air filters are located on the face-end outer casings of the generator, which means they are at the points with the least static pressure. Particular care is to be taken to ensure that these filters maintained properly.

Work to be carried out

Cleaning

  1. Clean the filters from contaminated atmospheric dust by tapping carefully, or by blowing through with compressed air.

  2. Wash with warm water if needed.

  3. However, if the dust extracted by the filters is greasy, an ordinary commercial detergent should be added to the water.

  4. If very greasy, ethylene tri-chloride may be used for cleaning.

  5. Replace the damaged or severely fouled filters by a new ones.

Dismantling (Removal and Disassembly)

  1. Remove the wing nut and the cover.

  2. Pull off the filter tube.

Assembly and Installation

  1. Mounting takes in the reverse order.
