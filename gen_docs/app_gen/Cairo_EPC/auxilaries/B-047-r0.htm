Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Water source must be available in the battery room .

  • Battery group must be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydrometer .

  • Avometer .

  • Thermometer.

  • Battery Hi tester .

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check general appearance and cleanliness.

  2. Measure the charger output Amps and Voltage .

  3. Check electrolyte level .

  4. Check cracks in cells or leakage of electrolyte .

  5. Check evidence of corrosions at terminals or connectors .

  6. Check ambient temp. and condition of ventilating equipment .

  7. Measure battery cells voltage and specific gravity .

  8. Measure pilot cell electrolyte temperature .

  9. Measure internal resistance of each cell ( ~0.375 mW )

  10. Check evidence of voltage leaks to ground

  11. Measure total battery voltage

  12. Put battery in service.
