Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I2, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Vertical 6.3kv Siemens-Allis motor of circulate water pump, 900HP, ANV & Condensate water pump, 1650HP,DP

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • After stopping to motor insure that circuit breaker of the motor is draw out .

  • Avoid contact with rotating parts .

  • Act with care in accordance with prescribed procedures , in handling and lifting this equipment .

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical toolbox .

  • Vibration monitor .

  • Grease - gun

  • Megger 1000 V

  • Avometer .

  • Suitable lifting device .

  • Resistor Kelvin bridge .

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

 

Check the running conditions while the motor in service

  1. Check the motor vibration not to exceed 60 microns .

  2. Check that there is no abnormal noise on the motor

  3. Check the bearing temp. on the Accurex in the control room not to exceed 87 °C.

  4. Check the motor stator temp. on the Accurex in the control room not to exceed 150 °C.

  5. Check the motor current reading of the 3 phases in the control room

  6. Check for any oil leak

After isolation the circuit breaker of the motor

  1. Disconnect all cables supply , protection , and the accessories

  2. Uncouple the motor and remove the coupling flange

  3. Remove the protection devices and the accessories of the bearing

  4. Remove the rotor away from the stator

  5. Clean the inside of the motor (the rotor and stator), ventilation ducts and the windings by dry compressed air

  6. Measure insulation resistance with surge arrestors disconnected, the insulation should be 100MΩ or greater after one min.

  7. Measure the winding DC resistance, must be the same for 3 phase windings

  8. Check bearing for damage and change it if necessary

  9. Clean intake and outlet air filters

  10. Reassemble the motor components and check that all bolts are well tightened and put the bolt stops .

  11. Couple the motor with the pumps shaft and connect all cables (supply , protection , accessories ) .

  12. Change the upper bearing oil (Type EDT heavy)

  13. Remove inlet & outlet plugs and grease the lower motor bearing with (GED6A2C5 grease) then replace inlet plug .

  14. Tighten the ground wire.

  15. Run the motor, after 10 min replace the outlet plug of the grease.

  16. Check the running conditions.
