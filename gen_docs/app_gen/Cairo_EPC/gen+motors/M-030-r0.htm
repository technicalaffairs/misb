Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be carried out every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block.

Horizontal 6.3 kv Ansaldo motor of, forced draft fan (bearing lubricating oil)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • After stopping the motor insure that circuit breaker of the motor is draw out .

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical toolbox

  • Vibration monitor

Work to be Carried Out

 

Check the running conditions (while the motor in service)

  1. Check the motor vibration not to exceed 60 microns .

  2. Check that there is no abnormal noise on the motor .

  3. Check the bearing temp. on the Accurex in the control room not to exceed 87 °C .

  4. Check the motor stator temp. on the Accurex in the control room not to exceed 150 °C .

  5. Check the motor current reading of the 3 phases in the control room .

  6. Check for any oil leak .

After isolation the circuit breaker of the motor

  1. Tighten loose bolts .

  2. Check ground wire, tighten if necessary .

  3. Check the oil level, increase if necessary .

  4. Clean motor outside surface to be free of dust, oil, moisture and other objects .

  5. Put the motor again in service and recheck running conditions .
