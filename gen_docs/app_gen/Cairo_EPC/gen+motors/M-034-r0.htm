Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

DC Motor 25HP, 125 VDC, Arm current 171 A Westinghouse, of Emergency lubricating oil pump of main turbine Westinghouse.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Unit outage is required

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger 1000 V .

  • Avometer .

  • Thermal test element .

  • Vibration monitor .

  • Electrical toolbox .

  • DC clamp ammeter .

Work to be Carried Out

Check the running condition

  1. Check operating current not to exceed   Amp .

  2. Check winding temp. with a thermometer .

  3. Check bearing temp. with a thermometer .

  4. Check motor vibration

Stop the Motor and Isolate its CB

  1. Tighten loosen bolts.

  2. Check ground wire and tighten if necessary .

  3. Clean outside of motor and ventilation air inlets .

  4. Measure windings installation resistance not to be less than 15 MΩ .

  5. Inspect the motor interior if it very dirty disassemble the motor and clean .

  6. Wipe the commutator with a clean canvas .

  7. Inspect the fit of the brushes to the commutator. If the commutator has a rough surface, it must be smoothed with a piece of sand paper

  8. Remove the inlet and outlet plugs and grease the bearings with (dolium-R grease) , then replace the inlet plug .

  9. Run the motor for 10 min before replacing the outlet grease plug

  10. Recheck the running condition.
