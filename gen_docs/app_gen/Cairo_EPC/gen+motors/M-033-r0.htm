Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I2, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • After stop to motor insure that circuit breaker of the motor is draw out .

  • A void contact with rotating parts .

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical toolbox .

  • Vibration monitor .

  • Grease - gun

  • Megger 1000 V

  • Avometer .

  • Suitable lifting device .

  • Resistor Kelvin bridge .

Work to be Carried Out

 

Check the running conditions while the motor in service  :-

  1. Check the motor vibration not to exceed 60 microns .

  2. Check that there is no abnormal noise on the motor .

  3. Check the bearing temp. on the Accurex in the control room not to exceed 87 °C .

  4. Check the motor stator temp. on the Accurex in the control room not to exceed 150 °C .

  5. Check the motor current reading of the 3 phases in the control room .

After isolation the circuit breaker of the motor:

  1. Disconnect all cables supply , protection , and the accessories .

  2. Uncouple the motor and remove the coupling flange .

  3. Remove the protection devices and the accessories of the bearing .

  4. Assemble the upper cover of the motor

  5. Clean the inside of the motor (the rotor and stator) ventilation ducts and the windings by dry compressed air .

  6. Measure insulation resistance with surge arrestors disconnected , the insulation should be 100 MΩ or greater after one min.

  7. Measure the winding DC resistance , must be the same for 3 phase windings .

  8. Remove the bearing cap & check the condition of the white metal and the clearances between shaft & holes , change the bearing if it exceeded 0.3 mm .

  9. Reassemble the motor upper cover and check that all bolts are well tightened .

  10. Remove inlet and outlet plugs and grease the bearings (Type GED6A2C5) and replace the inlet plug .

  11. Tighten the ground wire .

  12. Connect all cables (Power supply, protection and accessories) .

  13. Run the motor no-load for enough time and check the running conditions , replace the outlet plug of grease after 10 mints .

  14. Stop the motor and couple the motor with the pump .

  15. Run the motor and check the running conditions.
