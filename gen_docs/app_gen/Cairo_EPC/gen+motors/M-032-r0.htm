Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be performed every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block.

Horizontal 6.3 kv motor, Yaskawa of closed cooling water p/p, 300 HP, BDK-U& Fuel oil booster p/p, 350 HP, BSA-O

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence like ropes with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • After stopping the motor insure that circuit breaker of the motor is draw out.

  • Avoid contact with rotating parts.

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical toolbox.

  • Vibration monitor.

  • Grease, gun

Work to be Carried Out

 

Check the running conditions (while the motor in service): -

  1. Check the motor vibration not to exceed 60 microns.

  2. Check that there is no abnormal noise on the motor.

  3. Check the bearing temp. on the accurex in the control room not to exceed 87 °C .

  4. Check the motor stator temp. on the accurex in the control room not the exceed 150 °C .

  5. Check the motor current reading of the 3 phases in the control room .

  6. After isolation the circuit breaker of the motor

  7. Tighten loose bolts .

  8. Check ground wire, Tighten if necessary .

  9. Remove inlet & outlet plugs and grease the lower motor bearing with (GED6A2C5) grease then replace inlet plug .

  10. Clean motor outside surface to be free of dust, moisture and other objects .

  11. Run the motor for 10 minutes before replacing the outlet plug and recheck the running conditions
