Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

Solid state over current relay type ITE-51E to protect  6.3kv motors.

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Equipment which relay protect is isolated.

  • Disconnect the DC power supply.

Tools and Equipment

  • AC multi-amp model MS-2-E

  • Timer model MCS-5N.

  • Avometer, DC power supply 125 V.

  • Spray, Burnisher.

Work to be carried out

  1. Remove the cover.

  2. Remove the connecting plugs, then draw out the main body.

  3. Clean the relay using a spray.

  4. Clean contacts of the relay using a contact burnisher.

  5. Connect relay coil terminals ( 3 , 4 ) to multi-amp output.

  6. Connect relay terminals ( 7 , 8 ) to DC power supply.

  7. Connect relay terminals (7 , 12) to timer.

  8. Adjusting the Multi-amp at 200% current tap and record the tripping time.

  9. Repeat the step no. 8 at 300% , 400% current tap.

  10. Connect the relay terminals again and connect DC power supply.
