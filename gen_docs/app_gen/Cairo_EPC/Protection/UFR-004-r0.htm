Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

Solid static under frequency relay SDF-1 to protect the generator.

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Unit is out of service .

  • Disconnect DC power supply .

Tools and Equipment

  • AC power supply model EP-400

  • DC power supply (125 VDC)

  • Timer model MCS-5N

  • Avometer, Test leads

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Disconnect the terminals of the relay and get it out of the panel .

  2. Clean the relay using a spray.

  3. Clean contacts of the relay using contact burnisher

  4. Connect the relay terminals ( 8 , 9 ) to the testing instrument output.

  5. Connect the relay terminals ( 1 , 10 ) to the timer.

  6. Connect the relay terminals ( 4 , 2 ) to the DC power source.

  7. Adjust the frequency of AC power supply at 50 HZ

  8. Decrease the frequency slowly

  9. Record the frequency and tripping time at which the relay will operate

  10. Record the values in the test sheet

  11. Connect the relay terminals again and connect DC power supply
