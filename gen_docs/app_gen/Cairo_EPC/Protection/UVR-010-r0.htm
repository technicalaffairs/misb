Introduction

This document out lines the testing procedure P1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated Y1 in the title block

The measured voltage is coupled via an intermediate PT, which provides DC isolation from the primary circuit and rectifiers to one or two electronic triggers which upon picking up exit electronic timing elements

The output of each relay is contactors, and there are signal lambs for both pick-up and tripping, which remain illuminated until reset. the input circuitry of the relay is arranged to prevent interference on the input leads from entering the measuring system The output contact of the under voltage is energized when the input voltage is below setting tripping contact made.

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Gas turbine out off service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Injection device model Manta MTS-1710.

  • Test Leads.

  • Screws.

  • Power source 220 volt for injection device

 

Work to be carried out

  1. The main transformer off.

  2.  Remove the terminals F1 (29, 30)

  3. Connect the relay test unit to a power source.

  4. Connect the Injection device test leads between the voltage terminals at F1 (29, 30) as shown in figure (1).

  5.  Increase the injected voltage gradually to the nominal voltage then decrease the voltage gradually until the relay it pick-up.

  6. Record this voltage on the test sheet.

  7. Compare the result with final setting and measurement.

  8. Turn off the injection device.

  9. Connect the time terminal for the injection device to terminal F3 (17; 18) and inject the voltage which appeared in step 6 suddenly.

  10. Record the time on the test sheet.

  11. Compare the result with final setting and measurement.
