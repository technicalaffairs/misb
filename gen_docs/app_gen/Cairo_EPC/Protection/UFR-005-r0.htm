Introduction

This document details the testing procedure required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of the time under frequency relay. It is recommended this procedure to be carried out on an annually bases.

The document is formatted the way to minimize the possibility of error causing personal injury or damage to equipment.    

Solid state frequency relay with extremely high accuracy and stability for graded load shedding in system with an energy deficit (partial failure of generation or transmission units)

For splitting up a grid system by opening tie-lines to prevent complete system collapse

For isolating small systems having their own generation from the main system in the case of fault the latter (e.g. disconnection of an industrial system with its own generation).

For the protection of generators and auxiliaries, where frequency supervision can avoid damage to turbines and auxiliary drives

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Gas turbine out off service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Test set type Manta MTS-1710

  • Test leads

  • Screw - driver

  • Power source.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. The main transformer off

  2. Remove the terminals F1 (28,30)

  3. Connect the relay test unit to a power source (24 volt dc)

  4. Connect the injection device test leads between the terminals at F1 (28,30) as shown in the figure (1)

  5. Inject the setting voltage (100 volt) and the rated frequency (50Hz)

  6. Decrease the frequency gradually until the relay stage (D) pick-up

  7. Record the result on the test sheet

  8. Turn off the injection device and connect the time terminal on the injection device to terminal (17,18) o the relay test unit

  9. Turn on the injection device and inject the value appear i step (6) suddenly and record the time taken to pick-up this stage

  10. Record the result o the test sheet

  11. Repeat steps from step (5) to step (10) for stages (C;B;A) at terminals (18;19);(18;20);(18;21) for time respectively.
