Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

Under voltage relay Toshiba 1VU1D, is used for protection of 6.3 kv busbar

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence like ropes with caution marks surrounding the work area .

  • Bus bar which relay protect is isolated .

  • Disconnect the DC power supply

Tools and Equipment

  • AC multi-amp model MS-2-E

  • Timer model MCS-5N .

  • Avometer .

  • Test leads .

  • Spray .

  • Burnisher

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Remove the cover .

  2. Remove the connecting plugs , then draw out the main body .

  3. Clean the relay using a spray .

  4. Clean the contacts of the relay using a contact burnisher .

  5. Connect relay coil terminals ( 5 , 6 ) to the testing instrument output .

  6. Connect the relay terminals ( 1 , 11 ) to the timer .

  7. Adjust output voltage of testing instrument to equal the operating value of the relay setting and record the tripping time .

  8. Repeat the step No. 7 for 80% of the operating value .

  9. Connect the relay terminals again and connect DC power supply
