Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

Over current relay CO-8 for protection of the main and auxiliary power transformers

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence like ropes with caution marks surrounding the work area .

  • Unit is out of service .

  • Disconnect DC power supply .

Tools and Equipment

  • AC Multi-amp model MS-2-E .

  • Timer model MCS-5N .

  • Avometer

  • Test leads .

  • Spray .

  • Burnisher

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Disconnect the terminals of the relay and get it out of the panel .

  2. Clean the relay using a spray .

  3. Clean contacts of the relay using contact burnisher .

  4. Connect the relay coil terminals ( 8 , 9 ) to the multi-amp output .

  5. Connect the relay contact terminals ( 1 , 10 ) to the timer .

  6. Adjust the multi-amp output at 200% of relay current tap and record the tripping time .

  7. repeat step no. 6 at (300% , 400%) of relay current tap .

  8. Record these values on the test sheet .

  9. Connect the relay terminals again and connect DC power supply .

  10. Make jumper on terminals (1 , 10) to insure relay operation .

  11. Check that the indicator target of the lock-out relay is dropped .
