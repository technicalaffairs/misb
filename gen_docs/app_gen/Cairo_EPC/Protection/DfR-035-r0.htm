Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

Solid static under frequency relay SDF-1 used in protection of the generator

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence like ropes with caution marks surrounding the work area .

  • Unit is out of service .

  • Disconnect DC power supply .

Tools and Equipment

  • AC Multi-amp model MS-2-E.

  • DC power supply

  • Lock out relay .

  • Timer model MCS-5N .

  • Test leads .

  • Spray .

  • Burnisher

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Disconnect the terminals of the relay and get it out of the panel .

  2. Clean the relay using a spray .

  3. Clean the contacts of the relay using contact burnisher .

  4. Connect the relay terminals (1) with positive and terminal (2) with negative and terminal (10) with lockout relay .

  5. Connect the relay terminals (19,3) to the testing instrument output .

  6. Repeat No (5) with terminals ( 11 , 3) , (15 , 5) , (13 , 4) , (9 , 5) and ( 7 , 5) .

  7. Adjust the testing instrument output to equal the operating current of relay and record the tripping time (instantaneous) .

  8. connect the relay terminals again and connect DC power supply .
