Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

The burner fuel oil boosters P/Ps (3 per unit) have capacity of 908 L/mint at a discharge press. of 83 Kg/Cm2 and 115°C each under normal operation two P/Ps are in service the discharge press. of the P/Ps is controlled by pressure control V/V that recirculates the oil back to the suction of the burner fuel oil heaters

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the maintenance staff should the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, earplugs … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the fan motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of fan motor.

  • The fan must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery damper

Tools and Equipment

  • Contact thermometer

  • Standard set of tools

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the bearing temperature, change oil if needed or repair and change the bearing if needed.

  2. Check for excessive vibration and make alignment if needed (Normal 4.8 mm/s)

  3. Listen for unusual noise ,check the bearing and change it if needed.

  4. Check mech. seal for leakage and change the mechanical seal in case of leakage.

  5. Check the P/P for capacity and discharge press, repair any of the internal parts or change if needed.

  6. Check the inlet and outlet pipes and repair if needed.
