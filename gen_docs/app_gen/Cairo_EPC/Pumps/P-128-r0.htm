Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This pump (boiler area pit sump pump; 2 per unit) discharge the water from the boiler pit sump under boiler to water treatment area for treating

The technical specification of the condensate pump:  
RPM = 1450           Suction pressure: 304.8 mm H2O
T = 60/25C°            Discharge pressure: 6096 mm H2O      

HP = 2                     Q = 6.3 LPS   

Model = L.G 020 A,B

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the maintenance staff should the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves, earplugs … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of P/P motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of P/P motor

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

  • Electric lighting – water hose

  • Shavels, buckets, brooms

  • Grease mobilplex 47

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Lubricate bearing

  2. Listen for unusual noise and check the bearing if there is noise and change if needed

  3. Check bearing temperature

  4. Record P/P discharge press.

  5. Clean sludge from bottom of tank manually by the buckets and store in a closed containers and the get rid of them.
