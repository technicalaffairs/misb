Introduction

This document outline the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block.

The transfer Pump (3 pumps per unit) function is to transfer Mazout from storage thank to day tanks and to re-circulate the Mazout to storage tank if day tank fuel to keep the system warm and ready.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, earplugs … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker

  • Disconnect electric element terminals of motor

  • Closing V/V delivery

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

  • Dial indicator

  • Oil can – drain pan – grease gun

  • DTE heavy oil, Grease Mobilplex 47

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Change gear box oil MOBIL

  2. Relubricate P/P bearings

  3. Inspect shaft packing and keep packing leakage not more than 28 drops per min or change the packing

  4. Check P/P discharge press. and flow rate or check the internal parts of the pump and repair the defected parts.
