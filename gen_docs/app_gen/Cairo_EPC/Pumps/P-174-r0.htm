Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I3, which must be performed every two years as indicated by Y2 in the title block.

This equipment consists of six (6) double cases centrifugal P/P having twin suction first stages inter mediate press. Bleed – off from first stage, booster stage bleed - off

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for electrical equipments.

  • Close the suction and discharge valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

  • Chain hoist

Work to be Carried out

  1. Check and record vibration

  2. Inspect pump and turbine bearings

  3. Change lube oil filters (unless recently changed) or diff. press. is less than 1.4Kg/Cm2

  4. Inspect balance disc – if wear exceeds 0.030 inch drum should be replaced

  5. Inspect motor and gear box bearings (pump C)
