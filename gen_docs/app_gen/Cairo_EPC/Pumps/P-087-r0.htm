Introduction

This document outlines  the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every month and every 6 months as indicated by M1, M6 in the title block.

These pumps operate in the condensate and feed water chemical control system which provide a continuous, regulated supply of water conditioning chemicals to the condensate and feed water system
