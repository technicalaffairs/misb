Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This pumps operate in the condensate polishing system, which takes suction from acid (or caustic) storage tank and transfer the acid (or caustic) to day tank

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for electrical equipments

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check and record pump vibration

  2. If pump vibration is 1.5 to 2 of the last reading, dismantle the pump and check pump clearence

  3. Disassemble pump, check bearing and pump shaft
