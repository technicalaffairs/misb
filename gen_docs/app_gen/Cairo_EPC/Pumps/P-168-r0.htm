Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The pump takes suction from condensate storage tank and provides water for vessel backwash and rinses, resin sluicing, and chemical dilution.

The technical specification of the pump:  

Service description: Condensate polisher supply pump
Type: Horizontal – Centrifugal
Rating: 416.4 LPM
Pump head: 24.4 M
Design temp.:  27 °C
Drive HP:  5.07 
RPM: 2900
No. of pumps:  6

Safety Precaution 

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for electrical equipments

  • Close valve delivery and valve suction.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set.

  • Alignment tool.

  • New oil.(ATF220-Mobil oil)

Work to be carried out

  1. Check oil level, add if required.

  2. Change oil.
