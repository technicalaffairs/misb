Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

This pump (boiler area pit sump P/P, 2 per unit) discharge the water from the boiler pit sump under boiler to water treatment area for treating

The technical specification of the condensate pump:  

RPM = 1450

HP = 2

Model = L.G 020 A,B

Q = 6.3 LPS

Suction pressure: 304.8 mm H2O

Discharge pressure: 6096 mm H2O

T = 60/25C°

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the maintenance staff should the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, earplugs … etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of P/P motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of P/P motor.

  • The P/P must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery V/V.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set.

  • Electric lighting or any electrical source with low voltage – water hose.

  • Grease Mobilplex 47.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Lubricate the bearing.

  2. Listen for unusual noise, check the bearing and change required or any defected parts in the pump.

  3. Check bearing temperature.

  4. Record pumps discharge pressure.
