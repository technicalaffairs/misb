Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I3, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The boiler area sump pump (2 per unit) discharge the condensate from boiler area sump to water treatment area

The technical specification of the condensate pump:  
R.P.M = 1450            Suction pressure: 9144 mm H2O
T = 49/25C°               Discharge pressure: 304.8 mm H2O        

 HP = 10                    Q = 10.3 LPS

 MODEL = L.G P017 A,B

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the maintenance staff should the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, earplugs … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of P/P motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of motor

  • Isolated delivery V/V

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set

  • Electric lighting or any source of electricity with low voltage

  • Shavels, buckets, brooms

  • Water hose

  • Grease Mobilplex 47

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Lubricate bearing

  2. Start P/P and check packing leakage and change the packing if needed

  3. Listen for unusual noise and repair the cause of abnormal noise

  4. Check bearing temperature so check the oil and change if needed or check the bearing (Lubricate or change).

  5. Record P/P discharge press.

  6. Clean sludge from bottom of tank manually by the buckets, brooms and special tools and the get rid of them safely.

  7. Inspect placite lining of tank for checking or repair this layer of the tank.
