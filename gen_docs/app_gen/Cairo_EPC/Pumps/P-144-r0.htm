Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

Fuel oil pumps guarantee oil delivery to the burners for firing through two lines one to unit 5, and the other to the unit 6, each line has two fuel oil pumps and two heaters.

Technical specification:

  • Flow rate for Mazout pump = 120 m3/h

  • Temperature = 120 OC

  • Pump pressure delivery = 42 bar

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, ....etc.

  • Start the standby Mazout pump.

  • Stop & isolate the pump, and close the valves of the pump.

  • Disconnect the CB of the pump's motor.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set,  types of wrenches, rags, funnel,

  • Mobile oil, Mobile gear 627 for pump gearbox

  • Grease, Mobile/Mobilux Ep2 / Ep3 for pump bearings

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the pump.

    2.

  2. Change oil for pump gear box by opening screen, drain the old oil and fill the housing by a new oil  every 750 h.

  3. Grease the pump bearings every 800h.
