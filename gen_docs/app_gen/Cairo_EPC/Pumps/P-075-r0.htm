Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

Each blow down sump is equipped with two blow down sump pumps per unit, which is vertically mounted submerged P/Ps these P/Ps discharge the condensate to waste treatment system.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the maintenance staff should the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, earplugs … etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of motor.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard tool set, adjustable wrench.

  • Contact thermometer,

  • Temperature indicator.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Tighten all loose bolts and check for excessive vibration & listen for abnormal noise (Vibration allowable up to 4-8 mm/s).

  2. Check pump discharge press.

  3. Check bearing temperature (max 85C°) and change the oil if need or change the bearing.
