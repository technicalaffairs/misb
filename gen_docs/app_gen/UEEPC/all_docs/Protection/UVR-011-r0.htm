Introduction

The relays of the MVAG … series are instantaneous over and under voltage relays designed for general protection and signaling applications.

The first draft is initiated & tested by eng. Hany Bassuony Abdel Razek, approved by eng. Mahmoud Badawy, and accepted by general manager eng. Gamal Abdel Wahab head sector of inspection & quality control.

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety Helmet.

  • Unite or line should be out of service

Preliminary Steps to Prepare for Relay Testing

  1. This procedure is used to perform off load relay test.

  2. The test is divided into two tests

  3. The AC test to determine pick up and reset voltage.

  4. The DC test to determine the operating time.

 

AC Test  -  Phase A

  1. Connect the circuit as shown in fig. 1.

  2. Isolate the wire connected to pin 23at the relay.

  3. Switch on the Sverker and adjust the voltage to100V.

  4. Turn off the Sverker.

  5. Connect the wire to pin 23 at the relay.

  6. Switch on the Sverker to apply the 100 v  to the relay then check that the relay reset.

  7. Slowly decrease the voltage until the relay picks up and closes its contact.

  8. This is the pick up voltage.

  9. disconnect the trip signals from stop points at the severker.

  10. Slowly raise the voltage until the relay resets and open its contact.

  11. This is the reset voltage.

  12. Switch off the Sverker.

  13. Repeat the steps above for phase B and C

Note for phase B voltage points 25 & 26 and stop 6 & 8
         for phase C voltage points 27 & 28 and stop 10 & 12

 

DC Test

  1. Connect the circuit as shown in fig. 2.

  2. Isolate the wire connected to pin 14 at the timer MVTT14.

  3. Switch on the Sverker and adjust the DC voltage to 110 v.

  4. Turn off the Sverker.

  5. Connect the wire to pin 14 at the timer MVTT14.

  6. Reset the timer of the Sverker.

  7. Switch on the Sverker to apply the 110 v  DC to the timer .

  8. Check that the timer picks up and closes its contact after the shown time on the LCD of the Sverker.

  9. This is pick up time compare it with the setting time.
