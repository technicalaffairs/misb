Introduction

The type MIC relays are digital microprocessor based, non directional over current relays that protect against phase to phase and phase to ground faults it performs the following functions:

Inverse over current, including four characteristic curves, and four values for definite time protection, as well as instantaneous over current protection with programmable delay; all in the same relay

Phase and ground current measurement

Phase and ground current recorder and the operating time of the last trip

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, anti static hand probe to avoid electrical shock …etc

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required       

  • The generator must be isolated and work permit required.

  • Switch off DC power supply before inserting or withdrawing any card from the relay module.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Pick up current

  2. Time test

  3. Function test

To Get pick up current

  1. Connect the circuit as shown in figure by

  2. Disconnect the wire A8 from the relay to prevent lock out relay from operating

  3. Connect the manta current terminals at phase ADB8,ADB9 AT ROSETA XL AT RACK 6 because this terminals are connected to the relay coil terminals B8,B9

  4. Connect the relay points A8&A3 TO manta stop points

  5. Start increasing current from zero until pick up

To Get Time

  1. Use the same connection

  2. Adjust the current by short manta current terminals at 1.5 I

  3. Start inject current and record the time

  4. Adjust the current by short manta current terminals at 2 I

  5. Start inject current and record the time
