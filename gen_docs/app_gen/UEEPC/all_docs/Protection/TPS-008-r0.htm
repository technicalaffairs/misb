Preliminary Steps to Prepare for Relay Testing

This procedure is used to perform off load relay test.

The test is to determine the pick up voltage and time.

Test procedure

  1. Connect the circuit as shown in fig. 1.

  2. Switch off the DC supply feeding the relay.

  3. Adjust the time setting to zero.

  4. Switch on the DC supply feeding the relay.

  5. Switch on the severker.

  6. Raise the voltage slowly until the relay picks up and the red led lights.

  7. This is the pick up voltage.

  8. Switch off the severker.

  9. Switch off the D.C supply feeding the relay.

  10. Adjust the time setting to its normal value.

  11. Switch on the DC supply feeding the relay.

  12. Disconnect the wire at point 27 on the relay.

  13. Switch on the severker.

  14. Adjust the voltage to little more than the pick up voltage.

  15. Switch off the severker.

  16. Connect the wire at point 27 on the relay.

  17. Switch on the severker to apply the adjusted voltage suddenly.

  18. The relay trips after a time this is the pick up time.

  19. Compare the pick up voltage and pick up time with the settings.
