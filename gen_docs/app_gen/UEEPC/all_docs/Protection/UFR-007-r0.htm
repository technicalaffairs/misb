Introduction

This document lists the testing procedures P1, to be performed every six months bases as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The purpose of the relay is basically to give an indication that under frequency occurs into the generator, this protection is used to protect synchronous generators and prime mover. Which give alarm signal for 49.0 hz for 1.5 second delay & trip signal for 48.0 hz for 3.0 second delay, the feeding of this device from voltage transformers on its unit

  • Capable of block graphics

  • 9 pin-to-25 pin adapter

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

The unit is out of Service

  1. Check the unit out of service

  2. Connect the serial interface of the PC to the interface connector on the front of the REG 316

  3. Connect the cable between the serial interface connector on the frontplate of he protection equipment (25 pin sub-d socket) & on the PC (9pin or 25 pin sub-d plug) is an optical fibre cable type YX216-1

  4. Operate REG 316 program and simulate the under frequency protection by in binary inputs

  5. Reset the under frequency relay to its normal position

  6. Record the values (results) on the test sheet
