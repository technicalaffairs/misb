Introduction

This document lists the testing procedures P1, to be performed every six months bases as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The purpose of the relay is basically to give an indication that over frequency occurs into the generator, which give trip signal for 52.5 hz the feeding of this device from voltage transformers on 33kv bus bar

  1. When VT1 off the DC feeding will off instantaneously then will come back after one second in this case the feeding will change offer into VT2

  2. When VT1 come back the DC feeding will off instantaneously then will come back after 1.5 second

This protection can isolate by using disconnecting switch (H). 1H for unit 1 & 2H for unit 2 & 3H for unit 3

Work to be Carried Out

The unit out of Service

  1. Check the unit out of service.

  2. Disconnect the disconnecting switch for anther two units which in service

  3. Connect the variable frequency device into the terminals relay of the offer frequency device

  4. Inject the offer frequency device

  5. Reset the offer frequency relay to its normal position

  6. Record thr value (results) on the test sheet
