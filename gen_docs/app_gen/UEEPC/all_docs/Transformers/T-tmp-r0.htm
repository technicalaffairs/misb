Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • The staff should not carry any metals or keys in their pocket that may it fail in the transformers.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, safety belt.... etc

  • The unit is out of service and the transformers must be isolated and proper portable earth is required in each sides.

  • The CB at 3.3 KV must be in the test position.

  • It is recommended to use only fiberglass or wood ladders when working in the switchyard.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set as spanners, screwdrivers …. etc

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes .... etc

  • A set of portable earth for electrical discharge.

  • Wood ladder.

  • Fine clothes and cleaning materials for cleaning purposes

  • Voltage indicator

 

Works to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Be a sure that the two transformers are isolated from each side.

  2. Be a sure that the CB 3.3 kv in test position and the operating air is closed.

  3. Put the portable earth at 132KV side and at 11KV (generator side).

Procedures

  1. Make a visual inspection about the bushings if there a leak of oil a round them and check that the bolted connections are in a good condition. 

  2. Make the maintenance required for any hot spot if exist (from the Infera-red diagnostics or the visual diagnostic) which taken before.

  3. Clean all porcelain surfaces of the two transformer bushings and supporting insulators.

  4. Clean the bushings of the transformers at 132Kv and 11kv.

  5. Clean the CT of the phases and the lighting arrestors.

  6. Clean the transformers surface.

  7. At the same time make the maintenance and cleaning for the CB of the aux. Transformer at 3.3kv side and check the oil of the CB if it is dirty we change it.

  8. Check oil levels in tank, conservator and all oil filled bushings.

  9. Check oil temperature and winding temperature indicators, record maximum temperature indication from drag hand before resetting drag hand.

  10. Make a visual inspection of all cooling fans, remove any debris from fan screen by using blower, switch on cooling fan and listen to any abnormal noise.

  11. Check the condition of the silica gel breather, replace the silica gel if one or more sections are discolored, and check for oil level in bottom oil cup.

  12.  Open the washing water for main and auxiliary transformers.

  13. Dry the body of the main and auxiliary transformers by cleaning clothes.

  14. Turn on the cooling fans for drying manually.

  15. Finally we make a visual inspection at the two transformers to be a sure that there is nothing missed at them.

Return the two Transformers back to be ready for Operation

  1. First ensure that there no any thing missed at the two transformers and finishing the testing at the two transformers body.

  2. Remove the portable earth from 11 kv and 132 kv side.

  3. Restored the silica gel breather to its position.

  4. Get the wood ladder out of the two transformers.

  5. Restored the CB of 3.3 kv to its normal position.

  6. Make cancellation for the work permit (P.T.W).
