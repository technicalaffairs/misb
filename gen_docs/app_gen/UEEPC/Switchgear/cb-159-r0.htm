Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every three years as indicated by Y3 in the above title block.

 

The first draft initiated by eng.Gamal Nady, reviewed by director eng. Anwar Saber Barakat., revised by Mokhtar Abed El Rahman Awad, accepted by general director eng.Abed El Naser Maarof, and approved by eng. Gamal Deiab Head Sector of Assuit Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes .... etc

  • The breaker must be isolated from the network and earthed

Tools and Equipment

  • Maintenance tool kit, screw driver

  • Safety helmet, and safety shoes

  • 1000 v megger, 500 megger

  • Spanner tester, Silicone grease

  • Oscillograph probes,

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check visualy the correct indication when the breaker is operated

  2. Check for contact state at each operating position for quxiliary switches

  3. Check the operation of switch at low pressare 245 bar (normal oper)

  4. Check the protection circuit by creating phase disagreement

  5. Check the operation of breaker at rated and locking pressure

Enterior in General

  1. Check the tighening of bolts and nuts

  2. Check the rusting or peeling of paints

Operating Mechanism Box

  1. Check the oil level according to fig (1)

Measuring Test

  1. Measure the insulation resistauce for control circuit between terminal and ground with 500 megger which must be 2MΩ or more

  2. Measure the insulation resistance for main circuit between main circuit and gas tank with 1000 v megger which must be 1000 MΩ or more

  3. Check the dis connection of spale heater (C-15) by check the current between the space heater terminals

  4. Check the operation of the oil pump by check the initial oil charging time which must be 10 % from the data obtained last time

  5. Check the time requiered from raising the pressure from 315 bar to 335 bar must be 210 sec

  6. Check the accumulator gas (N2) pressure by starting up the pump at zero oil pressure then the pressure rises at stroke from several tens to about 200 bar the pressure which rose in this manner is the gas pressure.
