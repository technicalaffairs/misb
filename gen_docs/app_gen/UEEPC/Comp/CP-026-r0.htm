 Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block.   

The first draft is initiated by eng. Sherif Mahrous Omar tested by eng. Nafad Kamel Mahmoud, revised by quality engineer Gamal Abd Elwahab, accepted by technical expert eng. Hassan Elbehiry and approved by eng. Mohamed Gamal Diab Head sector of El-Kuriemat power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glasses,... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety glasses ...etc

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Check oil level in compressor crank case, but only with the compressor at rest, the oil level should never fall below the sight-glass

  2. Check for any unusual noises or vibrations

  3. Check for air leaks in piping and pipe connections

  4. Read and record gauge pressures and compressor running time indicator

  5. Clean compressor room floor
