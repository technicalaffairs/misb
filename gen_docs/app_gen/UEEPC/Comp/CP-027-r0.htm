Approved  Date :    31 Aug 2004

Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases or 400 hours of operation as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft is initiated by eng. Sherif Mahrous Omar tested by eng. Nafad Kamel Mahmoud, revised by UEEPCo. quality control general director eng Gamal Abd Elwahab, accepted by technical expert eng. Hassan Elbehiry and approved by eng. Mohamed Gamal Diab Head sector of El-Kureimat power station.

 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

  • Switch off 380 Volt AC supply to compressor .

  • Switch off 220 Volt AC control power

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of wrenches and screwdrivers

  • Wiping material and cleaning liquid

  • Oil for crankcase

  • Spare parts (air filters, oil filter, etc ...)

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Change the oil at intervals of 400 operating hours, or every one year which ever comes first.

  2. Remove screw plug to change oil, with oil strainer attached, let the oil drain off in to a container, clean out strainer with solvent, re-insert strainer and screw plug

  3. Fill compressor with new approved oil, approximately 3.5 liters of new oil is required

  4. After the oil change operation is completed the compressor must be started several times in succession since the suction line to the oil pump was emptied during the oil change operation.

  5. Check air filter cartridge, if it is dirty, blow the cartridge out with compressed air from the inside outwards, if paper elements in cartridge are damaged, install a new filter cartridge

  6. The valves of all compressor stages must be inspected and cleaned from all contamination and oily carbon deposits. It is good practice to have a spare set of compressor valves available to speed up the overhaul procedure .

  7. See if there are any cracks, damage and loosening on the air valves, suction and delivery valves.

  8. Remove the valves, and valves cleaned in gasoline or cleaning oil.

  9. When assembling valves, do not tighten one-sidedly.

  10. Supply valves a little oil.

  11. Remove first stage cylinder head, inspect and clean/replace suction valve and outlet supply valve, take great care that the valves are properly seated and the valve retainers are correctly placed and tightened

  12. Be careful to valve plates and valve seats not to damage on their surfaces.

  13. Remove second stage cylinder head, inspect and clean/replace suction valve and outlet supply

  14. Remove a diaphragm and valve seat. Check whether any crack or damage are found on their surfaces, clean /replace correctly and tightened. check whether pistons move smoothly.

  15. Properly clean out oil and water traps below the unloaders.

  16. Switch on compressor to blow out supply lines from carbon deposits for each successive stage.

  17. In the same way blow out the oil cooling lines from oil/water deposits

  18. Check fan wheel and blades, oil pump and all pipe coupling connectors

  19. When all suction and supply lines are connected, test run the compressor, observing the correct gauge pressure for each stage

  20. Check the oil pressure monitor switch, momentarily close the ball cock the compressor must shut down after about 10 seconds

  21. Check the operation of compressor room thermostat, controlling the rooms.

  22. Check the non return valves from the main tank to the compressor unit

  23. Check the compressed air main tank, momentarily opening the bottom drain valve to expel any water.
