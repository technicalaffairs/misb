Introduction

This document lists the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every four years as indicated by Y4 in the title block.

The first draft is initiated by eng. Sherif Mahrous Omar tested by eng. Nafad Kamel Mahmoud, revised by UEEPCo. quality control general director eng Gamal Abd Elwahab, accepted by technical expert eng. Hassan Elbehiry and approved by eng. Mohamed Gamal Diab Head sector of El-Kureimat power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as  helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

  • Switch off 380 Volt AC supply to compressor .

  • Switch off 220 Volt AC control power

  • Use standard personal safety equipment, safety helmet, safety boots, and safety glasses.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety glasses

Work to be carried out

  1. Check crank shaft, connecting rod, piston and cylinder if much wear found it must be replaced.

  2. Check LP piston ring (Wear limit is 1.0mm of end clearance & Normal end clearance is 0.3mm to 0.5mm).

  3. Check HP piston ring (Wear limit is 0.6mm of end clearance& Normal end clearance is 0.15mm to 0.25mm).

  4. Check oil separator (clean inside).
