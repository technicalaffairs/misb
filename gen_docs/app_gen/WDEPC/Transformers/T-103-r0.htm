Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedure P1, to be performed every two years as indicated by Y2 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit required.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety Helmet, safety shoes, pair of gloves ... etc

  • Disconnect the transformer from both High & Low sides before the maintenance.

  • Ensure that both sides of the transformer were temporary grounded.

  • Ensure that you work on  the disconnected transformer.

Tools and Equipment

  • .Electrical equipment kit

  • Dry cloth, dry air from air blower.

  • Megger 2500, 500 V.

Work to be Carried Out

Transformer HV test

  1. Measure the winding insulation resistance.

  2. Measure the DC winding resistance.

  3. Measure the turns ratio between high voltage side and low voltage side.

  4. Record the result.

Oil Insulation

  1. Measure the dielectric strength.

  2. Measure the neutralization number.

  3. Measure the dissolved gases in the oil.

Oil preservation system

  1. Check oil level and for proper relation with oil temperature.

  2. Check for oil leak.

  3. Check that the oil stop valve between tank and conservator is open

Transformer voltage tap changer

  1. Check for oil leaks.

  2. Check tap position.

  3. Check that bolts are tightened.

Cooling equipment

  1. Clean the heat exchanger surface.

  2. Check for oil leaks and rust.

Tank

  1. Check for oil leaks.

  2. Inspection of finished surfaces see if rain water gets collected on the tank top causes rusting on edges and corners.

Pressure relief device

  1. Inspect the pressure relief plate.

  2. Inspect the alarm contact.

  3. Check for oil leaks.

Bouchhltz relay

  1. Check whether or not any gas found in the relay.

  2. If any free gas is found in relay check the gas for decomposing gases such as hydrogen, methane, ethane, acetylene, carbon monoxide and so forth.

Protective relay

  1. Carry out the operation test

  2. Inspect the control circuit

Check sillicagel

  1. Check the color, change if required.
