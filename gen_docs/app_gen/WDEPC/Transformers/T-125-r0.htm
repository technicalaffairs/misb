Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Mostafa El-Zalabany, tested by eng. Samir Ouf Hassob, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia Diab : WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes and safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Ensure that the transformer was isolated, and proper earthing on both sides is done.

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable earth for discharging.

  • Complete tool kit of screw drivers and common keys.

  • Blower.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check oil level in transformer tank and conservator tank.

  2. Check for abnormal sounds.

  3. Check oil leaks visually from radiators, flanges, pipes and transformer tank.

  4. Clean the transformer tank & bushing porcelain from oil leakage and other dirty and retighten all joints leakage oil.

  5. Check the discoloration of silica gel in breather due to moisture in breathed air, if the color has turned to pink by more than 2/3 of total quantity (dry out or exchange it).

  6. Check the tightening of low & high voltage connections.

  7. Check transformer control cabinet and use blower for cleaning it.

  8. Take oil sample from transformer tank for analysis and break point test.

  9. Check the conditions of the earthing resistor.

  10. Check the good working of Buchholz relay and the protective relay of tap- changer.

  11. Check and inspect the good working of fire fighting system.

  12. Check for cracks damages and traces of oil leaking in the pressure relief plate.

  13. If the bushing is provided with a oil level gauge, check oil level and check for oil leaks.

Insulation of Windings

  1. Measure the winding insulation resistance.

  2. Measure the dielectric loss factor (tan δ) of winding.

  3. Inspect by other insulation judging methods. 

Insulating oil

  1. Measure the dielectric strength.

  2. Measure the neutralization number.

  3. Measure the water content.

  4. Gas analysis.

Buchhloz Relay

  1. Check whether or not any gas found in the relay.

  2. If any free gas is found in the relay, check the gas for decomposable gases such as hydrogen, methane, ethane, acetylene, carbon monoxide and so forth.
