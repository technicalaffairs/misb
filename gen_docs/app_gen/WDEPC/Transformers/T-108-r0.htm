Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

  • The transformer must be was isolated and proper grounding is required

  • Prepare necessary tools and spare parts after due planning.

  • Before commencing work make sure that the machine is isolated from the power supply and make sure again by making use of electric detector that the circuit is dead.

  • Ground transformer bushing terminals while working on the transformer, these instructions such as internal inspection please ask  Before commencing work make sure that the machine is isolated from the power supply and make sure again by making use of electric detector that the circuit is dead.

Work to be Carried Out

Routine maintenance and inspection

Transformer Temperature, and Load

  1. Read and record oil and winding temperature.

  2. Record ambient temperature, load (MW, MVAR) and voltage.

Notes:

  • Transformer temperature directly affects the life of the insulating material.

  • Maximum temperature rise limits are specified for the both oil and winding temperature.

  • In the daily inspection, check not only that temperatures are held within the maximum limit, but also that the temperature lies in a satisfactory range in relation to their values in the test report, load condition and ambient temperature.

  • Overloading of transformer should be made only in accordance with the applicable standard. 

Oil Level

Check oil level gauges.

Noises

Check for abnormal sounds.

 

Loose Connections

  1. Check for loose connections such as those found in main circuits, grounding circuits, auxiliary circuits, and in foundation bolts and others.

  2. Check valves for looseness.

Oil Leakage

Check for oil leaks in connections such as those of valves and meters-especially in gasket joints-welded parts and bushing .

Silica gel Breather

  1. Pay attention to the discoloration of the absorbent.

  2. Check the level of the sealing oil in cup.

Note

  • The absorbent will turn from blue into pink if its moisture content becomes in excess of 20 or 25% in the gravimetric ratio When more than 70% if its volume is found discolored replace or dry the   absorbent.

  • Too much sealing oil sometimes contaminates absorbent while less sealing oil causes ineffective sealing function. Check to see if the oil level is held within a specified limit at all times.

Cooling Equipment

For forced oil cooled or fan cooled type transformers, check for abnormal rotating sounds and vibration Check for dust on radiator surface.

On Load Tap Changer 

  1. Check for operating sounds.

  2. Record the number of tap changing operations.

  3. Check oil level gauge of conservator.

Control Cubicle

  1. Check for rust.

  2. Check that bolts are tight.

  3. Check that wire color.

  4. Check for losses connection.

  5. Check of insulation resistance.

Insulating Oil

  1. Measure the dielectric strength.

  2. Measure the  neutralization number.

  3. Measure the water content.

  4. Gas analysis.

Oil Preservation System

  1. Oil is delivered in drums tanks tank trucks on arrival

  2. Check the containers to ascertain that no water has entered into the oil by analysis of oil sampling.

  3. It is useful to perform a measurement of the dielectric dissipation factor (tan delta) and a measurement of the electric strength.

  4. Check oil level and for proper relation with oil temperature.

  5. Check for oil leaks.

  6. Check that the oil stop valve between tank and conservator is open.

  7. Refer to separate instructions, if any.

On  Load Tap changer (If provided)

Refer to separate instructions.

 

Breather

  1. Check the silica gel breather.

  2. Check the oil level.

Bushing ( Porcelain type )

The types and numbers of bushings mounted on this unit are as follows :

  • Three HV bushings  type I.P.C.O . SF6-300 K.V 630A.

  • One HV neutral bushing type I.P.C.O. 170kv -400 A.

  • LV bushings 12kv -2000A.

  • One LV neutral bushing 12 K.V-2000A.

  1. Inspect the terminal and bushing top shield.

  2. Check for loose connection or change in color.

  3. Check  for contamination and damage of porcelain.

  4. Check for oil leaks.

  5. Clean the porcelains

Tank

  1. This transformer is equipped with a vacuum-proof conservator ( atmoseal type) . this type of conservator  prevents any direct contact between oil and air.

  2. Check for oil leaks.

  3. Inspect the finished surfaces. See if rainwater gets collected on the tank top and causes rusting on edges and corners.

Pressure Relief Device

  1. Inspect the pressure relief plate.

  2. Inspect the alarm contacts.

  3. Check for oil leaks.

 

 

 

Buchholz Relay

  1. Check whether or not any gas found in the relay.

  2. If any free gas is found in the relay. Check the gas for decomposable gases such as hydrogen, methane, ethane, acetylene, carbon mono oxide and so forth.

  3. Refer to separate instructions.

  3. Water content.

  4. To be conducted in case the dielectric strength and neutralization number are poor.
