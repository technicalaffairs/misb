Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes and safety belt when cleaning suspension insulators.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Ensure that the transformer was isolated, and proper earthed on both sides.

Tools and Equipment

  • Portable earth for discharging.

  • Complete tool kit of screw drivers and common keys.

  • Blower.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check oil level in transformer Tank and Conservator tank.

  2. Check for abnormal sounds.

  3. Check oil leaks visually from radiators, flanges, pipes and transformer tank.

  4. Clean the transformer tank & bushing porcelain from oil leakage and other dirty and retighten all joints leakage oil.

  5. Check the discoloration of silica gel in breather due to moisture in breathed air, If the color has turned to pink by more than 2/3 of total quantity (dry out or exchange it).

  6. Check the tightening of low & high voltage connections.

  7. Check transformer control cabinet and use blower for cleaning it.

  8. Take oil sample from transformer tank for analysis and break point test.

  9. Check the conditions of the earthing resistor

  10. Check the good working of buchhloz relay and the protective relay of tap-changer

  11. Check and inspect the good working of fire fighting system
