Introduction

This document outlines the equipment  inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The first draft is initiated by eng. Emad Mohamed El-Tohamy, tested by eng. Sawsan Elsaid Ali, reviewed by eng. Gaber Aly Abd El-Mohsen, accepted by eng. Mohamed Abd El-Sadik Ahmed: general manager, approved by eng Aly Anor El-Faumy, and certified by eng. Mona Ahmed Al Sherqawy: WDEPC consultant engineer "A" and MPIS Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, … etc.

  • Open the unit area CB and turn switch to local position.

  • Disconnect switches (89.1),(89.2),(89.3) and (89.4) rack it out and lock it.

  • Connect earth switch 57 rack and lock it.

  • Disconnect potential cells and lock it.

  • Rack out 6kv CB and lock the bus bar inside the cell.

  • Disconnect neutral junctions and lock it.

  • Put portable earthing switch around the working area.

  • Connect earth switch before and after the main CB.

Tools and Equipment

  • Megger

  • Avometer

  • Greasing oil

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check alarm.

  2. Carryout the trip tests on turbine - Boiler and generator circuits.

  3. Re-tighten bolts.

  4. Heating of silica-gel.

  5. Clean the unit transformer LJP (and auxiliary transformer).

  6. Measure transformer insulation & tap changer if possible.

  7. Check the neutral point earthing connection of the transformer.

  8. Calibrate the oil temperature instruments.

  9. Calibrate the winding temperature instruments.
