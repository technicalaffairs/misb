Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block. 

When the generator and power transformer are over excited, the leakage flux increases and the results in heavy hysteresis and eddy current losses in non laminat parts occur. If the temperature rises due to the excessive losses, the insulation can be damaged and flashovers may occur.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ahmad Galal, tested by eng. Mohamed Rabee, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam of Damanhour power station and certified by eng. Gaber Farahat WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

  • The transformer must be was isolated.

  • Make sure CT Secondary circuits are shorted.

  • Ensure all CT path elements are secure after any test disturbance.

Tools and Equipment

  • ISA test set type ART/3 or Omicron

  • Voltmeter, Ammeter, test plug, and test leads

Work to be Carried Out

Relays Preparation

  1. Review the manual and drawings.

  2. Prepare the previous or commissioning results of the relay testing.

  3. Check the wires of the relay by hand.

  4. Check the AC source is available to the tester.

  5. Check the existence of the DC sources in panels.

  6. Disconnect the trip leads of the relay and connect one of them to the timer of the tester.

  7. Check all the leads used in tests

 

Test Steps

  1. Connect the voltage leads of a tester unit to the relay points according to the drawings and scheme.

  2. Insert the test handle in test switch.

  3. Connect the voltage leads of a tester unit to (9A_10A) in test hand the relay points according to the drawings and scheme.

  4. Inject voltage to each couple of points (9A-10A) till the red flag of the device displays.

  5. Record the results pick up value in 1st alarm at 110v.

  6. Decrees the voltage until the relay is reset and measure the voltage at reset 108v.

  7. Record the results reset value at 108v.

  8. Time test in the relay is connect terminal timer to{ B50_x5  (33_34)}in relay as connect terminal unit voltage to{B50_x2 (1_2)}in relay as inject the voltage to pick up relay and Record the results time .

  9. Switch off secondary injection t test set.

  10. Inject voltage to each couple of points (9A-10A) till the red flag of the device displays.

  11. Record the results pick up value in 2sd trip at 111v.

  12. Decrease the voltage until the relay is reset and measure the voltage at reset 110v.

  13. Time test in the relay is connect terminal timer to{ B50_x5  (31_32)}in relay as connect terminal unit voltage to{B50_x2 (1_2)}in relay as inject the voltage to pick up relay and Record the results time .

  14. Switch off secondary injection t test set.

  15. Return the relay to the panel.

  16. Return all connections to the normal position as before the test.

  17. Close the work permit
