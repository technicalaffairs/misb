Introduction

MDP relays are digital microprocessor based ,non-directional over current relays that protect against phase to phase  and phase to ground faults.

MDP relays performs the following functions:

  • Inverse over current including four characteristic curves and four values for definite time .

  • Instantaneous over current protection with phase and ground  current measurement

  • Operating time and fault current of the last trip.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

Work to be Carried Out

Relays Preparation

  1. Review the manual and drawings.

  2. Prepare the previous or commissioning results of the relay testing.

  3. Check the wires of the relay by hand.

  4. Check the AC source is available to the tester.

  5. Check the existence of the DC sources in panels.

  6. Disconnect the trip leads of the relay and connect one of them to the timer of the tester.

  7. Check all the leads used in tests

Test Steps 

  1. Connect the current leads of a tester unit to the relay points according to the drawings and scheme to test phase pick-up by increasing and decreasing the injection current gradually until the relay pick-up then record  the pick-up and resetting values in test sheet.

  2. inject current equal to 2 x pick-up value to test operating time then record the result in test sheet .by reduce the setting of instantaneous to 2x pick-up value and inject current and record that time.

  3. Repeat the steps for each phase according to the test sheet.

  4. Connect the current leads of a tester unit to the relay points according to the drawings and scheme to test ground pick-up by increasing and decreasing the injection current gradually until the relay pick-up then record  the pick-up and resetting values in test sheet.

  5. Testing the Relay and Protection Outputs

  6. Carefully follow these test procedure steps 

  7. Verify the test result match the relay setting.

  8. Test trip the circuit breaker associated with the relay.

Trip Test

  1. Request the operator to switch on the main circuit breaker.

  2. Operate the output auxiliary contact by injection to trip the circuit breaker.

  3. Check that the circuit breaker tripped.
