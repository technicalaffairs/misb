Introduction

These procedure details the steps required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of V32 relay. It is recommended this procedure be carried out an yearly bases the first preliminary steps to prepare for relay testing describes the activates required prior to testing.

The first draft is imitated & tested by eng. Mostafa Hasas, accepted by general director eng. Farag Abo Yousef, approved by eng. Abd ElMohsen Abd ElKhafar.

This document revised by eng.Mona Ahmed Elsharkawy  inspection & quality control 's  head sector and MPIS coordinator in WDEPC  

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Disconnect the DC power supply

Tools and Equipment

  • ISA test set (secondary injection voltage)

  • Avometer .

 

Work to be carried out
 Preparation

  1.  Review technical reference and manufacture manual.

  2. Obtain a copy of relay setting from field maintenance file and  compare it with  setting on the relay

 

Testing Procedures

  1. Isolate the secondary terminal of the voltage transformer.

  2. Connect the voltage test set terminals to phase R (1) ,phase S ( 3 ) and phase T (7)

  3. Inject the the three phase voltage (each phase = 57.6 V) to the relay then decrease the voltage to phase R gradually until the red flag for phase R appears then record VR (pick - up)

  4. Increase the voltage to phase R gradually until the red flag removed them record VR (reset)

  5. Repeat the steps from 3-4 but to the phase S, T

 

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service

  1. Return relay to service by removing the test terminal.

  2. Make sure VT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  3. If any equipment associated with the relay has been isolated, it should be returned to service by the following restoration steps

  • Breaker trip coils.

  • Breaker failure scheme associated this relay.

  • Disturbance alarm and fault recorder associated with this relay. Make sure VT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  1. Return the work permit.
