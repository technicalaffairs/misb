Introduction
This document lists the equipment inspection I1, to be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block
These procedure details the steps required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of A11 relay.
The first draft is imitated  and tested by eng. Mostafa Hassan, accepted by general director eng. Farag Abu Yousef, approved by eng. Abdel-Mohsen Abdel-Ghafar: head sector of Kafr El-Dawar PS.
This document revised by eng. Mona Ahmed Elsharkawy: WDEP inspection & quality control 's  head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precaution 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category
  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.
  • Safety fence like ropes with caution marks surrounding the work area .
  • The equipment isolation is required

Tools and Equipment

  • ISA test set (secondary injection voltage)
  • Clamp ampere or Avometer

 

Work to be Carried out               

Preparation

  1. Review technical reference and manufacture manual

  2. Obtain a copy of relay setting from field maintenance file and compare it with setting on the relay

  3. Obtain outage for transformer or equipment  protected by this relay.

 

Test Steps

  1. Connect the current test set terminals to point (4,5) for phase R

  2. Inject the current for phase R then increase the current gradually until the red flag for phase R appears then record IR (pick - up)

  3. Decrease the current gradually until the red flag disappear then record IR (reset)

 

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service 

  1. Return relay to service by removing the plug test

  2. Make sure CT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  3. If any equipment associated with the relay has been isolated, it should be returned to service by the following restoration steps:

  • Breaker trip coils
  • Breaker failure scheme associated this relay.
  • Disturbance alarm and fault recorder associated with this relay.
  • Make sure CT secondary circuits are in normal condition

  1. Return the work permit.
