Introduction

These procedure details the steps required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of D21 relay . it is recommended this procedure be carried out every 6 month bases on the first preliminary steps to prepare for relay testing describes the activates required prior to testing .

The first draft initiated by eng. Moustafa Osman ,tested and revised by eng.Ali Ghater, accepted by eng. Farag Abo Youseff :general manager, approved by eng. Gomaa Ghater:  head sector of Kafr el Dawar Power Station, and certified by eng. Gaber Mohmed Frahat : WDEPC inspection and quality control general director and MPIS coordinator.

 

Safety Precautions

  • The equipment isolation is required.

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety shoes, safety helmet ... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary voltage injection test set ( ISA Tester )

  • AVO meter

  • Clamp ampere.

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

 

  1.  Review technical reference and manufacture manual.

  2. Obtain a copy of relay setting from field maintenance file and compare it with setting on the relay.

 

Testing Procedures

  1. Isolate the secondary terminal of the voltage transformer.

  2. Connect the voltage test set terminals to point (3 ) for phase R and neutral voltage T to point (4)

  3. Connect the current terminals to point (1) IN and (2) OUT for phase R.

  4. Connect the timer test set to points (18,19).

  5. Inject the line voltage 100 V to the relay .

  6. Inject the phase current to the relay 5 A  .

  7. Change the current angle gradually until the relay operate .

  8. Record the angle of operate relay .

  9. Inject the phase current to the relay 3 A  .

  10. Change the current angle gradually until the relay operate .

  11. Record the angle of operate relay .

  12. Record time and current .  

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service

 

1.      Return relay to service by removing the test terminal .

2.      Make sure VT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

3.      If any equipment associated with the relay has been isolated, it should be returned to service.

  1. Return the work permit .
