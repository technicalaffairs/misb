Introduction

This procedure outlines the testing procedures P1, to be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Rasha Soliman, tested by eng  Mohamed Rabee, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Mohamed Abou Aiasha head sector of Damanhour power station and and certified by eng. Gaber Farahat WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet ... etc

  • Unit must be shutdown.

  • Circuit breaker open and local.

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection test set .

  • Suitable tool set.

  • Avometer, test leads

Work to be carried out

  1. Connect the current test set terminals to point (1-2) .

  2. Connect the timer test set terminal to point (15-16) .

  3. Inject the current then increase the current gradually until the red flag appears then record I (pick - up) and record the time .

  4. Decrease the current gradually until the red flag disappear then record I (reset)

  5. For find the time for trip increase the current until the timer red flag appear them record the time for trip .

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service 

  1. Return relay to service by removing the plug test

  2. Make sure CT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  3. If any equipment associated with the relay has been isolated, it should be returned to service by the following restoration steps:
         * Breaker trip coils
         * Breaker failure scheme associated this relay.
         * Disturbance alarm and fault recorder associated with this relay.
         * Make sure CT secondary circuits are in normal condition

  4. Return the work permit.
