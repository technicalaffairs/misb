Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1 to completely check the proper operation and calibration of the relay, this procedures must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block. the first preliminary steps to prepare for relay testing describes the activates required prior to testing.

The first draft initiated by eng. Rasha Soliman, tested by eng  Mohamed Rabee, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Mohamed Abou Aiasha head sector of Damanhour power station and and certified by eng. Gaber Farahat WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet & safety shoes.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Disconnect the DC power supply

Tools and Equipment

  • ISA test set (secondary injection voltage)

  • Avometer.

Work to be carried out

Preparation

  1. Review technical reference and manufacture manual.

  2. Obtain a copy of relay setting from field maintenance file and  compare it with  setting on the relay

 Testing Procedures

  1. Isolate the secondary terminal of the voltage transformer.

  2. Connect the voltage test set terminals to phase R (1) ,phase S ( 2) and phase T (3)

  3. Inject the the three phase voltage (each phase = 50 V) to the relay then decrease the voltage to phase R gradually until the red flag for phase R appears then record VR (pick - up)

  4. Increase the voltage to phase R gradually until the red flag removed them record VR (reset)

  5. Repeat the steps from 3-4 but to the phase S, T

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service

  1. Return relay to service by removing the test terminal.

  2. Make sure VT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  3. If any equipment associated with the relay has been isolated, it should be returned to service by the following restoration steps
         * Breaker trip coils.
         * Breaker failure scheme associated this relay.
         * Disturbance alarm and fault recorder associated with this relay. Make sure VT secondary
            circuits are in normal condition.

  4. Return the work permit.
