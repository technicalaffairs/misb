Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Rasha Soliman, tested by eng  Mohamed Rabee, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Mohamed Abou Aiasha head sector of Damanhour power station and and certified by eng. Gaber Farahat WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet ... etc

  • Unit must be shutdown.

  • Circuit breaker open and local.

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary injection test set .

  • Suitable tool set.

  • Avometer, test leads

  • Variable Resistance

Work to be carried out

Preparation

  1. Isolate the secondary terminals of VT.

Testing the Relay

  1. Open the links on terminals (1,2) to isolate the VT. secondary circuit

  2. Connect test leads between the voltage terminals (1,2) .

  3. Connect test leads between the bridge Resistance terminals  to terminal of the relay common (5) and the second terminal to the ground .

  4. Increase the voltage gradually until the 86 vac value

  5. Record the voltage value in the test sheet .

  6. Increase the gradually Variable Resistance until pick-up value

  7. Record the resistance value in the test sheet.

  8. Decrease the injection voltage gradually to Zero

  9. Decrease the fault Resistance gradually to Zero

  10. Make sure PT secondary circuits are in normal condition.
