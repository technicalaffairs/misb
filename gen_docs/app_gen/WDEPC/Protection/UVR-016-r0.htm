Introduction

These procedure details the steps required to completely check the proper operation and calibration of IRU1 -AC voltage Relay. It is recommended this procedure be carried out every four years bases the first preliminary steps to prepare for relay testing describes the activates required prior to testing.

The first draft initiated by eng. Moustafa Osman ,tested and revised by eng.Ali Ghater , accepted by eng. Farag Abo Youseff :general manager, approved by eng. Gomaa Ghater:  head sector of Kafr el Dawar Power Station, and certified by eng. Gaber Mohmed Frahat : WDEPC inspection and quality control general director and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • The equipment isolation is required.

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Personal safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety shoes, safety helmet ... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary voltage injection test set .

  • AVO meter

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

  • Review technical reference and manufacture manual .

  • Obtain a copy of relay setting from field maintenance file and compare it with setting on the relay

Testing Procedures

  1. Isolate the secondary terminal of the voltage transformer.

  2. Connect the voltage test set terminals to phase R (A3) ,phase S ( A5 ) and phase T (A7)

  3. Inject the three phase voltage (each phase = 57.7 V) to the relay then decrease the voltage to phase R gradually until the red flag for phase R appears then record VR (pick - up)

  4. Increase the voltage to phase R  gradually until the red flag disappear then record VR (reset)

  5. Repeat the steps (3,4) for the phases  (S , T )

  6. Inject  the three phase voltage (each phase = 57.7V) to the relay then increase the voltage to phase R gradually until the red flag for phase R appears then record VR  (pick - up) and time .

  7. Decrease the voltage to phase R  gradually until the red flag disappear then record VR (reset)

  8. Repeat the steps (6,7) for the phases  (S , T )

Return Relay and Associated Equipment to Service

  1. Return relay to service by removing the test terminal .

  2. Make sure VT secondary circuits are in normal condition.

  3. If any equipment associated with the relay has been isolated, it should be returned to service.

  4. Return the work permit.
