Introduction

This procedure outlines the testing procedures P1, to be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The purpose of the relay is basically to prevent damage on the turbine, if the driving torque less than the total losses in the generator and the prime mover, the generator starts to work as a synchronous compensator, taking the necessary active power from the network. The total losses, as the percentage of the rated power, may vary approx, from 1 to 3 %

The reverse power relay is provide with one step. The relay shall be set at 1 % of the generator rated power and shall give an alarm and trip after a time delay of 60 sec.

The first draft initiated by eng. Rasha Soliman, tested by eng  Mohamed Rabee, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Mohamed Abou Aiasha head sector of Damanhour power station and and certified by eng. Gaber Farahat WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety Helmet.

  • The line is out of service

  • Isolate the relay from PT`s & CT`s

Tools and Equipment

  • Secondary 1 phase injection test set

  • Multimeter, test wires

  • Test - plug

Work to be carried out

 The line is out of service

  1. Connect test leads between the test set and the voltage terminal (7&10) for phase ( R ) in the for the reverse power relay.

  2. Connect test leads between the test set and the current terminal (1&4) for phase ( R ) in the for the reverse power relay

  3. Connect test leads  between the test set and the time terminal (22&23) for phase (R) in the reverse power relay.

  4. Increase the current gradually until reach the operating value 

  5. Ensure that the relay operates at the setting current value (100)mA

  6. Ensure that the relay operates at the setting time value (60) sec

  7. Repeat for phases S & T

  8. Record this values on the test sheet

  9. Take off the equipments and the wires, then return the relay to its normal position
