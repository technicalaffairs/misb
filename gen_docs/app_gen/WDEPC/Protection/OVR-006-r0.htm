Introduction

This procedure outlines the testing procedures P1, to be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng, Ayda Otafey, tested by eng  Mohamed Rabee, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station and and certified by eng. Sania Attia Diab WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Unit must be shutdown.

  • Circuit breaker open and local.

Tools and Equipment

  • Use suitable tools

  • Instrument secondary injection test

  • Avometer

Work to be carried out

Preparation

  1. Isolate the secondary terminals of VT

Testing the Relay

  1. Open the links on terminals (64,67) to isolate the VT secondary circuit

  2. Connect test leads between the test set and the voltage terminal (1&2) for the relay.

  3. Connect test leads  between the test set and the time terminal (15&16) for   the relay.

  4. Increase the voltage gradually until the first stage value .

  5. Record the voltage value in the test sheet and the time .

  6. Decrease the injection voltage gradually to zero .

  7. Make sure CT and PT secondary circuits are in normal condition .
