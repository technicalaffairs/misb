Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

This relay is used as a backup short circuit protection at reduced voltage, the current required for operation will be corresponding reduced. At zero voltage operation is obtained with a current of less than 20% of rated relay current.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ahmad Galal, tested by eng. Mohamed Rabee, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam of Damanhour power station and certified by eng. Gaber farahat WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc

  • The transformer must be was isolated.

  • Make sure CT Secondary circuits are shorted.

  • Ensure all CT path elements are secure after any test disturbance.

Tools and Equipment

  • ISA test set type ART/3 or Omicron

  • Voltmeter, Ammeter, test plug, and test leads.

Work to be Carried Out

Relays Preparation

  1. Review the manual and drawings.

  2. Prepare the previous or commissioning results of the relay testing.

  3. Check the wires of the relay by hand.

  4. Check the AC source is available to the tester.

  5. Check the existence of the DC sources in panels.

  6. Disconnect the trip leads of the relay and connect one of them to the timer of the tester.

  7. Check all the leads used in tests.

Test Steps

  1. Connect the voltage leads of a tester unit to the relay points according to the drawings and scheme.

  2. insert the test handle in test switch.

  3. Connect the voltage leads of a tester unit to (9A_12A) in test hand and current leads of tester unit to (3A_4A) the relay points according to the drawings and scheme. to PH (R)..

  4. Inject current to each couple of points (3A-4A) AT 5A.

  5. Inject voltage to each couple of point (9A_12A) at 60v.

  6. Decrease the voltage until the relay is pick up  and measure the voltage at pick up  14v .

  7. Record the results pick up value in 1st alarm at 14v.

  8. Increase the voltage until the relay is reset and measures the voltage at reset 15v.

  9. Record the results reset value at 15v.

  10. Record the results pick up value in 2sd alarm at 18v.

  11. Increase the voltage until the relay is reset and measures the voltage at reset 19v.

  12. Record the results reset value at 19v.

  13. Connect the voltage leads of a tester unit to (10A_12A) in test hand and current leads of tester unit to (5A_6A) the relay points according to the drawings and scheme. to PH (S)..

  14. Inject current to each couple of points (5A-6A) AT 5A.

  15. Inject voltage to each couple of point (10A_12A) at 60v.

  16. Decrease the voltage until the relay is pic up  and measure the voltage at pick up  14v .

  17. Record the results pick up value in 1st alarm at 14v.

  18. Increase the voltage until the relay is reset and measures the voltage at reset 15v.

  19. Record the results reset value at 15v.

  20. Record the results pick up value in 2sd alarm at 18v.

  21. Increase the voltage until the relay is reset and measures the voltage at reset 19.7v.

  22. Record the results reset value at 19.7v.

  23. Connect the voltage leads of a tester unit to (11A_12A) in test hand and current leads of tester unit to (7A_8A) the relay points according to the drawings and scheme. to PH (T)..

  24. Inject current to each couple of points (7A-8A) AT 5A.

  25. Inject voltage to each couple of point (11A_12A) at 60v.

  26. Decrease the voltage until the relay is pick up  and measure the voltage at pic up  14v .

  27. Record the results pick up value in 1st alarm at 14v.

  28. Increase the voltage until the relay is reset and measures the voltage at reset 15v.

  29. Record the results reset value at 15v.

  30. Record the results pick up value in 2sd alarm at 18v.

  31. Increase the voltage until the relay is reset and measures the voltage at reset 19v.

  32. Record the results reset value at 19v.

  33.
