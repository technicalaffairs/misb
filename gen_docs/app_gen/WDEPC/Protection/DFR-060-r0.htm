Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

For use with three winding power transformers. the parts required for the third winding in addition to the differential relay by ancillary (yD203) it has three CT the necessary rectifier and tripping contactor

The first draft initiated by eng. Ahmad Galal, tested by eng. Mohamed Rabee, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam of Damanhour power station and certified by eng. Gaber Farahat WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used (safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc).

  • The transformer must be was isolated.

  • Make sure CT secondary circuits are shorted.

  • Ensure all CT path elements are secure after any test disturbance.

Tools and Equipment

  • ISA test set type ART/3 or Omicron.

  • Voltmeter, Ammeter, test plug, and test leads.

Work to be carried out

Relays preparation

  1. Review the manual and drawings

  2. Prepare the previous or commissioning results of the relay testing

  3. Check the wires of the relay by hand

  4. Check the AC source is available to the tester

  5. Check the existence of the DC sources in panels

  6. Disconnect the trip leads of the relay and connect one of them to the timer of the tester

  7. Check all the leads used in tests

 

 

Test steps

  1. Connect the voltage leads of a tester unit to the relay points according to the drawings and scheme

  2. The 1st at 22 kv

  3. Make dridge in terminal B50-x1 (37-38)a

  4. Inject voltage to each couple of points (21-24) in terminal (B50-x1) till the red flag of the device displays in PH (R)a

  5. Record the results pick up value at 0.7 v

  6. Decrease the voltage until the relay is reset and measure the voltage at reset 0.65 v

  7. Record the results reset value at 0.65 v

  8. Inject voltage to each couple of points (22-24) in terminal (B540-x1) till the red flag of the device displays. In PH (S)

  9. Record the results pick up value at 0.7 v

  10. Decrease the voltage until the relay is reset and measure the voltage at reset 0.65 v

  11. Record the results reset value at 0.65 v

  12. Inject voltage to each couple of points (23-24) in terminal (B50-x1) till the red flag of the device displays. In PH (T)

  13. Record the results pick up value at 0.7 v

  14. Decrease the voltage until the relay is reset and measure the voltage at reset 0.65 v

  15. Record the results reset value at 0.65 v

  16. Switch off secondary injection test set

  17. The 2nd at 6.3 KV

  18. Inject voltage to each couple of points (27-30) in terminal (B50-x1) till the red flag of the device displays. In PH (R)

  19. Record the results pick up value at 0.5 v

  20. Decrease the voltage until the relay is reset and measure the voltage at reset 0.41 v

  21. Record the results reset value at 0.41 v

  22. Inspect voltage to each couple of points (28-30) in terminal (B50-x1) till the red flag of the device displays. In PH (S)

  23. Record the results pick up value at 0.5 V

  24. Decrease the voltage until the relay is reset and measure the voltage at reset 0.41 V

  25. Record the results reset value at 0.41 V

  26. Inject voltage to each couple of points (29-30) in terminal (B50-x1) till the red flag of the device displays. In PH (T)

  27. Record the results pick up value at 0.5 v

  28. Decrease the voltage until the relay is reset and measure the voltage at reset 0.41 V

  29. Record the results reset value at 0.41 V

  30. Switch off secondary injection test set

  31. Return the relay to the panel

  32. Return all connections to the normal position as before the test

  33. Close the work permit
