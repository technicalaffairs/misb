Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an (1/4) annually bases as indicated by M3 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Sherif Kamal Assy, accepted by general manager eng Mohmed Kamel Afify and approved by eng. Gaber Mohmed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station & MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the pump electrically

  • Isolate the heating steam and all valves (delivery & suction)

  • Mechanical isolation

  • Open the drains

Tools and Equipment

  • Workshop kit (spanners & wrenches)

  • Screw drivers & gear pullers ... etc

  • Lubricating oil & general purpose grease

  • Power supply panel

  • Hoisting

  • Spanner & wrenches

  • Graphite packing 10 mm

  • Allen keys

  • Mobil gear oil

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the pump body, foundation & bolt

  2. Check the safety valves

  3. Check the suction & delivery valves

  4. Check the heating system

  5. Check the bearing

  6. Check the pump shafts & housing & impellers

  7. Check the pump diffusers

  8. Check the pump coupling & rubber & bolts

  9. Check the pump stuffing box

  10. Check the pump protection sleeves of the shaft

  11. Assemble the pump after repairing

  12. Adjust the alignment

  13. Check the pump after repairing (running)

  14. Test the all works

Preparation

  1. Operate to check the performance

  2. Define the troubles

  3. De-assemble the defective unites

  4. Remove the insulation

Insulation & Cladding

  1. Restore all valves to the operating normal position

  2. Clean the area after repairing
