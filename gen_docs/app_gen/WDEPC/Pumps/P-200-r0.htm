Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be preformed every 4000workinh hours as indicated by H4000 in the title block.

The motor of the pump: type ANVE - WT 001, Teco Electric & Machinery Co. LTD Taiwan

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • The motor have to be stopped and its breaker isolated before starting any maintenance procedure

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydraulic jack

  • Screw drivers set

  • Hydraulic torque wrench

  • Normal key set

  • Special key set

  • Allen key set

  • Nut key set

  • Alignment tools

  • Oil quantity & type

    • Pump thrust bearing: oil approximate 40 L oil (mobil DTE light) ISO - VG 32

    • Motor lower bearing: bearing No. (NU 226)

    • Motor lower bearing grease: Mobilux 3 (140 g)

    • Coupling grease: Mobilux EP 004 (0.86 dm3)

    • Motor upper bearing: oil approximate 60 L Oil (Mobil oil heavy medium) ISO-VG 68

  • Packing Quantity dimension, and type: (6 pieces - 10mm - LATT -YFLON 4788)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the (teeth, grease, gear seals, sealing faces) coupling and alignment coupling (air gap:40 (tolerance +2,-5)mm, radial:0.56, axial 0.22)

  2. Verify and check the oil (viscosity, acid No. & contamination) for motor upper bearing (oil approximate 60 L oil - mobil oil heavy medium - ISO-VO 68) and change if required

  3. Verification and check the oil (viscosity acid No. & contamination) for pump thrust bearing (oil approximate 40 L oil - mobil DTE light -ISO-VG 32) and change if required

  4. Verification of the grease mobilux 3 (140g) for lower bearing of motor and change if required

  5. Check the tighten of all bolts for the pump and motor

  6. Clean the cooling water pipes of thrust bearings (pump and motor)

  7. Check the goodness of inlet outlet sealing water drain and vent valves

  8. General cleaning for the external pump

  9. Check the packing (6pieces - IOmm - LATT, YFLON 4788) lantern ring shaft protecting sleeve and change if required

  10. Check the cooling for the packing
