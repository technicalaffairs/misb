Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed every 8000 operating hours as indicated by H8000 in the title block.

The pump function is provided with the boilers required fuel and with required pressure.

Work to be Carried Out
Main Cooling Pump

  1. Uncouple the motor from the pump by removing the coupling bolts.

  2. Check and record the alignment and the axial clearance.

  3. Drain the oil from guide bearing.

  4. Inspect and clean the oil cooler.

  5. Inspect and repack the gland with a new packing if required.

  6. Inspect and clean the cooling water tubes.

  7. Inspect the butterfly valve

  8. Inspect pump shaft and sleeves for repairing or replace it if required.

  9. Inspect guide bearing for repairing or replace it if required.

  10. Inspect the impellers for corrosion and replace it if required.

  11. Inspect the impellers wear rings and casing rings for repairing or replace it if required.

  12. Inspect the pump foundation.

  13. Realign the motor to the pump.

  14. Adjust the axial clearance.

  15. Reconnect the coupling bolts.
