Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The pump (figure1) function: to deliver oil to the oil heating station and then to the suction side of the high pressure pumps.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ahmed Mohamed Bakr, reviewed by eng. Omar Hamed Ibrahem, tested by eng. El-Sayed Abd Elrehim Mohamed, accepted by eng. Farag Hamza Farag, approved by eng. Abdel Mohsen Abdel Ghafar: Head Sector of Kafr Eldawar Power Station.

This document certified by eng. Mona Ahmed Al Sharqawy: head sector of inspection and quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety helmet, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Disconnect the power supply for this pump and tag it out.

  • Isolate the power mechanically by closing the suction and delivery valves and drain it.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Feeler gauge

  • Grease and Grease gun

  • Manual puller, Alignment device.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the alignment of the pump

  2. Ensure there is no any leakage from the pump.

  3. Ensure there is no any abnormal noise or vibration.

  4. Check the pressure in the delivery of the pump.

  5. Ensure there is no any leakage from the packing.

  6. Inject new grease using grease gun.
