Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves ...etc

  • Shutdown the pump.

  • Circuit breaker of pump racked out.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid rags

  • Oil Mobil DTE medium

Work to be carried out

Replacing the thrust bearing oil

  1. Rain the old oil from the pump bearing

  2. Clean the pump bearing housing

  3. Add the new oil to the proper level (mobil DTE medium)

  4. Check for any leakage
