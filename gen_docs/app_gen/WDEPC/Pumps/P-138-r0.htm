Introduction

This document outlines the high pressure feed water pump inspection, which must be performed every 1500 hours of operation

The pump technical data:

Type : centrifugal pump multistage        Speed:   2880 RPM   

No of stages (1-14)                             Power   2000 KW             Head: 1990  m

Suction press. 9 bar                             Max. flow rate:   200 m3/h

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set, Grease gun

  • Cleaning fluid, Turbo oil 32

  • Alignment device

Work to be Carried Out
Feed Pump

  1. Uncouple the hydraulic coupling from the pump by removing the coupling bolts.

  2. Check and record the alignment between the pump and hydraulic coupling.

  3. Clean the coupling and fill it with a new grease.

  4. Inspect the front and rear journal bearing.

  5. Inspect and repack the front and rear glands with new packing if required

  6. Inspect and clean the cooling water and oil tubes

  7. Inspect and clean the oil filter

  8. Realign the hydraulic coupling to the pump

  9. Reconnect the coupling bolts

  10. Inspect and repack the glands if required for this valves:
         * Main delivery         * Injection         * Heating                 
         * Min. flow      * Main cooling            * Suction           * Drain

Booster Feed Pump

  1. Uncouple the motor from the pump by removing the coupling bolts.

  2. Check and record the alignment.

  3. Inspect the front and rear bearing.

  4. Inspect and repack the gland with new packing if required.

  5. Inspect and clean the cooling water tubes.

  6. Realign the motor to the pump.

  7. Reconnect the coupling bolts.

Hydraulic Coupling

  1. Uncouple the motor from the hydraulic coupling by removing the coupling bolts.

  2. Clean the coupling and fill it with new grease between the hydraulic coupling and motor

  3. Inspect and clean the cooling water and oil tubes

  4. Inspect and clean the oil cooler if required

  5. Inspect and adjust the scoop tube

  6. Realign the motor to the hydraulic coupling

  7. Reconnect the coupling bolts
