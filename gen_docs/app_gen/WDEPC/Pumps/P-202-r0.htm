Introduction

This document outlines equipment inspection I1, to be preformed every 2000WH as indicated by H2000 in the title block.

The motor of the pump: type ANVE - WT 001, Teco Electric & Machinery Co. LTD Taiwan

Safety Precautions

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Wearing safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • The motor have to be stopped and its breaker isolated before starting any maintenance procedure

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydraulic jack

  • Screw drivers set

  • Hydraulic torque wrench

  • Normal key set

  • Special key set

  • Allen key set

  • Nut key set

  • Alignment tools

  • Oil quantity & type
    Pump thrust bearing: oil approximate 30 L oil (Mobil oil heavy medium) ISO - VG 68
    Motor lower bearing: bearing No. (NU 238)
    Motor lower bearing grease: Mobil plex 47 (350g)
    Coupling grease: mobilux EP 004 (2.7 dm3)
    Motor upper bearing : oil approximate 80 L oil (Mobil Oil heave medium) ISO-VG 68

  • Packing quantity dimension, and type: (4 pieces -20mm - CF - P)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the (teeth, grease, gear seals, sealing faces) coupling and alignment coupling (air gap:56 (tolerance +2,-5)mm, radial:0.127, axial 0.33)

  2. Verify and check the oil (viscosity, acid No. & contamination) for thrust bearing (pump and motor) and change if required

  3. Verify the grease mobil plex 47 for lower bearing of motor and change if required

  4. Check the tighten of all bolts for the pump and motor

  5. Clean the cooling water pipes of thrust bearings (pump and motor)

  6. Check all valves (drains, vents, ... etc)

  7. General cleaning for the external pump body

  8. Check the packing (4 pieces - 20mm - CF - P), lantern ring shaft protecting sleeve and change if required

  9. Check the cooling for the packing
