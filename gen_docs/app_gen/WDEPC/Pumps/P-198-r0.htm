Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be preformed every 1000 working hours as indicated by H1000 in the title block. (1000HS, 1EA-P001A)

The pump supplier KSB Germany type SNW 600-719

The motor type: ANVE-WT001, supplier: Teco electric & machinery Co. LTD Taiwan,

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution works surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the pump mechanically and the motor electrically

  • Be sure the motor of the pump is stopped and its breaker isolated before starting any maintenance.

  • Electrical Isolation:
    - Circuit breaker 1NB011 is opened
    - Switch off the DC Supply
    - Space heater  9NHA 31A- ICH-26 is open

  • Mechanical Isolation:
    - Close the discharge valve 1EA-V001A

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydraulic Jack

  • Screw Drivers set

  • Hydraulic torque wrench

  • Normal Key set

  • Special Key set

  • Allen Key set

  • Nut Key set

  • Alignment tools

  • Oil for pump thrust bearing approximate 15 L (Mobile oil heavy medium) ISO-VG68

  • Grease for Motor Lower bearing and coupling: (no. of  bearing NU224, grease Mobil plex 47: 120g)

  • Oil for Motor Upper bearing approximate 60 L (Mobile oil heavy medium) ISO-VG68

  • Packing qty, dimension, type: 4pieces, 95x127x16mm, RT

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Ensure the oil viscosity, acid no., contamination for thrust bearings (pump and motor)

  2. Ensure the grease Mobile plex 47 for lower bearing of motor.

  3. Check the tighten for all bolts of the pump and motor.

  4. Clean the cooling water pipes of thrust bearing (pump and motor)

  5. Check all valves (drains, vent, ... etc)

  6. Generally clean the external pump body

  7. Check the packing ( 4 pieces, 95x127x16mm, RT-P) and shaft protecting sleeve

  8. Check the rubber (8 pieces, No. 315)
