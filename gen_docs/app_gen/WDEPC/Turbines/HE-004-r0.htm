Introduction

This document outlines the low pressure heater inspection which must be performed every 8000 hours of operation

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Ahmed Khalil darwish reviewed & tested by eng. Sobhy Tolba Ahmed at 1 March 2009, accepted by eng. Abdel Fatah Mohamed  Shehata approved by eng. Gomaa Khater khadr: head sector of Kafr Eldawar power station

The high pressure heater technical data

Flow rate of feeding water 140 t/hr

Number of low pressure heater : 3

Size of low pressure heater surface of each heater : 80 m2

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, shoes, ...etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Main crane, temporary wood

  • Standard bolt drivers set

  • Welding

  • Measurement tools

Work to be carried out 

  1. Be sure the pipeline is drained

  2. Inspect coil leakage by pressurized the coil open the condensate drain and blocked any coil leaks

  3. Open heater bolt

  4. Inspect gasket and replace with a new gasket if necessary

  5. Close heater
