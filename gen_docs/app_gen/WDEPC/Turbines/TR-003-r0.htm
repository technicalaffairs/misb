Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I3, to be performed every two years as indicated by Y2 in the above title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Ragab Goada Abo-Zeed tested by eng. Emad Abraheem Gooda, accepted by general manager eng. Hassan Saeed Dosoki , approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia: WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes gloves ... etc

  • Shutdown the trash rack

  • Circuit breaker of trash rack racked out

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set..

  • Cleaning fluid, rags

Work to be carried out

  1. Grease the rope (Mobil arma 789)

  2. Grease the bearing supports (Mobil plex 47)

  3. Grease the push pulleys (Mobil plex 47)

  4. Inspect the oil of the hydraulic unit & reduction gearbox

  5. Grease the springs (Mobil arma 789)

  6. Repaint the metallic structures
