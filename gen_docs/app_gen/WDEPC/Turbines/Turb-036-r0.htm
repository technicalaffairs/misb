Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes gloves ... etc

  • Shutdown the turbine

  • Isolate the steam to turbine.

  • The oil system of turbine must be isolated mechanically and electrically.

  • Be sure turbine must be completely drained.

  • Circuit breaker of turning gear racked out

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Special tools for turbine.

  • Cleaning fluid, rags manual puller

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge

Work to be carried out

Unless the turbine shows an abnormal behavior in operation or during the periodic test (e.g. pressure and temperature changes in the impulse chamber, bearing oil and metal temperature changes, vibration amplitude changes, etc (.following maintenance program is recommended.

Inspection of Steam Valves

  1. Check the liquid penetrant or inspect the magnetic particle of HP stop valve body welding

  2. Check the liquid penetrant or inspect the magnetic particle of valve stem.

  3. Inspect the liquid penetrant of Stellite inserted on valve plug and seat.

  4. Check the stem bushing clearances.

  5. Check the stem for straightness.

  6. Inspect the filters.

  7. Inspect the liquid penetrant of valve cover studs.

Inspect the Servomotors

Disassembly and inspection of  the servomotors are included in the overhaul program of the valves.

Inspection of Rotor Glands

  1. Wherever possible without removing cylinder covers, inspect outer glands.

Maintenance of Gland Steam System and Relevant Control Valves

Maintenance of Oil System

  1. Test the water and clean the cooler water side.

  2. Empty and clean the oil reservoirs

Dismantling and Overhaul of One Journal Bearing

It will be chosen the bearing that during operations has shown imperfect conditions taking into account variations of white metal temperature and / or vibrations from normal values     

Inspection of high pressure oil system
 

A thorough examination of all control devices is imperative This will include:

  1. Disassemble each device.

  2. Clean the cup valve bellows orifices etc.

Disassembly and Inspection of Thrust and Journal Bearings
Overhaul of Protective Devices.
