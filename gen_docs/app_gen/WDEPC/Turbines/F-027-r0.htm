Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be preformed when ΔP=26 mm Aq for pre-filter & 38 Aq for Cubic Filter as indicated in the title block.

This unit contains pre-filter, cubic type air filter and strata air cleaner inertial seperator

The above components in the air intake are distribution as the following:

Strata air cleaner housing 2 sets, each set contains:

  • 1 strata air cleaner section

  • 1 secondary strata air cleaner duct

  • 1 secondary exhaust fan

  • 1 housing

Filter house -1 set: contain totally 144 Pre-filter and 144 cubic filters (72 filters on each side)

Blow - in door:

  • It is provided on the filter hose.

  • The blow-in door is installed to open automatically when the static pressure within the inlet house rise. above 1176 Pa (120 mm Aq) approximately, overcoming the doors own weight.

The pre-filter element is washable type and can be used twice and must be replaced at differential pressure 254.8 Pa (26 mmAq)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wearing safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Be sure that the unit is out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance set

  • Compressed air hose

  • Cleaning articles

  • Soft brushes

  • Long rope with basket

  • Vacuum cleaner

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Wash the pre-filter for twice at differential pressure

  2. Replace the cubic filters at differential pressure

  3. Check the system for corrosion

  4. Inspect the filter frame and the basket.
