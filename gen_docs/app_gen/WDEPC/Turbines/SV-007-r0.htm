Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

Safety valves function is to protect boiler from over pressure.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used (safety helmet, safety shoes, ...etc).

    •

  • Ear plugs and eye protection should be used when working on a valve which is under pressure.

  • Never strike a valve which is under pressure. Premature actuation can result.

  • Never stand in front of the discharge outlet of a pressure relief valve, which is under pres­sure.

  • Always approach and use any pressure relief valve with great care.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Suitable lifting device

  • Lapping grey cast iron emery paper.

    •

Work to be Carried Out         

  1. Check the O-ring sealing and  replace it if necessary.

  2. Check for external and internal leakage

  3. Check the tightening of the piston.

  4. Inspect the seat for damage, use for guiding a fixture with emery paper; figure 3-a or fixture that is mounted on drilling machine with emery paper P.V. see figure 3-b, if the repair of valve body is not possible, contact the manufacturer.

  5. Inspect the core and repair it on Lathe or replace it if repair is not possible.

 

Figure 1: Dismantling of Safety Valve

Control Instrument

  1. Clean and test the function of all parts of control instrument, if required replace membrane valve, or replace of other damaged parts / air hoses, manometers, reducing valve, ... etc.

  2. Check the air distribution between control instruments and safety valve from leakage and repair.

  3. Replace the tubes of air by stainless steal tube if required, if needed the valve of the air inlet system should be repaired / replace of sealing O-ring on cone / grinding of seat surface ... etc, and the cone should  be tightened to the spindle.
