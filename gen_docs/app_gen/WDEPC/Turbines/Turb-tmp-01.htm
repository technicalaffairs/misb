Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes gloves ... etc

  • Shutdown the turbine

  • Isolate the steam to turbine.

  • The oil system of turbine must be isolated mechanically and electrically.

  • Be sure turbine must be completely drained.

  • Circuit breaker of turning gear racked out

Work to be carried out

The time interval between two major overhauls, based on actual experience, is approx. 7 years, during which the unit has been kept in service for a minimum of 40,000 hours to a maximum of 60,000 hours. This time, however, will also depend on the type of operation performed. For that reason, it is recommended that a regular schedule of all outages, their causes and durations he set up monthly or every two or three months. It is also suggested that the data recorder be placed at manufacture’s disposal to give him useful information to determine the kind of inspection and the nature of. the action to be taken during the next overhaul, even though the work will not be performed under his supervision.
The operations to be done on that occasion, in addition to those required for annual maintenance, are basically the following:

  1.  Dismantling of turbine cylinder and removal of rotor

  2. Visual inspection and non—destructive tests of stationary and rotating parts

Non—Destructive Testing (NDT)

The normal practice is to perform checks as extensive as possible. The overhaul program should include the following check means:

  1. Liquid Penetrant Inspection (LP)

  2. Magnetic Particle Inspection (MX)

  3. Ultrasonic Testing (US)

 

Stationary parts  

Cylinder

 LP or MX inspection on critical points, at the various cylinder Section variations. Weld  inspection on stop valve, piping and connecting axial beams.

Stationary blades

LP inspection

Nozzle chambers

LP and MX inspection of nozzle

block and welding

Bolting 

LP Inspection

Pipings

LP and MX inspection

 

 

 

 

 

 

Rotating parts

  1. Blading, shrouds, LP inspection tenons.

  2. Rotor LP inspection on rotor recesses and gland zones

Welding inspection

A non-destructive test is recommended for all accessible welds working at high pressures and temperatures

  1. .Inspection of all bearings

  2. Check of alignment of stationary parts with rotor axis

  3. Check of rotor alignment

  4. Oil system overhaul

The following operations are recommended:

  1. Oil pumps overhaul.

  2. Inspection of valves in oil system.

  3. Water test and cleaning of cooler tube bundle water side.

Inspection of Gland Steam Sealing System

It will consist in inspecting valves and servomotors and checking the opening sequence.

Inspection of Turning Gear

Overhaul of Protective Devices
