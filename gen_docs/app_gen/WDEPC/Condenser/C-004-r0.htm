Introduction

This document outlines the condenser inspection which must be performed every 4000 hours of operation

Maintenance procedures aiming to guarantee the perfect efficiency of the components involved in the condensing system should be strictly observed as a matter of fact a decrease in efficiency of failure of any of these units will adversely effect the condenser performance

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Ahmed Khalil darwish reviewed & tested by eng. Sobhy Tolba Ahmed at 1 March 2009, accepted by eng. Abdel Fatah Mohamed  Shehata approved by eng. Gomaa Khater khadr: head sector of Kafr Eldawar power station

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, shoes, ...etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Rupper ball

  • Source pressurized water jet

  • Main crane temporary wood

  • Standard bolt drivers set

  • Measurement tools

Work to be carried out 

  1. Be sure the pipeline is drained

  2. Open condenser bolt

  3. Inspect gasket and replace with a new gasket if necessary

  4. Clean tube condenser by source pressurized water jet

  5. Inspect tube leakage by pressurized the shell and blocked any tube leaks

  6. Close condenser
