Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, to be performed every 6 months as indicated by M6 in the above title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves ...etc

  • Shutdown the pump

  • Circuit breaker of pump racked out

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Grease gun

  • Cleaning fluid, rags

Work to be carried out

  1. Fill the coupling with grease

  2. Take sample to check the thrust bearing oil chemical and physical
