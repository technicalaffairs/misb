Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, to be performed on an (1/4) annually bases as indicated by M3 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Sherif Kamal Assy, tested by eng. Mohamed Hussein Sherif, accepted by general manager eng. Mohamed Kamel Afify and approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station & MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Stop the unit

  • Isolate the air fan mechanically & electrically

  • Open the drain valves for (all boiler parts)

  • Open the drains

Tools and Equipment

  • Workshop kit ( spanners & wrenches)

  • Screw drivers & gear pullers ... etc

  • General purpose grease

  • Power supply panel

  • Hoisting

  • Allen keys

  • Lapping machine

Work to be carried out 

  1. Operate to check the performance

  2. Define the troubles

  3. Disassemble the defective units

  4. Remove the insulation

  5. Check external body and piping joint

  6. Check the valves (gland - seat - disk - bolts - screw) and replace defect ones

  7. Check the flanges & setting and replace defect ones

  8. Check the drain piping and replace defect ones

  9. Check the leakage

  10. Assemble the element

  11. Check the condition (open & close)

  12. Adjust

  13. Check after repairing

  14. Restore all valves to the operating normal position

  15. Clean the area after repairing
