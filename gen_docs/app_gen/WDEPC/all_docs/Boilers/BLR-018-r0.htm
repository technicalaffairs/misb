Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, to be performed on an (1/4) annually bases as indicated by M3 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Sherif Kamal Issy tested by eng. Mohamed Hussein Sherif accepted by general director eng. Mohamed Kamel Afify and approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour Power Station and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc.

  • Stop the unit

  • Isolate the air fan mechanical & electrical

  • To lose the pressure

Tools and Equipment

  • Workshop kit (spanners & wrenches)

  • Screw drivers & gear pullers ... etc

  • General purpose grease

  • Power supply panel

  • Hoisting

  • Scaffolding & Hoisting

  • Amyant

  • Welding equipment

Work to be carried out 

  1. Operate to check the performance

  2. Define the troubles

  3. Dissassemble the defective units

  4. Remove the insulation

  5. Check external body and joints

  6. Check the gates (inner quill) for the gases

  7. Check the flanges, setting, pivots & glands

  8. Check for any corrosion in the gate body

  9. Check the external isolating cover

  10. Check the adobes & external cement layers

  11. Clean from ashes

  12. Check all parts

  13. Assemble the element & close the doors

  14. Check the condition (open & close)

  15. Adjust

  16. Check after repairing

  17. Restore all valves to the operation normal position

  18. Clean the area after repairing
