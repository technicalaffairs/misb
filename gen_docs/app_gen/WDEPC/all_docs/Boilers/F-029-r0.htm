Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an (1/4) annually bases as indicated by M3 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Sherif Kamal Assy, accepted by general manager eng. Mohamed Kamel Afify and approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc.

  • Open the vent valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Work shop kit (spanners & wrenches)

  • Screw drivers & Gear pullers ... etc

  • General purpose grease

  • Power supply panel

  • Hoisting

  • Spanner & Wrenches Gaskets

Work to be carried out 

  1. Operate to check the performance

  2. Define the troubles

  3. Disassemble the defective units

  4. Remove insulation

  5. External body and joints

  6. Check the flanges, setting, pivots & glands

  7. Check for any corrosion

  8. Check the external isolating cover

  9. Cleaning from ashes

  10. Clean the filters

  11. Check the all parts

  12. Assemble the element

  13. Check the condition (open & close) & adjust

  14. Check after repairing

  15. Restoring all valves to the operation normal position

  16. Cleaning the area after repairing
