Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, ear plug and gloves pairs ... etc.

  • The motor must be out of service.

  • Circuit breaker in test position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set, Air Blower, Megger device

  • Greasing machine, Multimeter.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

  • Brushes, (soft brushes)

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Ensure that all fixing bolts are secured .

  2. Ensure that electrical connection are tightened .

  3. Check the isolators and clean it

  4. Check for water leaks clean cooler fins (air side) .

  5. Check for oil leaks.

  6. Check the operation of the motor from local and remote operation on test position.

  7. Measure winding insulation resistance.

  8. Check all protection devices of the motor by protection group.
