Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses ... etc.

  • The motor must be out of service.

  • Circuit breaker in test position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set, Air Blower, Megger device

  • Greasing machine, Multimeter.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

  • Brushes (soft brushes)

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean air - ducts.

  2. Ensure that all fixing bolts are secured.

  3. Ensure that electrical connection are tightened.

  4. Check the isolators and clean it.

  5. Replace the grease.

  6. Measure the winding insulation resistance.

  7. Clean the winding.

  8. Visually examine the winding.

  9. Check operation of the motor from local and remote operation on test position.

  10. Check all protection devices of the motor by protection group.
