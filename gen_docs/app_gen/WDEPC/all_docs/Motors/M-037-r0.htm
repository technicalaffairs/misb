Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, breathing-mask, safety glasses is required.

  • The motor must be out of service.

  • Circuit breaker in test position.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set, Air Blower, Megger device

  • Greasing machine, Multimeter.

  •  Fine cloth for cleaning purposes

  •  Brushes (soft brushes)

Work to be carried out

  1. Clean air - ducts

  2. Ensure that all fixing bolts are secured.

  3. Ensure that electrical connection are tightened.

  4. Check the isolators and clean it .

  5. Check operation of the motor from local and remote operation on test position.

  6. Measure winding insulation resistance.

  7. Check all protection devices of the motor by protection group
