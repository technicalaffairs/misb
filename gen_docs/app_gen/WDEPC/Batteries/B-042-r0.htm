Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • No smoking is perminted in battery room.

  • Use standard personal safety and health equipment as goggles, rubber gloves, rubber apron ... etc

  • Don't place any tools on top of battery cells (danger of short circuit)

  • Never use the same tools on lead cells you may have used before on alkaline cells

Tools and Equipments

  • Complete tool set, and glove.

  • Digital voltmeter, hydrometer.

 

 

 

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Read total battery voltage and record on battery maintenance card.

  2. Disconnect AC supply of  battery charger

  3. Inspect outside battery containers for cracks and leakage of electrolyte

  4. If you discover defective cell use the recommended procedure to replace with a new cell.

  5. Inspect contact area of inter connection, lugs, terminal connections, tightening and clean any salt deposits with distilled water.

  6. Lubricate terminals for cells with a thin film of vaseline.

  7. Record density for each cell and check that the levels are between the max. and min. marks. Readjust with distilled water, if required.

  8. Read voltage per cell and record on battery maintenance card

  9. Again read overall voltage of bank and record on battery maintenance card and compare the reading from step no1

  10. Compare the present readings with the last readings.

  11. Check ventilation and clean battery house.
