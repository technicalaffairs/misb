Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

The air compressors are used to supply the station with service air and instrument air

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the motor of this compressor.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding the motor of this compressor.

  • The compressor must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery valve.

  • Close the cooling water supply.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags, soft brushes ... etc

  • Manual puller, feeler gauge

  • Compressed air supply

  • Spray gun, grease

    •

Work to be Carried Out

Intake Filter

  1. Visually inspect inlet air filter elements and replace if necessary. Check the filter housing for corrosion and check the sealing surface.

  2. Remove the housing cover.

  3. Remove dirty cartridge.

  4. Clean bottom seal plate in base of filter housing.

Note:

The intake filter indicator light mounted on the compressor panel and when the pressure drop across the filter and the switch on the panel light indicating that the filter element should be replaced.

 

Oil Filter

The compressor has hydraulic type filter the filter is mounted down stream of the cooler and thermo static control valve.

  1. Visually inspect and replace the oil filter element. A single line type oil filter is furnished consisting of a 10 micron replaceable cartridge element.

Oil Cooler

  1. Visually inspect the oil cooler tubes.

  2. Carefully examine tubes for scale and clean if necessary. After cleaning, examine for erosion or corrosion.

  3. After maintenance inspection or cleaning, both shell and tube side should be carefully vented and full of liquid.
