Introduction

This document outlines equipment inspection I1, to be preformed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Nabel Mostafa, reviewed by eng. Hasan Alsayed Abdel-Azez, and, approved by eng. Abdel-Maged Abdel-Qawy Emara: Head Sector of Sidi Kirir Power Station.

This document certified by eng. Mona Ahmed Al Sharqawy: WDEPC Engineering consultant A and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution works surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • The motor have to be stopped and its breaker isolated before starting any maintenance procedure

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydraulic jack

  • Screw drivers set

  • Hydraulic torque wrench

  • Normal key set

  • Special key set

  • Allen key set

  • Nut key set

  • Alignment tools

  • Oil quantity & type

  • Motor non drive end bearing mobil oil 46

  • Motor drive end bearing mobil oil 46

  • Fan drive end bearing mobil oil 68,6.62 L

  • Fan non drive end bearing mobil oil 68,6.62L

  • Coupling EP2 grease

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

 

Electrical Isolation

  • Open the main motor breaker (1 NB 0115)                    

  • Switch off the DC supply                                             

  • Open the motor space heater (1NHA12A-CCH-28)     

  • Open the bearing oil sump (1NHA21A-ECH-18)           

Mechanical Isolation

  • Close the FD fan A Inlet Vane (1CR-FCV-1A)                    

  • Close the FD fan A outlet damper (1CRXV-151A (TSO))  

  • Close the closed cooling inlet 1EB-BA-604                        

  • Close the closed cooling outlet 1EB-BA628                

  • Close the cold xover duct damper (1CRXV-157 (TSO))      

  • Close the control air of inlet vane                                        

  • Close the control air of outlet damper    

Procedures

  1. Correct leakages for cooling water flow as required, flush piping as needed,

  2. Re-grease the cylinder grease until grease comes out

  3. Clean the air filter motor by dry air.

  4. Check visually the fan blades.

  5. Change oil of the float bearing.

  6. Change oil of fixed bearing.

  7. Purge old grease and re-grease I.V.C. of the actuator shaft bearing.

  8. Purge old grease of air cylinder  and re-grease.

  9. Purge old grease of flexible coupling drive end motor and re-grease.

  10. Change oil of drive end motor.

  11. Check visually the rotor, blade liners.

  12. Check visually-adjust if needed the damper blades, linkage.

  13. Check visually the inlet cone clearance and adjust if needed.

  14. Change oil the non-drive end motor.

  15. Check visually the rotor body bound bolts and adjust if needed.

  16. Tighten annually the foundation bolts, support steel.

  17. Check visually the shaft bearing seals, adjust and change if needed.

  18. Check visually the housing, inlet box and adjust if needed.
