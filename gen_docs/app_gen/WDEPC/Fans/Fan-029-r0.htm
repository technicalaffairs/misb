Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Disconnect the power supply of the motor

  • Be sure that the units & the flue gas fan are stopped

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required

Tools and Equipment

  • Cleaning fluid feeler gauge

  • Suitable keys from 12-46 mm

  • Filler (indicator)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspection of the bearing at evident wear increase radial clearance

  2. Check the coupling alignment

  3. Check the tightness of all screw (impeller, bearing, motor base)

  4. Check the lubricating cycle and clean the lubricating pipes

  5. Isolate mechanically the dampers by closing the suction and delivery dampers
