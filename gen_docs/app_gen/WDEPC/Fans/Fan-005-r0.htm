Work to be Carried Out   Drawing No. 1001-CP-105-M-001-0008-07

Preparation

Electrical Isolation

  1. Switch off main motor breaker No. 1NB0114

  2. Turn off DC supply.

  3. Turn off turning gear motor  No. 9NHA51 A-ODJ

  4. Turn off turning gear space heater No. 9 NHA 51A-OCH-10

  5. Turn off G.R fan bearing oil sump No. 1NHA 21A-ECH-17

  6. Turn off G.R fan motor space heater No. 1NHA 12A-CCH-24

  7. Turn off G.R fan turning gear (oil heater) No. 1NHA 21A-ECH-28

 

Mechanical Isolation

  1. Close suction dampers No. 1GR-XV-151A (RSO)

  2. Close suction dampers No.1GR-XV-152A (TSO)

  3. Close discharge dampers No. 1GR-XV-153A (TSO)

  4. Close discharge dampers No. 1GR-XV-154A (RSO)

  5. Close comb. air to G.R. fan No. 1GR-XV-155A (RSOO)

  6. Open suction seal to G.R. fan No. 1SL-XV-151A (TSO)

  7. Open discharge seal to G.R. fan No. 1SL-XV-153A (TSO)

  8. Close inlet valve of the closed circuit of cooling water No.1 EB-BA-606

  9. Close inlet valve of closed circuit of cooling water No.1 EB-BA-630

  10. Close control air of inlet dampers (flue gases & seal air)

  11. Close control air of outlet dampers (flue gases, heating & seal air)

 

Procedures

  1. Check visually damper blades and linkage; adjust if needed

  2. Change oil of gear box turning gear motor.

  3. Check visually housing, inlet box; adjust if needed.

  4. Check visually rotor and blade liners.

  5. Check visually rotor body bound bolts adjust if needed.

  6. Check visually shaft bearing and seals; adjust / change if needed.

  7. Check visually inlet cone clearance; adjust if needed.

  8. Re-grease turning gear  coupling.

  9. Re-grease turning gear motor.

  10. Re-grease drive end motor.

  11. Tighten the foundation bolts and support steel.

  12. Clean air filter motor by dry air.

  13. Change oil of float bearing.

  14. Change oil of fixed bearing.

  15. Check the cooling water flow; correct the leakage as required, and flush piping as needed.
