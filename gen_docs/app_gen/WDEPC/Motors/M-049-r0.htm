Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohammed Mostafa El-Zalbane, tested by eng. Samir Ouf Hasoob, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia: WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

 

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the appropriate safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet ... etc

  • Motor is out of service for some works if necessary.

  • Switch off the CB and withdraw it from its cell.

  • Switch off the DC power supply for this CB.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical equipment kit.

  • Megger 2500v.

  • Dry Cloth.

Work to be carried out

  1. Open the junction box and check the cable terminals tightening any loose bolt if any.

  2. Clean the junction box.

  3. Clean the body of the motor and ventilation holes using air blower.

  4. Clean the local and tightening the terminals.

  5. Grease the ball bearing after 3000 hours running.

  6. Measure the insulation resistance of the motor winding Using Megger 2500v.
