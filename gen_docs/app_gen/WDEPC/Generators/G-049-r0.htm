Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance inspection on 3 Ph, Turbo Alternator which must be performed every week, This procedure was applied in Damanhour Thermal Power Plant3*65mw since 1976,  The generator data: 81.25MVA, 10.5KV, 4.45KA, PF=0.8, 50HZ, 3000 rpm, Y connection

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Fatehy AboElkher, tested by eng.Samir Ouf Hassoub , accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station, and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia Diab WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety  category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves ... etc.

  • Gas turbine out of service at cold state.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • New brushes, Air blower, and Spray

  • Digital voltmeter, megger, clamp ammeter

  • Fine cloth and brushes for cleaning purposes

Work to be carried out

Bearings   

Inspect drains at oil vapour extractor and pressure pipes.

Seal Oil Supply System

Check oil filters and clean if required .

Coolers

Check vents .

Hydrogen System

  1. Check gas consumption .

  2. Inspect dry – type filters and clean if required .

Exciter Set

  1. Inspect air filters and clean if required .

  2. Inspect brushes .

Hydrogen System

  1. Check gas drier and clean if  required .

  2. Check thermal gas analyzer
