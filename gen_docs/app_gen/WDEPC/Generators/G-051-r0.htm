Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance inspection I3, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

This procedure was applied in Damanhour Thermal Power Plant3*65mw since 1976,The generator data: 81.25MVA, 10.5KV, 4.45KA, PF=0.8, 50HZ, 3000 rpm, Y connection .

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Fatehy AboElkher, tested by eng.Samir Ouf Hassoub , accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station, and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia Diab WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety  category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves ... etc.

  • Gas turbine out of service at cold state.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • New brushes, Air blower, and Spray

  • Digital voltmeter, megger , clamp ammeter

  • Fine cloth and brushes for cleaning purposes

Work to be carried out

Rotor

Measure insulation resistance

Exciter set

  1. Measure insulation resistance

  2. Overhaul  commutators (undercutting of mica if required)

  3. Inspect  magnet frame and armature winding and clearing if required

  4. Inspect  brush holders

  5. Clean  frames

  6. Check  brush assembly and adjustment of neutral axis

Coolers

Check vents .

Hydrogen System

  1. Check  gas consumption .

  2. Inspect  dry – type filters and cleaning if required .

Seal oil supply system

  1. Check  pressure gauge step valves

  2. Vent and check  pressure gauges and check contact setting
