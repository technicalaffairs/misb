Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures I4, which must be carried out from after  yearly as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

 This procedure was applied in Damanhour Thermal Power Plant3*65mw since 1976. The generator data: 81.25MVA, 10.5KV, 4.45KA, PF=0.8, 50HZ, 3000 rpm, Y connection

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Fatehy AboElkher, tested by eng.Samir Ouf Hassoub , accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station, and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia Diab WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued before start work and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, safety clothes and breathing mask when cleaning. ... etc,

  • The generator in case of shut down.

  • The circuit breaker of generator on test position.

  • Put ground on the generator windings.

  • Don't burn the cloth used in cleaning, collect it and send it to the dump

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • New brushes, Air blower, and Spray

  • Digital voltmeter, megger, clamp ammeter

  • Fine cloth and brushes for cleaning purposes

Work to be carried out

stator

  1. Measure insulation resistance

  2. Carry out hydrostatic test .

Rotor

  1. Measure insulation resistance

  2. Inspect bearing surfaces  and shaft collars .

  3. Check  ventilating duct for slip ring leads .

  4. Clean and inspect slip rings .

  5. Inspect slip ring brushes .

  6. Inspect brush holders .

  7. Check coupling flange and coupling at turbine and exciter ends .

  8. Check alignment .

Bearing

  1. Inspect bearing insulation .

  2. Thorough cleaning of bearing .

  3. Inspect bearing sleeves .

  4. Inspect oil pipes .

  5. Check spherical seats .

  6. Inspect oil grooves and pipes for oil jacking .

  7. Perform checking of oil jacking pump .

  8. Inspect  pressure control valve and non-return .

  9. Inspect drains at oil vapour extractor and pressure breaker pipes .

  10. Clean and inspect non- return valve of oil vapour extractor .

  11. Check oil pipes .

Seal oil supply system

  1. Check seal oil pumps ,motors .

  2. Inspect oil supple pressure controller , over  flow valve and reliet valve .

  3. Inspect seal oil pressure controller .

  4. Check oil filters and clean ,if required .

  5. Check automatic seal oil control system .

  6. Inspect isolation and non – return valves .

  7. Check performance of solenoid valves .

  8. Check pressure gouge stop valves .

  9. Vent and check of pressure gouge , check contact setting .

  10. Check performance of pressure gauge .

  11. Check thermometers .

Exciter set

  1. Check brush assembly and adjust neutral axis .

  2. Check field rheostat and voltage regulator .

Coolers

  1. Inspect  H2 coolers on waters side and clean if required .

  2. Checking of vents

  3. Inspect  H2 cooler gasket (on air side)

  4. Inspect  seal oil cooler gaskets

Shaft seals

  1. Measure insulation resistance

  2. Check oil tightness

  3. Check sliding faces of seal rings seal guides and seal housings.

Hydrogen system

  1. Check H2 pressure reduces .

  2. Inspect diaphragm and selector valves .

  3. Check gas pressure breaker .
