Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be carried out from three to six years as indicated by Y3:6 in the title block

This procedure was applied in Damanhour Thermal Power Plant3*65mw since 1976. The generator data: 81.25MVA, 10.5KV, 4.45KA, PF=0.8, 50HZ, 3000 rpm, Y connection

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Fatehy AboElkher, tested by eng.Samir Ouf Hassoub , accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station, and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia Diab WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should the suitable safety  category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes, gloves ... etc.

  • Gas turbine out of service at cold state.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • New brushes, Air blower, and Spray

  • Digital voltmeter, megger, clamp ammeter

  • Fine cloth and brushes for cleaning purposes

Work to be carried out

stator

  1. Measure insulation resistance

  2. Carry out hydrostatic test.

  3. Replace bushing gaskets.

  4. Dismantle and re-seal of bushing trough.

  5. Inspect winding and end turns.

  6. Check slot wedges for tight seating.

  7. Inspect winding conductors in slots.

  8.  Check condition and compression of core.

  9. Inspect ventilating slots in core.

  10. Check clamping flanges and fingers for tight seating.

  11. Dismantle bushing plates placement of gaskets.

  12. Dismantle stator front sections and end shields replacement of all gaskets.

Rotor

  1. Dismantle rotor.

  2. Measure insulation resistance

  3. Inspect bearing surfaces and shaft collars.

  4. Check ventilating duct for slip ring leads .

  5. Clean and inspect of slipping.

  6. Inspect rotor and slip ring fans

  7. Inspect slip ring brushes.

  8. Inspect brush holders.

  9. Check rotor wedges end bells and slip rings for tight seating .

  10. Inspect gas outlet ducts.

  11. Check coupling flange and coupling at turbine and exciter ends .

  12. Check alignment.

 Shaft Seals

  1. Check oil tightness

  2. Check sliding faces of seal rings seal guides and seal housings.

  3. Inspect seal ring contact faces.

  4. Inspect oil level controllers and alarm switches.

  5. Check thermocouples

Hydrogen system

  1. Check H2 pressure reducers.

  2. Inspect diaphragm and selector valves.

  3. Inspect pressure gauge stop valves.

  4. Check gas pressure breaker.

  5. Check gas drier and clean if required .

  6. Replace drier gel filling.

  7.  Check performance of liquid level alarm switches.

  8. Inspect CO2 flash tank (marl them fill if required ).

  9. Check high-pressure valves.

  10. Check thermal gas analyzer.

  11. Check performance of pressure gauges.

  12. Check thermometers.

  13. Check rod type thermostats.

  14. Inspect dry-type filters and replace if required.

Exciter Set

  1. Measure insulation resistance

  2. Check bearing insulation.

  3. Overhaul  communal (undercutting of mica if reqd).

  4. Clean frames.

  5. Inspect magnet frame and arm lure windings and clean if required.

  6. Check slot wedges and bind bands.

  7. Inspect couplings.

  8. Check alignment.

  9. Inspect air  filters and clean if required.

  10. Inspect air ducts and clean if required

  11. Check brush assembly and adjust neutral axis.  Inspect brush holders.

  12. Inspect brushes.

  13. Check field rheostat and voltage regulator.

  14. Regrease of antifriction bearings (grease renewal if reqd)

Coolers

  1. Inspect  H2 coolers on water side and clean if reqd .

  2.  Inspect H2 cooler gaskets ( on air side )

  3.  Replace H2 cooler gaskets

  4. Inspect surface protection of water boxes and renewal , if required

  5. Withdraw H2 coolers and clean it on gas side.

  6. Lisp of seal oil coolers on water side and clean if required  .

  7. Inspect seal oil cooler gaskets.

  8. Replace seal oil cooler gaskets.

  9. Clean seal oil coolers on oil side .

  10.  Clean vents.

Bearings

  1. Inspect  bearing insulation

  2. Through cleaning of bearing .

  3. Inspect bearing sleeves

  4. Inspect oil pipes

  5. Check spherical seats

  6. Inspect oil grooves and pipes for oil jacking

  7. Perform check of oil jacking pump

  8. Inspect pressure control valve and non-return valve

  9. Inspect drains of oil vapour extractor and pressure breaker pipes

  10. Inspect oil vapour extractor fan

  11. Clean and inspect non-return valve of oil vapour extractor

  12. Check oil pipes

Seal Oil Supply System

  1. Check seal oil pumps incl. motors

  2. Inspect oil supply pressure controller overflow valve and relief valve.

  3. Inspect seal oil pressure controller.

  4. Check oil filters and cleaning if required.

  5. Check automatic seal oil control system.

  6. Inspect  isolating and non-return valves.

  7. Check performance of solenoid valves.

  8. Check pressure gauge stop valves.

  9. Vent and check  pressure gauges check contact settings.

  10. Check performance of pressure gauges.

  11. Check thermometers.
