Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1 on gas SF6 bushing 220 k, which must be performed on monthly bases and every two years as indicated by M1/Y2 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Kamal Oshba, tested by eng. Reda Ragab Karam, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam , approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia: WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass... etc

  • Breaker should be isolated in full open position and grounding on both sides of CB is required

  • Switch off control current (dc) for CB, switch on the ventilation

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical tool kit and torque wrench

  • An industrial vacuum cleaner plus clean dry cloth rags

  • SF6 gas handing and reconditioning trailer, Sf6 gas detector portable pressure gauge

  • MP MOLYSLIP grease soapsuds and vacuum cleaner

Work to be carried out

Every month

  1. Verification of gas pressure

  2. Clean dust if required

Every two years

  1. Under service, dust, soot, salt etc.. may deposit on the insulator sheds thus lowering the insulator surface withstand capability.

  2. Check gas humidity.
