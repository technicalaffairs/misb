Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • The breaker must be at first in the opened and discharged status.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet.

  • A (2500 V) Megger

Work to be Carried Out

Electrical Checks

Insulation Tests

Primary Disconnects

A (2500 V) Megger is used to measures the insulation resistance (phase to phase and Phase to ground) a typical Value of the resistance is > 10’000 Mega Ohm.

Secondary disconnects

  1. Connect all the secondary disconnect pins by a conducting wire except pin 24 (ground pin),

  2. Measure the insulation resistance by a 500 V Megger

Primary Circuit Resistance

  1. Put the breaker in the closed position.

  2. Use a low resistance measuring instrument (100 A) rated.

  3. Resistance is measured between the breaker’s primary bars.

Vacuum Interrupter Integrity Test

This test is required to ensure that no loss of vacuum occurred. It needs a high voltage AC power source with variable gradual output.

  1. Put the breaker in the TRIP position.

  2. A dry cloth should very well clean the entire vacuum interrupter surface.

  3. Apply A 36 kV (rms) 50/60 Hz and 500 v/sec hold for 10 seconds.

  4. No breakdown should occur for the interrupter to pass this test.

  5. If a breakdown occurs at a voltage V1 then repeat the test, the interrupter will be accepted if it passes the second test.

  6. If the breakdown occurs again at V2>V1 then stop the procedure, remove the high voltage source, and Discharge any residual electric charge by a grounded cable, then restart.

  7. If the breakdown occurs again at V2 = V1 then stop the procedure, then the interrupter is said to fail passing the test.

  8. If the test failed for the third time, the vacuum is believed to be lost.

  9. Remove the high Ac Voltage source.

  10. Discharge any residual electric charge by a grounded cable.

 A DC source of 50 kV maximum for 10 seconds can be used but half way rectifier testers is not recommended for use.
