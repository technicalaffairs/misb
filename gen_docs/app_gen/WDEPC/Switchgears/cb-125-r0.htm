Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I3 which must be performed every two years or every six hundred operating hours of the air compressor which comes first as indicated by Y2 / H600 in the title block 

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Hesain Ibrahem, tested by eng Hamdy Taher Aly, accepted by general director eng. Shawqy Mahmoud Atta Alah, and approved by eng. Mohamed Mahdy Rezq Alah Al Nashar Head sector of Sidi Kirir

Also the first draft initiated by eng. Atef Abdel Karem Mohamed at Rabt Aswan 220kv substation, accepted by general directors eng. Abdel Razeq Aly Abdel Moniem & eng. Mousa Meawad Mousa, and approved by eng. Fawzy Mousa Mohamed: Aswan Network Head sector UE zone, EETC.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, and safety glasses

  • Breaker should be isolated, in full open position and grounding on both sides of CB is required.

    •

  • Shut down the power supply

  • Switch off control current (d.c)

    • for CB

  • Switch on the ventilation.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety shoes, and Gas detector.

  • An industrial - type vacuum cleaner, plus clean, lint - free dry cloth rags

  • Bottles of loctite 242 and 270 (by loctite, segrate, Milan, Italy)

  • A tool kits with metric spanners and keys

  • A tin of Molykote 111 greases (Fapa, Bergamo, Italy)

  • A tin of FOMBLIN UT 18 (Montedison, Milan, Italy)

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Carryout the procedures mentioned at MPIS document no. CB-124-r0 annually or 200 operation hours of compressor inspection

  2. Strip, clean up, inspect and re-assemble the intake and delivery valve assemblies 42 & 58, the non-return valve 15 as well as the breather and oil baffle placed on the crank case rear cover 46  figure 1, 2

    3.

  3. Check of the condensate resistor operation.

  4. Substitute the inlet and delivery valves in case of abnormal charging time.

  5. Substitute the compression rings in case of abnormal charging time.
