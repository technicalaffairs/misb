Introduction

This document lists the minor maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years or 500 operations for compressor which comes first as indicated by Y5 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Mostaf Bassuni Shokr, tested by eng Hamdy Taher Aly, accepted by general director eng. Shawqy Mahmoud Atta Alah, and approved by eng. Mohamed Mahdy Rezq Alah Al Nashar Head sector of Sidi Kirir

Also the first draft initiated by eng. Atef Abdel Karem Mohamed at Rabt Aswan 220kv substation, accepted by general directors eng. Abdel Razeq Aly Abdel Moniem & eng. Mousa Meawad Mousa, and approved by eng. Fawzy Mousa Mohamed: Aswan Network Head sector UE zone, EETCo.

 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear personal protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses

  • The circuit breaker must be switched off, isolated completely, and grounded an both sides.

    •

  • Earthing switch must be closed on both sides of the breaker.

  • Switch off control current for CB (d.c), Shut down the power supply

    • ,

  • Switch on the ventilation

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Carryout the procedures mentioned at MPIS document no. CB-124-r0 annually inspection

    2.

Air  Compressor

The type G 20 B motor compressor (figure 2) is designed to supply the compressed air to pneumatically operated switchgear. Figures are included inside this document

  1. Measure the time compressor needs to bring the air system from atmospheric to rated operating pressure, compare it with the time recorded during the commissioning test (if possible try to keep the same conditions as temperature)

  2. If the pump up time exceeds 15 % of the commissioning time then:

    1. Disassemble, clean up, inspect and if necessary replace the intake and delivery valve blades 42 and 58 as well as the related gasket. 

    2. Check the cylinder bore for out of round by measuring the diameter with an accurate gauge placed at right angle and parallel to the crankshaft. If the out - of - round is excessive, refinish the cylinder walls dringing the cylinder bore to a diameter equal to the diameter of the new piston with excess clearance of 0.08 mm.

Circuit Breaker

  1. Check the operating time for the circuit breaker opening & closing if the time values don’t correspond to those indicated in the factory certificates 30ms/35ms for opening, 60ms for closing if not OK clean the filters, orifices and control valves of the operating mechanisms then measure the operating time again.

  2. With the control circuit at its rated voltage and SF6 air systems at their respective rated pressures, perform same closing and opening operations, recording the operation times with a multi-channel oscillograph.

  3. Measure the SF6 gas moisture if it is in one pole is higher than 250 PPMv, the SF6 contained in it must be reconditioned and the molecular sieves replaced. ref. MPIS document no. TD-SS-006-r1 and the table no. 1 attached
