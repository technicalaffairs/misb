Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every three months as indicated by M3 or after Shutdown in the title block.

The first initiated by eng. Mohamed Mostafa El-Zalabany, tested by eng. Samir Ouf Hassoub, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam, approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat head sector of Damanhour power station, and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia Diab WDEPC Inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as helmet, shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc

  • Switch off the CB and withdraw it from its cell.

  • Switch off the DC power supply for this CB.

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical equipment kit.

  • Dry Cloth, dry air from air blower.

Work to be carried out

  1. Complete Clean for Circuit breaker.

  2. Check the closing and opening order.

  3. Check the Inter-Lock for gas pressure.
