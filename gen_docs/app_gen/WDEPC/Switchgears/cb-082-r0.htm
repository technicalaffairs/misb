Introduction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • The breaker must be at first in the opened and discharged status.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes.

  • Complete tool set kit, and Grease.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Put the breaker in the opened position

  2. Wipe up the lubricant in order to see the metal surface condition.

  3. Check that the burnished silver surface must be with no copper at more than one location.

  4. Re- lubricate the surface.

  5. Drops of mechanical oil ( Mobil 1 ) at each bearing.

  6. Apply a film of grease on the four corners of the closing spring guide.

  7. Check that the contact surface cleaned and lubricated.

  8. Grease the movable contact rod of the interrupter.

  9. Grease the secondary disconnects coupler pins.
