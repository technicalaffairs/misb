Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block for motor operator for out boor disconnect switch

The first draft initiated by eng. Amged Abd El Aziz, tested by eng. Wageh Khalil revised by MDEPCo. Quality control eng. Abel El Naggar accepted by eng. Ali Zahran general manager approved by Eng. Ahmed Sawan head sector of Nubaria power station and certified by Eng. Mohamed Nageb inspection and qulity head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

Tools and Equipments

  • Complete set of tools .

  • Tester (digital Avometer).

  • Set off spanner and screw drivers .

  • contact resistance meter .

  • Vacuum cleaner .

  • Motor oil, Gaskets

Work to be carried out\

  1. Check motor drive chain A light film of motor oil is sufficient, don't over - oil

  2. Check for excessive wear pitting or burning for electrical contacts

  3. Check for sealing effectiveness for door gasket and replace gasket if  worm or damaged

  4. Check the stroke of the disconnect switch mechanical and electrical

  5. Check the operation of the circle from manual position

  6. Check the operation of the circle from remote position

  7. Check the interlocks for operation of the disconnect switch
