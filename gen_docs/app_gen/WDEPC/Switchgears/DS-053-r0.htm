Introduction

This document outlines the equipment  inspection I1 , which must be performed every two years as indicated by Y2 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Kamal Oshba, tested by eng. Reda Ragab Karam, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Dorgam , approved by eng. Gaber Mohamed Farahat and certified by eng. Sania Diab Attia: WDEPC inspection head sector and MPIS coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass... etc

  • Breaker should be isolated in full open position and grounding on both sides of CB is required

  • Switch off control current (dc) for CB

  • Switch on the ventilation

Tools and Equipment

  • Electrical tool kit and torque wrench

  • An industrial vacuum cleaner plus clean dry cloth rags

  • SF6 gas handing and reconditioning trailer

  • Sf6 gas detector portable pressure gauge

  • MP MOLYSLIP grease soapsuds and vacuum cleaner

Work to be carried out

  1. Checking gas humidity (the value must be less than 500 ppm)

  2. Checking mechanical indicators

  3. Checking limit contact operation

  4. Greasing the moving pats of the operating mechanism system and of the transmission system
