Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

    •

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for the motor of this compressor.

  • Isolate the electrical cable feeding of the motor of this compressor.

  • The compressor must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery valve.

  • Close the cooling water supply.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags, soft brushes.… etc)

  • Manual puller, Feeler gauge,

  • Compressed air supply.

  • Spray gun, grease

Work to be Carried Out 

Intake Filter

  1. Visually inspect all inlet air filter elements and replace if necessary. Check the filter housing for corrosion and check the sealing surface.

  2. Remove the housing cover.

  3. Remove the top seal plate.

  4. Remove dirty cartridge and clean or replace as required.

  5. Clean bottom seal plate in base of filter housing.

NOTE: when a total differential pressure across filter housing of 15" w.c is indicated clean or replace filter element.

Inlet Throttle Valve

  1. Observe for freedom of movement of the inlet valve during the stroking procedure.

By Pass Valve

  1. Inspect the seals for damage. Replace damaged seals as required and reinstall valve.

Discharge Check Valve

  1. When inspecting the check valve, look for: rust, broken springs, damaged seals, freedom of movement

  2. Repair or replace as necessary and reinstall discharge check valve

Oil Suction Screen

  1. Inspect and clean the oil suction screen with solvent.

Oil filter

  1. Visually inspect and replace the oil filter element. A single line type oil filter is furnished consisting of a 10 micron replaceable cartridge element.

Oil Cooler

  1. Visually inspect the oil cooler tubes:

  2. Match mark and remove bonnets from oil cooler and inspect zinc anode for erosion for oxide deposits. Scrape to brighten surface and replace if more than half is corroded away.

  3. Carefully examine tubes for scale and clean if necessary .After cleaning, examine for erosion or corrosion.

  4. After maintenance inspection or cleaning, both shell and tube side should be carefully vented and full of liquid.

Mist Arrestor:

  1. Visually inspect the oil mist arrestor. Clean the housing and replace the. element if saturated.

  2. Take oil sample and analyze against Ingersoil-Rand oil Specification Replace oil if necessary         

  3. Rotate the bull gear by hand and feel for roughness in the bearings.

  4. Visually inspect the coupling. Lubricate if necessary.

  5. Remove and clean the condensate trap.

  6. Check function of pressure gauges and thermometers, recalibrate if necessary. Clean thermometer pockets.
