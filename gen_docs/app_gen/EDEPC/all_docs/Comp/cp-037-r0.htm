Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I3, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above titled block. 

This  compressor serves in compressing air for self-cleaning system which is using in cleaning the Gas Turbine GT air filters and HRSG 'Heat Recovery Steam Generator' inlet damper to control opening and closing.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Disconnect the power supply of the compressor.

  • Isolate the compressor mechanically.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Compressed air hose.

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags… etc).

  • Soft brushes..

  • Spray gun, Grease

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check safety valve.

  2. Inspect cooler for leaks, clean it if necessary

  3. Remove air filter element and also small air filter. Clean by air jet , inspect and change the damage one.

  4. Remove, dismantle and clean float valve of moisture trap.

  5. Change the oil of the compressor if the oil lost its good specifications.

  6. Check the oil filter, and the strainer

  7. Inspect the coupling to check the rubber joint and tightening of the bolts.

  8. Grease the bearings of the motor fan and compressor motor

  9. Replace all disengaged packings, e.g. gaskets, O - rings and washers
