Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

    •

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used (safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses).

  • Never use flammable solvents or carbon tetrachloride for cleaning

  • Never weld or perform any operation involving heat near the oil system. Oil tanks must be completely purged never weld on, or in any way modify, pressure vessels.

  • Never use caustic solvents which can damage materials of the air net, e.g. poly-carbonate bowls.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Megger (500 – 5000) volt, multimeter

  • Air blower

Work to be Carried Out

Air Flow

  1. Check air flow during loading.

  2. Check air flow during unloading.

  3. Check air filter.

  4. Check the air separator.

  5. Check pressure tank and connected fittings for tightness.

Oil System

  1. Check oil temperature.

  2. Check oil filter.

  3. Check the cooling and condensate drain system.

  4. Change the oil and valve inspection after 2000 operating hours at the latest, however, after 2 years.

Cooling System

  1. Cleaning the element of coolers

  2. Check the cooling and condensate drain system

  3. Check the air cooler (ca) and oil cooler (the cooling air is generated by fan)

  4. Check air fan wheel and blades

Electrical System

  1. Check compressor motor (M1)

  2. Check fan motor (M2)

  3. Check the bearings which must be cleaned and re-grease at time intervals of 18000, 12000 or 8000 hours for ambient temperatures of 20˚C.

  4. Check start/stop module which contains the compressor start and emergency stop bottom.

  5. Check start cubicle, which contains the starting equipment of the motors.

Electronic control module

  1. Check the electronic control module adapts the Operation of the unit, i.e. loading, unloading automatically starting and stopping depending on  the air consumption and a number of programmable conditions, such as:

  2. Check the unloading pressure.

  3. Check the pressure difference between unloading and Loading pressures.

  4. Check the compressor element outlet shut down Temperature .

  5. Check the control and display panel.

  6. Settings of the programmable data.
