Introduction

This document outlines the equipment maintenance procedures which must be performed for liquid fuel check valve when pressure difference between fuel nozzles pressure (∆P) > 75psi.

Check valve avoid the flow divider speed to increase boundessly, so it permits the fuel to pass into combustion chamber at certain pressure (90 to 100 psi).

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs … etc.

  • Turbine must be shutdown.

  • Disconnect power supply to driver motor.

  • Closing all valves controlling flow (delivery and suction valves) of the pump.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Rags, Cleaning Fluid.

Work to be carried out

Disassembly

  1. Disconnect the fuel lines from the fuel check valve.

  2. Remove the check valve by placing a wrench on the hex flats nearest fuel nozzle. 

  3. Left the check valve to work place.

Pressure Test Fuel Oil Check Valves before Valve Disassembly

  1. Use 90 to 100 psi air pressure and submerge check valve under water to check for leakage.

  2. If check valve opened at range (90 to100 psi) it be ok, and

  3. If it opened before pressure 90 psi or after 100 psi it must be disassemble to inspect the spring and flexible seal.       

Valve Disassembly

  1. Mount valve vertically in a vise or suitable holding fixture by clambing on hex of body portion ( outlet side)

  2. Using a box type wrench of proper size on the hex of the cap (inlet side) turn counter clock wise.

  3. Remove wrench and carefully turn out cap by hand for last two or three threads. Lift off cap.

  4. Remove body portion from vise, internal parts may now be tapped out by striking body against hand or other soft surface .

Inspection

  1. Check metal parts for damage, i.e., for nicks, and scratches caused by fluid contaminents, replace any damaged parts.

  2. Discard old flexible seal.

Valve Assembly:

  1. Mount valve body vertically as on disassembly

  2. Drop spring into valve making sure it seats properly on spring seat at outlet end.

  3. Assemble seal retainer over nose of poppet.

  4. Select new flexible seal of proper size.

  5. Assemble flexible seal over nose of poppet.

  6. Drop poppet, seal retainer. and flexible seal assembly into valve opening Poppet first.

  7. Carefully start cap thread into body thread and snug down with fingers

  8. Tighten cap with box type wrench of proper size.

Make a Pressure test after valve Assembly

Connect check valve to fuel nozzle and fuel line
