Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks to surround the work area.

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate valve mechanically.

  • Drain the pipeline, which connected to this valve. 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Spanner, gasket, spacer ring.

  • Cleaning liquid.

  • Blanco, anti seize.

  

 

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Be sure the valve was out of operation and the pressure inside the pipeline connected to the valve must be zero from the two end of the valve.

  2. Be sure the pipeline is drained.

  3. Clean from foreign material.

  4. Lap the disk wear or cracks or change the defect disk.

  5. Change the bearing if it is bad.

  6. Make machining for stem or replace the defect.

  7. Replace the pen if it is wear.

  8. Retighten the gland if it is leakage or replace it.
