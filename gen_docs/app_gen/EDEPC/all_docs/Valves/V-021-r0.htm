Introduction

This valve is used for feed the water to the drum and de-superheat the main steam.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks to surround the work area.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as: safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs .... etc

  • Isolate the valve electrically and mechanically.

  • Drain the pipeline, which connected of valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Suitable lifting device

  • Lapping tools and grinding compound.

   

Work to be Carried Out

Before isolating the valve mechanically

  1. Check any leakage from valve gland.

  2. Check any external leakage.

  3. Check any internal leakage.

  4. Check any leakage from the valve bodies ( cracks or mechanical wear ).

After isolating the valve electrically and mechanically

  1. Be sure the pressure inside the pipeline connected to the valve must be zero from the two end of the valve.

  2. Be sure the pipeline is drained.

Disassembly the valve

  1. Attach a sling to the actuator (1) assembly.

  2. Remove the stem clamp (2)

  3. Remove the packing flange (3) and yoke clamp (4).

  4. Lift the actuator assembly from the body (5) assembly and rem

  5. Screw off nut (6) of bolt (7) which connected bonnet (8) with valve body.

  6. Remove the bonnet from the body.

  7. Remove the bonnet gasket (9) and upper spaces gasket (10).

  8. Remove the packing (11) as follows:
    - Remove the packing flange and packing follower (12)
    - Using a packing tool, remove all packing.
    - Replace the suitable packing size on a part of shaft with the same diameter of valve spindle (13).

  9. Remove the spindle.

  10. Lift the disk stack (14) assembly from the body.

  11. Remove the seat ring (15) from the body.

  12. Remove the flex. gaskets (16) from the seat ring.

Inspect and Repair of all parts

  1. Check the body from any cracks or wear by using penetration fluids and repair it if needed.

  2. Check the seat ring and regrind it if needed.

  3. Check the spindle and repair it if needed or replace it.

  4. Check the flex.  gaskets and change it if needed.

  5. Check the bushing (17) and repair it if needed or replace it.

  6. Check the packing and change it if needed.

  7. Check the packing follower, packing spacer, packing flange and repair it if needed or replace it.

 

 

Assembly of the Valve

  1. Clean every parts well before any assembly.

  2. Assembly the valve parts in reverse sequence of disassembly

  3. Open and close the valve and be sure that the valve operation is correct.

 

Trouble Shouting

Problem

Action

Stem packing leakage

Inspect the steam and replace the assembly if it is damaged, also replace the packing

Excessive internal leakage

- Disassemble the valve, check for damage to the seat ring/spindle and seals and replace as necessary

- Seat grinding

Bonnet leakage

Check the parts and replace the damage parts
