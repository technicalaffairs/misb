Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every 12000 operation hours as indicated by H12000 in the above title block.

Check valve for fuel gas piping for GT frame 5 insuring the supplying of natural gas for gas turbine and to check the flow in one direction

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, earplug, and gloves pairs...etc

    •

  • Unit out of service.

  • Fuel system should be isolated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Box type wrench,

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly

  1. Remove the check valve from the fuel piping.

  2. Mount valve vertically in a suitable holding fixture.

  3. Using a box – type wrench on the her of the cap turn counter clock wise.

  4. Remove wrench and care fully turn out cap by hard for last two or three threads.

  5. Remove body portion from vise. Internal parts may now be tapped out by striking body against hand or other soft surface or by inserting small rod in out let end and gently pushing on poppet from the spring side.

Inspection

  1. Check metal parts for damage, replace any damaged parts.

  2. Discard old flexible seal.

  3. Valve may be cleaned.

  4. Allow valve parts to dry thoroughly before reassembling.

Assembly

  1. Mount valve body vertically as an disassembly.

  2. Drop spring into valve making sure it seats properly on spring seat at outlet end.

  3. Assemble seal retainer over nose of poppet making sure that the lip of the seal retainer is outward toward poppet nose.

  4. Select new flexible seal of proper size.

  5. Lubricate seal by dipping in fluid from the hydraulic system, proper lubrication is important to obtain correct seal positioning during assembly.

  6. Assemble flexible seal over nose of poppet so that seal retainer is now sand wiched between seal and poppet.

  7. Drop poppet , seal retainer, and flexible seal assembly into valve opening first so that poppet body slides over the spring . make sure spring seats properly in poppet.

  8. Place small amount of lubricant on threads of cap

  9. Carefully stat. cap thread into body thread and snug down with fingers, cap seat should properly position flexible seal and seal retainer automatically and should turn down with only finger pressure with in approximately ½ turn of wrench tight.

  10. Tighten cap with box type wrench.

  11. Make sure spring is properly seated at both sides.

  12. Install the check valve into the fuel piping.
