This document outlines the equipment Inspection list II, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

The steam converting valve has the task to reduce the hot steam within the pressure and at the same time to lower the steam temperature on to the disired dimension

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of valve hyd. station.

  • The valve must be isolated mechanically by no steam in its line.

  • Be sure the line must be completely drainage.

  • Check thermometers and assure that temperature is less than 40 °C.

  • Provide adequate scaffolding, lighting, platforms or ladders.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags.… etc)

  • Manual puller, Feeler gauge

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the gland packing (311) in regard of leakiness and if required immediately retighten.

  2. Check the tightening moment of the hexagonal nut (333) after the pressure test.

  3. Check the tightening moment of screws after the pressure test and required immediately retighten.

  4. Check the traveling time in case of the plant standstill.

  5. Check any external leakage.

  6. Check mechanical wear (remark the point of leakage and welded it with suitable electrode.
