Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedure P1 of main steam stop valve, depended on external or internal leakage. This valve is used for isolate main steam (90 bar, 520 ْ C)

Technical specification

Type of  Valve

Wedge Gate Valve

Size

250 mm (10 inch)

Drive

Motor drive

Class

1500

Operation condition

90 bar, 520 ْ C

Medium

Supper heated steam

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks to surround the work area.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as: safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs .... etc

  • Isolate the valve electrically and mechanically

  • Drain the pipeline, which connected of valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Suitable lifting device

  • Lapping tools and grinding compound.

 

Work to be Carried Out 

During the Boiler in service

  1. Check for any leakage from valve gland, retighten on bolts.

  2. Check for any internal leakage.

  3. Check for any leakage from valve bodies.

After shutdown the boiler

  1. Be sure the pressure in boiler reach to zero

  2. Drain the main steam line by opening drain valves before and after valve.

Disassembly the valve

  1. Prior to disassembling, open the valve more than 1/3 of total left.

  2. Remove the actuator bolts (12), and retighten the gland packing, and then lock the stem (05).

  3. Turn the actuator handle clock wise and disengage the actuator (7) from the stem (5), and then hang it with rope from the ceiling.

  4. Take out the yoke clamp, and disengage the yoke (11) from the body (1) then hang it with rope from the ceiling

  5. Loosen the nuts (15) on the bonnet bolts (14) at this point.

  6. Hit lightly on the upper rim of the retaining ring (6) so as to loosen it counter clock wise, then take away the retaining ring from the bolt, and remove the seal ring (8).

  7. Disengage the combination block comprising the stem(5), disc (3) and bonnet (2) etc. from the body (1). In this case the care should be taken to avoid damage to contact surface. And identification marks on the body and disc should be checked to avoid misplacing of the disc in reassembling.

  8. Remove the eye bolts (13) and eye bolts clamp.

Checking and Repairing

  1. Respective parts should be checked carefully, especially for :

  2. Wearing of the disc and seat surface, the lapping of the disc and seat, where necessary may be carried out by using lapping tools and compound.

  3. Hardening of the gland packing due to fatigue and renew it if needed.

  4. Wearing of the seal ring and the opposite surface of contact, repair or renew the seal ring.

  5. Wearing of the stem where it rope against the gland packing in the stem

  6. Wearing of the back seat.

Reassembly of the valve

  1. Clean every part well.

  2. Supply the graphite compound for respective threaded parts of the bonnet and eye bolts to prevent the seizure

  3. Assembly the valve parts in reverse sequence of disassembly.
