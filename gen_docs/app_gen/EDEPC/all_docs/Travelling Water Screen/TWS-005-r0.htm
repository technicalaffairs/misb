Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • closing delivery and suction valves of filters & coolers.

  • Be sure filters & coolers must be completely venting &  drainage for oil to clean tank.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Main Crane, grease gun,

  • Manual Puller, Feeler Gauge, Rags, Cleaning Fluid,

  • Dial Indicator, Vibrometer.

Work to be carried out 

Traveling water screen in service

  1. Check water level is available in pit.

  2. Check no alarms in operation panel.

  3. Start low speed and open wash valve.

  4. Check sound and vibration.

  5. Check all spray in service.

Traveling water screen out of service

  1. Check alignment and bolts fixation.

  2. Check Screen chain ,screen trays and seal plates, gear reducer.

  3. Check spray header &nozzles , drive chain sprockets.

  4. Check head sprocket tooth inserts & head shaft bearings.

  5. Check chain fixation, then retightens it (if it is needed).

  6. Check the validity of bolts and add or change it (if it is needed).

  7. Check the boot castings & boot plate and foot sprockets and bearings (if it is needed).
