Safety Precautions
  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.
  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.
  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.
  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles. ...etc
    •
  • The charger must be out of service and isolated.

Tools and Equipment
  • Complete maintenance tool set
  • Cleaning solvent 
  • Contact resistance tester
  • Megger (500 – 5000) volt
  • Multimeter
  • Air blower
