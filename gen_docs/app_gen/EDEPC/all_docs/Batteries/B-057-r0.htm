Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, safety Helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses, safety goggles, rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • Smoking is prohibited inside the battery room

  • The battery instructions must be displayed visibly on the wall..

  • Water source must be available in the battery room.

  • When working in the battery room be aware of parts under voltage.

  • The battery set is out of service if necessary.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete set of common keys

  • Thermometer, and Hydrometer

  • Digital multimeter.

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Ensure that the cells are clean and dry, external clean the caps.

  2. Check that connections are properly tightened, lubricate them and fully screw them.

  3. Check the temperature of the cells: constantly high temperature will increase the consumption of water and reduce the life of the battery whereas, constantly low temperature will lower discharge performance.

  4. Read and record the voltage, density and temperature of each cell.

  5. Check the electrolyte level and top them up with distilled water if necessary.

  6. Apply an equalizing charge.

  7. Carryout full charge-discharge cycle.
