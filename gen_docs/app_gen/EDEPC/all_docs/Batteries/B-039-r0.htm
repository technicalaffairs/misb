Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

The technical specification of the battery 48 vdc No. of cell = 23, Dc output = 46 vdc, float charge voltage = 51.3 vdc, boost charge voltage = 55 vdc, rated capacity=200Ahr

The technical specification of the battery 110 vdc No. of cell = 54, Dc output = 110 vdc, float charge voltage = 120.42 vdc, boost charge voltage = 129.6 vdc, current limitation = 16 ADC rated capacity=50Ahr

The first draft initiated by eng. Abd El Mohsen Mohamed; Manager of Nag Hammady Hydraulic PS, and certified by eng. Kamal Ragheb Haleem HPGC "MPIS Coordinator & QC general Director"

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • No smoking is permitted in battery room.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

  • Use the proper insulated tools.

  • Disconnect the power supply fuse.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard personal safety and healthy equipment as Safety helmet, safety shoes ...etc

  • Hydrometer thermometer and AVO meter

  • Sulfuric acid solution 30%

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the readings of the voltage for cell.

  2. Check the connection between the batteries.

  3. Record solution temperature and density for cell.

  4. Record the charging and discharging voltages.

  5. Cleaning the outer surface and check solution level.
