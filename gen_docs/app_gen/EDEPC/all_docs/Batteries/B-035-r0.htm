Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, safety helmet, breathing mask, and rubber gloves

  • The door of the battery room must be provided with NO Smoking signs.

  • The battery instructions must be displayed visibly on the wall..

  • Water source must be available in the battery room.

  • When working in the battery room be aware of parts under voltage.

  • The battery set is out of service if necessary.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard personal safety equipment, safety goggles, rubber gloves, and rubber apron.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Divide the numbers of batteries to 4 groups

  2. Test one group each week

  3. Record the volt and specific gravity for chosen  group and record the internal temperature  for one cell and record the air temperature .

  4. Check the specific value.
