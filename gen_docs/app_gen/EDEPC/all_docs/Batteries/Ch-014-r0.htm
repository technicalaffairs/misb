Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed every 6 month as indicated by M6 in the title block

The first draft initiated by : eng. Abd  ElAziz EL Bagoury, and tested by eng. Doaa  Anis and revised by quality control E ng. Adel  Abdel EL Kader EL Nagar at EDEPCo., accepted by general director eng. Mahmoud Saker and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talkha PS  

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • Caution marks must be hanged on a work place.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable Tool Set.

  • Digital Voltmeter

  • Brush, Blower.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check for correct operation of  D.C room air  condition.

  2. Check ventilation fan of rectifier.

  3.  Read input and output voltage and record

  4.   Switch off input and output supply.

  5. Check over heating compartment of rectifier cards.

  6. Check all abnormal cases inside the rectifier.

  7. Inspect visually fuses, contactors, capacitors, rectifier card and diodes.

  8. Check that the terminals are well tightened and cleaned.

  9. Check that  the terminals of the AC switch and DC switch are well tightened.

  10. Clean the rectifier from dusty air.

  11. Clean the floor of the D.C room.

  12. Switch on input and output supply.

  13. Check  float and equalize voltage.

  14. Check the current limit operation.

  15. Check input and output voltage and record.
