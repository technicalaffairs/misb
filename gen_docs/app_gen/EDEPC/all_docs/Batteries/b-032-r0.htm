Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list I3 witch must performed every 6 months as indicated by M6 in the title block The technical specification battery group

227 AH,S.G = 1.205-1.220AT 25Cº

This battery is used for UPS of the computers of main unit and control room computers.

The first draft is initiated & reviewed by eng. Nagy Bekheit Saleh, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad general director, approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station; HPSEPC, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem MPIS HPSEPC coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Use standard personal safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves

    • ... etc.

  • Isolate the batteries by switching off the output DC automatic switch

Tools and Equipment

  • Boots, goggles, plastic gloves

  • Distilled water, hydrometer, voltmeter

  • Grease for interconnections of batteries

  • H2 S 04 electrolyte

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connections should be tight and coated with an anti - corrosive agent

  2. Battery should be clean and free of corrosion

  3. Record the readings of S.G and voltage

  4. Adjust the batteries levels

  5. Put the batteries into work
