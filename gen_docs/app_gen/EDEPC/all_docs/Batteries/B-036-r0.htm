Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block

The first draft is initiated by eng. Mostafa Ali Kheder  tested by eng.Sherif Mahrous . quality control general director eng Gamal Abd Elwahab, accepted by technical expert eng. Hassan Elbehiry and approved by eng. Mohamed Gamal Diab Head sector of El-Kureimat power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, rubber gloves

  • Don't smoke in the battery room.

  • Use standard personal safety equipment (goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves)

  • Don't use any tools on top of battery.

  • All tools will be used must be insulated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard personal safety equipment, safety goggles, rubber gloves, and rubber apron.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Observe the general appearance and cleanliness of the battery

  2. Read total voltage of bank and record it on battery maintenance card.

  3. Disconnect AC supply to battery charger.

  4. Check the electrolyte levels and adjust if necessary 

  5. Inspect the container of cells for cracks and leakage of electrolyte.

  6. If you discover a defective cell use the recommended procedure to replace with new cell.

  7. Record density for each cell, add distilled water if level is below normal.

  8. Inspect inter-cell connectors and terminals of cells and clean any salt deposits with distilled water.

  9. Check terminal connections for tightness.

  10. Clean terminal connection with soft brush.

  11. Check and add a thin film of Vaseline to the terminal connections

  12. Check for vent cap damage.

  13. Read voltage per cell and record it on battery maintenance card.

  14. Return battery bank to regular service.
