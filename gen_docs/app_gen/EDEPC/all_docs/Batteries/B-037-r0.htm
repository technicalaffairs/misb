Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The first draft is initiated by eng. Mostafa Ali Kheder tested by eng.Sherif Mahrous quality control general director eng Gamal Abd Elwahab, accepted by technical expert eng. Hassan Elbehiry and approved by eng. Mohamed Gamal Diab Head sector of El-Kureimat power station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, rubber gloves

  • Don't smoke in the battery room.

  • Use standard personal safety equipment (goggles, protective clothing, rubber gloves)

  • Don't use any tools on top of battery.

  • All tools will be used must be insulated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Standard personal safety equipment ( safety goggles, rubber gloves, and rubber apron )

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Make a detailed visual inspection of each cell.

  2. Tighten all bolted connections to the specified torque values.

  3. Take and record connection resistances of each cell to cell, cell to terminal, inter-level and load connections.

  4. Remark any connections that are more than 20% above installation base value.

  5. Check integrity of the rack.
