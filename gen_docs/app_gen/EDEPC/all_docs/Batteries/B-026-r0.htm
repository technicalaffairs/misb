Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I3, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

Type: Lead Acid Battery – Maintenance Free

No. of Cells: 108

Cell Voltage: 2V

Capacity: 250 Ah

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, safety Helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses, safety goggles, rubber gloves and rubber apron.

  • The door of the battery room must be provided with sign of No Smoking.

  • The battery instructions must be displayed visibly on the wall..

  • Water source must be available in the battery room.

  • When working in the battery room be aware of parts under voltage.

  • The battery set is out of service if necessary.

Tools and Equipment

  • Thermometer, and Hydrometer

  • Digital multimeter   ( AVO – METER )

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Perform the monthly maintenance procedure.

  2. Measure and record the specific gravity in each cell. The accepted limit is ( 1.24  ± 0.02 ).

  3. Cheek and record the temperature of the electrolyte in each cell.

  4. Disconnect AC supply to battery charger.

  5. Read and record over all battery immediately after AC power is switched off and then after 15 Minutes.

  6. Record the voltage every hour for 8 hour duration.

  7. If voltage fall below 215v, stop the test and charge the battery set in boost mode for 5 hours .

  8. Return to the floating mode and compare the obtained readings with the readings recorded in previous years.

  9. Place the battery set back into normal operating condition.

  10. Measure and record the voltage and density for each cell in the Maintenance Record Data Sheet MRDS
