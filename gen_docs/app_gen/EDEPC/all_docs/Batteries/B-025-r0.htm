Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block

Type: Lead Acid Battery – Maintenance Free

No. of Cells: 108,   Cell Voltage: 2V,  Capacity: 250 Ah

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdallah Elsayed M, revised by quality control eng. Adel  Abd – EL kader El nagar at EDEP Co., accepted by eng El Sayed Attiah, and approved by eng. Awad Soliman Head Sector of Talkha PS.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses, safety goggles, rubber gloves and rubber apron.

    •

  • The door of the battery room must be provided with NO Smoking signs.

  • The battery instructions must be displayed visibly on the wall..

  • Water source must be available in the battery room.

  • When working in the battery room be aware of parts under voltage.

  • The battery set is out of service if necessary.

Tools and Equipment

  • Hydrometer, Digital multimeter  ( AVO – METER )

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect terminals of battery and clean any deposits with distilled water.

  2. Cheeks for correct position of all vent covers.

  3. Clean battery room floor with water.

  4. Check for correct operation of battery room exhaust fan.

  5. Measure and record the voltage and density for each cell in the Maintenance Record Data Sheet MRDS
