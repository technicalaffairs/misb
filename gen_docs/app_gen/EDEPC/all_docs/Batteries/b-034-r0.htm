                                                                Introduction

This document outlines inspection check list I3, witch must by performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block .The technical specification of the battery group - 1020AH,8 HR - Rate, S.G = 1-170

The first draft is initiated and reviewed by eng. Nagy Bekheit Saleh, accepted by eng. Badr Ibrahem Abdel Gawad general director, and approved by eng. Abdel Karem Abu Zied Ismaiel; Head Sector of High Dam Hydraulic Power Station,  Hyd.EPCo.

This document certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Hakem MPIS Hyd.EPCo. Coordinator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety helmet, safety shoes ..etc

  • Switch of the chargers, the isolating disconnecting switch

Tools and Equipment

  • Boots, goggles, plastic gloves, soda, clean water

  • Grease for interconnections of batteries

  • H2 S 04 electrolyte

  • Hydrometer

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connections should be tight and coated with an anti - corrosive agent

  2. Battery should by clean and free of corrosion

  3. Record the readings of S.G and voltage

  4. Adjust the batteries levels

  5. Switch on one of the two chargers

  6. Put the batteries on the equalizing state for 140 hours
