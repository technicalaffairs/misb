Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes and safety clothes when cleaning.

  • Isolate the rectifier/charger out of service and earth.

  • The presence of current in load is signaled by flashing caution lamp and a display segment.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes,

  • Screw driver set, Air blower

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

  • Portable earth discharging test loads

Work to be Carried Out

Visual Checks and General Cleaning

  1. Through cleaning of all power semi – conductors and of all control P.C. boards, remove foreign matter, if any.

  2. Check of tightness of all electrical and mechanical connections.

  3. Check that all P.C. boards are correctly plugged in their seats and that electromechanical components are well seated on their bases.

  4. Check that all vents be clean "remove possible digging material"

  5. Check that there is no condensate build up or water seepage.

  6. Check of the batteries.

Operational Checks

  1. Check that all signals and instruments furnish the correct indication.

  2. Check the correct operation of the system and/or equipment in the correct entirely.

  3. Check the setting value.

  4. If there are any failures in the equipment, check that all input and output voltages do correspond to the equipment plate rating, then, check possible alarm indication on the board front.

  5. Check that there is no condensate build up or water seepage.

  6. Checkout of the batteries.
