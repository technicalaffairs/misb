Introduction

This document outlines procedures P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The efficiency of the battery should be calculated during partial discharging  of the battery. The rated capacity of the battery, the charge and discharge characteristics should be available.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • No smoking is permitted in battery room.

  • Wear standard personal safety and healthy equipment as helmet, safety shoes, rubber gloves, ... etc.

  • Use the proper insulated tools.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multimeter.

  • Electrical load (high power resistors).

Work to be carried out

  1. Confirm that the battery is completely charged (Icharger = Iload) .

  2. Disconnect the battery charger and record the battery voltage and the voltage of each cell.

  3. Put the battery out of service and partially discharge the battery through suitable external load (4 ñ 5) hours.

  4. Read and record the discharging current each Ω hour , as well as the battery voltage and the voltage of each cell.

  5. Stop partial discharging after (4 ñ 5) hours.

  6. Connect the battery charger to recharge the battery . Read and record the charging current of the battery each 5 minutes and voltage per cell each ( Ω h) with great attention to the temperature of the cells.

  7. Continue in recharging of the battery together with recording the previous readings until the battery becomes completely charged when (Icharger = Iload) as in step 1 .

  8. Calculate the discharging capacity of the battery as the following :
     

              Average discharge current (I disch. ) av.   =            Σ I disch                        

                                                                                 Number of readings
     

  9. Calculate recharging capacity of the battery as the following :

               Average charging current (I ch. ) av.       =               Σ I ch             

                                                                              Number of readings

              Recharging capacity (AH) rech.     = (I ch) av  ◊  charging time

     

  10. Calculate

        Rated Capacity - [(AH) rech - (AH) disch]
