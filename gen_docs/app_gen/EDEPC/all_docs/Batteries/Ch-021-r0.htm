Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, goggles ...etc

  • The charger must be out of service and isolated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set, linen cloth, small screw drivers

  • Digital DC voltmeter

  • Brush, Air Blower or vacuum cleaner.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the dirt and dust which accumulated.

  2. Clean an insulating parts with a soft, dry linen cloth.

  3. Read and record the input and output voltage.

    4.

  4. Check lightening of bus bar connection.

  5. Check the monitoring units.

  6. Measure mains supply voltage and check whether all contactors are live.

  7. Check auxiliary circuit connection on devices and terminal strips for tight fit.

  8. Check with the test boot on whether all lamps are in working condition

  9. Check fuses for continuity and replace in case need.

  10. Check the charger for abnormal noise and smell.

  11. Make sure that the air condition is work well.

  12. Connect all items which are disconnected.

  13. Place the battery charger back into normal operating condition.
