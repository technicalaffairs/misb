Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, safety Helmet, breathing mask.

  • The charger must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multimeter ( AVO – METER )

  • Screw driver, Air Blower or Vacuum cleaner.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the out put voltage.

  2. Clean the cubicle ventilation grid.

  3. Place the battery charger back into normal operating condition.
