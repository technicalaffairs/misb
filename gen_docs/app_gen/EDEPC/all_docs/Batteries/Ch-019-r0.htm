Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be will trained and qualified or directly supervised by some one who is.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes,...etc

  • The charger must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set.

  • Digital voltmeter

  • Brush, Air Blower

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the correct operation of the DC room air condition.

  2. Read and record the input and output voltage.

  3. Switch off input and output voltage supply.

  4. Check over heating compartment of rectifier cards.

  5. Check all abnormal cases inside the rectifier.

  6. Inspect visually fuses, contractors, capacitors, rectifier cards and diodes. 

  7. Clean the rectifier from dust.

  8. Clean the floor of DC room.

  9. Switch  input and output  supply.

  10. Check input and output voltage and record .

  11. Check the ground alarm.

  12. Place the battery charger back into normal operating condition.
