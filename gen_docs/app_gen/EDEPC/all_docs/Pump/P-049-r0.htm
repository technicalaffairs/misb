Introduction

This document  outlines  the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

This Pump transfers raw water from Nile to the water treatment plant.

The first draft is initiated and tested by eng. Mohammed Abd El Kader,  reviewed by maintenance manager eng. Mohammed Farag, revised by EDEPC inspection & quality control eng. Abouda Elbana, approved by eng. Awad Fathy: head sector of Talkha 2x210MW power station , and certified by eng. Aly Mostafa Al Lethy EDEPC inspection & quality control head sector.

Technical specification

Type of pump                              125 CVEV – 265 – 15 / 2LN

Design: Vertical centrifugal pump

Pump capacity                             75.6 m3/h (21 L/s)

Specific energy                             35 m W.C. (345 J/kg)

Pump Speed                                 1480 r.p.m.

Bearing Type                               7210/T - 2209

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks must be surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, straight edge, Rugs.

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Pump In Operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it  and determine what to do (if it is needed).

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare them with the setting value then replace      the worn- out components (if it is needed).

Pump Out Of Operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens it (if it is needed).

  3. Check the validity of oil and add or change it (if it is needed).

  4. Check glands for leaking and re-packing (if it is needed).

  5. Check flanges for leaking and re-tight ( if it is needed ) .

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).
