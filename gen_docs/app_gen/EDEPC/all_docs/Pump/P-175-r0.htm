Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump  must be completely drained.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid manual puller

  • Feeler gauge & dial indicator.

  • Rages.

Work to be Carried Out

Pump in Service

  1. Record pump pressure reading.

  2. Check discharge quantity.

  3. Check and record the vibration level of the pump.

 Pump out of Service

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value ( and record it ).

  2. Make test for bearings oil and added or change it ( if it is needed ).

  3. Check flanges for water leakage.

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tighten ( if it is needed ).

  5. Check glands for water leakage and re-pack ( if it is needed ).

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint ( if it is needed ).
