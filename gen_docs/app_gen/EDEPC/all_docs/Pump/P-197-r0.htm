Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to performed every 8000 equivalent operating hours EOH as indicated in the title block.

The high pressure oil pump: floats the shaft of unit at turn on and turning on of unit.

The shaft turning or hydraulic pump: turn the shaft of the unit at turning on of unit.  

The auxiliary control oil pump: generate the emergency and security oil at the beginning for turn on of the unit instead of the main gear pump

The auxiliary lubricating oil pump: pumping the oil at the beginning and stop for turn on of the unit instead of the main gear pump.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Turbine must be shut down.

  • Fuel system ( solar – gas ) of gas turbine out of service.

  • Isolate the step – up transformer and it’s circuit breaker

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of all pump motors and disconnect the electrical terminal of all pump motors

  • Fire fighting system must be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Oil bath

  • Clippers

  • Heater

  • Rags

  • Bearings: ( 6309 , 6307 ) / Bearings: ( 6309 , 6309 )

  • Bearings: ( 7314 , uN314 ) / Bearings: ( 6209 , 6209 )

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Inspect the bearing of pump motor if it is not good change it as follows:

  2. Loose the face of motor and the case bearing bolts.

  3. Pull the motor face to out side.

  4. Determine the direction of bearing construction.

  5. Pull the old bearing using clippers.

  6. Install the new bearing using pressure and hot oil path ( care the direction of bearing ).

  7. Put grease for bearing case.

  8. Install the face motor.

  9. Retighten the case bearing bolts.

  10. Retighten the motor face bolts.

  11. Test the motor against rotation ( Manually – Electrical ).
