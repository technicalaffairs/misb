Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

This pump (4 pumps) function to wash the traveling water screens from plastics, wood, fungi, and greases.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the CB of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

  • Be sure that the pump is completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Rags, soft brushes

  • Filler, vibrometer, Dial indicator.

  • Grease.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Disassembly

The Pump in Operation

  1. Check the vibration level of the pump and record it.

  2. Check discharge, pressure and record them.

Disassembly

The Pump out of Operation

  1. Check the alignment and record it.

  2. Disconnect the motor.

  3. Unbolt the coupling between the motor and the pump.

  4. Remove the motor with its casing.

  5. Dismantle every pipes connecting to the pump.

  6. Remove the coupling key (10) and remove the coupling lower part.

  7. Remove the water shield (32).

  8. Remove the gland (241).

  9. Remove the packing (11) and lantern (225).

  10. Remove the upper shaft sleeve (233d).

  11. Remove the casted discharged head (952).

  12. Remove the flat gasket (3).

  13. Unbolt the screws and the nuts (X1) to remove the top column (957).

  14. Uncouple the coupling between the upper and intermediate shafts, remove the coupling and raise the upper shaft.

  15. Remove the guide bearing (( bushing (933) then the shaft sleeve (233c) )).

  16. Unbolt the screws and the nuts (X2) to remove the lower column (926).

  17. Uncouple the coupling between the intermediate and lower shafts, remove the coupling and raise the intermediate shaft.

  18. Unbolt the screws and the nuts (X3) to remove the discharge case (208).

  19. Remove the bearing (234) then the shaft sleeve (233b).

  20. Unbolt the screws and the nuts (X4) to remove the upper intermediate case (209).

  21. Remove the upper casing wear rings (223A) and impeller wear ring (224).

  22. Unbolt the screws and the nuts (X5) to remove the lower intermediate case (209).

  23. Remove the upper casing wear rings (223) and impeller wear ring (224).

  24. Raise the lower shaft with impeller.

  25. Unbolt the cap (237).

  26. Remove the stages of impeller and wear rings.

  27. Now the pump is completely disassembled.

Inspection

  1. Clean all parts of the pump.

  2. Change the bearings of the pump.

  3. Check all parts for any worn-out, replace the damaged.

  4. Check all parts for cracks, treat the cracks by grinding or remachining.

  5. Inspect the run-out of the shafts.

  6. Change the wearing rings.

  7. Measure the radial clearances for the pump and adjust them.

  8. Grease the bearings of the pump.

Assembly

  1. Clean all contact surfaces.

  2. Assemble the lower casing wear ring.

  3. Tight the cap (237) on the lower shaft.

  4. Mount the key (9) on the shaft.

  5. Assemble the first stage of impeller and fix it with the shaft by the key.

  6. Mount the impeller first stage lower wear ring and reset the shaft with the first stage on the suction casing.

  7. Mount the impeller first stage upper wear ring.

  8. Mount the shaft sleeves (233 & 233a) and guide bearing (234).

  9. Mount the upper casing wear ring and assemble the first stage cover.

  10. Assemble the lower intermediate case with the first stage on the suction case by tightening the screws and nuts (X5).

  11. Assemble the impeller second stage as followed in the first stage.

  12. Assemble the upper intermediate case with the lower intermediate case by tightening the screws and nuts (X4).

  13. Assemble the discharge case shaft sleeve (233b) and bearing (234).

  14. Assemble the upper intermediate case with the discharge case by tightening the screws and nuts X3

  15. Assemble the intermediate shaft with the lower shaft by tightening the coupling between them.

  16. Assemble the lower column (926) with the discharge case by tightening the screws and nuts (X2).

  17. Mount the shaft sleeve (233c) and bushing (933).

  18. Assemble the intermediate shaft with the upper shaft by tightening the coupling between them.

  19. Assemble the top column (926) with the lower column by tightening the screws and nuts (X1).

  20. Assemble the casted discharge head (952).

  21. Mount the shaft sleeve (233d), packing (11), lantern (225), then mount the gland (241) and mount the water shield (32).

  22. Mount the coupling between the pump shaft and the motor shaft, adjust the alignment and then fix the coupling.

  23. Assemble and fix the motor.

  24. Now the pump is completely assembled.

Starting the Pump

Before starting the Pump

  1. Open the suction and delivery valves of the pump.

  2. Check the direction of rotation of the pump (by pushing the electric button of the motor instantaneously).

Attendance and checking during Pump Operation

  1. Any water leaks.

  2. The discharge and pressure of the pump

  3. The vibration level.
