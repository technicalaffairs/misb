Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every three months or 2000 operating hours which comes first as indicated by M3 / H 2000 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety healthy, safety shoes

  • The motor have to be stopped and its breaker isolated before starting any maintenance procedure

Tools and Equipment

  • Lubricating oil typ : Mobil DTE Heavy medium - ISO - VG 68 ( or equivalent) approximately 75 Liters ( 60 for motor thrust, 15 for Pump thrust) see Note 1.

  • Grease type : Mobilex EB3 [or Lithium base equivalent Mobil - PLX 47] approximately 120 gm for regressing see Note 2.

  • Packing type: RT-P   4 pieces [ 16mm cross section]

Note 1: for both motor and pump thrust bearing oil should be changed every 4000 running hour (6 months)

Note 2: for motor lower bearing grease should be changed every 2000 running hour (3 months)

  • Grease Gun with - capacity 5 kg for changing grease

  • Grease Gun with - capacity 1 kg for Re-greasing

  • Torque wrench

  • Adjustable wrenches - with different sizes

  • Wrenches with width size (30,24,20)

  • Feeler gauge { flexible coupling clearance check ].

  • Mechanical tool kit

Work to be Carried Out   Drawing (dwg) no. 1001-CP-118-CP5-0004-04

Preparation

  Electrical Isolation

  1. Open main motor breaker, tag no. 1NB0206

  2. Turn off DC supply

  3. Open space heater No. 1NHA-12A-CCH-25

Mechanical Isolation

  1. Close suction valve No. 1AD-V-021

  2. Close discharge valve No. 1AD-V-027

  3. Close recirc valve No. 1AD-V-136

  4. Close discharge vent valve No. 1AD-V-025

  5. Close seal water valve No. 1AD-V-030

  6. Close pump cooling water valve No. 1EB-V-112 & 113

  7. Close motor cooling water valve No. 1EB-V-114 & 115

  8. Open drain valve No. 1AD-V-118

Procedures

MOTOR  type ANV-WT001,  mfg. TECO

  1. Check for oil color, quality and Taking sample for analysis change if required.

  2. Check for oil level. Top up if required.

  3. Lower bearing type - NU 238.

  4. Check for grease quality and color Change if required

  5. Flushing and cleaning for oil cooler if required. Flushing water pressure < 6 bar

  6. General cleaning and inspection for cooling water and oil connection lines for leakage (tighten if required)

 

PUMP  type WKTA300/6, mfg. KSB

  1. Check for oil color, quality and take a sample for analysis, change if required.

  2. Check for oil level and top up if required to the middle of side glass.

  3. Flush and clean the oil cooler if required. Flushing water pressure < 6 bar.

  4. General cleaning and inspection for cooling water and oil connection lines for leakage (tighten if required)

  5. Check backing and change if required

  6. Tight fit the gland (M 16 Bolts) if required or tight fit by hand only.

 

Flexible Coupling

  1. Check for clearance at the coupling using feeler gauge [ S=7-4+1]

  2. Check for maximum permissible wear of flexible parts {wear value should not exceed 7mm.s}

  3. Change flexible parts if required.

  4. Check tightened parts

  5. Check tightening torque between :

  • Motor and motor stool

  • Motor stool and discharge elbow

  • Discharge elbow and foundation ring

  • Upper and lower halves of coupling
