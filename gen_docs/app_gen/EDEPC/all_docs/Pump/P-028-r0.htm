Introduction

This Document outlines the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block

This pump used to supply cooling water for all plant auxiliaries.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mamdoh Anber tested by eng. Ali Abd Elsalam , and revised by EDEPC quality control engineer Abouda Elbana, accepted by general director eng. Yosry Abd Elmaqsood, and approved by eng. Awad Fathay head sector of Talkha Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Feeler gauge & dial indicator.

  • Rages.

 

Work to be Carried Out

Pump Disassembly

Pump in Service

  1. Check pump pressure and record it.

Pump out of Service

  1. Remove coupling cover.

  2. Check alignment before disassembly.

  3. Disconnect pump –motor coupling.

  4. Remove oilers.

  5. Disassembly pump bearings.

  6. Disassemble mechanical seal.

  7. Remove casing screws, nuts and dowel pins.

  8. Loosen top half by tighten two jacking bolts evenly.

  9. Remove upper casing using sling and hoist.

  10. Remove rotating element and place on padded supports

Inspection

  1. Inspect the following component of the pump:

  2. Check pump rotor run out.

  3. Check pump rotor for wear & cracks and damage.

  4. Check impeller for wear & cracks and damage.

  5. Check wear rings for wear & cracks and damage.

  6. Check bearing housing for wear & cracks and damage.

  7. Check ball bearing for damage.

  8. Check pump casing for wear and cracks.

Assembly

  1. Apply good cleaning for all pump parts.

  2. Reinstall pump rotor in the lower casing half.

  3. Put new gasket.

  4. Reinstall upper casing half.

  5. Rebolt upper and lower casing halves.

  6. Reinstall mechanical seal in both sides.

  7. Reinstall front and rear bearings.

  8. Reassemble motor – pump coupling.

  9. Do alignment between pump & motor shafts.

  10. Reassemble motor – pump coupling.

  11. Recheck alignment between pump & motor shafts.

  12. Reinstall coupling cover.

Starting the Pump

Before Starting the Pump, the following steps should be Checked:

  1. Open the suction and delivery valves of the pump.

  2. Connect electric terminal of the pump motor.

  3. Check the direction of rotation.

  4. Put the circuit breaker of the pump motor in service.

Attendance and Checking during Operation

  1. Check the bearing temperature.

  2. Check pump suction & discharge pressure.
