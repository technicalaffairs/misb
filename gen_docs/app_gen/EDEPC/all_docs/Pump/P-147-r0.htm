Introduction

This document outlines the details equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

This pump is used to transfer the coolant water from Nile River to condenser for condensing the steam from turbine to condensate water.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes.... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor .

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor .

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid

  • Feeler gauge & dial indicator

  • Rags

Work to be Carried Out

Pump In Service

  1. Check discharge and pump pressure then compare it with the setting  value and replace the worn – out components ( if it is needed ).

  2. Measure vibrations level for bearing and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do ( if it is needed )

Pump out Service

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value .

  2. Make test for bearings oil and add or change it ( if it is needed ) .

  3. Check flanges for water leakage  .

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens ( if it is needed ) .

  5. Check glands for water leakage and re-pack ( if it is needed ) .

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint ( if it is needed ) .
