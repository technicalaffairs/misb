Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I3, which must be performed every nine months or 6000 operating hours which comes first as indicated by M9 / H 6000 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Mohamed Helaly Abd Alkarem and tested by eng. Mahmoud Mohamed Zayed and eng Tarq Mohamed Rashad Ahmed at 18 Jan 2004, accepted by general director eng. Gaber Desoqy Mostafa, and approved by eng. Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety healthy, safety shoes

  • The motor have to be stopped and its breaker isolated before starting any maintenance procedure.

Tools and Equipment

  • Grease type: Mobilex EB3 (or iithium base equivalent Mobil - PLX 47) approx. 350 gm for regressing see note 2

  • Grease Gun with - capacity 5 kg for changing grease.

  • Wrenches with width size (30,24,20,16)

  • Mechanical tool kit

Note 1: for both motor and pump thrust bearing oil should be changed every 4000 running hour (6 months)

Note 2: for motor lower bearing grease should be changed every 2000 running hour (3 months)

 

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

Electrical Isolation

  1. Open main motor breaker, tag no. 1NB0206

  2. Turn off DC supply

  3. Open space heater No. 1NHA-12A-CCH-25

Mechanical Isolation

  1. Close suction valve No. 1AD-V-021

  2. Close discharge valve No. 1AD-V-027

  3. Close recirc valve No. 1AD-V-136

  4. Close discharge vent valve No. 1AD-V-025

  5. Close seal water valve No. 1AD-V-030

  6. Close pump cooling water valve No. 1EB-V-112 & 113

  7. Close motor cooling water valve No. 1EB-V-114 & 115

  8. Open drain valve No. 1AD-V-118

MOTOR    type ANV-WT001,  mfg. TECO

  1. Lower Bearing type - NU 238

  2. Check for grease quality and color, change if required

  3. Flush and clean the oil cooler if required. Flushing water pressure < 6 bar

  4. General cleaning and inspection for cooling water and oil connection lines for leakage (tighten if required)

PUMP     type WKTA300/6, mfg. KSB

 

Pump Thrust Bearing

  1. Check for oil color, quality and take a sample for analysis, change if required

  2. Check for oil level and top up if required to the middle of side glass

  3. Flush and clean the oil cooler if required. Flushing water pressure < 6 ber

  4. General cleaning and inspection for cooling water and oil connection lines for leakage (tighten if required)

Pump Stuffing Box and Shaft sleeve

  1. Check backing and change if required.

  2. Tight fit the gland (M 16 Bolts) if required or tight fit by hand only

Checking Tightened Parts  (Check tightening torque between)

  1. Motor and motor stool.

  2. Motor stool and intermediate lantern.

  3. Intermediate lantern, discharge cover, and discharge elbow.

  4. Discharge elbow and foundation ring.

  5. Discharge elbow and column pipe.

  6. Upper and lower halves of coupling.
