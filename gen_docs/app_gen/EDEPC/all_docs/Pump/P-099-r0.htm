Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be carried out every five years as indicated by Y5  in the title block

The pump function is to cool the outlet steam from LP turbine to convert the steam to condensate water, and also to cool the closed cooling water for accessories

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags

 

 

Work to be Carried Out 


Pump in Service

  1. Check the discharge quantity and pump pressure and record it

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump and motor and record them

Disassembly

  1. Remove the feed lines of lubrication water and oil cooling for pump

  2. Dismantle the bolts of the upper carden coupling and lift the motor

  3. Dismantling the carden coupling from pump side

  4. Drain the dearing housing from oil

  5. Dismantle the motor base

  6. Remove the coupling from the pump shaft and it's key

  7. Dismantle the thrust bearing (cover, deflector, rings, brace ring, ball bearing, springs, cooling coil)

  8. Dismantle the bolts between the discharge elbow and discharge line.

  9. Lift the pump and put it in the work place.

  10. Dismantle the suction element.

  11. Check the clearance between suction element and impeller.

  12. Remove the impeller after remove the impeller nose.

  13. Remove the bowl.

  14. Remove the stuffing box gland, lantern ring and gland cover.

  15. Dismantle the discharge elbow.

  16. Dismantle the lower journal bearing.

Inspection

  1. Check the stuffing box shaft sleeve for wearing and damage

  2. Check the bearing shaft sleeve (when radial clearance is higher than 0.7 mm it must be changed)

  3. Check ing the thrust - bearing box for wearing and damage

  4. Check the clearance between suction element and impeller

Assembly

  1. Clean the impeller and shaft

  2. Install the upper and lower sleeves

  3. Insert the lower journal bearing

  4. Install the impeller and it's nose

  5. Install the bowl and the discharge elbow

  6. Install the suction element

  7. Install the thrust box with a new gasket and oil sleeve

  8. Install the cooler coil

  9. Install the thrust bed

  10. Install the exterior ring of bearing

  11. Install upper thrust - bearing with its components

  12. Assembly the coupling of pump

  13. Install the rotor into pump

  14.  Assembly the cover of the thrust box

  15. Insert the packing rings and lantern ring and gland cover with out working tighten

  16. Put the pump into it's casing

  17. Install the elbow

  18. Assembly the carden coupling pump side and grease it

  19. Assembly the motor base frame

  20. Assembly the carden coupling motor side

  21. Fill the thrust box with oil

  22. Install the attached piping

  23. Attach the temperature gauge in the thrust box

  24. Attach the lines of cooling water for oil and motor

  25. Attach the lines of lubrication water.

Starting of pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump.

  2. Check the oil level.

  3. Check the cooling water valve (open) for motor and oil.

  4. Check the lubrication water pump.

Attendance and checking during operation

  1. Tighten the gland flange for normal leakage.

  2. Check the bearing temp pump.

  3. Check the temp and rate of cooling water for oil and motor.

  4. Check vibration.
