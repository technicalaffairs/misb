Introduction

This document outlines the equipment maintenance procedures I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The technical specification of the pump SOLOVIC pump
Q = 1000 M3 / H        H = 100 M        P = 400 kv        V = 6000 volt

The technical specification of the pump UGOSLAF  pump
Q = 1100 M3 / H        H = 107 M        P = 500 kv        V = 6000 volt

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintanance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes.... etc

  • Stop pump and switch off the power supply

  • Close the outlet and inlet valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

  • Set of wrench

  • Alignment equipment

  • Grease for lubricating

  • Cleaning material

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the alignment of the pump

  2. Operate the pump to check the noise

  3. Stop pump and switch off the power supply and close the outlet and inlet valves

  4. Dismantle the coppling

  5. Dismantle the shaft , impeller , protection ,cooling bushing and check for defects or cracks

  6. Check key and change if needed

  7. Check the bearings if needed

  8. Install the pump and make alignment with motor

  9. Install the coppling

  10. Operate the pump and check the load current
