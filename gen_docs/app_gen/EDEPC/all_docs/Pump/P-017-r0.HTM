Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Gamal Mostafa, tested at 30 May 2003, revised by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Abouda Elbana, accepted by eng  Ali Mostafa El  lithy, and approved by eng. Mostafa Alaam Talkha PS Head Sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, ....etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags.

 

 

 

 

Work to be carried out

Pump in Service

  1. Check discharge quantity and pump pressure then, compare it with the setting value and replace the worn- out components (if it is needed).

  2. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do (if it is needed).

Pump out of Service

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens it (if it is needed).

  3. Make test for bearings oil and add or change it (if it is needed).

  4. Check glands for water leakage and re-pack (if it is needed).

  5. Check flanges for water leakage.

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).
