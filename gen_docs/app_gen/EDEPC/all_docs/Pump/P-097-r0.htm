Introduction

This document outlines the minor maintenance procedures P1, must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

The function of this pump is to cool the bearing of main cooling water pump

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid. Manual puller

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags

 

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly

  1. Dismantle the coupling between pump and motor

  2. Check alignment.

  3. Dismantle the motor bolt's with base.

  4. Dismantle the pump casing from diffuser

  5. Dismantle the bolts from pump casing with base

  6. Take the pump out.

  7. Check clearance between impeller and diffuser.

  8. Dismantle the impeller nuts and remove the impeller

  9. Remove all parts of mechanical seal

  10. Remove the shaft with bearings from pump casing

  11. Remove the ball bearings and gland sleeve.

Inspection

  1. Inspect the ball bearing and shaft sleeve change it if it is needed

  2. Inspect the shaft runout must not exceed 0.03 mm

  3. Inspect the clearance between impeller and suction wear ring

  4. Inspect the face of mechanical seal parts

Assembly

  1. All parts must be carefully cleaned

  2. Reassemble bearings in the shaft and shaft sleeve

  3. Reassemble the shaft with bearings to the pump casing

  4. Reassemble the mech. Seal Parts

  5. Reassemble the impeller and tight the imp. Nut

  6. Reassemble the pump casing with diffuser

  7. Reassemble the auxiliary pipes (suction, delivery) and tight the bolts for pump casing with base 

  8. Check the alignment between pump and motor.

Before Starting the Pump

  1. Check isolating valves ( suction, delivery ) open

After Starting the Pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation  

  2. Check pressure.

  3. Check vibration.

  4. Check for abnormal noise

  5. Check the temperature of bearings

  6. Check mechanical seal leakage.
