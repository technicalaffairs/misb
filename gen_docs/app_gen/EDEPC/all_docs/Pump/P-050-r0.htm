Introduction

This document  outlines  the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block

This Pump transfers raw water from Nile to the water treatment plant .

The first draft is initiated and tested by eng. Mohammed Abd El Kader,  reviewed by maintenance manager eng. Mohammed Farag, revised by EDEPC inspection & quality control eng. Abouda Elbana, approved by eng. Awad Fathy: head sector of Talkha 2x210MW power station, and certified by eng. Aly Mostafa Al Lethy EDEPC inspection & quality control head sector.

Technical specification

Type of pump                             125 CVEV – 265 – 15 / 2LN

Design:                                        Vertical centrifugal pump

Pump capacity                             75.6 m3/h (21 L/s)

Specific energy                            35 m W.C. (345 J/kg)

Pump Speed                               1480 r.p.m.

Bearing Type                              7210/T - 2209

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks must be surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker for motor of this pump.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves and drain it.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rugs.

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Disassembly

 Pump In Operation

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure and record them.

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump set complete with the motor then, record them.

Pump Out Of Operation

  1. Measure the pump alignment and record it.

  2. Unscrew motor bolts and take it out.

  3. Measure the axial clearance of the pump and record it .

  4. Unscrew the shaft end nut and take the pump half-coupling out.

  5. Dismantle the attached pipes.

  6. Dismount the gland and take packing cords out.

  7. Dismount thrust bearing (7210/T) complete with the hanger.

  8. Dismantle discharge elbow and shaft guide pipes.

  9. Dismantle the shaft joints by unscrewing them from their mafe couplings.

  10. Dismantle suction lantern if used, then take suction slide radial bearing (2209) out.

  11. Begin dismantles the hydraulic part from suction.

  12. Dismantle impellers, sleeves and diffusers from the hydraulic part shaft after dismantling the pump clamping bolts.

Inspection 

  1. The validity of the anti-friction bearings ( thrust – radial ) .

  2. Check the radial clearances for pump by measuring the gap between wearing rings of diffusers and the distance sleeves on the shaft and records it.

  3. Check all parts for any worn-out.

  4. Check all parts for cracks.

  5. Inspect the run-out of shafts.

Assembly

  1. Install diffusers, sleeves, and impellers from the discharge side up to suction side and tight them with the clamping bolts.

  2. Insert shaft in the suction slide bearing with lantern and check radial and axial clearance of the pump and ensure that pump rotate easily, otherwise dismount and treat the causes.

  3. Install the shaft joints with their mafe couplings,

  4. Mount the shaft guide pipes.

  5. Install the hanger complete with discharge elbow  into shaft guide pipes.

  6. Mount the stuffing box with gland.

  7. Mount the thrust bearing and cover it.

  8. Install the pump half coupling.

  9. Lift the pump by crane and put it in its place.

  10. Fix the pump and tight all attached pipes.

  11. Install packing cords and tight gland bolts easily.

  12. Adjust the axial clearance of the pump by a value equal to one half of all axial clearance, by the nut.

  13. Mount the electric motor.

  14. Adjust the alignment between motor shaft and pump shaft and record them.

Starting the Pump 

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked :

  1. Check the oil level in the thrust-bearing pump bath

  2. Open the cooling water for the oil bath of the thrust bearing.

  3. Open the suction and delivery valves of the pump.

  4. Check the direction of rotation of the pump by pushing the electric button of the motor pump instantaneously.

Attendance and Checking During Operation

During operation of the pump, the following should be checked :

  1. The temperature of the anti-friction bearing (it should be not exceed 75°C).

  2. The temperature of cooling water.

  3. Any water leakage and oil leakage.

  4. The discharge quantity and pressures (suction and discharge) of the pump and record them.

  5. Check the vibration level and record it.
