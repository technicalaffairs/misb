Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed for fuel oil injection pump skid as indicated M6 in the above titled block

The first draft initiated by eng. Saad Samra, tested at 27 June 2003 by eng. Mohamed Abdel Azeem, revised by EDEPCo.quality control eng. Abouda Elbana, accepted by General Director eng. Mohamed Hamouda Al-Sarag and approved by eng. Moneer Mohamoud El-Wakeel Head Sector of Demiatta GT

Technical Specifications

Manufacturer

Sluzer-Weise.

Quantity

56.3 – 69.1 m3 / hr.

Liquid handled

Fuel oil.

Motor speed

2980 R.P.M.

Viscosity

11 – 15 mm2 / sec.

Motor power

250 kw.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically and electrically.  

Tools and Equipment

  • Compressed air hose.

  • Maintenance tool set.

  • Oil container, Refill – oil pump.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Drain all the old oil from the reservoir.

  2. Fill the reservoir with the same type of oil and put it back into standard position [2/3 of the reservoir minimum].

Note:  (If the oil level within the frame drops and the upper edge of the oblique cut get free then increase oil flow from the supply reservoir to reach the oil operating level and the oblique cut of the outlet tube is immersed again)
