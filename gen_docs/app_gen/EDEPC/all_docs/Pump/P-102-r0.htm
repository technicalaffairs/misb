Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed for washing pump  every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block

The function of the washing pump is to clean the fine screen.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out  

Pump in Service

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure then , compare it with the setting valve

  2. Check gland leakage           

  3. Grease bearing

  4. Check the temperature

Pump out Service

  1. Check Alignment between ( Motor – pump ) 

  2. Check bolts fixation, then retighten ( if it is needed )

  3. Check the packing of the gland 

  4. Check flanges for leaking.

  5. Check the pump casing against rust and repaint ( if it is needed ).
