Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng Medhat Foaad, tested at 03 June 2003, revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Abouda Elbana, accepted by general manager eng Ali Mostafa El_Lithy and Approved by Mostafa Alaam Talkha PS Head Sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should cany the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets shoes, gloves, … etc

    •

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor..

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags.

Work to be carried out

Disassembly

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure and record them.

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump set complete with the motor then, record them.

  3. Measure the pump alignment and record it.

  4. Unscrew the shaft end nut and take the pump half-coupling out.

  5. Dismantle the attached pipes.

  6. Dismount the gland and take packing cords out.

  7. Discharge the oil tank of the bearing and dismount the taper-roller bearing (29415) and the radial bearing (1213k).

  8. Dismantle the stand, which carries the electric motor.

  9. Take pump out of its place, after disconnecting Condensate tank.

  10. Dismantle clamping bolts and dismantle impellers and diffusers beginning from suction up to the last stage.

  11. Dismantle the lower shaft joint from the mafe coupling.

  12. Dismount the pipe guide and insert the other joint of the shaft.

Inspection

  1. The validity of journal bearing white metal castings.

  2. The validity of the anti-friction bearings.

  3. Check the radial clearances for pump by measuring the gap between wearing rings of diffusers and the distance sleeves on the shaft and record it.

  4. Check all parts for any worn-out.

  5. Check all parts for cracks.

  6. Inspect the run-out of shafts.

Assembly

  1. Clean the all contact surfaces.

  2. Mount the impellers and diffusers of the pump beginning from the delivery side up to suction side, then screw the clamping bolts.

  3. Fit the shaft with the pump into the bearing hunger and tight it to the pump casing.

  4. Fit the other joint of the shaft with the mafe coupling.

  5. Mount the guide pipe and the elbow complete with the hunger.

  6. Fit the gland sleeve and gland housing and its accessories.

  7. Install the stand of bearing housing and motor.

  8. Insert the tapered- roller bearing and cover it, then refill the oil tank of the bearing.

  9. Put the coupling half of the pump and secure it and adjust axial clearance.

  10. Lift the pump and place it in the Condensate tank and tighten its bolts.

  11. Adjust the axial clearance of the pump by lifting the entire rotor in the upward direction until it stops by means of the castle nut. At the same time, count the number of turns of the nut up to the moment when the nut is fully tightened.

  12. Tighten the attached pipes to the pump.

  13. Insert packing cord rings in the stuffing box and tighten it easily.

  14. Fit the motor on its stand.

  15. Adjust the alignment between motor shaft and pump shaft and record them.

Starting the Pump

Before starting the Pump, the following Steps should be checked

  1. Check the oil level in the thrust-bearing pump bath

  2. Open the cooling water for the oil bath of the thrust bearing.

  3. Open the suction and delivery valves of the pump.

  4. Check the direction of rotation of the pump by pushing the electric button of the motor pump instantaneously.

Attendance and Checking During operation

During operation of the Pump, the following should be checked

  1. The temperature of the anti-friction bearing (it should be not exceed 75°C).

  2. The temperature of cooling water.

  3. Any water leakage and oil leakage.

  4. The discharge quantity and pressures (suction and discharge) of the pump and record them.

  5. Check the vibration level and record it.
