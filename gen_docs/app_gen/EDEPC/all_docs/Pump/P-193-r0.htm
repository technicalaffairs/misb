Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

The main lube oil pump is used for pumping the oil needed for lubricating the all turbine parts, providing the bearings with oil and raising the rotor. Each unit has one AC main lube oil pump, two AC auxiliary pumps and one DC auxiliary pump.

Technical Specifications:    Speed = 2955 RPM        Total head = 29m        Flow = 207 m3 /H

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes ...etc.

  • Isolate the CB of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

  • Be sure that the pump is completely drained.

  • Be sure that the ST is out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Grease, rags

  • Filler.

  • Vibrometer, Dial indicator.

 

Work to be Carried Out

Before Disassembly

Pump in Operation

  1. Check the vibration level of the pump and record it.

  2. Check discharge, pressure and record them.

Disassembly

  1. Pump is out of operation.

  2. Check the alignment and record it.

  3. Unbolt the coupling between the pump shaft and the intermediate shaft.

  4. Remove the unit containing the intermediate shaft with the gear and begin disassembly of the pump.

  5. Unscrew  the hex screw ( 54 ), raise the washer ( 53 ) and raise the plate     ( 44 ).

  6. Unscrew  the Allen ( 48 ) to raise the bearing cover ( 47 ) and then remove the gland  ( 49 ).

  7. Remove the fixation nut ( 51 ).

  8. Remove the bearing ( 43 ).

  9. Unbolt the bolts ( 39 & 45 ).

  10. Raise the lubricating pin ( 38 ).

  11. Unbolt the nuts ( 31 ) and the studs ( 14 ).

  12. Raise the bearing support ( 36 ).

  13. Remove the bearing ( 37 ).

  14. Remove the ring ( 35 ).

  15. Remove the gland ( 33 ).

  16. Unbolt the nuts ( 8 ) and the casing studs ( 27 ) and then remove the support ( 13 ).

  17. Remove the wear ring ( 12 ) with the deflector ( 11 ).

  18. Unbolt the nuts ( 30 ) and the cover studs ( 28 ), then remove the cover support ( 29 ).

  19. Remove the cover gasket ( 17 ) and the mechanical seal ( 9 ).

  20. Remove the shaft chemise ( 10 ).

  21. Raise the pump cover ( 15 ).

  22. Unscrew the nut ( 21 ) and gasket ( 20 ).

  23. Raise the pump shaft with the impeller, remove the key ( 26 ) and unscrew the impeller nut ( 19 ) to remove the impeller.

  24. Remove the wear ring ( 23 ).

Inspection

  1. Clean all parts of the pump.

  2. Change the bearings of the pump.

  3. Check all parts for any worn-out , replace the damaged.

  4. Check all parts for cracks , treat the cracks by grinding or remachining.

  5. Inspect the run-out of the shafts.

  6. Change the wearing rings.

  7. Measure the radial clearances for the pump and adjust them.

  8. Grease the bearings of the pump.

Assembly

  1. Clean all contact surfaces.

  2. Assemble the impeller with the shaft by mounting the key ( 26 ) and screw the impeller nut ( 19 ) to fix the impeller with the shaft

  3. Put the wear ring ( 23 ).

  4. Screw the nut ( 21 ) and gasket ( 20 ).

  5. Assemble the pump cover ( 15 ).

  6. Assemble the shaft chemise ( 10 ).

  7. Put the cover gasket ( 17 ) and the mechanical seal ( 9 ).

  8. Put the cover support ( 29 ) and bolt the cover studs ( 28 ) and the nuts ( 30 ).

  9. Assemble the wear ring ( 12 ) with the deflector ( 11 ).

  10. Assemble the support ( 13 ) by bolting the casing studs ( 27 ) and the nuts ( 8 ).

  11. Assemble the gland ( 33 ).

  12. Assemble the ring ( 35 ).

  13. Assemble the bearing ( 37 ).

  14. Assemble the bearing support ( 36 ).

  15. Bolt the studs ( 14 ) and the nuts ( 31 ) to fix the bearing support.

  16. Bolt the bolts ( 39 & 45 ).

  17. Assemble the bearing ( 43 ).

  18. Assemble the fixation nut ( 51 ).

  19. Assemble the gland  ( 49 ), put the gasket ( 46 ), put the bearing cover ( 47 ) and screw  the Allen ( 48 ) for fixation.

  20. Assemble the plate ( 44 ), put the washer ( 53 ) and screw  the hex screw ( 54 ).

  21. Mount the coupling between the pump shaft and the intermediate shaft, adjust the alignment and then fix the coupling.

Starting the pump

Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked.

  1. Check the oil level in the oil tank.

  2. Open the delivery valve of the pump.

Attendance and checking during operation

  1. Check for any oil leaks.

  2. Check the discharge and pressure of the pump.

  3. Check the vibration level.
