Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures list P1, to be carried out every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block.   

This pump is a horizontal centrifugal single stage, single suction, the function of pump to circulate the cooling water in the cooling water circuit of the unit.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment.

  • Turbine must be shutdown.

  • Disconnect power supply to driver motor.

  • Closing all valves controlling flow (delivery and suction valves) of the pump.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs … etc.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller, Rags

  • Feeler gauge, dial indicator.

Work to be carried out

Pump in Operation

  1. Check the pressure and record it.

  2. Check the vibration level and record it.

Pump out of operation

  1. Measure the pump alignment and record it.

Pump Disassembly

  1. Remove coupling cover.

  2. Disconnect coupling.

  3. Dismantle the electric motor and take it out.

  4. Dismantle all attached pipes to the pump.

  5. Dismount the half coupling of the pump shaft.

  6. Unscrew the sleeve nut of the bearing and take it out

  7. Screw out the nuts fixing the pedestal to the pump casing

  8. Lift the pump to the work place and put it on its maintenance stand.

  9. Loosen the clamping bolts and remove them from the pump.

  10. Dismantle the impellers of the pump beginning from suction side up to discharge side

  11. Place suitable pad under the loosened shaft.

Bearing Removal

  1. Remove bearing housing screws.

  2. Using snap ring pliers, remove retiring ring from thrust end of shaft by notice.

  3. Remove bearings using bearing puller.

  4. Remove out board seal from coupling end bearing housing.

Impeller Removal

  1. Remove nuts gland, packing lantern rings and stuffing box bushings.

  2. Slide casing wear ring of impeller and shaft.

  3. Remove rotary portion of both seals remove shaft sleeves.

  4. Loosen setscrew on sleeves nut.

  5. Scribe a mark on the shaft to mark the location of the impeller hub.

  6. Drive or press impeller of shaft.

Inspection

  1. The validity of radial bearing from wear or corrosion.

  2. Check all parts for any worn-out.

  3. Check all parts for cracks.

  4. Inspect the run – out of the shaft (.001inch)

 Assembly

  1. Place the suction casing with the bolted bearing pedestal on an assembly stand

  2. Insert the shaft with mounted gland sleeves for the stuffing box and put it into the bearing pedestal through the stuffing box space of the suction casing and support the       shaft from the other side.

  3. Build in the radial bearing.

  4. Fit the impeller and ensure that the inner front wall of the impeller channel mates with  he Surface of the diffuser shim.

  5. Rotate the pump rotor and ascertain that it is easy rotating otherwise dismantle thep ump.

  6. Put the pump in its place and mount all attached pipes.

  7. Put the motor on its place.

  8. Adjust the alignment between motor shaft and pump shaft and record them.

Starting the pump

  1. Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked

  2. Open the suction and delivery valves of the pump.

  3. Check the direction of rotation of the pump by pushing the electric button of the motor     pump instantaneously.

Attendance and checking during operation

  1. During operation of the pump, the following should be checked

  2. Any water leakage and oil leakage.

  3. Temperature of bearing.

  4. The pressures of the pump and record it

  5. Check the vibration level and noises and record it.
