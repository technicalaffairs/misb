Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated Y1 in the above titled block.

This pump transfers the condensate from the hot well of condenser to the heaters then to feed tank.

GUINARD KSB

Condensate water.

Shaft

Vertical.

Type of bearing

Ball bearing (7316).

Discharge pressure

1475 RPM

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

    •

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the CB of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

  • Be sure that the pump is completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Filler.

  • Vibrometer, Dial indicator.

  • Rags, Grease.

Work to be Carried Out

Pump in operation

  1. Measure vibration level for bearing and compare it with the setting values, then analyze it and determine what to do [if it is needed].

  2. Check discharge, pressure and compare it with the setting values then replace the worn-out components [if it is needed].

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check the alignment and compare with the setting values.

  2. Check bolts fixation, then re-tight it [if it is needed].

  3. Grease the bearings of the pump. (type of grease is multi-purpose grease).

  4. Change the packing seal between the shaft and the wear ring.

  5. Check the leakage from the glands and flanges and treat it.

  6. Repaint the casing against rust.
