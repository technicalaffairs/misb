Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on a 6-month bases as indicated by M6 in the title block

The technical specification of the oil pump : capacity Q:8.3-151/s, total head H: 27-34.8m, speed n:2900 rpm, power: 7kw

The pump function is to transfer the oil from tank to another or to filtration station or from station to outside.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety equipment as safety helmet , shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc

  • Switch off Pump.

  • Use suitable warning boards

Tools and Equipment

  • Set wrenches and screwdrivers

  • Kerosene and cleaning material (rag. cloth)

  • Packing and grease

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. To carry out the outside inspection & to hear the pump in operation before stoppage for the revision for the purpose of normal operation of the bearings, vibrations, and leakage through the sealing joints

  2. To make the necessary cleaning

  3. To check up the fastening of the pump and electric motor to the base plate and also the state of poundation concrete
