Introduction

This Document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed for fuel leakage oil pump on a weekly bases as indicated by W1 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Saad Samra, tested at 27 June 2003 by eng Mohamed Abd El-Azeem, revised by EDEPCo. eng. Abouda El-Bana, accepted by  General Director eng. Mohamed Hamouda Al-Sarag, and approved by eng Moneer Mahmoud El-Wakeel Head Sector of Demiatta GT PS.

Technical Specifications
 

Type of pump

Screw pump type SPF 20

Casing material

Cast iron.

Manufacturer

Alweier

Seal

Mechanical Seal
  Liquid pump Fuel oil Efficiency 45-75 %
  Flow 37Lit/min. Motor speed 2900 R.P.M
  Pressure 4-6 kg/cm2    

Safety Precautions

  • Issue Work order including the teamwork names.

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically and electrically.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning articles.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Drain the leakage oil tank by opening of the filter drain cocks.

  2. Check the leakage oil tank and refill it up to its half of the content 

  3. Vent the pump.

  4. Check the ball valve at the delivery side of the pump and open.

  5. Check the sense of pump rotation by starting the motor temporality.

  6. Check the pump starts automatically, pumps the fuel oil out of the tank when the level is dropped to position pump off and stops automatically.
