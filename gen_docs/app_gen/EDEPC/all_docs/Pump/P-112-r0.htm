Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be carried out every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block. This pump is used to pumped feed water from feed water tank to high pressure heater.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Vibrometer, dial indicator.

  • Cleaning fluid.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Pump Shut Down

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare them with the setting value.

  2. Measure vibration level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do ( if it is needed ).

 

Pump Shut Down

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Check the bolts fixation, then retightens ( if it is needed ).

  3. Check the level and validity of bearing oil and add or change (if it is needed).

  4. Check the mechanical seal for leaking.

  5. Check flanges for leaking.

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).
