Introduction

This document outlines the equipment maintenance inspection I1, which must be performed every 1500 hours of operation. This procedure was applied in ABU QIR Power Plant since 1985 .

The first draft initiated by eng. Khamis Mohamed El Sayed, revised by eng. Ahmad El Sharkawy WDEPC inspection & quality control general director, accepted by eng. Ahmed Taha Hashim; Abu Kir PS Head Sector, approved by eng. Mohamed Abd El Sadek general director of Abu Kir PS,

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety helmet, safety shoes and following consignment be done:

Work to be Carried Out

 Booster Pump

  1. Clean the booster pump suction filter

  2. General cleaning and inspection for cooling water and oil

  3. Connection lines for leakage (tightened if required)

  4. Check packing and change if required

  5. Tighten fit the gland (M-4-Bolts) if required by hand only

  6. Check the pump fixation on the pump foundation

Main Pump

  1. Clean the suction filter of the pump

  2. Make sure of the correct packing for the pump and it is not solid if the packing is solid packing must be changed by anew of cress-set 11 mm of Fortina type.

  3. Make sure correct fixation of the pump on its base plate.

  4. Cleaning and greasing these valves
         * Main delivery valve and its BY-BASS
         * The minimum flow valve
         * Injection valve to manual reheating
         * Drainage valve

 Hydraulic Coupling

  1. General cleaning of the dust on coupling

  2. Check lubrication oil filter (clean it if required) and check operating oil for contamination

  3. Set actuator to min scoop tub position press 5g lithium. Soaped anti-friction bearing grease into each lubricating nipple of the control grease bright parts and check smooth running of control

  4. Check the oil level and increase if required with turbo oil T46. in case of too high oil level, check the operation oil for water content or change the oil (turbo oil 46)

  5. General cleaning for the gear box

  6. Check alignment of geared variable speed turbo coupling

  7. Check the gearbox fixation on the foundation

 

 

 

Coupling between Booster & Motor (aflm 10.25)

  1. Ensure that axial and redial alignment in limit

  2. Ensure that oil level in corrective position (olma 680)

  3. Ensure that the protective cover for coupling is fixation bolts

Coupling between Motor & Gear box AFLH-10-125

  1. Ensure that axial and redial alignment in limit

  2. Ensure that oil level in corrective position (olma 680)

  3. Ensure the protective cover for coupling is fixation bolts

Coupling between Gear box and Main Pump AFLH 11.70

  1. Ensure that axial and redial alignment in limit

  2. Ensure that oil level in corrective position (olma 680)

  3. Ensure that the protective cover for coupling is fixation bolts
