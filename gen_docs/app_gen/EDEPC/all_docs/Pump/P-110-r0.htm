Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Vibrometer, Feeler gauge, dial indicator.

  • Cleaning fluid.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Pump Shut Down

  1. Check discharge pressure and compare them with the setting value.

  2. Measure vibration level of the thrust bearing, compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do ( if it is needed ).

  3. Check the temperature of the thrust bearing and compare it with the normal value.

 

Pump Shut Down

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Check the bolts fixation, then re-tightens ( if it is needed ).

  3. Check the level and validity of oil and add or change ( if it is needed ).

  4. Check the gland for leaking and re-packing ( if it is needed ) .

  5. Check flanges for leaking.

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint ( if it is needed )
