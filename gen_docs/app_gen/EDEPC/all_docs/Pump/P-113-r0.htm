Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be carried out every four years as indicated by Y4 in the above title block. This pump is used to pumped feed water from feed water tank to high pressure heater.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Disconnect the suction & discharge flanges and all oil and water piping including the balancing line

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

  • Remove all instruments.

  • Remove the pump protection guard and insulating cover.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Vibrometer, wooden blocks.

  • Cleaning fluid, anti seizing paste.

  • Feeler gauge, dial indicator.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Pump Shut Down

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure and record them.

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump set complete with motor and record them.

Pump Shut Down

  1. Shut down the pump.

  2. Measure the alignment and record it.

  3. Dismantle the coupling.

  4. Lift the electric motor.

Disassembly

Journal Support –Front Side ( 351.01 – 352.02)

  1. Remove the centering pins (566.05), remove the screws (901.03) dismantle the half upper case (352.02).

  2. Lift the shaft slightly, rotate the half lower bearing (313.02) and the labyrinth seal ring (423.02) inside housing to remove them.

  3. Remove the upper part of bearing and labyrinth by disjunction as it is provided for stop pins that prevent their rotation.

  4. Dismantle the mechanical seal (433.01) but install again flange.

  5. Place the sleeve (18) and the ring in two half (19) in replacement of journal bearing (313.02).

  6. Install the upper support casing (352.02) and lock on the lower one (351.02).

  7. Lock the shaft by action on the screw 20.

Thrust Support Rear Side ( 351.01 – 352.02)

  1. Dismantle the lubrication screw pump (632.01) unscrew the screw (901.01), remove the support cover (169.01) and O ring (412.09).

  2. Disconnect the upper half of the support case (352.01).

  3. Dismantle the journal bearing (313.01) both thrust half bearings and labyrinth split ring (423.01) as mentioned before (on the front side).

  4. Straighten the lock washer (931.01), unscrew the bearing nut (923.01).

  5. With draw the locating sleeve (527.01).

  6. Remove the key (940.04) and with draw the locating ring (519.01).

  7. Sling the bearing housing by slightly pulling the lifting ropes.

  8. Remove the bearing housing.

Mechanical Seal ( 433.01 – 433.02 )

  1. Disconnect the flowing pipe from the seal flange 11.

  2. Loosen the screw 40 and insert the position plates 103 in the housing provided for sleeve 1, lock the screw 40.

  3. Loosen the stop dowel 57.1 and with draw the drive collar 58.

  4. Unscrew the connecting nuts of seal flange and in with draw the complete seal. 

Stuffing Box (Thrust Side) ( 451.01 )

  1. Remove the stuffing box positioning pins ( 566.01 ) and the delivery casing set screws.

  2. Disassembly the stuffing box (451.01) and O ring (412.07).

Balance Drum ( 603.01 )

  1. Unscrew the screw (914.06) of the drum tension bush (548.01), with draw the split ring (501.01) and remove it.

  2. Dismantle the space ring and remove O ring (412.1).

  3. With draw the balance drum liner.

  4. With draw the balance drum.

Delivering Casing (107.01)

  1. Unscrew four nuts (920.03), remove four tie bolts (905.01), replace them with four threaded bars (12) with washer (11.14) and nuts (13).

  2. Arrange all threaded bars, the four flange (10), insert washer (14) and lock every thing by nuts (13).

  3. Remove all tie bolts as mentioned before.

  4. Lift the delivery casing, remove it carefully from the last stage casing.

  5. Remove the diffuser (177.01) from the delivery casing and remove the O ring gasket (412.04).

Impellers – Inter Stage Casing, Diffuser  

  1. Install the plate (1) complete with guide (2) on the base plate, install the support (6) with screw (7) on the tooth of the inter stage casing to be removed (the last one).

  2. Lock the screw (9), adjust the height of inter mediate casing by acting on screw (7).

  3. Unloose the screw (13), remove the plate (10) and place it on the last inter mediate casing, detach it and let it slide on the support guide and remove O- ring (412.05).

  4. Remove the diffuser from inter stage casing.

  5. With draw impeller (last stage).

  6. Remove the key (940.02).

  7. With draw the spacer bush (543.01)

  8. Repeat the method mentioned above to remove all stages.                     

Shaft

With the aid of wooden blocks keep the shaft stable, unloose the screw (20) and remove the half coupling.

Journal Bearing Support Casing Disassembly (351.01 – 352.02)

  1. Remove the centering pins (566.05) and the screw (901.03).

  2. Dismantle the upper half housing.

  3. Remove the special tools (18-19)

  4. Sling the bearing housing by slightly pulling the lifting ropes.

  5. Dismantle the bearing housing positioning pens.

  6. Loosen the centering screws, unscrew the nuts (920.02)

  7. With draw the housing

  8. Disassemble the suction casing.

Inspection

  1. Check the working points surface of thrust disk for warn and cracks.

  2. Check the shaft run – out (should be not exceed 0.03 mm) .

  3. Check the diameter of the seal neck( 172 – 170 mm).

  4. Check the impeller hub external diameter ( 106 – 104 mm).

  5. Check the surface of spacing sleeves anti wear.

  6. Check the balance drum anti wear.

  7. Check the clearance between balance drum and balance drum liner (0.2 – 0.5 mm).

  8. Check the diameter of wear ring stationary and diffuser inter stage bush.

  9. Check all keys for warn and cracks..

  10. Check all bearings anti wear.

  11. Replace all gaskets and O- rings.

  12. Check the lubricating circuit. 

Reassembly

Suction Casing

House the O- ring gasket (412.01), assemble the suction casing and cooling jacket cover (165.02).

Pump Shaft

  1. Spread o thin layer of anti seizing paste on the shaft.

  2. Sling the shaft, lift it in the suction casing and place wooden blocks to avoid bending and deformation.

  3. Mount the mechanical seal flange

  4. Mount the bearing housing lower half without bearing nor labyrinth , screw the nuts (920.02), install the centering pins (566.03) and lock the screw (920.02).

  5. Install the special tools (18.19), install the half coupling and lock the shaft.

  6. Install the equipment for installation of the inter mediate casing.

Impeller, Inter Stage Casing, Diffusers

  1. Mount the key (940.02), fit the first stage impeller adjust the clearance between impeller and the seal ring (0.20 -  0.25 mm).

  2. Mount the diffuser (171.01) on the inter stage casing (113.01) fit the rotation preventing pin (562.01) and O – ring (412.03).

  3. With the aid of special tools. Sling the inter mediate casing and insert it on the shaft.

  4. Arrange four bolts with washer (11-14) and nuts (13) insert the plate (10) and lock the nut (13), remove the sliding equipment on the inter stage casing.

  5. Repeat above steps for assembly all stage.

 

Balance Drum

  1. Assemble the balance drum, mount the O-ring (412.10).

  2. Mount the spacer ring (504.01), mount the tension ring (548.01) and fix it by the screws (914.02 , 914.06).

  3. Mount the O – ring gasket (412.11) into its seat.

  4. Mount the balance drum liner

Delivery Casing

  1. Mount the tension ring (515.01), tie the screw (914.02).

  2. House the O – ring (412.04)in its seat

  3. Assemble the diffuser (177.01) in the delivery casing.

  4. Mount the delivery casing with the diffuser and the O – ring (412.05).

Tie Bolts

  1. Use four tie bolts with centering bush (546.01) and nut (920.03).

  2. Fit the tie bolts in the suction casing until the longest threaded part goes out from the delivery side.

  3. Mount the centering pin bush and nuts (920.03)

  4. Tighten the tie rod one nut, then on the opposite one.

  5. Unloose the four tie bolts, reset the pump perfectly on the base plate, lock the suction casing on the base plate.

  6. Place the delivery casing on the base plate without locking.

  7. Tighten threads (with torque set 250 kgm.). 

Stuffing Box

  1. House the O – ring (412.07 . 412.08).

  2. Assemble the cooling jacket cover (165.01).

  3. Mount the stuffing box complete with O – ring (412.06) fix it with the screw without locking.

  4. Mount the taper pins (566.01) to complete screw tightening.

Mechanical Seal (Both Sides)

  1. Arrange the rotating parts on the sleeve (1).

  2. Tight the stop dowel (57) gradually.

  3. Insert the flange (11) in the rotating part.

  4. House the plates (103), push the flange (11) against the stuffing box and lock it.

  5. Lock the flange (11), tight the nuts gradually and uniformly.   

Bearing Housing

  1. Mount the bearing housing lower half, screw nuts of the connecting studs without locking (coupling side).

  2. Mount the locating ring (519.01) and thrust bearing plate (384.01), mount the bearing housing lower half (thrust side), without locking the connecting studs.

  3. Move the bearing house down words by acting the centering screw (907.01), mount the lower half guide bearing (313.02).

  4. Lift the bearing house up to contact point of the bearing with the shaft, lean the shaft on the lower half and adjust the clearance (0.05 -0.08 mm).

  5. Lock the connecting nuts of the support diagonally and gradually.

  6. Assemble the half rings of labyrinth seal (423.01).

  7. Mount the four half thrust rings (413.02).

  8. Introduce the locating ring sleeve (527.01), lock washer (931.01) and screw down the bearing nut (923.01).

  9. Assemble the support complete with the cover.

  10. Reinstall the lubrication screw pump.

  11. Mount the insulation cover.

  12. Alignment and positioning.

  13. Pump locking.

Pump Startup

  1. Before pump start – up the following should be checked:

  2. Oil level.

  3. Cooling circuit open.

  4. Open suction and delivery valve.

  5. Check the direction of rotation of pump.

Attendance and Checking During Operation

  1. Temperature of bearing.

  2. Temperature of cooling water.

  3. Any water and oil leakage.

  4. Discharge quantity, pressure (suction, delivery) sides and record them.

  5. Check the vibration level and record it.

  6. Check the balance drum pressure.
