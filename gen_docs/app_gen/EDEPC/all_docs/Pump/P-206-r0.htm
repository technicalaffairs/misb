Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list I1, which must be performed every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Abdel Hamid Abu Zid, tested by eng. El-Sayed Mahmoud El-Kholy, accepted by eng. Sawsan El lithe general manager, revised by eng. Zakaria El-Shahat EDEPC MPIS coordinator and approved by eng. Mohamed Hussein Gomaa quality insurance head sector.

 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, eye glass, nose mouth dust cover .... etc.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Ensure that the inlet and outlet valve are closed if necessary.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Rags, cleaning fluid

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the oil on oil lubricated units

  2. Check grease lubricated bearings for specifications

  3. Check the packing for leakage and replace if necessary

  4. Check vibration readings of bearing housings

  5. Check alignment of pump and motor

Remedy Procedures

  1. Isolate the electric power before replacing oil or grease, also for repacking

  2. Increase the level oil if oil level decreased and replace the oil if its specifications are not suitable

  3. If the grease gives a whitish color, the grease must be changed and wash the bearing with a suitable industrial solvent cleaner, and replace the grease with the proper type as recommended

  4. If the packing leaks water slightly retight the packing cover bolts gently, and if it still leaks isolate the pump mechanically and electrically, then vent and drain water from inside, and loose the cover bolts

  5. Change the recommended packing and retight the cover bolts, close the drainage valve

  6. Open the suction and discharge valves, then close the vent valve after air bubbles stopped (filled completely with water)

  7. Connect the electric power again and run the pump to check the packing looks (10 minutes)

  8. After ten minutes (Rotation) score vibration readings for bearing housings and compared with the pervious readings

  9. If the alignment checked is not ok - shim up units if necessary

  10. Inspect the piping system unbolt piping at suction and discharge flanges also for supports

  11. Remove rags, tools and other things from the place.
