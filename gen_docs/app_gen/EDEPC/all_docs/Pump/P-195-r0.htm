Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

This pump is used to pump the oil needed to lubricate the turbine parts, to provide the bearings with oil and raising the rotor

Each unit has one AC main lube oil pump, two AC auxiliaries pumps and one DC auxiliary pump.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the CB of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

  • Be sure that the pump is completely drained.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Filler.

  • Vibrometer.

  • Dial indicator.

  • Rags.

  • Grease.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Disassembly

The Pump in Operation

  1. Check the vibration level of the pump and record it.

  2. Check discharge, pressure and record them.

Disassembly

The Pump out of Operation

  1. Check the alignment and record it.

  2. Disconnect the motor.

  3. Unbolt the coupling between the motor and the pump.

  4. Remove the motor after unbolting the screws ( 50 ) and the nuts ( 49 ).

  5. Dismantle every pipes connecting to the pump.

  6. Dismantle the upper bearing ( 3 ) as follow :-

  7. Unbolt the screws ( 56 ) and the nuts ( 23 ).

  8. Raise the bearing cover ( 2 ).      

  9. Dismantle the support ( 27 ) by unbolting the bolts ( 42 ) and the nuts (43).

  10. Raise the base plate ( 41 ).

  11. Raise the spacer tube ( 72 ) containing the pump shaft with the impeller and the bearings.

  12. Dismantle the intermediate bearing ( 61 )as follow:-

  13. Dismantle the connection for bearing cooling ( 73 ).

  14. Unbolt the nuts ( 67 ) and the screws ( 69 ).

  15. Raise the washers ( 66 ).

  16. Raise the bearing support, then raise the bearing.

  17. Dismantle the spacer tube by unbolting the nuts ( 36 ) and the bolts ( 35 ).

  18. Dismantle the lower bearing ( 16 ) as follow:-

  19. Unbolt the cover nuts ( 33 ) and the casing bolts ( 32 ), then raise the holding cover ( 15 ).

  20. Raise the upper wear rings ( 8 ).

  21. Free the impeller from the pump shaft by unbolting the impeller closing nut ( 11 ) and raise the lower wear ring ( 13 ).

  22. Extract the lower bearing ( 16 ).

  23. Dismantle the oil filter from the pump casing.

  24. Now  the pump is completely disassembled.

Inspection

  1. Clean all parts of the pump.

  2. Change the bearings of the pump.

  3. Check all parts for any worn-out , replace the damaged.

  4. Check all parts for cracks , treat the cracks by grinding or remachining.

  5. Inspect the run-out of the shafts.

  6. Change the wearing rings.

  7. Measure the radial clearances for the pump and adjust them.

  8. Grease the bearings of the pump.

Assembly

  1. Clean all contact surfaces.

  2. Mount the oil filter in the pump casing.

  3. Assemble the lower bearing on the shaft.

  4. Assemble the impeller with the shaft by bolting the impeller nut ( 11 ), rest them on the pump casing after putting the lower wear ring ( 13 ).

  5. Assemble the bearing holder cover ( 15 ) and bolt it with the bolts ( 32 ) and the nuts ( 33 ).

  6. Assemble the spacer tube ( 72 ) with the casing and bearing cover by bolting the screws ( 35 ) and the nuts ( 36 ).

  7. Assemble the intermediate bearing ( 61 ) on the shaft as follow :-

  8. Put the bearing on the shaft and put the bearing support.

  9. Assemble the lower and upper parts of the bearing.

  10. Put the screws ( 69 ).

  11. Put the washers ( 66 ).

  12. Tight the nuts ( 67 ).

  13. Mantle the connection for bearing cooling ( 73 ).

  14. Assemble the base plate ( 41 ).

  15. Assemble the support ( 27 ) by bolting the bolts ( 42 ) and the nuts ( 43 ).

  16. Assemble the upper bearing ( 3 ) as follow:-

  17. Mount the bearing.

  18. Put the bearing cover ( 2 ).

  19. Bolt the screws ( 56 ) and the nuts ( 23 ).

  20. Mount the coupling between the pump and motor shafts.

  21. Fix the motor with support by bolting the bolts ( 50 ) and the nuts ( 49 ).

  22. Connect all pipes of the pump.

  23. Now the pump is ready for operation.

Starting the Pump

Before starting the Pump

  1. Check the oil level in the oil tank.

  2. Open the delivery valve of the pump.

  3. Check the direction of rotation of the pump by pushing the electric button of the motor instantaneously.

Attendance and checking during Pump Operation

  1. Any oil leaks.

  2. The discharge and pressure of the pump

  3. The vibration level.
