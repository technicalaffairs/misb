Introduction

Function of water air pump is to extract the air which has leaked into the condenser by various parts and to maintain the required vacuum inside .

This document outlines the equipment Inspection list I1, which must be performed for water jet air pump every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags.

Work to be carried out

Pump In Operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings
  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure

 Pump Out Of Operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Check bolts fixation, then re-tight it (if it is needed).

  3. Check the validity of oil and add or change it (if it is needed).

  4. Check glands for leaking and re-packing (if it is needed).

  5. Check flanges for leaking and re-tight ( if it is needed ) .

  6.  Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).

Starting of Pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump before starting the pump for the first time.
  2. Check the quantity of oil in the thrust bearing sump .
  3. Open the inlet for cooling the oil bath of the thrust bearing .
  4. Check whether the inlet for flooding the stuffing box is open .
  5. Check whether the inlet for hydraulic sealing of the suspension is open .
  6. Check whether the delivery valve is closed .
  7. Open the delivery valve gradually after the set has been  started to operate at its full speed

Attendance and checking during operation

During operation, the following should be checked

  1. The temperature of the anti-friction bearing in the bearing top which should not exceed 75°C .
  2. The oil level in bearing top according to the mark on the oil gauge .
  3. The temperature of cooling water.
