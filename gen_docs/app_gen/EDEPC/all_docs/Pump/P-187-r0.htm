Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks to surround the work area is required.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used: safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker or the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pumps must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set. 

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, dial indicator.

  • Rags.

Work to be Carried Out 

Pump in Operation

  1. Checks discharge quantity and pressure and record them.

  2. Checks the vibration level of the pump set complete with the motor then, record them.

Pump Out of Operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value and record it.

  2. Make test for bearings oil and added or change it (if it is needed).

  3. Check flanges for water leakage.

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tighten (if it is needed).

  5. Check glands for water leakage and re-paint (if it is needed).

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).
