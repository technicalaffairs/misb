Introduction

This document outlines the equipment Inspection list I1, which must be performed for water jet air pump every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block .

The first draft initiated by eng. Medhat Fouad, tested at 27 May 2003, revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Abouda ElBana, accepted by General Manager eng. Ali Mostafa El-Lithy, and approved by eng. Mostafa Aalam Talkha PS Head Sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags.

Work to be carried out

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value,

  2. Measure vibration level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do (if it is needed).

  3. Check discharge pressure and compare it with the setting value then replace the worn-out components (if it is needed).

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tight it (if it is needed).

  5. Make test for bearings oil and add or change it (if it is needed).

  6. Check glands for leaking and re-packing (if it is needed).

  7. Check flanges for leaking.

  8. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).
