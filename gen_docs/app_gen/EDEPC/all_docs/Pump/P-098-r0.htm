Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed for main cooling water pump every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

The pump function is to cool the outlet Steam from LP turbine to convert the steam to condensate water, and also to  cool  the closed cooling water for accessories

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags

 

 

Work to be Carried Out 

Pump in Service  

  1. Check discharge quantity and pump pressure then compare it with the setting value

  2. Measure vibration level for bearing and compare it with the setting value, then  analyze it and determine what to do (if it is needed)

  3. Check the temperature of the cooling water

  4. Check the gland leakage, check the oil level

Pump out of Service

  1. Clean the oil cooler

  2. Grease the coupling

  3. Check the alignment and compare it with the setting value

  4. Check the leakage of flanges

  5. Check the packing of the gland and repacked if required

  6. Check bolts fixation, then retighten ( if it is needed ) . 

  7. Make test for bearings oil and add or change it ( if it is needed ) .

  8. Check the pump casing against rust and repaint ( if it is needed )
