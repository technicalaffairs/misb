Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, to be carried out every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block.

This pump is a vertical diagonal pump with swiveling of the impeller blade, its function to condensate the steam of turbine in condenser.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs … etc.

  • Cleaning fluid, manual puller, Rags

  • Feeler gauge, dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, straight edge.

Work to be carried out

Disassembly

Pump In Operation

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure and record them.

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump set complete with the motor then, record them.

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check radial and axial clearances of the pump impeller.

  2. Check alignment between pump shaft and motor shaft.

  3. Unscrew the bolts of the motor base plate and take it out.

  4. Dismantle the pump half coupling complete with the control shaft gear box after releasing the drive nut.

  5. Dismantle the electric connection bushing of the control shaft gearbox drive.

  6. Dismantle the discharge elbow from the base plate fixation and the discharge flange

  7. Lift the pump complete and place it on its erection pads in the workshop.

  8. Dismantle the cover of the bearings and discharge oil from the oil tank.

  9. Dismantle the cooling coil from its attached pipes.

  10. Dismount the tapered roller bearing and the angular contact ball bearing

  11. Dismantle the oil tank compete with the oil deflector.

  12. Dismantle the gland and remove the old packing cords.

  13. Dismantle the bolts of the bell mouth flange from the discharge elbow flange and lift the elbow up carefully avoiding damaging the shaft.

  14. Dismantle the impeller from the main shaft flangeand lift it up avoid to damage the impeller blades control shaft.

  15. Dismantle the cover of the impeller hub and unscrew the retaining nut of the of the control shaft with the swiveling disc of the impeller blades hinges.

  16. Check run-out of the main shaft and the control shaft, then repair if possible otherwise replace by new one.

  17. Check all parts from any worn-out and repair if possible otherwise replace by new one.

Inspection

  1. The validity of the anti-friction ball bearings.

  2. Check all parts for any worn-out and replace them by new ones.

  3. Inspect the run-out of shafts (allowance 0.03 mm).

Assembly

  1. Clean the casing of the pump from the mud and use steel brush to remove any rust and repaint.

  2. Clean all contact surfaces after removing the old gaskets.

  3. Insert the control shaft in the impeller and tight it with the retaining nut, then raise it up and down to ensure that the blades hinges working properly.

  4. Cover the impeller hub (ensure that the control shaft is centered in the journal bearing in the impeller cover).

  5. Lift the main shaft of the pump up and tight it with the impeller avoid to damage the control shaft.

  6. Lift all assembled parts complete (impeller-main shaft-control shaft) and insert them in the bell mouth.

  7. Install the discharge elbow to the bell mouth flange.

  8. Mount the oil tank in its stand at the elbow.

  9. Mount the oil deflector in the oil tank.

  10. Mount the angular contact bearing and the taper roller bearing , in the same moment  adjust the clearance of the pump impeller ( 6 mm ).

  11. Install the cooling coil in the oil tank and attach it to the water inlet and outlet pipes after cleaning all pipes by air blast, then re-fill the oil tank with clean oil.

  12. Cover the oil tank.

  13. Lift the pump and put it in its bedplate and tight it to the foundation and the discharge flange.

  14. Rotate the pump by hand and ensure that it rotates easily, otherwise interfere to check the reasons.

  15. Mount the gearbox drive after checking gears teeth and the roller bearings of its shafts and fill it with clean oil.

  16. Install the electric bushing connection.

  17. Put the motor on its bedplate and adjust the alignment, then tight its bolts.

  18. Tight the bolts of the coupling and corer them with guard.

  19. Repack the stuffing box and tight the gland bolts by hand.

Starting of Pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump before starting the pump for the first time.

  2. Check the quantity of oil in the thrust-bearing sump.

  3. Open the inlet for cooling the oil bath of the thrust bearing.

  4. Check whether the inlet for flooding the stuffing box is open.

  5. Check whether the inlet for hydraulic sealing of the suspension is open.

  6. Check whether the delivery sluice valve is closed.

  7. Open the delivery valve gradually after the set has been started to operate at its full speed.

Attendance and checking During Operation

  1. The temperature of the anti-friction bearing in the bearing top which should not exceed 75°C.

  2. The oil level in bearing top according to the mark on the oil gauge.

  3. The temperature of cooling water at discharge.
