Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in above title block.

This pump is used to transfer condensate water from condenser hot well to dearators to feed pump of HRSG.

Pump Technical Specification:

Manufacture Worthington
Model No. 10 – 12KK – H
Type Multi Stage - Vertical type
Rated Capacity 180 m3/hr
Pump discharge pressure 12 kg/cm2
Fluid pumped Condensate water
Design operating fluid temp 36.1 °C
Speed  1500 rpm
Bearing Type Upper 7226 thrust & Lower 6215 ball

Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge.

  • Rags.

Work to be carried out

Pump in Operation

  1. Checks discharge quantity and pressure and record them.

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump set complete with the motor then, record them.

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value and record it.

  2. Make test for bearings oil and added or change it ( if it is needed ).

  3. Check flanges for water leakage.

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-righting ( if it is needed ).

  5. Check glands for water leakage and re-pack ( if it is needed ).

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint ( if it is needed ).
