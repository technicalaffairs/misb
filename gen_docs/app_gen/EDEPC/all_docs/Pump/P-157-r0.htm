Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block.

This pump is used for cooling the hydrogen of generator.

Technical specification

Type of pump: 150-NHD-400-38-Meta 36
Design:  Horizontal centrifugal pump
Pump capacity: 268 t/h
Height 33 m
Pump Speed  1475 r.p.m.
Bearing Type: 6321

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller, Rags

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge.

Work to be carried out

Pump in Operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do.

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare them with the setting value then replace the worn-out components.

Pump out of Operation

  1. Remove the protective cover of the coupling.

  2. Check alignment between motor and pump.

  3. Shift electric motor away from the coupling.

  4. Disconnect auxiliary piping for cooling and for packing water.

  5. Screw out screw fixing bracket base to base plate.

  6. Attach carrying rope to the upper arm of the lantern.

  7. Screw out gradually all screws connecting lantern and spiral, then Move spiral with the help of forcing –off screws.

  8. Take out carefully the whole rotor from spiral box and shift it towards the motor.

  9. Screw out plug from the bottom part of the bracket and let out oil.

  10. Screw out runner nut, then remove mechanical galand respectively with runner nut washer (by using puller remove runner and take out fit-in spring from the shaft).

  11. Force out the cover of the spiral from the lantern with the help of two screw drivers and remove it carefully.

  12. Separate lantern from bracket by unscrewing nuts.

  13. Remove protective casing from the shaft.

  14. Pull down the coupling half from the shaft with a puller and remove coupling    spring from the shaft.

  15. Remove the covers of the bearing brackets after unscrewing the locking nut.

  16. Knock out the shaft to the side of the free end (towards the coupling) with the help of a wooden block.

  17. Heat up the bearing of the free end side and remove it from the shaft.

  18. Shift the shaft with the second bearing back to the opposite side towards the runner, then heat up the bearing and remove it from the shaft.

Inspection

  1. The validity of the anti-friction ball bearings.

  2. Check all parts for any worn-out and replace them by new ones.

  3. Inspect the run-out of shafts (allowance 0.03 mm).

Assembly

  1. Heat up the new bearings in oil bath and insert them on the clean shaft.

  2. Install the covers of the bearings and seal them against the bracket.

  3. Insert new GUFERO rings with covers through oiled shaft.

  4. Pay special attention to the cleanliness of the sealing surfaces during  inserting packing.

  5. Smear all threads with grease or with MOLYKA, if it is working in aggressive environment and in pumps located on open – air sites.

  6. Mounting the mechanical gland on the shaft of the pump.

  7. Mounting the impeller on the shaft end of the pump.

  8. Tighten firmly runner nut.

  9. Mounting the coupling half on the shaft

  10. Mounting carefully whole rotor in the spiral box and tighten it.

  11. Connecting auxiliary piping for cooling

  12. After completing the assembly alignment the flexible coupling of the pump and the motor (allowance 0.05 mm).

  13. Put the protective cover of the coupling

Starting of Pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump before starting the pump for the first time.

  2. Check the quantity of oil in the thrust-bearing sump.

  3. Open the inlet for cooling the oil bath of the thrust bearing.

  4. Check whether the inlet for flooding the stuffing box is open.

  5. Check whether the inlet for hydraulic sealing of the suspension is open.

  6. Check whether the delivery sluice valve is closed.

  7. Open the delivery valve gradually after the set has been started to operate at its full speed.

Attendance and Checking During Operation

  1. Check the temperature of the anti-friction bearing in the bearing top, should not exceed 75°C.

  2. Check the oil level in bearing top, should be according to the mark on the oil gauge.

  3. Check the temperature of cooling water at discharge.
