Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block.

This pump is used for drawing the leakage air from the circuit of cooling water.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge.

  • Rags.

Work to be carried out

Pump in Operation

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure and record them.

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump set complete with the motor then, record them.

Disassembly

Pump Out Of Operation

  1. Dismantle all attached pipes.

  2. Unscrew the bolts of the electric motor feet and take motor out .

  3. Dismantle the pump half coupling.

  4. Remove the air separator.

  5. Unscrew the bolts of the two sides of bearing carriers from the pump and take them out complete with bearings.

  6. Unscrew the nut tightening the stuffing box flange and withdraw flanges together with bearing.

  7. Remove the cap body together with valve distributing plate from the two sides of vacuum pump after unscrewing its fastening screws.

  8. Take off the complete vacuum pump rotor.

  9. Remove the plotter valve by unscrewing the screws in the inner side of valve distributing plate.

  10. Check all components of the pump and replace them by new ones if needed.

Inspection

  1. The validity of the anti-friction ball bearings

  2. Check all parts for any worn-out and replace them by new ones.

  3. Inspect the run-out of shafts (allowance 0.03 mm).

Assembly

  1. Assemble the valve distributing plates (plotter valves after cleaning their openings ) with the pump cover.

  2. Install one of the pump covers then insert the shaft into it .

  3. Mount the circulating wheel and sealing bushes to the shaft.

  4. Install the second cover of the pump.

  5. Mount bearings with their carriers and tight them to the pump (after greasing the bearings).

  6. Install the pump half coupling.

  7. Screw the electric motor bolts after adjusting alignment.

  8. Repack the stuffing boxes and tight them easily by hand.

  9. Install the air separator.

  10. Install all attached pipes to the pump.

Starting Of Pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump before starting the pump for the first time.

  2. Check the quantity of grease in the bearing sump.

  3. Check whether the inlet for flooding the stuffing box is open.

  4. Check whether the inlet for hydraulic sealing of the suspension is open.

  5. Check whether the delivery valve is closed.

  6. Open the delivery valve gradually after the set has been started to operate at its full speed.

Attendance and checking during operation

During operation, the following should be checked:

  1. The temperature of the anti-friction bearing in the bearing top which should not exceed 75°C.

  2. The oil level in bearing top according to the mark on the oil gauge.

  3. The temperature of cooling water at discharge.
