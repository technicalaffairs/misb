Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every four years as indicated by Y4 in the above title block.

This pump is used to transfer oil from main oil tank to journal bearing of turbine and generator

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect the electric terminals of the pump motor.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, ropes

  • Vibrometer, Feeler gauge, dial indicator

Work to be carried out

  1. Before pump shut down

  2. Check discharge pressure and compare them with the setting value.

  3. Check vibration level and record it.

  4. Shutdown the pump.

  5. Measure alignment and record it.

  6. Drain the oil from discharge pipe.

Disassembly (Refer to Figure 1)

  1. Pull out the knock pins and take off the motor

  2. Draw the coupling of pump side

  3. Remove the discharge pipe ( discharge line to base plate )

  4. Take off V- ring (27), base cover (29) and slinger

  5. Be careful pull out the pump from take and make the bottom up

  6. Dismantle the discharge pipe and the pressure gauge pipe

  7. Dismantle the strainer (12) and suction cover (11) from casing (6)

  8. Loosen the impeller nut (2) after straiten the nail of washer (42)

  9. Dismantle the impeller (1) from the shaft and remove the key (4)

  10. Remove the neck bush (9)

  11. Remove the support pipe and bearing housing with shaft from base plate.

  12. Dismantle the bearing cover (17)

  13. Remove the shaft with the ball bearing (14) from the bearing housing

  14. Loosen the bearing nut (16) after straiten the nail of washer (15)

  15. Draw the ball bearing from the shaft

Inspection

  1. Check the clearance between neck bush and shaft ( 0.08 – 0.15 mm )

  2. Check the clearance between impeller and wearing ( 0.32 – 0.4 mm )

  3. Check the impeller anti worn, check the dust and foreign matter in oil

  4. Check the ball bearing renew it ( if it is need )

Assembly (Refer to Figure 1)

  1. Mount the ball bearing, screw the bearing nut

  2. Insert the shaft with ball bearing in the bearing housing

  3. Assemble the bearing cover (17)

  4. Assemble the support pipe and bearing housing with the shaft to base plate

  5. Mount the neck bush (9)

  6. Mount the key (4), assemble the impeller with the shaft, screw it with the impeller nut (2)

  7. Assemble the strainer (12) and suction cover (11) with casing (6)

  8. Connect the discharge pipe and pressure gauge pipe

  9. Put the pump in the take

  10. Mount V- ring and assemble the base cover

  11. Connect the discharge pipe

  12. Assemble the coupling of the pump side

  13. Put the motor on it's place and make alignment, re tight the base plate

Pump Start up

  1. Before pump start up the following should be checked :

  2. Direction of rotation of the motor

  3. Be sure that the pump turn lightly and smoothly

  4. Check the liquid level if the pump casing is immersed perfectly in liquid

Attendance and Checking During Operation

  1. Discharge pressure and quantity and record it.

  2. Vibration level and record it
