Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be carried out every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block. 

The  pump function is to cool the outlet steam from L.P turbine to convert the steam to condensate water, and also for cooling the closed cooling water for accessories .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid. Manual puller

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometar, Straight edge, Rags

 

 

Work to be Carried Out 

Pump in service

  1. Check the discharge pressure and record it.

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump and motor and record them

Disassemble

  1. Remove the feed line of oil and water.

  2. Dismantle the coupling guard

  3. Check the alignment .

  4. Dismantle the H sleeve bolts of arpex coupling ( connecting rode) at the motor side .

  5. Dismantle the fastening bolts of the motor base and remove the motor with supporting frame

  6. Remove the Arpex coupling

  7. Raise the pump and take it to the work place carefully.

  8. Check the clearance of pump.

  9. Dismantle the adjusting nut.

  10. Pull the complete drive bearing off the pump shaft:

    1. Dismantle bearing covers

    2. Dismantle the non rotation.

    3. Dismantle the thrust bearing.

    4. Check the thrust bearing beds.

    5. Dismantle the stuffing box housing

  11. Remove the gland locking bolts and remove the bcking ring

  12. Dismantling the suspension pipeline , guide casing and split ring

  13. Dismantle the nut and hub tlange of the proppler van and remove it.

  14. Dismantle the sleeve of the stuffing

  15. Dismantle the sleeve of the lower bearing

Inspection

  1. Check the run out of rotor.

  2. Check the diameter of two sleeves

  3. Check all parts for cracks

  4. Inspect the validity of journal bearings and thrust bearing

  5. Check the coupling parts

Assembly

  1. Clean impeller and shaft surfaces

  2. Install the upper and lower sleeves

  3. Mount the Impeller and nut and hub flange

  4. Joint the suspension pipeline , guide casing and split ring

  5. Mount the stuffing box bearing

  6. Mount the stand still seal and its flange

  7. Mount the bearing housing

  8. Install bearing fixed part then put the bearing beds

  9. Install the lower sleeve bearing.

  10. Insert the packing rings and gland locking ring without working tighten.

  11. Insert the adjustment nut and adjust the van clearance with splitring to not less than ( 1 to 1.2 mm )

  12. Install the coupling of pump.

  13. Install the pump Rotor into it's casing and tight the nuts.

  14. Attach the connection rod of coupling

  15. Put the motor on its base and retarn the adjaust bolts on the original place and  tight the motor base bolts

  16. Assemble the coupling parts and adjust the alignment.

  17. Attach the coupling guard

Starting the Pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump

  2. Check vibration levels.

  3. Check bearing temperature

  4. Tight the gland flange 

  5. Check the oil level.
