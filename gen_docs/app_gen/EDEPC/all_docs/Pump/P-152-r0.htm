Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge

  • Rags.

  • Shims under pump feet.

  • Heating of connection bolts of stator.

  • Assembly flange.

  • Erection bolts. 

  • Equipment for heating up impeller hubs

  • Gauges (pressure, dial indicator with magnetic holder, feeler gauges)

  • Special spanners for rotor and stator nuts

  • Screw Jacks, Screw Jacks for rotor.

  • Standard spanners and other tools

Work to be Carried Out

Pump in Operation

  1. Measure vibration level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it to determine what to do

  2. Check discharge quantity, pressure, and compare them with the setting value then replace the worn out components

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Check the bolts fixation, then retightens ( if it is needed ).

  3. Check the validity of oil and purification by oil separator.

  4. Check the glands (mechanical seal) for leaking and replace if it is needed).

  5. Check flanges for leaking and retighten.

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).

  7. Check the cooling water (Pressure-return water)

  8. Check the thrust and radial bearing.

  9. Check the pressure of balance disk 1.5MP

  10.  Check the instruments (vibration, pressure gauge, temperature, ...etc
