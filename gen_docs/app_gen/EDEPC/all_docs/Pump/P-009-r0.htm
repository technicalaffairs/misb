Introduction

This documents outlines the equipment inspection procedure I1, which must be performed every six month as indicated by M6 in the title block.

This pump is used to push the cooling water through heat exchangers to cool the auxiliaries equipment.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should cany the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets shoes, gloves, … etc

  • Isolated the circuit breaker of the pump motor 

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the Pump Motor.

  • Isolate the Pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

  • Be Sure that the pump is completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Filler, Feeler gauge,

  • Rags, Grease

  • Vibrometer, Dial indicator

Work to be Carried Out

Pump in Operation

  1. Measure Vibration level for bearing and compare it with setting values then analyze it and determine what to do

  2. Check the discharge, pressure of pump and compare it with the Setting values the replace the worm components

  3. Check the temperature of bearing house (60°).

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check the alignment and compare with the setting value.

  2. Check bolts fixation then re- tight it (If it is needed)

  3. Change grease of coupling (multi- purpose)

  4. Change oil-lubricated bearing.

  5. Check the leakage from the glands and repacking (If it is needed)

  6. Check the leakage of flange and treat it (If it is needed)

  7. Check the gap of coupling according to the setting value.

  8. Check the casing against rust and repaint (If it is needed).

Starting of pump

Before  starting the pump, the following steps must be checked:

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump.

  2. Open the cooling water to each gland and the quantity of water is sufficient.

  3. Check the level of oil in constant level oilier for thrust bearing.

  4. Priming of the pump by opening suction valve, closing discharge valve and opening vent valve until all airs expelled and water flows through opening.

  5.   open the delivery valve gradually after the pump has been started to operate at its full speed.

Attendance and check during operation

  1. Check the discharge, pressure and vibration during operation.

  2. Check the oil level in the constant level oilier.

  3. Checks the temperature of cooling water at discharge.

  4. Check the temperature of thrust bearing housing and ball bearing end covers should not exceed 60°.
