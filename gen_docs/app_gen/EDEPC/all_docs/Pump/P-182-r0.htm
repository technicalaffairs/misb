Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

This pump transfers the condensate from the hot well of condenser to the condensate preheaters then to deaerator then to the feed tank.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the CB of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

  • Be sure that the pump is completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Rags, Grease, Filler.

  • Vibrometer, Dial indicator.

  • Grinding machine.

     

 

Work to be Carried Out

Before Disassembly

Pump in Operation

  1. Check the vibration level of the pump and record it.

  2. Check discharge, pressure and record them.

Disassembly

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check the alignment and record it.

  2. Disconnect the motor

  3. Unbolt the coupling between the motor and the pump.

  4. Remove the motor.

  5. Dismantle every piping connecting to the pump.

  6. Unbolt the pump from the barrel and take the unit and put it on a clean stand.

  7. Check the clearances of the pump before disassembly of the pump.

  8. Dismantle the thrust bearing.

  9. Dismantle the packing seal.

  10. Remove discharge elbow.

  11. Carry out the first bearing ( 515 ).

  12. Dismantle the first column pipe ( 107 ).

  13. Dismantle the screwed coupling ( 466 ) by dismantle two shafts ( 403 ) & ( 404 ). Don't loose the locating pin (408), then remove the shaft ( 403 ).

  14. Carry out the second bearing ( 515 ).

  15. Dismantle the pump with last stage of the impeller at first.

  16. Remove the shaft sleeve ( 415 ).

  17. Dismantle the fixations ( 1059 ) to extract the last stage diffuser ( 101 ).

  18. Unscrew the nut ( 4839 ) on a few threads& hammer the nut slightly until unlocking the cone ( 483 ) to remove impeller.

  19. Draw out impeller fitted upon its cone by sliding it on the shaft.

  20. Unscrew stud bolts ( 5119 ), then extract the suction casing ( 511 ).

  21. Free the nut ( 218 ) and the washers ( 217 , 219 ).

  22. Draw out the key ( 440 ).

  23. Draw the suction bowl ( 100B ).

  24. Then the shaft sleeve ( 418 ) is free now.

  25. Remove the shaft ( 404 ).

 

 

Inspection

  1. Clean all parts of the pump.

  2. Change all the bearings of the pump.

  3. Check all parts for any worn-out, replace the damaged.

  4. Check all parts for cracks; treat the cracks by grinding or remachining.

  5. Inspect the run-out of the shafts.

  6. Change the wearing ring.

  7. Measure the radial clearances for the pump by measuring the gap between wearing ring of the diffuser and the distance sleeve on the shaft and adjust them.

  8. Grease the bearings of the pump.(type of grease is multi-purpose grease).

  9. Change the packing seal between the shaft and the wear ring.

  10. Repaint the casing against rust.

Assembly

  1. Clean all contact surfaces.

  2. Mount the impellers and diffusers of the pump beginning from the delivery side up to suction side as follow.

  3. Fit the sleeve ( 418 ) on the shaft ( 404 ).

  4. Cap the suction casing ( 100B ) over the shaft with its bushing ( 528 ) with tape ( 5282 ) and wear ring ( 310A ).

  5. Slide the tools sleeve ( 311A ), this one will be thrust to the bushing ( 528 ).

  6. By using the nut ( 218 ), the first impeller (200B) fitted with taper ( 483 ) will be thrust inside the stage diffuser ( 100A ).

  7. Assemble second bearing (515) fitted with its lining ( 5152 ).

  8. Place the pin ( 408 ) on pump shaft.

  9. Screw the shaft coupling ( 466 ) by assembly the two shafts ( 403 , 404 ).

  10. Place the pin ( 408 ) on pump shaft.

  11. Assemble the column pipe ( 107 ).

  12. Continue these operations up to last bearing ( 515 ).

  13. Check the clearances of the pump.

  14. Reassemble the delivery elbow ( 102 ).

  15. Reassemble the stuffing box ( 600 ) and set new packing seal.

  16. Assembly the thrust bearing.

  17. Measure and adjust the clearance of the pump.

  18. Connect every piping connecting to the pump.

  19. Assemble the coupling between the motor and the pump.

  20. Fix support motor with fixation ( 5019 ).

  21. Adjust the alignment between the motor shaft and the pump shaft and record it.

 

Starting the Pump

Before Starting the Pump

  1. Check the oil level in the thrust bearing pump bath.

  2. Open the cooling water for the oil bath.

  3. Open the suction and delivery valves of the pump.

  4. Check the direction of rotation of the pump by pushing the electric button of the motor instantaneously.

During Operation of the Pump

  1. Check the temperature of the cooling water and the anti-friction bearing.

  2. Check for any water and oil leaks.

  3. Check the discharge and pressure of the pump.

  4. Check the vibration level.
