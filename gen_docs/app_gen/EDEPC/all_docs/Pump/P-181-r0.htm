Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

This pump is used to transfer the coolant water from the River Nile to the condenser for condensing the steam from turbine to condensate water

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the CB of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

  • Be sure that the pump is completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid.

  • Filler.

  • Vibrometer.

  • Dial indicator.

  • Rags.

  • Grease.

  • Grinding machine.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Disassembly

The Pump in Operation

  1. Measure the vibration level of the pump and record it.

  2. Measure discharge 9pressure and record them.

Disassembly

The Pump out of Operation

  1. Check the alignment and record it.

  2. Disconnect the motor.

  3. Unbolt the motor cover and raise it up.

  4. Unbolt the coupling between the motor and the pump.

  5. Remove the motor.

  6. Dismantle every pipe connecting to the pump.

  7. Dismantle the stuffing box.

  8. Unbolt the screw 118.01 (detail 7).(Figure 3)

  9. Unbolt the part (detail 4) (Figure 3) as follow:

    • Unbolt the nut 200.04.

    • Take out the washer 200.05.

    • Unbolt the screw.

  10. Raise the casing upper part.

  11. Disassemble the upper coupling (see detail 2) (Figure 4) as follow:-

    • Unbolt the nut 0201.02, raise the washer 0201.04 .

    • Raise the bolt 0201.01 .

    • Remove the nut 195.01 & the washer 195.02, then remove the pin 189.00.

    • Using a bronze shim or a press, take out the packings 257.01 & 257.00  and the bush 270.00.

  12. Unbolt the part detailed ( 5 ) (Figure 2) as follow :-

    • Unbolt the nut 117.03 and raise the washer 117.02 .

    • Unbolt the screw 117.01 and raise the washer 117.02 .

  13. Unbolt the part detailed ( 6 ) (Figure 2) as follow :-

    • Unbolt the pin 117.05 .

  14. Raise the intermediate casing..

  15. Mount the upper shaft and raise it.

  16. Disassemble the lower coupling 195 (see detail 2) (Figure 4) as mentioned in the upper coupling.

  17. Mount the intermediate shaft and raise it.

  18. Raise the lower shaft with the impeller and the guide bearing 140 as one unit by the crane after mounting it by wires.
         

  19. Disassemble the guide bearing 140 between the impeller and the lower shaft and take the lower shaft and the impeller out.

  20. The pump now is completely disassembled.

Inspection

  1. Clean all parts of the pump.

  2. Change all the bearings of the pump.

  3. Check all parts for any worn-out, replace the damaged.

  4. Check all parts for cracks, treat the cracks by grinding or remachining.

  5. Inspect the run-out of the shafts.

  6. Change the wearing ring.

  7. Measure the radial clearances for the pump by measuring the gap between wearing ring of the diffuser and the distance sleeve on the shaft and adjust them.

  8. Grease the bearings of the pump. (type of grease is multi-purpose grease).

  9. Change the packing seal between the shaft and the wear ring.

  10. Repaint the casing against rust.

Assembly

  1. Assemble the impeller with the lower shaft by assembling the guide bearing 140.

  2. Insert the lower shaft with impeller into the casing lower part.

  3. Assemble the lower coupling ( between the lower and intermediate shafts ) as follow :-

    1. Put the bush 270.00 around the shaft.

    2. Put the packing 257.00 and bush 257.01.

    3. Insert pin 189.00 to lock the bushes and the packing with the coupling and tight the screw 195.01.

    4. Assemble the upper and lower parts of the coupling by inserting the screw 201.01 and tightening the nut 201.02.

  4. Assemble the intermediate shaft.

  5. Assemble the intermediate part of the casing by assembling the two parts detailed ( 5 & 6 ) (Figure 2) as follow:

    1. Insert the pin 117.05 and tight it (detail 6 ) (Figure 2).

    2. Put washer 117.02 and insert the screw 117.01 ( detail 5 ) (Figure 2)

    3. Put washer 117.02 and lock by nut 117.03. ( detail 5 ) (Figure 2)

    4. Assemble the upper coupling ( between the intermediate and upper shafts ) as mentioned before in the lower coupling.

    5. Assemble the upper shaft.

  6. Assemble the casing upper part by assembling the two parts detailed ( 4 & 7 ) (Figure 3) as follow:

    1. Insert the pin 118.01 and tight it (detail 7 ) (Figure 3)

    2. Put washer 200.05, insert the screw 200.04 and tight it. ( detail 4 ) (Figure 3)

  7. Assemble the stuffing box.
        

  8. Connect all pipes of the pump.

  9. Assemble the motor by assembling the coupling between the pump shaft and motor shaft

  10. Put the motor cover and tight it.

  11. The pump now is ready for operating.

Starting the Pump

Before starting the Pump

  1. Check the oil level in the thrust bearing pump bath.

  2. Open the suction and delivery valves of the pump.

  3. Check the direction of rotation of the pump by pushing the electric button of the motor instantaneously.

During Operation         

  1. The temperature of the cooling water and the anti-friction bearing

  2. Any water and oil leaks

  3. The discharge and pressure of the pump

  4. Check the vibration level and record it.
