Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I2, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

The technical specification of the oil pump : capacity Q:8.3-151/s, total head H: 27-34.8m, speed n:2900 rpm, power 7kw

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety equipment as safety helmet , shoes, breathing mask, safety glass ... etc

  • Switch off Pump

  • Use suitable warning boards

Tools and Equipment

  • Set of common and special tools

  • Spare parts, gaskets and rubber

  • Kerosene and cleaning material (rag. cloth)

  • Packing and grease

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. To carry out the outside inspection & to hear the pump in operation before stoppage for the revision for the purpose of normal operation of the bearings, vibrations, and leakage through the sealing joints

  2. When stripping a pump, inspect all the parts as to determine whether individual parts are fit for further service, whether they should be replaced or subjected to repair

  3. Begin stripping a pump by detaching the pipe line

  4. Remove the pump housing cover & the impeller

  5. Strip the sealing gland

  6. Remove the pump housing from its bracket

  7. Take off the bearing housing caps with a drift knock out the ball bearing and shaft

  8. Take the ball bearing off the pump shaft

  9. Having inspected, repair or replaced parts, as required, begin assembly of the pump in reverse steps of stripping

  10. Before assembly, flush all parts and wipe them dry

  11. Having assembled the pump, back grease into the ball bearing housing

  12. Run the pump and check for the proper operation
