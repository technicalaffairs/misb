Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be carried out every six months bases as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

This pump is used for cooling the winding of generator stator

Safety Precautions 

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags.

Work to be carried out

Pump in Operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do.

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare them with the setting value then replace the worn- out components.

Pump Out Of Operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens it.

  3. Check the validity of oil and add or change it.

  4. Check glands for leaking and re-packing.

  5. Check flanges for leaking and re-tight.

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint.
