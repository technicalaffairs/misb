Introduction

This documents out lines the equipment inspection procedure I1, which must be performed every two years or at thrust bearing damage which comes first, as indicated by Y2 in the title block.

This pump is used to push the cooling water through heat exchangers to cool the auxiliaries equipment.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should cany the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets shoes, gloves, … etc

  • Isolated the circuit breaker of the pump motor 

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the Pump Motor.

  • Isolate the Pump mechanically by closing suction and delivery valves.

  • Be Sure that the pump is completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Filler, Feeler gauge,

  • Rags, Grease

  • Vibrometer, Dial indicator

Work to be Carried Out

Pump in Operation

  1. Measure Vibration level for bearing and compare it with setting values then analyze it and determine what to do

  2. Check the discharge, pressure of pump and compare it with the Setting values the replace the worm components

  3. Check the temperature of bearing house (60°).

Pump out of Operation

Disassembly of the Emergency Thrust Bearing for Replacement

  1. Measure alignment and record it

  2. Remove constant level oilier (251)   from thrust and bearing hosing (134) 

  3. Drain oil from housing.

  4. Dismantle cap screws on each end cover

  5. Dismantle thrust bearing end cover (109).

  6. Dismantle snap ring (361).

  7. Dismantle bearing cap (111).

  8. Rotate bearing hosing (134) 1805, so that the two locks are on the top.

  9. Remove thrust bearing (112) from shaft by use of a bearing puller.

  10. Slide thrust bearing collar (273).

  11. Slide thrust bearing housing (134) from shaft (122).

Inspection

  1. Thrust bearing house, thrust bearing end cover, snap ring, ball bearing collar, and end of shaft must be clean with kerosene.

  2. Inspect thrust bearing house (134) from pitting, wear and corrosion

  3. Inspect thrust bearing end cover (109) from pitting, wear and corrosion.

  4. Inspect thrust bearing cap (111) from pitting, wear and corrosion.

  5. Inspect snap ring (361) from pitting, wear and corrosion

  6. Inspect thrust bearing collar (237) from pitting, wear and corrosion.

  7. Inspect deflectors’ (123) from pitting, wear and corrosion.

 

 

 

Assembly of the Emergency Thrust Bearing Replacement

  1. Clean all contact surfaces before mounting.

  2. Wipe a small amount of grease on the oil seal lips in bearing housing.

  3. Place thrust bearing housing (134) on shaft, with the double locks up and slides it a long to approximately its correct location.

  4. Align locks on bearing end cover (109) with the grooves in lower half-casing and rotate bearing end Cover (180°) so that the locks are at the bottom.

  5. Slid thrust bearing collar (237) over shaft end and Push shaft shoulder. 

  6. Apply a film of oil to the bearing seat on the shaft.

  7. Install the thrust bearing (112) on the shaft (the bearing will be installed back to back) 

  8. Place snap ring (361) on the shaft groove.

  9. Screw bolt bearing end cover (109) to bearing housing

  10. Assemble bearing cap (111) being sure that the prick punch marks on the cap and the casing are on the same side.

  11. Tighten the nuts evenly.

  12. Check thrust bearing endplay.

  13. Refill with oil in constant level oilier.

  14. Check the alignment of coupling between driver and pump.

Staring of Pump

Before the starting Pump, the Following Steps must be Checked:

  1. Checked the direction of rotation of pump.

  2. Open the cooling water to each gland and the quantity of water is sufficient.

  3. Checks the level of oil in constant level oilier for thrust bearing.

  4. Priming of the pump by open suction valve, close discharge valve and open vent valve until all airs   expelled and water flows through opening.

  5. Open the delivery valve gradually after the pump has been started to operate at its full speed.

Attendance and Checked during Operation

  1. Check the discharge, pressure and vibration during operation.

  2. Check the oil level in the constant level oilier.

  3. Check the temperature of cooling water at discharge.

  4. Check the temperature of thrust bearing housing and thrust bearing end cover should not exceed 60°
