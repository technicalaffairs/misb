Introduction

This document outlines the cooling water pump inspection steps I1, which must be carried out every six  months as indicated by M6 in the title block

The pump technical data:
Type: centrifugal pump multi stage                RPM 2970
Manufacture: Ensival
No of stage    1                                            Power   15 KW
Suction press. 1.5  bar                                 Max. flow 75m3./h
Discharge press.   5 bar

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Use safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

    •

  • Shut down the pump

  • Disconnect the power supply & rack out the circuit breaker

  • Put red tag on the pump

  • Close the shut-off valves in the (suction & discharge & balancing & min flow) (VG 1GREOKEB5AA1)

  • Let the pumps cool down to about < 80ºC measured at the branches, then seal off the cooling water pipes and close all other supply lines

  • Loosen all auxiliary lines, such as circulation, cooling liquid, lube oil

  • The safety department must check the isolation and surrounding the zone of work

Tools and Equipment

  • Special tools, kit of alin keys, kit of keys

  • Crane, alignment devices & filler

Work to be Carried Out

Dismantling and Reassembling

  1. Remove coupling spacer

  2. Drain the oil / change oil

  3. Remove the casing adapter nuts

  4. Pull out the complete bearing housing with rotor and carry it eventually to the workshop to continue dismantling

  5. It is recommended to replace all the gaskets and o-rings at every reassembling

Testing Steps

  1. Make sure that the direction of the rotation

  2. If the pump has been filled with a cold liquid (temperature below 60 c) it can be started at any time

  3. At firs starting tighten the gland nut by hand

  4. After 10 min operation tighten each nut one sixth of turn

Packing Replacement

  1. Cut off electric supply

  2. Close isolating valve

  3. Remove the old packing

  4. Check if the shaft sleeve is not damaged

  5. Clean carefully the stuffing box, the sleeve and the gland

  6. Cut the new packing ring

  7. Insert the rings into the stuffing box and compress them evenly one after each other until it is completely packed

  8. Slightly tighten gland nuts by hand.
