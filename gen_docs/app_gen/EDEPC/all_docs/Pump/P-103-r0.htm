Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be carried out every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding work area is required.

  • Wear your safety equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, breathing mask, safety glass .. etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Rags

Work to be Carried Out 

Pump in Service

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure then , compare it with the setting valve

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump and motor and record them

 

Disassemble

Pump out of Service

  1. Dismantle the coupling guard between pump and motor

  2. Check the alignment

  3. Dismantle the motor bolts with base

  4. Dismantle the pump casing from diffuser

  5. Dismantle the bolts from pump casing with base

  6. Take the pump out

  7. Check the clearance between impeller and diffuser

  8. Dismantle the impeller nuts and remove the impeller

  9. Remove the gland and the washer

  10. Remove the shaft with bearings from pump casing

  11. Remove the ball bearings and gland sleeve

Inspection

  1. Inspect the ball bearings and shaft sleeve, change if necessary.

  2. Inspect the shaft run out, must not exceed 0.03mm.

  3. Inspect the clearance between impeller and suction wear ring

Assembly

  1. Clean all parts carefully.

  2. Reassemble bearings in the shaft and shaft sleeve

  3. Reassemble the shaft with bearings to the pump casing

  4. Reassemble the gland washer and packing and gland (not tight)

  5. Reassemble the impeller and tighten the impeller nut

  6. Reassemble the pump casing with diffuser

  7. Reassemble the auxiliary pipes (suction , delivery) and tighten the bolts for pump casing with base

  8. Check alignment between pump and motor.

Before Starting the Pump

  1. Check isolating valves (suction, delivery) open

Starting the Pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation

  2. Check the pressure

  3. Check the vibration

  4. Check the temperature of bearings

  5. Check that there is no noise.
