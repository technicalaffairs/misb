Introduction

This document outlines the Major Maintenance Procedures P2, which must be carried out every four years as indicated by Y4 in the above title block. This pump is used to transfer sea water from intake to condenser.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Ropes, and wooden blocks.

  • Vibrometer, Feeler gauge, dial indicator.

  • Cleaning fluid.

Work to be Carried Out

Before Pump Shut Down

  1. Check discharge pressure and record them.

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump and record it.

 

 

Pump Shut Down

Measure the alignment and record it.

Disassembly

  1. Un couple the electric motor from the pump and lift it, lean it on a wooden blokes to avoid damage of the half coupling.

  2. Dismantle hydraulic, instruments and remove them.

  3. Drain the oil from thrust support.

  4. Dismantle the discharge flange.

  5. Dismantle the frame from the foundation base plate.

  6. Sling the pump, lift it from the sump, leave it for a few minutes to drain it completely.

  7. Measure the radial clearance of blades and record it (0.5 – 1.25 mm). 

  8. Lean the pump in a horizontal position with the discharge hole on the ground with a wooden blocks to avoid damage of discharge hole.

  9. Place the discharge column on a wooden blocks of suitable high for easy disassembly.

Disassembly of Impeller

  1. Sling the bell mouth (138), disconnect it from the wear plate and remove it.

  2. Straighten the lock washer (931.1), unscrew the impeller locking screw (906), remove the impeller hub cap (260) and split ring (501).

  3. Sling the wear plate (135), disconnect it from the propeller bowl (104) and remove it.

  4. Sling the impeller (252), un thread it and remove the key (940.1).

  5. Beneath the shaft end on a wooden blocks of suitable high to avoid bending.

Disassembly of Thrust Bearing

  1. Remove the temperature sensor and air vent.

  2. Dismantle fan shrouds.

  3. Dismantle the fan and top cover.

  4. Dismantle upper retaining ring and lift out journal pads for inspection.

Disassembly of Upper Parts

  1. Dismantle the support and loosen the stuffing box.

  2. Dismantle the rigid coupling connecting the hollow shaft (213) with the inter mediate shaft (212).

  3. Disconnect motor stool (341) from the column pipe (711.02), unthreaded it along the shaft. Keep items horizontally to avoid sliding of the shaft.

  4. Sling the column pipe (711.01), unthread it along the shaft and keep it horizontally.

  5. Sling the inter mediate shaft (212), slightly put under stress the lifting rope.

  6. Sling the propeller bowl (104), disconnect it from the column section (711.01), insert some supports beneath the shaft end.

     

  7. Dismantle the inter mediate coupling (848) and disconnect the pump shaft (211) from the inter mediate shaft (212).

Inspection

  1. Check the blades for ( wear – corrosion – cracks ).

  2. Check the shaft  run out( 0.03 mm).

  3. Check the sliding bearing surface anti wear and scratches.

  4. Check all protection sleeves of the shaft and replace them if it is needs.

  5. Replace all gaskets.

  6. Check all keys and the tie bolts and replace them if it is needs.

Assembly

  1. Sling the column pipe (711.02). connect it with the motor stool (341).

  2. Sling the inter mediate shaft (212), keep it horizontally insert it and connect with the hollow shaft by a rigid coupling.

  3. Sling the pump shaft (211), connect it with the inter mediate shaft by the inter mediate coupling (848), beneath the shaft with a wooden blocks of suitable high to avoid bending.

  4. Sling the column pipe (711.01), connect it with the column pipe (711.02) diagonally and gradually bang the shaft.

  5. Sling the propeller bowl (104), connect it with the column pipe (711.01).

  6. Mount the key (940.01)

  7. Sling the impeller and mount it on the pump shaft.

  8. Sling the wear plate (135) and connect it with the propeller bowl.

  9. Check the radial clearance of impeller blades with wear plate.

  10. Mount the split ring (501) and assemble the hub cap nut (260).

  11. Mount the lock washer and screw the locking screw (906).

Assembly of Thrust Bearing

  1. Assemble the thrust bearing as follow.

  2. Assemble the thrust pads on their support face push in radial under the locating lip on the top of base plate.

  3. Insert pads stop and check clearance between pads and stops.

  4. With shaft support screw the collar and lock in position using bolts and lock washer.

  5. Insert journal pad stops into housing and screw it to base plate.

  6. Assemble journal pads into housing mount complete upper thrust ring assemble into housing and screw it.

  7. Place jointing on base plate and assemble casing to base plate.

  8. Mount the top cover to casing and screw it.

  9. Assemble fan completely on top of collar and screw with grub screw.

  10. Assemble the temperature sensor.

  11. Assemble half fan shrouds screw it with casing.

  12. Mount air vent and fit the drain plug with base plate.

  13. Insert the stuffing box with a new packing cords.

  14. Rotate the pump rotor and ascertain that it is easy rotating

  15. Lift the pump, place it in sump.

  16. Mount all hydraulic, and instruments and electric connections.

  17. Charge oil for thrust bearing.

  18. Lift the pump motor on its place.

  19. Adjust the align mint between motor shaft and pump and record it.

  20. Couple the motor completely with the pump shaft.

Starting the Pump

  1. Before starting the pump, the following steps should be checked.

  2. Check the oil level in the thrust bearing.

  3. Check the direction of rotation of the pump by pushing the electric button instantly. 

Attendance and Checking

  1. The temperature of thrust bearing.

  2. Any water and oil leakage.

  3. Discharge quantity, pressure and record them.

  4. Check vibration level and record it.
