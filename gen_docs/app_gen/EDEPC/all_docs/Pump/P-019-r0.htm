Technical specifications

Type of pump 

KSB – ETA 80 – 20VL.

Manufacturer

Siemens – Germany.

Type of fluid

Oil VG 46.

Shaft

Vertical.

Capacity

1.7 M3 / min.

Discharge pressure

5 Bars.

Temperature

55 C°.

Driver

Electric motor.

Power required

30 KW.

Speed

2940 RPM.

Bearing type

Ball bearing [6406 C3].

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, gloves, etc….

  • Isolate the CB of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • Isolate the pump mechanically by closing delivery valve.

  • Be sure that the pump is completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Filler. Rags.

  • Vibrometer, Dial indicator.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Measure vibration level for bearing and compare it with the setting values, then analyze it and determine what to do [if it is needed].

  2. Check discharge, pressure and compare it with the setting values then replace the worn-out components [if it is needed].

  3. Check the alignment and compare with the setting values.

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tighten it [if it is needed].

  5. Test the bearing oil, add or change oil [if it is required].

  6. Check the leakage from the glands.

  7. Check the leakage from the flanges.

  8. Check the pump casing against rust and re -paint [if it is required].
