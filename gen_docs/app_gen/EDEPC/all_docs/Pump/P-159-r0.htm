Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedure P1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block.

Technical specification

Type of pump: 250CVEV-400-30/2
Design:  Vertical centrifugal pump
Pump capacity: 378 m3/h ( 105 l/s )
Specific energy 90 m w.c. ( 883 J/kg )
Pump Speed  980 rpm.
Pump power  200Kw
Bearing type: 7315

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge.

  • Rags.

Work to be carried out

Pump in Operation

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure and record them.

  2. Check the vibration level of the pump set complete with the motor then, record them.

Disassembly

Pump out of Operation

  1. Check alignment and record it.

  2. Dismantle all attached pipes to the pump.

  3. Unscrew motor bolts and take it out.

  4. Lift pump from its place to the work place.

  5. Dismantle the gland of the pump and remove packing cords out.

  6. Dismantle the pump half coupling.

  7. Dismount thrust bearing complete with the hanger.

  8. Dismantle discharge elbow and shaft guide pipes.

  9. Dismantle the shaft joints by unscrewing them from their mafe couplings.

  10. Dismantle suction lantern if used, then take suction slide bearing out.

  11. Begin dismantling the hydraulic part from suction.

  12. Dismantle impellers, sleeves and diffusers from the hydraulic part shaft after dismantling the pump clamping bolts.

  13. Check all dismantled parts for worn-out and replace by new one (if it is needed).

Inspection

  1. The validity of the anti-friction ball bearings.

  2. Check all parts for any worn-out and replace them by new ones.

  3. Inspect the run-out of shafts (allowance 0.03 mm).

Assembly

  1. Begin the assembly from the hydraulic part first.

  2. Install diffusers, sleeves, and impellers from the discharge side up to suction side and tight them with the clamping bolts.

  3. Insert shaft in the suction slide bearing with lantern and check radial and axial clearance of the pump and ensure that pump rotate easily, otherwise dismount and treat the causes.

  4. Install the shaft joints with their mafe couplings

  5. Mount the shaft guide pipes.

  6. Install the hanger complete with discharge elbow into shaft guide pipes.

  7. Mount the stuffing box with gland.

  8. Mount the thrust bearing and cover it.

  9. Install the pump half coupling.

  10. Lift the pump by crane and put it in its place.

  11. Fix the pump and tight all attached pipes.

  12. Mount the electric motor and adjust alignment.

  13. Install packing cords and tight gland bolts easily.

  14. Do not forget to grease thrust bearing.

Starting of Pump

  1. Check the direction of rotation of pump before starting the pump for the first time.

  2. Check the quantity of grease in the thrust-bearing sump.

  3. Check whether the inlet for flooding the stuffing box is open.

  4. Check whether the inlet for hydraulic sealing of the suspension is open.

  5. Check whether the delivery sluice valve is closed.

  6. Open the delivery valve gradually after the set has been started to operate at its full speed.

Attendance and Checking During Operation

During operation, the following should be checked:

  1. The temperature of the anti-friction bearing in the bearing top which should not exceed 75°C.

  2. The temperature of cooling water at discharge.
