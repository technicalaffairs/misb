Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The technical specification of the turbine P max = 1.8 MW, H max = 5.5m,
Q = 50mˆ3/s, D = 3600 mm, speed = 90.58/1000 RPM,

The first draft initiated by eng. Abd El Mohsen Mohamed, tested at Nag Hammadi Hyd. PS, and certified by eng. Kamel Ragheb Halem: HPGC MPIS coordinator, QC general director.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintanance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Stop the unit and CB out, and earthing transformer

  • Hang the suitable warning boards

  • Put the up stream stop logs and dewatering the spiral case

  • Select the unit on manual mode and operate governor pump continuously

  • Open 100% wicket gate and put the safety pin in panel rack

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes

  • Set of wrenches, cleaning material

  • Alignment equipment

  • Grease for lubricating (multi purpose grease, Kaplan turbine)

  • Lifting manual crane

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Carryout the semi annual inspection as MPIS doc # Turb-002-r0

  2. Check and cleaning any leakage from the gear box and governor cooler

  3. Check runner and guide vanes surface for bad painting and crakes

  4. Check the guide vanes and runner clearance and compare with commissioning values

  5. Comparing the closing and opening time of servomotor

  6. Check the sealing and bolts for the inspection turbine doors

  7. Check the dewatering spiral casing valve

  8. Check the protection and control equipment
