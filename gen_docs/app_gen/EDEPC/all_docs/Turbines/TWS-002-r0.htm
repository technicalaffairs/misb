Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I2, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above titled block

The Traveling water screen system is used for cleaning of the Nile River water utilized for the operation of the combined cycle power station from plastics, wood .... etc

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

    •

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, ear plug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the screen mechanically and electrically.  

Tools and Equipment

  • Maintenance tool set.

  • Grease (multi purpose grease).

  • Wires to raise up the screen cover.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Grease the motor roller bearing.

  2. Grease the sliding roller bearings.

  3. Grease the sliding roller pins.

  4. Check any wear of the washing nozzles

  5. Check the bolts fixation to avoid the vibration
