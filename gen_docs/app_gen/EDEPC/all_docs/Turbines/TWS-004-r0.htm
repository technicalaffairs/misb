Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection  I1, to be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The Traveling water Screen function: cleaning the cooling water to the condenser from the impurities such as herbs, shells, papers and plastic bottles that coming with water to prevent clogging condenser tubes which coming from the  Nile River.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

    •

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the screen mechanically and electrically.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves

Tools and Equipment

  • Maintenance tool set.

  • Grease (multi purpose grease).

  • Wires to raise up the screen cover.

  • Cleaning fluid, Rages

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

 

Traveling Water Screen in Service

  1. Check the validity the mesh of the screen wires.

  2. Check the sprockets of the traveling screen.

  3. Check the spray nozzles of clogging.

  4. Check the chains of roller drive from wear.

  5. Measure vibrations level for the sliding roller bearings and compare it with the setting value , then analyze it and determine what to do ( if it is needed )

Traveling Water Screen out of Service

  1. Check the validity of the roller ball bearings of the sprockets.

  2. Check flanges for water leakage.

  3. Check the hinges pins between screens holder.

  4. Check any wear of the washing nozzles.

  5. Check the bolts fixation to avoid the vibration.

  6. Check any wear in the drive and driven sprockets
