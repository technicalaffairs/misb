Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Closing delivery and suction valves of steam side after shutdown

  • Be sure condensate pump remain in service

  • Be sure seal steam condenser exhauster

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Main Crane, Alfa Laval,

  • Manual Puller, Feeler Gauge, Rags, Cleaning Fluid,

  • Dial Indicator, Vibrometer.

Work to be carried out 

Seal steam condenser & Seal steam condenser exhauster in service

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare them with the setting value then replace the worn- out components (if it is needed).

  2. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do (if it is needed)

  3. Check leakage of water

  4. Check temperature in & out

  5. Check level & temperature

  6. Check the pressure in seal steam header ( above atmospheric ) is 35 mbar

  7. Check any alarm in pressure and temperature of seal steam margin.

Seal steam condenser & Seal steam condenser exhauster out of service :

  1. Check cut of steam from aux. and open vacuum breaker 

  2. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  3. Check bolts fixation, then retightens it (if it is needed)

  4. Clean oil by Alfa Laval and adjust oil level of pump.

  5. Check the validity of oil and add or change it (if it is needed).

  6. Check glands for leaking and repacking (if it is needed).

  7. Check the pump casing against rust and repaint (if it is needed).
