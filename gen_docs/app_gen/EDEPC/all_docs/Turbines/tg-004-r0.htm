Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in above title block.

The turning gear used for turning the rotor of turbine with low speed during shutdown and reach its zero speed.

Technical specification

Input speed                                        1670 rpm

Speed of satellite carrier                    215.5 1 / min

Speed ratio                                           7.75

Overrunning clutch                               800 rpm

Speed of pump wheel                         1670 rpm

Min. oil pressure of pump wheel          0.05 MPa

Slip                                                       0.5 %

Output speed                                     70 – 72 rpm

Transmitting torque moment                   63 Nm

Lubricating oil pressure                      0.1 – 0.17 MPa

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance stuff should be carrying the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the turning gear motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the turning gear motor.

  • Isolate the turning gear mechanically by cutoff the input oil.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge, Grease gun

  • Rags, Soft brush, Oil can.

Work to be Carried Out 

Disassembly

Turning gear in Operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do.

  2. Check revolution of turbine rotor and compare it with the setting value then analyze it and determine what to do.

Turning gear Out of Operation

  1. Remove the pelt drive of the motor.

  2. Shift electric motor away from the Turning gear.

  3. Disconnect auxiliary piping of oil.

  4. Screw out screw fixing bracket base to base plate.

  5. Attach carrying rope to the upper Turning gear.

  6. Take out carefully the whole Turning gear and put it on out side table.

  7. Dismantling the shift in hydraulic part.

  8. Dismantling Hydrodynamical coujpling.

  9. Dismantling the upper cover of Turning gear.

  10. Dismantling Epicyclic gear.

  11. Dismantling Overruning clutch.

  12. Dismantling Hydraulic Throwing gear.

Inspection

  1. The validity of the anti-friction ball bearings.

  2. Check all parts for any worn-out and replace them by new ones.

  3. Inspect the run-out of shafts (allowance 0.03 mm).

Assembly

  1. Insert new bearings on the clean shaft.

  2. Install Hydraulic Throwing gear.

  3. Install Overruning clutch.

  4. Install Epicyclic gear.

  5. Install the upper cover of Turning gear.

  6. Insert new GUFERO rings with covers through oiled shaft.

  7. Install Hydrodynamical coujpling.

  8. Install the covers of the bearings and seal them against the bracket.

  9. Install the shift in hydraulic part.

  10. Put new gasket on position of Turning gear in turbine.

  11. After completing the assemblies attach carrying rope to the upper Turning gear and put it on its position in turbine.

  12. Screw in screw fixing bracket base to base plate.

  13. Connecting auxiliary piping of oil

  14. Put electric motor on the Turning gear.

  15. Install new pelt drive for the motor.

Starting of Turning Gear

  1. Check the direction of rotation of turning gear before starting the Turning gear for the first time.

  2. Check the pressure of lubricating oil is 0.1 – 0.17 MPa.

  3. Check revolution of turbine rotor is 70 – 72 rpm.

Attendance and checking during Operation

  1. The temperature of the anti-friction bearing in the bearing top which should not exceed 75°C.

  2. Check the oil pressure in turning gear.

  3. Check lube oil pressure and level of oil tank.

  4. Check the temperature of oil.

  5. Check vibration for turning gear.
