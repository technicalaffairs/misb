Introduction

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued and maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Shutdown the unit.

  • Generator should be isolated.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of Generator

  • Disconnect the electric terminals of Generator

  • The oil of Turbine must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure Turbine must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge

  • Rags

Work to be Carried out

The maintenance works require putting the turbo-generator out of operation for a long period about 3 weeks. During such repair, the turbine bodies are not usually opened. Only the inspection of selected wholes or inspection is performed, e.g.:

  1. Inspect the bearings

  2. Inspect the control of the gland steam with instrument maintenance department

  3. Inspect of the control circuit of the lubrication oil temperature by instrument maintenance department

  4. Check the function of the starting-up equipment and the removal of defects

  5. Check the function of lifting pump or the adjustment of pressure the function of oil pumps driven by electric motors and of the oil steam fan or their repairs

  6. Clean and test oil cooler of turbine.

  7. Clean all magnetic filters of bearings of turbine.

  8. Clean fine screen of oil tank.

  9. Inspect and adjust the opening stroke of control valve of turbine.

  10. Moreover all the defects found out during the operation will be removed

  11. Purification of the oil of turbine by oil separator

  12. Check revolution per minute for turning gear.

  13. Check all protection of turbine shut as over speed, decrease the vacuum & temperature of bearing.
