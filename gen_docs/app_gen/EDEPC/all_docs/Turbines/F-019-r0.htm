Introduction

This document outlines the replacement procedure, which must be performed for primary air filters and Secondary air filters when filters differential pressure is greater than 4.2 mm water gauge, or emergency door open. Changing filters depending on ∆P according to the weather of Hurghada city.

Primary air filters and secondary air filters to clean the air from dust, small particles…etc.

Replacement of hight pressure filters after replacement of prefilter 3 times separately.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety Category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, eye glass, nose mouth "dust cover".... etc

  • Turbine must be shutdown

  • Fire fighting system must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Rags, cleaning Fluid.

Work to be carried out

Disassembly

  1. Disassembly the primary filters at the first then disassembly Secondary air filters.

  2. Remove Sealer Frame (or pre-filter).

  3. Remove the dirty Filter by gripping the top and bottom edges (Flanges) of the filter and pulling forward.

  4. Fold dirty filter carefully to trap all collected dirt and place In a box or can for disposal.

Assembly

  1. Assembly the Secondary air filters at the first then assembly the primary filters

  2. Insert the bottom panel of the filter about half-way into the Retainer, making sure that the folds in the filter line up with Retainer partitions.

  3. With both hands, carefully stretch the top panel up and over the upper ends of the wire dividers in the Retainer.

  4. Push both top and bottom panels of the filter in together until the flanges seat against the gasket around the Frame.

  5. Fold the edges of the filter and the flanges over the gasket of the Holding Frame to assure a good seal.

  6. Replace Sealer Frame (or pre-filter) so that all edges of the filter are clamped against the gasket on the Holding Frame and secure with Holding Clips.

Attendance and checking during Operation

During operation of the unit, the static pressure gauge should be checked ∆P<4.2 mm water gauge
