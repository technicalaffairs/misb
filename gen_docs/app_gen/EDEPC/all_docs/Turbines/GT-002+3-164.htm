Criteria to determine if the combustion chamber caps and liners

should be reused, repaired, or scrapped.

 

                                                              NOTE

 

a.

Combustion chamber liners are typical, therefore, they will not be discussed by specific drawing numbers

  

b.

Tungsten inert gas is the preferred welding process. The coated rod process and stick welding is acceptable when performed by competent personnel.

  

c.

Deterioration less than maximum allowable limits indicates that re-use in the "as is" condition is acceptable, based on the premise that consideration has been given to previously recorded data pertaining to the operating hours, firing conditions, and the subsequent
operations expected from the liners

 

 

            Checking and sizing of louver openings is not generally recommended except for those louvers which have been repaired.

 

            Repaired louver openings should be sized to ± .005" of drawings or comparison dimension, with care taken to maintain a .350" to  .375" minimum width of louve opening
