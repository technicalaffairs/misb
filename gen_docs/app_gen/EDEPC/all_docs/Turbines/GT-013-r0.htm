Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every 8000 equivalent operating hours EOH 8000 as indicated in the title block

The gas turbine uses air as working fluid which is drown in and compressed by the compressor, fuel is added and burnt in the combustion chamber to heat the air to turbine inlet temperature, the hot gases is expanded in the turbine.

The exhaust gases leaves the turbine through the exhaust diffuser for being discharged into the stack or to the heat recovery steam generator in the case of combined cycle power plant, the useful output is available at the compressor end coupling to drive the generator.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets, shoes, gloves, earplug ...  etc

  • Turbine must be shut down

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance set.

  • Special scaffold.

  • Compressed air hose.

  • Cleaning articles [fluid, rags ---- etc].

  • Steel meter, filler and venire caliper.

  • 24 Vdc electric light.

  • Penetration test.

Work to be Carried Out 

Area down stream from compressor

  1. Open intake casing door.

  2. Check intake casing for dirt accumulation [oil, dust].

  3. Check blade cleaning device for cracks

  4. Check compressor stage 0 stator blades for dirt accumulation, impact of foreign bodies, corrosion, coating wear and cracks.

  5. Check compressor stage 1 rotor blades for impact of foreign bodies, corrosion, coating wear and cracks.

  6. Check radial gap for compressor stage 1 rotor blades and record in MRDS.

  7. Close intake casing door.

Areas left, right - hand combustion chambers, and front of turbine

  1. Open combustion chamber manhole cover.

  2. Installation special scaffold.

  3. Check dual-fuel burner for dirt accumulation, corrosion and cracks.

  4. Check flame tube plate for dirt accumulation, corrosion, deformation and crack.

  5. Check flame tube with refractory bricks for corrosion, cracks, damage and looseness.

  6. Check over lap between flame tube and mixing chamber for crakes and grooves.

  7. Check radial gap between flame tube and mixing chamber.

  8. Check mixing chamber for cracks.

  9. Check overlap between mixing chamber and inner casing for wear and grooves.

  10. Check radial gap between mixing chamber and inner casing.

  11. Check inner casing for corrosion.

  12. Check gap circumference between inner casing and first stage turbine stator blades.

  13. Check stage 1 turbine stator blades for impact of foreign bodies, corrosion and cracks.

  14. Check radial gap for stage 1 turbine rotor blades.

  15. Close combustion chamber manhole cover.

 

 

Area down stream from turbine

  1. Open manhole cover at exhaust gas duct.

  2. Check radial blade clearances for turbine stage 4 rotor blades.

  3. Check exhaust gas duct for cracks and dirt accumulation.

  4. Close manhole cover at exhaust gas duct.

  5. Return work order.

  6. Arrangement with operation department to make compressor wash.
