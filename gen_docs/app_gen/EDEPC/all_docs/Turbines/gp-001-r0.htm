Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every 12000 operation hours as indicated by H 12000 in the above title block.

The fuel natural gas piping to supply the fuel to the fuel nozzles of GT frame 5

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, earplug, and gloves pairs...etc

  • Unit out of service.

  • Fire fighting system should be isolated.

  • Fuel system should be isolated.

  • Oil system should be isolated.

  • All switches in motor control center MCC panel should be isolated.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Remove one fuel gas piping.

  2. Remove fuel gas nozzle and blank out let ends of gas manifold pigtails with blind flanges.

  3. Blank off inlet to gas strainer using a flange drilled and tapped in its center for 1/ 4 inch.

  4. Connect 1/ 4 inch tubing from a single ( 0 – 500 PSI ) range pressure regulator to the tapped hole  in the flange installed on the fuel gas manifold.

  5. Provide a source of clean and dry air pressure.

  6. Remove the three pressure gages from the gas stop/control valve and plug the connections also disconnect the pressure switch and plug the connection.

  7. Open pressure regulator and raise pressure in piping to 280 PSI.

  8. Soap – suds all flanged, screwed and welded connections and joints and inspect for leaks.

  9. After all leaks have been corrects, gradually raise test pressure to 350 Psi and hold for 10 minutes, if no leaks develop, increase test pressure to 400 PSI hold the system for 3 to 5 minutes

  10. At the test pressure of 400 Psi soap – suds all joints fittings and welds and re-inspect for leaks.

  11. Remove blanking flanges and reconnect gauges and pressure switch.

  12. Install fuel gas nozzle.
