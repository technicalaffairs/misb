Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every 2000 equivalent operating hours EOH as indicated in the title block.

The water vapor separator separates the water vapor in the air of compressor and drain it .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets shoes, gloves, … etc

  • Turbine must be shut down.

  • The water vapor separator must be isolated mechanically by closing inlet and outlet.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluids (WD 40 – Kerosine – Turbosol).

  • Rags.

  • Compressor up to 10 bar.

  • Boiled Water.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle the water vapor separator

  2. Pull off the water vapor separator out side it's position.

  3. Clean Pipes and hoses by blowing through.

  4. Clean the inside filter of water vapor separator by any suitable agents (Kerosene or Turbosol or benzene).

  5. Assembly the water vapor separator with its, inside gaskets

  6. Insert the water vapor separator in its correct position.

  7. Tighten the water vapor separator from two sides in it's pipe by two hexagonal nuts using wrench 41.

  8. Check the leakage and treat it by re-tightening the water Vapor Separator.
