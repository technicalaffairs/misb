Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in above title block.

The oil system of turbine is used for lubricating and distributing circuits

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance stuff should be carrying the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the oil pump motor.

  • Be sure oil system of turbine must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge,

  • Rags

Work to be Carried Out 

Oil system of Turbine in Operation

  1. Check the oil level in main tank and analyze it and determine what to do.

  2. Check the pressure of oil and compare it with the setting value and determine what to do.

Oil system of Turbine out of Operation

  1. Check and calibrate oil pressure gauges.

  2. Check the oil level in main tank.

  3. Make test for oil main tank and add or change it (if it is needed).

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tight it (if it is needed).

  5. Check glands of valves for leaking and replace them if it is needed.

  6. Check flanges for leaking.

  7. Check the pipes casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).
