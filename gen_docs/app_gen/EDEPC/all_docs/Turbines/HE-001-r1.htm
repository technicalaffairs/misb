Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet and shoes.

  • Isolate the cooling water heat exchanger by closing the inlet and out let valve.

  • Drain the heat exchanger.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Air Compressor.

  • Rags.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Unbolt water boxes bolts.

  2. Dismantle the water boxes.

  3. Remove the used (old) gasket and replace with a new gasket if necessary.

  4. Clean all tubes compressed air and water.

  5. Clean water boxes by brush and water.

  6. Inspect tube leakage by pressurizing the heat exchanger and blocking any pipe leaks.

  7. Inspect water boxes coating and repair if needed.

  8. Reassemble the water boxes.
