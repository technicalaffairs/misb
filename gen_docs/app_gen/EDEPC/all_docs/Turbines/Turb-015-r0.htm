Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.   

Technical specification of Steam Turbine:

Nominal Output at Steam Quantity Rate                 670 t/h

At Oil Combustion                                                206 MW

At Gas combustion                                                210 MW

Nominal Turbine Speed                                       3000 rpm

Nominal Steam Pressure                                     16.42 Mpa

Nominal Steam Temperature                                  530 °C

Nominal Reheating Steam Pressure                        4 Mpa

Nominal Temperature of Cooling Water                  27 °C

Critical Speed of HP Rotor                                 2400 rpm

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should be trained and carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Shutdown the unit.

  • Generator should be isolated.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of generator

  • Disconnect the electric terminals of generator

  • The oil of turbine must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure turbine must be completely drainage

Preparation

  1. Reading during the normal operation before overhaul.

  2. To follow continuously the technical condition of the turbo-generator.

  3. To secure a permanent measurement of the rotor vibration.

  4. To secure the measurement of the foundation distortion by civil department. The measurement interval is selected according to the foundation behavior.

  5. To secure the check of the creeping of the material.

  6. To secure the necessary spare parts with regard to the cycles between the repairs.
