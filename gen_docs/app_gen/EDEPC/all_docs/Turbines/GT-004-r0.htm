Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every 20000 EOH as indicated by H 20000 in the title block

Technical Specification

  • Type of unit: GT 9 Dual

  • Type of Fuel: Gas or fuel Oil (Dual Fuel)

  • No of Combustion Chamber: One

  • No of Turbine Stage:  Four.

  • No. of Compressor stages: Fifteen.

The first draft initiated & reviewed by eng. Mostafa Hamza, accepted by general manager eng. Hamdy Elmelegy; revised by EDEPC inspection and quality control eng. Abouda Elbana.

This document accepted by eng. Samir Abdel Gilil: EDEPC MPIS coordinator, and certified by accountant Mahmoud Mohamed Soliman EDEPC admin. & financial inspection, and quality control general director and supervisor of inspection & quality control's head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, maintenance stuff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, breathing mask, ... etc.

  • Turbine must by shut down.

  • Fuel system (solar - gas) gas turbine out of service.

  • Cooling Water system of gas turbine out of service.

  • Isolate the step – up transformer and it’s Circuit Breaker and the service transformer for the unit.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of all pump motors and disconnect the electrical terminal of all pump motors.

  • Fire fighting system must be out of service.

 

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt ... etc.

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluids (WD40-Remover).

  • Feeler gauge – fernier caliper – Inside Micrometer – outside Micrometer

  • Grinding machine for blades

  • Crane 80 tons and 25 tons

  • Jacks (36.55….) mm.

  • Hammers, Rags, Vibrometer, Dial indicator

  • Chain Hoist (tackle).

  • Time schedule.

  • Recommended spare parts.

  • Tools and special tools.

  • Man power and maintenance equipment.

  • Shaft stand.

  • All measurement of unit are during normal operation.

 Work to be Carried out

Disassembly

  1. Close the manual fuel valve and manual gas valve.

  2. Dismantle the pin of CO2 cylinder inside the unit for fire fighting  system.

  3. Disconnect the electrical circuits of all lubricated oil, water, fuel, flame monitor, Blow off valves and so on ...

  4. Dismantle the bolts of piece No. 1 for intake of air compressor and rise it by crane 25 ton

  5. Dismantle and rise the ceiling of unit using crane 25 ton

  6. Dismantle the bolts of piece No 2 for intake of air compressor and rise it by crane 25 ton

  7. Dismantle the bolts of Blow off valves room guardian and rise it by crane 25 ton

  8. Close the blow off valves using 3.5 bar pressurized air and fix the closure using pieces of wood 

  9. Dismantle the bolts of burner crane and rise it by crane 25 ton

  10. Dismantle the bolts of burner, gas pipes, fuel pipes, and non return valves of burner, then rise the burner by crane 25 ton

  11. Dismantle the two flame monitors on the ladder's sides of combustion chamber.

  12. Dismantle and rise all pipes of combustion chamber.

  13. Dismantle and rise thermal block of the unit.

  14. Dismantle and rise propane gas cylinder outside the unit.

  15. Dismantle and rise the piece no. 3 for intake of air compressor using crane 25 ton

  16. Dismantle and rise the blow off valves using crane 25 ton

  17. Close the open place of blow off valves in the upper cylinder by plug ( face – piece ).

  18. Dismantle and rise the flame monitor at the ceiling of unit .

  19. Measure the radial overlapping clearance (S) and length (B), Diameter, and circumference and record it in measuring form No 3.    

  20. Measure the radial overlapping clearance (SA) and length (B), Diameter, and circumference and record it in measuring form No 4.

  21. Measure the movable compressor Row 1 clearance, the movable turbine Row 1 clearance and movable turbine Row 4 clearance and record it in measuring form No 2 .

  22. Move the finned segment of upper cylinder to back in direction of Exhaust.

  23. Measure clearance (S) as indicating in form No 1 and record it.

  24. Lifting the flange between the end of lower cylinder and beginning of stack using 5 ton jack.

  25. Dismantle the bolts which tighten between combustion chamber and upper cylinder,   then Lifting the combustion chamber using crane (80 ton).

  26. Measure the clearance of GT Row 1, and GT Row 4 and record it in measuring form   No 2

  27. Dismantle and lifting the ladder of combustion chamber.

  28. Dismantle and lifting the isolation about the upper part of combustion chamber.

  29. Dismantle and lifting the upper cylinder using crane 80 ton.

  30. Measure clearance (S) again and record it in measuring form No 1.

  31. Cut the inside welding between the two halves of hot gas casing (H.G.C).

  32. Dismantle and lifting upper part of hot gas casing using crane 25 ton.

  33. Measure the clearance of GT Row 1, and GT Row 4, and record it in measuring form No. 2

  34. Dismantle the bolts of coupling between GT rotor and intermediate rotor of gear box.

  35. Dismantle and lifting the upper part of combustion chamber using crane 25 ton

  36. Dismantle and lifting the cone of combustion chamber using crane 25 ton

  37. Dismantle and lifting the first tile carrier of combustion chamber using crane 25 ton

  38. Pull off six pins of combustion chamber to outside for 11 cm. using special tool then lifting the second and third tile carrier with container of them using crane 25 ton

  39. Dismantle and lifting the tiles ( finned segments ) from its carriers .

  40. Dismantle and lifting the upper part of blade carrier using crane 25 ton

  41. Dismantle and lifting the upper part of compressor diffuser using crane 25 ton

  42. Measure the clearance (S) and record it in measuring form No 1.

  43. Measure the blade and stuffing box clearances and record it in measuring form No 6.

  44. Measure the Bearing clearances and record it in measuring form no 8.

  45. Measure the clearance of coupling between shaft turbine and intermediate rotor  ( at gear box side ) and record it in measuring form No 16, vertical clearance and horizontal clearance and record it in  measuring form No 13.
    to Measure the Clearance Carryout the following Steps:
       a)     Tight Two jacks in the flange of gear box then the flange of gear box move 16 cm in direction of gear box
       b)     Connect the DC pump.
       c)     Connect the jacking pump of rotor.
       d)     Turn the Rotor in four position (0o , 90o  ,180o , 270o ).
       e)     Measure the clearance in four position and take the average and record it in measuring form No 16.

  46. Dismantle and lifting the upper bearing of turbine, compressor, and thrust.

  47. Rising the shaft of turbine using special tool (lifting rotor) with crane 80 ton and take it outside the unit

  48. Measure the blade and labyrinth clearance and record it in measuring  form No 10.

  49. Dismantle and Lifting the lower part of compressor diffuser using crane 25 ton

  50. Dismantle and Lifting the lower part of blade carrier using crane 25 ton

  51. Dismantle and lifting the lower part of hot gas carrier using crane 25 ton

  52. Dismantle all oil pipes of turbine bearing, compressor bearing and the two air pipes of stuffing box.

  53. Dismantle and lifting the lower bearing of turbine, compressor thrust and pedestals of them.

  54. Dismantle and lifting the lower part of stuffing box.

  55. Dismantle and lifting the cover of gear box by crane 25 ton

Inspection and Repair

  1. Inspect the upper part of blade carrier by vision after reversing it, using crane 25 ton for cracks, pitting, and so on  ….  

  2. Take the inlet Segment of blade carrier to outside and inspect it by vision for cracks, pitting, wear, and so on ….., if it needed to change, you must change it. 

  3. Inspect the blades for cracks, pitting, wear, and so on ….., and study the history of blades, if they are new at the last overhaul  do not change them, unless if we find some of them is not good we change them. If the blades at the last overhaul are recommended, we must change them.

Changing the Blades:

  1. For stationary blades, we take the blades for different stages outside the blade carrier using copper  bar ( 40 mm x 50 cm ) and hummer, then by the same manner install the new one and install some of shims between some blades according to clearance of expansion.

  2. for moving blades, we take the blades for different stages outside the rotor by taking off the welding of main lock and others locks, then take the old one outside the rotor  and install the new one on the rotor and weld the main lock and others.

  1. If we change the blades, we change the fiber mineral twin for second and third stationary stages  also change the sealing of  second stationary stage and all steel band for second, third, and forth stage.    

  2. If we change the blades, we inspect the stationary and moving heat shield segment and change the pieces which we need to change, to change the stationary heat shield segments loose the cylinder pin of these pieces and use the copper bar and hammer for taking these pieces outside the blade carrier, then put the new one using also copper bar and hammer to put these pieces inside blade carrier and tight it by new cylinder pin.

  3. Clean the bolts, studs, nuts and so on … using remover.

  4. Take off the balancing bolts outside plane 1 and plane 2, and taping the holes of them in two planes.

  5. Change the thermal block under the pedestal of bearing 1 by a new one .

  6. Check the two pipes of air for stuffing box of bearing 1 and change the gasket before and after the orifice of air for every pipe, then tight it in its position.    

  7. Change the expansion studs of compressor diffuser and their cylinder pins by new ones.

  8. Inspect the segment complete of hot gas casing by vision, and change it if needed.

  9. Inspect the segment sup. Ring complete of hot gas casing by vision and change it if needed.

  10. Tight the two halves of turbine and compressor bearing and measure its diameter in three positions from each side using the internal micrometer, if the readings inside the tolerance of bearing diameter, the bearing is good.   

  11. Dismantle the two halves of turbine and compressor bearing, and inspect them against cracks using detecting spray crack, if there is crack we must change the bearing.

  12. Inspect the tile ( finned segment ) carrier of combustion chamber , weld and for the legs grind the holes of tiles until the width of hole from up and down like to 11,6 – 11,7 mm.

  13. Inspect the finned segment for combustion chamber using vernire caliper, if the depth of wear in the piece is 4mm or more, this piece is rejected and change it.

  14. Inspect the gear wheels by vision and measure its clearances and record it in form No 7. 

Measuring the Clearance

  1. Clean the gear wheels using tinier and good rags.

  2. Use two indicators with magnetic bottom for gear A &B.

  3. Adjust the reading of two indicators to zero reading.

  4. Operate the jacking pump for lifting the intermediate shaft.

  5. Move the intermediate shaft simultaneously.    
    Take the reading of two indicators and calculate the difference between the two readings, this difference is the backlash between A&B.

  6. Repeat the steps from 1 to 6 for gears C&D.

  7. Repeat the steps from 1 to 6 for gears E&F.

  8. Repeat the steps from 1 to 6 for gears E&G.

  9. Record all readings in measuring form no 7.

  1. Paint the surface between fixed part of gear box and its cover by hylmar or loxiel 59 – 10 to make like a gasket to prevent the escape of oil, and then tight the two parts together.

  2. Inspect the hot gas casing using detector of cracks, then weld and grind the cracks, if the hot gas casing is very bad we change it

  3. Grind the inside old welding position of hot gas casing.

  4. Tight the upper and lower part of hot gas casing using the old bolts , then measure the readings B (Locking piece thickness for compressor diffuser) &B (slot thickness of compressor diffuser), C ( Locking piece thickness for blade carrier ) & C ( slot thickness of blade carrier) and A ( distance from lower locking piece of blade carrier to upper locking    piece of compressor diffuser), record it In form 11, and determine from these readings how we can assembly the two parts of hot gas casing :

    1. Very easy assembly (slots and locking piece parallel to each other)

    2. Easy assembly normal (small force to assemble like rattle by hand)

    3. Hard assembly (Hard to assembly like with a lever, arc bar, etc)

    4. Very hard assembly (very hard assembly like with the old of jacks etc ).

  5. Dismantle the two parts of hot gas casing.

  6. Inspect the inlet segment for blade carrier by observation and change some of these pieces if needed .

  7. Inspect the cover of shims for two bearing 1,2, if there is wear in it we increase this cover by welding then turn it .

  8. Inspect the paths of oil for bearing 1,2 against leakage and sure that they are good

  9. Measure the over lapping between tile carrier ring and combustion chamber inner liner and record it in form No. 3.

  10. Clean all meeting surface before installation using wire brush and oil stone.

  11. Change all gaskets.

  12. Inspect the blow-off valves against 1eakage, if there is 1eakage change the set of O-ring; also inspect the limit switch of it if it is not good change it.

  13. Inspect all flame monitor of unit change all gasket and change the glasses of them, and weld the body of them if needed.

  14. Inspect the injection nozzle fuel against leakage using pressure 100 bar if it is not good, change it.

  15. Inspect the porcelain isolation of joint for flam upper spark plug to lower spark plug if any part from this joint is not good, change it.

  16. Clean the hot gas casing manually.

  17. Make surge using compressed air of unit.

Assembly

  1. Install and fix the cover of gear box by crane 25 ton.

  2. Install and fix the lower part of stuffing box.

  3. Install and fix the lower bearing of turbine, compressor, thrust and pedestal of them.

  4. Install all oil pipes of turbine bearing, compressor bearing and the two air pipes of stuffing box.

  5. Install and fix the lower part of hot gas casing using crane 25 ton.

  6. Install and fix the lower part of blade carrier using crane 25 ton.

  7. Install and fix the lower part compressor diffuser using crane 25.

  8. Measure the blade – and labyrinth clearances and record it in measuring form No 10.

  9. Install the shaft of turbine using special tool ( lifting rotor ) with crane 80 ton.

  10. Test the Paths of Oil:

    1. Put locking flanges at the end of path bearing No. 1& path bearing No. 2 

    2. Turn DC Oil pump and Jacking pump.

    3. Turn the auxiliary lubricating oil pump 1500 rpm.

    4. Turn the auxiliary lubricating oil pump 3000 rpm.

    5. Turn the unit up to step 2.  

    6. Determine any leakage for oil, and make to stop it .

  11. Install and fix the upper bearing of turbine, compressor, and thrust.

  12. Measure the Clearance of thrust bearing:

  1. Turn the jack pump to float the shaft.

  2. Push the shaft to front up to end in direction of plane l.

  3. Put the pointer of indicator to plane 2 with adjusting the reading to zero.

  4. Push the shaft to back up to end in direction of plane 2.

  5. Take the reading of indicator, clearance of thrust bearing.

  6. Record the clearance reading in form No 8.

  1. Measure the clearance of bearing 1 according to:

  1. Take off a shim from the upper half of bearing, measure its thickness.

  2. Put a lead wire l mm thickness on the cover of bearing shim.

  3. Tight the cover of bearing with its pedestal.

  4. Loose the cover from its pedestal.

  5. Measure the thickness of lead wire.

  6. The difference in thickness between the readings of a & e is clearance of bearing 1.

  7. Record the clearance reading in form No. 8.

  1. Measure the clearance of bearings 2 like steps of measuring the clearance of bearing l, and record the clearance reading in form No. 8.

  2. Install and fix the covers of bearing 1, bearing 2 and thrust .

  3. Measure shaft lift bearing 1, so that put the magnetic table of dial indicator on the pedestal of bearing 1, and the pointer on shaft with zero reading, then turn the jacking pump and the reading is shaft lift bearing 1.

  4. Measure shaft lift bearing 2, so that put the magnetic table of dial indicator on the pedestal of bearing 2, and the pointer on shaft with zero reading , then turn the jacking  pump and the reading is shaft lift bearing 2.

  5. Measure the blade and stuffing box clearances and record it in measuring form No 6.

  6. Measure the clearance ( S ) and record it in measuring form No 1.

  7. Install and fix the upper part of compressor diffuser using crane 25 ton.

  8. Install and fix the upper part of blade carrier using crane 25 ton.

  9. Install and fix the tiles (finned segments) in its carriers (the three tile carriers).

  10. Install the three tile carriers in the basket of them, then put the basket inside the combustion chamber.     

  11. Install six pins of combustion chamber to its position using hummer, then slip the container of tile carriers including them to its position to down using crane 25 ton.

  12. Install and fix the cone of combustion chamber (conical part)on the basket using crane 25 ton.

  13. Measure the clearance of GT Row 1, and GT Row 4, and record measuring form No 2.

  14. Install and fix the upper of hot gas casing using crane 25 ton.

  15. Measure the clearance between hot gas casing and inlet segment of compressor diffuser, and record it in form No 5, if this clearance more than 5 mm we decrease it by welding the inlet segment by electrode UTP068 HH.

  16. Weld the two parts of hot gas casing from inside using electrode UTP068HH.

  17. Measure clearance (S ) and record it in measuring form No 1 .

  18. Release the 5 ton jack lifting which rises the flange between the end of lower cylinder and the beginning of stack.

  19. Make the alignment to shaft of turbine, to make the alignment change in shims of pedestal for bearing one or change the lower cylinder shims, or change the two types of shims together at the same time to change the horizontal clearace  which we measure during dismantle in form 13 up to 0.15 mm, and change in shim of bearing 2 to change  the vertical clearance also measured before  in form 13 up to 1.25 mm .    

  20. Measure the length of coupling bolts before tighten and after tighten, it must be within the limit of tolerance and record it in form 9 C.

  21. Turn the shaft turning for 10 Hrs before the open balance of shaft.

  22. Make the open balance for shaft using vibrometer, magnetic vibration sensor at plane 1, magnetic vibration sensor at plane 2, and speed sensor at plane 2
    To Carry out the Open Balance:

    1. Turn the unit without burning up to speed 1300 rpm and record parameters related to vibration at plane 1&2

    2. Enter the results of step a to vibration program to calculate the value of weight and its angle

    3. Install the weight at plane 2, and turn the unit up to speed 1300 rpm.

    4. Enter the results of step c to vibration program to calculate the new value of weight and its angle.

    5. Install the weight at plane 1, and turn the unit up to speed 1300 rpm.

    6. Enter the results of steps c&e to vibration program to calculate the final weights at plane 1, plane 2, and its angles.

    7. Punching the final weights in its positions

  23. Install and fix the ladder of combustion chamber

  24. Measure the clearance of GT Row 1, and GT Row 4, and record it in measuring form No 2 .

  25. Measure the clearance of coupling between shaft turbine and intermediate rotor ( inside of gear box ) and record it in measuring form No16, vertical clearance and horizontal clearance and record it in measuring form No 13. asfollows: 

  1. Tight two jacks in flange of gear box, the flange of gear box move in direction of gear box

  2. Connect the DC pump.

  3. Connect the jacking pump of rotor.

  4. turn the rotor in four position ( 00  , 900  ,  1800  , 2700  ) .

  5. Measure the clearance in four positions and take the average and record it in measuring form No 16.

  1. Install and fix the upper part of cylinder using crane 80 ton.

  2. Install and fix the combustion chamber using crane 80 ton and tight the bolts between the combustion chamber and upper cylinder.

  3. Return back the finned segment of upper cylinder to front.

  4. Measure clearance (s) and record it in measuring form No. 1.

  5. Measure the clearance of movable compressor Row 1, turbine Row 1 and Row 4, and record it in measuring form no 2

  6. Measure the radial over lapping (SA) and length (B), Diameter, and circumference and record it in measuring form no 4

  7. Measure the radial over lapping clearance ( S ) and length ( B ), Diameter, and circumference and record  it in measuring form no. 3

  8. Install and fix the flame monitor at the ceiling of unit .

  9. Release the plug ( face – piece ) in the upper cylinder for the blow – off valves.

  10. Install and fix the blow – off valves using crane 25 ton.

  11. Install and fix the piece no 3 for entry air of compressor using crane 25 ton.

  12. Install and fix Propane gas inside unit.

  13. Install and fix all pipes of combustion chamber.

  14. Install and fix the two flame monitor on sides the ladder of combustion chamber.

  15. Install and fix the burner using crane 25 ton.

  16. Install and fix gas pipe, Fuel pipe and non return valves of burner.

  17. Install and fix the crane burner using crane 25 ton.

  18. Release the wood pieces which close the blow – off valves, and connect the pipe of air which closes them during working unit.

  19. Install and fix the blow – off valves room guardian using crane 25 ton.

  20. Install and fix the piece No2 for entry air of compressor using crane 25 ton.

  21. Install and fix the ceiling of unit using crane 25 ton.

  22. Install and fix the piece no.1 for entry air of compressor using crane 25 ton

  23. Connect the electricity of all lubricated oil pump, water pump, fuel pump, flame monitor blow off valves and so on ……

  24. Turn the unit up to step 4 for testing leakage of and oil and fuel

  25. Install and fix thermal block of unit.

  26. Install and fix the pin of CO2 cylinder inside the unit for fire fighting system.

  27. Open the manual valves of fuel system ( solar and gas )

  28. Open the valves of cooling water system.

  29. Put fire fighting system in service.

  30. Make the close balance for shaft of unit using vibrometer, magnetic vibration sensor at plane1, magnetic vibration sensor at plane 2, and speed sensor at plane 2. as follows:

  1. Turn the unit until the burning occur and speed reaches to 4582 rpm.

  2. Inter the results of step 1 to vibration program to calculate the value of weight and its angle.

  3. Install the weight at plane 2 and turn the unit up speed 4582 rpm.

  4. Inter the results of step 3  to vibration program to calculate the new value of weight and its angle.

  5. Install the weight at plane 1 and turn the unit up to speed 4582 rpm.

  6. Install the results of steps ( 3 & 5 ) to vibration program to calculate the final weights at plane 1, plane 2, ad its angle

  7. Punching the final weights in its positions.

  1. Turn the unit with complete load for one week and observe its behavior.
