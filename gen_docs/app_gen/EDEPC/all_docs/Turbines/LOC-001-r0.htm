Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every four years as indicated by Y4 in the above title block.

LOC is used for cooling oil before oil is supplied to journal bearing of turbine and generator

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Unit out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Crane or hoist

  • Chain block and wire rope

  • Nylon sling ( two or three lines )

  • Eye bolt M24 ( three pieces each)

  • Wooden block for laying under the tube nest

  • Wrench, screw driver ... etc.

Work to be carried out

  1. Measure inlet and outlet temperature of oil and record it

  2. Measure inlet and outlet temperature of cooling water and record it

  3. Stop operation of heat exchanger ( first cut – off fluid of shell flow gradually "oil" and then cooling water).

  4. Drain both fluid left in heat exchanger through drain valves. 

Disassembly

  1. Dismantle the water chamber at both ends

  2. Remove the outer O ring fit to the O ring holder

  3. Remove the O ring holder and also the inner O ring

  4. Use the female  threaded holes provided on the tube sheet, screw in the push up bolts to separate the tube plate from the shell by about 50 mm

  5. Tie the nylon sling around the outer periphery of the fixed tube sheet

  6. Left up the tube sheet to the level where the center line of the shell matches with the center of tube nest

  7. Screw in the eye bolts into the threaded holes in place of push – up bolts and pull laterally with a chain  block and extract the tube nest

  8. When extract the length of about 70% of the overall length, tie another nylon sling around the baffle plate and extract the tube nest to its full length

  9. Upon completion of extraction lay the tube nest on a wooden blocks to protect the cooling tubes and baffle plates

Inspection

  1. Clean the internal surface of the shell

  2. Clean the external surface of the tube with air or light oil

  3. Clean sludge or deposit inside the tube with brushes or tube cleaner

  4. Check tube surface anti scratch and damage

  5. Replace O – rings

Assembly

  1. Sling the tube nest, lift it gradually, insert it in the shell, be sure that the center line of the tube nest matches with the center line of the shell

  2. Be careful to complete the insertion of the tube nest in the shell

  3. Mount the O ring older and the inner O ring

  4. Mount the outer O ring

  5. Assemble the water chamber of both ends

Re Operation

  1. Close drain valves

  2. Flow cooling water and confirm that no problem in the tube side pass

  3. Flow oil gradually in the shell side

  4. After the by pass filled with fluid air vent on each side are closed

  5. Check oil temperature ( in and out)

  6. Check cooling water temperature ( in and out )
