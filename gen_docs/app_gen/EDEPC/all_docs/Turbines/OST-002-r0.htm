Introduction

This document outlines the the major maintenance procedure P1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in above title block.

The oil system of turbine is used for lubricating and distributing circuits

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance stuff should be carrying the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the oil pump motor.

  • Be sure oil system of turbine must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge,

  • Rags

Work to be Carried Out 

Oil system of Turbine in Operation

  1. Check the oil level in main tank and analyze it and record them.

  2. Check the pressure of oil and record them.

Oil system of Turbine out of Operation

  1. fill the oil  into the central oil – handling system . 

  2. filter The oil and separate centrifuge .

  3. Remove the un-tightness of the piping and fittings .

  4. Clean the magnetic filters in the system of lubrication oil in front of the bearing stands.

  5. Clean the filters of turbine bearings.

  6. Clean the screens in the main oil tank .

  7. Discharge the mud  of the main oil tank .

  8. Check the glands of the oil pumps and their tightening if necessary.

Inspection

  1. Check The validity of the oil system of turbine.

  2. Check all parts for any worn-out and replace them by new ones.

Assembly

  1. Replace the oil piping including the fittings of defective fittings.

  2. Remove the impurities and possible residues of water from the dead spaces of the waste piping and the bottoms of the boxes and from the siphon of the turbine and generator connecting system .

  3. Clean oil will be re-pumped into the reserve tank where it is prepared for filling into the turbine oil system .

  4. The oil filling must be send for regeneration and a new filling must be used .  (the oil service life is usually 30 – 35 thousand hours)

  5. Test the Oil  during the first months of operation , we recommend to check oil at least twice in a shift .

  6. Check regularly water content in oil. For a permanent operation of the set will be allowed 0.2% water in oil. Percentage water content should not exceed max. 1%.

 

Starting of Oil System of Turbine

  1. Check the oil level in main tank.

  2. Check the pressure of oil.

  3. Open the inlet for cooling the oil.

  4. Check all valves of oil piping are open.

Attendance and Checking During Operation

  1. The temperature of oil should not exceed 75°C

  2. The oil level in main tank according to the mark on the oil gauge

  3. The temperature of cooling water at discharge.
