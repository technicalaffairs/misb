Introduction

This document out lines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block.

The function of this fan to draw – off steam – air mixture of steam glands exhaust of turbine.

Technical specification:

Design                         Horizontal Centrifugal Fan

Fan capacity                 0.4 m3/s

Pressure                       5130 Pa

Fan speed                    2900 rpm

Bearing type                 2311K ( clamping bush H2311 )

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the fan motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the fan motor.

  • The fan must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure fan must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller, Rags

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge.

Work to be Carried Out

Fan in Operation

  1. Checks discharge quantity and pressure and record them.

  2. Check the vibration level of the fan set complete with the motor then, record them

Fan out of Operation

  1. Remove the protective cover of the coupling.

  2. Check alignment between motor and fan.

  3. Shift electric motor away from the coupling.

  4. Disconnect suction mouth and flange of cover on a spiral box.

  5. Remove covers of the bearing brackets after unscrewing the locking nut.

  6. Dismantling the bearings with a removable tapered (sleeve adapter or a tapered sleeve is as easy as their fitting).

  7. Take out carefully the whole rotor from spiral box and shift it towards the motor.

  8. loose the bolt tightening impeller on a shaft .

  9.  Pull down the impeller by a puller (the puller will be fixed in two threaded holes in the impeller hub) and hang it onto a hoisting equipment .

  10. Pull down the coupling half from the shaft with a puller.

  11. List of defects found out during the fan operation.

Inspection

  1. The validity of the anti-friction ball bearings.

  2. Check all parts for any worn-out and replace them by new ones.

  3. Inspect the run-out of shafts (allowance 0.03 mm).

Assembly

  1. Smear all threads with grease or with MOLYKA, if it is working in aggressive environment and in fan located on open – air sites.

  2. Mounting the impeller on the shaft end of the fan.

  3. Tighten firmly runner nut.

  4. Install the bearings with a removable tapered in their position on the clean shaft.

  5. Mounting the coupling half on the shaft

  6. Mounting carefully whole rotor in the spiral box and tighten it.

  7. Adjusting the axial clearance of fan by shifting the whole rotor and fixed it with space washer.

  8. Install the covers of the bearings and seal them against the bracket.

  9. Connecting the suction mouth and flange of cover on a spiral box.

  10. After completing the assembly alignment the flexible coupling of the fan and the motor (allowance 0.05 mm).

  11. Put the protective cover of the coupling

Starting of Fan

  1. Check the direction of rotation of fan before starting the fan for the first time.

  2. Check the quantity of grease in the bearings sump.

  3. Check whether the delivery sluice valve is closed.

  4. Open the delivery valve gradually after the set has been started to operate at its full speed.

Attendance and checking during Operation

  1. The temperature of the anti-friction bearing in the bearing top which should not exceed 75°C.

  2. The vibration level of the fan set complete with the motor.

  3. The discharge quantity and pressure.
