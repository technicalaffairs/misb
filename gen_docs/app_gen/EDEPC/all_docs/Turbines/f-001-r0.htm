Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed when Δ P = 0.6 bar orafter 150 Op.Hrs as indicated in the above titled block.

 

The first draft is initiated by eng. Saad Samra, tested by eng. Mohamed Abdel Azeem at 28 June 2003, accepted by by General Manager eng. Mohamed El-Sarrag, revised by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Abouda El-Bana, and approved by eng. Moneer Mahmoud El-Wakeel Damietta CCPS Head Sector.


Technical Specifications

 

Manufacturer

Siemens.

Type of the filter

Magnetic Filter.

Suction Pressure

4 bar

Discharge Pressure

140 bar
Temperature 40 ° C.

 

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, and safety shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the filter mechanically by closing inlet & outlet valves.

  • De-aerate the filter by opening de-aerator valve before opening.

  • Drain the filter by opening the drain valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance set.

  • Compressed air hose.

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags…etc).

  • Soft brushes.

  • Magnetic pick-up flexible.

  • Spray gun.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the differential pressure. (0 bar)

  2. Dismantle the filter cover.

  3. Pull off the filter to clean it.

  4. Clean the filter.

  5. Replace the filter if it is required.

  6. Replace the gasket if it is required.

  7. Insert the filter in its correct position.

  8. Close the filter cover with gasket.

  9. Close the drain valve.

  10. Refill the oil filter with oil.

  11. Breath the air from the filter by opening the de-aerate valve, then close it.

  12. Open the inlet & outlet valves.

  13. Check the leakage and treat it by retightening the filter if it is required.
