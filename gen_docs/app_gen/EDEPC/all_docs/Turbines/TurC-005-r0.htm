Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be carried out in case of 10% plugging of the total tubes as indicated in the title block.

The function of the condenser is to condensate the outlet of turbine to complete water.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety ID category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs … etc.

  • Isolate the condenser mechanically by closing inlet and outlet valves.

  • Be sure condenser must be completely drained.

  • Scaffolding.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tools set.

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Cleaning fluid, anti seizing grease.

  • Vibrometer, feeler gauge, dial indicator.

  • Rope and wooden blocks.

  • Copper hummer.

 

Work to be carried out

General

Maintenance procedures aiming to guarantee the perfect efficiency of the components involved in the condensing system should be strictly observed as a matter of fact a decrease in efficiency of failure of any of these units will adversely effect the condenser performance. 

Inspection

  1. Carefully check for all condenser valves.

  2. Stuffing box of suction pumps

  3. Valves in vacuum service.

  4. Gasket for flange point.

  5. Tubes of condenser.

  6. This inspection determine the extent of corrosion and decrease periodic inspection to condenser and tubes.

Tube Removal

In case of re-change of some tubes due to its defection these tools must be available:

  • Milling cutter for tubes facing and beveling and for removal of welding material.

  • Tube wall reducing tools.

  • Tube collapsing tool

  • Tube knockout tool

  1. Remove the inlet and return water boxes or if is feasible open the hinged / bolted covers.

  2. Remove the seal welding if present with tool (1).

  3. Remove the expanded ends of the tube to a depth of approximately three fourth's the sheet thickness using tool (2)

  4. Collapse the tube ends with tool (3) to relax the tube to tube sheet joint exercise care to prevent damage to the tube seat in the tube plate.

  5. Insert the tool (4) and drift the tube free of the tube sheet in the direction of the inlet or flared end.

Reassembly

When few tubes are to be replaced the tools available through commercial channels required for tube installing are:

  1. Tube guide tool.

  2. Milling culler for tubes facing and beveling and for removal of welding material.

  3. Tube expanding tool.

  4. Tube welding machine.

Tube Installing

  1. Tube end and tube sheets shall be carefully cleaned and any presser votive removed with suitable solvent any corrosion is to be removed with fine emery cloth. It is fundamental that the contact surfaces be free of all foreign material.

  2. Use tool (1) to guide the tube through the tube sheet and the tube support. Maintain a contact axial force on tube as it moves into the condenser. If the tube meets positive resistance at the support plates rotate the tube while maintaining the axial force be careful not to score the tube set the ends to project from the tube sheet as per welding and expanding tube – tube sheet procedure shall be used.

  3. The tube is now ready to be joined with tube sheet. The welding and expanding tube – tube sheet procedure shall be used.

  4. Install all bolts for entering and exiting the condenser.

  5. Close the drain of condenser, then the condenser become ready  to operation.
