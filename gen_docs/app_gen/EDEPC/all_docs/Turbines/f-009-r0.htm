Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed when ΔP = 1 bar as indicated in the above titled block

These strainers are used to purify the water, which drawn from the Nile River and transfer to the steam turbines auxiliaries as heat exchanger, closed cooling pump, and vacuum pump from impurities.

65 Cο.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

    •

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the strainer mechanically by close inlet & outlet valves.

  • De-aerate the strainer by opening de-aerator valve before opening.

  • Drain the strainer by opening the drain valve.

  • Be sure that the strainer is completely drained and (Δ P = 0).

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance set.

  • Water hose.

  • Special fixture for pulling off the strainer.

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags… etc).

  • Soft brushes, Spray gun.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle the strainer cover.

  2. Pull off the strainer by its special fixture to clean it.

  3. Clean the strainer by water jet.

  4. Fire the strainer by benzene to get rid of any plastics or rags if it is needed.

  5. Replace the gasket if it is required.

  6. Insert the strainer in its correct position.

  7. Close the strainer cover with gasket.

  8. Close the drain valve.

  9. Open the inlet  & outlet valves.

  10. Breath the air from the strainer by opening the de-aerate valve, then close it.

  11. Check the leakage and treat it by re-tightening the strainer.
