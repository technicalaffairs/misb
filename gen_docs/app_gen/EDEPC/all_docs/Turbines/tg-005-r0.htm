Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspections I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in above title block.

The device used for slowly motion speed: 1/8 revolution per 3 minute. The ratchet turning gears is used for turning the turbine rotor after shutdown and reach its speed to zero.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance stuff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor (turning gear motor).

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the turning gear motor.

  • Isolate the turning gear mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Ensure pumps must be completely drained.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller.

  • Feeler gauge, Vibrometer

  • Rags

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check and record the discharge quantity & pressure of ratchet turning gear pump

  2. Check and record the vibration level for ratchet turning gear

  3. Check and adjust clearance between mating gears not exceed 0.25 mm)

  4. Check bearing clearance (0.0015 x shaft diameter)

During Operation

  1. Check the vibration level for ratchet turning gear.

  2. Check the lube oil pressure and level of oil tank
