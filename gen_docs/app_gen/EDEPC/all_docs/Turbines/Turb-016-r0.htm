Introduction

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Shutdown the unit.

  • Generator should be isolated, ''isolate the circuit breaker of generator''

  • Disconnect the electric terminals of generator

  • The oil of turbine must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure turbine must be completely drainage.

  7. Prepare the the locating bars.

  10. Prepare the supports for putting guide wheels.

  11. Prepare the working tables and bench vices.

  12. Prepare the bath for cleaning of small details.

  13. Preparing of gas welding sets.

  14. Prepare the argon welding sets.

The set Measurement before Inspection

  1. After dismounting the covers from bearing stands indicate the couplings are connected by fitted bolts, the rotors must be cold and temperature should not exceed 25 – 30 °C.

  2. Loosen the couplings and dismount fitted bolts, check the rotor alignment in the couplings, fig. 9

  3. Measure the run out of rotor MP fig. 1  

  4. Measure the axial clearance between moving and guide wheels – Fig. 10.

  5. Measure the axial and radial clearance of labyrinth rings in the casings and guide wheels fig. 5.

  6. Measure the axial and radial clearance of stuffing box in the casings fig. 2 , 3 , 4.

  7. The upper and lateral clearance of journal bearings fig. 13.

  8. Inspect the rotor alignment in the couplings (casings are lifted) – compare with   covered casings. Now set the rotors into working position and set the couplings one on to the other, measure them.

  9. After removal of rotors, insert a check shaft into turbine casings, set it according to the values from rotor alignment with a recess of bearing stands and measure the alignment of seal bodies and of guide wheels in a shaft of medium-pressure part

  10. Check by measuring the leveling of outer casings (outer MP rear 1.07 mm : inner MP – 1.07 mm ).

  11. Measuring of MP diaphragm wheel in open position fig. 10.

  12. MP upper outer casing put to down part and measuring of parting plane deformation --> Max. deformation --> 0.50 mm fig. 14.

  13. Tight of MP outer casing --> new measuring of diaphragm between 0.01 mm – 0.09 mm.

  14. Putting of check shaft to MP casing

  15. Measuring the positions of diaphragm and glands for adjusting of parts of MP upper casing, fig. 10

  16. Measuring of radial and axial clearances of MP through flow parts fig. 6.

  17. Before bulling out the rotor measure alignment with a recess in bearing stands and check the fitting of shaft fig. 10.

External Inspection of the Dismounted Turbine Parts

  1. Wheel No 9 of MP rotor is missing one segment of bandage above 5 pcs of blades.

  2. Checking of rings pos. 51¸55 of measuring sound for temperature of MP inner casing is very important!

  3. 1 pcs. of ring was free located on stuffing box during Dismantling of MP outer casing.

  4. Bolt pos. 79 – Mat. 15.320.5 was defected during oxygen cutting of nut pos. 91 on MP intercepting valve – left side must be change.

  5. After sand blasting of MP rotor we made carryout the Capilar test on blades – moving wheel No. 9

  6. Rotors – check whether bearing pins and plates are not damaged. Check whether blades of moving wheels are not bent, split or injured by foreign bodies or cracks.

  7. Turbine casings – check whether parting plane is not deformed and damaged. Check possible cracks of turbine casings.

  8. Guide wheels – check whether guide blades are not bent, split, or broken.

  9. Radial and axial bearings – check whether antifriction metal is not worn out, released or broken.

  10. Labyrinth rings – check whether edges are not worn out or split in the seal bodies and in guide wheels. The strips should not be cut off by labyrinth rings in a rotor.

  11. Next inspection e.g. of control bodies will be carried out in the course of dismounting and repair.

  12. Inspection of flap valves no. 6 ( stick of flap – unmoved ).

  13. Checking of oil system of turbine --> according impurities in filter – front stand and clean complete system include oil coolers and small dropping tank.

  14. The check of analog sensors of turbine.

  15. Check the thermometers of turbine.

  16. The check the bearing brasses by:

    1. The adhesion of the lining by a capillary method ( color detection liquid ) between the bearing metal and the supporting material, rare indications may be allowed when after 5 minutes are over, the color detection liquid will not form a larger color point than ø 6 mm at the developer OK.

    2. The connection of the lining with the steel supporting body of the bearing at the processed surface of the lining by a contact ultra-sound method OK.

Repairing

  1. Grading of MP steam flanges on connection pipes.

  2. Clean the parting plane bolts and prepare for checking.

  3. Clean and repair the diaphragm wheels, carries, and glands – upper casings.

  4. Grind the MP emergency stop valves cones and flanges.

  5. Grind the flanges – MP intercepting valves.

  6. Inspect and clean all drainage lines from MP parts of turbine including drainage system of main steam lines.

  7. Clean the inner parts of MP casings.

  8. Clean the MP casings, wheels, glands.

  9. Clean the parting planes of carries.

  10. Drilling – out bolt 42 ´ 3 mm in parting plane of inner MP casing.

  11. Clean the MP rotor by sand blasting (very fine piece of abrasive - granularity 1200) and checking crack by color detection liquid. During the blasting, the sliding surfaces e. g. the rotor pins, the gland parts must be protected to prevent their damage.

  12. Grind all cracks on MP diaphragm wheels out in circuit profile.

  13. Grind – lapping of flange joints on MP upper casing.

  14. Clean the carries of MP upper parts.

  15. Alignment of MP through flow part.

  16. Grind the nickelite seal for MP moving wheels 1a, 2a ( 5 cm ).

  17. Dismantle of bearing no. 3 – STEE sheet --> 5.75 mm.

  18. Clean of bearing stand for bearing No. 3 and 4.

  19. Clean of yokes, wedges and repairing of bearing composite surface for no.3 and 4 (no. 3–new shim).

  20. Clean by air MP casing including erection of all diaphragm and glands.

  21. Adjust the glands to MP upper casing.

  22. Small radial clearances on MP inner casing, so we are grinding the nick line on diaphragms 1a --> 5a.

  23. MP inner casing – repair of radial clearances.

  24. Repair of MP inner gland.

  25. Repair of radial clearance on diaphragms wheels in MP inner casing – upper part.

  26. The closing of MP glands.

  27. Check the thermometers of turbine.

  28. Replace of brass seal rings by new one on the piston ø 320 mm of MP intercepting valve servomotor in workshop.

  29. Clean all parts of turbine and replace the worn part by new one.

  30. Repair all labyrinth rings and replacing it if necessary.
