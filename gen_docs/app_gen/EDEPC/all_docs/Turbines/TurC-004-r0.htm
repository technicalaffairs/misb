Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, to be carried out every five years as indicated by Y5 in the above title block.

This Condenser is used to condensate turbine steam.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs … etc.

  • Stop the cooling water circuit of condenser.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the cooling water pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the cooling water pump motor.

  • The Condenser must be isolated mechanically by closing inlet and outlet valves.

  • Be sure Condenser must be completely drained.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller, Rags.

  • Tube cleaner flexible shaft.

  • Tube expander, Wood plugs.

  • Lighting inside the condenser with 24V voltage

 Work to be carried out

Disassembly

Condenser In Operation

  1. Measure temperature at inlet and outlet water in condenser and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do.

  2. Measure absolute pressure of condenser and compare it with the setting value then determine what to do.

 Condenser out of Operation

 Replacement of Damaged Condenser Tubes

  1. Preparation of workplace – secure the workplace with respect to protection and safety of labor. Provide the connection of electric power and compressed air, ensure lighting inside the condenser with 24 V voltage. Take-over the tools, displace auxiliary material in the workplace, arrange the erection of scaffolding, prepare new tubes to the place of installation .

  2. Discharge cooling water from the condenser water spaces , discharge condensate from the steam space .

  3. Open the condenser by loosening the flanged joints and open the covers of water headers. perform the opening of water headers step by step , according to the place of replacement of the damaged tubes .

  4. Dismantling of defective tubes – tap down the expanded end of tube in one tube plate and eject the tube. Take steps to ensure the prevention of the damaging of openings in the tube at the tube ejecting and its tapping down. After the dismantling of the damaged tubes clean all the openings in the tube plates and check whether there are no longitudinal grooves in them .

Inspection

  1. Preparation of new tubes.

  2. Check the cleanliness of the surface of tubes , the smoothness of the surface – the tube must be without deformations by depressing , without transversal and longitudinal cracks , rubbings , corrosion spots , larger slivers , bubbles or larger metallic inclusions on the surface

  3. Check the length of tubes , whether they correspond to the necessary assembly length of the condenser . Tubes for the steam-air mixture coolers are of different material mostly , the avoiding of their interchanging with the remaining tubes must be ensured

 

Assembly

  1. Assembly of new tubes – clean the tube perfectly, prior to its insertion into the tube plate in the places of expansion, by emery cloth , along the tube diameter, not longitudinally. insert the tube into the openings in the tube plates, expand the tube end in the front tube plate, shorten the other end of the tube and expand the tube in the rear tube plate. Drift the expanded ends, level them with the tube plate and chamfer the edges by angle cutter .

  2. Clean the condenser water spaces

  3. Test of steam space – arrest the supporting springs of the condenser by the turning of the supporting nuts, fill the steam space with water so that all tubes replaced were under water level. check the tightness of the steam space and the quality of the expansion of the individual tubes. Upon the inspection preformed discharge water from the steam space and loosen the supporting spring down the nuts to the original position .

  4. Condenser  closing – dismantle the scaffolding, check the tightness of the condenser covers, in the event of damaging replace the old packing by a new one . clean the contact surfaces thoroughly prior to the new packing mounting and stick on the new packing. Close the covers of water headers, tighten the flanged joints. Check the tightness of the condenser water space at the putting of the cooling water circuit into operation .

  5. Workplace liquidation – clean the workplace, remove the auxiliary material, check the tools and put them to their place , hand-over the works performed to the customer.

Starting of Condenser

  1. Check the front and rear manhole of condenser are closing.

  2. Check the nuts under the condenser are down to release the condenser against moving.

  3. Filling the condenser to the normal operation level.

  4. Check the cooling water circuit of condenser is operating.

Attendance and Checking during Operation

  1. Check the level condensate water in condenser.

  2. Check the temperature of inlet cooling water in condenser.

  3. Check the temperature of outlet cooling water in condenser.

  4. Check the absolute pressure in condenser.
