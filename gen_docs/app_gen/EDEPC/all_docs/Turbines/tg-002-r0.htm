Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in above title block.

This device used for slowly motion Speed: 3 rpm. The turning gear is used for turning the turbine rotor after shut down and reach its zero speed.

Note:

Since the turning gear is working for a limit time at shut down and beginning of operation (i.e. it will not work more than 180 hr/year maximum, so it does not need major maintenance, it needs only just inspection twice  a year or even if some noise is heard from it.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance stuff should be carrying the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Shutdown the unit.

  • Shut off turning gear and lube oil pump.

  • Disconnect feeding current for turning gear motor and disconnect motor terminals.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • Filler gauge

  • Dial indicator, vibration device

  • Grease gun

  • Soft brush, Oil can.

  • Cleaning articles.

Work to be Carried Out 

Disassembly

  1. Dismantle the bolts of the motor base.

  2. Check clearances between mating gears (Backlash) (not exceed 0.25 mm).

  3. Lift the motor and take it out of its place.

  4. Check gears visually for detecting any scales or cracks in it.

  5. Check bearing clearances (0.0015 x shaft dia.).

Assembly

  1. Cleaning all parts of turning gear

  2. Replace any damaged gears with other new ones (if it needed).

  3. Install the motor of the turning gear in its place.

  4. Check and adjust clearance between mating gears ( not exceed 0.25 mm )

  5. Check bearing clearances ( 0.0015 x shaft dia.)

  6. Connect the motor terminals and clean the place around the work area.

  7. Put the turning gear in operation and check any oil leakage or noise.

Attendance Checking during Operation

  1. Vibration for turning gear

  2. Lube oil pressure and level of oil tank.
