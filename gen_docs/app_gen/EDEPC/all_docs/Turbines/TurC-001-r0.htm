Introduction

This document  outlines  the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed for surface condenser every two months as indicated by M2 in the above title block.

This condenser is used to condensate the wet steam outlet from turbine to water.        

The first draft initiated by eng. Mamdoh Anber, tested by eng. Ali Abd El-Salam and revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Abouda Elbana, accepted by general director eng. Yosry Abd Elmaqsood, and approved by eng. Awad M. Fathay head sector of Talkha Power Station .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Close condenser inlet and outlet cooling water valve.

  • Open condenser cooling water drain valve.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Air Compressor.

  • Rags, Rubber balls.

  • Plastic Sheets.

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Open condenser manholes

  2. Clean All tubes.

  3. Clean water boxes.

  4. Inspect tubes for leakage by using vacuum and plastic sheets.

  5. Block leakage pipes.

  6. Inspect water boxes coating for damage.

  7. Repair damage coating if available.

  8. Change the damage gaskets.
