Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed every 2000 equivalent operating hours EOH as indicated in the title  block the hot gas casing collect the hot gases push it under pressure to move the blades of  turbine with velocity  4582  rpm.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets, shoes, gloves ..  etc

  • Turbine must be shut down

  • Fuel system ( solar – gas ) of gas turbine out of service

  • Isolate the step- up transformer and it's circuit breaker

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of all pump motors and disconnect the electrical terminal of all pump motors

  • Fire fighting system must be out of service

  • Hot gas casing must be in weather temperature

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Ladder

  • Detector fluids for cracks

  • Cleaning fluids ( W D 40 )

  • Rags

  • Crane 25 ton

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Dismantle the burner of unit

  2. Take the burner out side the unit using Crane 25 ton

  3. Put a ladder inside combustion chamber 

  4. Use the ladder to arrive the hot gas casing

  5. Inspect the hot gas casing using detector fluids of cracks

  6. If we have some cracks in the body of hot gas casing 

  7. We weld these cracks using the electrodes UTP 6170 CO.

  8. After we finish from welding take the ladder out side the combustion chamber and clean it 

  9. Install the burner in its position.
