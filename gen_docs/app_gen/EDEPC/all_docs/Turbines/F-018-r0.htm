Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection II, to be carried out on an  annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block or when filter differential pressure is greater than 15 PSI.

Main lube oil Filter function to clean oil from impurities and dirty.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety Category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, eye glass, nose mouth "dust cover" ... etc

  • Turbine must be shutdown.

  • Ensure that the inlet and outlet valves are closed

  • Drain the filter.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Rags, Cleaning Fluid.

Work to be carried out

Changing Filter Cartridge

  1. Close inlet and outlet valves.

  2. Open Drain and vent valves to drain filter completely before removing Cartridges.

  3. Untight the filter cover bolts.

  4. Remove cover and old filter Cartridge.

  5. Clean thoroughly the filter internal surface

  6. Insert new filter Cartridge

  7. Clean cover gasket and gasket seat

  8. Replace new gasket if it is hard or damage

 Reassembly and Pushing Filter in Service

  1. Place the filter cover and tight the cover bolts

  2. Open inlet and outlet valve

  3. Open vent to release air and close it when oil appears.

  4. Observe the differential pressure gauge reading less than 15Psi
