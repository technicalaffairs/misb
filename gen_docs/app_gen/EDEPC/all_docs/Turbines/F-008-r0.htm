Introduction

This document outlines the maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed when Δ P = 0.6 bar

The control oil pump filter is used to purify the oil that used in the oil control pump from dirt and impurities before the entrance to the stop and control valve.

The principle lube oil filter is used to purify the lubricating oil from dirt and impurities before the entrance to the turbine bearings.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

    •

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs.

  • Isolate the filter mechanically by close inlet & outlet valves.

  • De-aerate the filter by opening de-aerator valve before opening.

  • Drain the filter by opening the drain valve.

  • Be sure that the filter is completely drained and (Δ P = 0).

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance set

  • Compressed air hose

  • Cleaning articles (fluid, rags… etc).

  • Soft brushes, Spray gun.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Dismantle the filter cover.

  2. Take the filter out to clean it.

  3. Clean the filter.

  4. Replace the filter if it is required.

  5. Replace the gasket if it is required.

  6. Insert the filter in its correct position.

  7. Close the filter cover with gasket.

  8. Close the drain valve.

  9. Open the inlet  & outlet valves.

  10. Breath the air from the filter by opening the de-aerate valve, then close it.

  11. Check the leakage and treat it by re-tightening the filter.
