Introduction

Light fuel oil Filter cleans the light oil from impurities and dirty.

This document outlines the maintenance procedure P1, which must be performed for the Light fuel oil filter every one year as indicated by Y1 in the title block or when filter differential pressure is greater than 25 PSI .

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category, 

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, eye glass, nose mouth "dust cover" .... etc

    •

  • Ensure that the inlet and outlet valves are closed

  • Drain the filter

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Rags, Cleaning Fluide

Work to be Carried Out

Changing Filter Cartridge

  1. Close inlet and outlet valves

  2. Open Drain and vent valves to Drain filter completely Before removing Cartridges

  3. Untight the filter cover bolts

  4. Remove cover and old filter  Cartridge

  5. Clean thoroughly the filter internal surface

  6. Insert new filter Cartridge

  7. Clean cover gasket and gasket seat

  8. Replace new  gasket if it is hard or  damage

Reassembly and Pushing Filter in Service

  1. Place the filter cover and tight the cover bolts to 150 feet. lb torque

  2. Open inlet and outlet valve

  3. Open vent to release air and  close it  when solar  appears

  4. Observe the Differential Pressure Gauge reading Less  Than 25 Psi
