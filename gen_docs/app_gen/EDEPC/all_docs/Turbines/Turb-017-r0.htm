Introduction

This document outlines the major maintenance procedures P1, which must be performed every five years as indicated by Y5 in the title block.

Safety Precaution

  • A work permit must be issued

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves … etc.

  • Shutdown the unit.

  • Generator should be isolated.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of Generator

  • Disconnect the electric terminals of Generator

  • The oil of turbine must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure turbine must be completely drained.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Special tools for turbine.

  • Cleaning fluid, Rags, Manual puller

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge

  • Checking shaft.

  • Rings of checking shaft ( ø280 mm – ø300 mm – ø355 mm – ø430 mm )

  • Lifting device of rotors.

  • Lifting device of casings.

  • Heating device of bolts including heating elements and templates for bolts tightening.

  • Special wrench.

  • Assembly guide of casings.

  • Transport stands of rotors.

  • HP rotor stand.        MP rotor stand.        LP rotor stand.

Work to be Carried out

Preparation

  1. Reading during the normal operation before overhaul

  2. To follow continuously the technical condition of the turbo-generator

  3. To secure a permanent measurement of the rotor vibration

  4. To secure the measurement of the foundation distortion by civil department. The measurement interval is selected according to the foundation behavior

  5. To secure the check of the creeping of the material

  6. To secure the necessary spare parts with regard to the cycles between the repairs

Disassembly

Dismantling of LP Turbine

  1. The turbine out off turning gear.

  2. Dismantle the turbine cover ( housing ) above LP part

  3. Disassemble measurement equipment from turbine parts ( magnets, vibration probe, etc )

  4. Disassemble the connect pipe between MP – LP part

  5. Remove the insulation from LP part

  6. Loose the screws in parting plane of external gland covers for casing.

  7. Remove all plugs.

  8. Loose all nuts parting plane.

  9. Dismantle 2 x connecting pieces from external casing to internal casing.

  10. Assemble 4 lead rods in its place in parting plane of turbine casing.

  11. Dismantle the tubes for endoscope.

  12. Measure the Run out of MP-LP coupling tight Fig. 6.

  13. Remove the coupling bolts

  14. Measure the Run out of MP - LP coupling –free– Fig. 6 .

  15. Measure the alignment of MP – LP coupling– Fig. 7

  16. Measure the Run out of LP – Gen coupling – tight Fig. 6.

  17. Remove the coupling bolts .

  18. Measure the Run out of LP – Gen. coupling –free Fig. 6

  19. Measure the alignment of LP – Gen coupling Fig. 8

  20. Dismantle the outer LP casing

  21. By  means of press off  screws  lift the  cover of turbine casing.

  22. By  means of device for lifting of turbine casing  remove the cover  of  LP part

  23. Dismantle the screening.

  24. Dismantle the plugs and nuts of internal LP casing.

  25. Insert 4 lead rods in its place in parting plane of internal turbine casing.

  26. Loose the inner LP casing.

  27. Dismantle the LP inner casing.

  28. Measure the Run out of LP rotor.

  1. Dismantle the covers from bearing stands 5,6 .

  2. Measure the clearance of bearings No. 5,6 Fig. 11

  3. Measure the axial position of LP rotor in stator.

  4. Dismantle the  LP rotor.

  5. Put the check shaft in LP casing.

  6. Measure the LP through flow part– Fig. 5

  14. Dismantle upper part of LP stuffing boxes.

  15. Dismantle the LP rotor from casing.

  16. Measure the deformation of LP inner casing in planning part ( max. 0,55 mm – OK ).

  17. Check the space under LP diaphragms and upper section of condenser tubes,  without defect - OK.

  18. Measure the axial positions of inner stuffing boxes Fig. 2 , 3.

  19. Remove the down parts of bearings , B. scarf include if adjusting wedges.

Proceeding of Turbine Dismantling after Opening LP Casing

  1. Check the aligning of rotors in couplings ( observe water level in condenser )

  2. Measure and notice checking values ( run out)  for LP rotors in oil inserts .

  3. Realize read-out of rotor.

  4. Set the rotors to working position work side of ax. bearing side to generator and measure of all radial and axial backlashes in glands and in labyrinths of guide wheels .

  5. Check the axial setting guide wheels again the rotors.

  6. Dismantle the upper and lower part of axial bearing

  7. Remove LP rotors.

  8. Under supervision of specialist from turbine factory check all parts of turbine and determine necessary works during overhaul

The Set measurement before Inspection

  1. After dismounting the covers from bearing stands indicate the couplings are connected by fitted bolts, the rotors must be cold and temperature should not exceed 25 – 30 °C.

  2. Loosen the couplings and dismount fitted bolts, check the rotor alignment in the couplings– Fig. 7 , 8.

  3. After measurement dismount the tops of turbine casings and carry out:

  4. Indication ( run out ) of rotor LP – Fig. 6.

  5. Inspect the rotor alignment in the couplings (casings are lifted) – compare with covered casings –. Now set the rotor into working position and set the couplings one on to the other, measure them.

  6. An axial clearance between moving and guide wheels – Fig. 5 , 9 .

  7. An axial and radial clearance of labyrinth rings in the casings and guide wheels– Fig. 4.

  8. Align the MP – LP bearing  pedestals – Fig. 10.

  9. The upper and lateral clearance of journal bearings– Fig. 11.

  10. Before bulling out the rotors measure alignment with a recess in bearing stands and check the fitting of shaft.

  11. After removal of rotor, insert a check shaft into turbine casings, set it according to the values from rotor alignment with a recess of bearing stands and measure the alignment of seal bodies and of guide wheels in a shaft of the high-pressure medium-pressure and a low- pressure part.

  12. After the measurement dismount the seal bodies, the inner casings and carriers with guide wheels. Measure the evenness of parting planes by laying the tops of casings onto bottoms. Measure by slot plates from outside and inside. Admissible deflection of non-evenness of parting planes in new machines is 0,10 – 0,15 , in old machines 0,30 – 0,40 clearance between the bottom and top of the casing.

External inspection of the dismounted LP turbine Parts

  1. LP Rotor was strongly salted especially on the moving wheels no. 3 , 3a (Thickness 3mm) moving wheels 1 , 1a partly damaged, after hard contact with other part of LP casing.

  2. Moving wheel no. 1:
    - 2pcs of the blades missing – cut off.
    - 2pcs of the segments of the steel blade bandages  damaged.
    - Other blades has small marking and deformation through the contact with forging metal.

  3. Moving wheel no. 1a :-
    - 1pcs of the blades missing – cut off.
    - 3pcs of the segments of the steel blade bandages  damaged.
    - Other blades has small marking and deformation through the contact with forging metal.

  4. Other moving wheels 2 , 2a , 3 , 3a , 4 , 4a has without visually defects.

  5. Run-out of rotor between moving wheels 3 and 4 is 0,13 mm – it is out of perfect tolerance.

  6. Check the space under LP diaphragms and upper section of condenser tubes – without defect  - ok.

  7. Check the thermometers of turbine.

  8. The check of bearing brasses by:
    - The adhesion of the lining by a capillary method (color detection liquid ) between the bearing metal and the supporting material, rare indications may be allowed when after 5 minutes are over, the color detection liquid will not form a larger color point than ø 6 mm at the developer – OK.
    - The connection of the lining body of the bearing at the processed surface of the g with the steel supporting lining by a contact ultra-sound method – OK

Repairing

  1. Replace the LP rotor with new one.

  2. Machine 2pcs of new blade bandages of LP rotor - Repair to final profile 2 pcs of blade bandages ( grinding on the rotor position ).

  3. Clean the bearing  journal and coupling face.

  4. Clean the spare LP rotor – No. 1117.

  5. Clean the parting plane.

  6. Clean the  LP rotor and guide wheels by sandblast ( very fine piece of abrasive – granularity 1200 ) and check crack by color detection liquid and replace the defective blade if we need it. During the blasting, the sliding surfaces e. g. the rotor pins, the gland parts must be protected to prevent their damage.

  7. Clean the planning part of LP casing .

  8. Repair the LP stuffing boxes .

  9. Clean the oil stands .

  10. Modification of suspensions ( wedges ) of diaphragms 4 , 4a , 3a

  11. Align the front and rear stuffing  boxes in casing.

  12. Measure the diaphragms .( 4a , 3a and  4 )against check shaft.

  13. Produce new shims for 1 , 2 ,1a , 2a.

  14. Check the diaphragms shims.

  15. Measure the through flow part

  16. Repair of (3 pcs) of diaphragms.

  17. Measure the diaphragms in parting plane ( for alignment of upper part of LP casing ) .

  18. Modify the blade bandage on wheel no. 1.

  19. Repair of diaphragms  blades for upper LP casing.

  20. Put rotor in casing.

  21. Measure the rotor axial position and radial clearance , clearance of inserts against rotor down – ok  .

  22. Remove the LP rotor and LP diaphragms (Repair Ends of blade ).

  23. Clean the oil stands and the spaces under bearings.

  24. Clean and repair by the scraper white metal of bearings.

  25. Repair the seal sheets on the diaphragms.

  26. Clean the LP outer casing parting plane.

  27. Modify the front and rear casing inserts (upper ) in parting plane.

  28. Coupling bolts MP – LP modification of diameter in LP part acc spare LP Rotor coupling  60,0 mm.

  29. The revision of the turning gear of turbine ( the chance of seal rings , checking of bearings – OK repair of toothed clutch  of turning gear air key ).

  30.  The repair of measurement device for measurement of lift of rotors.

  31. Check of magnetic filters and screens in main oil tank – rough impurities much.

  32. The blind in filter body( NO 6,7) has continually leak – repair – OK.

  33. Clean the screens and filters in oil tank .

  34. Check the bearing( No . 5 and 6 ).

  35. Small hard imparities take from  bearing pockets.

  36. Agreement for leaning – bearing wile be flush by pressure of lifting oil in opposite direction in parting plane of bearings will erect the shims 1 mm . the impurities from bearing pockets flood by oil to the stands . 

  37. The connection of oil lifting pipes.

  38. Close the stand covers and flush by lift oil include lubrication oil.

  39. Check the bearing pockets after flush – OK without imparities.

The Recommendation For Repair of LP Rotor

In the consequence of indication 0,13 mm of  rotor shaft in place between moving wheels No. 3 and No. 4 is necessary the repair in production factory SKODA PLZEN . moreover of necessary repairs will be in factory provided sane corresponded with operation time of rotor.

  1. The straightening of rotor shaft.

  2. The change of damaged blades on the moving wheel 1 , 1a ( 14pcs , 21 pcs AS minimum ).

  3. The test of past on blades on the moving wheels.

  4. The change of steel blade bandages include machining.

  5. The equalization of both rotor couplings.

  6. The change of seal sheets on the rotor.

  7. The rolling of bearing journals.

  8. The check of the rotor bore.

  9. New balancing of the LP rotor.
