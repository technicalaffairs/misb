Introduction

This document out lines the details equipment inspection I1, to be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

The function of this fan is used to draw off steam air mixture of steam glands exhaust of turbine.

Technical specification:

Type of Fan  : RVJ 630 - 1
Design Fan : Horizontal centrifugal
Fan capacity : 0.4 m3 / s
Pressure : 5130 Pa
Fan Speed : 2900 rpm
Bearing type  : 2311K ( clamping bush H2311 )

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety, shoes, eye glass ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the fan motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the fan motor.

  • The fan must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure fan must be completely drainage.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Manual puller, Rags

  • Feeler gauge, Dial indicator.

  • Vibrometer, Straight edge.

 

Work to be Carried Out

Fan in Operation

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do.

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare them with the setting value then replace the worn- out components.

Fan out of Operation

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do (if it is needed).

  3. Check discharge pressure and compare it with the setting value then replace the worn- out components (if it is needed).

  4. Check bolts fixation, then re-tight it (if it is needed).

  5. Make test for bearings grease and add or change it (if it is needed).

  6. Check glands for leaking and replace it if it is needed.

  7. Check flanges for leaking.

  8. Check the fan casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).
