The combustion chamber tiles prevent the temperature from arrival to combustion chamber

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmets, shoes, gloves ..  etc

  • Turbine must be shut down

  • Fuel system ( solar – gas ) of gas turbine out of service

  • Isolate the step-up transformer and it's circuit breaker

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of all pump motors and disconnect the electrical terminal of all pump motors

  • Fire fighting system must be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Basket

  • Crane 25 ton

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Dismantle the domed cover 03 including burner and swirl.

  2. The crane cables are attached to the three support hooks 06 and the basket 07 is lowered into the combustion chamber

  3. Inspect the combustion chamber tiles (finned segments) if it is not good ( it have a big wear ) it must by changed.

  4. To change the combustion chamber tiles ( finned segments ) see fig. 2.

  5. Remove all six plates 01 on the swirl guide 02, do not dismantle swirl  guide.

  6. Disassemble the tow upper sight glass connection tubes 04.

  7. The basket 07 attached to the over head crane and lowered into the combustion chamber until the upper ring of the basket is slightly below the finned segment carrier 05 which is to by lifted out and replace the bad tile  ( bad finned segment)

  8. After changing the bad tile install finned segment carrier 05 in its position.

  9. Install the two upper sight glass connection tube 04.

  10. Install all six plates 01on the swirl guide 02.

  11. Install the domed cover 03 including burner and swirl.
