Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I1, which must be performed for Condenser of Turbine every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

This Condenser is used to condensate the steam of turbine.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required 

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs … etc.

  • Stopping the cooling water circuit of condenser.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the cooling water pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the cooling water pump motor.

  • The Condenser must be isolated mechanically by closing inlet and outlet valves.

  • Be sure Condenser must be completely drained.

 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning fluid, Rags,.Manual puller.

  • Tube cleaner flexible shaft.

  • Tube expander.

  • Wood plugs.

  • Lighting inside the condenser with 24 V voltage

Work to be carried out

Condenser In Operation

  1. Measure temperature at inlet and outlet water in condenser and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do.

  2. Measure vacuum of condenser and compare it with the setting value then determine what to do.

 Condenser Out Of Operation

  1. Check the condenser Completely drainage

  2. Check the air service for cleaning is available .

  3. Dismantling the front and rear manhole of condenser

  4.  Remove all the things which resist and prevent passes the water through the pipe of condenser

  5. Cleaning the pipes of condenser by flexible shaft (first cleaning)

  6. Cleaning the pipes of condenser by compressed air about 6 bar (sec. cleaning)

  7. Cleaning the pipes of condenser by compressed water about 6 bar (finally cleaning)

  8. Fixing the condenser against moving down during filling by lifting nuts under the condenser

  9. Filling the steam space condenser with the demi-water up to 10 m level and determine the leakage tubes and blend them by wood plugs

  10. Draining the condenser after checking the tubes are not leakage.

  11. Close the front and rear manhole of condenser

  12. Down the nuts under the condenser to the original position to release the condenser against moving.

  13. Filling the condenser to the normal operation level.
