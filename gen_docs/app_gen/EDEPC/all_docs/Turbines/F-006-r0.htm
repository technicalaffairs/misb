Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should be carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

    •

  • Wear safety helmets, shoes, earplug and gloves pairs ... etc

  • Be sure that the filters are out of service.

  • Open self – cleaning system when needed.

 Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance set.

  • Compressed air hose.

  • Cleaning articles

  • Soft brushes.

  • Magnetic pick-up flexible.

  • Spray gun

  • Lifting crane.

  • Long rope with basket

  • Vacuum pump.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Change 25% of the filters every 4000 operating hours

  2. Inspect the air filters; replace the damaged if it is required.

  3. Check the system for leaks on a regular base.

  4. Treat any leaks that found with a suitable sealing compound.

  5. Clean the filters automatically by self-cleaning system.
