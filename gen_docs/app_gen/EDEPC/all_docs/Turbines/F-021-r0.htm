Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection II, to be carried out every 1500 operation hours as indicated by H1500 in the title block

Main lube oil Filter function to clean oil from impurities and dirty.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety Category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, eye glass, nose mouth "dust cover" ... etc

  • Unit must be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, eye glass, ... etc

Work to be carried out

Disassembly

  1. Remove sealer frame (or pre-filter)

  2. Remove the dirty filter by gripping the T.P and bottom edges (flanges) of the filter and pulling forward.

  3. Fold dirty filter carefully to trap all collected dirt and place in a box or can for disposal.

  4. Remove new HP filter from box and open it by separating top and bottom panels.

Assembly

  1. Insert the bottom panel of the filter about half way into the retainer, making sure that the folds in the filter line up with retainer partitions.

  2. With both hands, carefully stretch the top panel up and over the upper ends of the wire dividers in the retainer.

  3. Push both top and bottom panels of the filter in together until the flanges seat against the gasket around the frame. Do not force the filter; if it does not slip into the retainer easily; check to make sure the media pleats are properly aligned with the dividers in the retainer.

  4. Fold the edges of the filter and the flanges over the edges of the filter and the flanges over the gasket of the holding frame to assure.

  5. Replace sealer frame (or pre-filter) so that all edges of the filter are clamped against the gasket on the holding frame and secure with holding clips.
