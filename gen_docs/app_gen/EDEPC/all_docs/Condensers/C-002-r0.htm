Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor on condenser.

  • Isolate all drain valve to condenser .

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage.

  • Open manhole on condenser 

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Main Crane, Alfa Laval,

  • Manual Puller, Feeler Gauge, Rags, Cleaning Fluid,

  • Dial Indicator, Vibrometer.

Work to be carried out 

 condenser in Service

  1. Check leakage water from any connection inlet or outlet of the condenser .

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare them with the setting value then replace the worn- out components (if it is needed).

  3. Check hammering in condenser and all drain to condenser .

  4. Check T.D.S of condensation and PH .

condenser out of Service

  1. Check bolted ,supported  and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Clean hot well and water box tow side  . ,

  3. Check bolts fixation, then retightens it (if it is needed).

  4. Check the leakage from inside tube  (if it is needed).

  5. Check the isulatited inside  water box tow side  . 

  6. Check flanges for leakage.

  7. Check any Corrigan inside  water box tow side    (if it is needed).

  8. Electrical low vacuum trip setting  MAG01 CP 002/3/4=0.3 bar

  9. Electrical low vacuum trip for by pass operation setting  MAG01 CP 002/3/4=0.6bar
