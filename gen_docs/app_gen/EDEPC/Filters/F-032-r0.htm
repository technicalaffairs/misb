Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Closing delivery and suction valves of filters & coolers.

  • Be sure filters & coolers must be completely venting &  drainage for oil to clean tank.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Main Crane, Alfa Laval,

  • Manual Puller, Feeler Gauge, Rags, Cleaning Fluid,

  • Dial Indicator, Vibrometer.

Work to be carried out 

Filters , cooler & Tank in service :

  1. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare them with the setting value then replace the worn- out components (if it is needed).

  2. Check leakage of oil & water

  3. Check temperature in & out

  4. Check level.

Filters , cooler & Tank out of service

  1. Closing delivery and suction valves of filters & coolers.

  2. Be sure filters & coolers must be completely venting &  drainage for oil to clean tank.

  3. Clean oil by Alfa Laval and adjust oil level of tank to site values which is (highest from top of tank = 500 mm & lowest from top of tank = 550 mm).

  4. Check bolts fixation, then retightens it (if it is needed).

  5. Check the validity of oil and add or change it (if it is needed).

  6. Clean the tank if it is needed & flashing the tank  with the oil requirement 10 m3.

  7. Check flanges for leakage.

  8. Check Filtration particle size clean or replace (if it is needed).
