Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes goggles, and rubber glove ... etc

  • The board should be isolated from both sides

  • The main 0.4 kv circuit breaker of the distribution board (incoming) should be switched - off

  • The sub circuit breaker of the distribution board (outgoing) should be switched - off

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete kit of tools

  • Voltmeter instrument

  • Clean dry rags

  • Test equipment

Work to be carried out

  1. Clean of auxiliary contacts with special type of spray

  2. Check all of power circuit and control circuit terminals for tightness

  3. Readjust the measuring instruments (ammeters, voltmeters)

  4. Clean the withdrawable modules with clean dry rags
