Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes eye glass, nose mouth dust cover ... etc

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution mark surrounding the work area is required

  • Ensure that the inlet and outlet valves are cosed if necessary

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Rags, cleaning fluid\

Work to be carried out 

  1. Check the oil on oil lubricated units.  

  2. Check grease lubricated bearings for specifications.

  3. Check the packing for leakage and replace if necessary

  4. Check vibration readings of bearing housings

  5. Check alignment of pump and motor

 

Remedy Procedures

Isolate the electric power before replacing oil or grease, also for repacking.

Decreased and replace the oil its     

  1. Increase the level oil if oil level specifications not suitable

  2. If the grease gives a whitish color , the grease must be change and wash the bearing with a suitable industrial solvent cleaner , and replace the grease with the proper type as recommended

  3. If the packing leaks water slightly retight the packing cover bolts gently , and if it still leaks isolate the pump mechanical and electrical , then vent and drain water from inside , and loose the cover bolts

  • Change the recommended packing and retight the cover bolts, close the drainage valve open the suction and discharge valves, then close the vent valve after Air bubbles stopped (filled completely with water)

  • Connect the electric power again and run the pump to check the packing looks.(10 minutes)

  1. After ten minutes (Rotation) score vibration readings for bearing housings and compared with the pervious readings.

  2.  If the alignment checked is not ok -Shim up units if necessary.

  •  Inspect the piping system unbolt piping at suction and discharge flanges also for    supports.

  •  Remove rags, tools and other things from the place.
