Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipments as safety healthy, safety shoes, ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor.

  • Disconnect electric terminals of the pump motor.

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves.

  • Be sure pump must be completely drainage

  • The pump must be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and suction valves of heatexchnger

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Main Crane, Alfa Laval,

  • Manual Puller, Feeler Gauge, Rags, Cleaning Fluid,

  • Dial Indicator, Vibrometer.

Work to be carried out 

Pump in Service

  1. Measure vibrations level for bearings and compare it with the setting value, then analyze it and determine what to do (if it is needed).

  2. Check discharge quantity and pressure and compare them with the setting value then replace the worn- out components (if it is needed).

Pump Out of Service

  1. Check alignment and compare it with the setting value.

  2. Check bolts fixation, then re-tightens it (if it is needed).

  3. Check the validity of oil and add or change it (if it is needed).

  4. Check glands for leaking and re-packing (if it is needed).

  5. Check flanges for leakage.

  6. Check the pump casing against rust and re-paint (if it is needed).
