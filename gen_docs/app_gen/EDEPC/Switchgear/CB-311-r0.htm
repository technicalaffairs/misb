Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • Circuit breaker is opened and racked out

  • Change remote / local control switch to local position

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • Digital voltmeter.

  • Megger

  • Clean brushes

 

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be carried out 

  1. Clean the mating surface of the metal clad

  2. Check the racking mechanism to make certain that it is in the disconnect position

  3. Check the voltage control at source of power and line drop between the power source and breaker

  4. Check the safety shutter inside the cubical slide freely

  5. Clean lubricate grease and penetrating spray for breaker operating mechanism

  6. Check mechanical position indicator

  7. Check the operating handle of the CB and the power plug operating device

  8. Check the electrical operation using test cabinet (if available) or the test position in the metal clad switchgear

  9. Examine the movable contact rod of the vacuum interrupter with the breaker open and wipe the lubricant off the rod
