Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on yearly bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Ahmed Abel Halim Elangger and reviewed bay eng. Mohamed Salah Badawy accepted by eng. Abdel Rahem Salam general director and approved by eng. Mohamed Enan Head sector of Damietta power station

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used, safety shoes, helmet breathing mask, safety glasses.

  • The unit must be out of service

  • Disconnect the circuit breaker and the ground shoe

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete tool set

  • Digital voltmeter.

  • Megger

  • Brushes

 

 

 

 

 

 

Work to be carried out 

 

Step Work to be carried out Setting reference value Result
1 Clean the mating surface of the metal clad ok - not ok  
2 Check the control voltage value at source of power and line drop between the power source and breaker 220 VDC  
3 Check the racking mechanism to make certain that it is in the disconnect position ok - not ok  
4 Close the CB manually ok - not ok  
5 Open CB manual manually ok - not ok  
6 Check charging and discharging of the CB ok - not ok  
7 Check the closing time of breaker 0.6-0.1 sec  
8 Check the tripping time of breaker 0.25-0.3 sec  
9 Checked the primary circuit insulation on the breaker phase to phase to ground ok - not ok  
10 Check the DC resistance 100 µΩ  
11 Lubricate the breaker operating mechanism ok - not ok  
12 Check the painting of CB and accessories ok - not ok  
13 Clean the disconnecting switch for the auxiliary transformer ok - not ok  
14 Check the disconnecting switch for the auxiliary transformer ok - not ok
