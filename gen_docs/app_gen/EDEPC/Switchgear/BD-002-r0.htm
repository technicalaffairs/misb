Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety & healthy equipment as safety shoes, helmet, gloves pairs, breathing mask, safety glass…etc

  • The unit must be out of service.

  • The bus duct must be de-energized and the bars must be connected to earth.

  • Check the insulation resistance with a megger prior and after maintenance to re-energize the bus duct.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

  • Megger device and digital voltmeter, voltage Indicator.

  • Spray fine cloth and brushes for cleaning purpose, Air Blower

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Loose cover down of generator.

  2. Check the flexible joint in the out let generator 15.75kv.

  3. If any flexible joint is not ok replaced it by new one "like over heat stress" ...etc

  4. Retighten the end terminal of flixable joint of generayor.          

  5. Clean all porcelain surfaces of all bushings 15.75kv out let of generator.
