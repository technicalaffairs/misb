Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued before start work

  • The maintenance staff should be trained, qualified, and carry the suitable ID safety category.

    •

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes, and safety clothes when cleaning.

  • Wear safety belt when cleaning insulators.

  • The generator must be in case of shutdown.

  • The personal shall perform the safety operation so as all the live parts are de energized, earthed and equipped with warnings before removing the protection and connection main circuit.   

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, safety belt, safety shoes, and complete tool.

  • Blower, brushes.

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

  • Megger to check isolation resistance (5000 V).

  • Voltage indicator, portable earth for discharging.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visually check the accessories.

  2. Check bearings for oil leaks and oil.

Return the Position

  1. Disconnect the ground on generator winding.

  2. Put the CB of the generator on the service position. 

  3. Lift up the safety fence with caution mark surrounding the work area

  4. Finish the work permit with coordination with shift engineer.
