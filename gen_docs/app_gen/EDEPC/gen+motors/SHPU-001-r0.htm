Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection  I1, which must be performed on monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block

Sodium hypochlorite is chlorine derivatives formed by reaction of chlorine with hydroxide sodium hupochlorite as chlorine gas used for

  • Disinfections

  • Control of microorganism

  • Control of tests and odor

  • Color reduction

  • Destruction of organic matter

Information

The reaction of hypochlorites and water form both hypochlorous acid sodium hydroxide, this cauese little change in pH. Hypochlorous acid and hypochlorite ion are oxidizing agent

Directives

  • Sodium Hypochlorite must be stores away form heat and sunlight

  • Sodium Hypochlorite is diluted by demi-water to avoid precipitation

  • Sodium Hypochlorite is avoided mixing with acid and FeC13 to prevent rapid generation of chorine gas

  • Wearing safety clothes and tools when contact with it

Technical characteristics of the equipment

AC input power

  • Nominal supply voltage                    : 6300 V

  • Phases                                             : 3

  • Frequency                                        : 50 HZ

  • 6.3 kv short-circuit level, symmetrical value
    (Min. assumed)                                : 100 MVA

AC input auxiliary

  • Nominal voltage, 3-phase                                    : 380 V-50 HZ

  • Nominal voltage, 1-phase                                    : 220 V-50 HZ

DC output

  • Number of rectifier units                                        :6

  • Rated DC current of each unit                               : 2600A

  • Rated DC voltage, at full load                                : 180 V

  • Operating range of DC voltage                              : 130-180 V

  • DC current control range                                       : 10   100%

  • Rectifying circuit                                                    : 3-ph Bridge

  • Pulse number                                                         : 6

  • Rectifying elements                                                : Thyristors

  • Type of automatic control                                      : DC constant current

  • Accuracy                                                              : 1% rated current

Rectifier unit

Containing a common cubicle one transformer, one thyristor rectifier and one control panel, for indoor installation

Work to be carried out

  1. The rectifiers are built with solid - state components and no maintenance is needed for the electronic converter part

  2. The only moving parts are the fan check yearly fan operation. All the electrical and electronic components must be periodically cleaned with a blown air to remove dust from heatsink and electric contacts

Step down transformer 6.3 kv / 132 v

  1. Cleaning transformer

  2. Check earthing connection

  3. Check bolt connection

Converter

  1. Cleaning any dust on the heat sink

  2. Screw connection of the main circuit

  3. Clean converter cubicle

  4. Check noise level of the fan

  5. Check air flow

  6. Cleaning air filter
