Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the motors.

  • Close all valves and open vents .

  • Replacing H2 with CO2 and replacing CO2 with air  .  

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Main Crane

  • Manual Puller, Feeler Gauge, Rags, Cleaning Fluid

  • Dial Indicator, Vibrometer.

Work to be carried out 

 Equipment in service

 

  1. Check the all valves  in service  (if it is needed).

  2. Check the coolant water available .

  3. Check the H2 pressure is good

Equipment out of Service

  1. Check the all valves outlet  in service

  2. Check the H2 line is closed  (if it is needed).

  3. replacing H2 by CO2 and replacing CO2 by Air   (if it is needed).

  4. Check flanges for leakage.

  5. Clean the cooler .
