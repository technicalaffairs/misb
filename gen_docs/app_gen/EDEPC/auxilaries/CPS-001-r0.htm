Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months bases as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The Cathodic Protection function is to protect the metallic parts from corrosion, this protection can be found in the following places: Beside the Nile water pumps, Beside the compensation tanks, the 400 Volt room for gas units, and the fire fighting building.
