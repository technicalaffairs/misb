Work to be Carried out

Power Impressed Anodes (Current Type)

Installed on fuel oil tanks consisted of three voltage transformer rectifiers every rectifier feed one tank.

Sacrificial Anode

  1. Installed on condenser cooling water pipes and service cooling water pipes

  2. Installed on condenser water box for steam turbine plant and consisting of iron plates, fixed to the tube sheet, is provided inside the water boxes to protect tubes and tube sheets and the inside surface of the water boxes is coated with epoxy (600 microns thick) for corrosion protection. studs for the installation of four sacrificial anodes per water box are installed.
