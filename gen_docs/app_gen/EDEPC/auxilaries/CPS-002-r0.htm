Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The Cathodic Protection function is to protect the metallic parts from corrosion, this protection can be found in the following places:
  - Beside the Nile water pumps              - Beside the compensation tanks
  - The 400 Volt room for gas units         - The fire fighting building

Rearrangement state: Connect all items which are disconnected.

The Transformer test :

  1. Remove both tap bars on secondary

  2. Read the AC I/P current on the AC ammeter
    The current will be very high if there is a short in transformer
    If the AC current is not greater than twice that shown in the test sheet than the transformer is OK
    The current is varying with the AC I/P voltage.

The No-Load  test :

  1. Disconnect the O/P leads

  2. The No Load voltage must be half of the normal voltage

  3. Record the I/P voltage and current

  4. Record the DC O/P voltage

  5. Vary the I/P voltage and record the O/P voltage and current

  6. Measure the bed resistance

  7. Bed Resistance = DC output Volt / AC output Amp
