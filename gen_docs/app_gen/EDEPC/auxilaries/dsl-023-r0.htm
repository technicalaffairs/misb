Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes, gloves ...etc.

  • Isolate the circuit breaker of the pump motor

  • Disconnect electric terminal of the pump motor

  • The pump must  be isolated mechanically by closing delivery and sactior valves

  • Be sure pump must be completely drained

  • Be sure oil must be completely drainage

Tools and Equipment

  • A full set of tools is normally ordered and supplied in a box with the turbe charger
