Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safty category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, safety belt ...etc.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Fine cloth for cleaning purposes.

  • Digital multimeter, Megger (500 – 5000) volt

  • Air blower, Lubricant

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the bridge movement by traveling forward and backward for position low and high.

  2. Check the main trolley movement by traversing left and right for position low and high.

  3. Check the hoist up and down for position low and high.

  4. Check the hoist up and down for 2 speeds.

    5.

  5. Check the limit switches of bridge limits at slow down and stop by traveling forward and backward.

  6. Check limit switches of main trolley for position slow down and stop by traversing left and right.

  7. Check the limit switches for position stop for main trolley by hoisting up.

  8. Check the limit switches for position slow down for main trolley by hoisting up slow down.

  9. Check the limit switches for position stop for main trolley by hoisting down, up, down, and anti overlap.

  10. Check the control of the crane with pedant that all operations is correct.

  11. Check the control of signaling and lighting that all operations is correct.

  12. Check the control of load test at 1.25 of total load.
