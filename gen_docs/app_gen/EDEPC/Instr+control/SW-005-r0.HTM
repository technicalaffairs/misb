Profibus is open bus standard for industrial automation. It defines characteristic signs of serial industrial bus, through which one could interconnect peripheral instruments in the lowest automation level and control PLC of middle level. Principle transfer medium is the standard metallic twisted line according to US standard EIA RS-485.

In Profibus network  there could be two types of stations:  Active stations - MASTER and passive stations - SLAVE, Architecture of transfer protocol is oriented to reference model OSI, responding to international standard ISO 7498.
