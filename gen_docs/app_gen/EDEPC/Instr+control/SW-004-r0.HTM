Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure I1, which must be performed every 6 months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Hesham El Sayed Mohamed El Assy, tested by eng. Mohamed Gad El Rab Hawad at 29 March 2003, reviewed by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Abdel Momem El-Emam, accepted by general director eng. Farouk Awad Aly, and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talkha power plant.

Win_D is, like Send31, a part of program package PERTINAX and is also run directly from Windows 95 desktop, from folder Pertinax. It serves for send-in of application programs through serial line, through Profibus-FMS network and also for diagnosing of application programs directly in original graphic form.

 Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the suitable safety category,

  • Make Backup for soft ware before work.

Tools and Equipment

  • Paper And Pen.
