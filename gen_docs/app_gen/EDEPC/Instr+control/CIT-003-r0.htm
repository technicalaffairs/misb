Introduction

This document out lines the testing procedures P1, to be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

This document out lines how to check & schedule service conductivity device (18devices) to measure the conductivity in [demy water & feed water &condensate water & saturated steam & polisher &cooling water in generator ].

The sample get on a sensor analyzer to measure the conductivity value and the device convert it to [4:20mA] or [0:50µA] go to a recorder

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

    •

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, rubber gloves, ground strap ...etc.

    •

  • The unit must be out of services if we work at the device, which measure the conductivity in the cooling water in generator.

  • For the other samples calibration not require the unit be out of services

  • Turn off the power during cleaning the sensor.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet and safety shoes, ... etc.

  • Suitable tool set,

  • Calibration solution known its conductivity in laboratory

  • Air blower

  • Cotton

  • Screw driver set 

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the conductivity device with blower.

  2. Open the sensor and clean it.

  3. Put the sensor in the calibration solution.

  4. Put the digital multimeter in series with the two wiring which measure (ma) according to the calibration solution.

  5. Return the sensor in its place.

  6. Record the results on the recorder and compare it with the local chemical device results.

Return the Device to Services

  1. Put the sensor in its place and screw it.

  2. Remove the digital multimeter and screw the wiring in its place.
