Introduction

This document out lines Lube oil system maintenance procedure I1, which to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block.

The System consists of R5502, GT 4033, R 1587, R 4591, R1586, R1588, R5201, R5205, 5206, R5210 GT 4031.

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohmed Aly Aly Hassan, tested at 01 Jan. 2003 by eng. Al Shahat Ahmed Mousa, reviewed by eng. Abd Almonem Alemam, revised by quality control engineer, accepted by General Manger eng. Hamdy Melegy and approved by eng.Yousry  Mohamed Aly Salama Al Shabab Power Station Sector  Head.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, gloves, ... etc.

  • Gas turbine should be out of service at cold state.

  • Oil pumps ( AC – DC ) off ( All mcc. C.B Risk out)

Tools and Equipment

  • Pressure tester > 20 bar.

  • Multimeter and connection wire.

  • Air blower, Contact cleaner.

  • Vernier hight

  • Wrench spanner set

Work to be Carried Out

Pressure Switch

  1. Disconnect the oil path to the pressure switch (PS)

  2. Connect the oil pump to PS

  3. Fix the pressure of pump at 0.9 bar

  4. Disconnect the terminal 1 or 2 to control circuits

  5. Measure the resistance of PS, open and close values ref. to setting values as in test sheet

 

Constant Pressure Valve (CPV)

  1. Release the lock of CPV

  2. Measure the pressure value if not OK adjust the length
