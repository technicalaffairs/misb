Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Unit at shut down mode

  • Switch off all CB's on the motor control center (MCC)

Tools and Equipment

  • Frequency source, Frequency meter

  • Screw driver, Jumpers

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect a frequency source and frequency meter to the turbine magnetic speed pickup (77NH) of the analog I/O modules < AIO3 > and jumper the remaining pickups in parallel to the first. Simulate HP turbine speed to < R > < S > and < T > 
    Note :
    It may be necessary to disconnect the magnetic pickup to prevent ioading down the test oscillator signal.

  2. Select "NORMAL" display mode and observe the HP turbine speed TNH on the CRT

     

  3. Adjust the frequency source to obtain frequency (HZ) equal to rated shaft speed (rpm) specified in the control spec-setting (5089HZ).Verify TNH displayed is 100%

  4. Adjust control gain constant TNK H2 if necessary record the new value of the PROM constant (11590 CNT15).

  5. Increase oscillator frequency to 125% of rated shaft speed and verify that TNH displayed is correct

  6. Disconnect the test oscillator and the test counter from the turbine magnetic speed pickups (77 NH) input of the analog I/O module < AIO3 > and disconnect the jumpers between the remaining pickups and the first.

  7. Reconnect the three magnetic pickups (77 NH 1,2,3) inputs to the analog I/O module (AIO3)
