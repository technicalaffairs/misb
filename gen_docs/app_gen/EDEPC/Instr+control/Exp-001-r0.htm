Introduction

This document outlines the checklist of the inductive transducer, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

The inductive transducer is used to measure the relative Expansion for MP& LP- turbine.

The transducer consists of two circuits see figure 1:-

  1. Exciting circuit

  2. Measuring circuit

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance stuff should be carry the  Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Unit should be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85

  • Screw driver

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check exciting circuit by measuring terminal resistance between two point nat .

    Col. and blue showed be   4 Ώ see figure 1

  2. Check measuring circuit by measuring terminal resistance between two point nat. Col .  and red  and point red  and  blue  showed  be  11 Ώ.

  3. Measure terminal resistance between point nat. Col   and blue showed be 22 Ώ

  4. Check The ground between any wire and matel sensor

  5. If the measuring values in step 1 & 2 & 3 are not the same values ( 4Ώ & 11 Ώ  &  22 Ώ )  than the transducer is defected  and should  be changed .
