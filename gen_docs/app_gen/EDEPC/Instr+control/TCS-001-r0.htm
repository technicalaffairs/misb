Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block and during overhaul

TCS consists of three parts

  1. Servo position controller (SPC) system

  2. Startup and load limited device

  3. Electro hydraulic converter (EHC)

Servo position controller (SPC) system

There are two SPC`s in control loop, one is primary and other is backup. The servo position controller (SPC) is a servo-valve driver that accepts a device net or 4-20 mA based position demand signal from a system controller, and accurately position proportional or integrating servo-valves. The SPC`s position feedback circuit includes integral excitation and signal conditioning circuitry for LVDT and RVDT based position sensing devices. The SPC`S associated PC interface software program allows a user to configure; auto-calibrate, dynamically adjust, and manually stroke the controlled servo

  

Startup and load limited device

The function of the hydraulic speed governor with starting and load limiting device is to operate the control valves to give the appropriate turbine steam throughout for the particular load condition

Electro hydraulic converter (EHC)

The electro hydraulic converter (EHC) is the connecting element between the electrical and hydraulic parts of the turbine control system. It converts the signals from the electric governor into hydraulic signals and amplifies them before transmitting them to the actuating devices

For testing of TCS perform the following

Check servo position controller (SPC) system. Check startup and load limited device, check elector hydraulic converter (EHC) review data base configuration and test l/O electronic modules

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Obtain a copy of (SPC, LVDT) maintenance manuals

  2. Obtain a copy of schematic loop wiring diagram and data sheets

  3. Backup configuration software for SPC

SPC test

  1. Check the system power supply

  2. Connect the SPC with PC by using RS-232 connection

  3. Ensure that the configuration of SPC is equal to original configuration

  4. If there are errors with the configuration, load it from the backup configuration

  5. Turn the SPC to manual mode via operator station (DSC) then give order 0 value

  6. Connect a voltmeter and an ammeter to the output of the SPC

  7. Give order to open 50% to SPC from operator station, measure the output current from the SPC, the SPC feedback from LVDT loop (current), and reading feedback on DCS and the mechanical opening percentage

  8. Increase the opening order by the rate 10% and record the reading each time

  9. The reading of LVDT feedback current and order of DSC to SPC and mechanical opening percentage must be equal.

      

Start up and load limit device test

  1. Check over load and there is applied volt on the motor terminals

  2. Check power supply on the positioner, and make loop check for mA feedback

  3. Give an order to open it 20%, 50%, 70%, 100% from operator station (DCS) then record the reading of DCS feedback, positioner feedback (mA) and mechanical stroke compare all reading if there is a problem, adjust the stroke

  4. All reading of DCS order, DCS feedback, positioner (mA) and mechanical stroke 

Electro hydraulic converter (EHC) test

  1. Disconnect the plunger coil from SPC circuit 2

  2. Connect plunger coil to injection device with voltage from(0-10)V or ampere from(0-200)mA

  3. Connect ammeter and voltmeter with the plunger coil

  4. Prepare the oil circuit to have trip oil pressure and remove all mechanical and electrical protection by turn the circuit to test mode

  5. Energize the solenoids SC11S244, SC11S241, and SC11S201 give an order to open with 100% to remove the protection of the hydraulic - hydraulic

  6. Inject a voltage on the terminal plunger coil from (0-10) V and record the readings in the table

  7. Measure the mA of the LVDT

  8. Record the percentage of EHC mechanical position

  9. Record the value of all oil pressure indicator, and at which value the control valve are open this for all control valve HP1,HP2,HP3,HP4,LP1,LP2

  10. Record the percentage of opening values of the control valves (feedback in DSC and mechanical position)

  11. Make a test for the limit switches of the control valve and stop valve of the HPT, and LPT, and check the opening percentage of the positioner

  12. Compare applied voltage, oil pressure, percentage of opening values of the control valves, LVDT (mA), and EHC mechanical position with SIEMENS document

  13. Compare percentage of opening values, limit switches in DSC with mechanical position
