 Introduction

This document out lines the testing procedures P1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

These meters used for Gas Fuel Control Voltage ( GCE ) – Speed Vibration

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety shoes, safety helmet ..etc

    •

  • The unit must be shut down.

  • The recorder must be out of service.

  • Disconnect the power supply of the recorder.

  • Ensure that the recorder was calibrated in the laboratory.

Tools and Equipment

  • Voltameter .

  • Current Injection Device.

  • Suitable tool set .

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect power from the meter.

  2. Dissemble meter to take it in laboratory to adjust meter.

  3. Inject meter through connect it with current injection device and observe reading on meter according to table.
