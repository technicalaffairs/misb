Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses

  • The unit must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Frequency counter (10 KHz)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect frequency counter to pin 1 and pin 31 from connector card A (1L7X )

  2. Turn master control switch to auto. Position and start the turbine.

  3. When speed arrive to FSNL we adjust speed by means of " raise – lower " switch to 95 % from rated speed (5089 ) RPM.

  4. Push and hold over speed test button of SSZB card on ( 1L1N ) to increase turbine speed ( >100% & < 110 % ) speed.

  5. Raise speed by means of "raise – lower" switch to 110% and check the turbine tripped and alarm over speed appear and record the trip speed (5589) RPM

  6. If the turbine not trip at 110% and arrive to 112% from rated speed. It must be tripped by mechanical over speed.

  7. If the turbine not trip at 112% from rated speed by mechanical over speed . it must be tripped by emergency stop push button then you must change speed trip card ( QOXC ) in 1L0J.

  8. Repeat test again from steps ( 1 to 6 ) and record speed when the turbine trip after changing the card (QOXC ) in 1L0J

  9. Disconnect the frequency counter, the master control switch to off position and check the ratchet system start.
