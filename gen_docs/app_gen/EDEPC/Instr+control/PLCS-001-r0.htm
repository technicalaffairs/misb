Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, anti static hand probe to avoid electrical chock, nose mouth dust cover, ... etc

  • The unit must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multi-meter.

  • Screw driver kit.

  • Hand held Radio wave for communication.

  • Antistatic vacuum cleaner for dust cleaning

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Visual inspection for the PLC system

  2. Clean the following parts:
    Panel rack, Electronic cards, Edge cards, Door filter, Air fan.

  3. Check the power supply must be   ( 110 AC ± 10 % ) , (+ve 24 ± 10 %)

  4. Retighten the wirings and cards

  5. Transfer from main processor to redundancy
