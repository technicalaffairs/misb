Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, anti static hand probe to avoid electrical shock, nose mouth dust cover, ... etc

  • Take care that the unit in service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multi-meter.

  • Screw driver kit.

  • Hand held Radio wave for communication.

  • Antistatic vacuum cleaner for dust cleaning

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the following parts:
    Panel rack, Electronic cards, Edge cards, Door filter, Air fan.

     

  2. Check the power Supply   ( 110 AC ± 10 % )

  3. Retighten the wiring and cards.

  4. No required calibration due to it have ibrom.

  5. Check the Papers.

  6. Check the ink.
