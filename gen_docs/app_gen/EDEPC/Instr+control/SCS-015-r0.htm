Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses

  • The unit must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Frequency counter ( 10 KHz ) .

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect Frequency counter to pin 1 and pin 31 from connector card A ( 1L7X )

  2. Install over speed test card furnished with gas turbine in place of the over speed trip card QOXC in 1LOJ. which causes over speed trip alarm appear.

  3. Press and hold (run push button) located on test card to cancel alarm.

  4. Turn master control switch to auto. Position and start the turbine.

  5. Remove hand from ( run push button ) located on test card after 14HR lamp light .

  6. When speed arrive to F.S.N.L. we adjust speed by means of " raise – lower " switch to 95 % of rated .

  7. Push and hold over speed test button of SSZB card on 1L1N. turbine speed  will ( > 100% & < 110% ) .

  8. Raise speed by means of " raise – lower " switch to 112% from the rated speed and check the turbine tripped and alarm over speed appear .

  9. If the turbine not trip at 112% from rated speed.  it must be tripped by emergency stop push button before 114% (<5800 RPM).

  10. Adjust mechanical over speed through mechanical maintenance.

  11. Repeat test again from steps ( 1 to 9 ) and record speed when the turbine tripped

  12. Disconnect the frequency counter, the master control switch to off position and check the ratchet system start.
