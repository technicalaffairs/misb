Introduction

The Load Control module L.C 2.4 (A20) will be in service after phasing the unit and its function to control the difference between the target load signal and the desired load signal. This Module is intended for the control of the turbo set output by means of turbine governor valves

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.
The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Farid, tested by eng. Ashraf Farid at 29 May 2003, revised by quality control EDEPCo. eng. Abdl Menem Al Emam, accepted by general director: eng. Aly Abd El-Whab, and approved by eng. Awad Mohamed Fathy; Head Sector of Talkha Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area, is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes …. etc

  • Unit out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85

  • mA injection set type Siemens  Calibrator O5 – 7NH2011

  • Screw driver

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect jumper between point x20:90 and x20:97 in rear control cabinet side NO: JC02 (Imitation condition: phased on)

  2. Disconnect point x20:108 (Imitation condition: trip oil ok)    

  3. Disconnect socket from card A31+54 type A2121B1 in front control cabinet side JC03 (Imitation condition: digital  speed).

  4. Connect jumper X20 : 90 , 99 (Imitation condition: boiler in load control)

  5. Disconnect signal vacuum point X10.3 and point x10:4 in rear control cabinet side NO: JC01.

  6. Disconnect socket from card A32 + 44 type D7210B2 to make failure inside module A32 to possibility Push bottom (Hand / Automatic) on desired Load to position A.

  7. Increase the load by push bottom + on target load equal 113Mw for example and observing increase departure of Load ......Figure 1

  8. Inject mA in point X20:1,2 slowly  and observing increase departure of  Load to zero   

  9. When the inject value equal 10 mA the load showed be 113 Mw (figure 2) then the departure of  Load equal zero and Push bottom (Hand / Automatic) on desired Load change to position Hand automatically.

  10. If step no. 9 not OK then check all circuit diagram for the module.

Return Circuit in Service

  1. Remove Jumper between point X20:90 and point X20:97.

  2. Connect Signal ( trip oil not ok ) point x20:108.

  3. Connect Socket of card A31+54.type A2121B1

  4. Remove Jumper between point X20:90 and point X20:99.

  5. Connect Signal vacuum x10:3 , x10:4

  6. Connect Socket of card A32 + 44 type D7210B2.
