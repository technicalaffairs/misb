Introduction

This document outlines the checklist I1, for all types of pressure, temperature, flow, and level indicators, which must be carried out on an annually bases as indicated  by Y1 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety helmet, shoes.

  • Close manual valve for Indicator line.

Tools and Equipments

  • Safety helmet, Safety shoes,

  • Pressure tester.

Work to be carried out

Steps to prepare for test

  1. Review technical reference material and manufacture’s manual.

  2. Obtain copy of indicator setting values and record it in its MRDS.

  3. Remove temperature indicator and take it to lab.

Testing Procedure

  1. Put the temperature indicator into the oven.

  2. Adjust the oven temperature to the correct value, check if the reading is correct.

  3. If there is any error, adjust the temperature indicator and repeat step 1 until reach the correct reading.

Return indicator to service

  1. Return temperature indicator to service by removing the oven.

  2. Return the pressure indicator back to its location.

  3. Place a copy of test result in the field maintenance file.
