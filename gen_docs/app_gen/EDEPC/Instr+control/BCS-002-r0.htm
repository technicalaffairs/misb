Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance stuff should carry the suitable safety category.  

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc.

  • Boiler must be out of service.

  • Don't allow to any material of fluids could cause a fire in the work area.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter  – Type Fluke 85.

  • Screw driver, Special wire connection.

  • Air blower & Spray – type: BW40

  • Indication lamp (24V)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Change burner mode to local control, Check the initial state of action elements in the local control box (MOS), open the MOS cover and check the signal panel by means of lamp test push button.

  2. If some of the signal lamps is off, the respective signal lamp should be replaced.

  3. If any of the signal lamps is ON, the fault must be removed.

  4. The signal lamps of the action elements are connected to the position-end switches which are in most cases a reason why the respective signal lamps are not activated.

  5. Cut the voltage from the cables to the igniter and the disconnection of cables on the igniter should be performed by a worker well acquainted with electric installation.

  6. The igniter is not subject to complete disassembling normally, but only in the case of damaged insulators, when replacing the ignition plugs or if insulation condition of the igniter is damaged and it is required to clean and dry it.

  7. Check ignition transformer:
    - Test the winding resistance.
    - Test insulation resistance.
    - Test the color and shape of spark.

  8. The sensor for flame detection forms Sylvania P 679 photoelectric cell using ultra-violet part of spectrum emitted by the flame service life maximum 8000 operational hours and replacement after this time.            

  9. The sensor consists primarily in cleaning the glass of the photoelectric cell, The requirement of maintaining the cables and contacts of the electric parts of sensor

  10. Check the card LID-13:  (Emission flame detector of the ignition burner)

  11. Check the card LUV-14:  (Ultra-violet flame detector of the load burner)

  12. Check the electromagnetic device (Solenoid-operated valves)

  13. Test of internal leakage, Check in case of it's fully open.

  14. Cleaning or replacing the filter on the control air line.

  15. The forced-feed lubricator dismantled once a half year at least, clean thoroughly ,check periodically and add the oil level , discharge impurities deposited at the container bottom.

Return Burner to Service

  1. Close the MOS cover.

  2. Return the burner components (igniter, sensor, solenoid valves, filter … etc) to initial state.

  3. Connect all power sources of the control system of burner.

  4. Keep the working area clean.

  5. Change burner mode to remote control.
