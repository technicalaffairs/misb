Introduction

This document outlines the equipment maintenance inspection I1  which must be performed every 1500 hours of operation on the Instrument and Control for Water Pumps

This procedure was applied in ABU QIR power plant since 1985, and the finial draft is issued in August 2004  

The first draft initiated by eng. Mohamed Ahmed Abd El Salam, approved by eng. Mohamed Abd El Sadek general director of Abu Kir PS, accepted by eng. Ahmed Taha Hashim Head Sector of  Abu Kir PS, certified by eng. Mona Ahmad El Sharkawy; WDEPC Inspection and Quality Control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety healthy, safety shoes

Tools and Equipment

  • Multi - meter

  • Hydraulic calibration

  • Calibrated pressure gauge

Work to be Carried Out

Instruments , Maintenance & Calibration

Manometers

  1. p10111, p10112, p10113, p10114, p10259, p10210, p10518

  2. Dismantle the glass cover of the manometer

  3. Dismantle the pointer with the special tool

  4. Dismantle the 2 bolts fixing the pointer marks

  5. Check the bourdon tub operation by hydraulic pressure tester

  6. Adjust the manometer by the internal adjusting screw using calbrated gauge

Thermo - Resistance Maintenance

  1. TTA (0104, 0105, 0106, 0306, 0305, 0507, 0508, 0509)

  2. Dismantle the nut fixing the thermo resistance

  3. Dismantle the thermo resistance terminals

  4. Measure the thermo resistance with ohm meter for good operation using standard table

  5. Connect the terminals and measure the resistance on the terminal connection

Flow Switch FSO 128

  1. Check the float direction with the water direction

  2. Test the make and break of the contact with ohmmeter

  3. Check the float arm in good state, and lubricate the moving arm

Pressure switch

  1. Check the pressure below in good state

  2. Connect the P.S to hydraulic pressure supply tester and adjust the pre-set point of the P,S in connection and break using ohmmeter

  3. Check the contact with ohmmeter

  4. Lubricate the mechanical moving parts of the pressure switch

Differential Pressure Switch's PD0115, PDSA0515

  1. Dismantle the D.P.S from its case & check the bellows of the differential P,S state

  2. Check the set screw is working to adjust the diff. pressure in increasing & decreasing the pressure

  3. Apply hydraulic pressure tester on the switch and check the operation of the contact in opening and closing, readjust if required dnd measure the contact with ohmmeter.
