Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Gabre Ali Ramadan tested by, eng. Mostafa Ibrahim,Alaa ELshal,. revised by quality control eng. Abdel Menem ALEmam at EDEPCo., accepted by general director, eng. Fawzy Sobhy Esmaile, and approved by eng. Monir Elwakel Head Sector of Damietta CCPS

Components

The Vibration System Consist of:

  • Two racks (16- cards)

  • Two cards power supply type3300/10: output = - 23.75 + .45 VDC

  • Two cards system monitor type3300/01

  • Six cards dual vibration type3300/15

  • Three cards differential expansion (3300/45) – one card differential expansion (  3300/46 ) – one card eccentricity  type3300/40 - one card dual thrust type3300/20.

  • Approximates (17 approximates)

  • Sensors (17 sensors)

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety Helmet, safety shoes ...etc

  • Unit in stand still.

Tools and Equipment

  • Multi-meter, Screw driver.

  • Contact cleaner

 

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Check insulation of wire sensor.

  2. Cleaning all cards.

  3. Check Tighten all connections at sensors, wiring, approximators

  4. Check the power supply of the system -23.75 + 0.5 VDC

  5. If the power OK, green lamp LED located in the monitor card is ON, means power supply is OK. If LED is off, then change the power supply card.

  6. Connect the DVM digital voltmeter as shown in the figure 1 and check the reading according to the table of MRDS

  7. If the reading of the channel is not acceptable adjust the gap of sensor to get the ref value.

  8. Repeat the steps for each bearing

  9. Remove all jumpers

  10. Close the work order.
