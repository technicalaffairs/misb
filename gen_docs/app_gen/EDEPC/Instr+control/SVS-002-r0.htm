Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedures P1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block

This document outlines the inspection checklist for electrical safety valve. There is one valve per unit. The major parts of the valve are (coil and a pressure switch). It is installed on the line of the main steam. The main target of it is to protect the main steam line from high pressure.

The switch will be closed and will supply the125 dc volt to the coil at a pressure of 144 kg/cm2 and the safety valve will be opened to decrease the high pressure. When the pressure decreases to 137 kg/cm2 the switch will be opened and disconnect the 125 dc volt to the coil, then the safety valve will be closed.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued; the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety Category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc

  • Isolate the valve manually.

  • Disconnect power supply.

  • Unit should be shutdown.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety helmet, shoes, and rags

  • Screw driver kit.

  • Pressure indicator

  • A hydraulic pump

  • Digital voltmeter, 125 DC volt external power supply.

Work to be Carried Out
Steps to Prepare for Test

  1. Review Technical reference material and manufacture’s manual.

  2. Obtain copy of (The Coil and the Pressure Switch) field maintenance File.

  3. Remove Coil and pressure switch Terminals by disconnecting wires (In the Terminal Cabinet).

Testing Procedure (Coil)

  1. Connect coil terminal to 125 DC voltage external power supply.    

  2. Turn on the power supply switch.

  3. The movement of the valve must be in the open direction.

  4. If no movement happened, check the coil-using multimeter and measure the resistance of the coil after disconnecting the terminals. If it's damaged, it has to be replaced with a new one.

Testing Procedure (Pressure Switch)

  1. Connect the multimeter to the terminals of the switch and measure the resistance. It has to be open.

  2. Inject a pressure using the hydraulic pump equals to the flowing calibration table

Open

137 kg/cm2

Close

144 kg/cm2

  1. Calibrate the switch to act like the status in the table.

Return Coil and Pressure Switch to Service

  1. Return coil to service by disconnecting it from the 125 DC voltage power supply

  2. Reconnect the wires to the cabinet termination.

  3. Return pressure switch to service by reconnect the wires to the cabinet termination.

  4. Connect main power.

  5. Open manual isolating valve.

  6. Place a copy of test result in the field maintenance file.

  7. Repeat all the previous steps for all other units.
