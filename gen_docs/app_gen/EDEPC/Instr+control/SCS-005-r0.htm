Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block,

The vibration sensor 39V 1,2,3. Dwg no. 158A7476 P008, Mfg: VITEC, USA, Vitec P/N: 602885-49T
The first draft initiated by eng. Taher Abdel Galel, tested by eng. Tarek El Sayed  Abdel Razeq at 02 June 2002, reviewed by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Abd El-Momem El-Emam, accepted by general director eng. Yousry Abd El-Maksuod, and approved by eng. Mostafa Allam head sector of Talkha PS

Safety Precautions

  • Unit must be shutdown

  • Work order must be issued

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital voltmeter

  • Suitable tool set

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Clean the external serves

  2. Check the cable of the sensor for tightness

  3. Check the output impedance IMP (Z) value < 500 ohms at temp (0 ºF to 400 ºF)

  4. Check the output voltage 200 mV at 1" / sec

  5. Repeat the steps from 1 to 4 for 39V2, 39V3
