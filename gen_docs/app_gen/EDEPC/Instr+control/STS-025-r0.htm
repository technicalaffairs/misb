  • Digital voltmeter

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Switch off the power supply of the processor to be checked  .

  2. Remove all the cards in the respective processor .

  3. Switch on the power supply and read voltages at the test ring points identified in the control specification drawing at  table (1) Slight differences from spec- should not be adjusted until the p.s is loaded.

  4. If voltage values are reasonable , Switch off the power supply and reset the cards  .

  5. Turn  the power supply back on and make final adjustments .

  6. If any voltage value is out of limit , adjust the value using the respective potentiometer indicated in table (1) {c.w  to rise the voltage & c.c.w to lower the voltage }

  7. Repeat step 6 above for all other power supply's.
