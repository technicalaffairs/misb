Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block to re-calibrate cards of Diamo (S) & (K), to ensure the limits of alarm and trip for all signals.
The first draft initiated by eng. Ramadan Helmy, tested at 04 May 2003, revised by quality control eng. Abdl Menem Al Emam at EDEPCo., accepted by: eng. Aly Abd Elwhab, and approved by eng. Awad Mohamed Fathy - Head Sector of talkha power station

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes, … etc.

    •

  • Turbine must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal computer type ( covtura 3/20 ) loaded  with diamo (S) software

  • Screw driver

  • Special wire connection

  • m.A Source  – Type Siemens Calibrator O5 - NH2011

  • Indication lamp ( 24v ) for control room .

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect the inlet signals of cable which need calibrated by removing the cabinet sockets and put the socket of m.A source.  

  2. Connect the special cable of computer to the processor diamo (s) card inside cabinet

  3. We make soft wear copy of the testing system form computer archive and open the page which we need.

  4. Inject inlet mA signal through the socket and check the reading limitation according to the specification sheet

  5. Increase mA until alarm lamp light on comparator card ( k752 ), control room, Information system and the computer ( diamo s )

  6. If the reading signal is the same ( diamo  s , diamo k , control room and information system ) , the system is ok .

  7. If any reading of signal not ok make adjustment for comparator  as mansion in calibration sheet . 

  8. If the comparator of ( diamo s ) not generate desired signal then make new copy of soft ware  from stored archive of computer to the card (S35) .and check again the test, if the test ok then the test complete.

  9. if the test not successful then change the card (s 35) and make again copy and check again.   

Return system in service

  1.  Remove the socket of mA source.

  2. Put the socket of signal in its position.

  3. Make rest of system.
