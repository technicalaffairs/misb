Introduction

This document out lines the equipment inspection to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.                              

The first draft initiated by eng. Rabea Mohamed Atia, tested by eng. Ibrahim Hefny at 09 May 2005, revised by EDEPC quality control eng. Abdel Menem Al Emam, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Ibrahem Azab, and approved by eng. Fayze Sobhe Ismaiel Head Sector of Abu Sultan Power Plant 4x150MW.

There is one Cabinet per unit .each cabinet divided in tow parts .for two different jobs: Soot blower  for boiler, Soot blower for Air heater.

We have one power supply for each cabinet, this power supply can supply the system by (110vac & 120vdc & 24vdc &12vdc &18.8vdc)

The (24&12 &18.8VDC) feed the controller cards (38 cards) like ( input, initial turn-on, alarm, universal alarm, "AND" permissive, "NOR" gated lamp driver, transfer, sequence, gated output, delay, multiplier, isolated diode, 5output diode, delay, relay cards) modules

The (120VDC) for driving dc header reducing station pilot solenoid valve (bv1 for boiler header, BV2  for AH, BV3for AH start up steam supply.

At the boiler there are one control valve watch, adjust the pressure of the header equal 35k bar and we have also 16 limit switch.

At the air heater we have one control valve watch, adjusts the pressure of the header equal 35kbar, two on/off valves at each air heater and two limit switches.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued ,the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category .

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, anti static hand probe to avoid electrical choke and electrostatic effects.

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet, Shoes, and rags.

  • Screw Driver Kit.

  • Voltmeter.

 

Work to be Carried Out

Steps to Prepare for Check

  1.  Review Technical reference material and manufacture’s manual.

  2. Obtain copy of  (soot Blower Cabinets) field maintenance File..

  3. Check the power supply and measuring the out put voltage.
          * In put voltage 110VAC.
          * Output voltage (120 & 24 &12 &18.8 VDC)

  4. Check the mechanical parts.

  5. Calibrated the control valve to adjust the pressure of the header.

  6. Check the limit SW.

Testing Procedure (Soot Blower)

  1. Open The Cabinet and Notice Each led on the system cabinet.

  2. If there is alarm we can know the problem.

  3. If there are no alarm the system is good.

  4. Repeat all the previous steps for all cabinets and for all units.
