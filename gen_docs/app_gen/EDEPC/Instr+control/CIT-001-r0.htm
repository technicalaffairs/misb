Introduction

This document out lines the testing procedures P1, to be performed on a monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

This document outlines the checks & schedule services CO% (2devices) & CO2% (2 devices) & O2% (3 devices) measuring devices.

These gases are the exhausts in the boiler, which their percentages must be measured by these devices to process them before getting out to the atmospheric air by the chimney.

by getting samples from 2 levels from the boiler and get into the analyzer in the device, their results appear on local indicating meter on the device and recorders at control room.

  • Test gas cylinder: [mixture of inert gas and a certain concentrations of the gas component which is to be measured by the analyzer, the concentration should be 60%:100% of the FINOR measuring range, for testing CO%&CO2% measuring device.

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85.

Work to be Carried Out

  1.  Change filter when its white color material is clearly discolored.

CO2% Device Test

  1. Before calibration we must check the mechanical zero of the indicating meter by switching off the FINOR, the needle should be exactly on the extreme left mark of the scale [electrical zero] if not, carefully correct by means of the set screw of the instrument.

  2. Switch off the device and connect the Nitrogen to the device after take off the sample from boiler

  3. Switch on the device, wait to stabilizer and check the reading still zero, if not correct the reading by the potentiometer (zero point)

  4. Switch off the device, remove the nitrogen and connect the gas cylinder test to the device

  5. Switch on the device, wait to stabilizer and check the reading should 20 % Volt, If not correct the reading by the potentiometer (span point)

CO% Device Test

Repeat the steps from 1 to 5 for range 0 V to 1 V

O2% Device Test

  1. Repeat the steps from 1 to 3

  2. Switch off the device, remove the nitrogen and connect the device to air

  3. Switch on the device, wait to stabilizer and check the reading should 5 VOL

  4. If not correct the reading by the potentiometer (span point)

Return the Device to Services

Connect to sample gas and switch to desired measuring range.
