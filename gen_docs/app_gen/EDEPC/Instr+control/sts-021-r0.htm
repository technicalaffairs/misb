Safety Precautions

  • Unit at shut down mode.

  • A work permit must be issued ,the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category .

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Switch off all CB's on the motor control center (MCC)

Tools and Equipment

  • Test oscillator, test counter.

  • Screw driver, Jumpers.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Select "DATA" list 16, logic forcing and enter logic signals L14HR, L14HM, L14HA and L14HS on the CRT display

  2. Slowly increase the oscillator frequency from zero and verify that the zero speed logic L14HR resets to logic "0" at the dropout speed given in the control spec. –setting adjust from constant TNK 14HR if dropout does not occur at the correct speed.

  3. Slowly decrease the oscillator frequency and verify that the zero speed logic L14HR goes to a logic "1" at the pickup speed given in the control spec. setting adjust from constant TNK 14HR if correction is required.  

  4. Continue to increase the oscillator frequency  and verify that all of the other speed logic signals change to a logic "1" at the pickup speed listed  in the control spec. –setting . adjust corresponding from constants listed if required as shown in table (1).

  5. Slowly decrease the oscillator frequency from full speed and verify that all of the speed logic signals resets to a logic "0" at the dropout speed listed in the control spec. setting adjust corresponding from constants listed if required.
