Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which must performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

REC 920 is a steam turbine control and safety systems it contains the following subsystems:

Speed Control System, Vibration System, Safety System, PLC system, Load Control System, Gland Steam Control System, By Pass Control System   

Components:  The speed control system consist of:

One Rack (9 cards):

- Two cards type AH112 - One card type AH113 - One card type AH111.

- One card type LV 110 - One card type MZ134 - Three cards type LV108

Three way servo valve.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety Helmet, safety shoes ...etc

  • Unit in stand still.

  • When working on steam valves oil circuit ensure that no one is working on the components of control oil system as valves, pipes, pumps, and so on  by coordinating with shift engineer.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multi meter,

  • Wire connection

  • Contact cleaner

 

Work to be Carried Out 

  1. Clean all cards

  2. Tighten all bolts and terminals.

  3. Check the card according to the MRDS (1)

  4. Calibrate the servo control valves according to the MRDS (2)

  5. Simulate the following Signal to make the system available.

  6. Jumper X16(21&22) to ensure that the lube oil pressure is OK.

  7. Jumper XX4(57&58) to ensure that the vacuum condenser is OK.

  8. Remove X16(97) to ensure that the Processing failure is OK.

  9. Put the control oil pump in operation

    10. .

  10. Remove the connections between the card and servo control valves.

  11. Inject the signal to the card LV108 and check the reading according to the MRDS (2).

  12. Remove all jumpers

  13. Stop the control oil pump

  14. Close the work order.
