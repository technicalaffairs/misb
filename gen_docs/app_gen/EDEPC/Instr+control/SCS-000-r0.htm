Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Personal safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety shoes, and safety Helmet.

 

Tools and Equipments

  • Digital AVO. Meter

  • Remover card

  • Special tool to adjust the variable resistance at the cards

Work to be Carried Out

 

Start up Card (SSKC) (1L 1R)

  1. Put voltmeter at between pin 24 & Acom. On the card SSKC

  2. Press and hold at push button (FIRE) at cart SSKC (1L 1R) it must be 3.6 volt if it is not correct, it must be adjust.

  3. Press and hold at push button (WU) at cart SSKC (1L 1R) it must be 3.3 volt if it is not correct, it must be adjust.

  4. Press and hold at push button acceleration (ACC) and button (TCC) together at cart SSKC (1L 1R) it must be 4.45 volt if it is not correct, it must be adjust from ACC .

  5. Press at push button (MIN) at cart SSKC (1L 1R) it must be 3.15 volt if it is not correct, it must be adjust.

  6. Press at push button (MAX) at cart SSKC (1L 1R) it must be 8.5 volt if it is not correct, it must be adjust.
