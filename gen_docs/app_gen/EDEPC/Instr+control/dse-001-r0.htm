Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, which must be performed every three months as indicated by M3 in the title block

Technical Specifications:

The 12 V -71- Detroit diesel engine (starting - 500 HP detroit diesel engine), the diesel is a 12 cylinder, 2 cycle engine, with a speed rating of 2300 rpm

The first draft initiated by eng. Tamer Ghonem, test by eng. Rasha Ellabban, accepted by eng. Sawsan El lithe general manager, revised by eng. Zakaria El-Shahat EDEPC MPIS coordinator and approved by eng. Mohamed Hussein Gomaa quality insurance head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes eye glass, nose mouth dust cover ... etc

  • The diesel engine must be shutdown for oil checks

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Oil injection pump.

  • Avometer.

Work to be Carried Out

Testing electrical solenoid valves

  1. 20 DV (diesel engine gas stop valve solenoid valve)

  2. 20 DT (diesel engine emergency trip solenoid valve)

  3. 20 DA (diesel engine accelerating solenoid valve - inter mediate)

Testing

  1. 20 DV (by energizing the relays (4D-1, 4DE-2) in the speed tronic panel and listing to valve in the diesel compartment)

  2. 20 DT (by energizing the relays (4D-1, 4DE-2) in the speed tronic panel and listing to valve  in the diesel compartment)

  3. 20 DA (by energizing the relays (20DA-1X) in the speed tronic panel and listing to valve  in the diesel compartment)

Testing the pressure switches (63 DM, 63QD)

  1. 63 DM (diesel engine - minimum speed).

  2. 63 QD (diesel engine lube oil pressure).

Testing (63 DM, 63 QD)

  1. By injecting oil by using oil pump from where the oil is entering the pressure switch then we put the leads of the Avometer on the pressure switch contact points and observe at witch pressure the contact points change its position from open to close and vice versa then we record these points in the table below

Testing the diesel starter motor (march)

  1. Check the brushes of the motor and changing them if needed.

  2. Measure the motor coil insulation.

  3. Run the motor on load by connecting the switch No: 4 in the motor control center and pressing push button 43 DE and observing the march motor running.
