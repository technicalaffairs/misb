Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and qualified maintenance stuff should carry the suitable safety category.  

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, ... etc.

  • Boiler must be out of service.           

  • Don't allow to any material of fluids could cause a fire in the work area.

Tools and Equipment

  • Personal computer type (cortura 3/20) leaded with diamo(S) software.

  • Screw driver, Special wire connection

  • mA source type siemens calibrator 05-NH2011.

  • Indication lamp (24V) for control room

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect inlet signals of cable (HC 04) which need to be calibrated by removing the cabinet sockets and put the socket of mA source.

  2. Connect the special cable of computer to the processor diamo(S) card inside cabinet (HC 04)

  3. We make software copy of the soot blower programming from computer archive.

  4. Inject inlet mA signal through the socket and check the reading limitation according to specification sheet.

  5. If the reading signal is the same, the system is ok.

  6. If the comparator of software programming does not generate desired signal then make new copy of software from stored archive of computer to the card (S 35) and check again the test ,if the test is ok then the test is completed.

  7. If the test not successful, then change the card (S 35) and make another copy.

  8. Check and adjustment for all soot blower care in local part.

  9. Check and adjustment the steam reducing station.

Return System to Service

  1. Remove the socket of mA source.

  2. Put the socket of signal in its position.

  3. Make reset of system.
