Introduction

Medium Speed Changer device (MSO) to adjust speed of turbine.

NOTE: Speed changer device is used to increase the turbine speed from heating speed (1200 rpm) to phasing speed (3000 rpm) when the conditions are OK (steam temp., steam pressure, turbine casing temp., turbine shaft temp. .......etc)

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.
The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Farid, tested by eng. Ashraf Farid at 22 June 2003, revised by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Abdl Menem  Al Emam, accepted by general director: eng. Aly Abd El-Whab, and approved by eng. Awad Mohamed Fathy; Head Sector of Talkha Power Station.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes …. etc

  • Unit in service under speed control (before phasing) i.e Not synchronizing

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter type -Fluke 85 at what step you used it ??

  • Screw driver.

 

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Change from parallel mode to Hygo Mode by pushing bottom Hygo from control panel then speed will increases automatically more than 3000 rpm (from15 to 25 rpm).

  2. Decrease speed by M.S.O (medium speed changer) manually to 2980 RPM and increase again to 3000 RPM by using the push bottom H / A in Automatic mode then Indication lamp of 3000  RPM light go (off). 

  3. Change setting from the comparator (A33) in diamo cabinet no: HC11 until lamp of 3000 RPM comes on  and change to Parallel Mode again.
    Note: When Changing from parallel to Hygo, speed increase from 15 to 25 rpm

  4. Change from Hygo mode to parallel mode and m.s.o open 60% to return the circuit into service
