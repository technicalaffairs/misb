Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet ...etc.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Unit should be at shutdown mode.

  • Switch off all CB's on the (MCC)

Tools and Equipment

  • Frequency source, Frequency meter

  • Screw driver, Jumpers

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect a frequency source and frequency meter to the turbine magnetic speed pickup (77NH) of the analog I/O modules < AIO3 > and jumper the remaining pickups in parallel to the first simulate HP turbine speed to < R > < S > and < T > 
    Note :
    It may be necessary to disconnect the magnetic pickup to prevent loading down the test oscillator signal

  2. Display L12H and L4Post logic signals on the CRT (Cathode Ray Tube)

  3. Increase the frequency until logic signal L12H goes to "1" the frequency should be per control syst settings. Check that alarm massage "electrical over speed trip"  at 5697+10% RPM is displayed. Check that L12H and L4Post indicate trip state in R.S.T

  4. Adjust overspeed trip constant TNKHOS if the trip setpoint is not per control syst setting, record new constant TNKHOS. If it is not OK replace the hardware card and repeat the step from 1-4

  5. Manually reset L86MR1 and force test permissive logic L83HOST to logic "1"

  6. Raise the governor speed setpoint using the speed control raise switch until the governor reaches the max limit.

  7. If the setpoint is not per control syst setting for max search speed TNKHOST readjust TNKHOST record new value

  8. Remove all instrumentation.
