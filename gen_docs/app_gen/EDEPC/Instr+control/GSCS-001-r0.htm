Components

One processing and control boards type µ R16

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Unit should be at standstill mode.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multimeter, screw driver

  • Wire connection

  • Micro console MP 11, bailey

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Cleaning all cards

  2. Check tighten all connections & wiring

  3. Connect the socket of the micro console to the gland steam control card (HP)

  4. Code 00  ←I (enter).

  5. Show the data in raise order by enter (val) ←I (enter).

  6. Comprise the present value to the one of the manual

  7. For any remarkable deviation of the two readings enter (var) ←I (enter).

  8. Then enter the right value

  9. Continuous until the procedure is end

  10. Close the work order
