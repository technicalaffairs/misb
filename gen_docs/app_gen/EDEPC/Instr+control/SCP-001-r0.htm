Introduction

This document out lines speed control and speed protection maintenance procedure P1 to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

This system control & protect the Gas Turbine, consist of: speed sensor & speed connection device: 102412, UT 464 2A, ELXT 5514 a, PT 5501a , VT5507

The first draft  initiated by eng. Mohamed Aly Aly Hassan, tested by eng.Al Shahat Ahmed Mousa at 01 March 2003, revised by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Adel El-Meniem Al Emam, accepted by General Director eng. Hamdy Abd El Moola Melegy, approved by eng. Yousry Mohamed Aly Salama Alam Head Sector of Al Shaba GT Power Station, and certified by eng. Aly Mostafa AL Lethy EDEPC inspection and quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes …. Etc

  • Ensure that the Gas Turbine is out of service, at cold state

Tools and Equipment

  • Dry Air Blower

  • Multimeter and connection wires

  • Contact cleaner.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Measure the voltage of the card and take the number of it.

  2. Record the results in the test sheet.

  3. Check values according to the setting values.

  4. If these values changed compared with the setting values then adjust this values using resistance (012-R3)

  5. If this values not accepted after adjusting then replace the card with a new one and adjust it according to the given values.
