Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, qualified maintenance staff carry the suitable safety ID category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Safety and healthy equipment must be used as safety shoes, safety Helmet ..etc

    •

  • Turbine shout down & all MCC CB off (racked out)

Tools and Equipments

  • Digital AVO Meter,

  • Remover card, Frequency meter

  • Speedtronic calibrator set model  (151A4925)

  • Special tool to adjust the variable resistance at the cards

Work to be Carried Out

14HR Speed Relay

  1. Connect ST calibrator connector A&B in OW and OV slots and jumper (1C, 1B) on the calibrator and connect frequency meter between 1C&G1

  2. Inject Speed from calibrator / 15 rpm and check 14HR light is on  and  relay is drop out  (no adjust )

  3. Decrease speed and check that 14HR is picked up and light is off   

14HM Speed Relay

  1. Alternat jumper from (1C, 1B) to (1C, 2B) on the calibrator

  2. Jumper pen 3 on SVSE card to A com

  3. Inject Speed from calibrator at  20% OF SPEED or ( 952 + 50 RPM ) and check that 14HM light is on  and  relay is pick up if it is not  adjust from (R205 )

  4. Remove jumper from pin 3

  5. Decrease speed and check that 14HM is light is off and relay is drop out at speed 92+- 25 RPM

14HA Speed Relay

  1. Inject Speed from calibrator at (2040 +100 RPM) and check that 14HA light is on and relay is pick up if it is not adjust from (R207)

  2. Decrease speed and check that 14HA is light is off and relay is drop out  at speed 1530 + 40 RPM

14HS Speed Relay

  1. Inject Speed from calibrator at ( 4845 + 100 RPM ) and voltage = 9.54v and

  2. Check that 14HS light is on  and  relay is pick up if it is not  adjust from (R202 )
