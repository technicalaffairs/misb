Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be performed after normal shutdown as indicated in the title block

The first draft initiated by eng. Adel Abdel Aziz, tested by eng. Abdelel Meneim Ibrahem Haikal at 16 June 2002, reviewed by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Abd El-Momem El-Emam, accepted by general director eng. Yousry Abd El-Maksuod, and approved by eng. Mostafa Allam head sector of Talkha PS

Safety Precautions

  • Unit must be shutdown.

  • Work order must be issued

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • 2 Spanner 25 mm

  • 2 Gasket

  • Sand paper (fine)

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check the spark plug assembly for freedom from binding.

  2. Check that the electrode in a good condition by pulling smooth the the top of electrode its self (take care not to pull from the cable)

  3. Check the leakage of hot gases.

  4. Check the HV cable for good connection.
