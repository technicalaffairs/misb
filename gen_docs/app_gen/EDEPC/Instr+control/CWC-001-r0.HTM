Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be carried out on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the above title block

Condensate water circuit function is to transfer the main condensate water from main condenser to feed water Tank (FWT) via Four LP Heaters.

Condensate water circuit consists of two condensate pumps with 6KV motor drives, open loop control & closed loop control for controlling condensate water circuit.

Open loop control (sequence control), which have releases and protections for sequence control group control (GC) & Sub-Group (SGC) and 6KV motor drive and its condensate Pumps.

Closed loop circuits function to control the main condenser level closed loop, feed water tank level closed loop, and minimum flow closed loop to protect the condensate pumps.

Main condenser level closed loop to control the condenser level via the main condensate control valve (from condenser to FWT).

Feed water tank level closed loop to controls FWT level via make up control valves (from FWT to make up tanks).

Minimum flow closed loop to control the minimum flow via minimum flow control valve (from discharge pumps to main condenser).

The closed loop circuits components are Level/Flow transmitters, control system (DCS), and motorized control valves (Siemens actuator).

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued; the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, anti static hand probe to avoid electrical shock …etc

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required       

  • The unit must be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multi-meter.

  • Screw driver kit, manual tools

  • Oil spry cleaner & Teflon roll.

  • Pneumatic Calibrator

  • External 24VDC power supply.

  • Current injector

Work to be Carried Out

For testing of condensate system perform the following:

First check releases and protections of the condensate pumps

Second check closed loop circuits of main condenser level, FWT level, and minimum flow

Preparation

  1. Obtain a copy of individual devices (level/flow transmitters, limit switches, flow switches, temperature switch, RTD, diff. pressure switch, position transmitter, motorize valve (Auma actuator), motorize control valves (Siemens actuator), power adjuster) maintenance manuals.

  2. Obtain a copy of SAMA diagram of condensate water control system.

First Releases and Protections of the Condensate Pumps

Releases and Protections of Sequence Control (GC & SGC) Condensate Pumps

  1. Check the Flow Transmitter of the main condensate water according to approved MPIS, and Checks low / high flow limit values setting. This Check needs the unit out service.

  2. Check the Limit Switches of close signal for HP Stop for valves and LP Bypass stop valves according to approved MPIS. This check needs the unit out service.

  3. Check  the Level Transmitter of the main condenser according to approved MPIS, and check high level limit value setting. This check needs the unit out service.

  4. Check Limit Switches of close signal for Min. flow control valve main condensate water and level control valve of main condenser level.

Releases and Protections of 6KV Motor Drive for Condensate Pumps

  1. Check the motor cooling water flow switch according to approved MPIS, and check signal F<Min. on the monitoring system

  2. Check the Limit Switches of downstream and upstream valves according to approved MPIS.

  3. Check the Limit Switches of discharge first stage and suction second stage valves according to approved MPIS.

  4. Check the motorize (auma drive) valve stroke of Polishing unit bypass according to approved MPIS.

  5. Check the thrust bearing temperature switch according to approved MPIS, and check signal T>Max. on the monitoring system.

  6. Check RTD of slot winding temperature of 6KV motor according to approved MPIS and check the limit values temperature setting.

  7. Check the diff. Pressure switch of condensate filter pump according to approved MPIS, and check signal DP>Max. on the monitoring system.

  8. Check the Level Switches of condenser level according to approved MPIS.

Second Closed Loop Circuit of Condensate Water Circuit

Closed Loop Circuit of Main Condenser

  1. Check the level transmitter according to approved MPIS.

  2. Check the devices termination and cable termination of control valve (Siemens actuator).

  3. Check the power adjuster (24VDC/380VAC).

  4. Check the open/close limit switches, open/close torque switches and check of its mechanism.

  5. Check the power supply position transmitter check of its mechanism.

  6. Check the stroke of control valve and adjust position transmitter according to stroke.

Closed Loop Circuit of FWT Level

  1. Check the level transmitter according to approved MPIS.

  2. Check the devices termination and cable termination of control valve (Siemens actuator).

  3. Check the power adjuster (24VDC/380VAC).

  4. Check the open/close limit switches, open/close torque switches and check of its mechanism.

  5. Check the power supply position transmitter check of its mechanism.

  6. Check the stroke of control valve and adjust position transmitter according to stroke.

Closed Loop Circuit of Minimum Flow

  1. Check the flow transmitter according to approved MPIS.

  2. Check the devices termination and cable termination of control valve (Siemens actuator).

  3. Check the power adjuster (24VDC/380VAC).

  4. Check the open/close limit switches, open/close torque switches and check of its mechanism.

  5. Check the power supply position transmitter check of its mechanism.

  6. Check the stroke of control valve and adjust position transmitter according to stroke.
