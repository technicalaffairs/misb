Introduction

This Document outlines the maintenance procedures P1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The hydrogen producing unit function is to produce hydrogen gas by electrical analysis to demi-water.

The Hydrogen Production Unit consists of:: one hydrogen gas electrolyser, two discharge compressors, one hydrogen gas storage tank, one bypass loop of generated hydrogen gas, one feed demiwater loop, and one hydrogen gas discharge bottles line.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, …etc

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • The hydrogen producing unit must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multi-meter.

  • Screw driver kit, manual tools

  • Oil spry cleaner & Teflon roll...

  • Pneumatic & Hydraulic  Calibrators

  • External 24VDC power supply.

  • Temperature Calibrator

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Preparation

  1. Obtain a copy of (level switches, pressure switches, temperature Switches, solenoid valves) maintenance manuals

  2. Obtain a copy of relay control circuit diagram of Hydrogen Producing unit   

Release and Protection Test

Hydrogen Gas Electrolyser

  1. Check pressure switch of suction pressure w.r.t MPIS doc PS-001-r0 and check this alarm on alarm window for Electrolyser.

  2. Check pressure switch of discharge pressure  w.r.t MPIS doc PS-001-r0 and check this alarm on alarm window.

  3. Check the solenoid valve of feed demi-water to electrolyser.

  4. Check the temperature switch of feed demi-water and check this alarm on alarm window for Electrolyser.

  5. Check level switches of hydrogen storage tank.

  6. Check level switches of demi-water/ hydrogen for drum level and check this alarm on alarm window.

Discharge Compressors

  1. Check pressure switch of suction pressure of each discharge compressor w.r.t MPIS doc PS-001-r0 and check this alarm on alarm window.

  2. Check pressure switch of 1st stage discharge pressure of each discharge compressor  w.r.t MPIS doc PS-001-r0 and check this alarm on alarm window.

  3. Check pressure switch of 2nd stage discharge pressure of each discharge compressor  w.r.t MPIS doc PS-001-r0 and check this alarm on alarm window.

  4. Check level switch of cooling water for each discharge compressor.

  5. Check level switches of hydrogen storage tank.

The Hydrogen bypass Loop Check

  1. Check the three ways of the solenoid valve in both manual mode and auto mode.

  2. Check high level switch.
