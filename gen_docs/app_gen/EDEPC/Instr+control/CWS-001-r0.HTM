Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be carried out every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block

The cooling water system is divided to two main subsystems:

Main cooling water (sea water) subsystem

Secondary cooling water (Demi Water) subsystem

  

Main cooling water (Sea Water) subsystem to condensate the steam inside the main condenser, condensate steam inside boiler feed pump turbine condenser and cooling Secondary cooling water main coolers.

Secondary cooling water (Demi water) subsystem function to cool 6KV motors and oil coolers

Main cooling water (sea water) subsystem is consisting of five branches for both unit1&2, and three branches for each units 3&4 .for each branch is consisting of primary sea water cleaner (Trash Rack), secondary sea water cleaner (Band screen) and 6KV sea water pump.

Secondary cooling water (Demi water) subsystem is consists of storage tank, Two secondary water pump, coolers and Emergency Secondary cooling closed loop circuit (If the secondary Cooling pumps are out of services, The emergency secondary cooling circuit will be worked for cooling the sampling system, air pre-heater (Lj) and recirculation pumps about emergency secondary cooling solenoid valves).

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued; the maintenance staff should be qualified maintenance manuals

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, anti static hand probe to avoid electrical shock …etc

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required       

  • Obtain a copy of relay control circuit diagram of Trash Rack & Band Screen cleaners

  • Obtain a copy of release and protection SAMA diagram of main/secondary pumps.

  • Obtain a copy of control circuit diagram for discharge valve (Flap Valve) of main cooling water pump

    •

  • The unit must be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multi-meter.

  • Screw driver kit, manual tools

  • Oil spry cleaner & Teflon roll.

  • Pneumatic Calibrator

  • External 24VDC power supply.

  • Current injector

Work to be Carried Out

Release & Protection Test for Main Cooling Water Subsystem

Primary Sea Water Cleaner (Trash Rack)

  1. Check the limit switches according to the approved MPIS and checks this alarm on alarm window and adjust the stroke of trash rack.

  2. Check level switches and check the mechanical cams of Manual/Auto operating of trash rack control according to the approved MPIS and check this alarm on alarm window.

Secondary Sea Water Cleaner (Band Screen)

  1. Check level switches and check the mechanical cams of Manual/Auto operating of control band screen for operation normal speed and high speed according to the approved MPIS and check this alarm on alarm window.

Main Cooling Water Pumps

  1. Check rtd of slot winding temperature of 6kv motor according to the approved MPIS and check the limit values temperature setting for release & protection of main cooling pump

  2. Check rtd of thrust bearing of main cooling pump according to the approved MPIS and check the limit values temperature setting for release & protection of main cooling pump

  3. Check ultrasonic level transmitter which measure sea water level according to the approved MPIS and check the limit values setting of sea water level for release & protection of main cooling pump, this check need the unit out service.

  4. Check cooling water flow switch of 6kv motor according to the approved MPIS and check the minimum flow setting for release & protection of main cooling pump.

  5. Check lubrication water flow switch of thrust bearing according to the approved MPIS and check the minimum flow setting for release & protection of main cooling pump, and check the automatic operation lubrication water pumps.

  6. Check the inlet& return hydraulic solenoid valves of discharge valve (flap valve) of main cooling water pump according to approved MPIS.

  7. Check the limit switches of percentages stroke of discharge valve (flap valve) of main cooling water pump according to approved MPIS.

  8. Check the relay control of hydraulic system for opening and closing discharge valve (flap valve) in automatic and manual made of main cooling water pump, this check need the unit out service.

  9. check the relay control of hydraulic system for operating the hydraulic pumps in automatic and manual mode of main cooling water pump, this check need the unit out service

Release & Protection Test for Secondary Cooling Water Subsystem

Secondary Cooling Water Pumps

  1. Check RTD of slot winding temperature of 6kv motor according to the approved MPIS and check the limit values temperature setting for release & protection of main cooling pump.

  2. Check flow transmitter of secondary cooling pump according to the approved MPIS and check the limit values setting of secondary water flow for release & protection.

  3. Check the level switches of secondary water tank according to the approved MPIS and check the minimum level for release & protection of secondary cooling pump.

Secondary Cooling Water Tank

  1. Check the level switches of secondary water tank according to the approved MPIS which opening and closing follow up valve automatically

Emergency Secondary Cooling Water Circuit

  1. If the secondary cooling pumps are out of services, the emergency secondary cooling circuit will be worked for cooling the sampling system, air preheater (Lj) and recirculation pumps about emergency secondary cooling solenoid valves.

  2. Check the emergency secondary cooling solenoid valves according to the approved MPIS which opening and closing follow up valve automatically, this check need the unit out service
