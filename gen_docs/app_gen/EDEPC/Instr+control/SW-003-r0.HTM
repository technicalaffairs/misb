Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedure I1, which must be performed every 6 months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Hesham El Sayed Mohamed El Assy, tested by eng. Mohamed Gad El Rab Hawad at 24 March 2003, reviewed by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Abdel Momem El-Emam, accepted by general director eng. Farouk Awad Aly, and approved by eng. Mostafa Alam Head Sector of Talkha power plant.

ZAT-D is system for regulation and control of technological processes. It is determined especially for use in energetic; it could be used even in other industrial branches. Process control is divided into several decentralized parts of system, interconnected through communication network. Entire system could be hierarchically divided into three basic parts:
     - Visualization system
     - Control system
     - Modular station, contingently other peripherals
