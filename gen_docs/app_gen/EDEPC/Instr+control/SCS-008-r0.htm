Introduction

This document outlines the inspection check list I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The first draft initiated by eng. Sawsan El_Lethy, tested by eng Wael Taha Yousef at 27 Feb. 2004, reversed by EDEPCo. quality control  eng. Abdel Menem AL Emam, and approved by eng. Mohamed Goda EL_Said General Director of Port Said G.T. Power Station, certified by eng. Aly Mostafa Al-Lethy, EDEPCo. Inspection and Quality Control Head Sector.

Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the personal protective equipment as (safety shoes, Helmet).

  • Turbine shout down & all Motor Control Center "MCC" CB off (racked out).

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital AVO. Meter, Remover card

  • Speedtronic calibrator set model (151A4925)

  • Special tools to adjust the variable resistance at the cards.

Card SSLB (1L 1U)

  1. When we turn “raise-lower” switch to raise check that ”raise” light goes on and set point increase to MAX. measured the voltage by connect voltammeter at point (1G) and (10H) at calibrator set { pin (39 ) of card SSLB }. It must be (-7 volt) if it is not adjust it must be adjust from R(124){4}  at card SSLB (1L1U).

  2. When we turn “raise-lower” switch to lower check that ”lower” light goes on and set point decrease to Min. measured the voltage by connect voltammeter at point (1G) and (10H) at calibrator set. It must be (5 volt) if it is not adjust it must be adjust from R(125){3}  at card SSLB (1L1U).
