Introduction

The pressure control Module PC 2.5 (A21) is intended for the control of admission (super) steam pressure by means of turbine governor

Module is intended for the control of admission Steam pressure by means of turbine Governor
This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.
The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Farid, tested by  eng. Ashraf Farid at 17 June 2003, revised by quality control EDEPC eng. Abdl Menem Al Emam, accepted by general director: eng. Aly Abd El-Whab, and approved by eng. Awad Mohamed Fathy; Head Sector of Talkha Power Station.

Safety Precautions  

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes …. etc

  • Unit out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85

  • mA injection set type Siemens Calibrator O5 – 7NH2011

  • Screw driver

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect jumper between point x20:90 and point X20:97 in rear control cabinet side JC02  (Imitation condition: phased on)

  2. Disconnect point x20:108. in rear control cabinet side JC02 (Imitation: Trip oil ok) 

  3. Disconnect socket A31+54 typeA2121B1 in front control cabinet JC03     (Imitation: digital speed) 

  4. Connect jumper between point X20:90 and point X20:99 in rear control cabinet side JC02  (Imitation condition: boiler in load control )

  5. Disconnect vacuum signal point X10.3 and point X10:4 in rear control cabinet side NO: JC01.

  6. Disconnect socket A32 + 44 type D7210B2 to ability change push bottom H/A on desired pressure to position automatic (A) .

  7. Inject mA Equal Admission steam pressure according to the attached curve on point X20:3,4 in rear control cabinet side JC02 and follow the attached curve.

  8. Measure volt on point A21+44:27  typeH3202B1 & A10+60:13 a type A2804B1and A21+44:30typeH3202B1 & A21+52:3  type A2110B1 to see that module cards tracing this inject value according  to the circuit  diagram.

  9. If measuring not good Chang the bad card .

  10. Change push bottom H/A on desired pressure to Automatic mode and observing departure of pressure.

  11. When actual pressure equal desired pressure  the push bottom H/A on desired pressure changed automatically  to hand mode and the push bottom lamp light

Return Module in Service

  1. Remove Jumper between point X20:90 and point X20:97 .

  2. Connect Signal ( trip oil not ok ) point x20:108.

  3. Connect Socket A31+54 typeA2121B1.

  4. Remove Jumper between point X20:90 and point X20:99 .

  5. Connect Signal vacuum x10:3 , x10:4

  6. Connect Socket A32+44 type D7210B2
