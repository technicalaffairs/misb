Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block 

The following specification:
- Control system : MARK II
- Total shipped : 1825
- Sequencing : Discrete solid state components
- Control : Integrated circuits
- Protection : Relays & solid state
- Display : Analog and digital meter ; solid state annunciator
- Input : Push buttons and bat handle switches
- Fault Tolerance : Manually rejectable failed exhaust thermocouples

Safety Precautions

  • Work order must be issued

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

  • Don't use an ultrasonic cleaner

  • Don't use compressed air

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set, spray contact cleaner

  • Digital voltmeter

  • Dry air blower, brushes

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Any indication of corrosion or discoloration at Speed tronic frame, due to heat, should be investigated and the equipment repaired or replaced as necessary.

  2. The relative humidity of the speed tronic control page location should be maintained at 50% or less to minimize any possible corrosion problems.

Circuit board

  1. First clean by dry natural air to remove dust

  2. Clean the board by use of detergent solution such as video spray with the aid of bristled brush

  3. Visually inspect the board for cleanliness and repeat step 3, if necessary
