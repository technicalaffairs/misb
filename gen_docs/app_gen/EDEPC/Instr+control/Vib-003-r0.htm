Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which should be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

This sensor used to measure the bearing vibration from turbine, generator and exciter bearings.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the safety and healthy equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, earplug, gloves pairs...etc

  • Unit should be out of service .

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multi-meter, type Fluke 85

  • Screw driver.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Measure the resistance between point 2 and point 1, it must be 1.8 kΩ.

  2. Measure the resistance between point 2 and point 3, it must be 2 kΩ.

  3. Measure the resistance between point ( 1, 2 or 3 ) and point 6, it must be ∞ Ω, see figure 1

  4. If the measuring value in step1 is not the value  (1.8 kΩ, 2 kΩ,.∞ Ω) then the transducer is defected and should be changed.
