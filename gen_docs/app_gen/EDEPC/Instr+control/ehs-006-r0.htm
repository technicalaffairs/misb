Introduction

This module to control the pressure at first stage turbine 14.1 Mpa

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.
The first draft initiated by eng. Ashraf Farid, tested by  eng. Ashraf Farid at 4 May 2003, revised by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Abdl Menem Al Emam, accepted by general director: eng. Aly Abd El-Whab, and approved by eng. Awad Mohamed Fathy; Head Sector of Talkha Power Station.

Safety Precautions  

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, safety shoes …. etc

  • Unit out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85

  • mA injection set type Siemens Calibrator O5 – 7NH2011

  • mV injection set type: Siemens Calibrator O5 – 7NH2011

  • Screw driver

 

Work to be Carried Out

Notes: Inject I/P Control Signal 4-20 mA corresponding measuring range to be controlled 0-16 Mpa.

  1. Connect mA generator to point X30:373, 374 in rear control cabinet side no. jc03.

  2. Take card no. A11+32 type(D3005B1) inside module A11 (Manual Valve) out of position to d-energized relay K3  inside card no. :A11+76 type(D7301B1).

  3. Inject 18.1 mA corresponding limit control signal 14.1 Mpa during this lamp H9 in front card A13+34 type (D7209B1) will be light.

  4. If the lamp H9 not light change variable résistance R3 in front card A11+56 type(H2502B1) until light comes.
