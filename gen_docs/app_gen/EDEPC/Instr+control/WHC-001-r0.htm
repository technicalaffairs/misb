Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection checklist I1, to be carried out on an annual bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The heating system is divided to two main subsystems:  Low Pressure heaters, High Pressure heaters.

LP heaters subsystem uses for heating main condensate water, the main condensate water flows from discharge main condensate water pumps to feed water tank via four LP heaters. The heat transfer between the steam extraction & main condensate water products condensate inside the LP heaters. The level controllers systems keeps the condensate water level inside each LP heater. RTD and T/C for measuring the inlet and outlet temperature of main condensate water for each LP heater, the high level switches LP heaters protects  the main condensate water pumps.

HP heaters subsystem uses for heating feed water, the feed water flows from feed water tank to main feed water control valve via two HP heaters. The heat transfer between the steam extraction between feed water products condensate inside the HP heaters. the level controllers systems keeps the condensate water level inside each hp heater, t/c for measuring the inlet and outlet temperature of main condensate water for each HP heater, the high level switches HP heaters protects  the main feed water pumps and operate HP heaters bypass solenoid valve .

Each LP heaters subsystem consists of displacer level transmitter, pneumatic drain valve (condensate water flows from heater to another heater), pneumatic emergency valve (condensate water flows from heater to main condenser), pneumatic positioner, electro-pneumatic positioner, electro-pneumatic converter, solenoid valves, level switches and limit switches.

Each HP heaters subsystem consists of displacer level transmitter, pneumatic drain valve (condensate water flows from heater to another heater), pneumatic emergency valve (condensate water flows from heater to main condenser), pneumatic positioner, electro-pneumatic positioner, electro-pneumatic converter, solenoid valves and limit switches.

Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, anti static hand probe to avoid electrical shock …etc

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required       

  • Obtain a copy of relay control circuit diagram of Trash Rack & Band Screen cleaners

  • Obtain a copy of release and protection SAMA diagram of main/secondary pumps.

  • Obtain a copy of control circuit diagram for discharge valve (Flap Valve) of main cooling water pump

    •

  • The unit and the system must be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Screw driver kit, manual tools

  • Digital multi-meter.

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Oil spry cleaner & Teflon roll.

  • Pneumatic Calibrator

  • External 24Vdc power supply.

  • Current injector.
