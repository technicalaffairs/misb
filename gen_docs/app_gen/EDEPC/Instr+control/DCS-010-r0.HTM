Safety Precautions

  • A work order must be issued, the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety ID category.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, anti static hand probe to avoid electrical shock, nose mouth dust cover, eye glasses ... etc

  • The unit must be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital multi-meter.

  • Hand held Radio wave for communication

  • Screw driver kit.

  • Antistatic vacuum cleaner for dust cleaning.

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. For standby unit, visually inspect the wiring connections.
    Retighten the wirings and cards every year    .

  2. Clean the following parts:
    Panel rack, Electronic cards, Edge cards, Door filter, Air fan

  3. Check the power supply Must be
     110 A.C ± 10 %
    +ve 24 D.C ± 10 %
    +ve 12 D.C ± 10 %
     +ve 5 D.C ± 10 %

  4. Transfer the stand by unit to service

  5. Transfer the first to standby unit and repeat the previous steps from 1: 3
