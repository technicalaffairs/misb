Introduction

This document outlines the testing procedures P1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The liquid fuel control system of GE GT mark II model no. 4930MS, Cards No. IC 3600 SFUA (1L-1I), SFKC (1L-1H) and magnetic Pickup (77FD1, 77FD2) P/N (184A8691G5) and servo valve (65FP) P/N (312A6077P1),

The first draft initiated by eng. Sawsan El_Lethy, tested by eng. Mohamed Hasan EL_Sehrawy at 02 Jan 2004,  reversed by EDEPCo. quality control  eng. Abdel Menem Al Emam, and approved by director eng. Mohamed  Goda EL_Said

Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued the maintenance staff should be qualified and carry the suitable safety category

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear the personal protective equipment as (safety shoes, Helmet).

  • Turbine must be shut down

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital AVO. Meter, Frequency meter

  • Speed tronic calibrator set model  (151A4925)

  • Remover card.

  • Special tool to adjust the variable resistance at the cards

Stability Gain Adjustment

Gain (Servo Volt Change/ VCE Change)

  1. Pull SFUA or disconnect feed back oscillator

  2. Set 1005 NHP

  3. Adjust (VCE) from card SSKC to obtain voltage at SCB LINE (24) equal= 0

  4. Read voltage at TP2 ref on SFKC as equ. = 0.3

  5. Adjust VCE to increase ref. By
    E = E0 + E1
    E = E0 + 4/ GAIN
    E = -0.03 + 4/3.2 = 1.22

  6. Adjust VCE until voltage at Tp2 (ref) on SFKC card is 1.22 volt

  7. Read servo voltage pin (24) adjust “STAB POT R 104” if necessary to obtain –4.0 ± 0.2 volt
