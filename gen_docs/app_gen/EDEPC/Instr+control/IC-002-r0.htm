Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every 3000 hours of operation of the I&C on the F.D fan operation

This procedure was applied in ABU QIR power plant since 1985 and the final draft is issued in May2005

The first draft is initiated and tested by eng. Mohamed Ahmed Abd El Salam, approved by eng. Khalil Aly Khalil general director of Abu Kir PS, accepted by eng. Aly Anwar El-Fayoumy Head Sector of  Abu Qir PS, certified by eng. Mona Ahmad El Sharkawy; WDEPC Inspection and Quality Control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear safety and healthy equipment as safety healthy, safety shoes

Tools and Equipment

  • Multi-meter

  • Hydraulic calibration pump

  • Calibrated pressure gauge,Oil pot

Work to be Carried Out

Thermocouples

NG 2 T (04tz, 02tz, 06tz, 08tz, 010tz, 012tz, 014tz)
NG 1 T (03tz, 01tz, 05tz, 07tz, 09tz, 011tz).

  1. Check the thermo couple terminal and clean it.

  2. Check the prob and clean it.

  3. Divide the thermo couple according to the following groups.
    (J, N, S, C, K)

  4. measure the mv according to the measurement temperature and calibrate it using hot oil pot.

Servo motor positioner

  1. Keep all connection tightened and check s the connection using soapsuds.

  2. Dismantle the rod for control valve and internal lines and clean it.

  3. Dismantle the filter and clean it from dust.

  4. Dismantle the nut fixing for the valve and put out the valve body and lub. it.

  5. Put light oil on the valve in the spring axis.

  6. Check the adjustment.

Positioner calibration

  1. Connect the air source with 20 PSI.

  2. Inject the signal from 3 to 15 PSI.

  3. Calibrate the zero by inject 3 PSI and adjust the zero from screw zero adjustment.

  4. Calibrate the max by inject the 15 PSI and adjust the max from screw large adjustment.

  5. Make sure for middle scale.

Converter calibration

  1. Make sure of electrical terminals tighten and check the supply air pressure.

  2. Check the terminal and connection (insulation).

  3. Check the converter by send the control electrical current to converter and measure the pneumatic o/p signal.

  4. Insject 4-20 MA and adjust the out put form 3-15 PS.

Maintenance

  1. Keep the air gap clear by covering the converter

  2. There are two rooms between them there is an orifice, dismantle the nozzle and put carefully a wire (0.2 mm diameter) through the orifice to clean it

  3. Keep filters clean
