Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, to be performed on an annually bases as indicated by Y1 in the title block.

The Card SGDD function to detect any ground faults on P12, N12, N5.3, N28, thermocouples, and A Com. "common".

The first draft initiated by eng. Taher Abdel Galel, tested by eng. Tarek El Sayed  Abdel Razeq at 14 June 2002, reviewed by EDEPCo. quality control eng. Abd El-Momem El-Emam, accepted by general director eng. Yousry Abd El-Maksuod, and approved by eng. Mostafa Allam head sector of Talkha PS

Safety Precautions

  • Turbine shutdown

  • Work order must be issued

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes ... etc

Tools and Equipment

  • 2 Digital voltmeter, 0-150 VDC   0-500 VAC, with freq. response of 10Hz to 20Khz, accuracy  0.01 V

  • Calibrator set cat no. IC 4988A100AA

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Connect a digital voltmeter POS to GND jack on front of SGDD card and neg to a red jack (TX-A-IN , OTA or OTB on thermocouple processing module)

  2. Check the (DVM) reading if is 0.3 volt D.C is ok if not adjust R25 until the reading is 0.3 volt D.C

  3. Place jumper between GND and P28Z (both on front of SGDD) and note meter reading still 0.3 volt D.C ± 25 millvolt D.C.

  4. Remove jumper

  5. Place jumper between GND and N12Z (Both on front of SGDD) and note meter reading still 0.3 volt D.C ± 25 millvolt D.C.

  6. Remove jumper

  7. Place jumper between GND and N12Z (Both on front of SGDD) check the (DVM) reading if is 0.3 volt D.C is ok. if not adjust offset pot R40 until the voltmeter reads + 0.3 volt D.C.

  8. Recheck steps from 1 to 7 to confirm that the calibration is OK

  9. Remove jumpers at steps 3, 5, 7.
