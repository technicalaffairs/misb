Introduction

The electro hydraulic converter - Module EHP12 for steam turbine control - consists of two parts: electromechanical and hydraulic part.

EHP12 module convert the electrical signal (mA) "out put of module A10 inside cabinet JCO1" to a mechanical signal to control the hydraulic oil pressure for high and medium turbine (control the ratio of opening the load valves).

This document outlines the testing procedure P1, which performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the title block.

 Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes …. etc

  • Unit out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85

  • Screw driver

Work to be carried out

  1. Connect jumper between point x20:90 and point x20:97 in rear control cabinet side NO: JC02 (Imitation condition: phased ON)

  2. Disconnect point (x20:108) in rear control cabinet side JC02  (Imitation condition: tripe oil OK)

  3. Disconnect socket of card A31+54 type A2121B1 in front control cabinet JC03 (Imitation: digital  speed)

  4. Press switch S1 on card A23+76 type D6209B1 in front control cabinet JC02 (condition: Basic mode)    

  5. Connect jumper between point X20:90 and point x20:110 in rear control cabinet side JC02 (Imitation condition: limiter > limit).

  6. Change variable resistance R1 in front card A10+60 type A2804B1 to adjust the o/p signal on point  A10+60:10,11 to 26v and put the control valves 1,2,3,4 in zero position.

  7. Make valves (1,2,3,4) completely open (100%) and check the o/p voltage at point A10+60:11,10  must be 6.3 Vdc if not ok adjust from r2 in front card A10+60  type A2804B1

  8. Make the valves again to zero position if the o/p voltage not equal 26 then readjust the variable resistance R1 .

  9. Repeat steeps 6, 7, 8 up to no change. 

Return Circuit in Service

  1. Remove Jumper between point X20:90 and point X20:97.

  2. Connect Signal (trip oil not ok) X20 :108.

  3. Remove Jumper between point X20:90 and point X20:110.

  4. Connect Socket A31+54 type A2121B1.
