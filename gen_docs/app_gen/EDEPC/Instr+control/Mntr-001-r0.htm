Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required,

  • Wear the appropriate safety equipment as safety shoes, safety helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses

  • Unit must be shut down.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set

  • Digital voltmeter

  • Air Blower.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect power and data cables from monitor.

  2. Disassemble screen & power supply.

  3. Clean the monitor & power supply using air blower.

  4. Disassemble the power supply unit and the CRT.

  5. Test the power supply and check if the voltage is within the limits of ( 160 , 100 , 170 , 6 , 25 , 18 ) VDC + 10%.

  6. If the voltage is OK that's mean the power supply is ok, if not adjust from the potentiometer.
    Note: If we can not adjust you must change the power supply.

  7. Reassemble power supply to screen.

  8. Reassemble screen in the same position.

  9. Connect all cable to screen.

  10. Reconnect power to it.

  11. Adjust screen, focus, and brightness (vertical & horizontal).

  12. Reconnect power to power supply unit and adjust it according to step 5.
