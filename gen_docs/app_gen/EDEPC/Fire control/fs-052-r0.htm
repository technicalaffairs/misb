Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed on monthly bases as indicated by M1 in the title block.

The test of the foam fire system without discharge of the foam solution should be made monthly. The test is to assure that the solenoid will open when receive the signals given by the two heat sensitive cables

Technical Specifications:

Heat Sensitive Cables:  
Type: Double cored insulated
Core Material: Stainless Steel
Insulation Breadown 90°c

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued.

  • The maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Maintenance staff should be well trained, qualified or directly supervised by some one who is

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

  • Wear the personal protective equipment must used as safety shoes, safety helmet ...etc

  • Secure the isolation of the foam fire system to prevent discharge of the foam to the fuel oil in the tanks

  • Before carrying out the test inform the authorities in charge

 

Tools and Equipments

  • Wrench set.

  • Screw drivers set, Pliers.

 

Work to Be Carried Out

  1. Close the manual isolate gate valve before the automatic deluge valve for each three tanks

  2. Simulate the alarm conditions of the two heat sensitive cables of the each tank apart

  3. Be sure that the solenoid of the deluge valve will open when receive the signals of the two heat sensitive cables

  4. Check the electric alarm transmission to the fire alarm panel

After the test

  1. Reset the alarm conditions and the alarm panel

  2. Be sure that the solenoid valve is closed

  3. Open the manual isolate gate valve

 

Now the foam fire system is in operational condition
