Introduction

The automatic deluge water spray system operation test should be made monthly.
The system is provided with a sprinklers, of the open type, fed by a water supply system through a deluge valve, the valve is actuated automatically by a thermal detectors rated at 58 °C located in the same area of the sprinklers. When the temperature causes the contacts of the thermal detector to close and the deluge to open, the pressurized water will flow immediately into the fire through the open type sprinklers

 

Preaction water deluge system operation test should be made monthly.
The system employs sprinklers, fusible linked type set at 74°C, fed by a water supply system through a deluge valve, the valve is actuated automatically by a thermal detectors rated at 58°C located in the same area of the sprinklers. When deluge valve opens, water flows through the sprinklers which may be opened by heat.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required

Preparations and Requirements

  1. Remote annunciator panel and system control panel being tested are powered.

  2. No alarm of malfunction is indicated on each control panel.

  3. Remote annunciator panel switch is in on position (alarm system feeding).

  4. Deluge valve is closed and riser isolating valve and gate valves are open.

  5. Sprinklers are installed and plugs in place of nozzles for deluge system test are installed.

Work to Be Carried Out

  1. Connect test detector to the control panel of the system being tested.

  2. Place a suitable heat source under the detector ( rated at 58 °C) and check that:
         * The fire alarm LED is lighting and acoustic alarm is sounding panel;
         * The solenoid valve is energized after 15 seconds after the fire alarm.
         * The fire alarm LED on the remote annunciator panel flashes as soon as fire is detected and 15 second after the light extinguishing system actuated LED and acoustical alarm signal is given.

  3. Press the alarm acknowledge pushbutton on the remote annunciator panel.

  4. Check that sounding off alarm on.

  5. Press the system pushbutton on reset control panel check that the alarm light is ON, check the valve field condition and provided for according to the case after resetting.

  6. Press the reset pushbutton on remote annunciator panel and return in normal condition.

  7. Check that no other alarm or fault indication is present either on remote annunciator panel or on the local control panel.


                                                         Now the system is in operation condition
