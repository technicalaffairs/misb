Introduction

The foam system provided as protection of the three fuel storage tanks. The system operates automatically on signal given by the two heat sensitive cables at each or can be initiated manually

Blanced pressure proportioning system

The principle of operation is based on the use of venturi proportioned (ratio controller). As water passes through the venturi it creates a pressure drop area at its throat this causes foam liquid to flow into the reduced pressure area the foam liquid flow is proportionate to the water flow through the venturi

 

System operation

Incase of fire the signal given by the two heat sensitive cables will cause the appropriate solenoid actuated valve to open.

The sudden drop in pressure will cal water from the main ring and at the same time the duty foam pump will start

The water foam solution will flow to the foam chambers of the fuel oil tanks foam will generate and will pour inside the fuel oil tank involved in the fire

The pressure switch at the deluge valve will be immediately give signal to the control room

The system is designed that can be iniated manually befor the intervention of the heat sensetive cables by opening the by-pass trim of the solenoid operated deluge valve

 

Operators should ensure the followings

  1. The water main always under pressure

  2. Sufficient foam solution at the foam tank

  3. Daily inspect foam equipments

Check and maintenance

  1. The concentrate foam solution must be checked for its expansion from time to time (monthly)

  2. Flush water supply pipes and foam pipesto remove scale (monthly)

  3. Check efficiency of deluge valves (monthly)

  4. Simulate "fault" or "alarm" conditions to test the foam fire system (monthly)

  5. Check pumps grease (monthly)

  6. Check pumps seals and tighten (yearly)

  7. Check the operation of the foam pumps and recirculate the foam to the tank
