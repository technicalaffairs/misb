Introduction

This document outlines the requirements for the installation of 4 hydraulically operated water monitors.

These water monitors shall be used, together with a fixed foam system, for the protection of the tank farm area.

When a tank is on fire, the foam system will protect the tank itself, and the water monitors will provide an adequate water cooling of the closed fuel tank (s) or to give coverage to fire fighting personnel in salvage or emergency situation.

The monitors and their corresponding equipment's shall be located inside the fuel tank diked area.

Each water monitor is manually operable, from a remote hydraulic desk, and locally through the hand wheels suitably mounted on the monitor rotating joints.

The oil distribution tubing between the hydraulic power pack and the monitor or oil motors, shall be mounted in a cable tray

The monitors are permanently connected to the pressurized water ring and have an individual capacity of 500 its/min (90 cum/h).

The monitor supply line is provided with an isolating valve, which is equipeped like most isolating valves with a device to indicate the passage of water.

Each monitor is further equipped with its own individual control valve in order to regulate the water flow according to the operator judgment.

The monitors can be operated manually or from a remote control station, they are capable of a vertical rotation of 150 o and a full horizontal rotation of 360 °.

The spray nozzle can be adjusted, also from the remote station, from a straight stream to a wide fog pattern.

Operators are advised to practice with the actual movements of the monitors in order to become familiar with the controls and to experiment the various possibilities of water throw and reach.

System description

The main components of the water monitors

 

The remote hydraulic desk (operation desk):
Main components

  1. Oil distributors handles

  2. Main power switch

  3. Pumps 1.2 on - off switches

  4. Oil pressure gauge

 

 

 

 

 

 

Oil Distributors Movements

  1. Water valve open - close

  2. Nozzle full - fog jet

  3. Monitor up - down (150°C)

  4. Monitor left - right (360°C)

The components of the hydraulic oil distributor unit:

  1. No 2 gear pump equipped with suction filters

  2. No2 electric motors 0.75 kw

  3. Oil tank equipped with level gless

  4. Emergency hand pump

  5. Discharge and return ball valves

  6. Pressure relief valve

  7. Check valves

  8. Flow regulators

  9. Remote hydraulic desk
