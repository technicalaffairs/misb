Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list I1 to be performed on a monthly as indicated by M1 in the above title block.

The 12v-71- detroit diesel engine (starting - 500HP detroit diesel engine), the diesel is a 12 cylinder, 2 cycle engine, with a speed rating of 2300 rpm.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued,

  • The qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution mark surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety, helmet, safety shoes, eye glass, nose mouth dust cover .. etc

  • The diesel engine must be shut down for oil checks.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Safety and healthy equipment.

  • Rags.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check oil level.

  2. Check visually oil viscosity and color.

  3. Check visually the pipes and fittings for oil leakage.

Remedy

  1. Refill diesel oil super duty (25/50) until the operating mark level (if needed to increase).

  2. If the working oil contains contamination and dilution:

    1. Open the drain plug.

    2. After drainage change the oil filter by a new one and retight the drain plug.

    3. Refill a new fresh oil as recommended with 32 liters.

  3. If oil leakage exist retight the filtings bolts and nuts.

  4. Clean with rags and suitable remover solution.

  5. Return and away rags and tools.
