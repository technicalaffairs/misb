Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list I2, to be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block

The 12v-71- N detroit diesel engine (starting - 500HP detroit diesel engine), the diesel is a 12 cylinder, 2 cycle engine, with a speed rating of 2300 rpm.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the maintenance staff should carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution mark surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety, helmet, safety shoes, eye glass, nose mouth dust cover .. etc

  • The diesel engine must be checked in operation and after shutdown for fuel system checks, air system checks, and torque converter

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set

  • Safety and healthy equipment

  • Rags

    •

Work to be Carried Out

In operation checks

  1. Fuel system operation (turbine crank) check leaks, speed governor operation.

  2. Air system:   a) Air line connections     b) Crank case breather.  

  3. Air box drains.

Shutdown checks

  1. Lubricating system checks.

  2. Fuel oil filter.

Remedy     The engine must be at shutdown.

  1. Drain sediment from fuel filters.

  2. Adjusting the speed governor using tachometer (speed meter).

  3. Tighten as required for Air line connections.

  4. Clean the breather of crank case ventilation.

  5. Open drain box drains, remove air box covers and examine the air box floor for water is found, wips, the air box dry, and remove and clean the air box drain tubes.

  6. Clean the blower screens.
