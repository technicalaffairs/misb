Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

    •

  • Wear safety and healty equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, earplug, gloves pairs, breathing mask, safety glasses

    • ... etc

  • Be sure that the emergency diesel is out of service.

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Measuring and checking tools.

  • Cleaning articles, Spray gun, Oil can.

  • Magnetic pick – up flexible.

    •

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Take an oil sample from engine crankcase to be analyzed, add or change, if it is required.

  2. Clean cooling system.

  3. Treat any leakage if it is required.
