Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area.

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment must be used: safety shoes, earplug , gloves pairs, helmet, breathing mask, safety glasses

    •

  • Be sure that the emergency diesel is out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Measuring and checking tools.

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Cleaning articles.

  • Magnetic pick – up flexible.

  • Spray gun,

  • Oil can,

  • Grease gun.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check engine oil, add or change, if it is required.

  2. Check oil filter and clean it if required.

  3. Inspect for oil leaks of connections, and treat any leakage if it is required.

  4. Check fuel oil level, and readjust it if it is required.

  5. Inspect coolant elements (water level – leakage).

  6. Grease the fanes of cooling system if it is required.

  7. Drain water and sediment from the fuel tank.

  8. Drain water and sediment from the air tank.

  9. Check air starting motor.

  10. Clean air start screen and check the differential pressure.

  11. Check injection synchronization.
