Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection list I3, which must be performed after 1000 operating hours as indicated by H1000 in the above titled block

Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

    •

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes, earplug, gloves pairs, breathing mask, safety glasses

  • Be sure that the emergency diesel is out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Complete maintenance tool set.

  • Measuring and checking tools.

  • Cleaning articles.

  • Magnetic pick – up flexible.

  • Spray gun, Oil can.

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Check injection fuel oil valve lashes (clearances) between the rocker arm and the bridge and adjust.

  2. Check valve bridge.

  3. Check injection timing.

  4. Check injection synchronization.

  5. Clean crankcase breather system.

  6. Clean and inspect oil filters.

  7. Clean oil filters and replace damaged element if it is required.

  8. Clean primary fuel filters and drain water.

  9. Take an oil sample to analyze it.

  10. Inspect exhaust – system and check for leaks.

  11. Inspect air filter and check restriction.

  12. Lubricate manual bearing device.
