Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes, ... etc

  • Close the isolate valves for level glass

  • Wait level glass to be cold

    •

Tools and Equipment

  • Screw kit

  • Grinder & grinding wheel

  • Cleaning tools & keys kit

 

 

 

 

Work to be carried out

  1. Internal inspection for evidence of corrosion or scaling

  2. Clean internal parts from the foreign bodies

  3. Check the glass replace if defect

  4. Check gaskets change the defect

  5. Check the mica change the defect

  6. Tighten the bolts at cold

  7. Worming the level glass slowly by opening the valves at min flow

  8. Retighten the bolts after worming and make the level glass in service
