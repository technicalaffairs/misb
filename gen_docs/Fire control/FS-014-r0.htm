Introduction

This document out lists the testing procedure P1, which must be carried out every six months as indicate by M6 in the above title block.

This document tell how to check & schedule maintenance fire fighting program in oil Mazot (20,000-2000-tons) & Solar tank(1000-m³)  fire fighting system test. for oil and solar tank .out of door. Fire fighting system. the system have foam tank (1500 gallon) capacity, control unit (FCI), water control valve, mixer water and foam, thermal sensors, 0.5 inch nozzle and manual switch.  

Note1. (Test Fire Fighting  System on oil  and solar tanks which is used foam and water by 3% and 97% as mixed ratio  respectively  in mixing line inside tank to make isolating material to separate between fir and oil or solar )

Note2: all thermal sensors inside tank

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipments safety helmet, safety shoes …. Etc

  • Unit out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85

  • Screw driver, French spanner

  • Safe flam source as flam match

  • Adjustable spanner.

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Close valves on foam tanks to check system by water only with foam .  

  2. Pull the manual then the system activated by control panel.

  3. Water go on throw spray around  the tank and flasher horn working .

  4. Clean the water spray around tank if  not passing water

Note:
Testing of fire attend out of tank only to keep system inside tank with treble . 

Return system  to service 

  1. Return the manual riles to it is position (OFF)

  2. Make rest from panel FCI for flasher horn .

  3. Return the control valve on water pipe in close position .

  4. Open the isolating valve on water pipe slowly .

  5. Open the isolating valve on foam  tank.
