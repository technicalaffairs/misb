Introduction

This document out lists the testing procedure P1, which must be carried out every six months as indicate by M6 in the above title block.

This document tell how to check & schedule service Transformers 1,2 CT  (10,20,30,40,50,60,70) in first unit and second unit respectively this system. in door ,using hallon in first unit and F.M.200 in second unit. Have cylinder containing (78) bonds of Hallon or fm.200 , according to unit number, 0.5 inch nozzle and cylinder head provided by metal spring. Two smoke detectors and to thermal detectors including to energized one of two sensor ( one smoke sensor and one thermal sensor) is necessary to energized system

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area,

  • Wear your safety helmet , shoes …. Etc

  • The unit and transformer must be out of service and isolated

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85

  • Screw driver, Smoke source

  • Adjustable spanner, French spanner.

Work to be Carried Out
  1. Transformer must be out of service and .per mated isolation by operator eng. Department

  2. Testing alarm only by supposed smoke source to smoke detector the alarm will be energized with starting flash lamp and horn voice tell alarm only

  3. Dismount the control head on Hallon cylinder .

  4. Start smoke source nearest the sensors (smoke, temperature sensors)

  5. When sensors energized (two smoke sensor with one temperature sensor )signal transmitted to cabinet control unit then fire fighting activated . 

  6. After 30 Second the control head energized and alarm horn & flashing lamp start

  7. If the signal not come to the cabinet k. p s ( step 4 ) then check wiring cable & sensors

  8. This test by using manual riles (ON) system will be energized and after 30 sec. the control head energized and alarm horn & flashing lamp start

Return system  to service 

  1. Return the Manuel to its position (off ).

  2. Make rest for control cabinet to stop Horne and flasher .

  3. Connect the control head on slender .

  4. Return the transformer in service .
