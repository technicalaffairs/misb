Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months indicated by M6 in the title block

This document outlines the testing of 6 Halon cylinders, each cylinder weight 13 kg as follows:

  • Gas Generator Package: Externally mounted on the compartment wall are ( 3 ) cylinders

  • Accessories Package: Internally mounted on the compartment wall is (1) cylinder

  • AC Generator Package: Internally mounted on the compartment wall are (2) cylinder

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes.... etc.

  • Unit should be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool  set.

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes.... etc

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect power supply of the system

  2. Disconnect the discharge heads from  all cylinders and clean

  3. Disconnect the solenoid valve assemblies from the cylinders

  4. Weight each cylinder (If at any time a cylinder shows a less in net weight it should be re–filled or replaced )

  5. Check the empty and full weights of all cylinders are permanently stamped on the cylinders valve bodies

  6. Each time the cylinders are weights( the date of weighting and the net weight  should be recorded on a tag attached to the  cylinders )

  7. Check the halon pressure.

Return System to Service

  1. Connect the discharge heads from all cylinders.

  2. Connect the solenoid valve assemblies from the cylinders.

  3. Put Unit into service.

  4. Connect power supply of the system.
