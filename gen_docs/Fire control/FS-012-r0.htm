Introduction

This document out lists the testing procedure P1, which must be carried out every six months as indicate by M6 in the above title block.

This document outlines how to check & schedule maintenance fire fighting program for out door transformers, in first unit and second unit.

The equipment have pressure water source 10 bar through deluge valve, output nozzle 0.5inch diameter, thermal eight sensors to sensing temperature at 80 C, and terminal control box FCI. in first unit & scorpidio in second unit feeded by 220V source or 24 v.dc u.s.a. American unit.  

The  first draft prepared by eng. Moustfa Awadi, tested by eng. Adel Shawki & Safety eng. Ashraf Gaber at 10 May 2003, revised by quality control eng. i & c Abdl  Menem Al Emam & EDEPC Safety eng. Medhat Kater, approved by eng. Awad Mohamed Fathy sector head of Talkha Power station, and certified by eng. Aly Mostafa AL Lethy EDEPC inspection and quality control head sector.

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued and the qualified maintenance staff should be carry the Suitable safety category,

  • Safety fence (rope) with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety helmet, shoes …. etc.

  • The unit and transformer must be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Digital Multimeter type Fluke 85

  • Screw drive and French spanner

  • Safe flam source as flam match.

  • Adjustable spanner.

Work to be Carried Out
  1. Transformer must be out of service and .per mated isolation by operator eng. Department

  2. Starting flame source nearest the sensor to reach temperature around transformer more than 80 C then sensor energized.

  3. When sensor energized the fire fighting system activated & starting alarm Horne with flashing lamp panel, the water isolating valve automatically open to push water around transformer to cooling it.

  4. If the sensor signal not come to the control unit panel ( step 3 ) then check wiring cable sensors and control unit and attended fault.

  5. To test manual riles close the isolating valve, return deluge valve and reset alarm (system normalized) 

  6. Open the isolating valve.

  7. Bull down  manual riles (ON) system will energized and water start to pushing around transformer to cooling

  8. If system not activated, then check manual relies and wire. 

Return system  to service

  1. Return the manual riles to it is position (OFF)

  2. Make rest from control panel for horn  and flasher .

  3. Return the deluge valve on water pipe in close position .

  4. Open the isolating valve on water pipe slowly .
