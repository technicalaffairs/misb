Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection check list, I1 which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block.

Deluge Valve is opened to allow passing water to mix with foam through ejector if two fire detectors operate at (90 °c)

Work to be Carried out

 

Testing

  1. Record the water pressure gauge (Part # 1).

  2. Close the main water isolate valve (Part # 4).

  3. Close the1/4 inch ball by pass valve (Part # 2).

  4. Open the emergency valve model (DL-A) to relieve water pressure in the pilot line and upper valve chamber.

  5. Notice the loss of water pressure reading on the pressure gauge (item # 1) and the diaphragm piston assembly retraction from the deluge valve seat.

Re-setting

  1. Close the manual emergency valve model (DL-A). 

  2. Open 1/4 inch ball by-pass valve (Part # 2).

  3. Notice that the pressure gauge (Part # 1) will be holding steady at full water supply pressure.

  4. Open the main water supply isolating valve (part # 4) slowly.

  5. Depress plunger on ball drip valve (Part # 3). To drain any water that may be accumulate in the outer chamber.
