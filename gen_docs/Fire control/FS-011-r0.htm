Safety Precautions

  • Work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff must carry the suitable ID safety category,

  • Safety fence fence with caution marks must be surrounding the work area,

  • Wear appropriate safety and healthy protective equipment must be used as safety shoes, helmet .. etc.

  • Gas Turbine must be out of service.

  • Don't allow to any material or fluids could be cause a fire in the work area

Tools and Equipment

  • Safety Helmet, belt, shoes, .... etc

  • Digital multimeter.

  • Proper air pressure supply (about 500 PSI).

  • 125 Vdc supply.

  • Gun for testing the fire detector

  • Suitable tool set (Thermometer 500 °C, Screwdriver, Changeable wrench, pliers)

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Solenoid Valve

  1. Review technical reference material manufactures setting / manual.

  2. Clean all solenoid valves, the cleaning interval will depending on the media and service conditions in general, if the voltage to the coil is correct, sluggish valve operation or excessive leakage will indicate that cleaning is required.

  3. Apply a proper air pressure to the inlet of the solenoid valve.  ref. value 500 PSI

  4. Check the leakage from the out let (must be none).

  5. Apply 125 VDC to the coil terminals.

  6. Be sure the solenoid valve opened and the air comes out from the outlet.

  7. If the valve is working good ok otherwise change the valve.

  8. Ensure that the CO2 gate is closed at the turbine compartment.
