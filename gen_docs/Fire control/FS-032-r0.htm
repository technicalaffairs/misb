Introduction

This document outlines the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months as indicated by M6 in the above title block

This Fire fighting system is working at turbine hall with CO2 using smoke, heat and  flame detectors.

The first draft is initiated by eng. Mohamed Ali Ali, tested by eng. Al shahat Ahmed Moussa, revised by EDEPC. quality control eng. Abdel menim Al Emam, accepted by general director eng. Hamdy Abdel Mawla Melegy  Head sector of El-Shabab GT power station .

This document is certified by eng. Samir Abd El Gilil EDEPC MPIS coordinator, and certified by accountant Mahmoud Mohamed Soliman; EDEPC admin and financial inspection, quality control General Director and supervisor of inspection and quality control head sector.

The Testing Scheme Consists of:

  • Early warning system FMZ 2001.

  • Fire alarm system.

  • Fire detection control panel FMZ.

  • Automatic fire detectors.

Safety Precautions

  • A work pemit must by issued and the qualified maintenance stuff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Wear your safety andhealth equipment as safety helmet, safety shoes ... etc.

  • Disconnect the two pins of CO2 cylinders

  • Disconnect the socket of line cable to avoid discharging of CO2 cylinders

  • Unit should be out of service

Tools and Equipment

  • Multimeter and connection wires.

  • Air blower, heat supply, smoke supply, electric power supply, contact cleaner.

  • Avometer AC/DC.

    •

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

  1. Disconnect CO2 cylinders in the GT unit by removing two bolts on the lever.

  2. Disconnect the socket of line cable to avoid discharging of CO2 cylinders

  3. Test the function on automatic fire detectors for:
         - Ionization smoke detector IMX 1001 E
         - Heat detector WMX1000 D 90
         - Flame detector FMX

  4. Test Line Rack (Line Card) as follows:
         - Adjust line voltage to 12 - 13 DC volts by poti. L-U
         - Adjust break threshold to (1.5 - 2.5) volts by poti. ST
         - Adjust  fire threshold to (1.5 - 2.5) volts by poti. F
         - Line current reading should by in the range of 12-42 mA(at - b1)

  5. Test Battery Rack (Battery Card)
         - Adjust battery charging tension 26.5 - 27.5 volts by potentiometer R26 (R9)
         - Adjust battery charging tension to 3 amps (Max) by poti. R30 (R3)

  6. Central Rack
         - Net and switching card adjust voltage out M1 M2 to 23 - 24 volts by poti R4
         - Main fire control card
         - Adjust impulse time to 1-5 sec by poti. R5

  7. Check fire threshold by operating end of the line detector or fire switch

  8. Check break threshold by removing end of the line detector or fire switch
