Introduction

This document lists the equipment inspection I1, which must be performed every six months bases indicated by M6  in the title block

This document outlines the testing of the AC Generator Package, Gas Generator Package, and Accessories Package fire extinguisher system, which contain two cylinders each of these are connected by manifolds and pipe work to discharge nozzles in the compartments.

Three lamp system state indicators, together with break glass manual operation units are also mounted on the outside of the compartment adjacent to each compartment access door

Safety Precautions

  • A work permit must be issued, the qualified maintenance staff should carry the suitable safety category.

  • Safety fence with caution marks surrounding the work area is required.

  • Wear your safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes.... etc.

  • Put system at position manual.

  • Unit should be out of service.

Tools and Equipment

  • Suitable tool set.

  • Safety and healthy equipment as safety helmet, shoes.... etc

 

 

 

Work to be Carried Out

Manual Test

  1. Put the system on manual position.

  2. Disconnect power supply of the system.

  3. Disconnect all connection for solenoid.

  4. Connect power supply of the system.

  5. By shorting across the detection circuit to simulate fire detector.

  6. Actuation operates a red Halon Released lamp at the control panel.

  7. Operates an audible alarm.

  8. Illuminate the fire warning lamps mounted on the outside of the compartment adjacent to each compartment access door.

Auto Test

  1. Put the system on out position.

  2. Disconnect power supply of the system.

  3. Disconnect all connection for solenoid.

  4. Connect power supply of the system.

  5. By shorting across the detection circuit to simulate fire detector.

  6. Actuation operates a red Halon released lamp at the control panel.

  7. Operates an audible alarm.

  8. Illuminates the fire warning lamps mounted on the outside of the compartment adjacent to each compartment access door.

Return System to Service

  1. Connect all connection for solenoid

  2. Operate the reset switch fitted to the front panel

  3. Reset all alarms avilable

  4. Put system automatically

  5. Repeat the steps (1-8) for manual tests for gas detector and accessories

  6. Repeat the steps (1-8) for auto tests for gas detector and accessories
